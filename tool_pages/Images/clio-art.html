About Clio Art

Clio.art is a professional AI tool developed specifically for artists aiming to streamline art production. It enhances the creative process by offering full control over AI, allowing artists to preserve their original design and ideas. The use cases of this tool include pose reference, making variations in received art, applying shading, environment art, and weapon design. Users can upload any reference or use preset postures to generate images according to their vision. The Inpaint mode option allows users to alter design elements and combine them to create variations. Artists can apply shading to their designs while maintaining the original idea. The tool also offers an option to monetize the artist's style by creating an AI style based on their artworks and offering access through a subscription. Clio.art incorporates ethical practices by sourcing with artists' consent. The tool is designed to be quick and user-friendly thereby, improving workflow without compromising on the quality of the art production. It further allows artists to create and share their unique style.
