About Hotpot

Hotpot.ai is an AI-driven platform that provides tools and templates to help users create amazing graphics, pictures, and text. It features an AI Art Generator to spark creativity and automate tasks such as creating device mockups, social media posts, marketing images, app icons and more. Users can also use the AI Headshot Generator to create headshots and glamour shots in any style or scene imaginable. Hotpot also offers an AI Photo Upscaler that uses AI to increase image resolution and optimize for people, products and art. The AI Object Remover and Background Remover tools allow users to quickly erase unwanted parts from images. Hotpot also features an AI Color Generator, Face Enhancer and Picture Restorer to help users customize images, as well as an AI Copywriter to help users conquer writer's block and create compelling stories, product descriptions, Google ads and more. In addition, Hotpot offers easy-to-edit templates to help users create social media graphics, including Facebook posts, Instagram posts, LinkedIn banners and more. Designs are free or $1 per graphic.
