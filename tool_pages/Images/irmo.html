About Irmo

Irmo is an artificial intelligence-based mobile tool designed for digital content creation with a unique focus on producing AI art. It stands out by leveraging a learning system similar to DALL-E and Midjourney, making the generation of unique and diverse images possible. Irmo allows its users to create an unlimited number of images, providing flexibility and freedom for content generation. This tool is versatile, allowing you to generate images using input images or texts. It also has great potentials in different sectors such as creating Non-Fungible Tokens (NFTs) or generating stock images for commercial usage. Irmo's unique selling proposition lies in its application of AI technology and its unlimited generation capacity which makes it an ideal tool for creative professionals, NFT artists, and digital agencies. Its conveniences include its accessibility on both the App Store and Google Play, suitable for various smartphone users.
