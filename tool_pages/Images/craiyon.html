About Craiyon

Craiyon, formerly DALL-E mini, is an AI model that enables users to generate images from text prompts. It is a free online image generator with a user-friendly interface that allows users to easily draw images. The AI model behind Craiyon is based on the Generative Pre-trained Transformer 3 (GPT-3) model and has been trained on a large dataset of images and text. The images generated by Craiyon range from simple drawings to complex works of art. The model is designed to be intuitive and to generate images that reflect the text prompt. Additionally, the model is capable of generating images with a variety of styles, including cartoon, photographic, and abstract. The model is also capable of generating larger images, but these images may take longer to generate. With Craiyon, users can also print their creations on a t-shirt or share their results on the forum. Additionally, Craiyon is committed to protecting user privacy and allows users to control how their data is used.
