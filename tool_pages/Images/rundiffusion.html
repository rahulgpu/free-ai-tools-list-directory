About RunDiffusion

RunDiffusion is a fully managed, cloud-based platform designed to facilitate the ease and speed of creating AI generated art through the use of automatic tools such as Automatic1111, Fooocus, and ComfyUI. The platform features rapid access, with private workspaces available in under two minutes, and minimal coding. RunDiffusion offers an array of open source AI tools to choose from, each offering unique capabilities. These tools include Automatic1111 for Stable Diffusion, Fooocus for creating high-quality images, and ComfyUI for advanced capabilities. Additionally, the platform provides professional Artificial Intelligence (AI) business services, tailored for different levels of expertise from novice to expert. It also offers tools such as Invoke for inpainting and versatile diffusion capabilities. RunDiffusion caters to a wide range of users, from hobbyists to professionals and businesses, with provisions for commercial licensing available. The platform allows its users to create realistic photos and fine-tune their creations with detail and creativity. A built-in file browser ensures your projects are saved and readily available for subsequent sessions. RunDiffusion also offers an intelligent feature called 'Smart Timer' which aids in managing session duration and cost control to avoid unnecessary charges.
