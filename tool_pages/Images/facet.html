About Facet

Facet is an AI image generation and editing platform intended for professional use in creative fields. It is designed to provide real-time, intuitive control over the generation process, thereby providing a powerful tool for creatives while maintaining a level of artistic control. Facet's functionality allows users to specifically control the placement of elements within an image, an imperative feature for detailed and controlled creative work. It features a system to manage and minimize complexity of image prompts, simplifying the fluidity and efficacy of the creative process. This supports the addition of not just textual, but also image references to prompts, enabling rapid adherence to specific subject and stylistic visions. Facet also presents the unique offer of customized AI models. Users can train these models with their own images, generating consistent and personalized assets in an efficient manner. A central aspect of Facet is its focus on collaborative work. It provides a shared canvas offering teams the capability to build, edit, and expand on collective ideas. This offers a dynamic and interactive platform for team-oriented creative ventures. Pricing and plans vary according to needs and offer a range of perks including, but not limited to, private image generation, real-time generation, content-aware image editing, and high-resolution image generation.
