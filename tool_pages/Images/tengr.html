About Tengr

Tengr.ai is an AI-based platform designed to assist both businesses and creative art professionals. Leveraging the power of generative AI, this tool enables users to create and edit images. It's intended to offer an unlimited scope of creativity and imagination. Users maintain full ownership of any content they generate with Tengr.ai, allowing them to freely utilize their creations commercially. The platform encourages shared growth, with its AI system learning from user feedback to continually augment its capabilities and improve user experience. Additionally, Tengr.ai hosts an array of resources on its website, including video tutorials and technical blog posts that actively support user engagement and learning. While the website uses cookies to enhance user experience, users have the option to opt out at any time through the platform's 'legal' menu. Tengr.ai's operational model thus combines technology, customer-driven evolution, enabling an active community of users for shared learning and collaborative growth. It's important to note that the exact terms of service and privacy policy specific to Tengr.ai should be referenced directly from their official website.
