About Splend

Splend AI is an AI-powered tool that allows users to create art, including portraits, artwork, and landscapes, based on a simple description. The tool combines the capabilities of Midjourney, Stable Diffusion, and Dall-E into a single app, providing users with a range of options and styles to choose from. Users can either use pre-defined styles or create their own unique styles to bring their artistic vision to life.Once the artwork is created, users can share their creations with the community by creating a profile and showcasing their work. This provides an opportunity for users to engage with other AI artists and receive feedback on their creations. The sharing feature is completely free of charge.As an app, Splend AI is available for download on both Google Play and the App Store, making it accessible to a wide range of users. By leveraging AI capabilities, users can unleash their creativity and produce incredible masterpieces.The tool also features a gallery of recent creations by Splend AI users, showcasing the diverse range of artwork that can be generated using the app. From a happy elephant in a jungle to abstract concepts with earthy elegance, Splend AI offers a platform for users to explore their imagination and artistic style.Overall, Splend AI provides an intuitive and user-friendly interface for creating art with the help of AI. With its wide range of styles and the ability to share creations with the community, Splend AI is a valuable tool for both novice and experienced artists looking to explore the possibilities of generative art.
