About Shader App

Shader App is an innovative tool for augmented reality (AR) content creation without the need of any coding knowledge. It provides users the ability to instantly customize their appearance with the assistance of artificial intelligence (AI). The application enables a dynamic interaction model where users can type, swipe, or use voice commands to build a personalized look. The app also provides capabilities to record and share videos integrated with custom AR effects, intended to improve engagement and provide amusing interactions with the user's audience. Shader App also facilitates collaboration functionality, where users can co-create and build AR effects with friends. The app also offers a beta testing opportunity via Discord, inviting early users to experience its features. The Shader app is backed by several prominent investors like Betaworks, On Deck, Mozilla Ventures, Greysoft, and Differential Ventures. In addition to its offerings, it provides contact support and is committed to privacy and terms of service.
