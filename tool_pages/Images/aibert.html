About AiBert

AiBERT is an AI-driven tool, developed by Kooya Inc, which integrates seamlessly with WhatsApp to enhance your communication and productivity. This WhatsApp Bot utilises the powerful capabilities of both ChatGPT and MidJourney. AiBERT offers the ability to instantly generate text and create stunning images, improving the quality of your messages and potentially saving you significant time and effort. This tool also features end-to-end encryption, ensuring the safety and privacy of your data, aligning with WhatsApp's security standards. Furthermore, AiBERT's functionality is continuously evolving, with regular updates enhancing its capabilities. The tool's services can be accessed 24/7 as long as you have an internet connection and using it is as straightforward as sending a text message within WhatsApp. Customers also have access to round-the-clock support in the event of any issues, enhancing user experience. Signing up and setting up AiBERT is a simple process that entails choosing a pricing plan that best suits your needs and creating an account. With AiBERT, a powerful AI-based assistant is just a single text message away.
