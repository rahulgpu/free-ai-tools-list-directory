About DRAI

DRAI – AI Art Generator Image is an AI-powered image-editing app designed for iOS devices such as iPhones, iPads, and iPods. It is powered by the latest version of the Table Diffusion v2.1 technology and provides users with a range of features for creating and editing images. The app provides users with a prompt generator, inpainting tool, upscaling, sketching, and uploading photos, as well as access to a feed of art examples for inspiration. The app also allows users to generate images in 4K resolution, create multiple requests at once, and access an “advise” feature when they need prompt ideas. It is rated 4.5 out of 5 with 30 ratings and is available to download for free with in-app purchases.
