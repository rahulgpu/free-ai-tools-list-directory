About AI Photo – Stable Diffusion Text-to-Image

AI Photo is an artificial intelligence tool that uses Stable Diffusion to convert text prompts into images. Encased within a user-friendly interface, the tool is currently facilitated on iOS, macOS, and iPadOS. AI Photo operates fully offline and is designed with privacy in mind. It ensures none of your text prompts or resultant images are sent outside your device. For safety, it is programmed to avoid accidentally generating harmful or offensive content. The utility of the tool is augmented by its compatibility with custom CoreML models for research purposes. A special feature of this tool is its implementation of SafetyChecker files while using these custom models. AI Photo functions optimally with higher RAM availability. The developer, App ahead GmbH, positions this tool as a catalyst for creative individuals, promoting the exploration of prompt-based image generation. AI Photo is ideal for users who prioritize data privacy and the capacity to operate offline.
