About GEN2D

GEN2D is an AI tool designed to generate images. It uses artificial intelligence technologies to develop visuals that can be used in a variety of digital platforms. The images produced by GEN2D are hosted online, providing an easy way to share or integrate them into your digital applications or websites. To use GEN2D, you need to have an AI Pay browser extension, which allows users to pay for the individual usage of AI services. The model of AI Pay is centered around non-subscription based services, meaning users are only charged for the actual AI services used. The extension can be easily installed and used to facilitate acces to GEN2D's image generation services.
