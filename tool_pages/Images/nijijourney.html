About Nijijourney

Niji・journey is a state-of-the-art AI tool that creates custom anime illustrations. Developed by a collaboration between Spellbrush and Midjourney, it allows users to create a wide range of images from cute chibi characters to dynamic action scenes. The AI can be accessed through the official Discord channels, where users can start a job by typing a prompt into the /imagine command. The U1, U2, U3, and U4 buttons can be used to upscale the creations and the V1, V2, V3, and V4 buttons can be used to create variations. Niji・journey is available for researchers, commercial inquiries and studio licensing. Terms of Service and Privacy Policy can also be found on their website.
