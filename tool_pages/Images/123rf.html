About 123RF

The AI Image Generator is a service that allows users to create unique, customized images from text prompts for various purposes such as websites, social media, and marketing materials. The tool uses artificial intelligence algorithms to generate images that simulate human creativity and often include surreal or abstract elements.The process starts with users providing a text prompt that includes perspective, subject, action, keywords, lighting, and style. The AI then generates four preview images that closely match the provided prompt. Users can review these images and choose the ones they like.Once the desired images are selected, users have the option to download and license them for personal or commercial use. The downloaded images come with a license that grants legal coverage of $25,000. This allows users to sell or commercialize the generated images while adhering to copyright laws, intellectual property rights, and the terms and conditions set forth by the AI Image Generator License.While there is a limit to the number of images that can be generated per day, this ensures fair access and a good user experience for all users. To access the AI image generation service, users need to be logged in with a 123RF account, and complimentary quotas are allocated to 123RF Subscribers with an active subscription plan or those who have purchased credits within the past 30 days.Overall, the AI Image Generator offers a convenient and efficient way to turn text ideas into visually appealing and unique images, empowering users to unleash their creativity without the need for advanced design skills.
