About Airbrush

Airbrush is an intelligent tool that facilitates the creation of original images using AI technology. It is designed to generate high-quality images which can be used in various projects, such as advertisements, websites, and presentations. The main feature of Airbrush is the capability to create AI-powered images, including stock photos, NFTs, and art, in just a few seconds, aiming to save time and resources for the user. It supports multiple AI engines for image generation, including Stable Diffusion, Stable Diffusion XL, DALLE-2, and more. Users can customize their search using keywords or tags, and also save images for quick and easy access later. Airbrush offers different pricing options to cater to different needs. In addition to its web services, Airbrush also provides a mobile app for Android devices with plans for an Apple app under development. The service gives full usage rights to users, enabling them to commercialize the images they create with Airbrush. The images can be used for commercial projects, including illustrations for books, concept art for games, graphics for websites, and more.
