About Image App

Image App is a powerful tool that specializes in AI image generation and model training. With a wide range of cutting-edge models available, including Stable Diffusion SDXL 1.0, OpenAI DALL-E, Kandinsky 2.2, and more, users can dive into the world of AI-generated images.One of Image App's key features is its model training platform, which allows users to fine-tune their own models. This custom model training capability sets Image App apart from other tools in the market, providing users with the flexibility to create unique and personalized AI models.The tool offers a variety of subscription options, including a free plan with limited features, as well as starter and unlimited plans for more advanced users. The starter plan includes 500 image generations per month, access to all image generation models, and one model training per month. The unlimited plan, on the other hand, grants users unlimited image generations per month and five model trainings per month, providing unrestricted creative possibilities.Image App prides itself on its unique and popular AI models, offering a wide range of styles and genres, such as Studio Ghibli, Minimalist Design, Classic Animation, Realistic Vision V5, and many more. This diverse selection ensures that users can generate any image they can think of using the latest and exclusive AI models available on the platform.Overall, Image App is a comprehensive AI tool that empowers users to generate high-quality and unique images using state-of-the-art AI models, while also offering the flexibility for users to train their own models.
