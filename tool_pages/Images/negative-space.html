About Negative Space

Negative Space Beta is an AI tool designed to convert written prompts into generated images. The user interacts with the system by typing a short description of an image they would like to create, at which point the AI creates an image that reflects the details in the prompt. A feature of this utility is that it allows users to create up to two images without creating an account. Once the image is generated, the user can choose to have the image printed on a variety of products including stickers, canvases, or posters. These products can be added to the shopping cart and purchased directly within the application. The finished product is then delivered to the user. In terms of support, Negative Space Beta provides a help contact for users to approach if they encounter any problems or need assistance with their experience.
