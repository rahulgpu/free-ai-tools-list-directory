About Deflamel

Deflamel is an intelligent design solutions platform that simplifies the design process with creative artificial intelligence tools. It offers a range of features such as an automatic content generator, smart creative tools, AI recommendations and unique design. Using the platform, users can create stunning designs in a couple of clicks from text descriptions and get inspired with relevant design suggestions from machine learning prediction. It also offers features such as neural style transfer, font prediction, color suggestion and more to give visuals a unique look with striking styles and effects based on the user's mood. In addition, it provides banner designs for emails, targeted advertising and personal offers, as well as book covers, social media posts and more. With Deflamel, users can create their designs in minutes, with unlimited file revisions and only pay when they are happy.
