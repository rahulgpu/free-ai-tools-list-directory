About Kartiv

Kartiv is a tool that leverages artificial intelligence to automate visual content creation, particularly geared towards eCommerce businesses and marketing agencies. This platform provides various features to create engaging visuals like product photos and videos that can boost sales by showcasing products in an appealing manner. It also empowers users with the ability to generate 360 videos, white backgrounds and sales boosters. Users can also effortlessly produce AI product photos that can inspire confidence in potential customers.Kartiv offers multiple creative variations tailored for products through its AI-powered optimization feature. It allows for rapid iterations, helping users find what resonates most with their target audience. The tool also supports the creation of videos that provide a multidimensional view of products, adding another layer of engagement for customers. Kartiv provides specialized solutions tailored for various platforms, including Amazon, Shopify, and Etsy. Users can leverage Kartiv to drive growth through visual storytelling, as compelling product photos and video content can enhance customer trust, boost conversions, and directly influence sales gains. This approach can also lead to significant time and cost savings, allowing businesses to primarily focus on their growth and sales.
