About Nack AI

Nack is a mobile app available on both iOS and Android that utilizes artificial intelligence (AI) to enable users to chat with the AI model ChatGPT and generate images using text-to-image generation tool. The app provides an intuitive and easy-to-use interface for a seamless user experience that feels like favorite chat apps. Users can easily search through their past chats for key messages and pin important conversations at the top of their list. In addition, the app enables users to share messages with friends and family across any social platform and has a lot more features in store to explore. Nack offers various pricing plans suitable for different budgets, starting from a free plan that comes with limited AI capability and basic chat & image functionality to a power user package that includes full AI capability, unlimited chat history with 45,000 credits monthly. The app has been designed to be affordable while offering users a comprehensive experience with AI-generated images. Besides, Nack offers a blog section to keep users up-to-date with AI news, such as prompt engineering and the top AI tools for designers in 2023. Overall, Nack allows users to enjoy the power of AI on their mobile devices with a comprehensive and affordable user experience.
