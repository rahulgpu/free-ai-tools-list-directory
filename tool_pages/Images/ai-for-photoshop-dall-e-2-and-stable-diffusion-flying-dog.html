About AI for Photoshop – DALL-E 2 and Stable Diffusion (flying dog)

Flying Dog for Photoshop, featuring DALL-E 2 and Stable Diffusion, is an advanced AI-based tool designed to significantly accelerate and refine the creative process for designers. It offers a range of features to automate and enhance various aspects of digital editing. It enables automatic content generation to adjust image aspect ratios (Outpainting) and allows the addition of text layers using AI text generation from a dedicated server. Users can interactively control text style, add prompts for specific results, and integrate the text into existing images. The AI brush functionality facilitates generation of objects with background removal. Integration of 3D objects from a vast library to build a 3D scene for image generation is also possible. Users can replace image backgrounds, cater to AI creativity prompts, create new environments via prompts, and more. The tool also supports increased image resolution, conversion of AI-generated images to SVG, allows selection from a range of controlNet or T2I Coadapter types, and offers different AI connectors. It can be launched locally or on the cloud, making it accessible for Mac users. The system supports data privacy and allows customization options including the use of custom models. It offers a quick-start feature that requires no prior technical skills to use. It is deemed cost-efficient with prices based on usage time, while also providing an environment free of content filters and keyword restrictions.
