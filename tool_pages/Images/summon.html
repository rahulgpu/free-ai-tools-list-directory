About Summon

Summon.AI is a free and open-source design tool powered by DALL-E-2, an artificial intelligence system developed by OpenAI. It enables users to generate, edit, and create variants of images. With Summon.AI, users can easily discover what other users have generated and share their own creations with the world through the new save images feature. It is specifically designed for use with the Figma Community platform, where users can share their creations with other Figma users. The tool also includes version history, tooltips for images on the Discover tab, and improved save permissions and settings flow.
