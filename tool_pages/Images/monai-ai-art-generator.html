About MonAI – AI Art Generator

MonAI is an AI-powered art generator application. It enables users to create AI-generated art quickly and with precision. By merely inputting a text prompt and choosing the desired style like Anime, Photorealistic, Digital Painting, users can generate highly creative art. The generated art can be customized based on user preferences and can be used to create custom wallpapers for mobiles. The app can also transform photos into drawings to guide the AI for creating the art. Its advanced menu offers additional features including an easy Keyword selector to fine tune the images. The application also enables users to exclude certain elements from the images through negative prompts. In addition to these, MonAI provides access to an array of AI models with continuous additions to explore the newest and most advanced AI art technologies. The application promotes a community of AI artists, offering a chance to be featured on social media. Despite being feature-rich, the application maintains an intuitive user interface.
