About CapGo

CapGo is an AI-enabled market research tool designed to work seamlessly with spreadsheet software. The tool significantly simplifies market research-related tasks by leveraging the power of advanced AI algorithms to provide real-time data directly from Google. With just a single click, users can populate their spreadsheets with useful and timely data for their market research needs. This not only saves significant time and effort but also ensures that users always work with the most up-to-date and accurate information. CapGo mitigates the risk of working with outdated or irrelevant data, which is a common pain point in traditional market research. To ensure user privacy and data security, CapGo has firm terms of service and a robust privacy policy. Moreover, there are certain limited use requirements that users must adhere to. CapGo is a potent tool backed by Antler and is an ideal addition to the arsenals of professionals and businesses that heavily depend on precise and rapid market research.
