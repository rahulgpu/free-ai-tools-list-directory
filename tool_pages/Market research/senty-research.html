About Senty Research

Senty Research is a powerful AI-driven tool designed to assist startups and small businesses in gaining valuable insights that can contribute to their digital product growth. It facilitates smarter, faster and cost-effective customer research by generating comprehensive research reports which reveal what customers think, feel, and want. The process begins with the user providing the name of an App Store app they would like analyzed. Once confirmed to be feasible, a payment link is sent, and upon receipt of payment, a research report is generated and emailed to the user. The report covers both quantitative and qualitative metrics spanning over the previous year from the time of report generation. The tool offers a quick turnaround, often within 24 hours of receiving payment. Product features include detailed analysis of a single app, discounted pricing on multiple reports, and customized solutions to cater to enterprise needs. Developed by seasoned experts in customer research, user experience, and consulting, the Senty backend utilizes advanced AI and research frameworks to provide rigorous and insightful analysis. This tool is suitable for app development businesses aiming to identify areas of improvements in existing apps or gather data on competitors' weaknesses.
