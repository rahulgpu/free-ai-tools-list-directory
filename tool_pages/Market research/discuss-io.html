About Discuss.io

Discuss.io is a cutting-edge tool designed for scaling qualitative market research. This tool is designed with several solutions tailored for different needs such as In-House Teams, Pharma, and Agencies. At the heart of Discuss.io is a platform powered with Generative AI that facilitates Live Research and Async Research, enabling a more efficient collection and analysis of data. An emphasis has been placed on user experience, particularly for mobile users, ensuring broad accessibility. The platform places a strong focus on Data & Security, with measures in place to ensure data privacy and safeguard data from breach or misuse. As a complete solution, Discuss.io even comes with a Recruitment tool, enabling the sourcing of participants for research globally. Furthermore, Professional Services are offered as part of Discuss.io's comprehensive package, facilitating assistance with platform-related needs. The tool also includes resources such as Guides, Customer Stories, and Videos to equip users with the necessary knowledge and real-life examples of how the platform can be beneficial to their market research needs.
