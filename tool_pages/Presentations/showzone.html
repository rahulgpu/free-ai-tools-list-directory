About Showzone

Showzone is an AI-powered presentation delivery application. Its primary functions include live broadcasts, AI-generated summaries, and interactive Q&A sessions. The application is designed to allow presenters to engage and inspire their audience during live presentations. It includes features for sharing QR codes without leaving the presentation, inviting the audience to participate in Q&A and provide feedback. The AI-generated summary tool allows presenters to provide brief, impactful summaries of their content, assisting in audience retention after the conclusion of the presentation. Showzone also includes a system for tracking audience engagement and identifying leads. The app support various formats, and presenters can use their preferred tools, such as PowerPoint, to build their presentations. Once the presentation is compiled into a PDF, it can be easily imported into Showzone. The app offers flexibility and can be used when presenting via a web browser or a desktop. Another feature enables audience members to join the presentation using their own devices. Use cases of Showzone include data gathering at conferences, enhancing audience engagement in workshops, and assisting in training initiatives across businesses by digitizing and distributing in-house information. Showzone is designed to provide impactful delivery of presentations across various scenarios and platforms.
