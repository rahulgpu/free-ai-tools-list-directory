About Peach App

Peach App is a unique tool that uses AI to make slides for great pitches. The tool allows users to type in all the necessary details for their pitch and then generates unique and personalized slides with generative texts. Peach App is easy to use and requires JavaScript to run. The tool is designed for English language presentations and is part of the Self_ project made by Experiments, with design by M-Y-S. The product was launched in 2023 and has received positive feedback on Product Hunt. Peach App's main purpose is to help users create professional-looking slides for their pitches quickly and efficiently. By using AI, the tool generates personalized content that creates visually appealing slides. The generative texts help users save time and effort in manually creating the content for their slides. Peach App is also beneficial for those who may have limited design skills, as it provides a high-quality design template. Overall, Peach App is a beneficial tool for anyone looking to create engaging and visually appealing slides for their presentations.
