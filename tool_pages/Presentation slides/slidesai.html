About SlidesAI

SlidesAI is an AI-powered tool designed to transform text into presentations instantly. This software allows users to automatically detail and structure any given body of text into engaging slideshow presentations. It streamlines the process of slide creation, making it efficient and less tedious. SlidesAI can be easily integrated into Google Slides for a smoother workflow. Besides creating presentations from text input, users can also customize the appearance of their slides by choosing from pre-designed color and font presets. AI capabilities extend to image search and suggestions, automatic citation search for slide content, paraphrasing sentence structures, and icon customization. The tool also boasts translation and support for over 100 languages and planning to offer video export and document upload features. SlidesAI could be an invaluable resource for individuals and teams seeking to economize time on manual slides creation, focusing more on the details and quality of presentations.
