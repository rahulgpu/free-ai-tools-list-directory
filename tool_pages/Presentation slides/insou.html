About Insou

Insou.ai is a powerful AI tool that helps users create captivating and impactful presentations. It streamlines the workflow by providing features such as an AI Copilot, Markdown support, content ownership, and a focus on narrative before design. The tool allows users to write presentations like a document, enabling a seamless storytelling experience.The AI Copilot feature utilizes advanced AI to provide real-time guidance and enhancement suggestions for creating powerful and resonating presentations. It offers an intuitive interface that allows users to craft presentations effortlessly, without the need for Markdown knowledge or reliance on rigid templates and complex editing.Insou.ai empowers workflow with versatile import/export options, integrated media, intuitive editing, and Notion-style ease. It also provides Notion-grade file organization and facilitates content sharing to enrich the community.The tool offers different pricing plans to cater to users' needs. The free plan includes 300 AI credits at sign-up, unlimited insous (slides), PDF and Markdown export with Insou branding, and basic analytics. The Plus plan, available at $8 per user per month, provides additional benefits such as 500 AI credits each month and the ability to remove the "Made with Insou" badge. The Pro plan, priced at $14 per user per month, offers unlimited AI creation, unlimited insous, removal of the "Made with Insou" badge, PDF and Markdown export, and advanced analytics.Users have praised Insou.ai for its AI-powered slide generation, ease of creating stunning slides, and time-saving capabilities.
