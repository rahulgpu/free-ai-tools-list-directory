About Glimmer

Glimmer is an innovative tool that harnesses the power of AI to create and present visually compelling presentations. It utilizes advanced AI technologies, specifically GPT-3 and DALLE 2, to generate presentations from user text and voice commands. A key feature includes simplicity, where users can effortlessly describe their idea with a sentence or more for Glimmer to generate a ready-made presentation. Users have the freedom to make modifications with AI assistance, whether that involves manual text entry or voice commands. Once finalized, presentations can be shared and exported easily, supporting file types such as PPTX or PDF. Beyond individual use, Glimmer also promotes collaboration, making teamwork smoother and more productive for presentation creation. It also introduces a fresh concept of presentation with Mixed Reality. The platform offers public API for developers to incorporate the capabilities of Glimmer into their products. Moreover, it provides deep analytics for a thorough examination of each presentation view, thereby providing useful insight. It also allows for increased audience engagement during the presentation process. Glimmer promotes the experience of users being the director of their own scenario.
