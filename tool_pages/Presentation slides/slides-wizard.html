About Slides Wizard

Slides Wizard is an AI-powered tool designed to create PowerPoint presentations and pitch decks swiftly and efficiently, simplifying the process markedly. By leveraging AI capabilities, specifically the GPT model, Slides Wizard generates slide content based on text provided by the user. In addition to generating text-based content, Slides Wizard also incorporates relevance-based images into the slides, with images pulled from a photo search engine, thereby enhancing their visual appeal. Users can create presentations from their own notes by simply pasting their outline into the tool, and the AI crafts a presentation according to this outline. The tool is compatible with both PowerPoint and Google Slides, providing flexibility and convenience for users. Not only does Slides Wizard allow for creation and online viewing of presentations, but it also offers the capabilities to share them with others, export to PowerPoint and Google Slides, and download them as a PDF. This tool can be used for creating slides on any topic. Slides Wizard offers both free and pay-per-use options, with the pay-per-use option charging on a per presentation basis, though charges only apply for downloads.
