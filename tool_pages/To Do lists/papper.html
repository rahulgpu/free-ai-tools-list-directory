About Papper

Papper is an innovative application available on iOS that enhances task management by bridging handwritten and digital tasks. Its primary functions comprise of converting handwritten notes into digital to-do lists, creating and managing task lists, and synchronizing task progression with Apple Reminders. Papper's distinct feature is its ability to transform the digital tasks into aesthetically pleasing, distraction-free printouts, which can be utilized to manually track task progression.The application uses advanced Optical Character Recognition (OCR) and Generative Pretrained Transformer (GPT) technologies to digitize users' handwritten tasks. It also allows instant syncing of task progression by scanning the handwritten marks from the printable checklists. Papper's design is task-focused, promoting higher concentration levels. The application also supports managing repetitive tasks through its smart adjustment feature, and a dark theme option is available.In addition, Papper provides access to multiple pre-made checklists associated with various subjects like wellness, sports, health, psychology, and productivity. Users can customize these checklists according to their preferences.Although Papper is free to use, more features and content can be unlocked through optional in-app purchases. The application is compatible with all iOS devices running iOS 16 and above.
