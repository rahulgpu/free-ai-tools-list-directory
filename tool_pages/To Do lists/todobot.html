About Todobot

TodoBot is an AI-powered to-do list app that aims to help users organise and manage their daily tasks more effectively. Its intelligent assistant utilises a research-backed approach to break complex tasks into smaller, more manageable steps, making it easier for users to stay focused and productive. The app's standout feature is its personalised coaching, which can spot when users are struggling to complete a task and suggest options to help them get it done. This virtual coach can provide users with step-by-step guidance and useful reminders to improve their efficiency and keep them on track. In addition to its AI capabilities, TodoBot offers a user-friendly interface that makes it easy to create, view, and manage your tasks. Users can also customise their settings to receive alerts and notifications at specific intervals to ensure they don't miss any important deadlines. Overall, TodoBot is an insightful and precise tool for individuals looking for a personal assistant to help them navigate their daily tasks with greater ease and efficiency.
