About Video Repair by Onerecovery

ONERECOVERY is an AI-driven tool designed to repair corrupted, damaged or blurry videos from various formats, such as MP4, MOV, M2TS, MKV, and 3GP, etc. The tool works by recognizing corrupted areas in the video footage using advanced AI tech and determines the best repair methods on a case-by-case basis without affecting the intact parts. It caters to videos damaged due to improper file transfer, recording or editing errors, OS crashes, viruses, and malware attacks. It can restore clarity to blurry footage, fix jittery motions, choppy or stuck playback, and recover missing audio among other repair capabilities. ONERECOVERY is optimized for different devices, and can fix corrupted videos from a computer, phone, SD card, USB flash drive, HDD, SDD, CF card, and leading camera brands such as Canon, Nikon, Sony, GoPro, DJI drones, and Panasonic. It allows you to preview the repaired videos before saving them, and is designed to work on both the Windows and Mac platform.
