About Trusli

Trusli's ContractPro with AI is an AI-powered solution designed to streamline contract drafting and review processes for in-house legal teams. The system facilitates efficient self-service by providing automated recommendations and pre-approved fallbacks, enabling businesses to negotiate effectively with counter parties without the constant need for legal team consultations. Users can effortlessly review 3rd-party papers as the system is capable of making markups based on an established set of playbook rules. ContractPro with AI significantly reduces contract cycle times by leveraging state-of-the-art AI to transform legal reviews. Furthermore, it preserves a transparent audit trail of every action taken during the process, enhancing legal operations security. Its end-to-end workflow includes an e-signature feature, which monitors all contract reviews and approvals on-platform, thereby ensuring a seamless signature process. This AI tool comes with seamless integration capabilities with major contract lifecycle management solutions, ERPs, Docusign, and existing tools. Trusli places a high emphasis on security, working with top-tier security experts and auditors to secure users' data, and maintains a SOC2 Type II compliance.
