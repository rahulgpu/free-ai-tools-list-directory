About Legitt AI

Legitt AI offers an assortment of AI-powered Contract Lifecycle Management (CLM) solutions aimed at simplifying business operations and automating agreement processes. This AI-powered platform hosts a range of applications built to streamline various aspects of contract management. The AI Contract Generator utilizes AI algorithms to expedite the process of creating legally-binding contracts. It analyzes data, generates customized contract templates and ensures compliance with legal standards. Legitt AI also features the AI Contract Reviewer, a tool that employs advanced algorithms to assess legal contracts for inconsistencies, errors, and potential risks, providing actionable insights and amendment recommendations. An additional feature, the AI Repo Analyzer, is designed to analyze and manage large repositories of contracts, providing insights and identify potential risks at scale, ideal for enterprise clients. Finally, AI Contract Tracking automates tracking and monitoring of contracts by providing real-time updates on contract status, milestones and deadlines. Furthermore, Legitt AI offers Legitt Lite, an end-to-end platform suitable for startups, freelancers, and small to medium-sized businesses (SMBs), to enhance productivity and contract management with minimal human intervention.
