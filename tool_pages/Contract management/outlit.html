About Outlit

Outlit is an AI-powered tool designed for Contract Intelligence. This tool aims to streamline and automate manual and tedious tasks in contract management which helps companies reduce workloads and minimize overhead costs. One of the significant features of Outlit is its ability to create contract knowledge bases. It assists users in consolidating all contracts into a single, searchable knowledge base that can provide insights, track historical contract comparisons, and discover contract trends. Furthermore, Outlit offers a risk analysis feature that allows users to understand all the risks in every contract as it has the capability to rank and flag all the legal and business risks. Another distinctive feature of Outlit is its negotiation capability. It can identify every potential negotiation opportunity within contracts, which could lead to significant savings. Outlit is trusted by startups and enterprise companies for its comprehensive approach in making contract management more efficient and less error-prone.
