About Attune health

The Attune Health Mobile App is an AI-powered health and wellness tool that enables contactless measurement of vital signs such as blood pressure, oxygen saturation, HRV, stress levels, and even Hemoglobin. The app uses video-based technology that analyses images taken by a smartphone camera to provide real-time measurements, and there is no need for any wearable devices. The tool is easy to use and provides gender-specific results for accuracy. It also guarantees the privacy of users' data and ensures that no one else has access to it. There is a wellness score feature that allows users to easily track their wellness markers. Users can download the app for free and can use it to measure biomarkers for their entire family, thus providing value for money. Additionally, the tool can be used by corporations to promote wellness among employees, leading to increased productivity and job satisfaction. Attune Health has a website where users can access resources such as a help center, blog, and roadmap. Overall, the Attune Health Mobile App is an innovative AI tool that enhances users' health and wellness by providing easy and affordable access to vital signs measurements.
