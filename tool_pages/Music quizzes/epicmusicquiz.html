About EpicMusicQuiz

EpicMusicQuiz is an online tool that allows users to create their own music video quizzes. This tool is designed with an interface that makes it easy to tailor a personalized quiz experience for users, and their friends, revolving around music videos. To make use of EpicMusicQuiz, your browser needs to have JavaScript enabled. Additionally, to ensure proper functionality and user experience, a screen width of at least 800px is needed. It's important to note that this tool relies heavily on JavaScript for many of its features and without it, overall performance may be impacted. As such, enabling JavaScript is a prerequisite for using EpicMusicQuiz.
