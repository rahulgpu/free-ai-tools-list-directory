About DOO

DOO is an AI tool designed to aid modern workplaces by providing access to AI-driven digital coaches. These coaches, referred to as 'mates', cover a wide variety of professions and areas of expertise, offering users the opportunity to leverage AI intelligence for seamless collaboration and productivity enhancement. Diverse in nature, these AI mates include talent acquisition specialists, business development coaches, UX researchers, marketing officers, and even fashion stylists. Each AI mate is meticulously trained in their respective profession, and they provide on-demand, personalized guidance to assist in various tasks within that particular domain. For instance, an AI mate who is a language learning coach would offer fluency training, vocabulary building and interactive learning. Another AI mate trained as a DevOps coach can help with continuous integration and deployment strategies, provide knowledge on automation tools and guide users through associated processes. The idea is to greatly improve the efficiency of many daily operations in different professions by bringing the expertise of these AI mates into workflows. Whether it's assistance with skewed market analysis, tailored academic planning, process optimization or even exec coaching, DOO aims to bring a meaningful impact to businesses across the industry spectrum through a blend of AI and human expertise.
