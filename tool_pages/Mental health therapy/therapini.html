About therapini

therapini is an AI-powered mental health support tool aimed at providing personal therapy services to users facing emotional or mental challenges like anxiety, depression, or trauma. This innovative tool is designed to deliver therapy through one-on-one text or voice conversations, breaking down the accessibility barriers often associated with traditional therapy. The service is accessible 24/7, offering comfort and guidance at any time, irrespective of the place. Therapini focuses on enabling users to begin their mental wellness journey by offering an initial free therapy session duration. Despite its therapeutic benefits, it's important to note that therapini doesn't diagnose or treat specific mental health conditions and should not replace professional medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. In emergency situations, users are advised to consult a licensed mental health professional or healthcare provider. Keeping in view the user's privacy, therapini adheres strictly to data protection and privacy laws, striving to provide a secure user experience.
