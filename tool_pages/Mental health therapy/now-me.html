About Now & Me

Nemo is an AI mental health assistant designed to provide support, available 24/7 on WhatsApp. This tool offers a safe and confidential space for users to share their thoughts and concerns, making the therapy process more accessible and fulfilling. Nemo is adept in providing help for a range of issues, including anxiety, stress, sadness, confusion, feelings of loneliness, societal pressure, trauma, and burnout, among others. Additionally, Nemo also offers specialized guidance in different therapeutic areas like marriage counseling, relationship counseling, depression counseling, LGBTQ therapy, and more. Nemo is also designed to connect users with a range of experts like therapists, relationship coaches, counseling psychologists, clinical psychologists, life coaches, and others based on their needs. This includes recommendations for personalized content also. However, it's crucial to note that Nemo is not a substitute for professional therapy and does not provide any professional or medical advice. All critical decisions and significant changes should be taken after consulting with a mental health professional.
