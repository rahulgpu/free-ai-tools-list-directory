About Wysa

Wysa is an AI-powered tool designed to offer mental health support. This tool provides an anonymous, secure space for individuals to express their negative thoughts and emotions. Wysa is engineered to assist users to cope with anxiety-related concerns and other emotional experiences linked to life scenarios such as pandemic lockdowns. It offers its services free of charge, ensuring that mental health services are accessible for all. Wysa caters not only to individuals but also serves as a workplace solution. Targeting employee mental health, it provides a platform that companies can utilize to ensure a supportive and mentally healthy workplace environment. Wysa offers conversational AI support as the primary level of care, in conjunction with human coaching for further assistance. Its services are intended to help users feel supported and are continuously refined based on clinical evidence, case studies and reports. The tool offers various services, like adult talking therapies and services dedicated to children and young people. Moreover, Wysa's conversational AI is capable of guiding users through structured programs and on-demand self-care exercises. It also provides the option of working with a professional for one-on-one sessions and delivering customised escalation pathways, providing a broad spectrum of mental health support.
