About Yosoku

Yosoku is a decision intelligence software, specializing in AI solutions for ecommerce. It employs advanced AI techniques to automate and optimize various aspects of online businesses, making it a capable tool for retailers of various sizes. The platform offers features such as inventory management, demand and cash flow forecasting, and analytics and insights on products and customers. Specifically, Yosoku leverages machine learning algorithms to create unique sku-specific forecasting models based on historical sales data to predict future sales and demand. Additionally, Yosoku provides a Shopify app that aids in inventory management, from determining the optimal volume of inventory to order, to securing the best deals from suppliers. The solution also takes care of purchase order automation, stock allocation and discount maximization. Key benefits highlighted are reduced holding costs, improved cash flow, increased customer retention and increased profit margins. Moreover, the AI tool also offers resilience against supply chain vulnerabilities and the capacity for brands to foresee peak periods for accurate ordering.
