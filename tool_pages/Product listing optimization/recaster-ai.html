About Recaster AI

Recaster AI is an Artificial Intelligence powered tool designed primarily for e-commerce platforms. Its main function is to transform product images into high-quality, SEO-friendly content. Users can import their product images in various formats such as .PNG, .JPEG, .JPG, .WEBP and the tool uses models offered by Open AI custom vision multi modals and Google Gemini to generate SEO optimized content. This content includes product titles, short SEO descriptions, detailed descriptions, keywords and meta tags, adding significant value to the platform's SEO efforts. One of Recaster AI's key features is its ability for bulk product imports from the Shopify platform. This is done by simply importing a CSV file from Shopify to Recaster AI, reducing manual effort and saving time. Future developments for this tool include the integration of Shopify, WordPress, and Amazon plugins. Currently, Recaster AI primarily supports Shopify and custom platforms, but plans to extend live support for WordPress, Alibaba, and Amazon platforms in the near future. Ideal for those running an e-commerce platform who do not have the resources, time or expertise to manage SEO and product content, Recaster AI is a cost-effective, efficient alternative to traditional content creation and SEO management.
