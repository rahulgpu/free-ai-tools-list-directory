About Facia AI

Facia is an advanced tool that utilizes facial recognition technology and 3D liveness detection capabilities to prevent fraud and spoofing attacks. By ensuring fast and accurate face matching and verification, it represents an essential addition to secure identity verification systems. Facia not only offers the world's fastest 3D Liveness Detection Platform, but it also accommodates a wide range of features such as face matching, face search, age verification and iris recognition. A distinguishing quality of Facia's face recognition technology is its ability to onboard real clients through advanced facial recognition services while assuring a quick and precise match with stored profiles. Facia's solutions cater to a variety of industries including retail, government, healthcare, and education, to name a few. The tool offers a customizable solution dovetailed to the specific requirements of each sector. Facia's applications are broad, as it can be used for anything from access control and employee productivity monitoring to attendance management, and from security bolstering via criminal identification to automation of immigration processes.
