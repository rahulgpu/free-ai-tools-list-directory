About Miraa

Miraa is an AI-powered tool that offers a range of features designed to enhance the way users engage with media files. One of its primary functionalities involves generating subtitles from media using AI technology. This implies that users can transcribe their audio or video content into text form, which extends its accessibility and usability. A central feature that distinguishes Miraa from similar tools in the market is its 'Seamless Echoing.' Here, it appears that the users have the freedom to follow the indicated subtitles and echo back at their convenience. This could be a beneficial feature for language learners or individuals who wish to practice their speech and pronunciation skills. Miraa also features an interactive AI explanation, opening up a communicative interface where users can raise queries and find resolutions in real-time. Another notable feature of Miraa is its realtime translation utility. This function allows users to translate the transcribed subtitles into their preferred language, making it an exciting tool for multi-language learners or those watching foreign media. It is made available on multiple platforms, including the Apple Store and Google Play Store.
