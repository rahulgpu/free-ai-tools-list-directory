About Zensors

Zensors is an AI platform that uses visual recognition technology to provide real world understanding and automation for businesses. The tool uses AI agents to harness spatial intelligence for customer-centric organizations, helping to boost revenue, customer satisfaction, and productivity. The Zensors solution includes the capability to monitor activities, manage utilization, and perform object counting. The Zensors platform supports multiple sectors including aviation, retail, and commercial real estate, among others. From streamlining operations to enhancing user experiences, Zensors provides its users with a toolbox to more effectively understand and manage their spaces. This is achieved by using AI technology to gather spatial data from existing hardware devices, forming meaningful insights about the user's environment. Moreover, Zensors ensures that data gathered respects visitor anonymity, meaning customers can interact with spaces freely without worrying about personal data being collected. The tool itself is flexible, growing in capability to match the specific needs and developments of an organization. The Zensors platform is also designed to facilitate real-time decision making, allowing teams to act instantly instead of deliberating over what to do next.
