About Reverso

Reverso is a free online translation service that specializes in translating texts between English and multiple languages including French, Spanish, Italian, German, Russian, Portuguese, Hebrew, Japanese, Arabic, Dutch, Polish, Romanian, and Turkish. The tool offers various features to enhance translation accuracy and efficiency. One of the key features is document translation, which allows users to instantly translate documents while preserving the original layout. Reverso supports translation for various file formats such as Word, PDF, PowerPoint, and Excel in over 25 languages.In addition to translation, Reverso provides integrated dictionaries to help fine-tune translations, offering synonyms and contextual examples. The tool also includes a spell-checker powered by artificial intelligence (AI) to revise original texts, resulting in higher-quality translations.Reverso incorporates the latest in neural machine translation (NMT) technology, allowing for accurate and natural-sounding translations. The NMT technology has been adopted by major companies and translation agencies.Furthermore, Reverso assists with language learning and communication by providing verb conjugation in multiple languages, including French, Spanish, German, Arabic, and Japanese.Reverso is available as a free app for various platforms such as Mac, Windows, iOS, and Android, enabling users to translate text from any application with one click. The tool offers millions of real-life examples and allows users to bookmark their favorite translations. Reverso Premium, a paid option, offers additional features such as translating longer texts and ad-free user experience. Overall, Reverso is a comprehensive translation tool that aims to help individuals and organizations communicate more effectively and accurately in multiple languages.
