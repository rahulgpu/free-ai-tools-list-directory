About Nara

Nara is an AI-powered digital sales associate designed to enhance customer engagement and boost sales for small and medium-sized online stores. The tool focuses on automating sales processes through various chat channels, including CRMs, Instagram DMs, and email. Nara seamlessly integrates into these channels, providing human-level support and personalized shopping advice, ultimately aiming to recreate the comforting experience of interacting with a helpful sales associate found in physical stores. The functionality of Nara revolves around three key aspects: 1. **Automated Actions:** Nara streamlines order tracking, cancellations, refunds, and other workflows, saving time for businesses by handling these processes from end to end. 2. **Omnichannel Human-level Support:** Nara ensures a consistent support experience across different chat channels, offering 24/7 support without the limitations of traditional text chatbots. 3. **Human Touch Experience:** Nara distinguishes itself by delivering a more personalized and human-like interaction compared to conventional chatbots, aiming to provide users with a more engaging and comfortable shopping experience. Nara offers pricing plans catering to various business needs, allowing users to choose from Starter, Pro, Unlimited, and Enterprise plans, with options for monthly or yearly subscriptions. Additionally, the tool provides an API for real-time updates and the ability to customize and tailor the assistant to specific business requirements. Nara's unique selling proposition lies in its ability to automate sales processes while maintaining a human touch, contributing to increased revenue and customer satisfaction for online businesses.
