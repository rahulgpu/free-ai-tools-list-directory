About RapportAI

EvaBot Sales IQ is an AI-based tool designed to enhance the effectiveness of sales processes. It provides accuracy and speed to augment high-velocity outreach efforts. One of EvaBot's key features is its proactive sales triggers, which increase the effectiveness of outreach by providing accurate and relevant insights about each account. These 'triggers' eliminate hours of research and skimming through data as they provide deep insights from various sources, like 10-Ks and earnings calls. Another feature is instant rapport building, where EvaBot enables sales professionals to understand and tap into prospective customers' unique passions and interests, moving beyond the usual LinkedIn data. The tool also has the function of natural impact messaging, allowing more meetings to be secured through the seamless transformation of insights into compelling messages. Finally, with the omnichannel outreach hub, EvaBot offers versatility in sales channels to enhance engagement, enabling the sales professional to conduct relationship-building beyond email-only interaction. It infuses calls and LinkedIn with compelling icebreakers and can integrate with power dialers for precise pitching. EvaBot Sales IQ is intended for any organization looking to enhance its sales engagements and achieve insightful customer interactions.
