About Chat360

Chat360 is a conversational AI chatbot builder designed to enhance customer engagement and conversion. Its omnichannel platform serves as a comprehensive solution for businesses looking to scale through conversational sales, marketing, and customer support. Chat360 boasts compatibility with numerous popular platforms, including WhatsApp, Facebook Messenger, Instagram, and websites, allowing businesses to reach customers through their preferred channels. Additionally, the service offers various integrations to expand its capabilities, such as Google Sheets, Shopify, Stripe, Gmail, Google Dialogflow, LeadSquared, Setu and Razorpay. It is engineered to cater to all types of industries and is trusted by a diverse range of global brand leaders. It's no-code interface enables the creation of bots that can generate leads, provide customer support and engagement, and facilitate business growth through conversational marketing. Further, the platform facilitates the automation of Instagram DMs and Facebook Messenger to leverage widespread user engagement. Chat360 also prioritizes lead generation by enhancing visitor to lead ratio on websites and offers features for WhatsApp Business API to generate increased leads, orders, and customer satisfaction. Lastly, through Chat360's resource center, users gain access to blogs and guides as well as potential partnership options.
