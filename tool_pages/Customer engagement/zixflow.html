About Zixflow

Zixflow is a versatile AI-driven platform designed to improve customer management, engagement, and retention. This all-in-one workspace platform enables users to customize their own Customer Relationship Management (CRM), workflow automation, and customer engagement processes through various channels such as email, SMS, or WhatsApp. Features within Zixflow include an omnichannel automation for outreach, lead capturing with smart forms, and an email finder tool for discovering and verifying prospect emails. Furthermore, Zixflow provides integrations with numerous applications. One key feature of the tool is the Zixflow AI which is a GPT-powered AI engine, capable of generating email content or being added to your CRM as a custom field. The platform makes it easy to automate communications, setup cadences, and build workflows for repetitive tasks, allowing teams to focus more on high-value activities. Zixflow is designed to benefit a variety of teams like sales, marketing, and support across various industries.
