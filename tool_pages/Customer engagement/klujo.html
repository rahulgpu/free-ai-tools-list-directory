About Klujo

Klujo AI is a gamified content platform designed specifically for marketers and marketing agencies. Its aim is to boost audience engagement and conversion rates through word-based games powered by AI. The platform allows marketers to create interactive and immersive storytelling content without needing to code. With Klujo AI, marketers can capture and prolong audience attention, build deeper connections with their target audience through enriched storytelling, transform visitors into high-quality leads, improve sales numbers by increasing conversion rates, and grow subscriptions and downloads. The platform offers fully customizable solutions for various industries, including tourism, consumer tech, recruitment marketing, travel and hospitality, sports and entertainment, and retail marketing. Klujo AI also provides a rewards solution that enables marketers to reward their audience for interacting with their experiences and sharing their data. By redeeming game points, consumers can enjoy a variety of rewards. The platform has received positive feedback from partners who have experienced improved lead generation and engagement using Klujo's gamification marketing system. Currently in pre-launch, Klujo AI offers a free and exclusive game to help train their AI. Marketers can claim their pre-launch pass to access the platform and enhance their lead generation strategies. The privacy and confidentiality of user information are prioritized, and users have the option to opt out of communications about products, services, and events.
