About Marketrix

Marketrix is an AI tool designed to enhance customer engagement by bringing websites to life with AI Sales Avatars. The tool uses AI to facilitate real-time, personalized interactions similar to human-like experiences. Marketrix's AI technology is used to improve pre-sales support, customer service, and lead generation. Over time, the system learns from each interaction and continually enhances its service. Besides, Marketrix features co-browsing sessions where the AI Avatars or Human Agents can surf the web simultaneously with the website visitors. Spot meetings with screen sharing are also supported, offering instantaneous connectivity with website visitors. Additionally, Marketrix provides beneficial insights into visitor behavior through its Traffic Analytics, enabling users to optimize visitor engagement and conversions. This tool replaces conventional chatbots with engaging AI avatars, creating a more human-like online service experience. To streamline the process, Marketrix can be seamlessly integrated into websites with a few lines of code.
