About Humley

Humley is a conversational AI tool that uses its platform, named 'Studio', to allow users to easily build and launch their own conversational AI assistants. Studio functions as a no-code platform, meaning users with little or no programming experience can also use it to craft self-serve experiences and manage the AI's outputs. This tool is designed to revolutionize customer experiences and enhance operational efficiencies. With Humley, users can create AI assistants that handle everyday queries, serve customers through intuitive flows, and connect customers to systems and knowledge with ready-to-use integrations. Besides conversation, Humley's Studio delivers other key benefits including refined control, insightful analytics, and streamlined access to AI services. The platform also offers features such as a multichannel support, data retention settings, and an option to capture feedback. Humley users have the ability to build their first AI assistant in under an hour, delivering time savings and improved efficiency to internal teams. Moreover, the Humley Studio platform allows users to harness latest AI models securely, including OpenAI LLM, and deploy conversational assistants swiftly.
