About Intellecta

Intellecta is an AI-powered tool designed to enhance customer experience at all stages of the customer journey. It employs generative pre-trained transformer models for instant response to customer inquiries across various platforms such as chat, social media, and email in any language, and is available 24/7. Trained on company-specific data, Intellecta can handle a variety of tasks from product inquiries to presales to returns and exchanges. The tool also automates social media engagement by liking and replying to comments, direct messages, and moderates by flagging and removing negative comments. Moreover, real-time insights can be extracted from social networks, email, and chat interactions to understand customer engagement and identify trends early. Customizable features allow the adoption of a companys individual brand voice and style. It keeps its knowledge continually updated by linking with a company's website, learning details about policies, products, FAQs, and more. It allows manual input for precise knowledge integration and customer support enhancement. The tool provides an AI copilot for ecommerce platforms and supports seamless integration with various e-commerce applications.
