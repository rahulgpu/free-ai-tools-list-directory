About Haptik

Haptik is an AI-powered Conversational CRM that helps enterprises manage the customer lifecycle. Utilizing Generative AI, Haptik provides a suite of tools to streamline customer support, enhance marketing efforts, and optimize sales conversions. Included in Haptik's offering is the u0022Contaktu0022 suite, which offers a unified platform for cross-channel customer conversations. Haptik's platform features a range of tools including an AI-powered web Q&A, AI-powered sales assistant, smart agent chat with an AI co-pilot, and intelligent analytics tracking user behavior. Offering 24/7 support, Haptik can provide dynamic responses to customer queries with CRM & Web API integration. The platform also supports contextual inbox for support agents, equipped with AI chat prompts, pre-loaded customer details, and intent detection for efficient query resolution. With Haptik's u0022Interaktu0022 solution, enterprises can execute extensive campaigns on WhatsApp to drive user engagement and marketing ROI. Lastly, the intelligent analytics feature gives businesses complete visibility of bot and agent performance to identify and address gaps in support.
