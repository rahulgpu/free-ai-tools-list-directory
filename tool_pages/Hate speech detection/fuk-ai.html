About Fuk.ai

Fuk.ai is an AI-powered tool designed to detect hate speech and profanity in content. It utilizes several Transformer-based neural network architectures, employing advanced natural language processing techniques. The primary intention of this tool is to maintain online content decorum by keeping hate speech, bigotry, and profanity at bay. Fuk.ai is particularly useful for user-generated content and can be integrated into apps or websites. It can be employed in a wide range of areas such as text analysis, live chats, comments moderation, and research. The tool also offers an API service allowing users to directly call in its hate speech and profanity detection functionality inside their applications. This API returns probabilities and indices of detected hate speech within the given text. Fuk.ai can be a valuable asset for content developers and moderators striving for safer, more inclusive online spaces.
