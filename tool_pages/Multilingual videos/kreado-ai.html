About Kreado AI

KreadoAI is an AI video creation and model creation platform that allows users to create multilingual oral videos with real or virtual characters. The platform offers AI empowerment to content creators and removes the need for studio photography or real-life models. Users can simply input text or keywords to generate custom AI-generated avatars and oral videos in multiple languages. The platform is suitable for various e-commerce business scenarios, including wigs, clothing, beauty makeup, and glasses.The KreadoAI platform is easy to use and offers a free service for creating multilingual oral videos and custom avatars. The system generates high-quality images and videos that perfectly integrate with the user's product. In addition, the platform also offers AI-generated marketing copy to help users promote their products effectively.Overall, KreadoAI is an innovative AI tool that helps content creators and businesses create high-quality oral videos, custom avatars, and AI-generated marketing copy.
