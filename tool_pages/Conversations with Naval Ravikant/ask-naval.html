About Ask Naval

Ask Naval (AI Bot) is a fan-made AI chatbot that allows users to interact with a virtual representation of Naval Ravikant, a prominent entrepreneur, investor, and author. The tool employs a ChatGPT-like model and provides its users with Naval's insights on a variety of topics such as personal development, philosophy, and investing. It's important to note that the bot is not officially associated or affiliated with the real Naval Ravikant. This AI tool utilises a considerable amount of resources for training, including Ravikant's tweets, interviews, and the book 'The Almanack of Naval Ravikant' written by Eric Jorgenson. Created as an experiment by fans of Naval, the bot strives to share Ravikant's valuable insights and philosophy with users who are eager to learn more from his life experiences and worldview without needing to tackle an ocean of information.
