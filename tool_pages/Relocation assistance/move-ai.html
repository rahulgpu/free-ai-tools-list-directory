About Move AI

Move AI provides a user-friendly medium to capture and create 3D animations. Its proprietary technology uses several advanced fields, such as artificial intelligence, computer vision, biomechanics, and physics, to convert 2D videos into 3D motion data. Aside from facilitating high-quality motion data capture, Move AI is also built on a markerless motion capture technique, eliminating the need for markers or suits. It offers single and multi-camera options for motion capture, capable of using standard cameras and phones. The technology allows for the simultaneous capture of up to 8 individuals in any environment, and provides complete body motion capture, including hand and finger tracking. Its high scalability makes it a suitable tool for both individual 3D animators and large production studios. The captured data can be easily exported to a user's preferred 3D software in FBX and USD formats. With a feature that lets you capture in large volumes, its Move One product supports up to 5m x 5m spaces, while Move Multi-Cam can cover up to 20m x 20m volume spaces.
