About Otterly

Otterly.AI is a tool dedicated to monitoring and ranking the visibility of your brand, content, competitors, and keywords across AI-powered search experiences. Besides general visibility, Otterly.AI has a specific focus on popular AI interfaces including ChatGPT, Google Bard/SGE, and Microsoft's Bing Copilot. This platform is particularly adept at monitoring your brand's presence and content visibility on updates such as GPT-4 from OpenAI, widely used to power ChatGPT and Bing Copilot. It also allows for visibility checks on Google's Gemini Pro, the underlying force behind Google Bard and Google SGE. As AI-powered searches surge in popularity and traditional platforms adopt conversational interfaces, Otterly.AI provides a means to adapt and optimize visibility within this transforming environment. The tool aims to be as beneficial to brand visibility across AI platforms as SEMrush or Ahrefs are for keywords, substituting traditional keywords with 'prompts' catering for AI interfaces. Otterly.AI advocates for measurable growth for your brand's visibility with an emphasis on modern Language Learning Models (LLMs).
