About CallBud

CallBud is an AI-driven application that primarily focuses on handling and making appointment calls for its users. It's designed to facilitate calls for a broad range of appointments including, but not limited to, hairdressers, restaurant reservations, or doctor's appointments. By using CallBud, users are relieved from the task of having to make such calls personally. Instead, the AI system incorporated within the app takes over the task. The core functionality of this tool revolves around convenience and time-saving. It aims to provide a seamless and hassle-free experience for its users who wish to delegate their appointment calls to an automated system. To get started, users only need to download CallBud on their Apple devices from the AppStore. It also has a Privacy Policy and Terms of Service to protect user information and clarify user rights and responsibilities, indicating its commitment to user safety and satisfaction.
