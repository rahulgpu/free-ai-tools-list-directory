About Bricksoft

Bricksoft is an AI-based tool designed to help businesses digitize their offline offerings, providing a bridge between traditional and digital commerce models. The main focus of Bricksoft is to leverage the power of artificial intelligence in rendering offline business operations into an online format. It may have applications across a wide variety of sectors, supporting firms as they make the transition towards more integrated, automated, and online-centric operational models. Please note: this tool requires JavaScript to be enabled to fully function.
