About Free AI Detector by ContentAtScale

The Free AI Detector is an AI content detection tool that can identify if content has been produced by AI models such as ChatGPT, GPT-3, and other AI models. It is an easy-to-use tool that takes the content you enter and checks it against AI models to give you a score on how likely it is to have been produced by an AI model. Additionally, Content at Scale offers an API for AI detection to check larger amounts of words at once and with more accuracy, as well as a platform that allows you to upload up to 100 keywords and receive back 100 entire human-quality blog posts without any human intervention. This AI content detection tool is a reliable, accurate, and efficient way to check the authenticity of content produced by AI models.
