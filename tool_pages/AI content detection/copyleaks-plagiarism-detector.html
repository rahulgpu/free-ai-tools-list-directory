About Copyleaks – Plagiarism detector

The Copyleaks Plagiarism Detector is a tool designed to identify potential plagiarism, detect AI-generated content and confirm authenticity and ownership. The platform uses advanced AI to detect slight variations including hidden characters, paraphrasing and AI-generated content, providing a more thorough approach to content protection. The Plagiarism Detector can detect multiple forms of paraphrasing, image-based text plagiarism, attempts to deceive detection software, plagiarism in source code, and supports over 30 cross-language scans.The tool has an enterprise solution designed to verify whether the content was produced by a person or AI. It offers full-spectrum detection that includes AI-generated content detection with 99.12% accuracy and can detect AI-generated content including ChatGPT4. The Plagiarism Detector supports multiple languages including English, French, Spanish, German, Italian, Russian, Polish, and many others. Additionally, the platform offers a scheduled recurring scan feature that automatically keeps content safe from potential plagiarism, which adds a new layer of protection for the content. The Copyleaks Plagiarism Detector is trusted by leading organizations and institutions around the world.
