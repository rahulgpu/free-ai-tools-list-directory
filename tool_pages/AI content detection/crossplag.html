About Crossplag

The AI Content Detector is a tool developed by Crossplag to determine whether a piece of text has been generated by an AI system. It uses advanced machine learning algorithms and ChatGPT detection technology to identify patterns and characteristics of AI-generated and human-written content. The tool provides an accurate rating score bar to evaluate the authenticity of the text. To ensure better results, it is recommended that the text is at least 200 characters long while presenting the tool with larger volumes of text results in improved performance. The AI Content Detector is useful for academic integrity purposes and can also aid in improving the quality and relevance of online content. The tool is offered for free and does not store any data during the analysis process. As of now, the AI detector supports only the English language, and Crossplag plans to add more languages in the future. The AI Detector gives an estimation of the likelihood that a piece of text was written by an AI system or a human. The percentage given by the tool provides an insight into whether the content is genuine or AI-generated.
