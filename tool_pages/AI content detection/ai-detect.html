About AI Detect

AI Detect is a tool that helps discern if content has been generated by a machine. Its prime function is to evaluate whether a piece of text was written by a human or created by an artificial intelligence algorithm. It is specifically designed to detect AI writing within any piece of text with great accuracy, and can distinguish content from various AI models, including the likes of ChatGPT, Google Gemini, Claude Opus, Meta LLaMa, and others. Additionally, AI Detect can evaluate the grade level of the writing and analyze content for 'human-like quality'. Lastly, the tool offers a summarizing feature which provides a condensed overview of the text. AI Detect is utilized by a diverse range of users, from individuals wishing to verify the human-like quality of their own AI-generated content, to businesses seeking to verify content authenticity on a larger scale.
