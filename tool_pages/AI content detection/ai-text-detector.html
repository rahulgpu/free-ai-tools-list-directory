About AI Text Detector

AI Text Detector is a tool designed to discern whether a piece of text, such as an essay or review, has been generated by an AI. This tool seeks to aid in ensuring the uniqueness and human origin of content thereby potentially minimizing penalties on social media and search engines that may result from AI-generated content. AI Text Detector supports numerous platforms including iPhone, iPad, Mac and Android. The tool employs a broad range of AI for text analysis including GPT3, GPT4, ChatGPT, Bard and LaMMDA. The user interface facilitates easy text scanning. Text can be entered by copy-pasting, importing a PDF, or scanning a physical document with the camera. Furthermore, it includes additional features such as a Plagiarism checker, which strives to guarantee the originality of content by providing a list of phrases and source links for every plagiarism match. A readability checker as an additional feature, aims at improving SEO by providing a detailed sentence by sentence report, including various readability indicators. The tool is also being developed for web to provide more accessibility. Finally, while it claims the highest accuracy and lowest rate of false positives it is recommended that users evaluate based on their specific needs.
