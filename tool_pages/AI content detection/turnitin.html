About Turnitin

Turnitin is an AI tool that provides a comprehensive solution for academic institutions to promote academic integrity, streamline grading and feedback, deter plagiarism, and improve student outcomes. It offers multiple features such as originality check, paper grading, plagiarism detection, writing feedback, and more. Institutions can choose from different plans depending on their needs. One of the recent features launched by Turnitin is AI detection to identify whether AI writing tools, such as ChatGPT, have been used. This feature helps institutions ensure that the student’s work and intellectual property are original and uphold academic integrity. The AI detection feature is highly reliable and proficient in distinguishing between human-written text and AI-written text. It is specifically designed for student writing and integrated into the institution's workflow, ensuring a seamless experience.Turnitin is a tool that empowers students to do their best, original work with built-in AI writing detection technology. It supports instructors by providing streamlined grading processes and actionable feedback that helps develop student's original thinking skills. Users can access a range of educational resources, including customer stories, white papers, infographics, training, and support. Turnitin is available in multiple languages, including English, Deutsch, Español, Français, Bahasa Indonesia, Italiano, 日本語, 한국어, Nederlands, Português, Русский, Türkçe, 繁體中文, and 简体中文. Overall, Turnitin is an essential AI tool for academic institutions that helps them safeguard academic integrity, promote academic excellence, and enhance student outcomes.
