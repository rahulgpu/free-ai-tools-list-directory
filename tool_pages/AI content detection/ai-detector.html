About AI Detector

AI Detector & Plagiarism Scan is a tool designed to differentiate between human-written and AI-generated text. It is beneficial for content creators, students, and teachers who wish to confirm the authenticity of their content. Search engines and social media platforms tend to penalize AI-generated content, so ensuring that provided content is human-generated is critical. AI Detector provides results in the form of a percentage indicating the extent to which the text is AI-generated. In addition to on-screen text, the application can scan physical documents using the device's camera and can also import and analyze PDFs. Users can also insert a link for the app to assess an entire website. Despite its thorough scanning capabilities, AI Detector remains user-friendly. The application is ideal for anyone wanting to verify if their content appears human-written, helping to avoid penalties from social media platforms and search engines.
