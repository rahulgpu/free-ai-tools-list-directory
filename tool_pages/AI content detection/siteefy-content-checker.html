About Siteefy content checker

The AI Content Checker is a browser extension tool developed by Siteefy that aims to enhance the publishing process by ensuring rapid and error-free content creation. It allows users to review and summarize web pages in multiple languages, ask specific questions about the page content, and receive relevant answers from the AI. The tool also highlights HTML headings to provide insights into the page structure and identifies links that open in new tabs, spacing issues, and broken links. With the AI Content Checker, users can click the "Review this page" button to have the AI analyze their content and provide tips on improving its quality. Additionally, users can ask the AI any question about a web page, and it will respond based on the context of the page. The tool offers extra features such as highlighting links that open in the same window and enabling users to gain a better understanding of their page structure.Benefits of using the AI Content Checker include faster content publishing, time-saving by automating content checking tasks, availability on any web page, reliable assistance from the AI, peace of mind knowing that content is thoroughly checked, and opportunities for learning and improving content creation skills. Notably, the tool is free to use. Overall, the AI Content Checker aims to streamline the content creation process, making it easier and more efficient for individuals working in various roles, including SEO specialists, digital marketers, and content creators.
