About Copyleaks – AI content detector

The 'AI Detector' by Copyleaks is a comprehensive tool designed to distinguish whether content was created by a human or an AI. It efficiently analyses the characteristics of the text and asserts if it is AI-generated or human-written. This tool supports the detection of AI models like ChatGPT, Gemini, Claude among others and works in over 30 languages, making it highly versatile for different content and audience types. Serving as an enterprise solution, it is positioned to support organizations in ensuring the originality of their content and verifying the human involvement in the content creation. It can determine within seconds if any content has been AI-generated with outstanding accuracy. The tool also indicates potential AI content if key functionalities of certain platforms like Grammarly, known for using ChatGPT and other gen AI models, are detected within the text. This detector can be a useful aide in content verification especially considering the surge in AI-generated content across various industries.
