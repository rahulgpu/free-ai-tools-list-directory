About iAuthor

iAuthor is an online tool designed especially for facilitating proofreading, content authoring and peer reviewing operations in a collaborative setting. It particularly suits academic work users who are looking for an effective means to render and modify their content. The tool's environment is HTML-based, providing familiar and simplified user interactions. It allows for an easy exchange of ideas and contributions primarily between authors and reviewers. An impressive feature is the swift AI integration and deployment facilitated by Quixl, an AI accelerator. This AI-empowered aspect enhances the process of digital authoring and editing, allowing for efficient and improved content production. The platform can be instrumental in accelerating time-to-market for writers, content creators and academics looking for an adept authoring tool. iAuthor emphasizes enabling users to bring their content to life with the help of AI, prioritizing productivity, and efficiency in their authoring process.
