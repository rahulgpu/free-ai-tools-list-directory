About LEWK

LEWK is an artificial intelligence-powered tool designed to introduce a heightened level of personalization and customization to online fashion shopping experiences. Incorporated seamlessly with any e-commerce storefront, LEWK functions as a virtual fashion stylist, making customized outfit recommendations and stylistic advice tailored to the individual shopper's tastes and preferences. It leverages machine learning technology to deliver shopable, AI-driven mood boards, providing consumers with a high level of interactivity and enabling them to explore different styles and fashion ideas. The technology behind LEWK is white-labelled, making it a suitable and easily integrable solution for fashion and apparel e-commerce companies looking to drive engagement, increase average order values, and boost brand profitability. LEWK not only enhances the customer shopping experience but also offers value to businesses by potentially stimulating higher conversion rates. It also boasts features that allow for chat integration, further enhancing the shopping experience by enabling customers to interact with their AI-powered fashion expert, obtain styling help, and receive personalized fashion advice.
