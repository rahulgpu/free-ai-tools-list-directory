About Aesty

Aesty is a personalized digital stylist application, aimed at enhancing your fashion choices. Acting as a style curator, Aesty has the ability to digitize clothes from real-life photos into a mobile wardrobe. The app offers instant style recommendations based on user-provided photos of their full body, taking into consideration their personal style preferences, personal features, occasion, and local weather conditions. Users are able to score outfits and receive tailored feedback based on their selections, as well as discover their ideal color palette through a seasonal color test. Additionally, Aesty has the ability to digitize a user's wardrobe by uploading mirror selfies and 'Outfit of The Day' images. The app features a manual outfit creation tool which users can utilize according to their own settings. It also proposes recommended brands based on the user's style preferences. The app is primarily designed for iPhones.
