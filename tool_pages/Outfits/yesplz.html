About Yesplz

ChatGPT Fashion Stylist is an AI-powered virtual personal shopper that is revolutionizing the way people shop for fashion. It uses the latest machine learning technology, combining natural language processing and advanced computer vision, to provide real personalization that caters to an individual's unique style. Users can engage in real conversations with the AI stylist, asking for style suggestions or specific fashion products in a comfortable writing style without needing to change their search behavior.The AI stylist offers personalized recommendations based on fashion intelligence that knows the latest styles and brands. It understands user preferences and remembers specific individual style choices, constantly searching to curate the best product recommendations for each user's taste. The system provides not only product recommendations but also styling suggestions and personalized curations unique to each user.For B2B customers, ChatGPT Fashion Stylist offers simple plug-in integration, working seamlessly with catalog systems and providing an easy way to customize the shopping experience. This makes it suitable for online fashion retailers who can benefit from advanced image tagging technology and the integration of ChatGPT to deliver product recommendations that cater to their customers' specific needs.The service is available for free in its beta version for end users, while eCommerce partners have flexible pricing options based on SKU size and traffic. The tool aims to enhance the fashion shopping experience by providing personalized and tailored recommendations, combining the power of AI with the latest trends in fashion.
