About TryOnHub

TryOnHub.AI is an Artificial Intelligence (AI) powered tool designed to enhance the online shopping experience. It offers a virtual try-on feature that enables users to virtually try on any outfit from top brands to the latest trends, transforming their shopping experience with authenticity and convenience. The AI tool was trained using a vast s of photos showcasing diverse model postures for precise body form recognition, aiming to offer an enjoyable virtual try-on experience for users. TryOnHub.AI also assists customers by giving them access to any fashion brand's latest collection and allows them to see how they look in any outfit using just a link or image. This can eliminate worries about buying clothing that doesn't fit well. For businesses, TryOnHub.AI offers capabilities to increase conversion rates and offer customers personalized buying experiences, as well as efficient, low-code solutions for virtual try-on. The process involves the tool identifying body position points from a user's uploaded photo to shape the figure, the tool examining the clothing's specifications to determine its dimensions and the advanced AI then matching body points with the outfit. The user can then see how clothes look on them before making a purchase. Future features include outfit recommendations based on the user's uploaded image, interactive garment customization, and social sharing and rating options.
