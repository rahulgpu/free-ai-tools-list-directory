About AskLain

Lain is an AI plugin designed to enhance productivity among engineers who use Slack. It's an intelligent tool that learns from and monitors your Slack channels, aiming to provide assistance in solving issues, whether related to onboarding or troubleshooting. The Plugin operates unintrusively, waiting for a situation wherein it can add value. When such a situation occurs, it indicates its readiness to help.Upon creating an account, users specify the Slack channels they want Lain to enable its service on. Lain then consistently monitors these channels for any potential use-cases. It's designed to have real-time and comprehensive knowledge of a channel's chat contents. Moreover, it provides cross-channel context as it can access information across different channels.Additionally, Lain is integrated with platforms like Github, Jira, and Confluence, expanding its knowledge base and allowing it to stay updated with the latest issues being discussed in your channels. It also possesses the most recent knowledge of documentation from major languages and frameworks.The interaction with Lain is intuitive, covering all aspects from initiating to concluding a conversation. Lain can greatly expedite the onboarding process by evolving naturally with changes and can act as a valuable team member during Site-wide Emergency Vehicle (SEV) situations. Lain specializes in learning unique problems encountered in individual JavaScript channels and assists developers in solving them.
