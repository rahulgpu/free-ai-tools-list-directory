About HideMyAI

HideMy.AI is an AI tool that enables users to transform AI-generated content into authentic, human-like writing that remains undetectable by AI detectors. With this tool, users can effortlessly input text created by AI models like ChatGPT and Jasper, and HideMy.AI's proprietary models will humanize the content, making it indistinguishable from content written by humans.The tool offers a free public demo with limited usage, allowing users to experience its capabilities. For higher limits and additional features such as multilingual processing and document uploads, users can sign up for a free HideMy.AI account or choose to subscribe to a Pro Plan starting at $5/month.HideMy.AI ensures that the reworded content reads like it was written by a human, bypassing leading AI detectors like Originality.ai and TurnItIn. By removing markers and watermarks and restructuring the content, HideMy.AI eliminates traditional AI indicators and allows the AI-generated content to be indexed and ranked by search engines without SEO penalties.The tool operates by pasting in the content or uploading a document, which is then processed by their custom-trained AI. It semantically understands the content, removes AI watermarks, restructures it while preserving facts and information, and automatically checks it against AI detectors. If the content does not pass, it will be reprocessed for free until it is undetectable.HideMy.AI provides a guarantee that the AI content will remain undetectable by AI detectors. In the event that the content fails to meet this criteria, users can report it on the platform and receive a refund of their credits.
