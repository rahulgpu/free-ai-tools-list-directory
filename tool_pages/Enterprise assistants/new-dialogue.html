About New Dialogue

New Dialogue is an AI tool that functions as a private assistant for your team, utilizing technology based on open-source GPT for generating contextual results based on your content. The tool grants the ability to reimagine customer and employee experiences through the provision of expert-level results derived from prompts, known as 'Semantic Search'. Furthermore, the tool allows users to talk directly with their operational knowledge, similar to interacting with a subject matter expert within your business. It accommodates effortless sharing of key prompt results across users via Zapier integration. This tool enhances workflow automation by integrating AI results into your workflows. To extend its usability and reach, New Dialogue can be embedded into your Intranet or Website, providing an AI Chat function. This feature supports your team and customers by acquainting the AI with your content. Thus, it sets the stage for a collaborative work environment enhanced by AI-driven interactions and experiences.
