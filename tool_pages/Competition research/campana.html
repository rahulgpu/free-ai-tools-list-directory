About Campana

Campana is an AI-powered tool designed for businesses to effortlessly monitor their competition. It provides a detailed digest of competitor activity, offering insights into their online maneuvers. The tool tracks competitors' changes on their websites, when they roll out new features, post on social media, and more, providing an easy way for businesses to stay updated about their competition. Campana's core functionality includes live monitoring of competitor activity and AI-powered competitive intelligence, providing thorough insights about competitors. It additionally features a service to send daily news briefings about latest industry news and media coverage about competitors. Campana also offers data on competitors' website traffic, asserting to assure industry-leading accuracy. Furthermore, it boasts a comprehensive data profiling, scouring the internet for relevant data and presenting the most relevant information to the user. Notifications ensure users are promptly aware of any updates with their competitors. All this information comes together to help businesses gain a competitive edge by better understanding their competition and the wider industry trends.
