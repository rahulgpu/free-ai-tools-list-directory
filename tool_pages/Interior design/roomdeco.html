About RoomDeco

RoomDeco AI is an advanced tool that uses artificial intelligence to generate virtual staging and interior designs. It transforms sketches into photorealistic renders and allows users to create unique interior designs by controlling style, interior type, and theme. The tool offers the ability to customize the design by selecting colors, fabrics, textures, and materials to achieve the desired vibe and style blend. It operates on a wide array of design styles and rooms, adding versatility to its functionality. RoomDeco AI also allows users to adjust quality, generate multiple images, and add custom watermarks for professional use. It offers a Chrome extension for easy access and immediate application from web images. RoomDeco AI is helping real estate agents, homeowners, contractors, and interior designers to visualize and realize their ideas in a simplified, efficient way, enhancing decision making in the design process.
