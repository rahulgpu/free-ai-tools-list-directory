About DreamyRooms

Dreamy Rooms is an AI-powered tool designed to instantly create and redesign interiors in multiple themes. It operates by analyzing a selected interior and using deep learning and advanced algorithms to apply design modifications. Key elements in the room such as colors, furniture, decor, and layout are adjusted, transforming the room into the preferred theme. Users can select from a wide variety of design themes, including but not limited to modern, classic, and minimalist styles. Dreamy Rooms provides a unique service catering to both personal and professional use, enhancing personal photos or images used for commercial purposes, such as in advertising or e-commerce. It offers both a free and a premium subscription, with the premium providing access to more themes, advanced customization, and other benefits. The service prides itself on its secure platform, implementing industry-standard security measures to protect user data. One important limitation to note is that users can only redesign one room at a time.
