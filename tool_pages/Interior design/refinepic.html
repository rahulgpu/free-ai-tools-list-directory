About RefinePic

RefinePic is an AI-based platform centered around Interior Design for homes and rooms. It provides an intuitive interface for users to upload an image of a room or house, specify their design preferences visually, and see a tailored design generated by the platform. Based on AI technology, this tool is capable of interpreting and applying the specified changes to provide a modified, visually appealing image within moments. It allows users to perform complex edits such as color adjustments, object additions, and scene transformations, keeping in mind their creative needs. This platform is designed for both beginners and experienced users alike, with an easy-to-use interface that makes advanced photo editing accessible to a widespread audience. Once the users are content with their edits, they can download the modified images for further use. RefinePic allows basic editing features to be used for free, and for more advanced features, users have the option to purchase credits or subscribe to the platform's premium plan.
