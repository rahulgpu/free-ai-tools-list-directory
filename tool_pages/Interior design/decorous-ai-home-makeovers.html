About Decorous: AI Home Makeovers

Decorous is an AI-powered tool targeted at interior design enthusiasts. Its primary function allows users to transform their spaces using the aid of artificial intelligence. Users can snap pictures of their rooms and the tool provides personalized interior design suggestions tailored to the featured room. It offers a wide range of popular styles, varying from mid-century modern to industrial, and curated color palettes that can be selected to align with the desired mood or aesthetic of the user. A distinguishing feature of the Decorous tool is the interactive aspect provided by its AI designer chat feature. This functionality offers users an interactive experience, allowing them to discuss their spaces with the AI for additional suggestions and ideas. Decorous is designed to help users elevate their living spaces, offering a new approach to personalized interior design. It's developed by Strange Mesa, and is available for free with in-app purchases on the App Store.
