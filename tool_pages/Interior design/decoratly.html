About Decoratly

Decoratly is an AI-powered tool that enables seamless transformation of interior and exterior spaces into different styles. The tool is designed for both professionals and individuals who desire a user-friendly and flexible design platform. The central feature of Decoratly is the ability to upload pictures of any part of your home or exterior area and apply desired stylistic transformations. Users have the opportunity to specify their style preferences, color schemes, furniture types, and other specifics, adding a layer of personalization to the design process. Decoratly's AI technology generates realistic and strikingly impressive design alterations that align with user preferences. The tool offers variety by offering two rendering modes called 'makeover' and 'enhance'. The 'makeover' mode enables radical transformations while 'enhance' allows subtle changes. Moreover, with an extensive array of 27 different style options, Decoratly gives users the freedom to experiment with numerous design ideas. The tool also houses a 'prompt builder', providing the option for users to create their own unique design prompts. Through this feature, users can bring their own unique vision to life.
