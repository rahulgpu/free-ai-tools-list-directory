About Xona AI

Xona.ai is an AI-generated interior design tool for transforming interiors instantly. Users submit an image of their space, select their preferred style, and let the AI generate the interior design. Xona.ai enables the exploration of technology combined with creativity, redefining the user's space with precision and beauty.It also features 'Magic Eraser', an advanced digital editing tool to remove undesired items or imperfections from images. This feature is particularly beneficial for professional photographers or hobbyists wanting to enhance their image quality.Another notable feature is its drawing tool, allowing users to sketch their design ideas and transform them into realistic AI-generated visualizations. This is ideal for designers and hobbyists, helping to streamline the design process by turning simple drawings into tangible visuals quickly, thereby promoting exploration and refinement of interior design concepts.Users can additionally leverage a curated styles selection developed in collaboration with top interior designers to further shape their interior design outcomes, reflecting the latest trends in aesthetic and functional design.The image generation process of Xona.ai is simple: it starts with the uploading of an image (supporting popular formats like PNG, JPG, and JPEG), style selection, and then the AI performs the transformation. This tool can work with various mediums for interior redesign, including 3D models, pictures, sketches, and drawings, promising high-quality results no matter the initial format.
