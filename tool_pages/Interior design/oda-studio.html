About Oda Studio

Oda Studio's Moodboard is a digital tool designed to simplify the home decorating process. It allows users to create custom mood boards with a range of styles and colors to choose from. The style options include mid-century modern, bohemian, minimal, and living room in. Color options include navy, salmon, olive, beige, dark gray, mint, and mustard. Users can also add plants, landscape wall art, and abstract wall art. The generated base image includes the user's selected style and color. The tool is powered by Oda Studio and is subject to their Privacy Policy and Terms of Use.
