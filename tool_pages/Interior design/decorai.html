About DecorAI

DecorAI is an AI-powered tool designed to generate unique and personalized interior designs within seconds. Users input an image or sketch of a room layout, and DecorAI quickly returns high-quality design ideas, saving time and increasing efficiency in the process. DecorAI offers multiple benefits such as a cost-effective solution to interior design needs by generating a wide range of design ideas for a fraction of the cost of hiring additional designers, making it an affordable option for those on a tight budget. Users can customize the AI-generated designs to suit their clients' preferences and needs. With DecorAI's technology, it's possible to transform sketches into real-life photos with a single click, providing users a wide range of room styles to select. Additionally, users can add distinct pieces of furniture to their designs for a more personalized look. DecorAI can be used by interior design agencies, interior designers, homeowners, and furniture retailers to provide innovative and personalized design solutions. For those looking to build their own personalized interior apps, DecorAI also provides an API.
