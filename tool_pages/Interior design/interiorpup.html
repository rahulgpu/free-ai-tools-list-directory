About InteriorPup

InteriorPup is an AI-powered tool designed to assist users in redesigning their rooms. By leveraging AI technologies, it enables users to create unique and personalized designs, tailored to their preferences, in a short amount of time. The tool addresses the challenges often faced during the design stage, such as choosing paint colors, coordinating matching colors, and arranging furniture. It offers three main modes of operation: Full Remake, Virtual Staging, and Room Retouching. In Full Remake, AI completely changes the room, including wall shape, color, and positioning. Virtual Staging mode allows for changes within the existing room structure. Room Retouching mode enables users to draw over the objects in an image for the AI to alter. InteriorPup introduces a time-efficient solution for repetitive design tasks, offering individual-purpose and subscription plans catering to small projects and continuous usage needs respectively. The tool includes a collection of design modes and styles, fast rendering, and priority support in the subscription plan. An interactive dashboard is provided where users can use credits to create their room renders.
