About Versy AI

Text-to-Space by Versy.ai offers the ability to create interactive virtual experiences through a text prompt. This tool uses Generative AI to build unique environments such as escape rooms, leaderboards, and product configuration experiences. It is designed to facilitate the creation of virtual spaces, reducing the costs associated with manual production. The tool is customizable, allowing businesses to train the Versy AI to create branded virtual experiences specifically tailored to their specific needs. The technology also makes use of user or company data, linking with systems such as CRMs, AI, and IoT, to dynamically construct user experiences. An additional feature of this tool is its capability to iterate quickly among different versions, allowing the testing of different ideas with ease. Its scalable design implies that numerous virtual experiences can be built, maintained, and interconnected simultaneously. It is not limited to traditional 3D spaces as it also enables the creation of other interactive experiences such as events, treasure hunts, and more.
