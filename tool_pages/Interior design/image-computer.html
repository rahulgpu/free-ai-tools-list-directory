About Image Computer

Room AI is a user-friendly, professional-grade tool that uses AI technology to create interior design solutions. The tool allows users to try out a variety of interior design styles for their homes and generate photo-realistic renders of room designs. With Room AI, users can upload an image of their room and have the software restyle it according to their chosen design preferences. Room AI caters to various room types and styles, providing a wide range of customization options for colors and materials, offering both preset and AI-generated color palettes. It also presents a selection of material options, enabling users to visualize different combinations in their interiors. This tool is built not only for homeowners but also for interior designers, architects, and real estate agents. For designers and architects, it simplifies the design process by generating diverse design ideas quickly. For real estate agents, it can showcase a property's potential by presenting various interior design possibilities, facilitating faster sales. Room AI supports both personal and commercial use, depending on the type of subscription held by the user. It promises secure transactions and protects user data while maintaining privacy.
