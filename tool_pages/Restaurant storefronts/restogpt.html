About RestoGPT

RestoGPT is an AI-powered tool that assists restaurants in creating custom online ordering storefronts without charging any commissions. By submitting the name and menu of the restaurant, RestoGPT's AI technology takes care of the entire process. The generated storefront includes various features such as universal point of sale (POS) integration, sales tracking, and delivery integration. With RestoGPT, restaurants can effortlessly start accepting and delivering orders on autopilot, eliminating the need for drivers and associated fees. The tool provides a seamless online ordering experience for customers, enabling them to easily browse and select menu items from the restaurant's digital storefront.By utilizing AI, RestoGPT streamlines the process of creating online delivery storefronts, saving time and effort for restaurant owners. The tool also offers flexibility by allowing integration with existing POS systems, ensuring compatibility with a range of restaurant operations.RestoGPT's AI technology offers an efficient solution for restaurants seeking to expand their online presence and connect with customers through an intuitive and user-friendly ordering platform. With its focus on commission-free service and automated operations, RestoGPT enables restaurants to enhance their online delivery capabilities and provide a seamless ordering experience for their customers.
