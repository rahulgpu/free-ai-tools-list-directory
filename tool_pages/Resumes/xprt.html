About XPRT

Xprtis is an AI-powered Chrome extension designed to facilitate job applications by generating custom resumes optimized for Applicant Tracking Systems (ATS). The tool takes a user's LinkedIn profile information and tailors it to a specific job they are applying for, enhancing relevance and assisting in bypassing the ATS for a higher chance of landing an interview. The Xprtis tool provides a solution to some of the common challenges associated with traditional resume crafting. It enables personalization and addresses the unique requirements of each job vacancy. Additionally, the AI ensures ATS optimization by implementing appropriate keywords and formatting, enhancing its approval likelihood. Besides, it provides time-saving benefits, as jobseekers input their details only once, eliminating the need for repeatedly manual crafting of resumes. The AI-crafted resumes, with their target-specific keywords and engagement-optimized presentation, could potentially positively influence response rates, thereby increasing interview chances. Importantly, Xprtis offers its services free of cost, making it accessible to a large number of job seekers.
