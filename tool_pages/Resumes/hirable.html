About Hirable

Hirable is an AI-enabled career platform powered by OpenAI's GPT. It offers a suite of tools designed to guide users through various stages of their professional journey, aiming to increase the success rate in landing desired jobs. One of the main features includes a job-specific resume and cover letter writer. This feature optimizes job documents to improve their performance in Employer's Applicant Tracking System (ATS) scans, enhancing the chances of reaching potential employers. Users can save drafts, adjust the tone and add relevant keywords. Furthermore, Hirable provides an application tracker to organize and monitor job applications. This feature enables users to store all application documents, add personal notes, and compile relevant interview questions for each application. Another key feature is its interview preparation tool. With a collection of interview questions, Hirable can analyze users responses and provide feedback based on experiences to improve interview performance. In addition, the platform is built to assist users in exploring new career roles, helping them understand what it takes to succeed in those roles by aligning their values, personality traits, and previous experiences with potential opportunities. This includes an AI writer for drafting role-specific resumes as per the chosen career transition.
