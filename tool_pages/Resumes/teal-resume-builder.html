About Teal Resume Builder

AI Resume Builder is an AI-powered tool designed to assist users in creating and customizing professional resumes. It enables users to add their information either by uploading an existing resume or by importing their LinkedIn profile. The tool provides real-time feedback and actionable recommendations to enhance the structure, format, and content of the resumes. It also offers an AI feature that generates resume summaries, achievements, and cover letters, helping to speed up the resume creation process. Users can choose from a selection of professional templates and have full design control to create an applicant tracking system (ATS) friendly resume with their personal style. The tool also assists users in matching their resume to job descriptions by highlighting keyword insights to build a tailored resume. Moreover, users can create and export multiple versions of their resumes, enabling them to customize each one with job-specific content. The tool aims to enhance job searches and help users land their desired jobs more efficiently.
