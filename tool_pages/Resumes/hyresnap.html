About HyreSnap

HyreSnap is an AI-based resume builder designed to create professional, ATS-friendly resumes. It offers a variety of customizable templates, aiming to elevate the prospects of job seekers by enabling them to present their skills and experience effectively. HyreSnap's AI feature reviews the resume against industry best practices, providing guidance and suggestions for improvement. Furthermore, it has a 'Job Match' feature that compares a user's resume with desired job descriptions to identify any missing skills or keywords. This is contrasted with real-time compatibility scores for job listings, which are calculated based on the alignment of the user's skills and qualifications with the job requirements. In addition to creating resumes, HyreSnap also provides an array of modern, creative resume templates that reflect the latest industry trends. It includes formats that utilize icons, symbols, and contemporary aesthetics to help a user's resume stand out from the crowd.
