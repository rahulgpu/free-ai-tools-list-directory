About Resume Worded

Resume Worded is an AI-powered online tool designed to provide immediate feedback and scoring on resumes and LinkedIn profiles. The platform is built with insights from top recruiters to accurately analyze resumes and give users actionable recommendations on how to improve their documents. Features include 'Score My Resume' which scores users' resumes based on the criteria recruiters and hiring managers look for. 'Targeted Resume' enables users to tailor their resumes to specific job descriptions, and 'LinkedIn Review' offers personalized feedback on users' LinkedIn profiles. The tool guides users in revamping and optimizing their resumes and LinkedIn profiles to increase visibility, draw more opportunities, and enhance their job-market competitiveness. The AI platform also provides a collection of resume samples from successful job applicants in top-tier companies across many industries and skills, offering users ideas to craft their resumes effectively. An added function of the tool is the facilitation of more effective job search funnels by enabling users to maximize the conversion of job applications to interviews. Resume Worded also operates in the educational sector, with universities and educational institutions using its AI platform to improve student outcomes and maximize operational efficiency.
