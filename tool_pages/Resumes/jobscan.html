About Jobscan

Jobscan ATS Resume Checker and Job Search Tools is an AI-driven tool that helps job seekers optimize their resumes for specific job listings so that they can increase their chances of getting interviews. The tool uses proprietary AI technology to analyze the job listing and compare it to the candidate's resume in order to identify the key skills and experience that recruiters are looking for. The tool then provides the job seeker with a match rate report that shows them how to optimize their resume to get more interviews. Jobscan ATS Resume Checker also helps job seekers beat Applicant Tracking Systems (ATS) by tailoring their resumes to meet ATS requirements. The tool reverse-engineers all the top ATS systems and studies recruiter workflows to ensure that the candidate's resume gets noticed in a sea of applicants. Additionally, Jobscan ATS Resume Checker provides interactive job search tools that include a resume builder, a cover letter optimization tool, and LinkedIn profile optimization. Finally, the tool has received positive feedback from users who have found it to be an effective and efficient way to tailor their resumes to match job listings and increase their chances of getting hired.
