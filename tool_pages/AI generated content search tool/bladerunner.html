About BladeRunner

BladeRunner is a browser extension aimed at identifying and highlighting text that is generated by artificial intelligence. It integrates directly with your browser and works on web pages during your browsing sessions. The extension is particularly useful for detecting and unmasking fraudulent or synthetic content such as fake product reviews, artificially-generated comments, and deceptive articles. The tool's main function relies on differentiating between content created by humans and those produced by AI models like GPT. Users can quickly understand the source of the text as BladeRunner employs highlighting and confidence scores. Additionally, this tool proves valuable across numerous sectors, including social media, where it helps detect spam, misinformation, or fake accounts; in education and publishing, where it can spot AI-generated text in exams, assignments, and research work; in e-commerce, where it can expose fake reviews or dishonest direct messages; and in government where it assists in monitoring for misinformation, incitement, and cyber threats. BladeRunner aims to help navigate the complexities of the digital space, particularly as AI-generated content becomes more pervasive and sophisticated.
