About DryRunAI

DryRunAI is an artificial intelligence tool designed to provide actionable and valuable feedback on presentations. The tool is created with the purpose of assisting users in honing their presentation skills, by analyzing both the content and delivery of presentations. Upon analysis, DryRunAI offers suggestions to advance the quality of your presentation. This tool replaces the traditional way of rehearsing presentations in front of a mirror by providing constructive feedback on improvement points. The feedback from DryRunAI is purposefully designed to be clear, relevant, and aimed at enhancing the delivery of engaging and effective presentations. DryRunAI's functionality is trusted and used by a broad spectrum of users to improve their presentation abilities.
