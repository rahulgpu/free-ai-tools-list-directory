About Mirage

Mirage is a sophisticated tool designed to detect the edited parts of an image using advanced machine learning. The tool also offers a feature to undo the modifications and restore the image close to its original form. Mirage's utility extends beyond simple detection as it allows the user to compare different images together, providing a single view for detailed examination too. Notably, the tool strictly requires the presence of a face in the images for its functionalities to work correctly. Mirage is designed to specifically identify alterations made via software like Photoshop and Facetune. Besides detection and undoing, Mirage aids in making image modifications such as changing hairstyles, adding layers, and emoting images. The tool stands out due to its ad-free user experience. It's important to note that while Mirage may collect data like user content, identifiers, and usage data, these are not linked to your identity, respecting user privacy.
