About ClarityPage

ClarityPage is an AI-driven platform that provides unbiased, up-to-the-minute news coverage spanning the left, right, and center of the political spectrum. By condensing and presenting top stories from various media sources, this tool gives users a balanced viewpoint on trending topics. The platform also clearly highlights news stories that are being ignored or overly focused on by one political segment, suggesting an attempt to either minimize or magnify the importance of those issues. Relevant topics covered by ClarityPage range from politics, science, to domestic and foreign affairs. The tool also offers an insightful coverage comparison tool, showing how different political factions are covering a specific topic. This allows for a deeper understanding of the media polarisation perceived in news reporting. Moreover, ClarityPage offers a weekly round-up feature that helps users stay in the loop about the most important stories in a given week.
