About I Doubt News

I Doubt News is an AI-powered news analysis and fact-checking platform that aims to combat misinformation by identifying and flagging articles that contain selective reporting, cherry-picked data, out-of-context quotes, framing, conspiracy theories, false equivalence, fear-mongering, emotive language, biased language, and appeals to authority. The platform analyzes news articles and rates them based on their trustworthiness using a scale from -1 to 0 to 1, with 0 indicating that the article is neutral and unbiased, and -1 or 1 indicating that the article is highly biased or manipulative. The platform also provides links to the original sources of the news articles and encourages readers to read them critically and with an open mind. The platform categorizes articles based on their level of trustworthiness, with articles rated as 0Trust being the most reliable and those rated as -1Trust or 1Trust being the least reliable. The platform covers a wide range of topics, including politics, business, regional news, and international news. It also identifies specific forms of misinformation, such as stereotyping, false equivalence, selective reporting, and emotional appeals. I Doubt News aims to promote media literacy and critical thinking by facilitating access to accurate and balanced news reporting.
