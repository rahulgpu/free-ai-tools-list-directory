About CollaborativeAI

CollaborativeAI is an open-source AI assistant platform designed to optimize workflows and enhance interdepartmental teamwork. It provides a range of AI models for customization to meet unique requirements, which includes popular advancements such as GPT-4, Gemini, and Claude. It allows businesses to create self-hosted AI assistants tailored to their operational needs, and these assistants evolve with the business growth. CollaborativeAI also emphasizes data security, offering users the comfort of hosting AI assistants on their own servers, thus retaining exclusive control over their data, from sensitive customer information to intellectual property. The platform encourages productivity and innovation by taking advantage of AI capabilities, presenting opportunities for process reinvention and the generation of groundbreaking ideas. With features such as departmental access and assistant assignment, tag-enabled chats for efficient data retrieval, file upload capabilities for AI analysis, and options for customizing AI assistants, CollaborativeAI is a comprehensive solution for businesses looking to harness the power of AI while maintaining ultimate control over data security and assistant customization. The platform is also continually improving with future enhancements like API-based function calls and Python code execution and integration with more APIs. A mobile version of CollaborativeAI is under development, promising accessibility and convenience for users.
