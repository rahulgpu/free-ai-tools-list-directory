About WhatDo

WhatDo is an innovative travel planning tool leveraging AI to curate personalized travel itineraries to users. The platform prides itself on catering to a global range of cities, providing unique experiences, attractions, and dining spots specific to individuals' interests. WhatDo allows you to plan urban adventures and everyday experiences, offering versatile services like planning trips to the beach, hiking destinations, and offering culinary exploration suggestions in food lover hotspots. Interestingly, the tool's function extends beyond itineraries to ensure users can entirely plan and book their travel experiences on one platform. This includes booking of experiences, attractions, and locales, redefining the standard for travel convenience. To guarantee the best travel plans, WhatDo uses data-driven recommendations and insights from travel experts. The goal is to create not only a personalized but also a well-informed travel plan through a seamless and efficient planning method. The platform was birthed from a team of tech innovators and travel enthusiasts seeking a streamlined travel planning solution that invigorates rather than drowns the excitement of travel. Regarded as forward-thinking in travel technology, WhatDo is committed to adapting to the evolving demands of modern exploration, leveraging their diverse and expanding travel destination catalog.
