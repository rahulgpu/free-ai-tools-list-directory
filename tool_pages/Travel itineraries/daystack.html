About Daystack

Daystack is a mobile and web-based app designed to help users organize their day. It allows users to create personalized playlists, called "stacks," for various contexts such as trip itineraries, workout routines, and to-do lists. Users can build stacks from scratch and customize them according to their needs. The app also offers a catalog of pre-existing stacks created by other users, providing inspiration and the ability to like and share stacks with friends.One of the key features of Daystack is its use of powerful generative AI technology. By using natural language prompts, users can harness the app's AI-powered stack creation capabilities. The technology, powered by GPT4, allows users to prompt and edit their stacks until they get a result that suits their requirements.Daystack is a multi-platform app available on Android, iPhone, and the web, providing flexibility in how users access and utilize it. It also offers location-enabled functionality, allowing users to search for locations and add them to their stacks. Additionally, the app supports media integration, enabling users to link and play YouTube videos directly within their stacks.With the option to join a waitlist for its upcoming release on Android, iPhone, and web platforms, Daystack aims to revolutionize how individuals organize their daily activities in a personalized and efficient manner.
