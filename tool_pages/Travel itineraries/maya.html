About Maya

Maya is an AI Travel Assistant that acts as your personal travel agent, available to you 24/7. It uses AI technology to personalize your trip, offering recommendations, itineraries, and travel advice based on your unique preferences and requirements. You can collaborate with Maya to create a high-level draft plan, which will then be transformed into a full, visual trip plan. This plan can be saved and shared with fellow travelers. Furthermore, Maya can suggest destinations based on the type of travel experience desired. Additionally, it can help you book hotels by recommending options that match your destination, budget, and accommodation preferences. Although Maya is unable to chat offline, the finalized plan can be accessed offline by downloading it as a PDF. Maya also provides the opportunity for travel agencies to implement it as their own AI travel assistant to improve client experience.
