About VoyagerAI

VoyagerAI is an artificial intelligence tool designed to assist with personalized travel planning. Its main purpose is to help users plan unique and rare trips, offering tailored suggestions for activities during different times of the day, such as morning, afternoon, and evening. VoyagerAI uses scanning technology, where users are asked to scan a specific code which then allows the tool to provide recommended activities or destinations. The AI analyzes multiple factors to come up with these suggestions, such as individual preferences, location details, and time of day. Users can input the generated search prompt in their notes app or another application of their choice, creating an easy-to-access travel itinerary. Therefore, VoyagerAI offers an innovative way for travellers to explore new locations and maximizes their experience by offering personalized recommendations.
