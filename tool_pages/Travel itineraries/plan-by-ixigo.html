About Plan by ixigo

AI Trip Planner - PLAN is an AI-based travel organization tool designed to help users effectively plan their trips. The tool provides features for creating custom itineraries with minimal effort. It is equipped with features such as flight, hotel, train, and bus booking, making the entire travel planning process streamlined and efficient. The AI Trip Planner also considers individual travel time preferences, budgets, and desired experiences to cater to unique travel needs. In addition to the fundamental travel arrangements, the tool also assists with local attractions and activities such as food, festivals, historical sites, shopping options, outdoor adventures, sports events, and much more depending on the traveler's preferences. Other extra features include group booking and visa booking services, further simplifying the travel preparation process for users. Weather conditions and air quality index are additional considerations for travel plan recommendations, ensuring the comfort and convenience of travelers. The AI Trip Planner caters to a variety of travel themes including but not limited to religious, cultural, nature, wellness, romance, luxury, and offbeat. Each suggested destination comes with the estimated cost, helping users make well-informed decisions.
