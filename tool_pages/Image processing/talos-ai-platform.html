About Talos AI Platform

The Talos AI platform is an intelligent suite of tools designed for processing images and extracting data from documents. Key features include image enhancement, segmentation, and watermark decoding. After the user uploads the images, Talos takes care of processing based on the specified requirements. Along with image processing, Talos also provides accurate PDF reading and data processing tools. Once users upload their PDFs, Talos reads and processes the data with high precision. Another notable feature of the platform is the automation of medical coding. When users upload medical data, Talos handles the coding swiftly and accurately. The platform showcases processing results through before-and-after comparison of the images. This simple and efficient process makes Talos a fully-featured tool for users requiring a tech-advanced solution for their image processing, document handling, and data extraction needs. Its role in transforming documents into actionable insights and images into high-quality files can significantly enhance business operations in diverse sectors.
