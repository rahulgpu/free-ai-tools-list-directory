About Packpack

Packpack is an AI-powered tool designed to enhance reading and research experiences. With one click, this tool allows users to save web content including articles, social media posts, e-commerce product information, videos and audios from a variety of platforms. The tool not only provides a storehouse for users to save and manage their online findings, but also integrates AI to deliver further insights. This involves generating AI summaries and providing AI-powered Q&A for the saved content, thereby allowing users to grasp key points and insights. With a feature termed 'Relevant Search', this tool allows users to find their saved content based on relevance, without the need for precise keyword matching. Audio and visual content is also handled intelligently with AI analyzing stored images and extracting and summarizing subtitles from videos and podcasts. For organization purposes, Packpack includes a 'Package' feature that enables categorization and grouping of saved content into collections that can be easily shared. The tool is available as a browser extension, and also provides applications on Google Play and Apple Store.
