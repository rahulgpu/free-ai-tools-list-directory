About Strut

Strut is an AI tool that turns rough notes into finished content quickly and efficiently. It is designed for creators, writers, and teams who want to streamline the content creation process. With Strut, users can capture their notes and have them transformed into professionally written content using AI technology. This eliminates the need to spend excessive time on crafting bullet points and ensures that the content sounds natural and on-brand.One of the standout features of Strut is its ability to replicate the user's voice and tone by analyzing examples of their previous work. This ensures that the content created using Strut maintains a consistent brand identity. Additionally, Strut allows users to repurpose existing content into different formats, such as blog posts, tweets, LinkedIn posts, newsletters, and more, with just one click.The tool also offers collaborative features, enabling users to brainstorm ideas, co-edit documents, and extract insights from their content with the help of AI chat. This promotes seamless teamwork and enhances productivity.Strut has garnered positive feedback from users who appreciate its unique functionality. It is worth noting that during its beta phase, Strut is available to users for free. The tool ensures user privacy by not utilizing their content or chat messages for AI training purposes.Overall, Strut is a powerful AI tool that empowers users to transform their rough notes into high-quality finished content efficiently, saving time and improving productivity.
