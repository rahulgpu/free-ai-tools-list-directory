About The Cape

Cape is a parenting app designed to provide science-based parenting advice to help parents respond to their child's development milestones on a daily basis. Backed by reputable scientific journals and articles from institutions such as Stanford, UC Berkeley, Harvard, and the University of Minnesota, Cape ensures that the information provided is reliable and trustworthy. With a complete feature set, Cape offers quick answers to parenting queries, boasting an average response time of 9.2 seconds, faster than typical internet searches. This allows parents to access the information they need promptly and efficiently. The app emphasizes its scientific foundation, assuring parents that they can trust the information they receive, helping them have peace of mind.For parents who may be unsure of what to ask, Cape provides existing questions as options to choose from, making it easier for users to navigate and find the guidance they need. Additionally, Cape takes into account specific child details when providing responses, ensuring that the advice is tailored to the unique needs of each child.Cape takes pride in being developed by parents, suggesting that it is designed with an understanding of the challenges and concerns faced by caregivers. Overall, Cape offers a practical and reliable tool for parents seeking science-based guidance in their parenting journey.
