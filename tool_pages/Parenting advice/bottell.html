About Bottell

Bottell is a personal AI parenting assistant that offers customized advice to parents regarding their child's specific needs. Using the power of ChatGPT, this tool is designed to provide empathetic and effective advice to parents at any time. After creating a profile for your child, including their age, interests, and challenges, you can ask the AI assistant specific questions or seek guidance on a variety of issues such as developing meal plans, scheduling, activities, educational guidance, coaching, and support. Moreover, this tool helps parents in solving problems, discovering new activities, nurturing their child, and tracking their progress. With its active development phase, Bottell offers a range of future features such as setting histories of past questions and responses, adding new topics and questions to their advice library and providing a customized feed of recommendations and content based on your child's interests and developmental needs. The tool website also includes a blog and company information such as legal and pricing that can be helpful for potential users. Bottell is an excellent AI tool that provides tailored and personalized parenting solutions to support parents in their parenting journey.
