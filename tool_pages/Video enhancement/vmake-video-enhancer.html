About Vmake Video Enhancer

The Vmake AI Video Enhancer tool is designed to enhance the quality of videos by converting low-resolution content into a high-quality format. Alongside restoring old or blurry materials, the tool can also optimize videos to potentially high standards up to and including 4K and 30FPS. Vmake's AI Video Enhancer accepts multiple file types such as .mp4, .mov, .m4v and .3gp. Users can easily upload up to 3 files at a time for enhancement via a straightforward process. After uploading the video, the AI algorithm auto-applies enhancements, adjusting key attributes such as brightness, contrast, saturation and sharpness. It previews the upgraded quality of the video, allowing users to choose their desired final format and video length before saving and exporting the finished product. This tool can be useful for a range of applications, from boosting audience engagement through enhanced video quality, to creating a more professional look to the content which can help reinforce brand reputation.
