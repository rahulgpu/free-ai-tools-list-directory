About ArchiVinci

ArchiVinci is a suite of AI-driven design tools tailored for architectural needs. The toolset includes the Sketch to Architectural Render AI Tool, Exterior Design AI Tool, Interior Design AI Tool, and Landscape Design AI Tool. Users can transform aesthetic sketches into detailed architectural designs and enhance numerous aspects of the design process ranging from exteriors to interiors and landscapes. Uploads of hand-drawn sketches or inputs of design preferences provide the foundation for the AI tools, facilitating customization of the generated designs, receipt of smart recommendations, and visualization of various architectural designs. ArchiVinci streamlines the design process, capturing user-centric features across the suite. It simplifies workflows from architectural renders to landscape planning through its seamless integration of customization, realism enhancement, and contextual considerations. The toolset can transform freehand sketches and 3D models into detailed architectural renders and provides smart plant selection algorithms for landscaping. ArchiVinci also provides capability for interior and exterior home redesign, across various 3D models. Despite primarily working with sketches and 3D models, flexibility is factored in to allow use of screenshots as a design reference, with an emphasis on preserving the integrity of design lines during rendering.
