About MyArchitectAI

MyArchitectAI is an AI rendering software developed to create photorealistic renders in a relatively short time. Users can upload their architectural or 3D designs from software such as Sketchup or Archicad, and the AI tool handles the rendering process. Being a cloud-based tool, it eliminates the need for any installations. The software supports designs in most CAD and 3D modeling software, provided they are in JPEG or PNG format. Upon upload, users can customize rendering visuals using the pre-defined styles and material types or by providing text instructions. The AI-based render engine then transforms the model into a high-resolution, photorealistic representation. Artists and designers may use this tool to iterate quickly, generate multiple variations of their designs, and impress clients. While primarily aimed at architects and interior designers, MyArchitectAI is a versatile tool suitable for generating renderings for various use-cases, such as remodeling projects.
