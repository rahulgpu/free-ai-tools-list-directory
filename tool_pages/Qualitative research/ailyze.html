About AILYZE

AILYZE is a multi-faceted AI-powered platform for qualitative research purposes. It's designed to extract themes, provide detailed answers to research questions, and support with direct quotes from various forms of documents including PDFs, DOCXs, and XLSXs. It also provides a rapid summarization of those documents. This makes it an ideal tool for those looking to deepen their understanding of a range of topics across multiple languages. AILYZE offers basic and advanced analysis. While the former focuses on extracting themes, answers, and supporting quotes from up to three documents, the latter takes it further with detailed thematic analyses and frequency analysis from up to 15 documents and is available exclusively for Pro users. AILYZE also includes an AI Interviewer feature, a mode that can autonomously interview users in any language, aiding in gathering comprehensive feedback. Additionally, AILYZE offers an Enterprise solution where the user's data is analyzed offline by data scientists utilizing powerful AI models. It ensures high security of your data with encryption procedures, and an adherence to responsible AI practices, generating plagiarism-free results. It is a game changer in the way it revolutionizes transcript analysis, rapid project evaluations, and sales opportunity generation.
