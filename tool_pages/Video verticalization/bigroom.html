About Bigroom

Big Room is an AI-powered tool that automatically converts landscape videos into a vertical format for posting on platforms like TikTok, IG Reels, Stories, and YouTube Shorts. With Big Room, users can quickly and effortlessly transform their videos to fit the vertical aspect ratio, eliminating the need for manual editing or re-shooting.The tool uses advanced AI algorithms to detect the most important people or objects in each frame of the video and automatically cuts to them, giving the edited vertical video a professional look as if it was filmed by a dedicated camera crew. Additionally, Big Room tracks subjects across frames and reframes the vertical clip to ensure that the focus remains on the right elements.Users also have the option to add automatic captions to their vertical videos, enhancing accessibility and engagement. For those who prefer to make final adjustments before posting, Big Room provides the ability to export sequence files compatible with popular video editing software like Adobe Premiere Pro, Final Cut Pro, and DaVinci Resolve.Big Room has already been trusted and used by well-known content creators and channels, such as First We Feast and Tasty, adding credibility to its functionality and performance. To use the tool, users can sign up for early access on the Big Room website.Overall, Big Room offers a convenient and time-saving solution for converting landscape videos to vertical format, making it easier and more efficient for users to optimize their content for popular social media platforms.
