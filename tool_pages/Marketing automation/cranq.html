About CRANQ

CRANQ is an AI-powered automation platform specifically designed to enhance and simplify your marketing efforts. It allows for easy creation and management of content marketing, web scraping and other follower-boosting actions. Leveraging advanced AI technology, CRANQ offers solutions to navigate the complexity and improve the efficiency of marketing workflows. Far from a only technical tool, this platform is user-friendly for both experts and beginners thanks to its prompt-based system. Users simply describe what they want to achieve, and CRANQ translates these prompts into actionable workflow steps, while also offering the freedom to include code for more complex requirements. CRANQ comes with several in-built features that do not require a separate OpenAI account, such as looping, AI logic and cronjobs. Additionally, the platform fosters a community where users can share, import and adapt powerful workflow templates to suit their unique needs. CRANQ prides itself on privacy, ensuring your workflows are not shared unless specific permission is given. The tool offers immediate, AI-powered advice on workflow errors, reducing the need for long tech support waits while facilitating on-the-spot problem diagnosis and rectification.
