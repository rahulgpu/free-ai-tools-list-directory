About Contractiq

ContractIQ is an AI-powered tool designed to significantly expedite the process of drafting and exporting contracts. It offers a seamless and efficient solution for creating precise contracts, allowing users to save time and deliver a more professional and reliable service to their clients.The tool provides a dynamic template selection that is tailored to various industries, ensuring that the generated contracts are relevant and accurate. Users can easily input critical information such as parties involved, clauses, and terms, with the platform guiding them throughout the process to address every element.ContractIQ stands out from other legal AI tools due to its understanding of the world. It has been trained on various sources, including Wikipedia, books, and the Internet, enabling it to reference facts about the world when necessary.Real-time editing capabilities are also offered, allowing users to effortlessly modify contracts using intuitive tools. The tool supports a range of export formats, including PDFs and Word documents, providing compatibility with any platform.ContractIQ offers a free Standard plan with limited features and support. However, users can upgrade to the Pro plan for unlimited contracts per month and priority support. Pricing for the Pro plan is affordable and scales with the growing needs of businesses.By joining ContractIQ, users can revolutionize their contract management process, streamlining and simplifying the creation of contracts while increasing their chances of success.
