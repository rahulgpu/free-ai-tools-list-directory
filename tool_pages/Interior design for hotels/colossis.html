About Colossis

Colossis.io is an AI-powered tool specially designed for the enhancement of real estate images. Its primary function is to create high-resolution virtual staging images. Users can have their room decluttered, rearrange furniture, and add decor, all in 8K resolution. The tool is geared towards helping real estate agents stand out in search results and attract more clicks and customers. Users are simply required to upload a source image per order, and the AI does the transformation. The images outputted from this service are professional grade and unique to each user's image and text input. The service ensures privacy by processing data onsite, deleting it after processing, and refraining from sharing user data with third parties. Data is fully deleted after 48 hours of processing. This tool has been significant in improving property listings, attracting more views, inquiries, and bookings. Its usage is reportedly simple and fast, even for those who are not tech-savvy.
