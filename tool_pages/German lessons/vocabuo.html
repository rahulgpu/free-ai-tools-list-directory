About Vocabuo

Learn vocabulary in the most efficient way. - Add words from your favorite content: YouTube, websites, text or wordlists. - Mark the words you already know - The AI takes care of the rest -> get sentence examples, descriptions and audio for every single word. - Practice with the spaced-repetition algorithm for maximum efficiency. Vocabuo is no-nonsense language learning app. It is not a silly "ai tutor" or and you will not be scolded by an owl. It's for people who want to truly learn a language and look for the most efficient way to do it.
