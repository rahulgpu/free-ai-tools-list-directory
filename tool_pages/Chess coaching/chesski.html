About Chesski

Chesski is an Artificial Intelligence (AI) tool designed to serve as a personal chess tutor. It provides interactive coaching in plain English to help users enhance their chess skills by understanding various strategies and gameplays. This AI-powered tool is known for its user-friendly approach as it utilizes everyday language, making it suitable and accessible for chess enthusiasts of all levels. Users can conveniently sign in using their Google accounts, indicating its provision for simplicity and ease of use. What sets Chesski apart is its emphasis on personalization, as it delivers personalized coaching sessions tailored to the user's skill level and learning pace. This tool is backed by robust AI technology, which allows it to analyse and comprehend various chess moves and strategies, subsequently providing insightful and comprehensive feedback to its users to improve their gameplay. It's also noted for its high-quality support service, ensuring a satisfactory and dependable user experience.
