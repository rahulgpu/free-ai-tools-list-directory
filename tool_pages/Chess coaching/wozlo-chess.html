About Wozlo Chess

Wozlo Chess is a chess coaching application designed to be used on iPads, iPhones, and iPod Touch devices. It utilizes a GPT AI model to provide live feedback on the user's chess moves as they play the game, thereby providing a dynamic, real-time learning environment. This application is also designed to give live justifications for the moves that the AI suggests, providing valuable insights to the user to improve their skills. The app also includes features like a dark mode for comfortable night-time use, an ad-free environment, and an unlimited number of moves for seamless gameplay. Additionally, users have the ability to undo moves and save up to 10 games for future reference. The app also offers an advanced GPT AI model available with the Wozlo Chess+ subscription. Please note that while this app does collect some data, such as purchase history, it doesn't link this to personal identifiers, thereby offering a certain degree of privacy to its users. Internet connection is required for all functionality.
