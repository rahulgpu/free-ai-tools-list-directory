About OTO work

oto is a comprehensive financial tool designed specifically for freelancers. It combines multiple functionalities such as invoicing, time tracking, expenses management, client management, and online contracts into a single platform. With oto, freelancers can effortlessly create proposals, invoices, and contracts without the need for manual work. The tool simplifies the process of invoicing for tracked hours, whether the freelancers work on an hourly basis or project-based. It also offers AI-powered proposals that are tailored to each specific project.One of the standout features of oto is its secure online contracts. These contracts are legally vetted and pre-written agreements, providing freelancers with peace of mind and saving them from the hassle of creating contracts from scratch.The tool also supports various necessary tools for building a perfect invoice, such as invoice templates. The client management feature allows freelancers to easily manage their clients by sharing progress updates including hours, invoices, proposals, and contracts.oto facilitates time tracking, allowing freelancers to record and share their live logged hours with clients through a shareable link. It also enables expense management, with the ability to categorize spending and simplify accounting by uploading receipts.For payments, oto offers the convenience of linking with Stripe or PayPal to accept online payments from clients around the world. It provides features like instant approval on proposals and the ability to create, sign, send, and track pre-made contracts with e-signature functionality.oto integrates with several popular platforms and services, including OpenAI, Stripe, PayPal, and Google.Overall, oto provides freelancers with the necessary tools and features to efficiently manage and automate their freelance business, eliminating manual tasks and streamlining their workflow.
