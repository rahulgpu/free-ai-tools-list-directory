About Signapse

Signapse AI is an artificial intelligence tool designed to break barriers by providing automatic sign language translation with a photo-realistic synthetic signer. The tool is created to solve challenging technical problems that have a genuine social good and offers access to sign language translation and interpretation solutions for clients. Signapse AI has a team of entrepreneurs, engineers, and researchers who build high-quality artificial intelligence solutions to enable accessibility for Deaf people to broadcast media, public spaces, and websites. Signapse AI uses synthetic signers who translate audio, text, and video in real-time. The synthetic signers are based on actual video footage of translators who are compensated each time their image is used commercially. The tool provides visual anonymization of sign language content online to enable safe sharing, and all personal data is deleted within seven days. Signapse AI is built on world-leading research and allows organisations to provide great access to Deaf people. The tool is designed to provide accessibility to an audience of up to 80,000 Deaf people. Signapse AI seeks forward-thinking clients that aim to build the next generation of access for sign language users and values working with those who want to be part of the future of equality, are commercially astute and able to effect change from within their organization. Signapse AI has partnerships with peerless quality of service and clarity of vision that includes cultural and linguistic ownership of sign by the Deaf Community. Signapse AI provides employment opportunities at Surrey Technology Centre, Guildford, Surrey.
