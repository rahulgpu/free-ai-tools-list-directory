About Lotlinx

Lotlinx is an AI-Powered Automotive Inventory Management Software designed to transform dealership operations. Its aim is to enhance profitability and optimize the turnover of vehicles. The software provides comprehensive solutions of Inventory Turnover, Dealership Profitability, and Data Intelligence. Using machine learning and predictive technology, Lotlinx Sentinel tracks various real-time inventory data and consumer behaviours, helping dealers to manage specific VINs that require attention. Dealers can reduce wasted advertising spend and increase profits through this service.Another part of the product suite is the Lotlinx VIN View Optimizer (VVO) which provides dealers with an accurate view of the visibility and engagement of each VIN, helping identify underexposed and oversaturated units. It also aids in the optimal distribution of campaign spend.The software also features Lotlinx VMX, a tool that utilises real time data to determine which vehicles need promotion, and the perfect timing for such promotion.Lotlinx Showroom is also part of the toolkit offered, transforming dealership pages into mobile-optimized ones to improve customer engagement and conversion, acknowledging the growing number of consumers who shop via mobile devices. Finally, Lotlinx Video enables hyper-targeted inventory advertising customised to specific VINs, improving customer experience while reducing waste in advertising spend. Overall, Lotlinx is about operational control and using data-backed insights to improve dealership performance.
