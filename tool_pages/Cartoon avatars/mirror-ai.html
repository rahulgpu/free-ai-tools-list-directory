About Mirror AI

Mirror AI is an Emoji Maker and AI Avatar Creator app that allows users to create their own personalised cartoon emojis and avatar characters. By leveraging advanced AI facial recognition technology, the tool can generate thousands of unique emojis and stickers based on a single user selfie. The emojis can be shared in various messaging platforms including WhatsApp, Facebook, iMessage, and Telegram, enhancing the personalisation and fun aspects of digital communication. The tool also facilitates creating and sharing custom sticker packs incorporating the generated emojis.Users can further customize the appearance of their emojis, such as hairstyle, clothing, skin tone, and accessories, to create an avatar truly representative of them. Mirror AI also features an extensive library of over 1000 pre-set stickers showcasing the user's avatar in different situations, emotions and meme formats. The app is also supportive of multiple languages, allowing users to express themselves in their native tongue. To enhance the user experience, the emoji keyboard feature can be installed on iOS or Android for easy access to expressive stickers. Mirror AI respects user privacy and ensures the safe storage and encryption of user data. Apart from digital offerings, Mirror AI offers physical merchandise, such as t-shirts and mugs, decorated with their personalised avatars.
