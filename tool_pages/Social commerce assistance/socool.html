About SoCool

SoCool is an AI-based tool designed to assist in the social commerce sector. It architects around the concept of providing a disposable AI assistant, which contributes to various aspects of social commerce. The tool has the capability to interact with potential customers, provide product suggestions based on customer preferences, and manage customer inquiries in real-time. However, to fully utilize its features, JavaScript should be enabled. The core benefit of using SoCool is its disposability each assistant is created for a single interaction, ensuring data privacy and customized conversations for every user. Primarily used in ecommerce platforms, social media selling, or any platform which incorporates the blend of social interaction and commerce, SoCool is versatile with application range. The AI nature of the tool allows it to manage large volumes of requests and queries, thereby increasing efficiency and improving user experience.
