About Uncrop by Stability AI

Clipdrop - Uncrop is an innovative tool designed to help you uncrop and manipulate various image formats. It expertly enhances not just portraits and landscapes, but also textures like fabric, metal, and wood. With the ability to effectively expand the dimensions of your photos, Clipdrop gives you the liberty to breathe new life into your cherished images or artwork with a panoramic effect for an immersive viewing experience. Beyond uncropping, Clipdrop offers a host of other capabilities. The 'Generative Fill' tool allows for the replacement, removal, or fixing of any element in pictures. The 'Universal Resizer' enables you to adjust your images for different social media platforms, while the 'Swap People' feature lets you interchange people within an image. The tool also includes a real-time 'Text-to-Image' generation feature called 'Instant Text to Image', helps in replacing backgrounds and removing unwanted objects or people from your photos. The tool also offers an 'Image Upscaler' feature that enhances the quality of images by upscaling, thereby removing noise and recovering details. You can even turn your sketches into live images instantly using the 'Sketch to Image' feature and generate multiple image variants with 'Reimagine'.
