About Tracecat

Tracecat is an open source, AI-native automation platform specifically designed for security teams. With it, users can build automation workflows, orchestrate alerts, and close cases swiftly. The platform serves as an alternative to services like Tines and XSOAR, providing similar functionality but with the addition of AI-assisted features. Users are able to deploy their first AI-assisted workflow in an estimated time frame of 15 minutes. It features a user-friendly interface accessible via the provided platform link. For further utility and viability, Tracecat is backed by an active community of developers and users around the globe. Support documents and the codebase are readily available on GitHub and their own documentation website. User suggestions and feedback are also encouraged to continuously improve the product. Tracecat offers services while prioritizing data privacy, carefully detailing their privacy policy and terms of services.
