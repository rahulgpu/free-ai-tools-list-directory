About Flowjin

Flowjin is an AI tool dedicated to transforming long videos or audio content into engaging short video clips. This tool is of great utility for content creators, marketers, and podcasters who aim to amplify their content's reach across various social media platforms. It uses AI algorithms to closely analyze your content, grasping in-depth topics, speaker nuances, genres, and styles to curate a distinct story in each clip. The tool automatically applies precise cuts, converts landscape videos into portrait or square formats, and adds stylish fonts for auto captions. Furthermore, Flowjin enhances your clips with auto-generated titles, descriptions, and keyword tags that can also serve as hashtags, custom branding with your logo, allowing for a seamless publishing process. Beyond clipping, it introduces advanced features such as speaker-spot video resizer for precision resizing and framing, AI speaker detection, AI audiogram generator, custom branded templates, and a snippets and shorts engine to generate compelling short clips from 15 to 60 seconds. Meanwhile, the feature for guest-specific clips highlights your special guests and collaborators. Flowjin can also convert your audio-only podcast episodes or online course contents into visually engaging video snippets. Essentially, it aims to increase your visibility, expand your audience, and save your time by simplifying and enhancing the content creation process.
