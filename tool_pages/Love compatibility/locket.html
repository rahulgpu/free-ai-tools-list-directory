About Locket

Locket is an AI-powered application designed to analyze the compatibility between two Instagram profiles. Users can enter any two Instagram handles, with the tool best suited for public profiles, and the system will proceed to perform its analysis. Leveraging AI techniques like data scraping, sentiment analysis, and pattern recognition, the tool quantifies affinity based on shared interests, interaction dynamics, and content alignment within the Instagram platform. Although it is positioned as somewhat of a compatibility or 'love' finder, the application can also be used to perform an assessment of commonalities/similarities between any two Instagram accounts, making it a flexible tool in social media analysis. The application provides a unique, innovative, and entertaining experience for users. It makes use of technology to give insights into social media behaviour dynamics. Please note that the analysis process may take approximately five minutes. Locket was created by Chidwi.
