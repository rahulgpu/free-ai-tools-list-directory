About Buzz Mail

Buzz Mail is an AI-powered tool which enables users to rapidly compose professional responses to email enquiries. This tool, found in Google Workspace, is designed to assist its users in maintaining a professional and timely communication with their customers, which can therewith contribute to increased sales. Utilizing the power of AI, Buzz Mail generates responses to incoming emails with just a click, making it easy and efficient to handle large volumes of customer communications. The tool requires a number of permissions for its functions, including access to manage drafts and send emails, view email messages when the add-on is running, run as a Gmail add-on, connect to an external service, and view user's primary Google account email address and any publicly available personal information. This tool may be suitable for businesses of various sizes and sectors seeking to enhance their email response protocol while simultaneously cutting down on the time invested in drafting responses.
