About EmailTriager

EmailTriager uses artificial intelligence to draft email replies autonomously, thus assisting in speeding up your email correspondence process. It effectively works in the background without necessitating the installation of any external software or browser extension. This tool simply requires you to sign in and indicate the types of emails you wish for it to learn from. Instructions could include past responses to certain emails, as an example, 'support emails' replied to in the last year. You also have the flexibility to exclude specific emails such as those from personal contacts. After completing this process, EmailTriager will start generating draft responses that will appear in your inbox, ready to be reviewed and sent. This can save significant amounts of time and allow users to concentrate on other important tasks. It is particularly beneficial for those who frequently deal with repetitive or similar inquiries through email. The application is designed with a user-friendly interface and maintains privacy and confidentiality of your data.
