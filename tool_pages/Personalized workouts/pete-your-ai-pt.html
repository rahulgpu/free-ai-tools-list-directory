About Pete: Your AI PT

Pete: Your AI PT is an AI-powered personal training mobile application designed to personalize and optimize your workouts according to your unique needs and fitness level. It adapts intelligently to your progress, crafting future workouts based on your current fitness level and goals, providing a customized experience regardless of your workout environment. The app is inclusive, catering to a wide range of users from beginners to advanced athletes. It offers sport-centric training, with workouts tailored to enhance skills and target muscles required for specific sports. This aims to improve overall athletic abilities and performance. For weightlifting enthusiasts, Pete provides scientifically-designed routines based on thousands of workouts and fitness research, and allows for split training to optimize muscle recovery and growth. The application also offers iconic sculpting training programs which are designed to help you achieve specific physiques, drawing inspiration from popular sports icons, influencers, superheroes, and supervillains. These programs focus on body parts and movements that define their iconic looks. Despite its extensive personalized workout experience, the developer does not collect any data from this app, making it a privacy-friendly choice for users.
