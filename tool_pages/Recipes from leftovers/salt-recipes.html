About Salt Recipes

I Have Salt is an AI-powered culinary tool designed to ignite users' cooking imagination and provide unique, personalized culinary experiences. This online tool assists in creating meals by transforming inputs of users' food cravings or available ingredients into detailed instructions for a customized recipe. Users simply start by identifying and entering their food cravings, be it a specific dish like 'garlic butter shrimp', an ingredient, or a broader cuisine type such as 'comforting soups'. Alternatively, users can list their available ingredients and the system will suggest a recipe. The AI is capable of recognizing a variety of culinary desires to suggest recipes that align with users' tastes or available resources. For those undecided on what dish to prepare or how to use their pantry items, the tool generates an instant unique recipe tailored precisely to their needs. The aim is to bring people closer to cooking and deliver the joy of creating a meal that's custom-tailored to their preferences or ingredients at hand.
