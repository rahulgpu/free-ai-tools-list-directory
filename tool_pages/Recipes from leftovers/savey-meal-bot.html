About Savey Meal-Bot

PAK'nSAVE Savey Meal-Bot is an innovative webtool designed to optimise food usage by creating unique recipes from leftovers. The tool is powered by OpenAI's ChatGPT language model, employing its generative AI capabilities to design new meal ideas. Users enter the types of leftover food they have, and the tool generates a recipe for use. This functionality aims to reduce food waste and encourage users to utilize all their available food resources, representing an eco-conscious approach towards meal preparation. However, it should be noted that these recipes are not reviewed by humans; they are AI-generated and should be used as a guide rather than an absolute standard. The tool may not guarantee the accuracy, relevance, or reliability of the recipe content generated. Also, it might not ensure correct portion sizes or a balanced meal. Therefore, users are encouraged to exercise their personal judgement before relying on or making any recipe produced by PAK'nSAVE Savey Meal-Bot.
