About FinFloh

FinFloh is an accounts receivables (AR) automation software designed to streamline AR operations and improve collections efficiency. This AI-powered tool helps in automating multi-channel follow-ups, which are tailored according to the buyer's credit risk and payment behavior. It features a collaborative platform to facilitate closer communication between buyer and seller teams, allowing disputes to be resolved expediently and payments to proceed more swiftly. The tool is also equipped with an automated cash application that enables swift invoice-cash reconciliation, minimizing manual effort and errors. One of its key features is the incorporation of buyer intelligence and data-based credit decisions, which allows for an informed, data-driven approach towards credit decisions and cash flow forecasting. FinFloh is known for easing the integration process with other platforms, creating a centralized data hub, and facilitating seamless management of AR. Additionally, the software ensures data security by maintaining compliance with SOC 2, GDPR and ISO standards.
