About Vyrill

Vyrill is a video intelligence platform aimed at enhancing the marketing strategies of e-commerce businesses. It focuses on analyzing user-generated content (UGC), providing brands with valuable insights into consumer behavior and sentiment. The platform offers various features such as to discover, analyze, and promote UGC to its customers, aiding in boosting engagement, conversions, and e-commerce sales. Furthermore, Vyrill enables brands to categorize their content based on sentiment, brand safety, demographics, engagement, topics, scenes, region, and creator type. It also facilitates launching robust marketing campaigns and licensing shopper-generated videos across e-commerce platforms, social channels, and marketing platforms. With its 'In-Video' search app, it heightens page engagement and accelerates shopping decisions by allowing shoppers to search for product features and key information within videos. Vyrill uses its proprietary AI technology to conduct a deep in-video analysis in various components of the video, including text, speech, and footage, providing comprehensive data on demographics, sentiment, and safety. It serves a diverse range of clients, from agencies and brands to retailers and tech platforms, offering a tool that enhances video marketing processes by leveraging AI technologies.
