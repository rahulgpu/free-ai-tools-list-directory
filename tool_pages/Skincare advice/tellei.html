About Tellei

Tellei is an AI-based beauty care app that provides a personalized skincare experience. The tool uses artificial intelligence to analyze your skin and consequently suggest customized routines and product recommendations that may enhance complexion radiance. The aim is to make the skincare process seamless and efficient for users. Apart from personalized skincare routines, Tellei also presents users with daily skincare tips to help them maintain healthy skin. The platform is designed to empower users in their skincare journey, encouraging self-discovery and self-care. Tellei also seeks to develop a vibrant community where beauty enthusiasts can explore new looks and celebrate their individuality. By using Tellei, users can manage their skincare, receive product recommendations, get notifications and alerts, and access beauty guides tailored to their preferences.
