About KELLS

KELLS Second Opinion Review is a tool that leverages Artificial Intelligence and remote dentists' expertise to provide a comprehensive analysis of a patient's dental condition and treatment plans. Users submit their dental records, such as X-rays and treatment plans, which are then evaluated by an AI system capable of identifying potential dental anomalies that may not be easily detected by human eyes. This initial AI-based review is further refined by remote dentists, improving the overall accuracy. Based on the reviewed data, KELLS gives unbiased, personalized treatment recommendations. The system verifies and confirms conditions like fillings, root canal, crowns, implants, extractions, dentures/partials, and deep cleaning/gum disease. Users will receive a comprehensive, interactive report that includes a status snapshot of cavity and bone health, detailed analysis of surfaced issues, potential actions with estimated pricing, and personalized notes. The aim of this tool is to offer a virtual way for patients to get second opinions on their dental conditions and treatment plans, making the decision-making process more informed and less overwhelming.
