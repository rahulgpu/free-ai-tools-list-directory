About Minerva Copilot

Minerva is a job search extension powered by ChatGPT. Its primary function is to facilitate and accelerate job hunting by automating certain tasks. These include generating personalized cover letters for job applications and automating referral requests. It also aids users in efficiently filling out job applications and generating their resume. In addition, it offers an AI-powered fit score, providing users insight into how well they might suit a particular job. This tool is offered as a Chrome extension and is part of the Workflow & Planning category of AI tools. Users have found it to have various usability levels, with some finding it straightforward while others struggle with its functionalities. Minerva is noted for respecting user privacy, as stated by the developer, committing not to collect, use, or sell user data to third parties or use it for unrelated purposes.
