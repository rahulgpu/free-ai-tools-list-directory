About SidekicAI

SidekicAI is a job application assistant designed to streamline your job application process. It acts as your personal assistant, helping generate cover letters faster and with more precision. The tool requires an input of your existing cover letter and a few skills and then proceeds to generate a customized cover letter for every job you are applying to. The sole requirement from your end is the job description of the role you are applying for. SidekicAI reimagines the cover letters based on the job descriptions, hence making every application unique and tailored. The tool continuously updates its AI to provide more accurate and relevant outputs. It prioritizes user privacy by storing all data locally, reassuring users of their data security. To ensure a hassle-free experience, SidekicAI has customer support readily available. Note that the platform is free to get started.
