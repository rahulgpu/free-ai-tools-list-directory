About Cover Letter AI

Cover Letter AI is a tool designed to assist users in creating professional, personalized cover letters. Leveraging the power of modern large language models, the tool is capable of generating cover letters in any language within a few minutes. Using Cover Letter AI, users upload or paste their CV, which is then converted into text. They then fill in initial information, such as the desired job description, company name, writing style, and the skills that the cover letter should highlight. The tool generates multiple versions of the cover letter that can be iterated and edited until the user is satisfied with the final result. This process significantly shortens the time traditionally spent crafting cover letters, while also ensuring they are tailored to the user's needs and the job at hand. Moreover, the tool ensures the privacy of users' data including job descriptions and company names, as this data is not stored on the platform's servers. Cover Letter AI, thus, provides a beneficial resource for job seekers, facilitating efficient and effective cover letter creation, across varying writing styles and languages.
