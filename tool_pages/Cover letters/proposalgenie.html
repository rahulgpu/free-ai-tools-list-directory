About Proposalgenie

Proposal Genie is an AI-powered platform that helps freelancers and small businesses quickly and easily create tailored proposals for job postings on Upwork. With Proposal Genie, users can enter job requirements and personal profile information, and Proposal Genie will automatically generate a proposal tailored to the job and user profile. Proposal Genie also includes a comprehensive dashboard, expert feedback, advanced AI algorithms, customizable templates, and a comprehensive database of skills and experience, all of which make it easy to create high-quality proposals in just minutes. With Proposal Genie, freelancers can save time, stand out from the competition, and increase their chances of getting hired.
