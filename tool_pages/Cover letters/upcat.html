About Upcat

UpCat - Upwork Assistant is an AI-powered tool aimed at enhancing the job search experience on the platform Upwork. The tool is designed to assist users in creating personalized proposals, thereby saving users from the task of prolonged writing typical to cover letters. Along with this, the software also offers real-time alerts to ensure users do not miss any relevant job opportunities. This AI tool is accessible in the form of a browser extension for easy integration and usability. Being a browser extension, users can add it to multiple browsers such as Chrome, Brave, Edge, and Opera. Trusted by a substantial number of Upwork users, it acts as a reliable job search and applicant tool delivering efficient results to both freelancers and employers on Upwork. It combines AI capabilities with an understanding of user preferences and requirements to automate parts of the job search process on Upwork, providing the user a simpler and smarter approach to finding opportunities.
