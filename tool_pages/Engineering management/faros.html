About Faros

Faros AI is an Artificial Intelligence (AI)-guided platform developed for Engineering Operations. It serves as an infrastructure tool for these operations, providing an integrated view across multiple performance indicators such as velocity, quality, and goals. The platform utilizes AI technology to enhance the clarity and efficiency of engineering operations and initiatives.Faros AI enables measurement of quality and productivity in fresh ways by creating a unified platform for operational data. This allows for more informed decision-making based on data rather than relying solely on intuition, thus improving resource utilization and effectiveness. It also allows users to connect their engineering data sources to Faros AI for a comprehensive overview of operations. The platform supports data ingestion, transformation, and visualization and is API-driven, making it extendable at each level.For different leaders and stakeholders, Faros AI provides various insights which include: identifying systemic issues affecting software delivery, measuring team health and tech debt, tracking progress towards goals, and spotting dependencies and bottlenecks in processes. Faros AI also allows for the creation of custom charts and dashboards if pre-exisiting modules do not fulfill an organization's needs.Faros AI is compatible with over 70 integrations, enhancing its adaptability and making it a one-stop-shop for engineering operations. These integrations help the platform to function seamlessly within a range of different technological ecosystems.
