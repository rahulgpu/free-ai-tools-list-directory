About Omni – Novel Translator

Omni is an advanced translation tool specifically designed for Asian novels. The tool functions by having users simply insert the link to the novel chapter they desire to translate. With its unique capabilities, Omni strives to deliver translation quality that is notably superior compared to conventional translation tools. The service integrates state-of-the-art, custom-trained Language Model (LLM) models to ensure a high-quality translation. This process yields translation outcomes that are designed to be highly comprehensible and enjoyable to end users. Omni also focuses on customization and consistency of terminologies throughout translations. It introduces an automatic detection system to maintain unity in terminologies across translations. Furthermore, Omni provides a platform for users to customize terminologies based on their preferences. Omni's free-to-use model offers a swift and efficient alternative to slow human translations, thereby ensuring a more enjoyable reading experience. The tool aims to remove the inconveniences of faulty machine translations and long human translation wait times. It is an optimal choice for readers seeking a seamless, quick, and high-quality translation experience. It is compatible across different devices, providing readers with further flexibility and reading convenience.
