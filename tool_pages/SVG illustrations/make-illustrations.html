About Make Illustrations

MakeIllustrations is an AI tool that generates vector illustrations for digital content on various platforms. Using a prompt and selected style, users can produce consistent, customizable illustrations in minutes. The generated illustrations are editable for custom tweaks, including color changes, and other modifications via an integrated editor. Additionally, a handy background removal feature is included to help users finalize their creative projects. The tool supports a 'Save & Export' functionality for storing creations in user's library or exporting in SVG/PNG format. Illustrations created with this tool are adaptable across different mediums like websites, apps, social media, and various other marketing touchpoints. Unlike traditional methods like stock images or manually created illustrations, MakeIllustrations provides an affordable and efficient alternative. It offers options for different illustration styles like Notion, Structured, and Humanist amongst others. The 'Your Generations' feature allows for access to a history of user's creations. All generated illustrations remain private, with privacy assured via row-level- security for backend databases. A variety of illustrations generated by the user community can be explored in the tool's showcase section. The tool offers a user-friendly refund policy to ensure satisfaction.
