About Vecentor

Vecentor is an AI-powered tool built for generating Scalable Vector Graphics (SVGs). Its main feature is enabling users to create coherent, easily editable SVG images for their design or frontend projects. Leveraging the capacity of both diffusion models and large language models, Vecentor aims to produce fine, inventive SVGs. It provides six default styles, offering users a range of options to choose from to suit their individual requirements. One of the primary strengths of Vecentor's generated SVGs is their editability. They are open-coded, meaning they can be modified in popular vector software like Illustrator or Inkscape, or even directly in the code. This feature makes Vecentor a versatile tool, catering both to designers who wish to work visually and developers who prefer to modify their graphics in code. In addition, the SVGs generated are 'code-ready', implying they can be readily used in a users coding projects. Overall, Vecentor aims to offer a creative solution for generating vector images that can enhance ones workflow.
