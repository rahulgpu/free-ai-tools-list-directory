About AI Toolbox for innovators

The AI Toolbox for Innovators is designed to enhance the process of idea generation and validation. Its purpose extends to providing help in testing, pivoting, or creating ideas promptly with a range of specialized free AI tools. The toolbox is currently being upgraded to include the latest advancements in AI and continues to be available for use in the interim. Tools included are the 'Brainstorm Buddy', which aids in sparking new ideas; the 'How might we statement' generator for providing multiple unique statements for each problem; the 'Persona Generator' for deeper understanding of target audiences; the 'Future Scenario Writer' for predicting possible industry futures; the 'Interview Script' for acquiring valuable insights through interview transcripts; the 'Research Brief' for clear and concise research on broad questions or topics; the 'Opportunity Scores' to identify and prioritize solutions to various problems; and the 'Problem Understanding Card' for gaining insights into specific issues. It should be noted that the system offers notifications on AI and innovation updates upon subscription.
