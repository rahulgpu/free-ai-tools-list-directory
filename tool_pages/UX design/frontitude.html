About Frontitude

This AI tool is designed to help design teams create engaging and consistent user experience (UX) content right within Figma, thereby streamlining the design and content creation process. It functions as a content co-pilot within Figma by providing content guidelines and copy suggestions that align with world-class products, instantly accessible to the user. This tool is particularly handy in eliminating the hassle of adhering to content guidelines manually, as it integrates these guidelines into your design system, thus enabling uniform writing. It also simplifies the design review process by embedding content guidelines into design components for quick scanning of content violations, thereby reducing review cycles. The UX Writing Assistant couples with the Frontitude platform to aid teams to create guidelines that connect to their design system, fostering further consistency in writing. This tool is lauded by product and content designers for its assistance in generating ideas and concise copy for complex topics, integrating seamlessly with Figma, boosting workflow speeds particularly in sprint tasks, and helping maintain a consistent voice-of-product in UX writing.
