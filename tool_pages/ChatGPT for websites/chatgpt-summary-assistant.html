About ChatGPT Summary Assistant

ChatGPT Summary Assistant is a free Chrome browser extension designed to help users summarize any web page, saving time on reading and learning. Equipped with the ability to support various AI models including GPT 3.5, GPT-4, and other models, it leverages cutting-edge technology to transform lengthy articles into concise summaries with just a few clicks. It not only supports official OpenAI APIs, including GPT-3.5-Turbo and GPT-3 but also permits manual activation of GPT-4 functionality. Among its range of features, it includes the ability to generate key point summaries for articles, customize ChatGPT prompts, support OpenAI API Key login, and provide sample prompts such as summarizing articles using key points or specified word counts, translating text into Spanish, simplifying text for children, and generating titles based on content. It is presented as a highly valuable tool for anyone engaged in studying, researching, or working, and needs to summarize web information quickly and efficiently.
