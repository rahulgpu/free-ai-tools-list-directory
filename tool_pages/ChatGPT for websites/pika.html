About Pika

Pika is a tool that enables users to turn their knowledge base into an AI chatbot, by learning from the content provided. Using advanced AI technology, exhibited notably by its GPT-4 driven abilities, Pika provides AI-powered natural language searching, facilitating easy and intuitive accessibility to complex knowledge bases. Its clients range from large corporations to small startups due to its flexible functionality. Pika can be used for internal chat systems, shareable chats, and chatbots, created from user content.The process of creating a personalized chatbot involves uploading files (such as PDFs or website URLs) from which the AI chatbot learns. This functionality enables the chatbot to answer a variety of questions about the user's product based on the content it has analyzed. There are customization options available, from setting the profile picture and chat themes to adjusting the length and format of the chatbot's responses.A major advantage of Pika is its swift efficiency; chatbots are set up in less than a minute after the information is provided. Moreover, Pika provides the ability to review chatlogs, providing insights into customer preferences and interactions. This could be crucial for businesses in understanding their customer needs better. While tailored for one-to-one interaction, Pika's chatbots are also inclusive towards mass engagement and are embeddable on limitless websites. These features make it widely compatible with popular websites and convenient for various sectors, be it an e-commerce website or a government portal. The pricing offers flexibility with various plans tailored to meet the needs of a range of businesses.
