About UseChat

UseChat.ai is a tool for creating custom GPT-3.5 trained chatbots that can be embedded on websites or applications. The platform's chatbots are trained on the content of a given website, enabling them to provide instant customer support intimately familiar with a business's products or services. Chatbots can be trained in minutes by simply providing UseChat.ai with a website URL. Training involves the platform crawling and scraping all site content, which it then uses to teach the chatbot to answer user queries based on the information. The chatbots can be tailored to match a brand's aesthetic and are capable of escalating complex queries to human customer support. User interactions with the chatbot are recorded and can be viewed in real time or later. Different subscription plans allow for use on a varying scale based on messaging needs and webpage volume. For custom requirements or specific features, a custom plan can be structured. UseChat.ai is a London-based business, created by SaaS founder Damien Tanner.
