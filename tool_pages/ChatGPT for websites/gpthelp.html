About GPTHelp

GPTHelp.ai is an AI-powered chatbot platform designed for direct integration with your website content. It provides an automated support system for your visitors by answering their questions based on the training it receives from the content of your website, files, and FAQs. Creating your own chatbot is simplified as it requires you to input your website URL and lets the AI carry out the rest. GPTHelp.ai is fully customizable; you can edit the design, including colors, size, icons, and you can also modify the AI's behavior. You can choose a name, assign a role, and select its attitude to align with your specific needs. Additionally, it provides a feature for monitoring the chatbot's conversations helping to evaluate its performance. You can view the chat history, take over conversations and alter the AI's responses if necessary. Once your website's address is entered, GPTHelp automatically fetches all the pages on your website and trains the AI chatbot on this content, creating a unique customer service tool specific to your website.
