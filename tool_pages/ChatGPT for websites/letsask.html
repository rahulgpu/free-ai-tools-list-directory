About LetsAsk

LetsAsk.AI is an AI chatbot builder powered by OpenAI's ChatGPT technology. The platform enables the creation and integration of conversational chatbots into websites or applications without the need for web programming expertise. The platform's primary function is to convert text, files, or a website into a chatbot capable of engaging with customers and answering their queries based on the input data. The AI is able to interpret data in about 95 languages, and it adapts to respond in the language in which the question is asked. Other features include the ability to customize the chatbot to align with a brand voice and usage scenarios, and to insert it into specific locations on a website. LetsAsk.AI ensures strong security measures, with processed files deleted after use and raw text stored with encryption. It adheres to GDPR regulations, ensuring user data privacy and control. It integrates natively with Discord and can be integrated with other platforms using Zapier. The platform offers multiple subscription plans, each designed to cater to unique needs, from individual creators to large businesses.
