About SiteSpeakAI

SiteSpeakAI is an AI tool that provides a custom trained chatbot for websites, powered by ChatGPT. The chatbot is designed to answer questions about the products and services offered by businesses in real-time, 24/7. SiteSpeakAI offers the ability to train the chatbot using custom data and PDFs from the website. The tool offers several features to enhance the chatbot's functionality. The chatbot can be trained and retrained to stay up-to-date with the latest information from the website. It is also customizable, allowing businesses to match the chatbot's colors, style, and voice to their brand. Installing the ChatGPT powered chatbot is made easy with SiteSpeakAI, as it requires only a single line of code and does not need any plugins or extensions. SiteSpeakAI provides detailed analytics on visitor interactions with the chatbot, empowering businesses to improve their lead conversion rate. Overall, SiteSpeakAI aims to enhance customer engagement, generate leads, and boost sales for websites. It is relied upon by over 50 customers, and it is trusted by websites serving millions of users. For installation purposes, the tool offers step-by-step instructions on integrating the chatbot into various platforms, including HTML, Shopify, Notion, WordPress, Carrd, Bubble, and Webflow. Support is available through the tool's help center, where businesses can also find resources on creating a chatbot, training ChatGPT with their own data, and contacting the SiteSpeakAI team.
