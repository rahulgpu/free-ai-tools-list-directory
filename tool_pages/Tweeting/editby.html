About Editby

Editby is an AI tool designed to streamline the creation of high-quality, SEO-optimized content while preserving the distinctive style of a brand. This tool targets the challenges of gaining organic traffic by enabling the production of content tailored for high SERP rankings. Editby is intended to be used as a facilitated instrument, not a replacement for the writer, ensuring unique, personalized content. It involves the utilization of custom templates to uphold a consistent output. In addition, it fosters the integration of an existing content spectrum. The utility provides a unified platform for SEO and content creation. It provides suggestions on short-tail and long-tail keywords for Google EEAT-optimized content, as well as real-time SERP analysis to monitor the position of your companys keywords. Editby also allows the creation of rich content by sourcing information from various forms of media including blog posts, videos, and papers. Furthermore, it includes features to maintain consistency across all content formats, while highlighting a brand's unique styling. AI-powered recommendations on content performance are provided, as well as suggestions for trending content based on Google and Youtube searches. It has anti-plagiarism properties to avoid content duplication and the integration of respective writing style. The capability to detect existing links and automatically add citations within your content is incorporated. For content output, Editby supports publishing on various platforms such as Wordpress and Ghost and allows exportation as a DOCx file. The tool ensures the privacy and safety of user data.
