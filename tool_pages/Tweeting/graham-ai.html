About Graham AI

Graham.AI is a tech tweet generator created by Orel Biton and Ariel Verber. It is a tool designed to help tech influencers create tweets quickly and accurately. The tool uses AI to generate tweets based on a chosen topic. It offers a wide range of topics from Elon Musk buying Twitter to AI taking over, as well as topics specific to tech influencers such as co-founders, raising money, and startup ideas. The tool is able to generate tweets that are relevant to the topic and that sound like they were written by a tech influencer. The tool also offers the option to contact the creators and to support them with a donation. Graham.AI provides an easy to use and effective solution for tech influencers who are looking to create engaging tweets quickly and accurately.
