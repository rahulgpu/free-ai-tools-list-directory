About Otto – AI biographer

Otto is an AI-powered tool designed to act as your personal biographer. It facilitates storytelling and knowledge sharing through conversations. Users simply start talking to Otto and the system requires no notes or extra preparations to operate. During these conversations, Otto transcribes the discussions swiftly, allowing for a smooth and stress-free experience that feels akin to talking to a non-judgmental friend. Subsequently, these transcriptions are processed into concise sessions, each of which is designed to extend only to short 10-minute intervals. This means users don't need to set aside large amounts of time to share their stories. After each conversation session, Otto creates draft chapters that users can review, ensuring that information is accurately captured. The finalized product is a professionally written biography or the narrative of a business enterprise. This tool continues to update your story with new sessions and can capture fresh insights and dimensions about you or your business. Overall, Otto combines the tasks of a personal interview, transcription, and professional biography writing into one accessible platform.
