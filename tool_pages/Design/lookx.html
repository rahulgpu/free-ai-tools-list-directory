About LookX

LookX is an AI-integrated platform developed by a cross-disciplinary group of architects, designers, and techies. It aims to provide AI-based solutions and products specifically designed for creative industries, including graphic design, industrial design, and construction.The platform offers a range of features and tools that cater to different creative needs. For architecture design and interior design, LookX provides a variety of tools such as Train Custom Models, which allows users to train AI models by uploading images with similar styles and generate images matching their desired styles. Additionally, the platform offers a Multi-Platform Plugin, enabling users to integrate LookX AI into software like SketchUp and Rhino for an enhanced creative process.Other features include the Style Adapter, which enables users to effortlessly craft images in their preferred style by providing a reference image. The Upscale Image feature allows users to enhance image quality and details, while the 1-Click Duplication feature helps generate images with similar effects to those found in the Creations Plaza, providing insights into how other users achieved their results.LookX also incorporates an extensive library of architectural terminology through its Vocab Template feature, assisting users in crafting high-quality prompts for their creative projects. Furthermore, the Prompt Assistant automatically expands specific prompts into detailed paragraphs, enhancing the quality and depth of creative ideas.Testimonials from industry professionals highlight the platform's potential to revolutionize architectural design through its remarkable advancements in AI tools.Overall, LookX is an AI platform designed to empower architects and designers by providing them with AI-integrated features and tools tailored to their creative needs.
