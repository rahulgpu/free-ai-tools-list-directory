About Brainwashd

Brainwashd is an Artificial Intelligence (AI) tool accessible as a Google Chrome Extension. This tool is designed to analyze the context of tweets and news articles in real-time with the goal of identifying misinformation. Users can employ brainwashd to scrutinize tweets or news content simply by dropping the corresponding link into the tool. Once the tool completes the analysis, it prompts outcomes such as potential biases, inaccuracies, and lack of context in the selected content. This gives users deeper insights into the credibility and accuracy of the information present in the tweets or news pieces they come across. Furthermore, the tool facilitates sharing the analysis report on Twitter. Brainwashd aims to help users better understand the context of the content they encounter online and combat misinformation, therefore promoting the spread of genuine and reliable information. The application is adaptable for both the Chrome extension and the web application. The user not mandatorily needs to have an OpenAI API Key to have access to this tool.
