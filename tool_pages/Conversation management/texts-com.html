About Texts.com

Texts is a multidimensional messaging app designed to consolidate messages from all substantial messaging platforms into one unified inbox. This tool supports messaging platforms such as iMessage, WhatsApp, Telegram, Signal, MessengerX, Instagram, LinkedIn, Slack, and Discord DMs. With a focus on privacy, Texts ensures that messages are sent directly to the respective platforms and never touch the Texts servers. In terms of functionality, Texts offers features such as chat summaries, which provide automated summaries of ongoing conversations, and draft responses powered by ChatGPT. It also offers a translation feature to translate user responses into their preferred language. Additional productivity functions include a comprehensive message search feature, message archiving, and setting chats as unread by default until a response is given. Noteworthy elements like Stealth Mode enable users to read messages without notifying the sender, a scheduling option for sending messages, a 'Snooze Messages' feature, customizable UI, and dark & light modes are part of Texts. The app operates on a monthly subscription model and does not use customer data to generate profit.
