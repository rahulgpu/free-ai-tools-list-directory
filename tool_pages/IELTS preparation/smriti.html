About Smriti

Smriti is an AI-powered tool specifically designed to evaluate IELTS Writing Task 2 essays. Its main function is to facilitate immediate and precise grading based on key IELTS criteria such as response to the task, lexical resources, coherence, cohesion, and grammatical range and accuracy. Following an assessment, Smriti provides a detailed IELTS Band score along with feedback on the user's performance. This instant feedback assists in identifying the areas of strength and improvement for the user, leading to noticeable enhancements in writing skills and boosting their confidence. The AI algorithms employed by Smriti are continually refined to ensure accurate evaluations, aligning with the standards set by IELTS. It is highly endorsed by IELTS experts and has been found to be a reliable and insightful aid in improving IELTS writing scores by numerous students. Moreover, it has the capacity to review handwritten essays, expanding its flexibility. Smriti's evaluations offer valuable insights, helping students to understand their weaknesses and enhance their writing skills for the IELTS exam.
