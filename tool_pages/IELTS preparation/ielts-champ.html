About IELTS CHAMP

IELTS CHAMP is an AI-powered tool designed to aid users in preparing for the IELTS exam, with a particular focus on the Writing and Speaking sections. Utilizing mock exams, IELTS CHAMP aims to mimic the authentic IELTS exam structure and conditions, offering real-time grading and personalized feedback based on user responses. This feedback is tailored to highlight areas where improvement is needed, supplemented by an IELTS band score for criteria-related performance. The tool offers practice sessions which include an extensive library of IELTS Academic and General Training Writing Task 1 & 2 questions, and timed tasks to replicate real exams. The speaking practice is structured into three parts, akin to the real examination, enabling users to record and playback responses. The AI integration ensures instant, detailed, and accurate feedback, making it a conducive environment for self-study. IELTS CHAMP is accessible online and allows potential users to commence with a free trial.
