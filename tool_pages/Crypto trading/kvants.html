About Kvants

Kvants AI is a decentralized asset management platform that provides access to AI-driven quantitative trading strategies developed by leading hedge funds. Aimed at both regular and crypto investors, users can analyze, select, and invest in strategies directly through the platform. These strategies integrate artificial intelligence interpretation models into rational trading models, which helps inform and support better investment decisions. Users can connect their Centralized Finance (CeFi) exchange to the Kvants Plus+ App via API. Kvants AI also offers non-custodial DeFi and Omni Chain Quant Vaults that use Quant strategies. The platform highlights its focus on due diligence concerning the funds it offers and asserts that it verifies the reported trading returns of each AI-driven Quant Strategy before listing them. Users can also access a Robo Advisor for recommendations on suitable trading strategies based on their individual risk appetite and return expectations.
