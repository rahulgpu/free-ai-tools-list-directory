About Fama One

Fama.one is an AI powered crypto investment platform that allows users to access cutting-edge machine learning algorithms to automatically trade popular crypto assets. With Fama, users can select their risk tolerance and will be provided with over 30 data points about the performance of the models in real time. It is non-custodial and users control their funds completely from their wallet. Fama has generated returns between 2020-2023 when tested against newly seen historical data. However, past performance does not guarantee future returns and users should be aware that their capital is at risk. Fama provides access to its models in an open and secure way and makes investing easy for everyone, regardless of their experience and capital level.
