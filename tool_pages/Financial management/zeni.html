About Zeni

Zeni is an all-in-one financial operations platform developed with the purpose of simplifying and optimizing finance-related tasks for startups. It combines artificial intelligence and real finance professionals to efficiently manage various financial operations including bookkeeping, bill payments, and reimbursements. Further, it provides fractional CFO services and support for annual startup tax procedures. By implementing Zeni, startups can gain clear and impactful insights into their financial performance, assisting in decision-making processes. The platform delivers real-time updates on a spectrum of key financial metrics enabling startups to identify and respond to trends that could impact their operations. The AI technology implemented by Zeni is designed not only to handle manual, tedious aspects of bookkeeping, but also to build automation around various processes. This allows their team of finance experts to concentrate on providing strategic guidance on financial planning, budgeting, taxes, and investor relations. Zeni's platform is also effective in providing services to CFOs, finance leaders, COOs, operations leaders and VC firms, offering them valuable financial insights and guidance.
