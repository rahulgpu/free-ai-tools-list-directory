About Slayed

Slayed.ai is an AI-powered financial management tool designed to streamline business financial processes. This tool caters primarily to operatives in freelance, contracting, and small business industries. Its suite of features includes Invoicing, Payments, Estimates, Expenses, and Reports. The intuitive invoicing tools enable businesses to create and track professional-grade invoices, and directly send them to clients via email or shareable links. The payment solutions accept a variety of payment methods, provide instant notifications for successful payments, and simplify the transaction reconciliation process. The software also assists in creating professional estimates swiftly, allowing easy conversion into invoices, and efficient follow-up with clients. Slayed.ai's expense tracking feature helps businesses to capture and categorize expenses, and even produce insightful reports. Furthermore, it provides comprehensive reporting tools to track income, expenses, and profitability, along with custom reports to identify potential areas of improvement. Slayed.ai leverages automation to facilitate the scheduling, dispatching, invoicing, and payment collection processes, fostering greater business efficiency. It also supports mobile accessibility, allowing users to manage operations from anywhere. Although Slayed.ai is currently in beta, it shows great promise as a tool that can enhance business productivity by simplifying financial management.
