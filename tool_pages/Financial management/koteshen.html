About Koteshen

Koteshen is a financial tool that automates billing management for businesses. It prioritizes the elimination of invoicing stress and offers businesses the ability to get paid in a timely manner, while also enhancing financial clarity. With Koteshen, businesses can cease relying on cumbersome spreadsheets. The system allows users to create and send beautiful invoices and estimates. It also offers resources for automating finances with just a few clicks. Koteshen's features include the automatic conversion of accepted estimates into payable invoices, saving time on billing long-term clients with recurring invoices, and the provision of important notifications regarding the status of an invoice (opened, viewed, paid, ignored). This assists in streamlining what could otherwise be tedious manual tasks. The software also includes built-in tools for improved financial reporting by permitting easy export of data into Excel for further analysis. It additionally ensures that receipts are organized in one place for efficient record-keeping. The tool offers simplified, tier-based pricing to cater to a spectrum of businesses, from startups to enterprises, with varying degrees of advanced features.
