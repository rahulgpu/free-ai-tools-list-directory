About Candle GPT

Candle GPT is an AI tool that interfaces with the Candle Finance application to provide users with a robust platform for managing their financial accounts. Using the power of ChatGPT, it enables users to view account balances, organize spending, place orders and transfers, and perform a variety of financial tasks simply by asking. With an extensive list of supported financial services from Robinhood to Bank of America to Paypal, Candle GPT provides a user-friendly way to monitor and manage finances across multiple platforms all in one place. Security is a central concern for Candle GPT. When connecting a financial account to Candle, user credentials remain on the user's device and are never shared. Users can control the level of access and permissions given to ChatGPT for their Candle account. Included in its features is the ability to revoke ChatGPT's access to the Candle account and linked financial accounts at any time. Despite its multifaceted functionalities, getting started is straightforward - users can download the Candle app on their iOS or Android device, link their financial accounts, and begin using the chat function to manage their finances. Candle GPT seeks to simplify financial management by combining the convenience of AI chat assistance with rigorous security measures.
