About Fiskl

Fiskl is an artificial intelligence (AI) powered accounting tool designed to assist small businesses, startups, and freelancers in managing their finances. It offers a broad range of functionalities, including multi-currency invoicing, payments, automated synchronization with banks around the world, tax tracking, and more. Fiskl presents a real-time cash flow and financial overview, providing users with complete financial control. Its effortless bank sync feature automates the accounting process, while it allows users to take payments globally, meaning clients can pay in any currency simplifying the process of dealing with multiple currencies. Another significant feature is its AI-powered reconciliation, reducing effort in reconciling the books. Besides, Fiskl incorporates integrations across major financial platforms for seamless financial management. Also, it supports localized pricing in over 200 countries, making it a global solution that streamlines finance across continents. The tool offers automation and team permissions, making it easier for teams to manage their time, banking, expenses and other pertinent financial activities. The digital platform also acknowledges the future of accounting's reliance on AI and caters to this need with its innovative features.
