About Qashboard

Qashboard is an AI tool designed to provide users with a comprehensive, bird's eye view of their finances. The tool allows users to connect all of their financial accounts, including debit, brokerage, investment, and credit card transactions, to analyze and track their finances in one intuitive dashboard. One of its key features is the ability to track recurring transactions to help identify trends and avoid unexpected charges. Qashboard also offers an interactive Q&A conversational interface named AskWallet, providing actionable insights and answers to financial queries such as income, affordabilities, and tax deductions. For data and privacy, Qashboard allows users to supply their own database, ensuring no access or storage on their end. The user's data remains unseen by Qashboard and they have full control over their data including editing, deleting, and exporting. Qashboard supports different plans offering various features such as unlimited chats, account connections, and access to community components, among other features. Lastly, Qashboard intends to roll out a GPT plugin for more advanced chat operations. It is suitable for individuals looking to organize, streamline, and understand their finances better.
