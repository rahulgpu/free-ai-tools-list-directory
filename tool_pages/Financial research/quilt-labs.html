About Quilt Labs

Quilt Labs offers an integrated AI platform aimed at simplifying financial research. The platform provides access to extensive financial data from various sources such as SEC filings, live newswires, and company presentations. It allows users to uncover facts and trends and extract relevant data using advanced keyword search capabilities and word tense handling. With features like AI-powered conversion of language into tables and a guidance locator to quickly spot forward-looking statements in transcripts, Quilt helps users understand trends and build models with ease. The platform also offers unique features such as Factual Generative AI, which is always grounded with sources, KPI Detector to identify KPIs from any financial document, and AI Agents that can plan and execute complex workflows on top of your data. It prioritizes security, permitting use of all features securely within your datarooms. Quilt Labs is adaptable, serving a broad range of users, including mutual funds, multi-strategy firms, sellside to buyside, business development to independent researchers.
