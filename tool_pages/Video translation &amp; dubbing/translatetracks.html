About TranslateTracks

TranslateTracks is an AI-powered dubbing and video translation service aimed at increasing global audience engagement. The platform offers high-quality, expert-verified dubbing and translation services designed to be cost-effective and accurate. It utilizes proprietary best in class AI models, supplemented by a team of expert localization professionals. This creates high-quality dubbed content that is said to be indistinguishable from human dubbing. The typical process includes creators providing original content, which the TranslateTracks team, in combination with their AI tool, transcribes, translates, and dubs. The platform grants users access to customize the resulting subtitles and dubs. TranslateTracks also offers a unique feature for YouTube content creators, enabling them to create multi-language audio tracks, thus removing language barriers. They additionally provide complete video translation services, including dubbed and screen translated content that is synchronized for realistic lip-action on video. Along with dubbed audio tracks, users receive access to the platform for tweaks and original as well as translated subtitles. With TranslateTracks, users are thus equipped to reach a global audience, overcoming language and cultural barriers.
