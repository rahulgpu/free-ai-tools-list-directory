About Titan

Word Titan is a GPT-3 Word Add-In for Microsoft Word that allows users to access the capabilities of GPT-3 to improve their writing with just a few clicks. It integrates with GPT-3, allowing users to adjust the temperature and simplify, rewrite, and explain paragraphs using pre-defined and custom prompts. Installation is easy and fast, and the add-in is suitable for professionals, lawyers, accountants, consultants, and anyone with a PC with Microsoft Word. It includes a 7-day, no questions asked money-back guarantee. The add-in can save users an average of minutes per month, and a portion of each purchase is donated to an e-learning hub in Aboriginal Communities. This gives young Aboriginal people access to learning hubs and free public Wi-Fi for communications and conducting necessary everyday business, helping to reduce linguistic, geographic and cultural isolation.
