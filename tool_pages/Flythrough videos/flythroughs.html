About Flythroughs

Flythroughs by Luma AI is an intuitive app that allows users to create professional-grade Flythroughs using AI technology with just their iPhone. This tool aims to eliminate the need for expensive drones and outdated equipment, providing a more accessible and cost-effective solution. By utilizing breakthrough NeRF and 3D generative AI developed by Luma Labs, Flythroughs enables the easy generation of cinematic 3D experiences without any special training or equipment.Flythroughs by Luma AI offers users the ability to showcase their spaces, whether it's a property listing, an architectural design, or any other environment that users want to present in an engaging and immersive way. This tool helps create a mental picture for viewers, giving them a better understanding of the space's flow and unique features.One of the key advantages of Flythroughs is that it eliminates the need for location-based capturing. Users can create walkthrough floorplans right from the comfort of their own homes, avoiding scheduling headaches and saving time.With Flythroughs by Luma AI, users can capture videos, add details, and generate stunningly realistic and lifelike 3D experiences in a matter of minutes. The app integrates a 3D camera path AI, which enables one-touch generation of photorealistic Flythroughs.Users can also explore the collection of Flythroughs created by others, offering inspiration and showcasing the capabilities of this AI-powered tool.Overall, Flythroughs by Luma AI provides an accessible and efficient way to create high-quality Flythroughs without the need for expensive equipment, making it a valuable tool for various industries such as real estate, design, and architecture.
