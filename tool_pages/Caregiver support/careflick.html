About CareFlick

CareFlick is an AI-enabled platform designed to facilitate elder care and team management in senior care businesses. With its integration with assisted living technology, CareFlick presents a suite of features designed to simplify operations, enhance care quality and facilitate team coordination. One of the standout features of the platform is Yana, an AI co-pilot that can facilitate communication in multiple languages. Users can log daily care routines using the platform's intuitive system, which in turn provides smart insights into the progress of care receivers. CareFlick also offers functionalities for seamless team coordination, enabling responsibilities to be shared, updates to be provided, and collaborative work to be pursued effortlessly. The platform also facilitates learning through its provision of bite-sized insights backed by latest research and best practices in caregiving. CareFlick comes with capabilities for secure care management, including EHR, HIPAA compliant care documentation, and data sharing among all stakeholders in a senior care company. It aims at minimizing medication errors, paperwork inefficiencies, and administrative duties, while improving the quality and continuity of care. Users can effortlessly add a custom number of care receivers and onboard their care teams, while tracking health and wellbeing through daily logs. CareFlick also offers task management, medication management, customizable condition assessments, care reports and analytics.
