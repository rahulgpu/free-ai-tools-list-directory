About ATTITUDE – AI Dating Assistant

ATTITUDE is an AI-powered dating assistant designed to help enhance dating interaction through its technology that crafts perfect responses. The tool aims to facilitate the start of new connections, deepen existing relationships, and refresh conversations that might have stalled. Users need to upload a screenshot of a text message conversation, and the tool suggests the best reply, which users can then send. This functionality can potentially augment a user's attractiveness in a conversation, potentially leading to better dating outcomes. As a feature, the AI-provided responses are unlimited, highlighting the tool's versatility in various conversation scenarios. ATTITUDE also prides itself on the ability of the AI to provide smooth conversation lines, offering an ad-free user experience. This tool requires a subscription for access to its key features. Privacy is also a key emphasis, and according to the developers, while some data may be collected, it is not linked to user's identity. The application is available in English and requires iOS 12.0 or later for iPhone, iPadOS 12.0 for iPad, and iOS 12.0 for iPod touch.
