About ImageCreator

ImageCreator, a professional Generative-AI plugin for Photoshop, enables you to create digital art quickly and efficiently. Its comprehensive features include the high-quality generation of images, detailed selections, and the prompt editing of provided inputs. The software is known for its support of SDXL 1.0, a powerful tool for image generation and manipulation. Three primary methods for image generation can be utilized: TXT2IMG, IMG2IMG, and FILL. Users can further tailor their images with various models and parameters, demonstrating the extent of the software's flexibility. ImageCreator also includes ControlNet, supporting multiple control models and process settings for creating images with various styles and details. In addition, the software allows users to customize models and directly use them in the Photoshop plugin through its ModelHub feature. ImageCreator encourages exploratory creative projects with its multitude of models for unique digital art creations. Its user-friendly installation and support system add to its ease of use and accessibility. By integrating seamlessly with Photoshop, ImageCreator allows users to reap the benefits of AI image generation without requiring a separate platform or additional resources. Its robust features and flexible design provide a comprehensive tool for digital artists to enhance their workflow and explore new creative possibilities.
