About ToWords

ToWords is an AI tool that converts YouTube videos into written content. The tool is designed to help users transcribe various types of videos such as news pieces, meetings, interviews, or podcasts. ToWords is also capable of processing audio content from Zoom or Google meetings, audiobooks, and YouTube shorts. This functionality caters to different user needs, from generating SEO friendly content to creating transcripts for accessibility purposes. The tool features a vast language support, including Afrikaans, Arabic, Welsh, Danish, German, English, Spanish, Farsi, French, Irish, Scottish Gaelic, Hebrew, Hindi, Indonesian, Italian, Japanese, Korean, Malay, Dutch, Portuguese, Russian, Tamil, Telugu, Turkish and Mandarin Chinese. Users do not need to download the video beforehand; they can simply use the YouTube link. Once the video or audio is processed into text, users have the freedom to edit the content as they wish. Plus, the platform offers varying subscription tiers offering different features, allowing users to choose a plan that best matches their needs.
