About Sider AI

Sider is an advanced AI assistant that integrates with your browser to provide a wide range of services on any webpage. Sider comprises different AI tools, including ChatGPT 3.5/4o, Claude 3 and Gemini 1.5, and can assist with tasks such as chatting, writing, reading, translating, and image testing. Its tools can support chats with links, images, PDFs, etc. Sider is designed to be a one-stop AI assistant, incorporating functionalities such as an AI Chatbot, AI Reader, AI Writer, and integrated image tool. It supports group chats with multiple AI systems. Each user inquiry can be addressed by all bots, thereby providing a variety of answers. Sider aims to enhance productivity by reducing time spent on tasks such as picture designing, article writing and solution searching. It offers assistance anytime and anywhere, allowing for use in diverse situations. It also aims to foster creativity and knowledge expansion. Sider is available on multiple platforms and can be added as a Chrome or Edge extension.
