About Casper AI

Casper AI is a tool developed to facilitate workflow for professionals. As a Chrome Extension, this tool leverages AI to simplify understanding of web pages and PDFs by providing summarizations. Casper AI is useful across several professional disciplines such as analysts, strategists, policymakers, and business developers. This tool has been designed not only to distill industry news, but also to facilitate sharing of thoughts effectively, and facilitate understanding of new policies and their effects. Casper AI aims to integrate with existing tools, personalize content for users, and draw on data from public sources and personal emails. It offers simple and flexible operation while generating new insights to help users take appropriate actions. This tool uses advanced AI applications and other software to make users work faster, thus increasing efficiency. Casper AI collects data from various sources, highlights vital insights, analyzes data, and summarizes important news into short notes for easy sharing. This tool enables the user to focus on important tasks, minimizing time spent on mundane ones. It is worthy to note that Casper AI is committed to privacy protection, neither selling users' data to third parties nor using it for unrelated purposes.
