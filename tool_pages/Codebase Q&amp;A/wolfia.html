About Wolfia

Wolfia is an AI-powered tool designed to streamline the filling of Request for Proposals (RFPs), Request for Information (RFIs), and security questionnaires. It uses advanced AI agents for automating a significant part of the process, driving more accurate and quicker responses. The tool works by generating responses based on established policies and existing questionnaires filled out by the user.In addition to supplying precise answers, Wolfia provides detailed citations for greater transparency and validation of the given answers. It offers a feature for contextual responses, allowing users to adjust the length and tone of AI responses according to their preference.Wolfia is equipped with functionalities for importing documents and sheets to autofill questionnaires with AI-generated answers. It seamlessly syncs with existing data sources, keeping its knowledge base current to ensure up-to-date responses.With its emphasis on accuracy, Wolfia offers traceable answers with accurate references to avoid any misinformations or inaccuracies, termed as 'hallucinations' in the tool's terminology.Used and trusted by a range of fast-growing companies, Wolfia is known for its ability to speed up the completion of security reviews. It helps businesses efficiently handle large questionnaires, turning compliance processes into a competitive advantage and enabling the team to concentrate on other high-priority tasks.
