About TigerEye

TigerEye is an AI-powered tool designed to streamline go-to-market planning and execution for businesses. The platform provides a variety of features that aid in improving business operations. Key offerings include Business Simulation and Scenario Planning, where users can create detailed projections for different quarters or years, testing growth stimulators, exploring segmentation models, and comparing various hiring plans. The tool provides predicted outcomes for each scenario to guide users towards the best growth pathway. It also provides segment definition features that help in assigning every account to the correct segment based on attributes, location, and relationship. In addition, TigerEye provides real-time CRM territory definitions sync, collaborative pipeline management to monitor current performance and forecasts for every team and rep, and spend and cost management to manage profitability. This platform fosters collaboration within Go-to-market strategic planning, making execution more aligned and the teams more accountable. TigerEye is a comprehensive solution for various roles including sales, finance, revenue operations, leadership, and marketing.
