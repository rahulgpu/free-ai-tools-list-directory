About Vid2txt

Vid2txt is an offline transcription application designed to transcribe both video and audio files. It simplifies the transcription process by working locally, eliminating the need for costly subscriptions and data sharing. Its primary appeal is its straightforwardness and simplicity, as its function is encapsulated in a simple drag-and-drop interface. The app supports a wide range of file formats including mp4, mov, wmv, mkv, avi, flv, wav, mp3, m4a and others, and provides transcription outputs as .TXT, .SRT, and .VTT files. Every transcription is generated offline offering users fast and secure transcription experience.By design, Vid2txt is focused, doing one thing but doing it excellently. Its simplicity and accuracy make it an ideal tool for various users including content creators, journalists, students, business professionals, researchers, and even those who are hearing impaired. For instance, content creators can use it to create transcripts of videos or podcasts for SEO and closed captioning, while students can use it to quickly transform recorded lectures into readily editable transcripts. It aims to be affordable, providing unlimited transcriptions with no quotas or hidden fees. Users make a single purchase and retain lifetime access to the app's transcription capabilities, positioning the app as an 'anti-subscription' solution for those in need of regular transcription services.
