About Clixie

Clixie.ai is an AI-powered platform used to make videos interactive without requiring coding knowledge. It makes regular video and audio files interactive by integrating features such as hotspots, quizzes, chapters, and bookmarks. This platform is geared towards various sectors including Corporate Training, Sales Training, K-12 and Higher Education and Continuing Education. Clixie.ai also offers an easy, intuitive authoring tool for updates or changes and a 'Data-at-a-Glance' dashboard for content engagement analysis. The platform allows for content to be published quickly and easily to the most powerful platforms. Clixie.ai is designed to be suitable for varied purposes, such as sales materials or investor presentations. Users can effortlessly distribute their interactive content across multiple platforms. The AI interactivity suggestions are an integral part of the platform, ensuring optimal learner engagement.
