About Spot a Bot

Spot a Bot is an artificial intelligence tool designed to visualize and illuminate the potential effects of machine-generated posts on Twitter, particularly in terms of how they might impact current trending topics. By exploring the sphere of influence these automated posts have, it is possible to gain a better understanding of the dynamics of social media interactions and how synthetic input can shape or disrupt them. The tool requires JavaScript to operate, underlying its dependence on modern web technologies. However, it is important to note that the tool may not conclusively prove whether a post is machine-generated or human-made, but it provides indicators and patterns that can assist in making educated guesses. Its focus is predominantly on Twitter, and it might not be as effective on other social media platforms. It does not provide specific counts or statistics but is geared towards visual representation of influence and trends. As AI and machine learning become pervasive in shaping online discussions, 'Spot a Bot' offers unique insights into this evolving dynamic. Its objective is to raise awareness and promote transparency in online engagements.
