About EmoGPT

EmoGPT is a free Google Chrome extension that utilizes the power of artificial intelligence to help generate personalized email replies, follow-ups, rewrites, and cold outreach emails for Gmail users. It is powered by ChatGPT, a large language model trained by OpenAI, which allows it to analyze incoming messages and generate engaging, relevant, and personalized responses. While the main features of EmoGPT will always be free, future innovative and advanced features may require additional resources or team collaboration. EmoGPT is a reliable, secure, and user-friendly interface providing users with exclusivity to both OpenAI’s ChatGPT and Gmail. EmoGPT prioritizes users' privacy and only stores settings locally in the user's browser. It communicates exclusively with OpenAI’s ChatGPT and Gmail and does not have permission to share data or make requests to any other internet resources due to strict and granular permission rules. Once installed, the extension can be accessed via a smiling emoji button which appears in the toolbar of the email window in Gmail. Users can then configure the desired email format and click the “Compose” button to automatically generate high-quality emails. EmoGPT is a valuable tool for Gmail users who aim to enhance their email communication skills and save time while boosting productivity.
