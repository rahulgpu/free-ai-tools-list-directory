About Blinkfeed

Blinkfeed is an AI-backed productivity tool that aims to optimize email handling. It is built around features like automatic summarization of email threads, the discovery of urgent messages, semantic filtering of spam, and the generation of highly-tailored responses. It offers users the capacity to quickly review, edit, and dispatch email responses with a unique level of accuracy. Blinkfeed's AI also takes into account the user's calendar, preferences, and knowledge base in generating such responses, hence significantly saving time.Alongside these, Blinkfeed has functionalities that help users automate certain tasks. This includes following-up on emails, responding to repetitive queries, and organizing emails. These tasks can also be set up in plain English, and users are able to inspect and approve all automated actions.In terms of interface, Blinkfeed supports markdown for editing messages, offering effortless formatting for quick message crafting. Additionally, it features plugins for flexibility and customization based on user preferences and a keyboard-centric navigation for fast-paced management of emails.Future integrations currently in development include calendar synchronization and file analysis for a more holistic approach to productivity. Pricing plans are engaged with differing volumes of email thread analysis capabilities, earmarking the tool as suited for everyday email users, developers, and high-volume professional communicators.
