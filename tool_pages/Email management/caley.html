About Caley

Caley.io is an open-source email client that leverages AI to streamlines email interactions and operations. It offers a smarter approach to email management by understanding user habits, sorting emails, and prioritizing tasks, thereby allowing users to focus more on high-impact work. Unique features include AI-crafted responses that mimic the user's unique writing style, delivering personalized replies for every interaction. Caley.io also revolutionizes newsletter management with a clutter-free approach, offering easy unsubscribe options and analytic insights into your subscription habits. Further, it enables deeper understanding of email trends with built-in analytics profiling communication patterns and providing actionable intelligence for smarter email management. For productivity maximization, Caley.io incorporates keyboard shortcuts to streamline email processing, minimizing the reliance on mouse clicks. It also includes smart calendar integration by transforming email conversations into synchronized calendar events and facilitating seamless integration with existing calendar setups to prevent overlaps. Users have the option of lifetime access to Caley.io which includes features like AI writing, AI automations, unlimited analytics, priority support, and the flexibility to self-host. Compatibility currently extends to Gmail and G Suite accounts, with exploration underway to incorporate other providers such as Outlook.
