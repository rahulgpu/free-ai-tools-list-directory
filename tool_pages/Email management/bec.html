About BeC

BeC is an AI-powered email management tool that organizes and prioritizes emails, reducing notifications for only the important ones. It integrates with the user's existing email inbox and automatically assigns a specific priority to each new incoming email based on the user's attributes and habits. Unlike traditional email management programs, it does not require fixed rules or filters to configure, and users can choose up to 10 priority levels. BeC sends personalized notifications based on the user's priorities, allowing users to set notifications for each device independently to avoid double notifications. Additionally, BeC introduces cumulative notifications, which disturb the user only once for new and multiple priority emails, preventing continuous distractions or missed urgent emails. BeC supports 10 languages and standard email domains like Gmail, Outlook, and Yahoo for private emails. Users can enjoy a completely automatic, dynamic, and personalized email management system for both work and personal lives without having to waste time manually tagging or moving each new incoming email.
