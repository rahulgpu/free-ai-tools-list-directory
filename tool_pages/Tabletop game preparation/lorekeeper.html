About LoreKeeper

LoreKeeper is a digital assistance tool designed to ease the preparation and gameplay processes for Tabletop Role-Playing Games (TTRPGs). Developed by Indie developers, it aims to eliminate game preparation by enabling users to design adventures in a swift and efficient manner. LoreKeeper leverages AI technology to facilitate the building of expansive game worlds, crafting of intricate social hierarchies, and creation of captivating non-player characters. It also aids users navigate complex rule sets without needing to remember every rule. The tool is suited for a variety of role-playing systems, including but not limited to, D&D 5E, Cyberpunk, The Black Hack, and Pathfinder. Additionally, LoreKeeper has inherent flexibility that allows users to create a wholly unique system. It is currently in beta testing and free to sign up without requiring a credit card. The platform can be used in any language, ensuring its appeal to a diverse global user base.
