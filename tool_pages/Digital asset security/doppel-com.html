About Doppel.com

Doppel is a modern tool for digital risk protection. It focuses on detecting and addressing phishing and brand cyber attacks that target numerous prominent companies. The tool is utilized by many leading cybersecurity teams to provide protection against threats both on traditional and emerging digital platforms. Doppel offers different types of products including Anti-Phishing, VIP Protection, Threat Intel, Anti-Piracy, and Anti-Counterfeiting. It is applicable across various industries such as finance, tech, crypto, retail, media and government sectors. With the power of advanced AI models, the system scans millions of websites, social media accounts, mobile apps, paid ads and other online platforms, categorizes the identified threats such as brand infringement and phishing scams, and automatically removes such threats once detected. Additionally, Doppel provides integration with domain registrars, social media, app stores, digital marketplaces, the dark web, and numerous platforms on the Internet. This offers users comprehensive visibility and automated protection against external threats.
