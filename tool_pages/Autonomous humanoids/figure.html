About Figure

Figure is an AI robotics company that specializes in the creation of general-purpose humanoids. This pioneer company leverages advanced AI and humanoid robotics to design efficient, practical, and industry-leading robots for various real-world applications. Figure's flagship product, the Figure 01, is a commercially viable autonomous humanoid robot designed to operate efficiently in our human-centric world. Building on the dexterity of the human form and cutting-edge AI, Figure 01 is capable of performing more than single-function tasks, making it a versatile solution for various industries. It can lend support across sectors such as manufacturing, logistics, and warehousing to retail, potentially alleviating labor shortages and reducing the number of workers in unsafe jobs. The humanoid form factor allows for efficient movement and the use of tools, and it can carry out activities like climbing stairs and lifting boxes. With a dedicated and experienced team, Figure is actively making strides in the field of humanoid robotics, adding momentum to the race of AI-powered humanoids development.
