About Mitra – Call People with AI

Mitra is an AI-based phone calling assistant that is designed to carry out conversations on behalf of the user, delivering real-time information on individuals and businesses promptly. Users can eliminate the hassle of calling by texting Mitra, which is programmed to provide detail-oriented and precise communication. The functionality of this AI tool is not limited to a single device but can be synchronized across multiple devices. Mitra makes use of advanced language and speech AI technologies to accomplish this.Users can ask Mitra to call anyone and deliver specific conversation points. The application is also capable of making multiple calls simultaneously and managing those calls effectively, providing summaries of each interaction. The inclusion of customization capabilities means that users can instruct Mitra to convey specific types of information or act in a certain way during the conversation. It uses the caller's ID, ensuring that known recipients are more likely to answer the call. Mitra offers an efficient and effective way to manage digital relationships and communications.
