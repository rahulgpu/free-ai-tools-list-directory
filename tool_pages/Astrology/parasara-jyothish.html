About Parasara Jyothish

Parasara Jyothish Bot (formerly JyothishGPT) is an AI-powered Vedic Astrology chatbot. It utilizes OpenAI's GPT 3.5 model to analyze users' birth charts and provide predictions. The bot is trained in various modules, details of which can be found in the release notes. There is a demo video available that explains how to use this chatbot on messaging platforms like WhatsApp and Telegram.Users can select their subscription model, which includes a one-month or three-month option. The pricing varies for Indian and foreign residents and is provided in Indian Rupees and USD respectively. Once the subscription is selected, users can apply for access by filling out a form, and access to the bot will be granted within 24 hours.The tool is presented by InnovativeAstroSolutions, which is a popular astrologer on Quora. More information about InnovativeAstroSolutions can be found on their website and social media platforms like Facebook and YouTube.Overall, Parasara Jyothish Bot is an AI-driven astrological tool that offers personalized predictions based on users' birth charts. It leverages the power of GPT-3.5 and aims to provide accessible and convenient astrological guidance to users through popular messaging platforms.
