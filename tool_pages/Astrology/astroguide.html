About Astroguide

Ask the Oracle is an AI-powered tool that provides various services related to astrology, numerology, tarot reading, and dream interpretation. The purpose of this tool is to help users gain self-knowledge and uncover the mysteries of the universe. The services are all powered by OpenAI's GPT-3.5 Turbo, an advanced AI language model. Upon accessing the tool, users are greeted with a chat window that asks them what they are seeking. Users can then choose from a range of services, such as Dream Meaning, My Zodiac Information, Tarot Reading (Major Arcana), Get Your Numerology Reading, Know Your Vocational Map, Discover Your Power Animal, Create Your Birth Chart, Love Calculator, and Chinese Zodiac. The tool uses AI technology to provide personalized readings and interpretations based on user input. Users can also choose to have three tarot cards drawn for them, which may provide insight into their current situation or provide guidance for the future. Overall, Ask the Oracle is a useful tool for individuals interested in exploring the world of spirituality and gaining a deeper understanding of themselves and their place within the universe. Its integration of AI technology allows for a more personalized experience and provides accurate readings.
