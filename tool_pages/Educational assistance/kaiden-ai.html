About Kaiden AI

Kaiden AI is an intelligent assistant designed to aid educators by streamlining lesson planning, grading, and data analysis tasks. This tool is equipped with an AI-powered chat and content generator, which assists users in creating quizzes, lesson plans, and similar educational content. It also includes an AI grader, designed to promptly analyze student work, thereby saving educators time while providing informative insights. The platform is designed to be integrated easily within existing workflows - users can upload and process files directly from browsers or Google Drive with support for multiple file formats (including images, PowerPoints, .doc files, text files, and CSVs). Not just this, the tool can extract key points from various documents, interacting with the provided content for educational purposes. To ensure the creation of personalized content, Kaiden AI allows for customization making use of additional instructions provided by the educators. This enables a focus on specific areas or formats for the quizzes and lesson plans. The tool also incorporates a feedback system wherein educators can submit feedback, facilitating continuous improvement of the platform based on user needs.
