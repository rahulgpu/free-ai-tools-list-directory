About Pictorial

Pictorial is an AI-powered tool that facilitates the creation of visuals for websites and web applications. Whether you are struggling with your site's visuals or are searching for new inspiration, Pictorial's generative AI can help by creating reliable, customized graphics. The AI processes the ideas, concepts, and intentions behind your site's URL to create these visual assets. These are not just run-of-the-mill graphics; Pictorial produces a variety of design options based on your input, displaying multiple aspects of your content in diverse styles to ensure professional, appealing, and ready-to-use designs. Additionally, Pictorial offers a useful feature called 'REFACTOR', through which users can guide the AI by iterating or generating alternatives to the designs created, ensuring the end result is an image that aligns with the user's vision. Pictorial positions itself as the first application of generative AI for graphic creation that's truly useful for businesses. It aims to help you create powerful visuals from any type of content, effectively acting as your AI-powered visual generator.
