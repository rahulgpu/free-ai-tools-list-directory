About Copyleaks

Copyleaks is an AI-based platform specializing in plagiarism and content detection. It offers a range of products to cater to various needs; these include AI Detector, focusing on verifying whether content was human-written or AI-generated, Plagiarism Detector, deployed to detect direct plagiarism, paraphased content, similar text, and verify originality. Codeleaks, a unique tool, detects AI-generated code, plagiarized and modified source code, along with providing aspects of licensing details. Other distinct products include Writing Assistance, AI Grader, and Gen AI Governance, focusing on enforcing enterprise-wide policies, ensuring responsible generative AI adoption, and proactively addressing any potential risks. Copyleaks provides solutions for diverse use cases, along with APIs for integration. It ensures safeguarding content, maintaining originality, and promotes responsible AI adoption. Lastly, Copyleaks offers multi-language support, expanding its reach to users worldwide.
