About PlagAI

Plag.ai Plagiarism Checker is a comprehensive tool designed for detecting plagiarism in various types of documents. It employs an AI-driven system to provide unique and precise plagiarism detection results. Its comparison database contains billions of articles, allowing a broad spectrum for cross-checking and verification. This product is predominantly used by educational institutions across the globe for checking scientific articles, thesis, research papers, essays, and other academic documents. The software offers a freemium model, where users can get approximate plagiarism scores for their papers for free before deciding to purchase a detailed report. The tool also extends its utility for different use-cases like checking for plagiarism in website content, duplicate content in papers and articles. It's operational and highly functional across multiple languages, making it a globally accepted platform for plagiarism checks.One of the unique value propositions of Plag.ai is the partnership with heavyweight data aggregators like CORE that allow the tool to cross-verify papers against a vast database of scientific articles. The service assures full confidentiality and does not share any personal data with third parties. It has been in operation for more than 10 years and is endorsed by many of its users across various demographics, including students, educators, and professionals.
