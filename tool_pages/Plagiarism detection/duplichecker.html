About Duplichecker

The Plagiarism Checker tool offered by DupliChecker is a free and accurate online tool that allows users to check the originality of their content. Users can simply copy and paste their text into the tool to detect any copied content. The tool supports multiple file formats, including .tex, .txt, .doc, .docx, .odt, .pdf, and .rtf, and provides results in a percentage format.The tool offers several features to ensure user privacy and security. It guarantees 100% privacy by not saving any uploaded material and trashing the content after checking for plagiarism. This eliminates the risk of data leakage and protects the privacy and security of the user's content.The Plagiarism Checker utilizes AI-based technology for accurate detection of plagiarized content. It provides a unique content ratio in percentage, allowing users to determine the similarity of their content to other sources. The tool also identifies the plagiarized resources and offers a "Make it Unique" feature to remove any instances of plagiarism.Overall, the Plagiarism Checker by DupliChecker is a reliable tool for writers, teachers, students, bloggers, webmasters, and anyone who wants to ensure the originality of their content. It offers multiple file format support, accurate results in percentage format, privacy guarantees, and AI-based technology for detecting plagiarism.
