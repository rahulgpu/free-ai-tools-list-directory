About Plansom

Plansom's AI project management tool is designed to multiply productivity and enable operational excellence by streamlining strategy formation, task delegation, and prioritization. Featuring an 'AI Plan Generator', it greatly simplifies plan creation avoiding the need to start from scratch, producing detailed business roadmaps instantly. The platform also hosts smart algorithms that analyze tasks, ranking them based on impact and effort, ensuring your focus is directed towards high-priority items. To augment decision-making for complex tasks, the platform extends AI-driven instructions and troubleshooting guidance, essentially working as an expert on your team. The tool also prioritizes seamless collaboration, incorporating communication within the task framework, enhancing team coordination and collaboration. It also allows real-time tracking of teams performance, from individual tasks to overall company objectives, enabling swift identification of issues and ensuring projects are path-aligned. The tool also allows for rolling out custom strategy templates, aligning your organization towards common business goals. Plansom offers a host of ready-to-use strategy templates suited for different industries for rapid business efficiency improvement.
