About Matilda

Matilda AI is an AI-powered all-in-one workspace designed to enhance project management and team collaboration. Positioned as a solution to the inefficiencies observed in traditional project management tools, Matilda offers a comprehensive suite of features spanning documentation, task management, meetings, and customer interactions. The tool's functionality revolves around three key steps. First, users can effortlessly create projects and tasks with Matilda's auto-generating capabilities, streamlining the planning process by providing project plans, timelines, and more. Second, Matilda aids users in starting each day with a perfectly prioritized to-do list, utilizing automatic task scheduling and real-time project forecasts. Finally, the tool introduces Matilda Co-Pilot, a feature that enables users to complete tasks up to 2X faster by intelligently integrating knowledge from the workspace and learning from past completed tasks. Matilda caters to diverse teams, offering flexibility across various industries and sizes. It serves as an intuitive platform for organization, productivity, and collaboration, featuring modules for knowledge base management, issue tracking, sales CRM, personal task management, planning and goal-setting, customer service, product roadmap visualization, meeting notes, and marketing automation. The tool also emphasizes cost-effectiveness, presenting a consolidated alternative to using multiple separate tools. With a transparent pricing model, Matilda offers a 90-day money-back guarantee, allowing users to explore the workspace risk-free and experience its potential in reducing "work about work" while empowering teams to achieve their goals.
