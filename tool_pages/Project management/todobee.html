About Todobee

Todobee is an Artificial Intelligence (AI) powered project management application that is designed for individual users and small teams. The app aims to help users manage their projects by avoiding procrastination, enhancing focus, and facilitating task completion. Todobee boasts a beautiful and intuitive interface that allows users to manage an unlimited number of projects and tasks. The service promises complete privacy, premium support, and a commitment to small teams. It also has an AI Progress Dashboard and additional features like a 'Drag and Drop' organization system, assignment of tasks, setting of due dates and pinning favourite projects. The application offers a free 7-day trial period for users to try and experience its features. Furthermore, the Todobee platform also has an affiliate program that provides generous commissions for those who refer new users. Note: The unique pricing model offers unlimited access to all their features for either a monthly subscription or a one-time lifetime payment.
