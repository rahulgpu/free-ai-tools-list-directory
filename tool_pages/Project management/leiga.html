About Leiga

Leiga is an AI-assisted tool designed for project management with specific focus on aiding developers, project managers, dev leadership, and product managers. The AI technology facilitates automated updates to keep projects on track and teams focused. The tool streamlines planning, tracking, collaboration, and decision-making processes. It offers features such as effortless team management, which allows for monitoring and adjusting each team member's workload and progress. Automated workflow customization enables handling of repetitive tasks such as reminders for completed issues or task creation based on team progress. With its AI technology, Leiga offers chat-based report generation, AI-aided project management providing real-time data analysis and risk identification. The AI info hub feature allows for instant access to business updates and future plans. Additionally, AI is utilized in development subtask generation and streamlining document creation. The tool uniquely allows embedding into your IDE, enabling project updates through a command without switching screens, thus enhancing productivity. With Leiga, it's possible to monitor time allocation, screen record bugs, and get a cohesive view of ongoing projects for strategic real-time adjustment. Leiga also aids in assignment balancing, requirement delivery, and project timeline management. The tool is adapted to remote work with features like screen recording and AI transcription, promoting collaboration irrespective of location.
