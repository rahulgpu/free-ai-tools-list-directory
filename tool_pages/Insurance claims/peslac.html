About Peslac

Peslac is an AI/ML Insurance Platform that offers a comprehensive and streamlined solution for insurance management. It focuses on three key areas: simplified onboarding, real-time verifications, and smart fraud detection. With Peslac, companies can experience a swift and effortless onboarding process. Their real-time identity verification solutions ensure secure sign-ups, allowing businesses to establish meaningful relationships with their customers from the start. The platform also provides advanced fraud detection tools. Through machine learning algorithms, Peslac can identify unusual patterns and anomalies in insurance claims, instantly alerting the team to potential fraudulent activities. Additionally, artificial intelligence is used to conduct comprehensive risk assessments and detect high-risk policies and potentially fraudulent claims.Peslac maximizes data correlation by cross-referencing information from multiple sources, including government databases and historical claims. This approach helps uncover hidden connections and detect potentially fraudulent activities.For businesses without existing insurance systems, Peslac offers an intuitive dashboard that serves as a powerful tool for initiating and managing insurance offerings. The platform's API-first approach ensures seamless integration with existing systems, enabling effortless data exchange.Overall, Peslac empowers insurers to handle claims with precision, detect potential fraud, and enhance operational efficiency. It offers tailored insurance solutions that are secure, personalized, and adhere to industry standards of confidentiality and data privacy.
