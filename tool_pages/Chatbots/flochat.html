About Flochat

Flochat is a conversational AI platform that allows users to build powerful and engaging chatbots and automations for exceptional customer support. It offers a free 14-day Pro trial and supports over 15 messaging channels. With Flochat, users can create chatbots and automations to deliver outstanding support on full auto-pilot.The platform features multi-channel broadcasting, artificial intelligence, automation, and a flow builder for creating customized workflows. It also integrates with GitBook AI, allowing users to access AI-powered documentation.In addition to its core features, Flochat provides a range of resources and support. These include a blog, help documentation, developer API, contact information, and various integrations. It also offers plans for users to choose from.Flochat's multi-channel automation capabilities enable users to connect with their customers on their preferred messaging app. It allows for two-way chat automation, a unified support dashboard, auto-assigning agents, and broadcast promotions.Furthermore, Flochat synchronizes existing tools into conversations, delivers personalized experiences by extracting data from integrated tools, and optimizes social media customer relationship management (CRM). It enables businesses to automate first-line customer support with AI chatbots, providing real-time assistance and relevant information.Overall, Flochat offers an all-in-one solution for creating and managing chatbots and automations, enhancing customer support, and streamlining communication across multiple channels.
