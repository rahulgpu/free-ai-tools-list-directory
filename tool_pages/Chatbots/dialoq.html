About Dialoq

Dialoq is a no-code AI powered chatbot builder that allows you to upload your own data and create chatbots easily and with no coding knowledge. It supports various data formats including PDF, .txt file, .md file, plain text and URLs. The tool provides options to customize the appearance of the chatbot, set its initial message and tone, and even embed it on your website. Dialoq version 1.1.0 offers additional features such as GPT-4 support, chat streaming, and options to adjust the temperature and similarity score.Setting up Dialoq is easy and requires only three steps; uploading your own data, customizing your chatbot, and embedding it on your website. Dialoq also provides a demo and free trial with no credit card required. The chatbot can be asked anything about Dialoq, allowing users to test how well it works.Dialoq has been featured on SaaS AI Tools and is compliant with privacy policies, terms and conditions, and refund policies. Overall, Dialoq makes it easy for anyone to create an AI powered chatbot without having to know how to code or go through complex AI tools.
