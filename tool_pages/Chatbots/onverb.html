About OnVerb

OnVerb is a platform with a focus on customizable AI interactions to personalize the way AI comprehends and responds to user's queries. It utilizes system prompt technology for users to provide detailed context, guidelines, and examples to shape these responses. Some features include an AI marketing copywriter that adheres to user-set style guidelines, and an AI research assistant that draws information from chosen academic sources. The platform assures stringent data encryption and privacy policies, its usage does not contribute to the training of other AI models or services, and it does not compromise sensitive user data. The goal of OnVerb is to optimize professional tasks, enhance operational business efficiency and facilitate creativity. The platform leverages advanced AI models such as GPT-4, Claude 3, and Mistral, for customized responses. Beyond general usage, certain enterprise plans offer unique instance customization of the OnVerb app with unique URLs, and access to human training and implementation consultation. An impressive range of AI models are accessible via a single interface, allowing for custom context and data management.
