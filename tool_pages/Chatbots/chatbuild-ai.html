About ChatBuild AI

ChatBuild AI is an intuitive platform enabling users to create custom-trained AI chatbots for their websites, without requiring any coding experience. The tool supports the generation of chatbot widgets, which can be fully customized and embedded into websites to enhance user interaction. These chatbots can be trained on custom data, providing tailored interactions for website visitors. Users can also monitor chat history and utilize built-in analytics to continually improve the functionality and accuracy of their chatbots. Aside from its primary function of creating chatbots, ChatBuild AI also allows users to upload files to train the chatbots, giving a range of possibilities for using URL links, sitemaps, and CSV files for the creation of a very personalized and intelligent chatbot. They offer global language support making it accessible to users from anywhere around the world. Finally, ChatBuild AI offers a free trial period allowing potential users to explore its features and gauge its effectiveness in improving their website user experience.
