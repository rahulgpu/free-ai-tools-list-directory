About Wonderchat

Wonderchat is an AI chatbot builder that allows users to create their own custom ChatGPT chatbots from a link to their knowledge base in just 5 minutes without any coding knowledge. The chatbot learns from the text on the user’s website and is capable of answering any question related to the sitelink, making customer support faster and more efficient. The tool offers customization options, including setting the role and profile photo of the chatbot to reflect the brand identity and tailoring the response length to the user’s preferences. Once the chatbot has learned all the information from the webpages of the website, it can be easily embedded on the site by copying and pasting a simple line of code. Users can read and review chatlogs, monitor the efficacy of their chatbot by checking the customer feedback, train the chatbot to learn from its mistakes, and review analytics to improve performance. The tool is available in various pricing plans based on monthly message quotas, pages stored per chatbot, number of customizable chatbots, API access, and more. Wonderchat supports all languages, and users can embed the chatbot into any website by inserting a simple script into the HTML code. The tool is compatible with popular websites and has been positively reviewed by its beta users. Wonderchat offers a free plan, and its refund policy is enforced to avoid system abuse by users.
