About Humy

Humy.ai is a tool that allows small teams to create custom AI assistants without any coding required. It offers a no-code builder that enables users to create and launch personalized AI assistants in just 10 minutes. The AI assistants created with Humy.ai are designed to deliver value, save money, and create engagement with tailored interactions. These assistants can remember, contextualize, offer recommendations, and proactively address queries, fostering deeper connections with customers and driving conversions.With Humy.ai, both tech novices and seasoned experts can easily create their own AI assistants, turning their vision into a digital reality with a few clicks. The tool also provides customizable solutions, where users can harness the power of ready-made AI assistants or have a team of experts customize a solution to fit their unique needs.Humy.ai supports over 100 languages, ensuring that customers from around the world can feel heard and understood. It goes beyond translation by capturing the essence, emotion, and cultural nuances of different languages.The tool offers various use cases, including interactive FAQ and support, education and coaching, sales assistance, AI concierge, and document analysis. It also integrates with various channels and platforms, allowing users to deploy their AI assistants seamlessly.Powered by GPT-4 and GPT-3.5, Humy.ai provides best-in-class AI capabilities. It ensures data security, follows GDPR compliance, and allows users to gain deep insights into user behavior. The tool also provides a simple four-step process to define the behavior of the AI assistant, upload relevant data, embed it in various communication channels, and gain insights for performance optimization.Overall, Humy.ai empowers businesses to create their own AI assistants quickly and easily, enabling them to enhance customer experience, streamline operations, and deliver personalized interactions without needing coding expertise.
