About NewRA

NewRA is a cloud-based AI tool that enables enterprises to quickly build intelligent chatbots powered by their data. With its compatibility to modern AI models, NewRA assists in managing enterprise conversations by repurposing, organizing, and automating interaction based on the company's data. It also provides a platform for building highly tailored applications using customized knowledge bases, which enhance AI-driven decision-making and operations. NewRA offers features like live playground testing, which allows users to test AI integrations and make real-time adjustments to prompts based on incoming data streams. Furthermore, it allows users to streamline sales operations, facilitate customer service, create engaging content, carry out contract reviews, navigate through vast information, and automate HR tasks. Additionally, NewRA assists in scheduling, managing, and summarizing meetings, enabling intellectual property tracking and compliance checks, and allowing powerful data analysis and visualization. Moreover, it provides an easy-to-use interface where prompts can be customized or chosen from a library, and it supports peripheral systems integration.Overall, NewRA has broad applicability and has demonstrated potential to be used as a tool for enhancing and automating a number of business processes.
