About Gali

Gali is an AI chatbot tool specializing in generating intelligent and personalized customer interactions on websites. It leverages AI GPT technology to train on your unique data, adapting to your specific resources like websites, FAQs, documentation, and more. This allows for insightful conversations that are directly aligned with your brand and customer needs. Its main uses include increasing website conversion rates, enhancing customer support, and providing instant access to your documents. Gali enables the creation of different types of chatbots, each serving a unique purpose, such as sales bots for lead generation, customer support bots for handling inquiries, wiki and docs bots for internal documentation access, and fully customizable bots based on user requirements. Additionally, Gali integrates seamlessly with various platforms and tech stacks, allowing for effortless backend interaction via endpoints. This feature can be leveraged for various custom uses cases like proposing products based on user preferences or qualifying leads by asking questions. A noteworthy benefit of Gali is that it supports all languages available in OpenAI, ensuring a wide reach. Gali also provides insightful analytics from chat interactions to help improve copies and even products.
