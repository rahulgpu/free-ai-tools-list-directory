About Arsturn AI

Arsturn is an AI tool designed to facilitate the creation of custom ChatGPT for your website. This tool leverages the power of natural language understanding to help you build a sophisticated chatbot rapidly. The objective of this tool is to make the process of deploying an automated chat support easier without requiring extensive programming skills. It uses the underlying technology of OpenAI's GPT allowing for a highly intelligent conversational AI. While Arsturn claims to enable creation of a chatbot in under a minute, the set-up time might vary based on the customization required. Nevertheless, it's designed to be user-friendly and efficient. To use Arsturn, enabling Javascript is necessary as it's a web-based application. It allows for a range of customizations to meet specific business needs. Users can tailor the chatbot's responses based on different prompts and scenarios, ensuring a human-like interaction experience for the visitors of the website. This results in more personalized, timely, and efficient customer support which could improve customer satisfaction and engagement rates.
