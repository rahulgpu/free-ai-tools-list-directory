About Bottomright

Bottomright is an AI-powered chatbot that utilizes OpenAI technology to provide automated customer support on websites. It trains itself using data from your website, allowing it to offer context-aware answers. With Bottomright, you can quickly add a custom-trained chatbot to your website in just a few minutes.The tool addresses the common challenge of providing customer support without the need for human availability 24/7. This is particularly beneficial for solo founders or startups who may not have the resources to maintain constant customer support coverage. By automating customer chat, Bottomright aims to prevent delays in response time, ensuring that every visitor receives prompt assistance.The AI chatbot offers customization options, allowing users to change its appearance to align with their brand. The built-in editor included with the free plan enables easy customization of different UI elements. Additionally, the chatbot conversations are accessible through a dashboard, providing valuable insights into customer preferences and pain points. This information can be utilized to improve products and support services.Bottomright offers different pricing plans, including a free plan that provides access to 99% of its features. The free plan allows training on up to three pages and up to 100 messages per month. Paid plans provide increased capabilities such as additional training pages and messages.Overall, Bottomright is a user-friendly and efficient tool that empowers website owners to enhance their customer support experience by integrating an AI chatbot trained specifically with their website's content.
