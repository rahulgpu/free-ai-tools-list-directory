About Mevo

Mevo is a no-code chatbot platform that allows users to create and customize chatbots without any coding knowledge. With Mevo, individuals and businesses can interact with their audience by creating human-friendly chatbots for their business, organization, or personal use. The platform provides powerful features such as the ability to customize the look and feel of the chatbot, share it easily, receive notifications, and track analytics.Mevo offers two types of chatbots: rule-based chatbots and AI-powered chatbots. Rule-based chatbots can be created for websites, landing pages, or any other purpose. Users can customize the chatbot's appearance and publish it within minutes. AI-powered chatbots, on the other hand, allow users to teach their products and services, FAQs, and even provide raw text or PDF files for the chatbot to access and respond to.Mevo offers transparent pricing with a free forever plan that includes all the core features needed to get started. There is also a Pro plan with increased limits, priority support, and additional features, and an Enterprise plan with dedicated support and infrastructure for companies.Overall, Mevo is a user-friendly and affordable tool that empowers individuals and businesses to create and deploy chatbots without requiring coding knowledge, making it easier than ever to interact with their audience and gather information.
