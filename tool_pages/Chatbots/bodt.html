About Bodt

Bodt.io is a no-code AI chatbot builder that allows you to create personalized chatbots for your website without the need for coding knowledge. The platform is designed to enhance customer interaction and lead generation by training the chatbots on your website content. With Bodt.io, you can build AI-powered chatbots in just three simple steps. First, you connect your website, and the platform retrieves all your pages, displaying them in an organized list. Next, you choose the pages from the list that you want your chatbot to learn from and start the training process. Once trained, the chatbot is ready to answer any queries related to your business, providing 24/7 customer support, engaging with site visitors, and offering enhanced website search capabilities.In addition to its chatbot building functionality, Bodt.io offers integrations with popular tools like WordPress, Shopify, WooCommerce, Laravel, Transistor, and YouTube to streamline the training process using your own data.The platform also serves as a centralized knowledge hub for your organization, storing information such as product details, visions, client contact history, and more. It can be used for onboarding, training, and operations, providing role-specific training and generating data-driven analytics reports. Bodt.io offers flexible pricing plans, starting from a pay-as-you-go option to an enterprise plan with a dedicated account manager.Overall, Bodt.io provides an intuitive solution for building AI chatbots that automate customer support, lead generation, and provide a centralized knowledge hub for businesses.
