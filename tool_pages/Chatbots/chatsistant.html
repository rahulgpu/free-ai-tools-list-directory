About Chatsistant

Chatsistant is a conversational AI framework that enables users to design, deploy and manage AI chatbots with no coding required. These AI chatbots can handle inquiries, provide personalized assistance, and automate tasks, which can be significant for businesses aiming to improve customer satisfaction and operations scalability. With features for customizing bot responses and training them on accuracy, users can enhance the AI's knowledge base effectively. The chatbots developed using Chatsistant integrate with multiple platforms such as Shopify, Messenger, WordPress, Wix, Square Space and more. An additional feature of Chatsistant is its capability to tap into data from diverse sources, including PDFs and all file types, ensuring that communication is accurately tailored to user interactions. Businesses can also use Chatsistant to train an AI chatbot that acts as a 24/7 customer service rep. This can lead to efficient responses, reduced wait times, and reduction in customer service costs. Furthermore, Chatsistant takes security seriously and works towards maintaining high standards of information security, including working towards achieving ISO 27001 certification. Another unique feature of Chatsistant includes a Live Chat Support for human escalation, allowing human agents to intervene and converse directly with users when required.
