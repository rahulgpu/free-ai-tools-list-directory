About Direqt

Direqt is an AI platform utilized for crafting custom chatbots specifically tailored for individual websites. The primary purpose of these AI-driven chatbots is to facilitate organic and engaging conversations with visitors, in a bid to increase site stickiness, user engagement, and content loyalty, leading to heightened on-site revenue. Direqt's AI chatbots are trained comprehensively using an entity's existing content, allowing them to be naturally embedded within the site and initiate authentic discussions reflecting the site's inherent voice and persona. Additionally, through Direqt, chatbots can be located at any point on the site for optimal impact. Another noteworthy feature of Direqt is its ability to monetize these engaging interactions through in-chat advertisements or subscription prompts. Direqt caters to a wide range of top publishers, aiding them to amplify reader engagement, boost on-site duration, and generate revenue from chatbot interactions. The platform's unique approach has resulted in it gaining recognition and patronage from leading publishers around the globe who use Direqt to power their chat experiences.
