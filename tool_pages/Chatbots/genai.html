About GenAI

Genai is an online platform that offers the ability to create, customize and deploy AI chatbots for a varied range of applications. It is used for purposes such as customer support, learning and research, and data collection from various sources including websites, PDFs, and videos. Without the need for coding, Genai lets its users build context-aware chatbots that answer queries based on the data provided. It enables the automation of customer support, available round the clock, thereby potentially enhancing customer satisfaction and lowering support costs. Genai also features tools for chatbot performance monitoring and improvement. Additionally, it offers a new approach to learning, where user can load their favorite podcasts to find specific information quickly. The chatbots created in Genai can be well-customized aesthetically to match the user's brand. Furthermore, Genai offers a means to monetize user knowledge by sharing it with the world through training their chatbot with their unique knowledge. It provides for quick integration on websites or apps with API and an emphasis on privacy and security. It allows for public sharing and monetization. The platform maintains a floating widget and inline widget for ease of integration with user's websites or applications.
