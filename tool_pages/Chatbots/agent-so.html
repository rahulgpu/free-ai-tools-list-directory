About Agent.so

Agent.so is a comprehensive AI platform allowing for the creation and training of customized AI agents quickly and with ease. The platform employs a simple-to-use interface where users can simply drag and drop files or even add entire sitemaps and links for the process of agent training. Agent.so aims to enhance business capabilities in diverse fields such as internet marketing, e-commerce, education, legal, real estate, relationships, engineering, entertainment, and entrepreneurship, amongst others. Users get to interact with hundreds of AI agents, each possessing their own unique characteristics, style, and area of specialization. The platform also features an AI Apps Hub containing ready-made AI apps. It allows real-time web browsing via web search and comes with features for sharing chats and inviting ambassadors in order to earn rewards. The AI agent tool emphasizes privacy with advanced encryption features. It is designed to accommodate a wide variety of use-cases, making it suitable for users from all walks of life.
