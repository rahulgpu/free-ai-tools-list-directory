About Juji

Juji cognitive AI chatbots are interactive AI tools that facilitate the process of creating and operating chatbots across various sectors, such as education and healthcare. Using pre-built cognitive AI templates, users can easily set up AI chatbots on their websites without the need for any coding skills. With their cognitive intelligence capabilities, these AI chatbots can undertake complex human interactions, not just performing routine tasks but also demonstrating advanced human soft skills like active listening and reading between the lines. The platform also leverages computational psychology to gain an understanding of users' psychographic insights from their conversation texts, hence offering more personalized guidance. Juji emphasizes accessibility, enabling non-IT professionals to create and manage custom cognitive AI assistants with ease, akin to using commonplace software like PowerPoint or Excel. Further enhancing its user-friendly approach, Juji offers a no-code AI chatbot builder to enable the rapid reuse or customization of powerful cognitive AI assistants. Lastly, Juji cognitive AI chatbots offer a high degree of flexibility, allowing the creation of a single cognitive AI assistant or a team of them, which the users can run in the cloud or on premise.
