About MyAskAI

My AskAI is an AI-powered tool that allows users to save time by easily getting AI answers from various content sources such as documents, websites and their Google Drive or Notion. Users can create their own AI assistant in less than two minutes, customize its appearance and responses to questions, embed it on their website, add it to their Slack, Teams, Messenger or WhatsApp or share a public link. My AskAI offers fast and accurate answers, based on OpenAI's advanced AI technology (using either GPT-3.5 or 4), that are truthful and can be relied on. Users can personalize their AskAI to match their brand's personality and create multiple ChatGPTs as per their needs. My AskAI also offers multilingual support with over 95 languages and complies with GDPR regulations, ensuring complete control over user data. It features a range of pricing plans catering to different use cases, from free to enterprise-level, with enhanced security features to protect user data. My AskAI is suitable for teams, companies, and audiences who want to quickly access and share accurate information without wasting time searching and scrolling. With its fast and convenient access to information, it helps save users hours of reading and searching for content, especially common questions that are asked repeatedly.
