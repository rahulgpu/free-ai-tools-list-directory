About Twinning

Twinning is an AI tool that allows influencers to create a virtual clone of themselves to interact with their followers. The tool enables influencers to engage and communicate with their fans through an AI twin without the need for direct involvement. To create an AI twin, users provide their personal information, content type, and fan demographics. They then record a 5-15 minute audio clip, which is used to generate the AI twin. After a short processing time, influencers receive a link to share with their fans, enabling them to connect and chat with the AI clone.Twinning offers different pricing plans based on the user's number of followers. The plans include unlimited interactions, professional voice cloning, audio messaging, and texting features. The tool also provides analytics to track engagement and performance.The estimation of earnings is also provided, based on the number of followers and the assumed percentage of followers engaging with the AI twin for a certain amount of time each day. However, it's important to note that these earnings are estimated and may vary.Overall, Twinning offers influencers a unique way to stay connected with their followers by providing an AI clone that can chat and interact with fans on their behalf.
