About Dante AI

Dante AI is an AI chatbot builder focused on creating personalized AI chatbots based on user-specific data. It assists partners to build chatbots that are capable of engaging, educating, and converting through intuitive technology. Users can upload a variety of file types, share website links, images, and videos which the platform then uses to generate intelligent chatbots. These AI chatbots can offer deep insights related to the provided content and are trained on user-specific data. The tool can be easily used without any coding skills and can be integrated on user's websites swiftly. Dante AI can connect with thousands of applications via Zapier integration, proving its high compatibility. It also prioritizes security by storing data on encrypted AWS servers. Dante AI extends its capabilities by being language-friendly and supports over 100+ languages. This tool comes with GDPR compliance, ensuring data protection and privacy for the users.
