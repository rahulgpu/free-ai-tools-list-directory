About Katonic

The Katonic Generative AI Platform is a no-code tool that enables users to build, train, and deploy custom AI applications and chatbots without the need for coding. The platform offers a selection of 75 large language models (LLMs) to choose from, allowing users to leverage the power of Generative AI.With the Katonic Generative AI Platform, businesses can improve employee productivity, decision-making processes, and customer experience. The platform ensures enterprise-grade security, minimizing any risks to data.This tool offers a range of functionalities, including the ability to build AI-powered chatbots and digital assistants that can access and process information from various sources. The platform also allows users to identify and extract essential information from unstructured text and generate personalized executive summaries.Additionally, users can read and classify written input without the need for extensive examples, generate content for specific purposes with expert-level accuracy, and build content moderation systems.The Katonic Generative AI Platform also provides future capabilities that are currently under development, such as code assistance, semantic search, recommendation systems, and customizable models.The tool stands out for its choice of multiple LLMs, ensuring highly accurate results. It also offers enterprise-grade security, enabling it to be installed on-premises for enhanced data protection. The platform is scalable, allowing users to train and deploy even the most powerful LLMs.Overall, the Katonic Generative AI Platform empowers businesses to harness the potential of Generative AI without the need for coding expertise, while delivering accurate results and ensuring data security.
