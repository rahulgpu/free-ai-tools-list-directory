About BotSquare

BotSquare is a low-code AI application development platform that enables users to create, customize and deploy AI applications with ease. By using a drag-and-drop editor, users can easily build and customize their apps, essentially similar to assembling Legos. It uses an advanced Natural Language Processing (NLP) module to facilitate impactful conversations with accurate responses amongst AI applications. It stands out by multiplying the capabilities of AI apps with their trained Language Learning Models (LLMs) and flexible vector data sources. Moreover, it provides diverse AI tools ranging from generative models to phone and messaging integration. Users can deploy AI apps across various channels such as WeChat, websites and SMS instantaneously, creating immersive experiences by merging text, image, audio and video seamlessly. It also offers a task management system enabling users to combine, share and sell modules effortlessly. BotSquare encourages creativity and lowers entry barriers, allowing everyone from students to business owners to create AI bots tailored to their requirements. No coding skills are required; however, coding options are available for more advanced users for full functionality.
