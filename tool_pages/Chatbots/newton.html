About Newton

Newton is an AI-based tool that allows users to build their own chatagents utilizing a no-code solution. The platform's primary purpose is to help boost sales and support by creating chatbots that align with the user's business goals and needs. This integration into the business also aims to transform operations, making them more efficient and productive. Designed for a variety of users, Newton offers a range of applications for agencies, businesses, and personal use. Agencies can create and sell custom ChatGPTs without needing any coding expertise, and businesses can integrate Newton to enhance customer services, streamline operations, and create tailored AI solutions with minimal need for extensive training. On the other hand, the tool can be used personally as a study aid, transforming study materials into a personalized AI companion and providing instant research assistance. Newton provides a simple, transparent, and cost-effective pricing model catering to startups, businesses and enterprises, making it a versatile and accessible tool for a wide range of users and their varying requirements.
