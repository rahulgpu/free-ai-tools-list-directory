About Aivia

With Aivia, teams can benefit from a secure and private environment optimized for business use. The platform offers access to powerful AI models, including the renowned ChatGPT-4 and Google AI, tailored specifically for business applications.Aivia enables users to customize and train their AI assistants without the need for coding, using their own data. They can also take advantage of pre-trained Specialist Assistants in various fields, such as marketing, sales, finance, operations, and engineering.Collaboration tools are available to foster teamwork and enhance AI capabilities collectively. The platform offers a database with over 2500 prompts to accelerate cognitive tasks, eliminating the need for ad-hoc databases. Additionally, Aivia's AI Academy provides professional development opportunities for users of all levels.Data privacy and security are paramount in Aivia.
