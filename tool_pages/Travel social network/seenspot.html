About Seenspot

Seenspot is a travel planning tool that combines social media and travel online resources. It enables users to snap, upload, and share their travel videos with the Seenspot community. The tool offers an interactive travel map to check for activities nearby or globally and uses artificial intelligence to search the web for the best prices for stays. Users can record videos up to 30 seconds, while they can upload videos up to 60 seconds. In its Discover feature, users can view the latest travel videos and search hashtags or other travelers to follow. The Trip Planner feature allows users to plan their trips, search for and bookmark over 100+ million hotspots including restaurants, hotels, and more. It also offers a feature to book travel necessities like hotels and activities while striving to find the best rates from multiple partners. Users can tag their video uploads with a location for others to see, though this feature takes the privacy of users into consideration, with the actual location being optional. It provides real-time notifications when someone interacts with their videos or follows them, and allows for chat with family and friends. This tool aims at fostering a travel community where users can share their travel stories.
