About Ursule

Ursule is an intelligent assistant guided by GPT AI technology, largely used to optimize work tasks and reduce time spent on mundane tasks that require a certain level of attention. It is capable of generating text, translating text, managing emails, and assisting with marketing and communications tasks. Ursule speaks and operates in more than 37 languages, offering a platform to not only perform translations, but also to work directly in the language of the user's choice. It supports text analysis, preparation for meetings, marketing and communication management, and more. To enhance security, it offers 2-Factor Authentication, secure connections, and stores data in French servers. A notable feature of Ursule is its ability to write text in seconds versus the hours a human writer may need to draft the same content. Some of its future integrations include Speech to Text, Web Search, document analysis, and a graphic studio. Ursule is also equipped with modules for intelligent chat and more than 50 templates for different types of communications. It offers the option to use AI to generate unique images based on text descriptions.
