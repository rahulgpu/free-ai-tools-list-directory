About AI Thingy

AI Thingy is an AI-enabled text copy generator designed to assist various users including copywriters, marketers, managers, scientists, students, and creators. It aids users in the rapid creation of unique, engaging, and conversion-optimized content pertinent to a broad spectrum of marketing channels or formats. The tool has great potential in creating ad copies, blog posts, website content, social media posts, product descriptions, and emails. Users initiate the process by providing some context in form of a few sentences, following which the AI generates original copy accompanied by a set of the most relevant keywords. The output copy is customizable, allowing users to better tailor it to their specific needs. Additionally, AI Thingy offers a number of convenient features such as smart templates, varied tones of voice, and SEO-friendly output. A history feature allows easy navigation and search through previous outputs. The tool also has capability to generate related images to complement written content. Another advantageous aspect is the offered compatibility with multiple platforms in the form of a Slack Bot, Chrome Extension, and Figma Plugin, providing easy access and enhancing its usability. Its pricing model operates on a pay-as-you-go basis and in the form of credit packages with different ranges and costs.
