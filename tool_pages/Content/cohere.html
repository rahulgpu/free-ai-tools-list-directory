About Cohere

Cohere is a leading AI platform for enterprise. It offers large language models (LLMs) and Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) capabilities that are specifically designed for enterprise use-cases to resolve real-world problems. There are several major components of this platform. 'Command R+' is one of their most advanced models.The platform is optimized for generative AI, search and discovery, and advanced retrieval, making it ideal for dealing with the language of business. Cohere's models are designed to augment and elevate the global workforce to help businesses thrive in the AI era. The 'Command' models are utilized to construct production-ready, scalable, and efficient AI-powered applications. 'Cohere Embed' helps in unlocking the potential of your enterprise data by training their models on the language of business, providing a highly performing embedding model that supports over 100 languages. Additional features include 'Retrieval & Rerank' for the provision of reliable and up-to-date responses and advanced retrieval for applications requiring RAG. Cohere offers intuitive and secure deployment options, accommodating both on-cloud and on-premises enterprise environments. The platform also offers a 'Playground' for businesses to explore the latest AI advancements, 'LLM University' for generative AI-based courses, and comprehensive developer documentation for easy integration of Coheres AI models into apps and workflows.
