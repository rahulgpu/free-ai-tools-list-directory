About GravityWrite

GravityWrite is an AI-powered writing tool that assists content creators in generating high-quality content for various platforms including blogs, ads, emails, and social media. The tool can generate up to 5,000 words for free. The user starts by selecting the type of content they want to create and then provides a brief description about the brand and product, after which the AI-powered content generator provides multiple options for each campaign that the user is working on. The tool claims to save time, boost engagement, and increase creativity by giving users access to over 80 professional AI writing tools. GravityWrite caters to various audiences including bloggers, marketing teams, e-commerce stores, and entrepreneurs who desire to take their content creation to the next level. The tool offers three pricing plans, the first being the free plan that offers 5,000 words per month, the starter plan which costs $15/year and offers 50,000 words per month, and the pro plan which costs $49/year and offers 250,000 words per month. Customers can access GravityWrite's professional AI tools instantly upon signing up for any of the three plans. The tool offers support for over 30+ languages and tones, and 10+ content categories. GravityWrite also offers its users priority support and premium support options on the starter and pro plans, respectively. A trial version is available before purchasing, and customer support is provided to cater to any queries or issues that may arise.
