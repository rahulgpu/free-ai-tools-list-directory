About Jasper.ai

Jasper is an enterprise-grade AI tool designed to enhance the speed and performance of marketing teams. It serves as a 'central nervous system' for various content-related tasks, helping to streamline communication and accelerate outputs. The tool offers AI-assisted content creation, taking ideas to execution faster and with better outcomes.The AI Copilot feature provides insights and analytics, not only increasing speed but also improving the performance of marketing activities. It includes robust security controls and options for integrating with existing platforms through APIs, extensions, and integrations.Jasper boasts several core features. In 'Campaigns', it assists teams with the production of complete marketing campaigns from one brief. 'Brand Voice' uses AI to mimic a brand's unique voice and style, while 'Chat' presents an AI chatbot with a human-like engagement. In addition, 'Art' provides users with the ability to generate unique images from imaginative influences.Furthermore, Jasper furnishes multiple language capabilities, allowing users to create on-brand creative copy in various languages. With 'Templates', users can create high-quality copy for over 50 different use cases.Finally, 'Jasper Everywhere' extends the functionality of Jasper directly into the user's platform, upgrading their workflow by integrating AI. This can be accessed through browser extensions for a more widespread application of the AI tool.
