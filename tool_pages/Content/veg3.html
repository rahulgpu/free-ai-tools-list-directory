About Veg3

VEG3 is an artificial intelligence marketing assistant designed to help vegan business owners and animal rights charities write better marketing copy. It works by taking instructions from the user and creating marketing copy based on thousands of examples of successful vegan marketing copy. VEG3 understands the ethics behind veganism and can detect patterns that only a machine can recognise to create intelligent marketing copy optimized for business objectives. It can be trained to write better and better copy over time by upvoting or downvoting any response from VEG3. There is a generous free plan that includes 5,000 monthly credits which can generate more than 3,000 words of marketing copy. VEG3 is a great tool for vegan business owners and animal rights charities to create effective marketing copy.
