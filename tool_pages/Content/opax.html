About Opax

Opax is an AI-powered tool designed for seamless integration with Adobe Experience Manager to provide unparalleled content generation capabilities. Aiming to solve the challenge of 'writer's block', Opax is capable of generating captivating and relevant content based on an outline, bullet points, or even merely a page title. It is designed to benefit diverse sets of users from beginners to advanced users, courtesy of its intuitive user interface and guided prompt templates. Allowing the users to stay in control of the prompts ensures customisation and personalisation requirements are adequately met. One of the major highlights of Opax is its direct integration into the Adobe Experience Manager, eliminating the need for tab-switching while working on content. This automation feature is further advanced with Opaxs ability to automatically format the generated content to match the specific styling of a user's site. It offers a transformative solution for creatives and marketers alike to enhance their digital experiences while unlocking more of their creative potential.
