About Copymatic

Copymatic is an AI-powered copywriting and content writing tool that automates the process of creating digital ads, website copy, blog content, and more. It leverages the GPT-3 AI language model to generate unique and human-like copy in a matter of seconds. With Copymatic, users can adjust the creativity level and the tone of voice to generate content that captures attention and converts. The tool can also be used to generate blog titles, intros, outlines, meta tags, social media posts and ads, product names, and product descriptions. Copymatic can also check for poor grammar and rewrite content in a unique and smart way. Plans are tailored for businesses of all sizes, with a free trial available for 10 credits to generate about 1,000 words of blog content. The AI-powered tool also offers a Wordpress plugin, API access, SEO keyword research, plagiarism checker, volume-based pricing, and premium technical support.
