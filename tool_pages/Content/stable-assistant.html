About Stable Assistant

Stable Assistant is a chatbot developed by Stability AI, utilizing the company's advanced text and image generation technology. The platform is designed to offer an array of creative capabilities, including image generation from text inputs, chat services, and text-based content creation. Stable Assistant is powered by two main technologies: Stable Diffusion 3 and Stable LM 2 12B. These technologies are capable of generating impressive imagery from prompts and enhancing text generation, respectively. Stable Assistant also includes a feature labelled 'Stable Image Services', which presents functionalities like 'Search and Replace', 'Remove Background', 'Creative Upscale', 'Control Structure', 'Outpaint', 'Conservative Upscale' and 'Sketch' for image optimization. Additionally, users can use the 'Stable Video' feature to transform text or image inputs into captivating videos. Stable Assistant can prove to be a vital tool for tasks including drafting blogs, editing scripts, or even developing ideal captions for images.
