About Simplified

Simplified is an all-in-one app designed for modern marketing teams to save time and collaborate more efficiently. It features a vast library of free images, videos, and audio clips, thousands of designer templates, and content writing capabilities in multiple languages. It also has a content calendar for scheduling and publishing posts to social media, and all of its features are free to use.The app offers a variety of tools for graphic design, AI writing, video and animation, social media planning, and more. Its AI image generator, background remover, magic resizer, animation maker, content rewriter, long form writer, and Instagram Reels are especially popular. It also has posts and stories, online ads, ecommerce, and business tools, as well as a wide selection of design and video templates.Its AI writer tool provides users with the ability to generate images from text, create SEO-driven copy, and write long-form content in 30+ languages and 10+ tones. It also has a content rewriter, company bio generator, and a hashtag generator. Finally, users can publish content to social media channels using its social media scheduling and planning tools, and analyze the performance of their posts with its social media analytics.
