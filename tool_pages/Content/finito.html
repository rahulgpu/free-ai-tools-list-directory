About Finito

Finito AI is a versatile tool designed to integrate artificial intelligence capabilities into any application or website. Its functionality is triggered by selecting text and pressing the control key. Once activated, Finito opens a list of commands that enhance and modify the selected text using its underlying AI technology, ChatGPT. This AI-based software aids in tasks such as grammar correction, writing improvement, idea generation, language translation, and answering queries among other applications. Beyond its primary text-related functionalities, Finito allows users to pose questions directly to it, adding another layer of interaction. Finito offers a personalizable experience, with users being able to customize their commands and shortcuts according to their preference. Finito AI is available to download free of charge for Mac and Windows operating systems.
