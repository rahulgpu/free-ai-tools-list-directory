About ChatLabs

ChatLabs is a centralized platform offering access to various AI models from different providers. It has integrated several advanced tools like OpenAI, Claude, Gemini, Llama, Groq, Mistral, and others to provide users with a variety of AI functionalities. The platform provides support for more than 30 professional models, maximizing efficiency in AI-related tasks. Users can utilize the tools to chat, write, web search, and even generate images with models like DALL-E 3 and Stable Diffusion. The additional features provided by ChatLabs include AI assistants and bots which can be pre-set or personalized for users' specific needs. Users can also use the platform to generate AI-derived images and utilize voice input for text-to-speech functionalities. Advanced features like pinned chats and search functionalities ensure efficiency in chat organization at larger scale. Users have the ability to upload documents, PDFs, images and gather relevant information from these files. A notable function is the split-screen mode which allows for a side by side comparison of the performance of different AI models. Compatible with the Chrome browser, users can bring AI functionalities to every tab. The platform offers two subscription plans, 'Bring your Keys Plan' and 'Professional Plan', both providing different levels of access to AI models and features.
