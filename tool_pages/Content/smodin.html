About Smodin

Smodin is a multi-lingual writing assistance platform designed to assist students, writers and internet workers with their daily tasks. It offers a suite of tools and apps to help users improve their writing, including a text rewriter, plagiarism checker, auto citation machine, summarizer and multi-lingual translator. The platform supports over 20 languages, including English, Arabic, Bengali, Bulgarian, Chinese, Danish, Dutch, Esperanto, Filipino, Finnish, French, German, Greek, Hebrew, Hindi, Hungarian, Indonesian, Italian, Japanese, Korean, Latvian, Lithuanian, Malay, Malayalam, Marathi, Norwegian, Polish, Portuguese, Romanian, Russian, Serbian, Slovak, Slovenian, Spanish, Swedish, Tajik, Tamil, Telugu, Thai, Turkish, Ukrainian, Urdu and Vietnamese. The platform is free to use and offers an AI powered Writer tool and Rewriter tool to help users quickly create content. Other popular tools include a Plagiarism Checker, Multi-Lingual Translator, Summarizer, Smodin Omni, Citation Generator, Image to Text and PDF Parser, Multi-Lingual Grammar Correction, Real Time Translated Subtitles, Speech to Text and Text to Speech. Smodin is trusted by millions of users every month and provides a reliable and easy-to-use writing assistance platform.
