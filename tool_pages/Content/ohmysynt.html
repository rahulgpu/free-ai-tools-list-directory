About Ohmysynt

AI-Synt is a tool that offers personalized AI content for brands, aiming to speed up production cycles and generate brand-tailored, commercially-safe content at a larger scale. It enables marketers to create content that is specific to their brand, resulting in a more engaging experience for their audience. The tool allows users to train a digital copy, known as AI-Synt, on their existing content. With AI-Synt, users can insert humans, products, or characters into generated scenes, giving them full control over the generation and editing process. This feature provides flexibility and customization options for content creation.AI-Synt also boasts fast generation speed, enabling users to produce content efficiently. It offers unlimited image generation and unlimited generative editing, along with free prompting and presets. The aspect ratios supported are 1:1, 4:5, 16:9, and 9:16, ensuring compatibility with various platforms and devices.The tool is trusted by reputable brands and enterprises, including Pernod Ricard, Olmeca Tequila, CGTN, The Diigitals, and Google Startup. It offers different pricing plans, tailored to both enterprises and small to medium-sized businesses (SMBs), allowing them to create regular content at scale. The tool also provides a free trial period, allowing users to experience its features before committing to a subscription.Overall, AI-Synt is a valuable tool for marketers looking to streamline their content production process, generate brand-specific content, and enhance engagement rates.
