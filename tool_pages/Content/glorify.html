About Glorify

Glorify is an online graphic design tool primarily designed for e-commerce business owners. It provides users with the ability to create professional and high conversion social media posts, ads, infographics, presentations, and more. The tool hosts AI-powered features, including AI Images for generating any image from a text prompt, AI Product BG for inserting products into AI-generated scenes, and AI Writer for creating compelling copy for designs. The tool also features a Background Remover for creating product-sharp images, and a Batch Editing feature for creating and adjusting designs at scale. Glorify hosts a library of over 11,000 customizable templates for various themes and purposes, easy image editing tools, and access to premium stock photo libraries. The tool also enables collaboration among teams with features for organizing design files into projects and creating professional slideshows for presenting ideas. Future updates include the integration of a video editor, live collaboration features, batch processing, plugin marketplace, and more AI tools.
