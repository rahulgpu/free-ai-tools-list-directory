About Pixelied

Pixelied is a free online graphic design suite that provides users with a versatile image editing and graphics creation tool. Designed to boost creativity, this lightning-fast photo editor is ideal for various purposes such as marketing, e-commerce, and social media designs. Users can easily create a wide range of visuals, including social media covers, ads, stories, and posts for platforms like Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn, and Twitter. For video platforms like YouTube and Twitch, users can design thumbnails, banners, intros, panels, and offline banners.The tool also offers options for creating banners and headers for Twitter, email, Etsy, Soundcloud, LinkedIn, and Patreon. Additionally, users can design image quotes, Pinterest pins, podcast cover art, Kindle covers, eBook covers, and Wattpad covers. Pixelied provides a variety of photo effects and filters, enabling users to enhance their images with adjustments such as brightness, sharpness, hue, saturation, tint, vignette, and contrast. The tool also offers a range of image editing features like blur, flip, rotate, resize, crop, merge, overlay, and more. It even includes an image converter, SVG editor, icon maker, PNG maker, and a photo enhancer.With a wide selection of templates available, users can create professional-looking designs for social media posts, covers, and ads, as well as YouTube thumbnails, banners, outros, Twitch panels, and more. The tool also offers templates for banners, email headers, Etsy and Soundcloud banners, Patreon banners, eCommerce images, blog featured images, podcast cover art, eBook covers, and image quotes.Overall, Pixelied is a powerful online graphic design suite that empowers users to create eye-catching visuals for various online platforms and purposes, enhancing their creativity and design capabilities.
