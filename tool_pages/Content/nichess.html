About Nichess

nichesss is an AI-powered writing and copywriting software designed to create diverse types of content, assisting users in generating blog posts, ads, social media content, business ideas, and even poems. A notable feature of this tool is its ability to alleviate writer's block by swiftly producing content at the click of a button. nichesss offers over 150 tools for various content needs, ranging from business name generation, blog post title creation, to conversion of text from active to passive voice and vice-versa, acronym generation, and AI-based image creation. The software also includes an AI Art Prompt Helper which generates art prompts to be used with nichesss, DALLE-2 or Stable Diffusion. A dedicated author bio generator and AMA tool are also included, making it a comprehensive solution for various writing requirements. Furthermore, the platform offers a free trial and a no-contract subscription plan, making it accessible for users to explore its potential.
