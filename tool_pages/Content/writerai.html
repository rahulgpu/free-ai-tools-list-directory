About WriterAI

WriterAI is an artificial intelligence powered content writer application designed to generate original and quality content in seconds. Leveraging advanced AI technologies, users can create a wide variety of content ranging from blog post ideas to actual blog posts, and update them later using the inbuilt editor. WriterAI also includes a Tweet Generator feature, allowing users to generate and share tweets on specific topics. The application further extends its functionality by generating unique and engaging captions for users' social media posts instantly. In addition to blog and social media content, WriterAI supports content generation for platforms such as YouTube, Facebook, and Instagram. All content generated by the user is saved for future reference, and can be updated, favourited, and shared at the user's discretion. Despite its robust offerings, the application ensures privacy by not collecting any data from its users. WriterAI is primarily designed for iPhone, but is also compatible with iPod touch, Mac with an Apple M1 chip or later, and Apple Vision.
