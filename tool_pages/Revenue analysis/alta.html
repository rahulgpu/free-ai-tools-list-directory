About Alta

Alta is an AI-powered business observability platform designed for data-driven teams to discover, understand, trust, and collaborate on data effectively. The platform primarily focuses on enhancing business revenues by providing comprehensive visibility and confidence into revenue engines. Alta accomplishes this by facilitating alignment between every flow in your business to increase revenue. It offers real-time data collection and metric tracking capabilities. Its connectors enable integration with platforms like Salesforce, HubSpot, Stripe, and Tableau to further leverage data for driving impact. Users can customize and visualize their metrics with Alta's metrics library, offering predefined templates and metrics coupled with industry best practices. The Alta platform also comes with an AI assistant, Alta Copilot, which provides instant, actionable insights via push notifications across various communication channels such as Slack, email, SMS. It is built to help its users stay updated with changes in revenue flows and optimize quickly. The platform also promotes collaboration, enabling various stakeholders to work in a unified platform to achieve revenue targets more efficiently. The focus on enterprise-level privacy and security is one of their key selling points, boasting SOC2 compliance.
