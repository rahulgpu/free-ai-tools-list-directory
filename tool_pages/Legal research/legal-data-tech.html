About Legal Data Tech

Legal Data LTD is an AI-powered tool developed by lawyers for lawyers that modernizes legal research. It has a unified search line capability that understands synonyms, legal jargon, abbreviations and can correct spelling mistakes while providing suggestions. This search functionality allows lawyers to find all types of legal information including laws and case studies relevant to both commercial and criminal law on one single platform. The Legal Data LTD tool goes beyond providing law-related information. It includes a platform where lawyers can create professional profiles and be discovered by potential clients globally. It makes the process of finding the right lawyer less challenging by allowing users to discover and view detailed profiles of lawyers from anywhere in the world. Another feature of Legal Data LTD is an AI-assistant named FlyBot that has been trained specifically on chosen laws and case studies to provide accurate legal answers. The sources from which the information is derived is then provided for more thorough research. Legal Data LTD offers an intuitive interface that can make legal research quick and easy and changes the traditional approach to legal services.
