About vLex

vLex is an AI-powered legal research platform designed to revolutionize legal practices by increasing productivity. It offers a wide range of products and solutions to cater to different needs within the legal profession. Among these are tools for legal research like vLex Legal Research, DocketAlarm, and FastCase Legal Research. vLex also has solutions for connecting your data, like the vLex Cloud and Vincent AI. For data analysis, vLex provides analytics tools like Docket Alarm Analytics and vLex Analytics. The platform also includes tools such as Chrome Extension, WordPlugin, and vLex Mobile along with practice management solutions like NextChapter, Quolaw, and Eunomia. For bigger organizations, vLex provides enterprise solutions like Legal Data APIs, Iceberg AI, and custom solutions. This platform is designed to work with everyone from solo and small firms to large law firms, law schools, government agents and more. However, its utility doesn't end at research and using its primary tools as vLex offers further resources in the form of user guides, video walkthroughs, training, and a knowledge base to help users navigate their platform and its capabilities.
