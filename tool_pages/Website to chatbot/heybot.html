About HeyBot

EmbedAI is a platform designed to create AI chatbots utilizing ChatGPT models. The platform was developed out of a need to manage large volumes of information and provide swift automated responses to user queries. Users are empowered to train the AI on various sources such as files, websites, and even YouTube content, facilitating a comprehensive learning process for the chatbot. Users can also customize the appearance of the chatbot to align with their brand identity, including logos, color selections, and more intricate style details. One of the other distinguishing features of the tool is that the created chatbot can be shared as a chat bubble, an embed code, or a link, providing flexibility based on the user's requirements. Notably, EmbedAI is also capable of integrating with other applications via API or Zapier, enabling greater ease of use within existing software ecosystems. One of the core capabilities of the platform is its support for queries and responses in over 100 languages, making it a multilingual solution. EmbedAI allows users to use the power of AI across different platforms like WordPress, Shopify, and Bubble among others, enabling tailored AI solutions on a diversity of web interfaces. Users can change the initial prompt for the AI, and the platform supports the ingestion of data in various formats including .pdf files and web links. The AI and the data are hosted on secure servers.
