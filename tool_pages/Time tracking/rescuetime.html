About RescueTime

RescueTime is a time management software designed to help users understand their daily habits and increase productivity. This automated tool provides detailed reports and insights about the time spent on various apps, websites, and even offline activities, eliminating the need for manual time tracking. Its defining feature is the ability for users to block distracting sites and apps that impair focus, thereby optimizing productivity. RescueTime also enables setting personalized goals, which, with the assistance of real-time alerts, guide users behavior throughout the day. An additional feature, referred to as FocusTime, reduces digital distractions, further boosting productive hours. For those looking to integrate productivity tracking into their existing workflow, RescueTime provides integrations with popular applications. There is also an extension, 'Timesheets AI' that allows users to efficiently track and assign time to different projects or clients. Whether it's meeting schedule management or distraction-blocking, RescueTime offers a comprehensive package for time management and productivity enhancement.
