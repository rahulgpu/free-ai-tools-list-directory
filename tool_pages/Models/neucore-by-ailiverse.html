About NeuCore by AIliverse

Kookree offers AI technologies that are accessible and open to everyone with a central philosophy rooted in transparency. They are committed to open-sourcing their research and models to advance humanity's well-being. Kookree introduces two primary AI tools: 'Chicken' and 'Sensemaker'. 'Chicken' is an AI tool that can create realistic and imaginative videos from provided textual data. The AI interprets the text and generates video content that aligns with the embedded information making it suitable for creative content production. 'Sensemaker', on the other hand, is a multimodal AI tool that comprehends videos in a human-like manner. This can be useful in multiple scenarios like video analysis, content moderation and machine-based video interpretation. Kookree's AI models are engineered for optimal speed and efficiency without compromising the quality of results. The models are deployable via serverless APIs, public cloud, or on-premise installations, providing a wide array of flexible deployment options. Plus, by employing sparse training techniques, Kookree's models claim superior performance speed compared to traditional deep learning models. Heralding a mission to leverage AI's potential to solve complex challenges, Kookree aims to develop safe, beneficial, and universally accessible AI technologies.
