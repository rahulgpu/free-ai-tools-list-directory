About Kili

Kili is an automation platform designed to assist sales, finance, and operations teams by reducing manual and repetitive tasks. Its interface, the visual canvas, allows users to build custom solutions using natural language and Large Language Models (LLMs), facilitating easy customization based on specific business needs. Additionally, Kili offers seamless integration with existing business applications to ensure synchronization of data across different systems in the organization. Once established, it allows automation processes to be executed on demand, triggered by emails, a set schedule, or events. The platform accommodates diverse use cases, from invoice processing and sales order processing to Request for Proposal (RFP) responses and contract management, among others. For instance, it can match invoices to purchase orders, creating custom approval flows and providing real-time exception notifications to reduce manual data entry and improve accuracy.Moreover, Kili enables automation of sales operations. It not only offers capabilities for automating invoice tracking and bank reconciliation, but can also find hyper-personalised leads and enrich lead data within CRMs. Contract management is streamlined by the extraction of key terms from vendor and customer contracts, allowing for automated notifications of upcoming renewals and reducing manual review time.By providing real-time logs and notifications for operations, Kili ensures a high degree of transparency and accountability while maintaining privacy and security.In all, Kili presents extensive ways for companies to leverage AI to automate processes and operations, enhancing accuracy and efficiency across departments.
