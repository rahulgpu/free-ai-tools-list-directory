About Olli

Olli.ai is an AI data platform that empowers teams to make data-driven decisions. It provides an intuitive interface where members can ask questions using natural language, and Olli will answer with data-backed insights. Olli also automatically locates the data needed to generate reports and dashboards, and provides explanations for accuracy. It also has an intuitive editing tool to style dashboards, and the ability to export to favorite tools. Olli grows smarter as it is used, as it actively generates relevant insights to help teams meet their objectives. All of this is done in a fraction of the time it would take a team to manually search for the data and insights they need. With Olli.ai, teams can make their data-driven decisions 10x faster.
