About Painted Saintly

Painted Saintly is a unique tool leveraging AI to transform user-provided photos into portrayals of saintly figures. The use of advanced AI complicates the process of creating highly distinctive, digital prints and personalized gifts. To start, users create an account and initiate a new project. Upon project setup, users provide twenty high-quality personal images for AI processing. Based on these images, the tool generates a set of 30 custom digital portraits in around half an hour, although the exact time may vary based on system load. The quality of the portraits is strongly tied to the quality of the images submitted by the user. Final portraits can be used for various digital purposes such as social media posts. Additionally, users are permitted to select their favorite portraits and order them printed on physical items such as candles, canvases, or mugs. The tool is particularly focused on user privacy, ensuring user data privacy through strategies like storage encryption, immediate removal of images and models post-portrait creation, and 30-day deletion of generated portraits. The tool costs $9.99 per project, which includes the creation of 30 custom portraits. Lastly, Painted Saintly provides customer support to address any unanswered questions or concerns.
