About GoEmailTracker

GoEmailTracker is a Google Mail extension that allows for the tracking of emails sent through the service. It provides an easy-to-use interface that's user-friendly and provides detailed reports and analytics to help you make data-driven decisions to improve email performance. Its features include tracking the number of recipients who have opened the email, clicks on links, and recipients' geolocation when opening or clicking on emails. The extension also has advanced security features to protect data, and users receive real-time notification pop-ups when emails are opened. GoEmailTracker is a valuable tool for businesses and individuals who use email marketing. It helps to improve the effectiveness of campaigns, identify engaged recipients, and increase conversions. It tracks opens, clicks, click-through rates, and geolocations of recipients, providing insights into which emails are most successful. It also has a 24/7 support system, email templates, and AI email writer using chatGPT and openAI. GoEmailTracker offers a free and premium plan, with the free plan including unlimited email opens and link tracking, and the premium plan providing more comprehensive tracking with history and statistics of opens and link clicks. The software is privacy compliant, and it never shares data with third parties. GoEmailTracker is a valuable tool for businesses and individuals who want to optimize their email marketing campaigns for better results.
