About Vocal Remover by Media.io

Online Vocal Remover by Media.io is an AI-powered tool used for separating and removing vocals and background music (BGM) from any song. It allows users to isolate, extract, or remove instrumentals, vocals, or an acapella portion of a song, effectively outputting the different elements without compromising audio quality. The tool can recognize several languages such as English, Spanish, Japanese, French, Chinese, German, Korean, Indian, and Russian. This separation enables the creation of new compositions using the separated music components. It is especially useful for music producers, music enthusiasts, arrangers, and video creators. Furthermore, the Online Vocal Remover can be used to produce karaoke tracks, create new mixes by remixing vocals or instrumental tracks, extract instrumental music for practice or work/study background music and even convert songs into acapella. The tool can handle most major audio and video formats, including MP3, MP4, and WMV. Users can upload their audio or video files and the tool will automatically separate the vocals and instrumentals in high-quality 44KHz sound. There is also a desktop version offering enhanced performance, fast file uploads, and greater resilience against network fluctuations.
