About JAQI

The JAQI AI Industrial Materials Search Engine is an AI-enabled platform that's purpose-built to streamline and digitise workflows in various sectors, notably Industry 4.0. Specifically designed for e-commerce, marketplaces, portals, and ERPs, this tool has a unique capacity for translation and search in industrial contexts. The search engine simplifies data entry, allowing users to copy and paste queries or type directly into it, which it then processes with its AI technology. This can help increase efficiency by delivering accurate search results. An outstanding feature of this tool is its patented search technology that promotes fast and easy product discovery across any catalog in any format. Moreover, JAQI translates buyer shorthand, converting the buyer's descriptions into searchable terms, thus facilitating convenient and effective product searches. Additionally, it supports multiple-item searches, which allows users to search for many items simultaneously, enhancing productivity. The tool's modular architecture enables easy integration into e-commerce applications like webstores and ERPs, enhancing search speed and accuracy for customers. Overall, JAQI AI Industrial Material Search Engine is designed to be a powerful tool for industrial B2B suppliers.
