About Heardeer

heardeer is an AI-led platform for conducting user and customer interviews. This tool focuses on facilitating the collection of insightful user feedback by automating the interview process, freeing up the user to handle more complex tasks. Interviews conducted through heardeer are personalized and in-depth, driven by a voice AI that is capable of creating custom, expert-level interview questionnaires to suit the specific goals of the user. The tool also includes features for generating screener questions, setting logical conditions for screeners, and organizing user groups for targeted responses. In addition to its advanced automation capabilities, heardeer has a multilingual feature, conducting interviews and working in up to 15 languages, without the need for human translators, thereby catering to global users and offering real-time, culturally nuanced engagement. The introverted nature of the AI led interview process provides participants with a comfortable space to share their honest opinions and thoughts. Additionally, all interview results are gathered in your preferred language, thereby making it possible to understand cross-cultural user needs. Data security is also of paramount importance to heardeer, all collected user interviews are encrypted and protected with multiple layers of security.
