About BodyCompAI

BodyCompAI is a fitness platform designed to provide personalized health and training advice. The artificial intelligence (AI)-based tool aims to streamline your fitness journey by providing insights tailored to your unique body composition. It works by analyzing a photo uploaded by the user, then crafting a customized workout plan based on your physique and targeted fitness goals. The AI tries to identify your body's fitness potential and constructs exercises to enhance it. In addition to workout plans, BodyCompAI offers tailored nutrition advice to augment fitness efforts from a dietary standpoint. It generates detailed meal plans that can be adjusted to cater to various dietary restrictions, such as vegan, vegetarian, or gluten-free diets. Furthermore, the platform continues to adapt the provided workout plan based on your progress and preferences, keeping the fitness journey aligned with your needs and objectives. For users unsure or skeptical about how the AI understands their body composition, the platform uses advanced algorithms to assess your uploaded image, estimating crucial metrics like muscle mass and fat percentage among others.
