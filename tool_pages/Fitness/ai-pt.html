About AI-PT

AI-PT is an AI-powered workout generator that allows users to create unique workouts tailored to their preferences. With just a few seconds, users can generate a personalized workout based on the input they provide, such as body focus and duration. The tool aims to provide a solution for those who do not have access to a personal trainer.Once a workout is generated, users have the option to schedule it for a later date, ensuring they can easily incorporate it into their fitness routine. The tool also saves the workouts to a personal library, enabling users to find and access their workouts effortlessly.AI-PT offers both a free and premium plan. The free plan allows beginners to get started with limited features, including storing up to 5 workouts in the library, limited body part focus, and limited equipment selection. The premium plan, available for a monthly fee, offers more advanced features such as unlimited workout storage in the library, advanced body part focus, and advanced equipment selection. Users can also share their workouts with friends.For additional information, users can refer to the frequently asked questions section, which addresses topics such as free trial availability, workout creation process, workout customization options, and exercises instructions.Overall, AI-PT provides a convenient and customizable solution for individuals looking to generate personalized workouts without the need for a personal trainer.
