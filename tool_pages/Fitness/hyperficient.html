About Hyperficient

Hyperficient uses AI to analyse all of the hypertrophy scientific literature, generate science-backed personalised programmes designed to build muscle as efficiently as possible, and also educate people on the science behind lifting. If you consistently go to the gym, you'll build muscle; but by aligning your workouts with science you can maximise your gains. Save hours in the gym. Make faster progress. Instead of getting your gym information from social media, Hyperficient teaches you everything you need to know about science-based training. It is a comprehensive knowledge base. This tool is perfect for people who want to maximise their gains in the gym, save time, and learn more about how to lift in alignment with science
