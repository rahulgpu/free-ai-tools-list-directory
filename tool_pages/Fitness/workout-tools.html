About Workout Tools

Workout Tools is an AI-powered personal training tool designed with the intent of making workout regimens more effective, personalized, and tailored to the user's specific needs. The resourcefulness of this tool is exemplified in its adaptability which includes customizing workout sessions based on parameters like the user's gender, age, height, and weight. Workout Tools leaves room for a selection range focusing on specific muscles or involving a full-body training depending on the user's preference. Additionally, there's a further benefit in that users can adjust their workout regimen to coincide with the equipment available at their disposal. With a vast database of over 800 exercises, users can be assured of finding a session best suited to them for optimal results. Moreover, the platform offers both standard and premium subscription plans. The premium plan includes additional customization and the flexibility to express preference or aversion towards certain exercises, which is intended to help improve overall user experience. The tool is also equipped with a feature to generate new workout sessions which adds more depth to the range of its functionalities.
