About Zing Coach

Zing Coach is an AI-enabled mobile fitness application designed to deliver personalized workout sessions to users. Using proprietary algorithms and AI, it customizes exercises and training intensities based on individual users' body composition, fitness levels, and activity. The App offers a body composition scan providing insights with lean and fat mass percentages that are used to create workout regimens tailored to each user's unique body data. Moreover, Zing utilizes a motion-tracking Fitness Test, tracking the user's performance in various exercises to ensure the correct form and prevent injuries. The app encompasses over 400 guided exercises, learning users' preferences over time for effective and enjoyable workouts. It further adapts based on users' timings, travelling schedule, and daily activity patterns. Zing Coach is also capable of calculating users' daily caloric needs and suggests suitable nutrition guidance. The app integrates with Apple Health to provide a comprehensive fitness program and employs computer vision technology to monitor user's movements during workouts. Overall, Zing Coach offers a flexible, data-driven, and user-centred approach to fitness training.
