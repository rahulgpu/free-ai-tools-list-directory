About Planfit

Planfit is a free AI-powered fitness app primarily designed for gym beginners. It offers personalized workout plans generated by an AI trainer named 'Max'. The app creates individualized workouts tailored to users' goals, strength level, training ability, past workouts, and available gym equipment. It adapts over time by optimizing sets, reps, and weights with each user's workout. Planfit also provides detailed exercise guides, including clear video instructions, useful trainer tips, correct forms, and even breathing techniques. A distinct feature of the app is its ability to calculate and display progress visually so users can closely monitor their muscle recovery, exercise achievements, and workout balance. Max, the AI trainer, provides ongoing motivation and guidance throughout the user's fitness journey. Planfit also offers the functionality to view and share workout results. The targeted exercises, intuitive performance tracking, and personalized training provided by the Planfit app can contribute to an effective and balanced fitness routine.
