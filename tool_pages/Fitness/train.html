About TrAIn

TrAIn is an Artificial Intelligence-powered tool that generates tailor-made training programs for individuals aiming to achieve their fitness goals. It provides an ideal, science-based training regimen specifically designed to fit the users' personal objectives. Being personalized, the tool is designed to help gym-goers avoid aimlessness, stay motivated, and evade potential injuries often associated with one-size-fits-all exercise routines. Each program crafted by TrAIn is devised considering the uniqueness of every individual, thus preventing frustration or disengagement experienced with generic workout programs. The programs generated start from introductions to bodywork, then progress to high-intensity workouts and specific exercises with complete guides on execution, sets, reps, and rest. In addition to physical training, TrAIn offers lifestyle training programs. An interesting feature of TrAIn is its ability to adapt and evolve the workout routine based on the individual's progression, helping them stay on track towards their goals. The platform also provides support to manage injuries that may occur during training. Users can access these services through a monthly subscription. TrAIn emphasizes prompt delivery, and offers round-the-clock support to answer user queries on system operation and payment procedure.
