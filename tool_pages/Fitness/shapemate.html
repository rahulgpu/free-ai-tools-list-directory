About Shapemate

Shapemate is an AI tool designed to promote and simplify healthy living. It amalgamates assorted fitness applications into a conversation on WhatsApp, facilitating effortless tracking and managing of workout programs and calorie consumptions. With this intelligent tool, users can generate custom workouts and healthy recipes. Additionally, Shapemate offers a feature to study and save user's meals for a more personalized dietary approach. On the exercise front, Shapemate enables users to input their workout details to calculate calories burned. The tool provides a unique way to chat with the AI to get regular training suggestions, nutritional tips and create personalized workout programs. Shapemate stands out as an easy-to-use system with availability around the clock, personalized recipe options, and a composite solution for nutrition and training queries.
