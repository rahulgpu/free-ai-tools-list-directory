About MyBuddyFit

ElloeAI is an AI-based Fitness Coach designed to provide users with customized fitness and nutrition advice according to their unique health goals. The tool is designed to support a range of goals, from general health improvement to specific objectives such as muscle gain or weight loss. Convenience is a key component of ElloeAI with users being able to log their meals via text or photo and receive workout recommendations by asking the platform's AI, named MyBuddy. In addition to these features, the tool provides daily reports containing personalized feedback and tips related to diet and lifestyle modifications. This AI tool seeks to serve as a comprehensive solution for users' health and fitness needs by combining meal logging, personalized recommendations, and feedback in one platform. With the advantage of AI technology, ElloeAI mimics the role of a personal trainer, fostering accountability and help users to keep track of their progress.
