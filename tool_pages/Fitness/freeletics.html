About Freeletics

FREELETICS is an AI-based fitness tool that designs individual workout plans according to your fitness level. The tool focuses on high-intensity training, facilitating users to engage in physical exercise anytime and anywhere. The targeted plans provide personalized guidelines designed to motivate and challenge users to achieve their fitness goals more proficiently. The FREELETICS tool is not just about providing one-off workout plans. It establishes an understanding of the user's fitness level and continuously develops and modifies the plan as the user progresses. The flexible and accessible nature of this tool accounts for different lifestyles and circumstances, making it suitable for those with busy schedules or limited resources. Therefore, providing a user-friendly and personalized fitness experience is the core facet of FREELETICS. Please note that the tool may require an Internet connection for initial setup and plan modifications.
