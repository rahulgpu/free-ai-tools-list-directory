About MoveMentor

MoveMentor is a personal mobile coach that helps users improve their exercise technique through AI technology. With the MoveMentor mobile app, users can simply put their mobile phone in front of them and start exercising while the AI system analyzes their movements and provides personalized recommendations for corrections. This innovative tool is designed for individuals who engage in offline or online training and may have doubts about the correctness of their exercise execution without a coach. It is also suitable for those who strive to improve their physical fitness and health, value comfort, and prefer to work out in the comfort of their own space.MoveMentor offers several key features and advantages. First, users receive real-time feedback and corrections of their movements, ensuring that they perform exercises correctly and reduce the risk of injury. Second, this tool is cost-effective compared to hiring a personal trainer. Third, users can access help and support from qualified trainers. Fourth, MoveMentor provides the convenience of working out anywhere and anytime without compromising the quality of workouts. Finally, users can be confident that the training provided by MoveMentor only benefits their health.MoveMentor offers different subscription packages, including a trial package, standard package, and premium package. These packages include features such as personalized exercise recommendations, training plans, goal-based recommendations, and tracking of progress. Users can leave a request on the waiting list to be notified about the start of sales and choose the package that suits their needs. Overall, MoveMentor aims to enhance exercise performance and ensure effective and safe workouts with the assistance of AI technology.
