About SmartPrep

SmartPrep is an AI-powered tool designed to streamline lesson planning process for educators. The tool enhances the efficiency of lesson planning by reducing repetitive tasks and freeing up time for teachers to focus more on in-class teaching. It offers a wealth of teaching resources including free templates, project-based learning (PBL), understanding by design (UbD) frameworks, and various assessment tools. Exercises based on different standards, such as PBL, IQL, Flipped Classroom and UbD, are available to foster creativity in lesson planning. Additionally, the tool assists in collecting relevant teaching resources from the web, including web pages, images, videos, and academic content, reducing the time spent on manual searches. It also includes an instant resource collection feature which uses AI to search the web for relevant materials. Teachers can also create a variety of customizable assessments such as quizzes, MCQs, FRQs, Essay Prompts, and Discussions. Trusted by teachers worldwide, the platform provides structured yet flexible templates for lesson planning. Its range of offerings also extends to up-to-date informative articles on diverse educational topics.
