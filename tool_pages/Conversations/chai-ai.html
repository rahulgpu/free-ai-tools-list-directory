About Chai AI

Chai AI is a platform specializing in conversational generative artificial intelligence. The platform gathers language model developers and chat AI enthusiasts with the goal to make advances towards Artificial General Intelligence (AGI). In addition to functioning as a hub for generative AI, Chai AI also presents a unique opportunity for developers to create language-based models, likened to YouTube's platform serving content creators. Leveraging their proprietary dataset of user-bot messages and machine learning, the models trained are optimized for entertainment, providing an immersive and engaging user experience. The team is composed of individuals from diverse professional backgrounds, including ex-Quant Traders and Researchers, each bringing a unique perspective and skill-set to the platform. Chai AI demonstrates a dedication to innovation and a commitment to creating a digital environment conducive for the growth and development of AI technology, mapping their progress through research publications on methods such as reward models, rejection sampling, blending and reinforcement learning with human feedback.
