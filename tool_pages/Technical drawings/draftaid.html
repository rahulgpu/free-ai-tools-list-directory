About DraftAid

DraftAid is a tool designed to streamline the fabrication drawing process by utilizing advanced AI technology. It targets the inefficiencies of traditional methods such as lengthy fabrication times, misallocation of engineering expertise, and susceptibility to human error. DraftAid automates the creation of fabrication drawings from 3D models, greatly reducing the time required and ensuring precision and consistency. It works with all major 3D modeling software and is designed to free designers from trivial and repetitive work. DraftAid is easily integrable with existing CAD software, enhancing current workflows without the need for disruptive changes or extensive retraining. It offers extensive customization options to align with specific project requirements, ensuring consistently accurate and high-quality drawings. The tool allows for a one-click initiation of the drawing process and provides an option for quick review of the automatically generated drawings before finalization.
