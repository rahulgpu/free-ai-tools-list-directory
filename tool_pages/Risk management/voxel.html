About Voxel

Voxel is an AI-powered site intelligence platform intended for various industries, such as food and beverage, logistics and supply chain, manufacturing, and the ports and customs sector. This multi-functional tool is designed to enhance the safety and efficiency of operations and to empower leaders to make strategic decisions. The platform provides intelligence, visibility, insights, action functionalities, and boasts robust security features. It offers multiple resources including case studies and blogs. Voxel can be particularly useful for insurance companies and employers, helping protect the workforce and accelerate operations. The tool is provided by a dedicated team, with a careers section on their website for further growth of the organization. Users are able to login and request a demo to fully experience the capabilities of the platform. Overall, Voxel is a versatile tool that leverages AI to help improve decision-making and increase operational efficiency within businesses.
