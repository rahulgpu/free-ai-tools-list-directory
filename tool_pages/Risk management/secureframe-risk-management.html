About Secureframe Risk Management

Secureframe's Risk Management solution is an AI-powered tool that automates the assessment and management of risks in an organization. It aims to save time and reduce costs associated with maintaining a strong risk management program. The tool follows the ISO 27005 methodology to effectively assess risks in an environment and make informed decisions for a security compliance program. It allows users to assess and document treatment plans to meet the criteria for frameworks such as SOC 2, ISO 27001, PCI, and HIPAA. With a risk library that includes NIST risk scenarios for categories like Fraud, Legal, Finance, and IT, organizations can easily add these risks to their risk register for tracking. Users can also track changes made to individual risks and view snapshots of the risk register to demonstrate the steps taken to enhance security posture.Secureframe's Risk Management solution enables organizations to link controls to known risks, allowing for coordinated risk management strategies with compliance requirements. This helps assess residual risk and identify any gaps in the risk management program.The tool offers customization options, allowing users to adjust scoring scales, risk score groups, and use custom tags to categorize risks according to their business needs. Additionally, the solution provides intuitive dashboards that visually monitor an organization's risks over time, displaying heat maps, summary tables, trend charts, and more. This allows for easy communication of the health of the risk management program to executives, auditors, and other stakeholders
