About MosaicML

MosaicML, now integrated with Databricks, is a comprehensive generative AI platform designed for training and deploying large language models (LLMs) with a focus on simplicity, scalability, and efficiency. The platform caters to users looking to streamline AI model development, providing open-source, commercially-licensed models like MPT-7B and MPT-30B that can be easily integrated into applications. MosaicML offers specialized components such as MosaicML Inference for secure deployment, MosaicML Training for pretraining or finetuning custom models, and a large model stack for simplified training and deployment at scale. The platform emphasizes data privacy, enterprise-grade security, and complete model ownership, allowing users to train models in any cloud environment with the flexibility to switch seamlessly between clouds. With a commitment to transparency, MosaicML enables users to own the models trained on their data, facilitating introspection and better explanation of model decisions. The platform is designed for interoperability, cloud-agnosticism, and enterprise-level reliability, seamlessly integrating with existing data pipelines and experiment trackers. Users can iterate faster with MosaicML's efficiency optimizations, addressing engineering, systems, and research challenges. The modular training stack allows users to choose and modify components based on their specific needs, enhancing flexibility and ease of implementation. Supported by a research team and trusted by ML experts, MosaicML has a track record of successful implementations, such as training Ghostwriter 2.7B LLM for code generation. The platform's prominence is further highlighted by its integration with Databricks and its recognition in the AI community through news coverage and customer testimonials.
