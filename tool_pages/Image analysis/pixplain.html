About Pixplain

Pixplain is an AI-powered tool developed by Merlin designed to unlock visual content. The application lets you interact with images and videos intuitively, enhancing user experience with AI clarity. Accessibility to this tool is simple and involves adding an extension to your browser. Following this, users can freely select any website of their liking to initiate the tool. The main feature, Pixplain Capture Bar, will be activated by clicking the 'Explain this page' button provided on the webpage, thus activating a selection window. Users can then drag and select any desired area on the screen or opt to select the entire screen before proceeding to capture. After the image has been successfully captured by Pixplain, users input a query to receive insightful responses.Running on top AI models such as GPT-4, GPT vision, Claude, and Llama, Pixplain ensures efficient outcomes. One distinctive feature is the implementation of GPT Vision, aiming to provide superior results. Additionally, Pixplain allows users to move its window freely for the most optimal on-screen view. Users are also granted the flexibility to easily copy, update, or change prompts, thus facilitating smoother creative processes.
