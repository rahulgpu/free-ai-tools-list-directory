About Visnet

Visnet is an AI framework designed for research and deployment of off-the-shelf AI models. It features a universal neural network interface compatible with state-of-the-art neural net models related to NLP and Deep Vision Systems. This framework supports modular frontend, serverless multi-compatibility, and Vercel deployment. Visnet's high performance gateway includes Universal ASGI Gateway, DDoS protection, IP filtering, and an authentication protocol layer. Its core AI models are capable of tasks such as translation, license plate recognition, and face feature matching. Visnet's face feature matching system analyzes facial features for identification, emotion, and age detection. The framework also supports drone structural inspection and audio transcription. Visnet is a valuable tool for industries requiring AI solutions for surveillance, autonomous drone inspections, and advanced image and video analysis. Built by a team of committed researchers, the goal of Visnet is to harness the powers of AI to solve distinct problems, providing custom solutions for various clients. Its research team specializes in deep vision systems, addressing complex problems related to surveillance and advanced image and video analysis. The current collaborations extend to companies providing surveillance solutions.
