About Legaliser

Legaliser is an AI-powered platform that utilizes legal AI technology to simplify and streamline document processing for both professionals and the general public. It offers various features aimed at transforming legal tasks, ensuring efficiency, simplicity, and accessibility.With Legaliser's AI legal assistant, users can quickly upload and analyze contracts, as well as create new ones. The platform's AI technology provides instant contract insight, highlighting key details without the need for extensive legal experience or expensive attorneys.Legaliser's AI-driven insights assess the fairness of contract terms, pinpointing unbalanced clauses and providing users with unparalleled insight for informed decision-making. The platform also conducts risk analysis, identifying potential risks in contracts to enable users to make informed decisions and navigate potential issues before signing.Anomaly detection is another key feature of Legaliser, allowing users to easily spot unusual or non-standard clauses within contracts. This technology enables users to identify potential issues and negotiate optimal terms.Legaliser caters to a wide range of users, including small and medium-sized businesses without extensive legal support, large enterprises with complex contract needs, startups, entrepreneurs, and individuals who need to analyze and understand contracts for personal or business purposes.The platform offers comprehensive AI tools such as contract analysis, drafting, insights and suggestions, and reporting to empower users in negotiation, comprehension, and decision-making. Legaliser also provides multiple pricing plans tailored to different user needs, accompanied by email support and personalized assistance options.
