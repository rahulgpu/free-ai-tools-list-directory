About goHeather

goHeather is an AI-powered app designed to streamline contract reviews. This platform offers free real-time analysis to facilitate efficient decision-making. Trained by lawyers, goHeather's AI engine is expertly equipped to navigate common-law contracts worldwide. The tool is secure, SOC-2 compliant, and user-friendly, providing quick and cost-effective contract insights.With goHeather, users can review any contract before signing, potentially saving hundreds of dollars on lawyer fees. It functions similarly to a lawyer, recognizing and discussing most issues in a contract. The AI is localized to the user's jurisdiction and understands the side of the contract they are on.The process of using goHeather is simple and fast. Users can upload their contract in PDF or text format onto the secure platform. The AI engine then analyzes the contract, presenting its findings and discussing each issue comprehensively. Users can leverage these insights to negotiate better terms, identify risks, and confidently finalize their contracts.Additionally, goHeather provides various pricing options. There is a free version that allows for two contract reviews, while premium accounts have additional credits and access to more advanced AI models. The tool supports most common-law jurisdictions worldwide and can review a wide range of contracts, including employment contracts, purchase agreements, rental agreements, and more.goHeather prioritizes the privacy and security of user data. The platform is SOC-2 compliant and does not save the entire contract after the review is complete. Only the insights are saved. For enterprise clients, there is an option to host a private version of the AI.
