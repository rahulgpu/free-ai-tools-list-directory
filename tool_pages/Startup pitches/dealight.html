About Dealight

Dealight is an AI-powered tool designed to facilitate the process of pitching business ideas by aligning pitches with relevant investors. Dealight uses advanced AI to provide real-time insights for refining pitch decks based on publicly available and private data sources. The AI analysis involves various elements like market research, competitor analysis, team analysis, and social media analysis. It also includes simulating potential follow-up questions from investors. Besides meticulously analyzing your business, Dealight scores your pitch deck based on each on-boarded investor's preference. The tool plays a matchmaking role by connecting you with suitable investors who give your pitch deck a high score. Users firstly upload and analyze their pitch deck, then refine their deck based on the provided analysis feedback. Once satisfied, users submit an application along with their refined pitch deck. Dealight's ultimate goal is to pair you with investors whose interests align with your pitch's promise. It is important to note that Dealight ensures the safety and security of users pitch decks, storing them securely and only sharing them with third-party investors upon user's permission.
