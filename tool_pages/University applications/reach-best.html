About Reach Best

Reach Best is an AI-powered app designed to assist high school students as they apply to universities around the globe, including countries like the USA, UK, Canada, Australia, and Japan. With its inherent AI capabilities, it aims to simplify the university application process by allowing students to choose universities that match their profile, predict their chances of admission, and assist in writing application essays. Reach Best provides accurate predictions with its advanced algorithm models, facilitating an edge in college admissions. The functionalities of the app are also incorporated with real student reviews to provide realistic insights regarding the universities. Additionally, a notable feature of Reach Best is its 24/7 personal support team, ready to assist students in ensuring successful university applications. Furthermore, the AI explanations feature provides comprehensive feedback for students to improve their applications. Reach Best is not only a tool for students, but also a platform aiming to reduce stress and burnout for teachers and counselors involved in the application process. The app is tailored to fit the needs of aspiring university students, providing a streamlined, user-friendly platform for university applications.
