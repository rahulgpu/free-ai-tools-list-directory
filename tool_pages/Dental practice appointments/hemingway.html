About Hemingway

Hemingway is an AI-powered tool designed specifically to automate patient scheduling processes for dental practices. The platform offers features that cover various aspects of patient engagement and appointment management. Feature highlights include AI-powered reactivation, on-call receptionist services, and automated rescheduling. Hemingway effectively manages patient engagement by automatically scheduling patients who do not book an appointment or go inactive, aiming to reduce the time and effort dental practices invest in managing these tasks. The on-call receptionist component captures patient appointment requests at all hours, ensuring that there are minimal missed opportunities for engagements and bookings. Moreover, Hemingways automated rescheduling capability enables dental practices to efficiently manage last-minute cancellations by rescheduling the appointments based on pre-set rules. Hemingway also offers intelligent text services, allowing two-way communication regarding appointment times and booking confirmation. Further, the Hemingway voice AI integrates into existing phone systems to aid in convenient patient interaction, while the smart chat feature allows an embedded AI chat widget on your site for increased engagement and booking. Hemingway aims to streamline and simplify appointment management tasks, facilitating seamless communication between dental practices and their patients.
