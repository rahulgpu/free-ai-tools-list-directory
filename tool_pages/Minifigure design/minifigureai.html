About MiniFigureAI

MiniFigureAI.com is an AI-powered tool that allows users to transform their personal headshots into unique mini figures. The process is fairly straightforward - users upload their headshot in jpeg, png, or webp formats, and the tool generates a new mini figure image inspired by the uploaded headshot. MiniFigureAI uses OpenAI's DALL-E-3 API, a sophisticated AI system that specializes in generating original images from descriptive inputs, to create these mini figures. Despite utilizing user-uploaded images for this creation process, MiniFigureAI.com assures users that it does not store these headshots within its own systems. However, OpenAI may retain user inputs for a short period to ensure service provision and prevent misuse. Once created, the mini figures are saved within the systems of MiniFigureAI.com and are made publicly viewable in the website's 'MiniFigure Wall'. For those interested in creating mini figures for larger groups or teams, MiniFigureAI extends its service - interested parties can email expressing their specific requirements. Feedback about the tool and its functionalities can also be submitted via email.
