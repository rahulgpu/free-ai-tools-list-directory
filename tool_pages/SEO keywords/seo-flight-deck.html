About SEO Flight Deck

SEO Flight Deck is an AI-assisted tool designed to streamline the process of tracking and improving a website's Google search rankings. It is designed with the small business owner in mind, offering an alternative to manually monitoring their SEO results and using potentially outdated Google search console data. The tool also positions itself as a simpler, more affordable alternative to other more complex and costlier SEO platforms. Core features include real-time keyword rankings statistics, insights on these statistics, and AI-powered keyword grouping, effectively delivering detailed, targeted results. Along with these features, SEO Flight Deck also sends out informative emails compiling all essential updates straight to the user's inbox. The software focuses primarily on user accessibility, making the process of SEO management less cumbersome for business owners by providing insights into the aspects they will actually use. For users beyond the scope of the basic package, custom packages can be tailored according to the user's specific needs.
