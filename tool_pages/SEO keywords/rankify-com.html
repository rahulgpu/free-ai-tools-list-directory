About Rankify.com

Rankify is an Artificial Intelligence-powered tool designed for Search Engine Optimisation (SEO) keyword research. Its primary function is assisting users in identifying relevant keywords based on the description of what they wish to research. This tool works based on semantic description, allowing users to input seed keywords or a description of the topic they want to investigate. Rankify is particularly beneficial for SEO teams, freelancers, agencies, and for keyword research in general. Beyond suggesting related, long-tail, and short-tail keywords, the system features the ability to generate blog topics and FAQs that align with the user's area. It also provides color-coded keyword difficulty to help gauge the competitiveness of a suggested keyword. Additionally, keywords can be segmented into different lists for future use. The tool further extends its utility by allowing users to manage multiple projects within their dashboard. It boasts a significant advantage over other software by providing outputs with predominantly high-volume keywords, displaying SEO metrics, matching buying intent, and comprehending search volume for both long-tail and short-tail keywords.
