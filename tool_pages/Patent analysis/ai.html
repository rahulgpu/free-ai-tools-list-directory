About &AI

&AI is a tool designed to streamline the process of patent due diligence. It provides comprehensive information within seconds, reducing the time typically required for this task. This tool features a variety of capabilities using AI technology. First, it generates detailed claim charts for any patent or claims against any references and allows users to fine-tune these charts or export them to formatted Word or Excel documents. Secondly, &AI offers a prompt prior art search by identifying the most relevant prior art for a user's patent or application, thus offering savings in time and cost as compared to outsourced search firms. The tool also assists in streamlining patent applications and office actions by analyzing the robustness of proposed claims and identifying potential gaps in prior art. Additional features include 'Search&AI' which allows users to search through their documents and USPTO data semantically or with keywords, and 'Chat&AI', a chatbot engineered to understand patents and legal material, enabling users to chat about and summarize their documents and USPTO data. &AI's mission is to alleviate the difficulties often associated with patent due diligence, allowing users more time to focus on important tasks.
