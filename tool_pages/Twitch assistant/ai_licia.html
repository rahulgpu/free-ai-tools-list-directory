About ai_licia

Ailicia is an AI-powered virtual assistant designed to assist Twitch streamers. Offered by NovaSquare Ltd, this tool's primary focus is to support channel moderation and enhance viewer engagement for Twitch streamers. Ailicia achieves this by interacting with both the streamer and the viewers through the chat, hence, adding an additional layer of support and making the stream more entertaining and interactive. Features of Ailicia include a unique 'text-to-speech' function, which allows Ailicia to express different emotions through her intonation. This feature introduces a new level of interaction. Ailicia also includes a customizable 'listening mode' where the AI assistant can respond to the user's voice. Further, Ailicia provides integration with existing streaming tools, where you can control it via StreamDeck and also synchronize it with your heart rate data via Pulsoid. Ailicia also allows customisation of its mood and personality through its dashboard personality settings to fit the exact vibe of the stream. Overall, Ailicia is a tool designed to aid streamers in creating more engaging content, reaching wider audiences and managing their channels more effectively.
