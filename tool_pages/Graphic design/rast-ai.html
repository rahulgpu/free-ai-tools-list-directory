About Rast.AI

Rast.AI offers a suite of AI-powered applications designed for automated graphic design. This includes AI Logo Maker and AI Poster Maker that is used to generate logo, poster, flyer, banner, and ads imagery based on directives the user inputs. The primary features of the AI Logo Maker include creating logo designs specifically derived and tailored from words and allowing for customizable designs capable of scaling vector graphics output. On the other hand, the AI Poster Maker transforms words into a poster with customizable templates and layouts. It features a high-quality graphics library, text effects, typography options, and a range of image enhancement filters. This tool is also equipped with brand management features that support integration of users' brand assets such as logos, colors, and fonts, ensuring consistent branding across all marketing materials. Personalized designs tailored to user's specifications can be effortlessly achieved. Additionally, it provides a one-click export option that allows users to easily share their designs across various platforms in multiple formats. Lastly, it includes a gallery where users can see images generated from the AI logo maker and poster maker.
