About Eververse

Eververse is a tool designed to assist product teams in managing and planning product roadmaps. Leveraging AI, it allows teams to explore problems, ideate solutions, and prioritize features. Among the key functions is the capacity to automatically categorize customer feedback, and use sentiment analysis techniques to interpret its nature, potentially revealing important themes, customer pain points and desires. A smart, rich editor with AI-assisted writing makes creating a backlog simpler and efficient. Eververse can also help teams prioritize their backlog through automatic RICE scoring. Customizable feature statuses allow adaptation to suit specific workflows. The tool promotes easy roadmap creation, facilitating the marking of important events and customizable views. An activity log keeps the team updated on various activities within the product team, and a real-time view feature displays who's active on Eververse and what they are working on. Eververse has an advanced changelog editor, and the ability to generate updates using AI. Integration capabilities are comprehensive, with options to connect to platforms like Zapier, Intercom, Slack, and others. It also provides an API for custom integrations and workflow automation. Additionally, the Eververse portal lets teams publicly share their product roadmap and collect feature votes from users.
