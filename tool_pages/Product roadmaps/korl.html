About Korl

Korl makes product communications to internal stakeholders and customers more effective and much less time-consuming. It syncs and analyzes project data from sources such as Jira, Linear, and documents in Google Docs, Notion, or Confluence. Then it auto-generates consumable roadmap presentations and product updates, each tailored to a common use case and audience. When you create a presentation or product update in Korl, it auto-populates the latest project data and enhances it with AI. Users have the flexibility to choose from different out-of-the-box templates as well as customize content with just a few clicks. Common use cases include customer presentations, internal stakeholder updates, all hands meetings, weekly status reports, and sprint priority reviews.
