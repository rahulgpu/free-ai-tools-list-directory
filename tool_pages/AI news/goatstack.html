About GoatStack

GoatStack is an Artificial Intelligence-powered newsletter tool. It is designed to assist you in refining your access to knowledge by personalizing a curated collection of the latest updates on chosen topics. The key feature of GoatStack is its AI Agent, which is programmed to sift through and analyze a multitude of scientific papers on a daily basis and handpicks the essential ones. This AI Agent sends you a personalized insights newsletter based on your selected interests. These insights capture breakthroughs, challenges, techniques, roadmaps, and resources from various fields in AI, including Large Vision Models and Large Language Models. GoatStack aims to efficiently save users' time by providing concise yet comprehensive updates in under three minutes. The tool offers flexibility to personalize the content, allowing users to set the specificity or generality of the topics. Besides the AI-curated newsletters, GoatStack also fosters a community through AI paper reading events. The platform provides different pricing tiers with distinct features, and it offers integrations with platforms like Atlassian, Gitlab, Slack, and Telegram in its highest tier.
