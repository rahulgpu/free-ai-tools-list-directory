About Debatia

Debatia is a global AI-judged debate platform facilitating real-time debates through both voice and text input options. The platform offers a unique feature of multilingual support, accommodating debates across 12 different languages and thus enabling users to break geographical barriers and engage with debaters worldwide. Its powerful matchmaking feature pairs users based on their proficiency and skill level in debating, achieved through a built-in algorithm. The topics for debate on Debatia span a wide range of interests, from pet choices to policies on healthcare, education, privacy, and renewable energy. Debatia's key differentiator is its AI Judging System; it leverages OpenAIs ChatGPT's AI capabilities to deliver a fair judgment in debates, effectively addressing the potential bias encountered in human-judged debates. This unique feature provides users with unbiased results while also ensuring an interactive and engaging debating experience. It's worth noting that Debatia is a product of Toketa Inc.
