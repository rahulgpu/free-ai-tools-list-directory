About Rephrasely

Rephrasely is an AI-powered tool designed to assist users in a variety of writing tasks across more than 100 languages. Its primary function is to paraphrase and rewrite the content of the text while retaining the original meaning. This tool is equipped with 12 free plus additional premium modes that deliver various styles and nuances to the rewritten text. Apart from paraphrasing, Rephrasely offers additional features such as plagiarism checking in over 50 languages, text composition from provided keywords, as well as translation services. In the 'Compose' mode, users can write faster by creating paragraphs instantly from input keywords for a variety of document types including articles, cover letters, essays and more. The plagiarism checker helps ensure the uniqueness of the text. Powered by state-of-the-art AI technology, Rephrasely can produce variations of your text suiting a range of styles. It offers paraphrasing solutions for various writing needs from sentence rephrasing to paragraph-level context consideration. Whether generating new content from keywords or rephrasing existing text, Rephrasely aims to streamline and enhance the writing process.
