About BlogToPin

BlogToPin is a digital tool designed to streamline the creation and scheduling of Pinterest pins derived from blog post content. It aims to increase organic traffic to your blog by generating engaging pins. You can customize the pins' design according to your branding, from a range of pre-existing templates, color palettes, and font groups, removing the necessity for specialist design skills.The tool features an automation process where users can schedule pins, up to a month in advance, saving you time by reducing your manual labor. There's also a feature for automating engaging titles and descriptions for your pins, although you are able to input your own if you prefer. BlogToPin strategically schedules the release of your pins for optimal audience reach and engagement, working without your constant supervision. The tool favors diversity by creating as many unique pins as possible.In addition to its design and scheduling features, BlogToPin also monitors the performance of your pages and ensures varied content is presented to users, minimizing content duplication. This tool supports even those websites with complex design structures, as it omits non-essential UI components like suggested articles and recommended products to maintain focus on high-quality pin generation.
