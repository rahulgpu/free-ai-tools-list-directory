About Jane Cal

Jane Cal is an AI-powered scheduling assistant designed to streamline meeting planning by quickly finding the most convenient times. This tool offers smooth integration with users' calendars and inboxes. Through an 'Inbox First' strategy, Jane is effortless to activate from within a user's email environment. Jane assists in planning by understanding natural language requests to set up meetings. This enables users to mention specific dates or times such as 'next week', 'tomorrow afternoon', or 'Monday at 1pm' and Jane can interpret these requests and schedule accordingly. Prior to each meeting, Jane can disseminate emails to attendees to help in creating an agenda. Additionally, Jane is capable of adding relevant attachments to your calendar meetings. A significant feature is Jane's capability to schedule anyone, dependable not just for internal scheduling but also useful in arranging meetings with attendees outside of a user's organization. Jane Cal is easy to set up, requires no credit card to get started and offers a free package until users decide to upgrade to a premium offering. Finally, Jane Cal ensures compatibility with multiple popular calendar tools.
