About Hermae AI

Hermae AI is an Artificial Intelligence Assistant designed for enterprises, primarily focusing on enhancing the efficiency of their design system. Its principal objective is to increase development speed, facilitate smoother engineer onboarding, and swiftly synthesize vast amounts of documentation data. This tool majorly aids onboarding acceleration, contractor efficiency, and frontend adoption across organizations by providing a clear departure from bottlenecks. Hermae AI can easily be embedded into your documentation site where it aims to answer queries, provide feedback, and generate code.This custom-built AI assistant functions based on the user's documentation. It works towards boosting frontend productivity by supporting an instant feedback system, decreasing the need for time-consuming remote meetings. It is compatible with Storybook and requires zero configuration for usage. It supports internal teams to build faster with your frontend system.The process of integrating Hermae AI into your system is simple. It involves information gathering, data processing for AI supplementation, customization of integration, and continuous support for integration success. It does not require any private information, is developed to respect privacy, and is working towards achieving SOC 2 and ISO 27001 compliance. The tool is designed to significantly increase efficiency while reducing engineering costs, thereby contributing to substantial time and cost savings for companies.
