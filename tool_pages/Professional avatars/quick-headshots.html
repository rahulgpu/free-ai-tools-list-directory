About Quick Headshots

Quick Headshots is an AI-based tool that transforms selfies into professional-grade headshots. Users begin by uploading ten or more high-quality selfies, fulfilling the following conditions: front-facing, only one person in the frame, no sunglasses, face coverings, caps, or hats. The photographs are then fed into the AI model that generates headshots based on the selected styles and backgrounds. These styles and backgrounds comprise a selection of 20 option sets, all created to cater to a wide range of professional contexts. The tool is designed to deliver the generated headshots to the user's inbox within a few hours. Leveraging AI technology, Quick Headshots provides a cost-friendly and efficient alternative to traditional photoshoots. The platform ensures strict data privacy, storing user photographs securely and using them solely for the specified purpose of AI headshot generation.
