About ProShots

ProShots is an AI-based tool used to generate professional headshots. Using ProShots, users can select a preferred location, pose, and attire for their headshots. This tool takes this information and generates a realistic headshot at the user's convenience. The need for hiring a professional photographer or purchasing expensive lighting equipment is eliminated. The process involves uploading photos, selecting the styles users prefer, and letting the tool generate the headshots. ProShots offers a variety of locations, poses, and attire to choose from, allowing users to create multiple images to their liking. These images can be saved in high-resolution quality. It ensures user privacy by clarifying queries regarding the handling of uploaded photos. ProShots is available for download on both iOS and Android platforms. The time taken to generate images and refund policies, if any, can be found in the FAQs section. It offers a unique approach to creating professional-looking headshots using artificial intelligence.
