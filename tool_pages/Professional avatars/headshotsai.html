About HeadshotsAI

Headshots AI is an AI-powered tool that allows users to generate professional headshot images quickly and easily. It is designed to enhance online presence and is ideal for social profiles, resumes, and professional portfolios. The tool is trusted by professionals worldwide and provides a quick and efficient way to obtain high-definition headshots.To use the tool, users can upload four or more high-quality selfies that meet certain criteria, such as being front-facing and having only one person in the frame without glasses or hats. The AI algorithm then processes the images in approximately 20 minutes, after which the user receives an email notification. The resulting headshots are of exceptional quality and can be used for various purposes.Headshots AI offers different pricing plans to suit different needs. The Starter plan includes one credit and is perfect for individuals who want to enhance their online presence. The Basic plan, which includes three credits, is more suitable for professionals who require frequent profile updates. The Premium plan offers the best value with unlimited possibilities and includes five credits.The tool is open-source, powered by Leap AI, Supabase, and Vercel, which showcases its reliability and the expertise behind its development. Overall, Headshots AI offers a reliable and efficient solution for generating professional headshots using AI technology.
