About InstaHeadshots

Insta Headshots is an AI-based tool designed to transform ordinary selfies into professional quality business headshots, with a level of realism equivalent to photos taken by a professional photographer. Using the technology, users upload a dozen or more quality selfies, ideally front-facing with clear facial features. The AI system then uses these images to meticulously train a model of the user's face, which allows for the generation of realistic headshots. This process takes around 90 minutes, after which the user has access to a set of high-quality headshots. With this tool, users can procure professional-grade photos without the need for a physical photoshoot or a professional photographer. The headshots produced are ideal for corporate portfolios, business profiles, and more. Additional features include a variety of stylistic options for the headshots, and these results are available in high definition. The tool maintains a secure environment for personal data and assures users of full ownership rights over the generated images.
