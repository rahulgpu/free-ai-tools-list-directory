About Resume Photo

AI-Generated Professional Headshots is a tool designed to provide users with professional headshots created using artificial intelligence. The service aims to make obtaining a professional headshot quick, affordable, and hassle-free. Users simply upload a set of 4-6 selfies, which should be of high quality with the face as the main focus, without any unusual attire such as hats or sunglasses. From these selfies, the tool's AI technology then generates a set of 16 professional headshots in different styles and backgrounds. The AI technology developed for this tool emphasizes maintaining a high resemblance to the original photos to ensure natural, yet professional looking results. The resultant headshots can be used for various professional purposes, such as LinkedIn profiles, resumes or websites. Ensuring privacy and security, user data used in the process is claimed to be used exclusively for the generation of the headshots and is deleted after 7 days. This tool aims to help users enhance their online professional presence with the use of AI technology.
