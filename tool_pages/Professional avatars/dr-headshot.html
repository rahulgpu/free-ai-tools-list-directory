About Dr. Headshot

Dr. Headshot is an AI tool designed to generate business headshots. Mainly used for professional applications such as LinkedIn profiles, CVs, and business websites, this handy tool offers an easy-to-use interface where users can upload their preferred photo, select a background, and choose an outfit for the AI to create a headshot. It offers two services, a free version and a premium version. The free version, while not as high-quality as the premium service, provides users with an adequate headshot for temporary use. This service is completely free and does not require credit card information. For users seeking professional-grade headshots, the premium AI service is recommended, but at a cost. The tool takes privacy seriously, ensuring all uploaded photos are deleted after processing and are not stored. Notably, this AI tool primarily caters to English, French, German, Italian, and Spanish speaking users and is developed in Europe.
