About Try it on AI

Try it on AI is a machine learning powered tool to help users create stunning professional headshots and portraits in half the time and cost of photography studios. The platform works by creating an AI model of the user through submitting selfies and photos. With this model, the user can then get 35 high quality headshots in various styles delivered within 48 hours. The user can pick their favorite headshot and have it stored for up to 30 days. This tool is perfect for those needing professional headshots for LinkedIn, acting headshots, or dating profile pics. Additionally, Try it on AI can also generate influencer content, team portraits and lifestyle portraits.
