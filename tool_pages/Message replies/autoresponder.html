About AutoResponder

AutoResponder.ai is an application designed to automatically respond to messages on popular messaging platforms including WhatsApp, Facebook Messenger, Instagram, Telegram, Signal, and Viber. The app enables users to easily customize automatic responses, making communication more efficient, especially for businesses that receive a high volume of messages. AutoResponder.ai also extends its functionality beyond sending out automatic replies. It supports creation of custom rules for different messages, allowing for personalized interactions with the recipient. Moreover, the app can connect to a user's web server to process messages and work in conjunction with AI models such as ChatGPT or Dialogflow AI. It is also compatible with Tasker to trigger custom actions. The tool is available to download from Google Play Store and accessible from a mobile device for on-the-go use. Note that while the tool can help reduce the workload of responding to numerous messages, the ability of the bot to answer accurately or effectively can largely depend on the quality and specificity of the programmed responses.
