About ArxivGPT

ArxivGPT is a Google Chrome extension specifically designed to summarize and elucidate key insights from arXiv papers. As an AI-powered tool, it allows users to grasp the key concepts and main ideas of a paper at a glance, aiming to save users time while keeping them informed about the latest developments in their field of interest. Users can access the functionality of ArxivGPT with a simple click and the tool will then summarize the content of the selected paper. Despite its primary function as a summarizer, ArxivGPT also provides a feature that allows users to ask questions for further understanding. This makes it a helpful tool for researchers, students, or anyone needing quick and succinct information from arXiv papers. It's been developed with a focus on enhancing productivity and facilitating workflow in academic and research contexts. Despite its capablities, reviews indicate some limitations which could be considered for further development and improvements.
