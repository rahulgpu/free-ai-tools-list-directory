About Auto Backend

Auto Backend is an AI-powered tool designed for the swift and efficient creation of backends for various applications. This user-interactive tool allows the users to quickly layout and describe the specific functionality they need from the backend for their application, thereby eliminating the need to write complex codes. It is highly versatile, with a capacity that can range from creating a simple to-do list to cloning more complex systems like the Twitter feed or retrieving Ethereum balances, depending on the user's needs. Due to its user-friendly interface, users can easily modify or update the backend functionalities as per their requirements. Despite its simplicity, Auto Backend is capable of handling heavy traffic without performance issues. An interesting aspect is its provision to explore the existing gallery which hosts a variety of backends created by various users that can be used as a blueprint or inspiration. It should be noted that Auto Backend is currently experiencing rate limits in response to high demand.
