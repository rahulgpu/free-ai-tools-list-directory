About NexLev

NexLev is an AI-powered tool designed to guide YouTube content creators in discovering and monitoring profitable niches, channels, and keywords on the platform. The features provided by NexLev help to speed up the process and efficiency of finding trendy niches and monitoring its potential saturation. The tool harnesses AI technology to reduce manual browsing and provide real-time suggestions. Additionally, NexLev serves as an extensive database with over 150,000 YouTube channels which can be further streamlined using 21 unique filters. This provides opportunities for users to discover highly successful channels and monitor their performance metrics. The tool provides a comprehensive view of any channel's average monthly views, total revenue generated, RPM and more. It also has a hands-on filter system in-place that guides users to explore profitable niches and predict their revenue potential. It allows users to save any criteria like channel or keyword for future references. Its ongoing curation process assists users in finding perfect and various niches. Furthermore, NexLev encourages its users to become a part of a larger community of content creators and provides prompt ongoing customer support.
