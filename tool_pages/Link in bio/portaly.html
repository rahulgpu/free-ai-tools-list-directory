About Portaly

Portaly is an AI-powered tool designed to swiftly turn any website or profile URL into a personalized link-in-bio landing page. The tool uses intelligent algorithms to streamline the process of converting your website or profile into an accessible landing page, making it easier for audiences to engage with your content. Portaly comes with over 20+ functional blocks to fully customize your landing page. You can personalize these pages by rearranging every element using the tool's drag-and-drop feature. Additionally, to make audience engagement more dynamic, Portaly includes a built-in contact form and analytics toolkit. It also features compatibility with numerous platforms, such as Behance, GitHub, Figma, and Notion to create a more comprehensive online presence. Portaly simplifies the process of organizing online interactions and hence is an invaluable tool for creators and businesses alike.
