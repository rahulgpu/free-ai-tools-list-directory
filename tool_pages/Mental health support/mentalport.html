About Mentalport

mentalport is an AI-driven app offering personal mental health coaching and tools to help individuals manage stress, anxiety and depression. The tool aims to integrate seamlessly into the users' lifestyle, providing support whenever and wherever it's needed. Combining augmented cognition and artificial intelligence, mentalport offers personalized mental wellness tools and techniques directly to a user's smartphone to help cope with daily life stressors and achieve personal goals.The individualized approach offered by this AI tool allows it to adapt its methods to suit the user's unique needs and personal circumstances. This AI coach uses systemic coaching techniques, encouraging users to consider their mental health in the context of their whole environment.Apart from its AI capabilities, the mentalport app provides access to personal 1:1 coaching sessions with psychology professionals. Users can book these sessions instantly and without a long waiting period.Moreover, mentalport is not limited to the personal sphere. An upcoming feature of the app is its use in the corporate setting, where it can help manage the mental well-being of employees.Thus, mentalport is designed to make maintaining mental wellness an easy and user-friendly process, adapting to individuals' needs and helping them live a fuller, less stressed life.
