About Opinoz

Opinoz is an innovative platform designed to combat the spread of inaccurate information on the internet. It brings together community wisdom and sophisticated AI technology to parse through the vast amounts of data and extract trustworthy opinions and insights. The platform targets the challenges of information overload, lack of concise summaries, time investment, and social media bubbles. Opinoz works by means of community-driven fact-checking where users can vote on the accuracy of shared opinions, thereby helping to highlight the most credible information. It employs AI-powered summarizations to condense lengthy discussions and articles into quick overviews and key arguments, and potentially identify and flag potential misinformation or heavily biased statements. The platform also advocates for exposure to diverse viewpoints with the help of the voting system and AI summaries. The tool actively promotes the 'not sure' option, encouraging users to consider varying perspectives. Lastly, Opinoz is integrated with a fact-checking system that debunks inaccurate and misleading facts circulating on social media, thereby presenting accurate information. Built with a vision to promote verified information and diverse perspectives, Opinoz stands as a revolution in managing online information.
