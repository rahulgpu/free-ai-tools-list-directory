About Redactive AI

Redactive AI is a tool designed to facilitate interactions with your permissioned data through an advanced chat interface. The main focus of the tool centers around enhancing accessibility and ease of getting insights from your data. The advanced chat is powerfully built on a variant of GPT for generating conversational interactions. Users can sign up and log in to chat with their data. The tool is primarily solution-oriented, aiming to serve community builders who need to extract insights from their data in an interactive and user-friendly manner. To maintain data privacy and integrity, Redactive AI emphasizes on using permissioned data. This makes it possible for the user to comfortably harness their confidential data, knowing that the tool respects and maintains data boundaries. Summarily, Redactive AI combines advanced AI techniques and intuitive design to create a user-centered interface for interacting with and understanding your data in an effortless and secure way.
