About VoiceDrop

VoiceDrop.ai is a platform that leverages AI to operate as a ringless voicemail software provider. Ringless voicemail enables you to send a voice message to voicemail inboxes without a call being made, thus eliminating possible intrusion. The tool uses AI techniques to clone a user's voice, creating a personal and consistent point of contact on a large scale. It enables you to upload your complete contact list, facilitating the mass delivery of personalized voice messages. These messages or 'drops' can be used for various campaign rolls outs, such as sales calls, notifications, reminders, or marketing initiatives. With a focus on providing a measurable impact, the platform aims to significantly increase scheduled sales calls and callbacks. VoiceDrop.ai is a business-to-business SaaS product and offers a free trial period for new users to experience the software. In addition to the software, detailed information on usage, features, and pricing are available on the VoiceDrop.ai website, which also offers booking for a product demo.
