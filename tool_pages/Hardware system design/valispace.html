About Valispace

ValiAssistant, offered by Valispace, is designed to streamline engineering tasks, especially in the realm of requirements engineering. The tool seeks to automate and simplify various steps in the engineering process. It starts with the generation of requirements, where it swiftly converts a rough description of a components capabilities into a list of formal requirements. This conversion process involves brainstorming, editing, and refining by engineers. For further detail, a single requirement can be broken down into more specific ones, leading to a more comprehensive design.It also optimizes requirements, enhancing them to be precise, unique, and quantifiable while maintaining automation for a smooth review process. Revisions such as these can apply to single or multiple requirements, upgrading ambiguous customer requirements into implementable ones. Also, proposed values get instant updates making them ready for calculations.The tool also allows for the parametrization of requirements. It recognizes parameters with quantities and physical units, leading to an instant connection to the system's calculations and simulations. This feature ensures full traceability and smooth updates in cases of changes in requirement text.In sum, ValiAssistant offers a comprehensive solution for requirements engineering by effectively turning rough ideas into concrete, precise, and implementable requirements. It also keeps the parameters flexible for revisions and dynamic for connection with the system's functionality.
