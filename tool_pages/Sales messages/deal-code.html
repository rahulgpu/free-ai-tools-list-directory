About Deal Code

The Dealcode AI Sales Message Generator is an integrated tool designed to streamline and enhance sales outreach and follow-ups. One of its core functions is to compose highly personalized messages at a faster rate to increase efficiency and achieve better conversion rates. It is easily accessible through its Chrome extension. By simplifying the process of searching for potential customers on Linkedin and creating customized messages with one click, it notably cuts down the time spent on research and manual writing. The AI technology behind this tool is based on successful sales processes, data science and has been trained using a large amount of sales data points and evidence-based research. Its goal is to automate the generation of personalized conversations, thereby saving time and increasing productivity for sales professionals. Verified by sales experts and trusted by B2B sales professionals, the Dealcode AI Sales Message Generator aims to elevate sales success for individual SDRs, BDRs, Sales Managers, Sales Leaders, Sales Teams, and Business Owners.
