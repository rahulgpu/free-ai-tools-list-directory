About Aili

Aili is a personal AI assistant aimed at enhancing the way you work and interact with your personal data. It leverages advanced AI models including the likes of GPT-4 and Claude 2, and integrates them with your personal data for a customized user experience. This tool works across multiple platforms and synchronizes your chat history on different devices. Aili allows its users to interact with the content of their documents and engage in a conversation with the model for every document selected. Additionally, it enables users to have a conversation with the content on the current webpage, creating summaries tailored to match various webpage content types. It also has a superior AI chat experience feature where one can choose the most suitable character to generate a reply during the course of a conversation. Aili also provides an 'unlimited web collections' feature, allowing even free users to add unlimited web pages to favorites for future reference. It is accessible on Android, iOS, and web clients, with the ability to log in via Apple and Google accounts. Aili offers free use with basic models and offers an upgraded package that grants unlimited access to advanced models.
