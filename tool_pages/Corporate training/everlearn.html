About Everlearn

Everlearn is an AI-driven platform specifically designed for goal-oriented training of frontline staff across various industries such as retail, hospitality, logistics, among others. The platform provides effective learning mechanisms and end-to-end service from identifying opportunities for improvement to delivering training and ongoing optimisation based on real-time data to help businesses reach their goals. It is not a one-size-fits-all solution; the Everlearn system is tailored to the needs of frontline staff, providing benefits like reduced training time, increased up-selling, and decreased staff turnover.It features an ease of integration with leading Learning Management Systems and is optimized for mobile use considering that frontline staff are typically not desk-based. The platform also emphasises creating a people- and goal-centric experience, avoiding bloated feature lists that may not contribute to concrete business results.Everlearn's dynamic system operates 24/7, continuously optimising the training approach based on data gathered in real-time through proprietary methodologies. The platform's unique data-driven engagement results in enhanced engagement and increased productivity of frontline staff. By investing in training through the Everlearn platform, businesses can expect improved turnover, customer ratings, profitability, and overall productivity along with fewer safety incidents.
