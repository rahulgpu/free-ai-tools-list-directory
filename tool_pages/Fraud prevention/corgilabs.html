About CorgiLabs

CorgiAI is an end-to-end fraud detection and prevention suite designed to unblock revenue and reduce losses from fraud. It is built on a core of customizable and explainable AI technology. The tool offers automated, optimized machine learning algorithms for fraud detection and proactive enforcement to control fraud and block rates. CorgiAI allows businesses to tailor the fraud prevention to their specific needs, with ML models adapted to their market and region. The tool provides fully transparent metrics and benchmarks to analyze model performance and supports data-driven decision-making with payment risk insights. Integrating with CorgiAI is quick and simple, with automated integrations that allow businesses to start using the tool in just 5 minutes without requiring any coding. The integration directly with payment providers enables real-time data analysis. CorgiAI also emphasizes user control and understanding, allowing businesses to stay on top of their data and models. The tool provides insights to fine-tune fraud prevention strategies and offers explainable AI for a clear understanding of the fraud detection process.CorgiAI's team consists of experienced professionals, including the founder and CEO, head of data science, account manager, COO, strategy and partnerships lead, product designer, software engineers, and data scientists. The tool prioritizes privacy and complies with privacy policies and terms of service. Businesses can contact CorgiAI with any questions or to kickstart their fraud prevention strategy.
