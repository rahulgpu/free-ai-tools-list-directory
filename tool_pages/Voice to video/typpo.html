About Typpo

Typpo is an AI-powered mobile application that allows users to create engaging videos in seconds by simply speaking into their phone. With its advanced AI technology, Typpo transforms users' words into visually stunning animations, making it accessible for anyone to share their ideas with the world without requiring design skills. Users can easily share their videos on various social media channels or send them as messages to friends and family. Typpo is the first design tool that is powered by voice, making it stand out from other design tools in the market. It is also touted as the fastest motion design tool ever built. The app's aim is to empower its audience to generate endless branded messages for free, rather than paying for ads that may not resonate with the target audience. Created by BeatPitch Inc, a Science portfolio company, Typpo is available for download on Apple's App Store. It saves users time and effort by eliminating the need for them to manually input text or create design elements for their videos. Typpo is a user-friendly and accessible tool that enables anyone to create captivating videos in no time.
