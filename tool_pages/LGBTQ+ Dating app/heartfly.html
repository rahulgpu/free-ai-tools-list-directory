About Heartfly

Heartfly is a dating and relationship app catered specifically to the LGBTQ+ community, targeting people who identify as Lesbian, Gay, Bi, Trans, and Queer. It intends to provide a platform for these users to find friends and potential partners in Europe and around the world. Being exclusive in its design for the LGBTQ+ community, it aims to create a safe, inclusive space for users to connect with like-minded people. Users can create unique profile pages and the app utilizes a machine learning algorithm in order to match users with similar interests who are geographically close. To ensure a secure environment, the app verifies all profiles and images, with users given the option to verify their photos as an extra measure of authenticity. Geared towards enhancing the LGBTQ+ dating experience, Heartfly presents an alternative to a range of existing apps, such as Grindr, Skruff, Taimi, Archer, Romeo, and Heartstopper, thereby expanding the choice and diversity within LGBTQ+ dating platforms.
