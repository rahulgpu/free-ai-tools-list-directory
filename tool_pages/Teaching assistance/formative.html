About Formative

Formative AI is a tool that aims to transform teaching and learning experiences by generating new items, lessons, assignments, and more. It is designed to be simple to use and accessible with just a single click. With Formative AI, educators can auto-generate standard-aligned questions and hints for learners, improving the efficiency and effectiveness of their teaching methods.The tool encourages educators to activate the 'Beta' features in their app settings to gain access to the AI capabilities. By doing so, they can take advantage of the innovative features offered by Formative AI and create a more engaging and personalized learning environment for their students.The developers emphasize the importance of ethical artificial intelligence (AI) and offer resources, such as articles and interviews, to educate users on how to avoid AI bias. They also provide a community platform where educators can connect with certified instructors and champions to share their experiences and best practices.Formative AI is suitable for various subjects, including math, science, English language arts, world languages, social studies, and social emotional learning (SEL). It offers real-time feedback, allowing educators to track the progress of their students and make timely interventions.To get started with Formative AI, users can sign up for a free account or explore the paid plans available for schools, trusts, and businesses. The tool also provides access to a library of resources, webinars, training centers, and a help center for additional support and guidance.In conclusion, Formative AI is an innovative tool that empowers educators to enhance teaching and learning experiences by generating questions, assignments, and hints, while promoting ethical AI practices.
