About Fobizz

Fobizz Tools is a suite of digital tools assisted by AI, specifically designed for schools. The toolset provides an array of functionalities from font-end user interface to in-depth data analysis, aimed at promoting efficient learning processes and easing classroom management. These tools are developed with and for teachers, with a focus on simplicity, privacy, and security. Fobizz Tools are also GDPR-compliant, ensuring that the collection and use of data adhere to the legal guidelines for data protection. Due to its reliance on JavaScript for optimum functionality, users are recommended to ensure that JavaScript is enabled in their browser settings. Notably, Fobizz Tools underscores an attention to privacy, adding an extra layer of trust in its service. This toolset, therefore, serves as a comprehensive resource for digital education, facilitating seamless and secure integration of AI-assisted tools in the learning environment.
