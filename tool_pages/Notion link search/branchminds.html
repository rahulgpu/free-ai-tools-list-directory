About Branchminds

Branchminds is an AI research assistant that is embedded right within your Notion workspace. It reads your Notion content and helps you to find relevant web links related to the topics that you care about. The widget is designed to make research more efficient, by eliminating the need to switch tabs between Notion and other tools. It pulls content from your specified Notion page and uses it to find relevant web links from the entire web. You can also use it to generate ideas and brainstorm, as the AI can generate from the inputs you give it or generate a completion to your Notion content. It is still in Beta and the team would love to hear feedback and feature suggestions.
