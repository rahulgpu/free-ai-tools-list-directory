About SeeingAI

Seeing AI is a free mobile application tailored for individuals who are blind or have low vision. Functioning as a talking camera, this app utilizes artificial intelligence (AI) to provide auditory descriptions of the user's surroundings. Developed collaboratively with the blind and low vision community, Seeing AI serves as an ongoing research project, constantly adapting to user feedback and advancements in AI research. The primary objective of Seeing AI is to enhance the daily lives of users by offering assistance with a variety of tasks. This includes reading text, describing images, and identifying products, contributing to increased independence and accessibility. The app's design prioritizes user inclusivity, aiming to bridge the visual gap through innovative AI technologies. As a user-friendly application, Seeing AI is available for download on both the App Store and the Play Store, making it accessible to a broad audience. Its continuous evolution reflects a commitment to refining and expanding its capabilities based on real-world user experiences and advancements in the field of AI. Seeing AI stands as a noteworthy example of technology harnessing AI to empower individuals with visual impairments in navigating and understanding their surroundings.
