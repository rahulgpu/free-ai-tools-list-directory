About Ocelot

Ocelot is an AI-driven platform for higher education designed to optimize student engagement and success. Its primary function is in providing tailored and timely communication, ensuring that the right students receive essential information precisely when needed. The platform is trusted among many higher education institutions for promoting enrollment, retention, and persistence in students. The AI-enabled features of Ocelot enhance uniformity in campus-wide communications, deliver personalized financial aid information, and support proactive communications for recruiting and admissions. In addition, there are offerings for personalized one on one connections for student affairs. The platform's AI Workflow serves as the core that operates diverse elements of the system. It supports two-way texting for direct guidance, a chatbot for immediate question handling, live chat for socially distant student service, among other features. The platform connects seamlessly with various systems making it an integrated part of the higher education communication ecosystem. The goal is to ensure that every student receives the comprehensive support they need, leaving no student behind in their pursuit of education.
