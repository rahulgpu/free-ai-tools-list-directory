About Ankara

Ankara AI is an AI tool designed to generate automated narrations and commentaries for videos. This tool allows users to upload a video and choose a voice and a prompt, which the AI then uses to create a unique narration or commentary. Ankara AI can be used in a wide range of applications, including sports commentaries for games like soccer or NBA, captivating voiceovers for TV ads, and engaging narrations for video content.The process of generating a narration is straightforward. Users upload their video, select a voice from options such as Fable, Alloy, Onyx, or Nova, and enter a narration prompt. Once these selections are made, Ankara AI takes care of the rest.It is important to note that Ankara AI does not store any user data, video content, narration prompt, or audio generation, ensuring user privacy.This AI tool aims to provide an automated solution for producing narrations and commentaries for videos, without the need for human intervention. By leveraging the power of AI, Ankara AI offers users the convenience of generating captivating narrations tailored to their specific needs.
