About Minvo

Minvo is an all-in-one social media video creation tool primarily designed for extracting shorts from long videos. It leverages artificial intelligence to identify and edit video shorts swiftly. Trusted by creators and businesses alike, it offers fast and straightforward features to deliver pro-edited clips. The application incorporates seamless social posting, enabling users to post and schedule content directly to various social accounts. The easy editing function is enhanced by transcript and timeline editing, which facilitates the addition of subtitles, emojis, b-rolls, and music effortlessly. Plus, the tool can frame faces automatically in vertical shorts and suggests emojis to enhance engagement. It also comes with a Magic B-Roll component to add relevant b-roll automatically to heighten viewer engagement. Users can add engaging background music to further grasp viewer attention. The tool supports posting and scheduling to Instagram, TikTok, YouTube, and more. Minvo also features transcript-based editing, streamlining video editing even more. It is ideal for a broad range of users, including video editors, agencies, YouTubers, entrepreneurs, podcasters, and more.
