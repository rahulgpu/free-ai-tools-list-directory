About Shortmake

ShortMake is an AI-powered tool that allows users to transform their ideas into viral videos quickly and effortlessly. It uses artificial intelligence to generate a script, a voice-over, select images or video illustrations, add subtitles, and edit everything into a single video suitable for platforms like TikTok and YouTube Shorts. Users can customize their videos by personalizing the voiceover, choosing background music, editing the AI-generated script, and selecting illustrations for the final video montage. ShortMake is suitable for all types of video content, whether it's for social media, websites, or YouTube channels. The tool offers high-quality AI-generated voices, with options for hyper-realistic and standard quality voices. This ensures a professional touch to the videos. The process of generating a video with ShortMake is quick, taking less than 8 minutes from start to finish. All users need to do is provide their ideas, and ShortMake takes care of the rest.Overall, ShortMake simplifies the video creation process with AI-powered editing, making it swift and unique. Users can bring their ideas to life without technical constraints and experience the future of video creation.
