About Hypergro

hypergro is an AI-powered tool that enables brands to leverage user-generated content (UGC) through personalized video ads for efficient customer acquisition. The platform combines AI technology with the creativity of content creators to deliver a unique brand voice that stands out from the competition. With hypergro, brands have control over the video creation process through a dynamic dashboard. They can easily navigate, manage, and organize creator content, preview and review it, and maintain full control over the visual narrative. The tool also ensures that all influencer posts align perfectly with the brand's identity through seamless collaboration with the brand's team.Real-time campaign tracking allows brands to make data-driven decisions. The platform provides insights into campaign performance, enabling brands to identify what works and what doesn't, and make necessary changes on the go. hypergro emphasizes being responsive, flexible, and smart in achieving measurable results.The videos created with hypergro are designed for high-performance and tailored to the brand's voice and global trends. They aim to engage the target audience effectively and stand out visually.Additionally, hypergro assists content creators in monetizing their creations by offering knowledge, strategies, and tools to turn their passion into a thriving business venture.The tool is known for its authentic influencer marketing that actually works, whether for product launches, creating genuine content, or capitalizing on trends. Brands can book a demo to experience its power firsthand. hypergro is developed by CREATORVERSE TECH PRIVATE LIMITED and is available on major app stores.
