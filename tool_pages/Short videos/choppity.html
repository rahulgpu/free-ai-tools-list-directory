About Choppity

Choppity is an AI-powered tool that enables users to quickly and easily turn long videos into short, engaging clips for social media platforms such as TikTok, LinkedIn, and more. With Choppity, users can automatically add animated subtitles and center faces in the videos, allowing for more effective engagement with the content. In addition, Choppity ensures compliance with accessibility standards by including captions.Choppity is designed to be significantly faster than traditional video editing software, providing users with the ability to edit 60 second videos in under 5 minutes. Additionally, Choppity is far more affordable than outsourcing video editing on Fiverr, with its “Starter” plan equating to just £0.33/minute.Choppity has been institutionally backed by Durham University’s Venture School, and it is committed to empowering creatives to stay creative. It also uses innovative technology to automate video editing for marketers and content creators.Users can sign up for Choppity for free, without a credit card required. The website uses cookies to enhance the user experience, and the privacy policy and terms of service can be found online.
