About Traq.ai

Traq.ai is an AI-driven conversation intelligence platform for sales teams. It records and transcribes sales calls, takes notes, and provides insights into what buyers care about, the competition, and obstacles in the way. It provides sales teams with powerful analytics and insights to inform their strategies and maximize their win rates. Traq.ai provides a call recording feature that records everything a buyer wants and needs to build trust and maximize wins. It also has a conversation intelligence feature that gives sales teams the knowledge they need to improve productivity, effectiveness, and win rates. Additionally, it has an AI note-taking feature that eliminates manual notes and helps build trust with customers and prospects. There is also a pipeline visibility feature that provides knowledge on which deals are likely to close (and when) to build fact-based revenue projections. Finally, it has a sales enablement feature that uses actual voice-of-customer data to create impactful sales enablement materials and inform go-to-market strategies. Traq.ai is designed to help sales leaders, sales reps, RevOps, and recruiting teams reach superhuman levels of success. It provides ultimate clarity for every deal, simplifies the sale, optimizes sales and revenue, and provides better listening and better results. Traq.ai provides insights and analytics that are objective and unbiased. It turns every conversation into data and finds the words that reveal what buyers are thinking.
