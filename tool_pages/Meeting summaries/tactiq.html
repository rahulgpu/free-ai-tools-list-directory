About Tactiq

Tactiq.io is an AI-driven tool designed for enhancing the productivity of meetings, whether they are hosted on tools like Google Meet, Zoom, or Microsoft Teams. The tool features live transcription of meeting conversations, creating detailed and accurate transcripts for later review. This function eliminates the need for manual note-taking, enabling participants to focus on the meeting while the tool handles the notes. In addition, Tactiq.io employs AI algorithms to provide summaries of meetings, highlighting key action points and relevant items. The tool also facilitates post-meeting tasks by curating the key meeting summaries and action items into a specific format. This feature makes it easier to update team members who were not present at the meeting. Furthermore, Tactiq.io's extension for Google Chrome allows it to transcribe meetings in real time without recording them, allowing attendees to participate with ease. The additional AI prompts functionality allows for the generation of custom prompts based on user preferences. Moreover, the tool caters to a wide range of use cases, including customer meetings, strategy meetings, team meetings, and one-on-one calls, serving professionals from various roles including product and project managers, sales professionals, designers, marketers, engineers, recruiters, solopreneurs, and entrepreneurs.
