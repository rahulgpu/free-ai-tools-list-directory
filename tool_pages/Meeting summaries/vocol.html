About Vocol

Vocol.AI is a one-stop meeting collaboration software designed to supercharge your work efficiency by turning voice data into actionable insights. Powered by advanced Natural Language Processing technology, Vocol.AI generates accurate transcripts from audio and video recordings, complete with summaries and topic analyses. It supports three languages (English, Japanese and Chinese) and offers translation of the transcript into more than 25 languages. The advanced collaboration features allow you to highlight key moments, comment on any part of the transcript, tag team members, and share the file with your team. Vocol.AI helps you turn ideas into accomplishments by generating actionable tasks from the transcript, with each task linked back to the conversation's precise moment, enhancing clarity and decision-making. You can also assign tasks to your teammates, set priorities and use auto-reminders to keep everyone on top of their assignments. Vocol.AI currently offers plans for both individuals and teams, so you can pick the features that best match your needs. Getting started with Vocol.AI is very easy! Simply register for a free account with your email (alternatively, you can log in with your Google or Microsoft credential) and you will get 200 V-points (minutes) that will be automatically credited to your account. There are many different ways to collect more credits, or V-points. For more information about our current promotions, please visit our website. Come join the Vocol community today and boost productivity for you and your team through voice collaboration!
