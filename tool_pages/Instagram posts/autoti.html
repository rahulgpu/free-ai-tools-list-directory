About Autoti

Autoti is an advanced AI tool designed to revolutionize the process of posting content on Instagram. Autoti helps users create content quickly and efficiently, allowing them to focus on marketing activities. The Instagram Post Creator feature offers a drag & drop interface for creating content in seconds and scheduling it for future posting. The Caption Maker feature generates engaging captions that will help users increase their engagement rate. The Hashtag Generator automatically pulls the best hashtags based on user suggestions. Autoti also offers a Post Scheduler feature that helps users optimize their profile for advertisement and the Instagram algorithm. Pricing plans range from €12.99 to €50 per month, with a 3 day free trial available.
