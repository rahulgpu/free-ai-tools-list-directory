About Squigl

Squigl is a generative AI platform that focuses on enhancing illustration and visual communication. This platform is primarily tailored to meet the needs of educators, Learning and Development (L&D) teams, and Chief Learning Officers across the globe. Squigl, a Microsoft Partner, is defined by its unique fusion of technology, artificial intelligence, and storytelling, with a primary objective of harnessing the creative potential of AI. The platform's core mission is to teach AI how to draw, now a feasible goal given the significant advancements in the field of generative AI, automated machine learning, and software engineering. Squigl has roots in the belief that humans have an inherent preference for illustrative visual language and it aims to leverage this appreciation for visuals within the educational and corporate landscapes. Squigl employs the use of Scribology, a storytelling methodology that combines artistry and technology to enhance engagement and retention of information. The platform has powered over 5,000 projects, providing them with a distinctive edge through creative, science-based storytelling and artistry.
