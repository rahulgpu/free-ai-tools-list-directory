About Raftwise

Raftwise is a Chrome extension designed to enhance LinkedIn interactions and improve social networking efforts. Leveraging artificial intelligence, it generates individual, conversation-starting comments tailored to each LinkedIn post. These comments are designed to feel as if they are organically composed, steering clear of the manufactured feel often associated with automated comments. Raftwise's primary goal is to boost engagement and foster more meaningful interactions on LinkedIn by providing comments that resonate, thus helping users to expand their networks. It also aims to save users time by streamlining the process of commenting, reducing the need for lengthy brainstorming sessions or reliance on generic comments. The tool completely respects user data privacy and does not sell data to third parties. Overall, Raftwise provides a more efficient and socially engaging LinkedIn experience, enabling users to maximize their LinkedIn presence.
