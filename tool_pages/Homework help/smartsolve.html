About SmartSolve

SmartSolve is an artificial intelligence driven tool designed to assist students and teachers by providing accurate and instant solutions to academic problems. The tool is useful across a wide range of subjects such as mathematics, science, history, and more. SmartSolve aims to enhance the learning process by offering precise answers to academic problems and queries. It ensures comprehensive support across all platforms and types of questions.There are three distinctive ways SmartSolve can be utilized. The first, Direct Integration, allows the users to get instant answers on various educational platforms including Blackboard, Canvas, Cengage, and McGraw Hill. The second function, Highlight, gives the users the ability to highlight and instantly solve any question found online, while the third feature, Snapshot, enables users to capture pictures of complex or image-based problems and receive solutions. SmartSolve is designed to be undetectable while browsing, ensuring customer privacy through proprietary techniques. Moreover, SmartSolve is available in multiple languages, making it a globally accessible tool. For user convenience, SmartSolve can be added directly to your browser, used on a mobile device, or as an application downloadable from the App Store or Google Play. Various plans, including trial, monthly, and a 6-month option are available for users to choose from based on their needs. With numerous positive reviews, it is evident that SmartSolve is an appreciated resource among both teachers and students. Designed with next-generation AI technology, the tool continues to enhance the way users approach their academic studies.
