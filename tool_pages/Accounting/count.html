About COUNT

COUNT is an AI-Powered accounting tool designed for small businesses. It automates accounting tasks, providing seamless data organization and time-saving benefits. It offers features that help in auto-categorizing transactions, thus reducing manual entry errors. It introduces intelligent discrepancy alerts aimed at monitoring for duplicates, errors, and unusual activity, ensuring your books stay accurate and trustworthy. The COUNT tool allows users to set rules for auto-categorisation, tagging, and task assignments, increasing efficiency in the accounting processes. COUNT makes invoicing simpler by offering features for generating and sending professional, customizable estimates and invoices in a few clicks. Additionally, it provides various payment options and automated payment reminders, facilitating quicker payments and improved cash flow. COUNT brings real-time reports regarding the financial health of the business, allowing timely and informed financial decisions. With a personalised dashboard, transactions are easily monitored and business insights are more focused. COUNT enhances business collaborations by providing a platform where team members can discuss finances and budgets directly on transactions and reports. Furthermore, it integrates with an extensive network of over 12,000 financial partners, serving as a comprehensive all-in-one financial management tool.
