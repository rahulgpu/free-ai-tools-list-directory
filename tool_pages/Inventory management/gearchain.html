About GearChain

GearChain is a mobile inventory management platform that allows users to track their inventory in real-time. Enabled by artificial intelligence and blockchain technology, the platform enhances workflow and ensures data integrity. Users can operate it on Android and iOS devices, downloading the GearChain Decentralized App (DApp) from relevant app stores and then quick-registering using a Google or Microsoft account. The platform's no-code builder makes it easy for users to customize aspects for various inventory projects, and to automatically generate forms facilitated by AI. GearChain's barcode scanning feature is facilitated by AI and machine learning, and is designed to speed up and enhance accuracy. The platform also comprises a role-based access control system, ensuring data security and integrity. Integrated blockchain technology offers data transparency and traceability. Users can automatically record their barcode scans directly onto a blockchain, enhancing inventory management and supply chain processes. The platform also seamlessly integrates with Google Sheets and Excel for easy control and sync of data. Additional integration with legacy applications and ERPs via API is also supported, making GearChain a comprehensive solution.
