About Fluxon

Fluxon is an AI tool that specializes in hyper-realistic voice generation. It allows users to transform text into audio with lifelike voices in any language. The tool offers a voice cloning feature that can replicate any voice using less than 10 minutes of example audio. Users can also create conversations in the same audio file, utilizing multiple voices.Fluxon provides various functionalities, including voice synthesis for both individual voices and conversations, the ability to list all available voices, and the option to create lip-sync videos. It offers a REST API, allowing developers to integrate AI speech generation into their applications.The tool has a wide range of use cases. It can generate professional and realistic voiceovers for marketing and demo videos, produce clear and high-quality audiobooks with different voices for each character, create humanlike voices for non-player characters (NPCs) in gaming, facilitate professional translation and dubbing in any language, enable more natural-sounding voices for chatbots, and automatically convert text content into podcasts.Fluxon aims to deliver humanlike voices quickly and easily, providing an intuitive user experience.
