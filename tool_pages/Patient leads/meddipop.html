About MeddiPop

MeddiPop is an AI tool designed to match qualified patients with medical practices. The tool offers an intuitive dashboard to help medical practices keep track of patient leads. With the use of MeddiPop, medical practices can avoid heavy lifting in the process of attracting new patients and growing their practice. The AI routes patient leads to the most relevant medical practices based on their location, insurance, income, and other demographics. The tool is designed for the top health-care industries such as plastic surgery, dermatology, ophthalmology and cosmetic dentistry. The tool is also designed to be user-friendly and can be set up easily. MeddiPop offers new leads every day and practices only pay for the leads they receive. The tool is efficient and provides an all-in-one dashboard for easy tracking of leads, messaging patients, patient information, billing, and invoicing. MeddiPop is currently in beta mode and available for a limited pricing period. The tool is suitable for healthcare professionals who are looking to grow their patient base and focus more on patient care, while leaving lead generation and patient-matching to AI.
