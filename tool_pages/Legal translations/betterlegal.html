About BetterLegal

BetterLegal Assistant is a Chrome extension that uses AI-powered technology to simplify complex legal language for easier comprehension. The tool is suitable for tech professionals, investors, and entrepreneurs who need to understand legal documents such as contracts, terms of use, and privacy policies. With just a few clicks, BetterLegal Assistant helps users understand the general meaning of such documents and provides a starting point to familiarize themselves with legal concepts and terminology. It saves time and effort by quickly providing clear and accessible explanations of legal text. However, it cannot provide specific legal advice tailored to an individual's unique circumstances.The tool uses generative AI technology and large language models to analyze and translate dense legal language into layman's terms. It works seamlessly to break down complex legal jargon into easy-to-understand terms that even a child can grasp. The extension also features a Pirate Mode that adds a fun and engaging twist to legal text translations.BetterLegal is a company based in Austin, Texas, focused on making the confusing and complex tasks of setting up and maintaining a business easier for entrepreneurs. Its tool aims to ensure that individuals and businesses are well-informed and equipped to make the best decisions for themselves.
