About Journalie

Journalie is an AI-driven journaling application designed to transform thoughts and experiences into captivating visual stories. Users simply speak about their day or any subject, and the AI takes care of transcribing their words, generating a unique visual page from their story. One key feature of Journalie is its ability to analyze the user's transcription to help determine and display the primary emotion from the day's entry. This emotion, along with the entry, is then saved to a calendar, providing an intuitive and interactive way of reflecting and looking back on past experiences. The app aims to make journaling an effortless and engaging experience, presenting personal stories in a fresh, creative way. The user has control over who can access their journal, offering them the freedom to keep entries private or share them with others. This function adds a social dimension to the app, encouraging connections between users. Therefore, Journalie serves as more than just a journaling tool; it's also a personal space for expression, self-discovery, and community.
