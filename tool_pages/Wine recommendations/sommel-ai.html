About Sommel-ai

Sommel-ai is an artificial intelligence powered tool designed to help users discover ideal wine pairings for their meals quickly and without hassle. The tool analyzes and uses the information from pictures of the user's dish or menu, and any listed ingredients, to provide the perfect wine pairing. This can be as specific as the user wishes; they can provide the entire meal or just a single dish. Apart from simplifying the wine selection process, Sommel-ai is suitable for a range of users; whether they only pair drinks and dishes occasionally, or those who frequent new restaurants and want to experiment with many possible pairings. All of these services are available under different plans which users can subscribe to according to their preferences and needs. Sommel-ai also takes into account all local and international laws regarding beverage consumption and data privacy. It urges users to be of the legal drinking age in their jurisdiction when using the service, implying a strong stance on responsible drinking and user safety. Users can handle disputes or complaints via the 'Manage Account' button on their account page, showing a commitment to resolving issues in line with their terms of service and the law.
