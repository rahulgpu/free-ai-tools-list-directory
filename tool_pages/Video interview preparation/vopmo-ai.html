About Vopmo AI

Vopmo AI is a job interview assistance tool that focuses on creating compelling video introductions or pitches for job seekers. Users provide their CV and job listing to Vopmo which then generates a personalized introduction speech. This is showcased via a 'digital twin' - a representation of the candidate. The tool includes features such as application video pitches ranging from 45 to 120 seconds, post-response introductions, and interview preparation videos. Custom videos, reviewed by human experts, aim to enable candidates to leave memorable impressions on prospective employers. Vopmo offers unlimited revisions within a specific timeframe post-delivery, and dissatisfaction reported within a defined period could be eligible for refunds. The platform promises a swift and secure solution - it crafts a custom avatar-based video promptly after receiving an order and necessary documents. Completed videos are delivered to the user via email within a speculated duration.
