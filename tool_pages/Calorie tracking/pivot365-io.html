About Pivot365.io

Pivot365.io is an AI-powered calorie tracking tool designed to foster lifestyle changes that promote healthier eating habits and weight maintenance. The tool operates by interacting with the user's photographic input of meals, snacks, and beverages consumed throughout the day. After analyzing this data, the AI provides insights that aid the user in understanding and reaching their dietary milestones. Additionally, the tool includes a weight tracking feature, which can guide the user towards their weight management goals. The primary objective of Pivot365.io is not short-term dieting but cultivating long-term lifestyle alterations that lead to enduring results and improved wellness. The tool is currently in development and will be launching in the future. In addition to its calorie tracker and weight management capabilities, the application comes with analytics features designed for targeted in-app messaging and push notifications, potentially facilitating promotional offers and brand awareness campaigns. The company plans to expand into new markets and is open to partnerships and investment.
