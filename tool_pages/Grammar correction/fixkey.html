About Fixkey

Fixkey is a tool designed primarily for macOS users, geared towards amending grammar within any application. This mechanism uses advanced artificial intelligence to primarily detect and correct any typographical errors, thereby aiming to enhance the quality of writing. In addition, it allows users to insert custom prompts at any time, adding versatility and personalisation. With a single keystroke, users can rectify the grammar of a line or even an entire paragraph. Moreover, the tool offers fully customizable shortcuts to activate its functions. It is also written natively for macOS, promising lightweight, efficient performance. Fixkey stands out for its extensive language support, claiming to aid more than 200 languages using Llms. Another feature under testing is dictation that utilizes modern offline Whisper models. Fixkey officially supports over 300 applications and uses offline models which are fine-tuned specifically for correcting grammar. Its user reviews suggest positive feedback regarding its speed and ease of use across various applications and text fields. Fixkey is claimed to be universally applicable, aiming to improve the typing experience everywhere on macOS.
