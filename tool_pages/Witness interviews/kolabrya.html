About Kolabrya

Kolabrya is an AI-powered tool designed for lawyers and workplace investigators. The tool eases the process of conducting and documenting interviews by eliminating the need for manual drafting of witness interview notes and summaries. It offers automated interview summaries and searchable interview segments that are immediately available. With Kolabrya, users can safely record and save interviews in both video and audio format. It integrates seamlessly with popular conferencing platforms such as Zoom, Teams, and Google Meet. The tool enhances transcription accuracy by leveraging technology from Deepgram, and is capable of processing large volumes of documents, offering custom reporting. Security is prioritized with full AES 256 encryption, Microsoft-recommended Azure infrastructure, and other end-point and token security technologies. A distinguishing feature is its capability for real-time meeting intelligence which adds value to the report generation process. Kolabrya is recognized as a part of Microsoft for Startups Founders Hub, VentureLab Accelerator, and Zoom App Marketplace.
