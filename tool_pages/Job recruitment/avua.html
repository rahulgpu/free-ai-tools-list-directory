About Avua

Avua is an advanced recruitment solution powered by AI. It is dedicated to simplifying and enhancing the recruitment process for job seekers, employers, and recruiters by making the talent acquisition process smarter, faster, and more precise. Leveraging artificial intelligence, avua is specialized in key growth industries including Energy, Technology, Finance, and Pharmaceuticals. Among its features are a DeepTech AI Matching system, a 60-90 Second Video Resume section, Intelligent CV Parsing, Proficient JD Parsing, and GPT-based verification. The DeepTech AI Matching employs Contextual Intelligence and Comprehensive Parameters to deeply examine CVs and job descriptions, ensuring precise matches and assigning suitability rankings. The Video Resume feature provides insights beyond just qualifications, uncovering attributes that set candidates apart. The Intelligent CV Parsing automates the extraction of key information from varied resume formats using deep learning techniques. The Proficient JD Parser extracts pertinent details from raw and unstructured job descriptions, emphasizing intrinsic meanings over literal contents. The GPT based verification prioritizes quality over quantity, aiming to enhance hiring processes and unlock new career opportunities.
