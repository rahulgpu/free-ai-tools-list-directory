About Quil

Quil is an AI recruiting software designed to streamline hiring and interviewing processes. The tool primarily focuses on automating tasks often handled manually such as note-taking and updating the Applicant Tracking System (ATS). During video, phone, and in-person meetings, Quil's AI takes notes, allowing the interviewer to concentrate on the interaction with candidates. The platform can be customized to set up specific note categories for targeted information capture. These categories sync seamlessly with your ATS, eliminating the need for manual data entry. Furthermore, Quil provides an AI-powered recruiting assistant to gather high-quality feedback and track candidate performance with automated scorecards. The software also offers seamless integration with major ATS platforms like Greenhouse, Gem, Lever, Bullhorn, BambooHR, JazzHR, Jobvite, SmartRecruiters, Workable, Tracker CRM, Crelate, and Thrive TRM. Quil's meeting types feature allows users to tailor meeting notes according to specific needs and customize interview notes to showcase unique aspects of each meeting. This tool aids recruiters in efficiently conducting interviews and enhancing overall hiring experiences.
