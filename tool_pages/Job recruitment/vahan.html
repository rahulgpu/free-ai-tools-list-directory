About Vahan

Vahan is an AI-based chatbot designed to facilitate the hiring of blue and grey collar workers in India. Offering a high-volume hiring solution, Vahan aims to assist businesses in scaling their workforce without the typical pains of managing recruitment processes. The chatbot is designed to automate hiring, significantly reducing overhead costs and complexities associated with vendor management. By leveraging AI-assisted workflows, Vahan helps businesses recruit high-quality workers that cater to their specific requirements. The tool is built to offer the quick onboarding of workers during peak seasons and to maximize reach through tailored hiring campaigns. Furthermore, it offers workforce staffing support, which includes payroll management. Vahan is effectively used in various industries and has contributed to the operational growth of several trusted startups in the country. The service operates across India, sourcing candidates from diverse locations to meet businesses' unique hiring needs.
