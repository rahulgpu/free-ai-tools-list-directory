About ZapHire

ZapHire is an AI-powered recruitment tool designed to streamline the hiring process. Its algorithms rank, categorize and curate candidates based on skills, experience, and expertise, allowing to save both time and resources. ZapHire's AI additionally performs comprehensive background checks using data from top platforms like Github, Stackoverflow, Twitter, LinkedIn and ProductHunt, providing accurate scores and ranks to make the selection process reliable. The tool offers candidate sourcing services that leverage AI technology to identify and attract skilled workforce. It presents a large pool of candidates, drastically reducing time-to-fill, while enhancing candidate quality and generating cost-effective sourcing. It also parses data from multiple channels, regularly updates insights and ranks profiles, showing the most relevant information for decision making. Some of the attributes provided for informed hiring decisions include details of the projects worked on by the candidate, certifications, tools used, language proficiency, community activities, job location, education among others. The comprehensive process in which ZapHire operates can be described in four steps: data collection and indexing; data enrichment; candidate validation and ranking; and presentation of results. It's a valuable tool for recruitment agencies sourcing top talent for their clients and internal HR teams building a workforce for startups.
