About Kiru AI

Kiru AI is an Artificial Intelligence powered tool designed to streamline recruitment processes. The solution is integrated with leading job boards and ranks resumes with a high level of precision, aiming to facilitate a hassle-free hiring process. Key features include Kiru's AI Workflows which conduct candidate search, maintaining high accuracy. The tool also comprises smart features like intelligent candidate matching, and the ability to create custom workflows, which the provider suggests can enhance recruitment efficiency. Advanced OCR, NLP, and ChatGPT AI solutions parse resumes across diverse file formats. Deep learning capabilities are incorporated to improve resume analysis, making data extraction more efficient. Other features are Automation Scoring, AI-Driven Workflow, and comprehensive candidate insights. Kiru provides resume parsing using advanced technology and an AI-guided Job Description (JD) Assistant that improves job posting. Kiru applies smart auto-rejection capabilities to the screening process to filter out candidates who don't meet specific criteria. Real-time recruitment analytics, workflow automation, and auto-interview scheduling are additional features that aim to aid the hiring process. The tool aims to offer an end-to-end solution for recruitment, from candidate acquisition to hiring.
