About Recruitgenius

RecruitGenius.ai is an AI tool designed to automate and streamline the recruitment process. It focuses on skills-based candidate screening, freeing recruiters from tedious CV reviews. The platform uses AI to automate various parts of the hiring process, offering features such as AI Auto Screening, One-way Video Interview, Smart Direct Interview Scheduler, Talent Pool Management System, Candidate Relationship Management (CRM), Analytic Reports and ATS integration. RecruitGenius.ai provides a dashboard that keeps track of emails, candidate status, scheduling, and analytics relegating the need for other tools like Excel, Trello boards, and emails for recruitment purposes. In addition, RecruitGenius.ai provides solutions for interview scheduling challenges and aids in the creation and choice of role-specific interview questions. The platform also offers options for setting different types of questions during interviews, such as verbal, multiple choice, text, or coding questions making the review of interviews more efficient and comprehensive. In addition to these capabilities, RecruitGenius.ai offers a free version with some restrictions and premium versions with expanded capabilities, including unlimited CV parsing and interviews, custom ATS/HRM Integration and instant support.
