About ActiveInterview

ActiveInterview is an AI-powered tool designed to automate the recruitment process, enhancing efficiency and decision-making in hiring. It provides a platform for conducting automated video interviews, allowing candidates to seamlessly record and submit their responses. This tool helps streamline the candidate evaluation process through AI-assisted job listing creation, thus helping employers ask pertinent questions for each role. Post-interview, team members can collaboratively review and rate responses in real time, making the tool highly suitable for remote and distributed teams. The AI-driven interface also generates interview transcripts, making it easier to refer back to essential candidate information. Additionally, the collaborative review option fosters team-wide involvement in the hiring decision, further enhancing the suitability of the candidate. The application offers coherence and flexibility in interview coordination by enabling organizations to set flexible time frames and bulk invitations. It also allows for a cohesive brand experience by providing personalized 'company branding' features. ActiveInterview integrates seamlessly with other software, including Zapier, improving the overall recruiting experience. It also offers customer support through email assistance, live chat, and zoom, ensuring a steady and reliable user experience. It's noteworthy that the tool safeguards candidate responses and job listings by offering varied data retention periods depending on the chosen plan. In its commitment to improve the hiring process, ActiveInterview serves as a comprehensive, intuitive, and user-friendly AI tool that leverages technology to simplify the recruitment process.
