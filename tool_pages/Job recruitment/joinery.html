About Joinery

Joinery is an AI-powered recruitment platform designed to streamline and enhance the hiring process. It incorporates an AI-driven approach in candidate identification, evaluation, engagement, and final decision making. This innovative tool offers wide-ranging hiring solutions to help businesses build efficient teams. A key feature of Joinery is the candidate summary cards for quick, bias-free screening, allowing user to identify suitable candidates efficiently. Additionally, the platform utilizes AI for insightful candidate evaluations with Culture & Fit Scores, establishing precise alignment of candidates with organizations skill and culture requirements. The AI-powered Hiring Assistant ensures continuous engagement with candidates, making the hiring journey a seamless experience. In-depth candidate profiles provided by the system aid in making informed hiring decisions reflecting the entire hiring process.Joinery also revolutionizes resume screening with intelligent AI parsing for key details and creating concise summaries. The tool further promotes a fairer recruitment environment by anonymizing resumes for unbiased comparison, maximizing diversity, equity, and inclusion. With Joinery, manual candidate review is efficient, with automatic assessments revealing exceptional potentials often missed in traditional screenings. Finally, the unique data-driven Culture and Fit Scores offer a real-time leaderboard of candidates, providing transparent hiring decision-making and discussions. Joinery goes beyond simple applicant tracking - it is a Hiring Engagement Platform that transforms how companies interact with their candidates throughout the hiring process, leading to more precise and successful recruitment outcomes.
