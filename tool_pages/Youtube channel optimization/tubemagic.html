About TubeMagic

TubeMagic is an online suite of AI-powered tools built to assist YouTube content creators. Designed to optimize YouTube operations, the tool helps to increase views and subscribers by providing AI-powered titles, descriptions, tags, and scripts. TubeMagic supports finding relevant topics, script creation, and video optimization. The platform also offers features for idea generation, where based on specific channels, unlimited video ideas can be drafted. The tool includes an automatic script writer that provides a selection of templates for video scripts. TubeMagic also serves to optimize videos with AI-generated title, description, and tag predictions. The tool also includes 'Keyword Research' that allows users to search keywords based on search volume and competition, along with a special 'magic' score that weighs them against each other. There is also a 'Video Research' feature, which lists high viewed videos on YouTube, sortable by different filters. The platform supports over 95 languages for descriptions, titles, tags, community posts and video ideas. TubeMagic, while offering a range of features, also ensures that the AI-generated content is unique, high-quality and plagiarism-free, thus making it SEO-friendly.
