About Blue – ChatGPT for Mac

Blue is an AI assistant built for MacOs that integrates the power of ChatGPT into your applications and documents. It is always available on your Mac and can be activated by pressing option + space. Blue can analyze, summarize or translate the content on your screen within seconds, making it a helpful tool in coding, brainstorming, or report writing. It supports in-app interactivity and uses the latest AI models such as GPT-4 for enhanced user experience. Using AppVision, Blue comprehends your data context and provides better assistance by understanding what you're working on. In terms of data privacy, Blue is GDPR-compliant and safe to use in a professional setting; it ensures data is stored on-device and encrypted with AES-256 using your Apple ID. In terms of execution, Blue offers a seamless interaction with your apps and data without the need for browser tabs or copy-pasting. It also uses AppVision to capture intricate spatial information, further enriching the user experience. All your data, including messages and files, are stored and encrypted on your Mac. Blue ensures user data is not used for model development, adhering to strict privacy standards. Powered by Microsoft, this AI assistant is suitable for both individual and professional use and offers different pricing plans. It integrates neatly into your daily workflow, increasing general productivity and task output quality while reducing mundanity.
