About Thinkbuddy

Thinkbuddy is an all-in-one AI suite designed to enhance productivity on MacOS systems. It's a tool that leverages AI technologies providing features that enable seamless integration of voice commands to maximize daily efficiency. Thiknbuddy utilizes AI models such as GPT-4, GPT-3.5, Claude 3, Groq, and Gemini which allows the user to choose based on their preference. Users can ask questions aloud in any language, take screenshots to provide context for AI-driven assistance, and set up shortcut keys that radically transform their work process. The software also provides a 'Quick Chat' feature for immediate queries and allows users to customize their prompts and keystrokes. Thinkbuddy offers OpenAI Whisper-powered voice-to-text capabilities for responding to emails and drafting messages. The versatility of the tool extends to its text selection feature, which allows the selection of texts to trigger automated AI-powered actions. A user can respond to emails, translate or summarize articles, and even engage in deep discussions with the AI. Thinkbuddy offers different pricing plans to cater to various user needs, from free versions for an introduction to its capabilities to more extensive plans for power users.
