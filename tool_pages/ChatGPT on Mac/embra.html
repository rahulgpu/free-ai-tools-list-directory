About Embra

Embra is an AI assistant designed to streamline workflows for individuals and teams across various professions. The tool connects your data, apps, and team to save time and facilitate efficient work. It is accessible from multiple platforms including macOS, Web and Mobile, ensuring availability at all times. Embra's notable features include the ability to set a global shortcut to pull it up instantly and private audit usage that allows you to control your AI. It's also designed to ensure privacy for you and your team. Embra integrates with your apps and allows you to connect your data, enabling intelligent queries and automations. You can also create commands for your AI and distribute them to your team with a click. It helps in automating repetitive work, producing high-quality context for emails and meetings, speeding up internal search, creating high-quality content, and performs research and summarization tasks. Whit Embra, you can unlock higher engineering velocity as the AI is adept at coding. Particularly designed for teams, Embra facilitates collaboration in threads, shared automations, and overall visibility and control over AI usage. Whether it's sales, support, managerial, or leadership roles, Embra provides tailored solutions for efficient work and performance.
