About AutoPod

AutoPod is a set of Adobe Premiere Pro plug-ins that enables automatic editing for video podcasts and shows, reducing the production time required for editing. With AutoPod, you can edit multi-camera sequences automatically regardless of camera configurations, allowing up to 10 cameras and 10 microphones. The plug-in is customizable, allowing you to use any editing method, including multi-camera and standard cutting. AutoPod also offers a jump cut editor, which automatically creates cuts in footage based on silence, perfect for social clips where engagement is essential. The jump cut editor lets you input a decibel cutoff for your particular microphones, making sure the cuts are precise and seamless.AutoPod's social clip creator automatically resizes sequence settings and footage sizes based on the selected aspect ratio (1920x1080, 1080x1350, and 1080x1920). It also adds auto-reframe, watermarks, and end pages based on your preferences. The clips created are stored in a folder and may be batch exported with a single click. This plug-in is user-friendly and easy to use, saving video editors lots of time as it automates several processes they would have done manually. It is customizable, enabling you to save any preset you consistently use. AutoPod is designed by editors for editors to optimize their editing experience.
