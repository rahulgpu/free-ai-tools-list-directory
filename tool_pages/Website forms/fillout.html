About Fillout

The AI Form Builder is an innovative tool that allows users to create various types of forms using artificial intelligence. With the first AI form maker, users can conveniently generate surveys, forms, and quizzes by harnessing the power of AI-powered question generation. Additionally, the AI Form Builder offers the option to intelligently import existing forms from Google Forms, PDF documents, or raw text.To create a form, users can either import an existing form or utilize the AI form builder to generate a new one. The process involves choosing an import method, describing the desired form, or copy/pasting questions to import a specific form. While waiting, users have the opportunity to customize the form's appearance by selecting a theme according to their preferences.The no-code editor provided by Fillout enables users to further customize their forms with ease. Notably, the AI Form Builder's capabilities extend beyond initial form creation. The AI continually offers automatic updates for drafted forms, allowing users to effortlessly make changes as needed.The tool offers language optimization to enhance the effectiveness of questions and optimize language based on best practices, potentially boosting conversion rates. Additionally, Fillout's AI generates images and themes that align with the user's brand.The AI Form Builder offers a free plan with unlimited forms and a monthly submission limit of 1000. The tool is powered by Fillout, an industry leader in form-building technology. For further information and assistance, users can access the comprehensive help resources provided by Fillout.
