About Makeforms

MakeForms is a comprehensive SaaS tool designed to assist in the creation and management of custom forms, surveys, and quizzes for various websites. Beyond the surface-level ability to create forms, this tool also enables users to manage approval workflows, verify leads' contact information, and make sense of data using different view options. MakeForms includes a series of useful applications such as quiz, poll, form, survey, test, and contact form makers. It supports the creation of various types of surveys like NPS, CSAT, and CES, making it a versatile tool for a multitude of use cases, including feedback collection, research, lead generation, registration, application forms creation, appointments scheduling, calculators, and quote builders. MakeForms also features different form types such as One at a Time, Step Forms, and All at Once to cater to different user preferences. It also provides tools for team collaboration, data organization, form design customization, form sources, form embedding, submission verification, and access control, among others. Furthermore, this tool is utilizable across various industries including healthcare, education, marketing, HR, real estate and finance, making it a highly adaptable system.
