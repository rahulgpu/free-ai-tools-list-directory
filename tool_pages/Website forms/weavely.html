About Weavely

Weavely is an AI form builder designed specifically for Figma users, allowing them to turn their visual ideas into interactive, web-based forms. The tool enables a seamless transition from designing to publishing and collecting responses, all within the same platform. With Weavely, users can build custom forms for varied use cases such as user research, marketing, and surveys. For user research, forms with excellent UX can be created to gather valuable insights; for marketing, forms can be embedded in live websites to capture leads and customer information; for surveys, the tool facilitates in gathering opinions for market research, academic study, or customer satisfaction. What makes Weavely stand out is its ability to combine design elements and logical conditions, allowing forms to adapt and respond based on user input. Moreover, there are no restrictions on how forms can look, from the structure to the colours and typography, everything can be customized. The tool also supports a limitless collection of responses in varied forms such as text, images, speech, or location. This flexibility and freedom of design make Weavely a comprehensive and robust solution for creating online forms.
