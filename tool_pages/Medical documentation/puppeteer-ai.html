About Puppeteer AI

Puppeteer AI Patient Intake is a tool designed to streamline the process of onboarding patients in hospitals and clinics. With the intended purpose of reducing medical staff burnout, the tool automates data collection from patients by transforming intake forms into a digital, conversational experience. Input is double-checked for accuracy in real time. To account for patient-specific contexts, the tool adapts its approach, adjusting questions to ensure they are directly relevant to each unique patient. This offers the twofold benefit of enhancing patient experience while also improving the quality and comprehensiveness of information recorded. Medical professionals then receive this data in summarized format, so it's easier to make informed care decisions. In line with the sensitive nature of medical data, Puppeteer AI Patient Intake is HIPAA Compliant. While the basic version allows for up to 10 completed forms, the paid version increases this limit and offers customized setup and access to managed services. Health institutions interested in trying out the software have the option to upload existing intake forms or experiment with a generic demo.
