About AutoNotes

AutoNotes is an AI-driven tool used in the healthcare sector, particularly in the fields of Mental Health, Speech, and Occupational Therapy. The tool is dedicated to helping professionals streamline their documentation processes, offering a variety of functionalities for creating and managing progress notes. The key feature of AutoNotes is generating clinical documents such as SOAP (Subjective, Objective, Assessment, Plan) Notes, DAP (Data, Assessment, Plan) Notes, and Treatment Plans. It provides customizable document templates for Individual Progress Notes and Group Note templates, aiming to improve the tracking of patient encounters and treatment evolution. The tool allows healthcare providers to summarize sessions efficiently, capture treatment plan goals and methods, and include evidence-based practices. Additionally, it provides an option for dictation to convert speech into text, ensuring an easier and faster note-taking process. It also features a secure document storage system for maintaining client records and protecting private information. Another version, the Enterprise Plan, offers additional services, customizable documents in line with the specific needs of the organizations.
