About Dubformer

Dubformer is an AI-powered solution for dubbing and video localization, primarily aimed at the media and entertainment industry. It offers end-to-end localization service including AI dubbing and voice-over, and also AI-powered subtitles and closed captions. One of the major services provided by Dubformer is AI dubbing which includes creating realistic and natural-sounding voice-overs in multiple languages, accurate and culturally sensitive translations along with human quality control, and AI mixing for immersive soundscapes. Furthermore, Dubformer features a unique advantage of AI-powered dubbing, boasting benefits such as multiple voices included in the price, post-delivery corrections, high scalability, affordable cost, fast delivery, and lip-sync alongside voice performance. The tool also makes video content more accessible and engaging for diverse audiences, with verification and adaptation carried out by human professionals, and professional spotting. All AI-powered scripts and translations are checked and verified by localization experts, ensuring high quality control.Dubformer is trusted by various sectors within the media industry including TV channels, content distributors, streaming services/OTT, media production, media service providers, and influencers. One of the tool's main attractions is its cost-effective localization and high-speed production, which enables content providers to maintain quality, reduce costs, and expand their global reach.
