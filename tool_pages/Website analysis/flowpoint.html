About Flowpoint

Flowpoint is an AI-driven web analytics tool that helps maximize online success by uncovering the root causes of poor conversion rates. It harnesses advanced machine learning and cybersecurity algorithms to provide actionable insights for improving website conversion rates, SEO, UX/UI, and content engagement. Flowpoint automatically captures where users click, where they scroll, the time spent on different sections and many more. It also offers standard and advanced event capturing, a lightweight script optimized for performance, and GDPR and CCPA compliance features. With Flowpoint, users can track where visitors come from and where they go on their website, receive AI-powered recommendations for boosting their sales, and make data-driven decisions to grow their business. It prioritizes impactful areas for conversion rate optimization, leveraging cross-domain funnel tracking for a comprehensive understanding of the customer journey. The tool offers technical audits, testing, and user behavior analytics to help websites optimize their performance and achieve their goals. Flowpoint provides several resources, including case studies, expert audits, and testimonials demonstrating its effectiveness in helping business owners improve their online presence. It also offers integration support, website flow setup, and data analysis and monitoring to ensure the success of its users.
