About CreateMyTest

CreateMyTest is an AI-powered tool designed to convert written documents or YouTube videos into test format in a matter of seconds. The AI analysis is capable of translating content into various question styles including multiple choice, true/false, matching, and fill in the blank questions. This tool enhances studying experience by turning regular notes into engaging knowledge check tests, aiding in more effective knowledge retainment. Additionally, CreateMyTest targets to reduce test anxiety by offering unlimited practice sessions to its users. This allows anyone to transform their learning materials into interactive quizzes for self-assessment at any time. The AI system supports conversion of content on virtually any topic, making the application useful for a range of subject matters. CreateMyTest implements a flexible pricing strategy, offering different packages based on users' needs. Users initiate their experience with a free trial, and subsequently they can choose varying one-time payment options according to their word count. For user support, assistance is readily available via email.
