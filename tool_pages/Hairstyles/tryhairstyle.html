About TryHairstyle

TryHairstyle is an AI-driven digital tool that generates a variety of hairstyle images from a single uploaded selfie. The AI model essentially provides a virtual makeover that allows users to see how different hairstyles would look on them, thus helping them avoid regrettable haircuts. After a high-resolution image is uploaded, the AI model begins training and generates results in about 30 minutes. Users receive their customized hairstyles via email and they can also access them in their dashboard. The quality of the output is highly dependent on the quality of the input, hence in-frame, high-quality selfies taken in a well-lit environment tend to yield the best results. The tool also values user privacy, and the payment process is secure. Users have a choice between several packages including Starter, Professional, and Elite, each offering a varying number of unique photos. An additional offering is the Christmas Pack, which includes festive-themed images. The tool, thus, provides a cost-effective, AI-based tool for creative exploration of different virtual hairstyles.
