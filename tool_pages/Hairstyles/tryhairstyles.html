About TryHairstyles

TryHairstyles is an AI-driven virtual hairstyling tool that allows users to try on new hairstyles instantly. Users can upload or take a selfie and choose a hairstyle from an extensive library available on the platform. The chosen hairstyle can be instantly applied to the uploaded photo, giving the user a view of their new look before making a change in real life. The tool thus offers risk-free hair makeovers, providing users the confidence to experiment with new styles, avoiding possible haircut regrets. In addition to a large variety of hairstyles ranging from bold, classic, trendy to unique, TryHairstyles is also set to offer personalized recommendations based on the user's face shape and features. The colors section of the platform enables users to experiment with different hair shades. The tool also provides insights into trending haircuts for users keen on staying up-to-date with the latest styles. For ease of use and accessibility, TryHairstyles maintains a flexible pricing system and is available across different devices.
