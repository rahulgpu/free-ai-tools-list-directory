About Hairstyle AI

Hairstyle AI is a tool powered by AI technology, allowing users to virtually try on a wide array of hairstyles before committing to the actual haircut. Applicable for both male and female haircuts, the tool is designed to enhance self-confidence by allowing users to discover styles that complement their features. Users upload selfies against a plain background, with good lighting and a natural smile, after which the AI generates a diverse catalogue of hairstyles for the user to try. The generated hairstyles are indistinguishable from real photos, offering a realistic preview of how the user would look in each style. Hairstyle AI promises a fast turnaround, and offers the output in a set of 120 HD photos showcasing 30 unique hairstyles in 4 different poses. The tool ensures user privacy and data security, storing the submitted photos only for the duration of the session and ensuring their subsequent deletion.
