About SigniFi

SigniFi is a web-based divination platform that combines AI, blockchain, and traditional practices such as tarot and astrology. It aims to help users find meaning in life by providing personalized guidance through AI-driven tarot readers, astrologers, and fortune-tellers. Users can chat with these experts to receive insights and advice tailored to their specific needs. Additionally, SigniFi allows users to create unique and personalized non-fungible tokens (NFTs) that showcase their life traits and potential. This feature allows users to express their individuality and explore the intersection of technology and spirituality. The platform also offers opportunities for users to earn, spend, and receive airdrops of the project token, $SIGNI. This token can be used to unlock more fun experiences within the SigniFi ecosystem.The roadmap outlines upcoming phases, including the launch of the AI Tarot bot on Telegram, the official launch of the SigniFi AI Tarot Web App, the release of a personalized NFT mint system with airdrop campaigns and paid services, the establishment of the $SIGNI token economy, and the introduction of the SigniFi Genesis NFT Collection. Future releases include a limited tarot NFT collection and the introduction of diverse AI-driven divination methods.Overall, SigniFi aims to provide a unique and innovative approach to divination, merging ancient wisdom with cutting-edge technology to help users navigate and discover the meaning in their lives.
