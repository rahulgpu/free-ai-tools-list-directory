About Flick – AI assistant

Iris is an AI-powered social media management tool. It is specifically designed to assist businesses in growing their brand through effective social interaction. Iris provides a comprehensive solution for social media strategy, content planning, and content creation. Iris enables users to visually plan their content ahead of time and find optimal hashtags. Its sophisticated analytics helps users track and evaluate their social media performance.Apart from these primary services, Iris also provides extensive resources for learning and further improvement. These include tips and tricks through blogs, free social media training through an academy, and videos providing aid in various aspects.Moreover, Iris also provides a remarkable feature by being available as mobile applications for iOS and Android platforms. Allowing easy access to hashtags, scheduling, and analytics tools on the go.In addition, Iris' unique Brand Hub allows users to create quality, on-brand social posts consistently. Iris analyzes existing social posts to generate highly engaging captions, ensuring the content is always relevant and specific to the user's business.Iris also focuses on developing a tailored social plan for your business. It incorporates the use of generated content pillars based on users' business goals, and Iris' learning from thousands of businesses across various industries. Iris also outlines a clear and detailed plan for users, for what they need to post each month.Overall, Iris serves as a comprehensive tool for businesses, enabling them to harness the power of social media through strategic use of AI.
