About FeedAI

FeedAI is an Artificial Intelligence (AI)-powered tool designed to facilitate the creation of social media content, making online engagement more dynamic and manageable. The primary functionality of this tool is transforming user input, based on specific topics, into optimal visuals, captions, and hashtags suitable for social media. This process is aided by features such as AI-generated images, personalised captions, and smart hashtags, all designed to heighten social media engagement. The multilingual support system allows users to cater to international audiences by generating content in the language of their preference. The application also offers a credit system, allowing users to control their social media budget by either subscribing to an unlimited plan or buying credits as required. A user simply provides a brief description of their desired post, and FeedAI leverages advanced AI technology to generate suitable imagery, caption, and hashtags, which can then be reviewed and posted directly to a social media account. Though FeedAI is designed to block the generation of illegal, inappropriate, or explicit content, usage must still be responsible and ethical. Any attempt to generate such content will consume credits. Users commit to complying with the End User License Agreement (EULA) by purchasing credits or subscribing within the app.
