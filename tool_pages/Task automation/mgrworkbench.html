About MgrWorkbench

MgrWorkbench.ai is an AI-powered tool designed to simplify and speed up business-writing tasks. It is intended to help managers effortlessly draft employee performance reviews, development plans, and business letters. It features an interactive feedback system that can create feedback for behaviors, as well as a best practice-driven approach to creating performance reviews, so managers can focus on providing feedback rather than writing. The tool is offered with a simple and risk-free pricing structure, with options for free, weekly, monthly, or annual plans. All plans provide access to regular features, including components, reviews, development plans, and workbench access, but the more expensive plans also include email support. MgrWorkbench.ai is designed to revolutionize the performance management process by providing an easier way to write for business. It also provides contact and legal information, including Terms of Service and Privacy Policy.
