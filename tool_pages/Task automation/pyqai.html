About PyqAI

Pyq is an AI platform that allows users to build custom AI solutions using a simple API. This platform provides the flexibility to create an AI solution from scratch or use pre-existing solutions. Users can train their own models, automate workflows, or interact with documents via a chat function. Additionally, Pyq places an emphasis on models that are target-specific, interpretable, grounded in user data and privacy conscious. Not only do the models deliver results, but they also provide justifications, allowing users to understand the AI's process and identify areas for enhancement. The platform ensures that the user's model and training data do not have to leave the secure confines of the user's cloud infrastructure, safeguarding their privacy and intellectual property. Furthermore, Pyq provides ready-made solutions that can be integrated into their system quickly, designed to solve common business problems. For businesses looking for precise, tailor-made AI solutions, Pyq provides enterprise solutions. These solutions provide the flexibility to train and run AI solutions on the users own cloud or on Pyqs platform. Pyq's unique offerings empower users to build AI solutions that not only deliver consistent and reliable results, but also cost less and run faster than off the shelf models.
