About Magical AI

Magical AI is a free AI writing assistant tool powered by GPT4 technology. It is designed to automate tasks, which includes drafting emails and other forms of online messages. The tool integrates with different applications and can be used for varied functions from sales to customer support. It offers features like a text expander, autofill, AI email writer, and auto form filler. Furthermore, it eliminates the need for AI training as it understands the context of conversations, provides smart suggestions and improves results through live messaging. The AI assistant helps in avoiding spelling or grammatical errors and can be accessed anywhere on the internet. Functionally, users can customize and easily generate responses to emails, InMails, etc., with one click, utilizing the referenced info from the original message for personalized replies. The tool is also capable of extracting data from search results, facilitating quick and efficient database updates. Despite its advanced AI technology, Magical AI is simple to use, requiring no complex programming or AI expertise from the user. It empowers individuals and teams to minimize their busywork and boost their productivity.
