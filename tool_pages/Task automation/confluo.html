About Confluo

Confluo App is a productivity tool that utilizes advanced AI models, specifically generative pre-trained transformers (GPT), to help users complete tasks more efficiently. By leveraging the power of GPT AI, Confluo offers an auto-complete feature for tasks that require multiple internet searches and browsing web pages. This functionality saves users time and effort by automating the process of gathering information.The app also provides an easy-to-use task management system, allowing users to effectively organize and track their activities and projects. Users can easily create, update, and prioritize tasks, enhancing their overall productivity.Additionally, Confluo offers collaboration features that enable users to connect and work together with others. This functionality fosters teamwork and enables seamless communication and coordination among team members.To further boost productivity, Confluo includes a focus mode, which helps users stay focused and eliminate distractions while working on their tasks. This feature ensures that users can concentrate on their work without interruptions.Confluo App is free to download and use, without any advertisements. Users can install the app and benefit from five self-completing tasks per month and one collaborative activity for free. The app is available on both iOS and Android platforms.For any inquiries or support, users can reach out to Confluo through email or contact them via postal address. The dedicated support team is readily available to assist users with their questions and concerns.
