About Lampi.ai

Lampi AI is a secure and confidential AI copilot designed to assist businesses by improving their workflows and productivity. This AI-powered platform enables extensive searches across an organization's knowledge base, including internal documents, servers, and apps. It also aids in high-quality content generation, brainstorming, and more, fully leveraging the organization's existing knowledge resources. It is particularly useful for teams, with the ability to share workspaces, and can even attend meetings to provide insights and summaries. One of the distinctive features of Lampi AI is its proficiency in over 80 languages, assisting multinational teams in breaking language barriers. The tool can efficiently curate and rate data, and facilitates reinforcement learning from human feedback, continuously improving the relevance of the information it provides. Lampi AI can generate and summarize documents, provide insights and ideas. It can be tailored to benefit different professions and fields of expertise, including consulting, corporate finance, sales and business development, legal, human resources, customer services, marketing, tech, and devs. On top of these features, Lampi AI places a high emphasis on data security and privacy, ensuring data confidentiality and integrity by using strict security measures.
