About Tuesday

Tuesday is an AI-powered spreadsheet tool that automates work processes by utilizing autonomous AI assistants. These assistants have the ability to browse the internet and provide answers to user queries. With Tuesday, users can conveniently upload data either by importing a CSV file or copying and pasting directly into the tool. The AI technology in Tuesday enables users to prompt the assistants to research each row of data, saving users valuable time and effort. The tool also offers the capability to export data in CSV format, allowing users to access their work from anywhere.Tuesday is designed to streamline various tasks and offers a range of automation possibilities. Users can automate tasks such as writing cold emails, researching sales leads, researching investors, translating text, turning blogs into social media posts, localizing apps, writing SEO articles, summarizing user feedback, conducting sentiment analysis, triaging customer requests, and summarizing articles. By harnessing the power of AI, Tuesday aims to simplify workflows and deliver increased efficiency.Overall, Tuesday is a powerful AI tool that combines the functionality of a spreadsheet with the capabilities of web scraping and Google search integration. Its AI assistants and automation features make it a valuable asset for individuals and teams looking to optimize their work processes and maximize productivity.
