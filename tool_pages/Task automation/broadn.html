About broadn

Broadn: AI Assistant for Efficient Work AI in Your Inbox: Broadn introduces an AI Assistant, Herbie, that you can access from email to manage tasks and increase productivity. Herbie's Capabilities: More than just an assistant, Herbie can assist in a variety of domains including marketing, research, and sales. He can handle complex tasks autonomously, allowing users to focus on other priorities. Interaction Mechanism: Users can task Herbie by sending him an email. Once the task is complete, he'll send an update. Content Creation: Herbie has the capability to generate content. His online connectivity ensures the material is up-to-date and relevant. Research: Instead of hiring a separate team for market analysis and research, Herbie can carry out these tasks. He can accomplish extensive research projects in significantly reduced time. Business Analysis: Herbie can create presentations and analyze both internal and external data swiftly. He can process and interpret vast amounts of numerical data accurately.
