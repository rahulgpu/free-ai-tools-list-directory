About Zeligate

Zeligate is an innovative AI tool designed to streamline the talent recruitment process. It focuses on automating repetitive tasks, managing workflows, and refining processes thus enabling teams to focus on more meaningful and impactful tasks. Zeligate's functionality ranges from assisting in writing and posting job ads to evaluating candidates and managing meeting schedules. It exhibits an interactive and adaptive AI system that learns from your work to meet your specific needs. Additionally, it enables tasks delegation using text prompts and voice commands, and it's capable of learning from these tasks to continuously improve its services. Its recruitment process is efficient and seamless, providing services such as instant job postings through text or voice commands, a resume match feature that offers concise summaries for quicker decision-making, and a streamlined interview process that includes handling the first screening and scheduling of interviews. It's designed to unshackle users from mundane tasks and foster higher levels of creativity and productivity.
