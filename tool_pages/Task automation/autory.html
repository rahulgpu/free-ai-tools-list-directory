About Autory

Autory is an automation tool aimed at increasing business productivity by automating repetitive tasks. These tasks are often tedious, time-consuming, and distract employees from more meaningful work. Autory focuses on developing bespoke solutions, tailored to the unique needs of a specific business or workflow. Prioritizing a collaborative approach, the process begins with the customer sharing their workflows and related challenges. After understanding these intricacies, Autory proposes an automation solution crafted to address those specific issues. Upon the customer's confirmation, an account for their business is set up and work commences on creating their bespoke automation solution. A distinctive feature of Autory is the continuous improvement and evolution of its automation methods. As the platform discovers and develops new ways to enhance their automation methods, those updates are shared with clients to improve their solutions. Another aspect of Autory's service provision is its personal support. The company aims to partner automation with a human touch to effectively meet customer needs and queries. Thus, despite its heavy concentration on automation, significant tasks such as customer interaction remain human-led. Their pricing model is focused on the value it provides by saving their customers' time, facilitating easy business scaling.
