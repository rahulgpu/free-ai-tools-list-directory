About Eilla

Eilla AI is an AI-driven platform designed to redefine the conventional workflow of Mergers & Acquisitions (M&A), Venture Capital (VC), and Private Equity (PE) deals. It offers automation of routine tasks while also providing solid support for complex decision-making processes. With a focus on emulating the quality of industry professionals, Eilla AI manages to transform the management of deal flows, thereby enhancing efficiency and productivity. Users can obtain early access to explore the platform's features and capabilities, and shape its ongoing development through their feedback. The platform also provides an 'Insider Program', primarily intended for select companies, allowing them a high level of involvement with the executive and engineering teams to directly influence the development of new features. While the platform aims to drive transaction efficiency, it also places a significant focus on fostering close interactions with the user community, intending to create a user-driven and performance-oriented environment. An enquiry and support system is in place for users requiring assistance or aiming to explore broader potential collaborations.
