About Silly Robot Cards

Silly Robot Cards is an AI-driven platform for creating unique greeting cards for any occasion, such as Birthdays, Valentines, Christmas, etc. The platform asks for specific input about the recipient, such as their relationship to the sender, their hobbies, interests and other personal details, in order to generate a bespoke card that is printed and dispatched. Friendly and intuitive, the interface allows for full personalisation, from crafting messages to uploading images. An additional key feature is the sites attention to sustainability, making use of water-based inks and sustainable paper while ensuring that all their products are vegan-friendly and recyclable. The company places important regard for users' privacy: all uploaded pictures are deleted off the server within a month. The cards created on Silly Robot Cards are good quality, printed on glossy card paper and come complete with a paper envelope, sealed in a biodegradable, protective bag. The service is available worldwide, with international shipping, adding further convenience for users looking to send personalized greetings across borders.
