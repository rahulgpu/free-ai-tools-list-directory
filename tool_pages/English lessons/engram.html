About Engram

Engram is an AI-powered grammar checker tool designed to improve written English. Propelled by ChatGPT, it provides assistance in correcting errors concerning grammar, spelling, punctuation, and word usage. The tool is optimized for non-native English speakers and endeavors to make their writing more natural. Beyond simple mistake rectifications, Engram offers contextual corrections addressing aspects like the appropriate usage of articles, correct word order, selection of words, and accurate spellings to enhance readability. It enlarges writing assistance scope by offering alternate suggestions as part of its features. Engram caters to a diverse range of users, including students, working professionals, and anyone needing to communicate in English even if English is not their native language. It is considered particularly beneficial for students practicing for ESL exams such as TOEFL, TOEIC, or IELTS and professionals required to write in English for their work. User experiences highlight the tool's accuracy and simplified interface which conveniently indicates the advised edits.
