About Bookbud

BookBud.ai is a web-based service designed to support self-published authors in creating both fiction and non-fiction books with the assistance of AI technology. The platform offers tools that allow authors to quickly write and publish their books in various formats, including ebooks, print books, and audiobooks. One of the key features of BookBud.ai is its efficient AI-powered writing capabilities, which enable authors to create high-quality books in a relatively short amount of time. The service also provides options for ghostwriting, allowing authors who prefer a more hands-off approach to still benefit from the platform.By using BookBud.ai, self-published authors can level the playing field with traditionally-published authors, who have historically had a significant advantage. The platform aims to make self-publishing a profitable and accessible business opportunity, offering affordable pricing plans that start at $15 per book.BookBud.ai streamlines the publishing process by providing rapid ebook release services, with formatting and distribution ready within approximately an hour. Authors can also enjoy hassle-free distribution to major platforms, ensuring global visibility for their work. The platform additionally offers print book formatting options for $10, as well as the innovative opportunity to create audiobooks using advanced AI-cloned voices through a collaboration with AuthorVoices.ai.With over a decade of industry expertise, BookBud.ai provides aspiring and established writers with a comprehensive roadmap for successful publishing, guiding them from the initial idea to effective promotion. The platform strives to be a trusted and reliable resource, empowering authors to transform into successful publishers.
