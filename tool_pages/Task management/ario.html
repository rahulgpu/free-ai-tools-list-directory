About Ario

Ario serves as a personal, proactive AI assistant designed to help manage and organize daily tasks and reminders. Ario is an AI that takes context into consideration, enabling it to help you manage your tasks more effectively. It helps avoid schedule conflicts by knowing your availability and alerting you in case of any overlapping appointments or events. It customizes suggestions based on your and your family's interests. For example, it could provide event suggestions based on your child's favorite hobbies or books, or suggest party ideas ahead of time for your child's upcoming birthday. Furthermore, Ario also integrates with your Amazon account to ensure you never miss a return window. A strong focus of Ario's development is maintaining user privacy and data ownership, treating your data with care and respect. It aims to revolutionize managing daily tasks by giving practical assistance and reminders in a personalized manner based on your preferences, schedule, and lifestyle. Ario is deployed via an app, which enables easy access for users.
