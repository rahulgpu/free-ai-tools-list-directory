About Aruarian

Aruarian is an AI-driven task management solution aiming to streamline and enhance personal productivity. As the pioneer in AI task management solutions, Aruarian removes the tediousness associated with traditional to-do lists by automating list creations and details search. The AI, referred to as 'Ari', acts as a personal assistant, responding to user prompts to add tasks, organize lists, and retrieve specific information, all on-the-spot. Not just limited to task management, Aruarian promises an easier and more effective lifestyle through anxiety-free task planning and execution. This makes it an ideal tool for individuals looking for a seamless, intuitive, and thoughtful method of managing daily tasks and information. Aruarian is an early-stage, bootstrapped startup, funded by community backing, which means the income generated is invested back into the development and expansion of its services. Operating on a subscription-based model, users gain complete access to Aruarian's AI assistance upon subscription. Its continued development and improvements, as well as future early accessed capabilities, justify the subscription pricing. The founder, Yash, was inspired to create Aruarian as an alternative to manual and less efficient productivity tools, thereby making a difference in the productivity app landscape. Aruarian is currently offering beta access.
