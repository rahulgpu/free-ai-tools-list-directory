About Snapsked

Snapsked is an AI-based tool designed for efficient task management and organization. Utilizing intelligent extraction capabilities, it interprets data from a range of sources, such as screenshots, emails, online chats, and web pages to identify and catalog actionable items. Once these tasks are uncovered, Snapsked uses its AI to review and categorize the relevant specifics such as due dates, important notes, or links related to each task, and integrates them into your calendar. This process allows for a streamlined way of managing tasks in one cohesive space. Additionally, the tool has facility to handle a variety of data formats, whether the information comes from informal chats or professional emails. Snapsked aims to simplify the process of turning visual data such as screenshots and photos into functional task lists, contributing to productivity and time management. Snapsked represents a direction of Spacefold AI's exploration in combining AI and productivity tools to create pragmatic solutions.
