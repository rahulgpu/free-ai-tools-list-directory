About Reetail

Reetail is an online store builder designed with simplicity and user-friendliness in mind. It allows small businesses to set up their own e-commerce platforms without requiring coding knowledge. This tool is created to be straightforward, requiring minimal setup, which makes it suitable for small operations that may find other platforms too complex. Reetail offers a variety of features including professional theme, checkout, and payment processing right out of the box. Users can customize their stores by adding their own logo, colors, fonts, and even a custom domain. The platform is integrated with Stripe for seamless handling of products, orders, and customers and also manages taxes. Other features include multi-currency support, compatibility with mobile devices, ability to sell both one-time and recurring products, and more. Reetail is fast by design and ready for use with no coding required. It adopts a no-fee transaction policy and all the costs are covered in a fixed monthly subscription fee. The tool differentiates itself from other e-commerce platforms by providing a simplified way to sell products online, automatic SEO optimization, and hassle-free payment systems.
