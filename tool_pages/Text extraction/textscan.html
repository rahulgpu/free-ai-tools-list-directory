About TextScan

TextScan AI is a productivity-enhancing mobile application designed to offer seamless and efficient chatting capabilities. It is an AI-powered tool that incorporates text conversion from images which facilitates ease in communication while saving users the hassle of manual typing. Users can utilize their mobile's camera to scan text from various images and the app will convert these images into editable and shareable text. Aside from this, the application allows users to interact with an AI chatbot, providing an alternate means of communication and making it even more convenient for various user scenarios. Notably, it also supports voice messages, further enhancing its usefulness and versatility. TextScan AI, a free app available on the Apple App Store, has been designed for iPad and can be used on iOS, iPadOS, and Mac devices with the required operating system versions. The developer, Talat Eryilmaz, has emphasized a commitment to user privacy, with no data collection taking place through the app. The application is regularly updated for improved user experience and added features.
