About Remysec

Remy is an AI tool that supports product security by identifying and mitigating potential security risks during the design phase. Remy's main purpose is to discover upcoming engineering work, perform automatic triage, and help hasten design reviews. Remy specifically targets two pain points: ensuring visibility of planned engineering tasks that may introduce risks, and managing the overwhelming workload of manually tracking risky engineering plans. Beyond that, the tool automatically initiates reviews, reducing the likelihood of delays and miscommunication, and streamlining the kick-off process with dynamic questions directed at engineering teams. Remy aims to plug risk coverage gaps by automatically generating relevant questions based on context from the user's internal guidelines and policies. This approach results in a tailor-made, comprehensive risk mitigation plan each time. In summary, Remy offers a robust AI-based solution for managing product security, ensuring early identification of potential risks and optimizing design review processes.
