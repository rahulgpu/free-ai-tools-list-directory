About Warden

Warden is an AI copilot designed to aid security reviews by enhancing the productivity of security engineers. It primarily targets the reduction of security backlogs that tend to slow down product launches, while also aiming to minimize security vulnerabilities which can be detrimental to business operations. One of its key functionalities includes the generation of technical architecture diagrams based on documentation provided and questions about the particular use-case. These diagrams assist security engineers to identify potential issues effectively. Warden is competent at understanding your project at a granular level, and subsequently generates a comprehensive list of risk factors. Moreover, it also offers AI-generated mitigation suggestions that the security engineers can review, helping product teams in building more secure products. Warden is configured to work with any application, making it highly versatile and adaptable for different security workflows. While the product's pricing is dependant on the number of seats, it offers a 14-day free trial to allow potential users to explore its functionalities.
