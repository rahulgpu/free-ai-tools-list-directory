About Buddy AI

BuddyAI is an AI-powered tool that aims to simplify and enhance the job search process. This tool acts as a companion, saving users time and frustration by sending job alerts directly to their inbox. By doing so, BuddyAI keeps users effortlessly informed about new opportunities across the internet, effectively eliminating the need for solo and repetitive job hunting.To get started, users can easily set up their job email alerts with BuddyAI, joining the platform with their Google account. Importantly, user privacy is prioritized, as emails received are free from unnecessary content and users' email addresses are never shared with any third parties.In addition to job alerts, BuddyAI offers users the option to subscribe to its News Teller, providing them with weekly product and job updates directly from the BuddyAI team. This feature aims to enhance users' knowledge and increase their chances of finding the right job opportunities. Users have the freedom to unsubscribe from this service at any time.Developed by tech professionals, BuddyAI caters specifically to individuals in the tech industry. This tool seeks to revolutionize the job search experience by providing users with relevant and timely job opportunities, ultimately simplifying and improving their job search journey.
