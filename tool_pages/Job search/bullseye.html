About Bullseye

Bullseye is an AI-powered tool designed to streamline the job-hunting process. It brings precision into job searching, resume building, and cover letter creation by providing users with examples, tools, and related content. Built around the philosophy of precision job matching, Bullseye uses artificial intelligence to identify the best job matches for users based on their skills, experience and career aspirations. It personalizes job applications according to each job description and aids users in showcasing their unique skills and qualifications. Additionally, Bullseye's automated approach strives to save job seekers the time traditionally spent on manual job searching. In its mission to equip users with the necessary skills to enhance their job search, the platform also has an educational blog that shares knowledge and insights on improving the accuracy and effectiveness of job seeking. The tool also offers resume builders which can potentially improve visibility among employers. Its core vision is to deliver an accurate and seamless job hunting experience, whilst aligning job opportunities with users' aspirations, values and skills
