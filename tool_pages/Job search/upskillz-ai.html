About Upskillz.ai

Upskillz.ai is an AI tool designed to match individuals with AI or Machine Learning (ML) job opportunities based on the information provided in their resumes. The tool not only identifies potential job roles the user may be qualified for, such as AI Engineer, ML Engineer, Data Scientist, and various lead and senior positions, but also recommends relevant courses to help the user further upgrade their skills and increase their job market competitiveness. This process focuses on synchronizing personal career aspirations with the demands of the job market. The tool features an interactive chatbot that guides the user through the application, offering job selections and providing helpful pointers towards their desired careers. Please note that using this application implies agreement to its Privacy Policy and Terms of Use. Users are encouraged to provide feedback via email to help improve the platform's overall user experience.
