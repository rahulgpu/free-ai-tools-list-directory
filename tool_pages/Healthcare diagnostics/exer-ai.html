About Exer AI

Exer AI offers an all-in-one digital health platform designed to deliver real-time insights on diverse musculoskeletal (MSK) conditions. Exer AI's goal is to streamline in-clinic treatments and foster effective remote care pathways. The platform has several components:Exer Scan: This tool uses AI to screen and monitor MSK-related issues without sensors or hardware, reducing the risk of human error. It assists in distinguishing between hundreds of acute or chronic MSK-related disorders precisely.Exer Health: This component of the platform caters to at-home patient engagement, providing AI-based monitoring along with real-time guidance to ensure good form and protocol adherence.Automated Care Pathways: This concept centralizes treatment plans and improves patient outcomes with AI analysis and verifiable computer vision data.Exer IQ: This helps to capture outcomes, analyze population health, and predict risks using standardized or custom-made patient response surveys.Remote Therapeutic Monitoring (RTM): This AI-based RTM software produces objective, auditable data needed to satisfy public and private payers.Exer AI elegantly supports comprehensive care in areas like orthopedics, senior care, neurology, primary care, and rehabilitation. Its key features position it as a valuable tool for proactive fall prevention, functional outcome evaluations, diagnosis and tracking of MSK disorders, and personalized treatment plans with remote monitoring, thus leading to improved patient outcomes.
