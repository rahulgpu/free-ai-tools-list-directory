About NamelyAI

Namely AI is an AI-driven platform designed to automate sales calls, offering an innovative way to improve efficiency while reducing associated costs. It presents itself as a personal AI sales assistant capable of generating qualified leads in a simple and effective manner. The system can automate various elements of the sales process, such as sales calls and responses, crafting product or service pitches, addressing objections, and overcoming sales challenges. It also gathers customer insights, helps in scheduling demos, and sends detailed email summaries with call highlights and demo schedules. Namely AI's other features include conducting automatic A/B tests on sales calls for improved results, generating comprehensive call logs for review, and engaging in natural, conversational dialogues beyond sales topics. The platform boasts around-the-clock operation and advanced AI capabilities to expertly navigate objections and secure demos. It is designed for instant scalability, enabling it to handle high call volumes. Namely AI offers an alternative to traditional human sales representatives, promising to reduce costs, increase efficiency, and enable effortless business scaling. It does not require commission or healthcare benefits and operates without breaks or downtime.
