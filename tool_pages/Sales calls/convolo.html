About Convolo

Convolo.ai provides a suite of communication tools aimed at boosting sales, improving lead conversion and enhancing ROI. These tools are designed to facilitate both inbound and outbound sales campaigns. Among the tools offered for improving outbound sales operations are services for lead routing, lead distribution, and lead responses. For inbound sales, the service provides various dialing solutions including multi-line dialer, power dialer, auto dialer, and sales dialer. In addition to these, Convolo.ai supports lead generation from the website and has technology to accelerate the process from lead capture to sales call, aiming to avoid losing deals to competitors. Another notable feature of Convolo.ai is the capability for CRM integration allowing businesses to tailor their workflows as needed. These tools should help businesses optimize their sales strategy, speed up their sales processes and improve client communication. The company's tools are trusted by numerous businesses.
