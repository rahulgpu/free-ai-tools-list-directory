About Staircase AI

Staircase AI is a customer intelligence and analytics platform. Its primary focus is on uncovering churn risks and opportunities by analyzing a multitude of customer signals and transforming them into actionable insights. The platform offers a versatile range of use-cases, including predicting and preventing customer churn, discovering growth and expansion opportunities, boosting productivity and team efficiency, and offering visibility and control via a 360 data warehouse. It facilitates AI-powered health scores to evaluate the status of the customer base. The product is designed to cater to different personas, such as customer success leaders and managers, sales leaders, and professionals in various service fields. Staircase AI collects and analyzes diverse data, including emails, tickets, messages, calls, and product usage, and automatically converts these customer signals into churn risks and growth opportunities. It allows proactive responses to recognized risks or opportunities. Beyond the analysis aspect, the platform ensures governance and visibility with a unique 360 data warehouse. Another key feature is enhancing efficiency and productivity, allowing strategic, efficient, and productive revenue choices without dependence on CRM perfection.
