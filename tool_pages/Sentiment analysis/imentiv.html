About Imentiv

Imentiv AI is an advanced Emotion AI tool that enables users to create emotionally engaging video content. By analyzing the emotions of actors in videos, Imentiv AI provides deep insights into the emotional impact of the content. This understanding allows users to anticipate how their audience will perceive their videos and create content that resonates with viewers on an emotional level. With the goal of enhancing audience engagement and maximizing ROI, Imentiv AI helps users analyze ads, videos, and other content through AI-driven emotion analytics.Imentiv AI offers a unique feature by providing the expertise of trained psychologists alongside AI analysis. These psychologists conduct a detailed analysis of the videos, examining each frame to assess emotional connections with the audience. They also identify any psychological biases or heuristics used to emotionally affect the viewers. This interdisciplinary approach allows users to refine their messages and create more impactful content.Using Imentiv AI is a straightforward process. Users can simply upload videos with human characters via drag and drop or by pasting a YouTube URL. The AI processes the video, recognizes faces, and detects emotions. The tool provides accurate emotion analysis, and users can gain deeper insights through the psychologists' review. The final report includes a visual representation of the emotions expressed by the actors, as well as an overall emotion analysis of the video and emotions of individual actors in each frame.Overall, Imentiv AI is a comprehensive tool that combines AI-driven emotion analytics with the expertise of trained psychologists to help users create emotionally intelligent videos that resonate with their audience.
