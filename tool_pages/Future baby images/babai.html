About BabAI

BabAI is an AI tool designed to generate images of potential offspring based on the images of two individuals. It computes possible facial feature combinations based on the parents' photos and outputs a total of 16 images. These images depict a male and female child each at four different lifestages: baby, toddler, teenager, and adult. The tool thereby provides a speculative glimpse at a couple's potential biological children.One of BabAI's key strengths is its simplicity and user-friendly interface. Users only need to submit images of two individuals, and wait for the tool to generate potential child images. These results are delivered directly via email.Beyond entertaining user curiosity, BabAI can also be a fun tool for surprising friends or loved ones, offering a humorously speculative look at what potential children with various partners might look like. The tool emphasizes privacy and does not require subscription.However, it should be emphasized that whilst the AI aims to combine facial features realistically, accurately predicting genetics is extremely complex, and the results should be considered speculative and for entertainment purposes only.
