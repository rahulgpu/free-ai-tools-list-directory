About Maskmyprompt

MaskMyPrompt is an AI tool that allows users to anonymize their prompts before sending them to ChatGPT. This tool is designed for users who want to protect their confidential data by changing the names of people, organizations, and locations in their prompts to made-up ones. With MaskMyPrompt, users can highlight the names that need to be masked, and the tool will replace them with fictional counterparts automatically.To ensure data privacy, all prompt data stays private in the user's browser window and is never saved or sent to any server. MaskMyPrompt is written with amazing Transformers.js and jointly programmed by Mike Ushakov and ChatGPT. To report bugs, to request features or to share use cases, users can contact MaskMyPrompt via email or Twitter.Overall, MaskMyPrompt is an AI tool that enables users to control their data and ensure their privacy when interacting with ChatGPT. By automating the anonymization process, this tool makes it easier for users to protect sensitive information in their prompts.
