About CreateBookAI

CreateBookAI is an AI-powered tool designed to assist in generating children's books. It enables users to create illustrated children's books within a short timeframe, removing the need for advanced writing or graphic design skills. Users have a variety of adjustable parameters to influence the generation of the book, including the title, main characters, their companions, the timeline, moral lessons, and the number of pages. The AI also generates four illustrations per page, and users can select which they prefer, or regenerate the image if required. Any text produced by the AI can also be edited freely by the user. Once generated, the children's book is provided in an editable, printable square format that can be exported to PDF. Users have full ownership over the generated book, including the selling rights and copyright. This tool is operated via a credit-based system. However, specifics regarding the cost should be checked directly on the platform.
