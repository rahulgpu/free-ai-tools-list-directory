About Pillser Ask

Pillser Ask is an AI-powered tool that provides personalized supplement recommendations based on individual health goals and needs. It allows users to search for specific supplement benefits, evaluates potential risks, compares different products, and offers personal advice according to given user context including, but not limited to, age, sex, and fitness level. It uses advanced AI models and an extensive database of AI-generated research to provide evidence-based answers about supplementation. It's important to note that while the tool is designed to assist in making informed decisions about supplementation, the AI could make mistakes. Therefore, information provided by Pillser Ask should be independently verified. Furthermore, to gain access to a more personalized experience, users are encouraged to sign up and establish a health card profile. The tool does not provide medical advice and users should seek professional medical advice where necessary.
