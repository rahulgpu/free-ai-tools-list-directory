About SmarterFolder

SmarterFolder is a semantic image search tool that allows you to efficiently search for photos on your local drive using AI technology. The tool is specifically designed to find images based on a user's textual description of what is in the picture. This introduces a new, intuitive way of finding photos on your device, bypassing the limitation of manual search or defining complex filters. The application enhances user experience by integrating AI capabilities to understand and interpret the description given to search for photos. Since the application searches inside your local drive, no photos will leave your device ensuring full privacy. The App is available for MacOS users. The tool's primary focus is to revolutionize the way users search for their photos by leveraging AI technology while maintaining privacy by conducting the whole photo searching process on the device itself. SmarterFolder offers privacy policy and support for the users to understand how the tool operates without compromising personal data. Despite these robust features and capabilities, user-friendliness remains at the forefront of the app's design.
