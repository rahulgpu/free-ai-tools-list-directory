About Tarotnova

AI Tarot Nova is an AI-powered Tarot Reading Master that offers free tarot readings, tarot card meanings, and daily tarot draws. It is a free tarot reading app that utilizes artificial intelligence to provide guidance and insight. Users can ask specific questions in various categories such as love, career, and finance. The tool allows users to pick their own tarot cards using different spreads, including a 3-card spread, a 1-card spread, and a yes or no spread, among others.AI Tarot Nova aims to provide clear guidance for personal journeys. It offers personalized and interactive conversations through AI-powered tarot readings. The tool ensures a consistent and objective experience by eliminating human bias. Additionally, it prioritizes privacy and security by not requiring users to reveal personal information during the readings.It utilizes the Rider-Waite Tarot deck, created in 1910, known for its captivating and influential illustrations by Pamela Colman Smith. The deck provides a rich symbolism that enhances the tarot reading experience.While AI Tarot Nova is designed for entertainment purposes, it encourages users with serious problems or concerns to seek professional help. The tool is committed to user privacy and safety, as stated in its Privacy Policy and Terms of Use.Overall, AI Tarot Nova is a free AI-powered tarot reading app that offers personalized and objective tarot readings, allowing users to gain insights on various aspects of their lives.
