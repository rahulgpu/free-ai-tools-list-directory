About My AI Tarot

My AI Tarot Mobile is an artificial intelligence-powered tool that offers free interactive tarot readings online. The tool provides a variety of tarot spreads catering to a range of questions and areas of life. Its services include basic readings such as the '3 Cards Tarot Spread' which explores past, present, and future aspects of a situation, to more complex readings like the '5 Cards Tarot Spread' and the 'Pentagram Spread', which incorporate multiple elements for a more comprehensive view. The tool specifically offers 'Love Tarot Readings' too, focusing on relationships and emotional connections. My AI Tarot Mobile also provides a 'Yes Or No Spreads' function, for users looking for straightforward, simple readings. Additional features include an 'Interpretation Tool' to decipher tarot card meanings and a 'Tarot Quiz' to practice. The tool can be used for fun, personal exploration or as a guide for deeper introspection. It is designed to provide helpful insights, tailored to the specific queries or issues of the user.
