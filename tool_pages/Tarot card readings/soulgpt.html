About SoulGPT

SoulGPT is a Tarot card reading application for Mac users, designed to bring the wisdom and insights of the Tarot at the user's fingertips. The application uses advanced artificial intelligence capabilities to provide accurate card interpretations and personalized guidance tailored to user's life situations. Key features of SoulGPT include interactive Tarot readings, in-depth card interpretations, and personalized advice based on the unique combination of cards drawn in a reading, card interactions, and the overall energy of the reading. The application takes a positive and constructive approach, helping users prepare for the reading's insights, whether cautionary or encouraging. To ensure comprehension, SoulGPT concludes readings by asking users a question related to the reading, encouraging reflection and understanding of the cards' messages. SoulGPT offers a menu for user-defined experiences and caters to varied interests, including dream meanings and astrology. Despite its specialization in Tarot readings, SoulGPT presents itself as a tool for self-discovery and personal growth, aiming to guide users through life's challenges and provide valuable insights into their past, present, and future. To use SoulGPT, an OpenAI API key is required, effectively ensuring user's data privacy as the API key is stored locally and not shared with the tool developers.
