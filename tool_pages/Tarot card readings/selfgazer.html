About Selfgazer

Selfgazer is an AI tool that offers psychological tarot readings and self-exploration for users seeking personal insights and revelations. By utilizing classic tarot symbolism, it provides a platform for individuals to embark on a self-discovery journey. Through this tool, users can gain deep psychological insights about themselves.The tool offers an entertaining and thought-provoking experience, allowing users to delve into their inner selves. Whether users are seeking guidance, introspection, or simply an engaging pastime, Selfgazer aims to provide a space for self-exploration.It is important to note that Selfgazer does not possess the capability to diagnose or treat any mental health conditions. Instead, it is intended solely for entertainment purposes. Users should be aware that the tool is designed to offer psychological tarot readings and insights, rather than professional psychiatric advice or therapy.Overall, Selfgazer offers a user-friendly interface that enables individuals to engage in self-reflection through the lens of tarot symbolism. It aims to provide an entertaining yet meaningful experience for those looking to gain personal insights and explore their inner world.
