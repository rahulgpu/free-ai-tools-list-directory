About Loopy

Loopy is an AI-powered tool by Maxed Labs designed to manage and respond to user reviews on the App Store and Google PlayStore. With the aid of artificial intelligence, Loopy automatically replies to user reviews, providing personalized, insightful, and friendly responses. These features aim to bolster the app's store rating, reduce user churn, and minimize costs. Furthermore, Loopy offers a unique feedback triage system. Each piece of feedback, whether a bug report or a feature request, is automatically sorted and directed to the appropriate department for review, maximizing efficiency and ensuring every user's feedback is appropriately considered. Loopy further offers insightful reports, providing weekly overviews of app performance and feedback, which can be shared with team members and leadership for strategic decision-making. Although it is designed to cater to all user reviews, its prime focus is listening to user requirements and addressing any current issues that customers are facing, in an attempt to enhance overall user satisfaction and retention.
