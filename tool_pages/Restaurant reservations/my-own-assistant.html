About My Own Assistant

My Own Assistant is an AI-integrated tool designed to enhance the restaurant reservations experience. It utilizes various features to provide comprehensive assistance similar to a dedicated personal secretary. The AI operates through a user-friendly Telegram chat interface, where it receives and processes requests for restaurant reservations. Key functions include managing reservations, sending timely reservation alerts, and translating voice messages for convenient and personalized communication. The assistant also ensures accuracy in reservation details through automatic call transcription. Additionally, My Own Assistant is capable of making necessary calls to restaurants to book or cancel reservations on behalf of the user. It aims to provide a stress-free experience by handling the often tedious task of restaurant reservations, allowing users to enjoy their dining plans without hassle. Currently, the tool offers its services in the USA and Canada.
