About Consultan AI

Consultan is an AI-powered mock consulting interview platform designed to assist individuals in preparing for their upcoming consulting interviews. This tool offers a comprehensive range of features to enhance your practice sessions.One key feature is the instant expert feedback provided by an unbiased AI. This allows you to receive immediate feedback on your responses and provides helpful suggestions for improvement. The feedback is designed to simulate the evaluation you would receive from a human expert.What sets Consultan apart is its availability free of charge. This has been made possible thanks to the contributions made by @Tameyer41. It is an open-source platform, and its source code is available on Github, allowing for community collaboration and improvement.Consultan offers a wide selection of interview questions from renowned consulting firms like McKinsey, Bain, BCG, and Deloitte, among others. This ensures that the tool provides relevant and high-quality content to help you practice effectively.By utilizing Consultan, individuals can engage in realistic mock consulting interviews, leveraging the power of AI to receive immediate feedback on their performance. Whether you are a student preparing for a consulting internship or a seasoned professional honing your skills, Consultan is a valuable tool to enhance your preparation for consulting interviews.
