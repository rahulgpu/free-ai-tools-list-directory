About Interviewsby.ai

Interviewsby.ai is an AI-powered tool that helps job seekers prepare for interviews. It offers custom tailored mock interviews with real-time feedback from ChatGPT, a language model that can understand and interpret human language. Users can generate relevant and realistic interview questions by telling the tool about the desired position. The tool's ability to generate questions reduces the risk of users practicing with irrelevant or outdated questions. With interviewsby.ai, users can practice their interview skills in a safe environment and receive immediate feedback on their performance. The feedback is tailored to each user's performance, highlighting areas of strength and areas that need improvement. Providing feedback in real-time gives users the opportunity to make adjustments to their interview style and learn from their mistakes. The tool was made by Jake Rosenthal and Chloe Pappas, two experts in the field of language models and natural language processing. The precise nature of the feedback and the tool's ability to generate realistic questions make it a valuable tool for job seekers who seek to improve their interview skills. Interviewsby.ai is a useful and practical AI tool for job seekers who want to nail the interview and land their dream job.
