About Liftoff

Liftoff is an AI-driven mock interview tool specially designed to aid users in preparing for job interviews. The platform provides a wide selection of questions and solutions, acting as a comprehensive interview simulator. Liftoff utilizes artificial intelligence to create realistic interview scenarios, aiming to enhance the user's interview capabilities and confidence. The platform is fully accessible and offers a community feature. This enables users to connect with each other, facilitating collective learning and exchange of ideas. Their community feature fosters a collaborative environment where users can support each other through their interview preparation journey. As an open-source tool, Liftoff also provides access to its code repository on Github. Users who wish to explore or contribute to its software development can do so via Github platform. A demo is also available for users who are interested to try before fully committing to its use.
