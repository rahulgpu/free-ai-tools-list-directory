About RightJoin

RightJoin is an AI-powered tool that helps data scientists practice for and ace their upcoming interviews. With RightJoin, users can take unlimited customized mock interviews tailored to specific companies they are interviewing with. The AI tool provides instant feedback on each answer given and overall performance in a detailed feedback report. Users can also re-take the interview to build confidence and achieve their interview goals. Additionally, RightJoin allows users to set specific goals such as improving certain skills or storytelling abilities to better prepare for their interviews. To get started, users simply need to create a candidate profile by uploading their resume and selecting a job posting or specific topic to be tested on. RightJoin uses this information to personalize the interview questions to the user's needs. RightJoin is aimed at data scientists in the job market who are looking to improve their interview skills and increase their chances of landing their dream job. The tool also offers a community aspect where users can help each other and provide support throughout the job search process. Overall, RightJoin is a useful and practical tool for data scientists looking to prepare for their interviews and improve their interview skills.
