About Huru

Huru is an Artificial Intelligence (AI)-powered tool designed to help individuals prepare for job interviews. It provides an interview coaching environment where users can practice unlimited mock interviews tailored to specific career positions or job postings. Huru's intuitive interface offers an experience of a real interview, and builds user confidence by providing instant, personalized feedback on answers, including an analysis of content delivery and vocabulary. Apart from that, Huru also features an extensive library of realistic, job-specific questions across diverse industries and roles that ensures comprehensive preparation. Through Huru's AI coach, users can refine their interview skills with actionable advice, ultimately improving their chances of acing the real interview scenario. Huru is not only an app available on iOS and Android platforms but is also accessible via a Google Chrome extension. This extension allows users to instantly generate practice questions from actual job postings on LinkedIn, Indeed, Glassdoor, and other top job boards, giving a realistic feel of potential questions likely to be asked during an interview for a particular job post. Furthermore, Huru's AI provides in-depth feedback on user responses targeting clarity, relevance, communication style, confidence projection, vocabulary and language enhancement. Huru is a useful tool for job seekers aiming to improve their interview skills and enhance their chances of landing their dream job.
