About Interview Buddy AI

Interview Buddy is a voice AI application designed to assist users in honing their interview skills. It tailors practice questions based on the individual's resume and career ambitions, offering a personalized practicing experience. The tool is well-suited for different types of interviews, adapting to the specific needs of applications in fields such as tech, management, or creative industries. It analyzes user responses and provides feedback on areas of improvement, helping users in refining their interview delivery. A unique feature of Interview Buddy includes the ability to customize interviews. Users can upload their resume and job description, and the AI will extract key details to generate the most pertinent questions. Its functionality further includes the allowance for users to review transcripts of their interviews at a later time. Interview Buddy assures user privacy, storing interview responses only for the purpose of later review and not utilizing any user data for AI training or any external purposes. It offers a free plan with limited interview practice sessions along with a paid plan for additional and lengthier interviews for more comprehensive practice.
