About Writtal

Writtal is an AI-powered platform designed to enhance the career success of its users. The tool leverages artificial intelligence to assist in creating impactful resumes, cover letters, and professional legal documents. In addition to its writing capabilities, the tool offers AI-based mock interviews, serving as a innovative resource to bolster your interview skills. This cutting edge tool is geared towards individuals keen on taking their career to the next level. It operates on the premise of optimising career-oriented documents and facilitating competence in interview scenarios. The tool requires JavaScript to function correctly.
