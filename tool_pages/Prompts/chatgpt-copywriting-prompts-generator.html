About ChatGPT Copywriting Prompts Generator

The ChatGPT Prompt Generator is a purpose-built tool that utilizes OpenAI's ChatGPT to generate copywriting content. Encased within an MS Excel Masterfile, the tool comprises four worksheets: Copywriting Prompts Generator, Video Ads' Prompt Generator, Video Ads Prompts Specimen, and Blogposts' Prompts Generator.The Copywriting Prompts Generator helps to easily create persuasive contents by auto-generating prompts based on six user-defined variables, namely: Product/ Service, Target Audience, USP, Pain points, Case Study, and CTA, along with 14 preset copywriting models. Users input these variables and the tool outputs copy prompts that can be input into ChatGPT, which then generates final copies.The Video Ads' Prompt Generator allows for the creation of scripts for video ads, regular reels, and shorts. It requires mere input of details about the storyline, ideas and the product or CTA to generate a prompt. Again, this prompt can be entered into ChatGPT to obtain a picturizable script.The Video Ads Prompts Specimen demonstrates what the generated prompts will look like, and the Blogposts' Prompts Generator aids in producing blogposts. A keyphrase, topic or idea is input and a prompt is generated encompassing attributes ideal for high-quality web content.In essence, the ChatGPT Prompt Generator is a supportive tool for content creators, assisting in generating exhaustive content prompts to feed into the ChatGPT model.
