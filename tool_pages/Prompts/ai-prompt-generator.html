About AI Prompt Generator

The AI Prompt Generator - Notion Template is a tool designed to assist users in creating effective prompts for AI image generation software like Midjourney or DALL-E. It helps users to form detailed, natural-sounding prompts to guide AI software in generating the specific images they desire. The template offers adjustable parameters to suit users' experience levels, ranging from casual to professional. The user-friendly interface eliminates the need to type anything directly into the AI software, enabling easy copying and pasting from Notion to the AI applications. In addition, the tool provides a tutorial available via YouTube, accessible through an embedded link allowing users to see the template in action. The template can be easily duplicated to users' Notion workspace by clicking the 'Duplicate' button found on the top right corner. Further queries and feedback can be addressed to the provided email. Note that JavaScript is required to make use of this product. Lastly, the template has received positive ratings, adding confidence to its usability and effectiveness.
