About Surmount

Surmount AI is a trading software that enables users to trade smarter and manage their investments using data-driven strategies. With Surmount AI, users can easily connect all of their investment accounts, including equities, crypto, and forex, in one place for convenient viewing, management, and automation. The platform allows users to automate their investments by finding and evaluating pre-built investment strategies with genuine track records. Users can access historical data for performance, holdings, and risk level, and seamlessly deploy strategies into their existing accounts with just a few clicks.Additionally, Surmount AI empowers users to turn their investment ideas into potential profit by building genuine automated investing strategies. The platform provides options for both no-code strategies and AI-generated text-to-code strategies, along with a web-based Python IDE for ease of use.Surmount AI aims to democratize access to passive trading by making it available to individuals and institutions alike. By offering a marketplace for users to create and share trading strategies, Surmount AI allows users to contribute to and benefit from a community-driven approach to investing.The platform has garnered praise from industry experts, including former quants and executives, who have commended Surmount AI for its ability to bring the power of hedge funds and family offices to everyday investors, introduce retail trading strategies using AI technology, and provide a transparent and customizable trading experience.Overall, Surmount AI provides modern-day investors with robust tools and resources to make informed investment decisions and optimize their trading strategies.
