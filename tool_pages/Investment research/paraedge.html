About Paraedge

Paraedge is an AI-powered platform that is centered on augmenting investment research procedures. Capitalizing on a blend of AI-driven analysis, fundamental and alternative data, it aims to provide a more efficient and effective way for investors to navigate markets. A distinguishing feature of Paraedge is its usage of natural language tools, tailoring the platform's appeal to individual investors. Users can input a ticker to access a range of features such as information about recent company filings extracted by a chatbot, data from foreign company transcripts, as well as insights into emerging markets garnered from alternative data. Moreover, the platform touts itself to be equivalent to having the expertise of ten investment analysts at one's disposal. In a broader perspective, Paraedge's approach revolves around leveraging live alternative data, aiming to deliver quality insights into equity and commodity markets, thereby facilitating a more data-driven investment methodology.
