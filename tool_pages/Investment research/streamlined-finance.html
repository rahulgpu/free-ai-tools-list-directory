About Streamlined Finance

Streamlined Finance is a modern investment research tool designed to assist individual investors and professionals in making informed investment decisions. The platform is focused on providing pertinent financial information on various investment entities including US Stocks, ETFs, Cryptocurrencies, Bonds, and more. It enables users to discover and analyze stocks, compare their fundamentals, and offers features for identifying under or overvalued stocks, high growth stocks, and dividend stocks. One of its strengths is in data visualization that transforms complex financial information into easy-to-understand charts and tables. The AI-powered Analysis feature summarizes earnings transcripts and distills news articles, enhancing your fundamental analysis efforts. One key aspect is the ability to view multiple charts simultaneously, compile watchlists, and research macroeconomic data. Streamlined Finance also provides options for building custom dashboards to monitor various metrics, trends or specific lists of stocks. Dashboards are organized around specific areas of financial interest such as the Federal Reserve, US Inflation, and Eurodollar, enabling users to access comprehensive overviews. Furthermore, it aids in portfolio management, allowing the users to build uncorrelated portfolios. This tool is aimed at those who want to approach investing with confidence and appreciates data to support decision-making. With a focus on simplifying the research component, Streamlined Finance aims to make the process of discovering, analyzing, comparing, and investing in stocks more efficient and insightful.
