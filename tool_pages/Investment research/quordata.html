About Quordata

Quordata is an AI-powered investment research tool that simplifies the process of making investment decisions by providing real-time, actionable insights from online text. It offers custom dashboards, plain-English explanations, and AI-powered insights that empower financial decision-making. With Quordata, users can analyze text from various sources, including Twitter, Reddit, LinkedIn, and the Wall Street Journal. The tool utilizes proprietary spam, sentiment, and biterm topic models to process aggregated text and generate key metrics. These metrics are presented in robust dashboards, which include an executive summary with AI explanations, closely related topics, and the composition of sources.Quordata has garnered positive feedback from users, who appreciate its ability to boost confidence in trading decisions, provide easily digestible information, and track public sentiment efficiently. Users have the option to choose between the Basic and Pro plans. The Basic plan offers access to real-time metrics on social sentiment for up to 10 companies per month, basic analysis of publicly traded companies, and access to a growing community of like-minded investors. The Pro plan, available for a monthly fee, provides unlimited access to real-time metrics on social sentiment for all publicly traded companies, advanced AI-driven analysis, actionable insights, and priority access to new features and updates.Overall, Quordata aims to provide investors of all levels with the best AI-powered investment research tools, helping them streamline their research process and make better-informed investment decisions.
