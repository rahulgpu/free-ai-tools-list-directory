About Siftree

Siftree is a tool designed to streamline community management for social media managers, content creators, and others managing various audiences across multiple platforms. The tool's principal function is to 'sift through the noise' and identify key conversations, topics, and insights across all platforms. Siftree employs artificial intelligence to consolidate all conversations to a single location, mitigating the need to log into multiple applications and thus offering a more integrated approach to community management. Beyond conversation consolidation, Siftree also uses its AI capabilities to quickly identify unanswered questions, categorize comments based on their sentiment, and summarize your community's main discussion topics. Such features aid in managing the influx of comments and messages, enabling users to provide timely support for their community members and promptly address questions or complaints. With Siftree, users may also gauge their community's sentiment by exploring positive, neutral, and negative conversations. This tool is primarily designed for and well-suited to social media managers, community managers, and content creators.
