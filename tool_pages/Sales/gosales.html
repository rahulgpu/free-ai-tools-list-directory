About GoSales

GoSales is an AI-based tool designed to enhance sales and customer engagement by leveraging WhatsApp automation. The tool aims to make customer interactions conversational and interactive, improving sales operations and productivity without a considerable workforce. GoSales provides the experience of real-time conversations, instantly addressing customer queries, turning each interaction potentially into a sale. The tool trains the WhatsApp number with all the vital business data for customer interactions. This automation tool avoids spamming customers with long messages, instead encouraging conversation-like interactions.GoSales can be beneficial in increasing conversions by broadcasting the right updates and offers with real-time call-to-actions. It offers an AI chatbot builder that allows users to create and deploy chatbots without requiring any coding. Furthermore, users can automate daily tasks, such as follow-ups and collecting feedback, while the tool efficiently integrates with the existing CRM tools for streamlined workflows.In terms of API integration, GoSales provides integration with a variety of major apps. For instance, it integrates with Google Sheets to enable real-time data reading and writing. For e-commerce businesses, GoSales integrates with WooCommerce to showcase products and enable WhatsApp purchases with sync orders for an optimal customer experience. It also works with Tabby for a smooth checkout experience and seamlessly connects with Zapier for further integration possibilities.
