About InboxPlus

InboxPlus is an AI-powered tool designed to automate multi-touch follow-ups, enhancing communication and sales conversion strategies for businesses. This tool provides a mechanism for personalized automated communication that helps streamline the sales cycle. With targeted communication sequences, users can tailor their follow-ups based on customer interactions and behavior insights. InboxPlus comes with multi-touch sequences that ensure messages reach your audience via their preferred channels, thus enhancing the response rates.In an effort to boost productivity, InboxPlus automates routine tasks, thereby freeing up time for other high-impact activities. An additional feature is providing consistency in brand voice across all communication platforms through the use of AI-crafted templates and sequences. InboxPlus also provides insight into campaign performance through detailed analytics. The tool integrates seamlessly with Gmail through a Chrome extension, reducing the need to juggle multiple applications. It also supports the creation of email templates using AI, allowing for efficient crafting of personalized emails that captivate and convert audiences. Workflow is further simplified with the provisioning of team collaboration options and single-click application of AI-crafted templates in Gmail. InboxPlus also allows for the setting up of multi-touch sequences and management of all communication activities in a single sequence. Daily reminders, task tracking, and an overview of tasks ensure users stay on track with their communication efforts. Other notable features include email tracking, AI Email Templates, task reminders, template management, team management, and contact management. Though primarily a tool for sales organizations, InboxPlus has found utility across various domains including teams focused on customer service and interaction.
