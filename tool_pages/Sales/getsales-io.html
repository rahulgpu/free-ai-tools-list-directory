About GetSales.io

GetSales.io is an AI-powered Sales Engagement Platform that employs an Account-Based Approach for B2B sales automation. It encompasses a range of features including LinkedIn Outreach and Cloud Automation. The platform allows for targeted, personalized 1-to-1 interactions with potential clients and provides several functionalities for seamless sales process management. GetSales.io features built-in CRM functionality for organizing teamwork and managing lead statuses, engagement insights, notifications, and implementing an account-based marketing approach. It also introduces an omni-channel approach for sales with tools for email automation, LinkedIn automation, and even SMS/WA automation. Moreover, it equips businesses with no-code workflows builder to create omni-channel campaigns and workflows with added data security. The platform additionally includes robust analytics with features for reports, dashboards, A/B testing, reply analysis, and sales developmental representative performance. It comes with an AI-powered dashboard for in-depth channel analytics, which aids in improving sales processes through insights into conversion rates, engagement rates, lead listings, and activity monitoring. GetSales.io offers a high level of personalization by enabling the use of segmented audience data for Facebook & LinkedIn advertisements and also comprises tools for auto-responding on platforms like LinkedIn, emails, and SMS/Whatsapp. Furthermore, it also allows businesses to import/export data in CSV/Excel format and from platforms like Sales Navigator, Zoominfo, and UpLead. Lastly, GetSales.io includes a feature for tracking visitors and utilizing chat routing for more effective engagement.
