About DeckBird

DeckBird is a presentation hosting and sharing tool that utilizes AI to transform everyday presentations into digital sales funnels. With its enhanced AI capabilities, users can curate and share presentations, increasing sales leads by 100%. The tool offers features such as AI voice and video curating, allowing users to add a personal touch to their presentations. It provides high-quality videos and audios, with options to choose from various languages and avatar voices. Collaboration is made easy, as team members can design and edit presentations together.DeckBird also enables users to embed presentations in emails, increasing click rates and avoiding bounce backs from email servers. It supports email marketing services like MailChimp and provides automatic tracking of viewing statistics.Furthermore, the tool allows users to share presentations on social networks, boosting reach and audience engagement. It has built-in Search Engine Optimization (SEO) features to drive more traffic to presentations. Users can easily share presentations on platforms like LinkedIn, Twitter, Facebook, WhatsApp, WeChat, and embed them on websites. The tool automatically pushes presentations to the Google search engine and optimizes them for indexing.DeckBird includes an on-deck calendar for scheduling meetings, ensuring efficient communication with the sales team and audience. It supports Google and Microsoft calendars, as well as platforms like Zoom, Google Meet, and Microsoft Teams. Meetings can be easily scheduled by the audience while they are on deck, and automatic assignment to sales team members, email confirmations, and reminders are provided.With DeckBird, presentations are always up-to-date as they can be updated in real-time, slide by slide, enabling users to share a single link that provides the latest version. This eliminates the need to send around the entire presentation separately.
