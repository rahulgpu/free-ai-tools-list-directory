About AutoBiz

AutoBiz is an AI-powered sales assistant designed specifically for e-commerce businesses. It offers a range of features to help boost sales and improve customer experience. These include smart sales consulting and effective sales closing, which provide natural and engaging sales suggestions based on customer input. This can lead to increased sales conversion rates and a better overall customer experience.AutoBiz also provides automated order creation, streamlining the ordering process and improving customer satisfaction. The AI sales assistant is optimized to provide automated customer care, helping businesses retain customers and increase sales.With AutoBiz, businesses can handle scalable customer volumes, making it easy to manage large numbers of orders. The tool also offers omnichannel support, allowing businesses to connect with multiple sales channels to further increase sales.In addition, AutoBiz enables seamless human-AI collaboration, ensuring that customers receive the best possible service. There are also upcoming features, such as content creator and marketing tools, as well as actionable insights through built-in analytics.AutoBiz is built using OpenAI's GPT technology, which enables it to support over 120 languages and dialects. The tool offers customization options, allowing businesses to tailor customer interactions to their specific needs.AutoBiz is suitable for businesses of all sizes, from small and medium enterprises to larger corporations. It provides a user-friendly interface that simplifies the setup and configuration process, making it accessible to users without technical expertise.
