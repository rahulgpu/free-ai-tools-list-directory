About AskToSell

AskToSell is an AI-powered sales automation tool designed to expedite business growth by automating the sales process across all deal sizes. The tool incorporates AI Sales Agents, CRM, and automatic responses until a deal is finalised, aiming to reduce costs, streamline teams, and increase customer satisfaction. It works by automating key tasks in the sales cycle including high-quality outreach and lead qualification, objection handling, scheduling, sending proposals, setting reminders, and CRM entries. With a focus on both outbound and inbound tasks, AskToSell enables a companys sales team to free their time for strategic activities such as supervising AI Sales Agents or pursuing high-value conversations. To use AskToSell, businesses define the product they want to sell, upload leads, assign autonomous agents, launch campaigns, and monitor their growth in revenue. AskToSell also features approval workflows keeping you in control of important actions. The tool can be set up in a matter of minutes, offering a fast and efficient solution to sales automation. Designed to be scalable, businesses can deploy more agents quickly depending on their needs.
