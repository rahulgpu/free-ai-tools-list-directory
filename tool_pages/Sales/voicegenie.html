About VoiceGenie

VoiceGenie is a generative AI-powered sales automation tool which uses voice-based technology. It employs AI voice bots to automate the sales processes, designed to assist both startups and enterprises. It aims to provide solutions to a leaky, low-converting, and expensive sales funnel by establishing brand awareness, educating the buyers, and creating personalized discovery experiences. The AI voice bot in charge of this process has been programmed to mimic human interaction, giving it the ability to understand and respond to buyers empathetically. It offers tools for lead qualification by determining a buyer's likelihood to buy, their suitability, and readiness. It also helps in nurturing leads with continuous engagement campaigns. As part of the bottom funnel activities, VoiceGenie simplifies processes such as appointment booking and demo scheduling while also identifying cross-selling and upselling opportunities during customer interactions. Once the tool is set up, the AI voice assistant can provide real-time intelligence from customer conversations and yields valuable insights for informed business decisions. However, the most notable feature is its capability to work tirelessly, consistently reaching out to nurture leads, schedule appointments, or provide updates, thus eliminating funnel leakages.
