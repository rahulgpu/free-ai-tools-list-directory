About Cresta

Cresta AI is an enterprise-grade Artificial Intelligence (AI) platform built specifically for contact centers. This intelligent tool harnesses the power of generative AI to enhance customer conversations and maximize their value. It offers live coaching, provides insights across customer calls, and has a self-service feature, equipping teams to improve the effectiveness of their customer interactions. Cresta AI is designed to facilitate a wide range of use cases including sales, customer care, retention, and collections, making it useful across various industries such as airlines, automotive, finance, insurance, retail, telecommunications, and travel & hospitality. Cresta AI is comprised of multiple components. There's the 'Conversation Intelligence' which uncovers the real drivers of contact center performance, 'Agent Assist' that uses real-time AI for precise and impactful guidance, and 'Virtual Agent' for automating conversations. Additionally, Cresta AI offers advanced contextual understanding by training its state-of-the-art models on user-specific data. With its scalable architecture, Cresta AI ensures ultra low-latency transcription and inference at the speed of conversations, making it suitable for real-time guidance. The solution also provides a no-code command center, enabling non-technical leaders to train, test, and deploy AI models. Overall, Cresta AI aims to reinvent contact centers by pinpointing performance drivers, reducing costs through targeted automation, and empowering employees to work smarter and faster.
