About Tellhop

Tellhop is an AI tool designed to streamline construction management through Procore. The tool leverages AI technology to allow users to handle various tasks hands-free during their commute, which enhances productivity by eliminating manual data entry tasks. With Tellhop, users can dictate and sync daily reports, timecards, and follow-up emails directly to Procore while on the move. In addition, the tool can send text message reminders and initiate email or phone follow-ups with contacts as directed by the user. On-site data input is also facilitated, even in low or no connectivity conditions, as the tool can sync updates once back online. Tellhop also assists with task management by setting up reminders, keeping records accurate and organized, and automating certain tasks such as assignment of cost codes. Furthermore, users can get started quickly with minimal setup required and there is the ability to text instructions directly to the AI. Updates and improvements are continually incorporated to expand the tool's capabilities.
