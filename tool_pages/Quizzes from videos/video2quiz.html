About Video2Quiz

Video2Quiz is an AI tool that allows users to create a quiz or test based on any video in a matter of seconds. It is designed to verify and validate human knowledge in a professional and simplified manner. The tool helps to separate information from knowledge, which is essential for skills development and proficiency. Users can easily upload their videos to the platform and create quizzes or tests based on the content of the video. Video2Quiz is a product of K3, an AI service provider that helps companies, teams, and enterprises to transition to the era of AI and increase profits by 10x. The K3 brand is a subsidiary of Klick Data, a public company with offices in Stockholm and Cairo. The platform offers a free trial, and users can change the language of the site to English (United Kingdom), Svenska or Arabic (اللغة العربية). The company provides contact details on their site for customer support, including email and WhatsApp support. In summary, Video2Quiz is a tool that simplifies the process of creating quizzes and tests from video content, with the aim of validating human knowledge for skills development and professionalism.
