About Retool

Retool AI is a tool that allows users to quickly integrate artificial intelligence (AI) into their applications and workflows. It offers pre-built blocks that enable users to incorporate AI functionalities into their projects. With Retool AI, users can easily connect their business data securely and create custom AI tools for their businesses in a matter of minutes. The tool provides the flexibility to use any AI model and offers support for various AI actions, including those provided by OpenAI, Anthropic, and Azure. Users can integrate these actions into their workflows, automate manual tasks, and build custom AI workflows by chaining together business logic, data connectors, and dynamic prompts. Retool AI emphasizes data security, offering self-hosted deployments, permissions, audit trails, single sign-on (SSO), and version control features. Users have complete control over how data is shared with AI tools and can ensure that it remains secure. Additionally, Retool AI enables users to build AI-powered search capabilities for their business data using its managed vector store. This feature allows users to store and index their business data into vector embeddings for efficient AI retrieval. Users can also build contextual AI bots by uploading content from websites, documents, or SaaS tools and running workflows to extract text from various sources. The tool also provides AI-enhanced developer tools to expedite the process of building custom business software. Users can generate code and user interfaces, and leverage over 100 pre-built components to create bespoke and intuitive experiences. Overall, Retool AI empowers users to integrate AI into their applications and workflows effectively, saving time and resources while enhancing business processes.
