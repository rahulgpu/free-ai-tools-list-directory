About Facial Assessment Tool

QOVES Facial Assessment Tool is an AI-powered tool that uses facial recognition technology to identify and analyze facial features. It uses over 35,000 images and 112 parameters to accurately assess the skin type and cosmetic concerns of an individual. It can also predict age, race, and sex with a high degree of accuracy.Once the image is uploaded, the tool will scan it and provide a report which includes a summary of the potential cosmetic concerns and possible solutions. It will also provide recommendations on skincare and beauty products, as well as articles related to the identified cosmetic concerns. With this tool, users can gain insight into their skin type and find the best solutions to their cosmetic concerns.
