About Redro

Redro is an AI-based tool specifically designed for the fashion industry. It is intended to revolutionize the way fashion is worn and perceived, providing users with a unique experience of transforming photographs into tangible outfits. This technologically advanced tool provides a platform for creating original outfits using AI, enabling users to draw inspiration from a variety of online sources. These sources could be ranging from social media platforms featuring diverse styles to high-fashion blogs depicting the latest trends. What sets Redro apart is its 'Selfie Mode'. This feature uses AI technology to scan user's current outfit and provide insights into style enhancements and modifications to elevate their style quotient. It effectively transforms any regular wardrobe into a sophisticated collection of outfits suitable for different occasions. Another compelling feature of Redro is its AI-powered window shopping experience. In essence, Redro is fashion-enhancing software that not only uses AI to shape the future of fashion but also allows users to turn their fashion visions into reality.
