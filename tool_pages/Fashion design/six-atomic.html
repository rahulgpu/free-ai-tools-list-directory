About Six Atomic

Six Atomic is an AI-driven tool providing innovative solutions to enhance the apparel supply chain. The platform offers various AI tools to facilitate real-time fashion design, grading, and 3D simulation of patterns. The tool is geared towards accelerating the product-to-market timeframe by assuring apparel goods can be created, graded, and made ready for delivery within hours of being sold. Six Atomic focuses on personalization, enabling the creation of unique garments for individuals rather than market segments. Furthermore, it supports various business models, including an on-demand manufacturing approach that minimizes inventory risk. Six Atomic features modules like Synthesis 1.0, which harnesses AI for automated pattern grading and modular design libraries, enabling designs to be created in minutes. Another module, INFERENCE, functions as a body measurement predictor to ensure a perfect fit for customers. Custom AI solutions are also offered for enterprise brands to further leverage AI in accelerating their production and SKU launches. Six Atomic's platform is designed for both small brands and large enterprise operations.
