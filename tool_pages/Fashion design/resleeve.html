About Resleeve

Resleeve: Materializing The World's Fashion Dreams. We offer 2 unparalleled design and marketing solutions: 1. Generates infinite one-of-a-kind fashion designs. Resleeve offers this through a range of features. Such as new designs, design variations, sketch-to-product, moodboard-to-product and retouching designs. 2. AI-crafted photoshoots of a garment based on 1 input image. Allowing users to generate photoshoots and showcase their products in real-life scenarios without having to hire models or use any props. The tool is suitable for fashion designers within teams, freelancers, owner-led businesses and fashion enthusiasts that need to create great designs and market them quickly and efficiently. Resleeve is designed to transform the design process, enabling users to create appealing designs in a matter of seconds, explore endless design concepts and possibilities and showcase them for the world to see.
