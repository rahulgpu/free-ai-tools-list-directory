About ChatMe.CPA

ChatMe.CPA is an artificial intelligence-enabled chatbot specifically developed for Certified Public Accountants (CPAs). It's a tool built and trained by accountants to aid in various tax and finance situations. The chatbot is trained to provide CPA advice and help users study for the CPA exam, covering parts like REG, FAR, BEC, and AUD. It's designed to answer queries just like an accountant, offering personalized responses that go beyond generic AI responses. The chatbot can research Dutch GAAP, US GAAP, and IFRS. It can assist in making comparisons between IFRS, Dutch GAAP, US GAAP, UK GAAP, German GAAP, and others. It's also capable of instantly constructing user-requested formulas. This tool works in popular messaging apps including Instagram, Telegram, Messenger, and WhatsApp. It offers features including ChatGPT integration, voice-to-text functionality, 24/7 uptime, memory retention, no usage limits, with add-ons like PDF-reader and image-reader. Custom integration for platforms such as Slack, Teams, WhatsApp, and SMS is also possible.
