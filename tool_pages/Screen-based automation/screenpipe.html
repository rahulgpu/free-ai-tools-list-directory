About ScreenPipe

Screen is an AI tool designed to convert visual information on your screen into actionable tasks. It leverages artificial intelligence to transform your screen's content into custom actions on the web according to your specific instructions. What sets the tool apart is its operational transparency and collaborability being open-source. Developers and enthusiasts who want to contribute to or alter its functionality can freely access the source code. Given the tool's linkage with your screen, privacy compliance and terms of usage are strictly adhered to. With Screen, users are allowed the flexibility to turn visual screen data into meaningful and custom actions on the web.
