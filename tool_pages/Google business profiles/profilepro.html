About ProfilePro

ProfilePro is a free Chrome Extension designed to optimize Google Business Profiles. It uses AI technology to automate Google Business posts, generate optimized review responses, and suggest the most SEO-optimized business description for the user's business. It also provides SEO-optimized service and product descriptions to help businesses get found by potential customers on Google Maps. With ProfilePro, businesses can manage their Google Business Profiles more effectively and improve their local search results. The tool includes a white glove onboarding call with a local SEO expert, and it supports multiple languages, including English, French, Spanish, Portuguese, Urdu, Hindi, Tagalog, Dutch, Korean, and German.
