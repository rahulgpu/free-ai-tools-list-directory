About Velo.ai

Copilot is an AI-powered bike light and camera designed to enhance safety measures for cyclists. It actively monitors the road behind the rider; using artificial intelligence to discern when a vehicle safely negotiates past a cyclist or to highlight potentially aggressive or distracted drivers that are approaching unsafely. Copilot communicates its findings through audible alerts, notifying the cyclist of any vehicle approaching or overtaking. Coupled with a smartphone, Copilot can provide users with visual alerts. To further alert approaching drivers, it alters its light patterns and colors to draw attention to the cyclist, acting preemptively to prevent potential accidents. Beyond its lighting and alert capabilities, Copilot records data and video for each ride a cyclist takes. The technology also incorporates features like predictive risk estimation to monitor driver behavior, in a bid to help cyclists ride more intelligently and avoid potential crashes. The overall mission is to give a cyclist broader situational awareness, significantly boosting their confidence and safety on the roads.
