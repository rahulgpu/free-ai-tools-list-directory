About Blueish

Blueish is an AI-powered platform that promotes team building and employee engagement through humor-based newsletters. The platform is designed to serve remote teams, friends, and communities by delivering personalized content that focuses on humor and bonding. Blueish learns the unique traits of each team, such as humor style, interests, inside jokes, and culture. Based on these insights, it creates weekly newsletters that feature team-centric stories, memes, and humor. The newsletters are designed to mimic a workplace version of Hollywood, with an intentional funny twist. By making humor a regular part of the team's week, Blueish aims to increase engagement, productivity, and job satisfaction. Blueish further offers flexibility with its subscription plans, allowing users to choose from Basic, Team, and Pro plans, each offering additional features like advanced analytics and daily prompts for further personalization. The platform emphasizes a user-friendly experience, with simple setup processes and no need for lengthy onboarding or content creation. By supporting casual communication, Blueish aims to improve corporate culture by fostering stronger relationships and reducing the risk of staff turnover.
