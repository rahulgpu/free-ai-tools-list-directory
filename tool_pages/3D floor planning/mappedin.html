About Mappedin

Mappedin Maker is a user-friendly, AI-assisted indoor mapping tool. It enables users to quickly and easily create detailed, accurate maps of indoor locations such as schools, malls, offices, and other venues, without requiring prior mapping experience. With the help of AI technology, users can upload a floor plan of the venue and the tool then converts this into a robust digital map. Alternatively, users can create a map from scratch with the iOS Maker App. The tool includes features such as AutoMap for automatic placement of objects like doors, walls, and windows, and a 3D interactive map viewer. Users also have the ability to edit their maps, add connections, move points of interest, and export the map in various formats or publish it for wayfinding purposes. One of Mappedin Maker's unique offerings is its focus on safety. It allows for easy location of key safety elements like emergency exits and fire extinguishers, ensuring up-to-date maps for emergency response departments. Mappedin Maker, as part of the broader Mappedin product range, supports the digitization of indoor spaces.
