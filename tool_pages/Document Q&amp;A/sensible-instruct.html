About Sensible Instruct

Sensible is an intelligent document-processing tool catering especially for developers requiring high-volume data extraction from multiple document types. It has capabilities to parse data from a variety of document formats such as resumes, invoices, contracts, bank statements, utility bills, and more. By leveraging advanced parsing techniques, Sensible allows for seamless integration into existing systems through comprehensive APIs and developer toolkits. The platform offers effortless automation with state-of-the-art methods including LLM parsing and visual layout-based rules. The aim is to handle the complexities of data extraction while allowing developers to focus on crafting excellent products and features. Sensible also caters to diverse industrial requirements, ranging from financial services, insurance to property management by maintaining its competence to accurately extract structured data from relevant document types. Additionally, the platform is designed to integrate seamlessly with applications using SDKs, APIs, and webhooks, and is capable of scalable operations in accordance with fluctuating document-processing needs.
