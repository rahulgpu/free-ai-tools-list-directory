About Tomate.ai

ToMate is an AI tool designed to help businesses answer client messages more efficiently. It empowers users to respond to client inquiries using artificial intelligence that has been trained on their own messages, files (such as PDFs, Excels, and Words), and websites. With ToMate, users can train their AI response system effortlessly by uploading documents such as FAQs and information about their services. The tool also provides a Chrome extension that allows users to interact with popular platforms like Gmail, Outlook, and WhatsApp.One of the key advantages of ToMate is its ability to generate contextually relevant and engaging responses based on AI-driven context detection and response building. By harnessing the power of AI, ToMate aims to save time and ensure that every email sent is tailored to the specific context and topic.To address privacy concerns, ToMate emphasizes GDPR compliance and offers a PrivacyPurge IQ feature that proactively erases personal data in emails, reinforcing trust and security.The tool can be seamlessly integrated with platforms like Gmail, Outlook, and WhatsApp, allowing users to generate high-quality responses on any platform. Additionally, ToMate provides a manager's admin space for team management, with features like monitoring platform usage, creating teams, and assigning roles.ToMate offers different pricing plans based on users' communication needs, with a free plan available for up to 100 responses per month. Advanced plans provide higher response limits, larger storage capacities, and support for more users and documents. Customized plans are also available upon request.
