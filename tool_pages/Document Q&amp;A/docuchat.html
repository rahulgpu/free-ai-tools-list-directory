About Docuchat

DocuChat is an AI Chatbot tool primarily designed to expedite information retrieval by delivering instant, accurate responses. The tool allows users to train chatbots using their documents and websites. The trained chatbots can then be seamlessly embedded into user sites to continuously deliver responses round-the-clock. DocuChat incorporates smart citations for verified answers, is customization-focused for alignment with the user's brand identity and offers options for private or public sharing. It facilitates in-depth analytics tracking for performance optimization, and promotes team collaboration with role-based permissions and shared knowledge base. The tool also supports white labeling for a consistent brand experience. DocuChat utilizes current AI models and has multilingual capabilities. It places a strong emphasis on data privacy and security, ensuring user control over data, non-usage of user data for AI training and compliance with GDPR and CCPA regulations. The tool has also opted out of OpenAIs data sharing program.
