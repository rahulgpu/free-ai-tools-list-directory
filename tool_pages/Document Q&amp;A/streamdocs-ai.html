About StreamDocs.ai

StreamDocs.ai is an AI tool designed for smart conversations with PDFs to gain quick results, better-informed decisions, and key insights. Its intent is to simplify the process of handling documents. Core functionalities include instantly locating the required information, even within extensive user manuals or company policies. Complex legal documents, contracts are translated into plain English to provide clarity and ease decision-making. A remarkable aspect of this tool is its capacity to extract critical points and summaries from diverse sources such as long reports, research papers, and government documents, using a single query. Users can hence manage and understand their documents more effectively. StreamDocs.ai is skilled in advanced OCR and advanced text extraction technology, ensuring best-in-class accuracy and efficiency. The tool is designed to confidently solve challenging PDF problems, having 30 years of industry expertise backing it. Packages ranging from free to paid monthly and annual plans are available, allowing scalability based on user requirements.
