About ScholarTurbo

ScholarTurbo is an AI tool that allows users to use ChatGPT to chat with PDFs. Users can upload any PDF and start asking questions about it. The tool supports GPT-4 for paid users and GPT-3.5 for free users. ScholarTurbo values privacy and uses cookies to enhance browsing experience and analyze traffic. The tool categorized cookies into Necessary, Functional, Analytics, Performance, Advertisement, and Uncategorized. Necessary cookies are essential for enabling basic functionalities while Functional cookies serve to perform specific functionalities such as sharing website content on social media. Analytical cookies help understand how visitors interact with the website and provide information on metrics such as the number of visitors, bounce rate, and the traffic source. Performance cookies help in analyzing the key performance indexes of the website while Advertisement cookies provide visitors with customized ads based on previously visited pages and analyze the effectiveness of advertisements. Finally, Uncategorised cookies are those being analyzed for classification. Users of ScholarTurbo can also reach out for support, feature requests or bug support through the platform. Overall, ScholarTurbo is a useful AI tool for those looking to chat with PDFs using ChatGPT and offers various functionalities to enhance user experience.
