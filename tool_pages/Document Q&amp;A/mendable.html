About Mendable

Mendable is a platform designed to help businesses build AI chat applications. Its fundamental offering includes training a secure AI on technical resources to provide insightful answers for customer and employee questions, aiming to offload the routine query resolution from the team. Widely embraced by businesses from startups to Fortune 500 companies, Mendable is designed to be flexibly deployed as a knowledgeable technical AI helper anywhere. It accelerates adoption by speeding up onboarding with an AI assistant within the app.Mendable embraces an enterprise-grade security model, complies with SOC 2 Type II standards, and supports multiple single sign-on mechanisms including SAML 2.0, OpenID Connect, and OAuth 2.0. The platform uses role-based access control (RBAC) to ensure proper access provision and offers customizable secure data connectors to various platforms like Google Drive, Salesforce, Zendesk, and more.Adopting a user-friendly approach, Mendable allows users to easily teach the model based on specific needs and implement corrections to answers generated by the model. It also offers continuous training by allowing for corrections to incorrect responses, thereby keeping chat applications always updated.The solution can be integrated with a variety of components ranging from search bars to chat bubbles to full command-line interfaces built on its API. Mendable exhibits great deployment flexibility, empowering users to easily deploy internally or externally, and provides the ability to send and query data from anywhere.
