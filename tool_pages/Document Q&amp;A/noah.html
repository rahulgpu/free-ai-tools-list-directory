About Noah

Noah is an AI work assistant designed to streamline work processes and enhance productivity. It can be integrated with popular tools such as Google Drive and Notion, allowing users to interact with their files and content more efficiently. With Noah, users can ask questions, receive answers, summarize content, and even draft emails. A standout feature of Noah is its enterprise-ready security and compliance measures. Tavrn, the company behind Noah, prioritizes data protection by implementing best industry practices. They use SOC 2 compliant data centers based in the US and ensure that all data is encrypted both at rest and in transit. Notably, user data is not utilized to train AI models, preserving privacy and confidentiality. Noah offers a free trial with certain limitations, including a lifetime limit on messages and restrictions on the number of Google Drive files and Notion pages that can be accessed. For users seeking fuller functionality, a subscription plan called Noah Plus is available for $20/month. This plan offers unlimited daily messages, a higher limit on Google Drive files and Notion pages, and unlimited synchronization for each integrated tool. Priority support is also provided. For companies of all sizes, an enterprise plan is offered, featuring additional benefits such as integration with Slack and Microsoft Teams, as well as Zendesk, HubSpot, Stripe, and Salesforce. Custom workflows tailored to team needs, multiple seats, and live support are also included. Pricing details for this plan can be obtained by contacting the company directly. Overall, Noah offers a reliable AI work assistant solution that enhances productivity and safeguards data while providing seamless integration with various tools.
