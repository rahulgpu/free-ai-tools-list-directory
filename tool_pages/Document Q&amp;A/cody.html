About Cody

Cody is an artificial intelligence (AI) tool that operates like an employee trained on a custom-created business knowledge base. Its primary functionalities include supporting a team, providing instantaneous answers to business queries, troubleshooting problems, and generating innovative ideas. Cody can be trained specifically for a business using documents, manuals, or by crawling a website which then can be used to provide precise responses.Cody's functionality is based on securely accessing data from various formats like PowerPoints and PDFs. It generates instant, context-rich responses and sources its answers from the information previously integrated. Businesses can utilize this tool in myriad ways, such as swiftly completing tasks such as content creation, translation, or crafting professional emails. Moreover, Cody proves useful in problem-solving, helping teams troubleshoot issues and exploit the full potential of their digital tools. It also serves as a creativity facilitator, providing insights and unique perspectives based on previous conversations and contextual history.Furthermore, Cody is easily adaptable and can operate in various languages, making it versatile for international businesses or diverse linguistic environments. It also provides functionalities like the generation of specialized responses based on specific documents, and keeping track of all interactions through the interface, API, or widgets. These capabilities are essential for businesses looking to streamline their processes, optimize productivity, and enjoy more strategic decision-making.
