About IngestAI

IngestAI is an AI-powered tool that enables users to create chatbots on popular messaging apps such as Slack, Discord, and Telegram. It allows users to upload and store their knowledge base in various formats such as Markdown, TXT, Word, Excel, and PowerPoint. The tool can then generate precise AI/Chatbot-generated answers for questions about relevant materials. IngestAI also provides a community platform for users to connect and share their experiences with the tool. Additionally, the platform offers an educational program, tutorial materials, and product/service descriptions to help users better understand the tool and its capabilities. IngestAI is free to use and provides a convenient way to quickly and easily create chatbots on popular messaging apps.
