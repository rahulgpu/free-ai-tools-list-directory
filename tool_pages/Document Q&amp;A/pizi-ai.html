About Pizi AI

Pizi AI is an advanced tool designed to digitize, analyze, and simplify complex documents. With a focus on multilingual support, Pizi can work with numerous file formats in over 100 languages. The fundamental feature of this tool is its ability to understand and summarize long documents, make translations, and solve mathematical problems. Pizi AI delivers nuanced document analysis by processing intricate structures and minimizes AI 'hallucinations' to ensure coherent and factual information extraction. Alongside, its OCR technology helps unlock text from scanned PDFs and images, making this tool ideal for working with diverse file sources. Pizi AI is also equipped with a unique formula recognition feature which interprets mathematical formulas, thereby simplifying the process of mathematical analysis. Additionally, Pizi provides a high level of privacy by offering users the option to delete their encrypted files at any time. This tool can be beneficial for various fields, including legal practice and education. Lawyers could leverage Pizi AI for efficient document review, case analysis, and legal research, while teachers and students could use it for preparing lessons and solving complex mathematical problems respectively.
