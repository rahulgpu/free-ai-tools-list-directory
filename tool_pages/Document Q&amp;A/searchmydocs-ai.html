About SearchMyDocs.ai

SearchMyDocs.ai is an AI-powered tool that turns your private document library into an intelligent searchable entity. It aims to provide a more efficient way of finding specific pieces of information from extensive and varied document collections. Users can upload files in multiple formats including PDF, Markdown, Word, Excel, Powerpoint, and HTML, as well as several image formats, and then search, chat or ask questions about the content of these documents. The searching process uses deep learning models and Optical Character Recognition (OCR) capabilities to extract text, key-value pairs and tables from the documents. This allows users to not only search the documents but also ask complex, nuanced questions and get specific, contextually accurate answers instantly, thus streamlining the process of information retrieval. The tool also places a high priority on user privacy and data security, with customer data encrypted at rest and in transit. Role-Based Access Control (RBAC) ensures that the documents and resources are accessible only to the authorized user.
