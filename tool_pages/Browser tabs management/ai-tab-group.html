About AI Tab Group

AI Tab Group is a browser extension tool designed to organize and categorize browser tabs automatically for enhanced user convenience and productivity. Leveraging the capabilities of AI and specifically the OpenAI's API, it allows users to maintain an organized and clean browser environment. This tool is compatible with popular browsers such as Chrome and Edge.The primary feature of AI Tab Group is the automatic categorization of tabs. Users activate this by clicking on the extension icon and selecting the 'Options', where they would then enter the OpenAI key. The tool will then group tabs based on similarity or relation, making it more convenient to navigate through multiple tabs.Further features are available with the transition from the Free Plan to a Monthly or Annual Plan. These include the ability to view hierarchical tabs on a single page and the option to save groups in markdown format or as bookmarks.Additional features are under development, offering the potential for increased functionality and user convenience in future updates.Despite its vast capabilities, user privacy is a priority for AI Tab Group. The tool follows established privacy guidelines, and users are encouraged to review their privacy policy to better understand data usage.AI Tab Group is recommended for anyone seeking to increase their browsing efficiency, particularly those who frequently work with numerous tabs open simultaneously.
