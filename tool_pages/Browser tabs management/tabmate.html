About TabMate

TabMate is an AI-powered browser extension that presents a robust solution for efficient tab management. Its primary use case is to solve the common challenge of handling excessive active browser tabs. The tool incorporates an experimental AI technology that categorizes and organizes tabs automatically as the user continues to browse. Users have the flexibility to further declutter and reorganize their browser environment. Moreover, TabMate is designed to work with Google Chrome and most Chromium-based browsers, including Edge, Brave, Opera, Vivaldi, among others on both macOS and Windows platforms. It's loaded with various features to enhance user experience and productivity. These consist of customizable groups, the ability to pin tabs and groups, keyboard shortcuts, dark and black mode, and group tabs by date and domain. Furthermore, it also provides options for labelling tabs with text and color identification. While oriented toward AI automation, it offers substantial control for customization, giving user complete control over their browsing space.
