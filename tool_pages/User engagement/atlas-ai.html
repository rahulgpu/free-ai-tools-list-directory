About Atlas AI

Atlas AI User Guides are advanced conversational AI companions specifically designed to increase user engagement on websites. The suite offers three AI assistants with varying levels of interactivity and capability: Atlas Assistant, Atlas Copilot, and Atlas Concierge. Atlas Assistant is a basic AI conversational aid capable of answering questions and providing guided dialogues about your product or service. Atlas Copilot deepens the interaction by building user profiles to offer tailored recommendations and reminders, while also aiding with complex tasks. The most advanced, Atlas Concierge, carries out customer service queries, schedules appointments, makes purchases, and more. Integration of Atlas assistants is facilitated through three available interfaces: an embeddable chat widget, an app sidebar, or a full-page retrieval app. These platforms ensure seamless integration with your existing interface and offer varying degrees of interaction based on the complexity of the tasks and level of engagement required. Atlas AI User Guides emphasize personalization by tailoring conversations to each user's needs. They also offer consistent 24/7 assistance, cementing their place as invaluable assets in enhancing user experience and interaction.
