About Caelus AI

Caelus AI is an Artificial Intelligence tool designed to help businesses automate the acquisition of new users. The tool works by monitoring keyword mentions of problems and competitors across Twitter and Reddit, subsequently auto-replying to these mentions. The user selects the keywords to trackthese can be pain points, product-relevant terminologies, or competitor namesand Caelus AI keeps running 24/7 in the cloud, keeping tabs on new mentions across the social platforms. Upon identifying these keyword mentions, Caelus AI can auto-reply or notify the user to personally respond. Notably, Caelus AI is designed to learn from existing Twitter and Reddit posts to ensure the AI's responses are in alignment with the user's tone and style. In essence, Caelus AI serves as an always-on, auto-piloted assistant to leverage social media interactions for user growth.
