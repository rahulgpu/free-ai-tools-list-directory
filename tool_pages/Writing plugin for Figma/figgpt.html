About FigGPT

FigGPT is a compact plugin that offers an integration between ChatGPT and Figma. Primarily designed for Figma and Figjam, the tool empowers users to develop and tailor copy within these design applications. FigGPT provides multiple functionalities such as personalized quick actions and bulk editing capabilities to change the style, length, or formatting of text layers with just a single click. Custom prompts can be created in order to carry out user-specified tasks swiftly. In addition, FigGPT has the ability to return language responses in multiple languages, allowing flexibility in copy creation for a global audience. Another key feature is the 'Global prompt suffix' that enables a prompt to be applied to all actions consistently, thus enhancing the consistency of text across designs. It also includes a 'Magic Populate' feature that allows a user to populate designs with sample data at the click of a button, thereby expediting the design process through the integration of ChatGPT's AI capabilities. FigGPT therefore facilitates users to optimize their design processes and enhance the quality of their text layers in Figma and Figjam platforms.
