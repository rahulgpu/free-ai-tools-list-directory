About TeamAI

TeamAI is a collaborative AI platform designed for teams and businesses similar to ChatGPT. It offers a range of features to simplify team collaboration within a ChatGPT-like environment. The platform allows users to utilize models from OpenAI, Anthropic, Google, and Meta without the need for API keys. It enables sharing prompts, collaborating in conversations, and organizing resources. Users can customize their AI assistant's chatbot experience by selecting characters for their assistant and choosing from a variety of models, including ChatGPT and GPT-4.TeamAI supports document-based conversations, allowing users to upload documents and ask their AI assistant questions about them. The platform also offers organizational features such as folders and subfolders to keep chats organized. Users can browse over 150 built-in prompts or create their own prompt library for their team. They can also customize output settings, selecting language, tone, writing style, and format according to their preferences.Additionally, TeamAI provides options for custom branding, allowing users to use their own name, logo, and branding elements. It supports hosting on custom domains and offers the ability to invite team members to collaborate within the workspace. The platform is available in different pricing plans, including a free plan that allows up to 100 users. It also offers premium plans with additional features such as multi-model support, access to specific models like LLaMA and Claude, and the ability to upload and store documents.
