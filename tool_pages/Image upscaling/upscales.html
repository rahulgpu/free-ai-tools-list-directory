About Upscales

AI Image Upscaler is a tool designed to enhance the quality of images automatically using artificial intelligence (AI). The AI has been trained on millions of images, which allows it to accurately upscale images while preserving original details and colors. The tool can upscale an image to a significantly higher resolution, focusing on the main object in the photo and refining the details to achieve professional-grade quality. Apart from enhancing the quality of an image, this tool also aids in image retouching and sharpening, providing visually appealing results. Functionalities range from upscaling the details and colors to improving the overall image quality without requiring substantial technical knowledge. The tool is available for use on all devices and can be accessed as a web tool or downloaded as an app from both the Apple Store and the Google Play Store. It offers a user-friendly interface, making it accessible for beginners to use effectively. The tool is intended to be free for all users, aiming to bring creativity to the masses at a fraction of the cost of professional tools.
