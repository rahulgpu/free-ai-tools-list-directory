About PageOne

Fuse is an AI-powered news application providing tailored news coverage from multiple trusted outlets. Accessible on both iPhone and Android, the app features a personalized news feed, comprehensive news reports, and notification options for stories of interest. The personalized news feed is tailored to individual user interests, based on the articles and videos they engage with. Fuse employs artificial intelligence and machine learning algorithms to continuously collect, organize, prioritize, and personalize news stories from a range of major news media outlets. The app organizes content by stories and topics, keeping users in tune with the top stories from the U.S. and around the world. Its algorithm determines these top stories by analyzing the frequency of reporting from several sources and social media references. Apart from articles, Fuse also indexes related videos and tweets from major channels and verified Twitter users respectively. Users have the capacity to categorize and follow stories they deem important and can receive updates about these stories through notifications.
