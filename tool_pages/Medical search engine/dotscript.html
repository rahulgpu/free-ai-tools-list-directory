About DotScript

DotScript is an Artificial Intelligence (AI) based medical information system intended to furnish users with credible and easily accessible information on various diseases, drugs, and symptoms. Designed for broad accessibility, it caters to both general and specific medical queries. DotScript operates by sifting through multiple resources to provide an optimal response to a user's question, striving to eliminate incorrect or ambiguous medical information. Notably, the system requires no sign-ups or payments. With a focus on user convenience, DotScript is functional across various platforms, including desktop and mobile devices. Its features have been optimized to deliver solutions swiftly, reducing wait time for users. DotScript incorporates two specialized chatbots: The Genetic Bot and The Generic Bot. The Genetic Bot is programmed to deal with queries focused on the genetic aspects of medical science, discussing diseases, syndromes or symptoms. The Generic Bot, conversely, possesses knowledge on modern and herbal medicine, making it the preferred choice for everyday medical questions.
