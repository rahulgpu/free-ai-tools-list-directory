About Dr.Change

Dr. Change is an Artificial Intelligence (AI) assistant designed to aid users in establishing beneficial daily habits and routines by leveraging science-backed methodologies. This platform positions itself as an effective tool for habit and routine development, adding value to anyone seeking to enhance their daily regime. Through interaction with the user in a chat interface, Dr. Change provides personalized assistance in habit formation and enhancement. The AI assistant facilitates habit building through a variety of methods, guiding users to follow evidence-based approaches for habit creation. Further services and information can be accessed through links to an integrated 'Squad' platform, habit method cards, and the related blog. Lastly, users have the option to download the Dr. Change app on iOS and Android devices, extending its accessibility and ensuring that users can benefit from its features from anywhere, at any given time. In essence, Dr. Change offers a comprehensive, informed, and user-friendly approach to habit improvement and implementation.
