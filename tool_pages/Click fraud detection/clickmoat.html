About ClickMoat

ClickMoat is a software designed with a special focus on detecting and preventing click fraud in Google Ads. It promises to offer a secure and enhanced advertising experience by blocking fake impressions and clicks. As a tool for Pay-Per-Click campaign management, it protects your digital ad campaign from various potential threats such as competitors, academic researchers, internet bots, spamming software, and random clicks. Its goal is to increase the Quality Score of your ads by eliminating invalid bot traffic and accidental clicks, potentially saving you money on your ad budget.An integral part of its offering includes a user-friendly dashboard that provides a comprehensive overview of the clicks and savings you made, invalid clicks blocked, and detailed reports for the campaign, keywords, and country. It also allows clients to manually blacklist certain IP addresses or automatically block VPN IPs through customizable protection rules.Additionally, their service provides insights into the reasons for blocking specific IPs, giving users an educated understanding of their traffic quality. For instances of suspected click fraud, ClickMoat offers the feature to download a full click report, empowering users to manually request a refund from Google. Lastly, ClickMoat provides continuous support for all users and offers its services across multiple advertising platforms, assuring wide-ranging compatibility for diverse business needs.With these features, ClickMoat aims to provide a well-rounded solution for advertisers to safeguard their budgets, optimize the return on their ad spend, and maintain a clean advertising ecosystem.
