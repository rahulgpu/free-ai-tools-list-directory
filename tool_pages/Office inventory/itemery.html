About Itemery

Itemery is an office inventory software designed specifically for small and medium businesses. It provides an AI-based solution for inventory management and control of property. One of the key benefits of Itemery is its integration with Excel and Google Threads. It allows users to import an existing property database into the software with just two clicks, enabling easy access to the system from anywhere in the world. The AI technology utilized by Itemery allows for quick addition of items to the software's database. By using the mobile app, users can simply point their phone camera at an object, and the neural network will automatically recognize and add it to their inventory. The software facilitates easy organization of the working system, making it convenient for those responsible for inventory accounting within a company to track all items and their status efficiently. Taking inventory is made simple through Itemery. Users can either scan a barcode or QR code using their phone, which automatically adds the item to the database, or manually add it through the web version.The software provides useful features such as locations, allowing users to display the location of items in a structured way, a dashboard representing the total number of assets and audits, and an Itemery app for quick scanning and auditing. Itemery caters to a wide range of professionals, including CEOs, CFOs, IT managers, accountants, office managers, and administrators, enabling them to control and manage their company's assets from anywhere in the world.Please note that the above description is based on the available information provided and may not include all features or accurately reflect the latest updates of the tool.
