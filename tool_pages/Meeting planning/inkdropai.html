About InkdropAI

Inkdrop is an AI-powered meeting assistant that helps to make video meetings more efficient. It is powered by ChatGPT, an AI assistant that helps keep meetings short and on-topic. It also helps to quickly turn conversations into minutes, follow-ups and tickets with just one click. Inkdrop also helps to reduce the number of meetings and Slack messages by allowing users to ask questions about past meetings in their company. Additionally, Inkdrop is backed by a well-rounded team of builders and serial entrepreneurs in the AI space and has secured partnerships that believe in their mission.
