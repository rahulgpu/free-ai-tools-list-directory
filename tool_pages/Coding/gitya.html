About Gitya

Gitya is an AI-powered GitHub assistant designed to enhance your GitHub workflow and save valuable time. With its advanced AI capabilities, Gitya seamlessly integrates into your existing GitHub processes, allowing you to spend less time on bugfixes and minor requests and more time on high-impact engineering.One of Gitya's key features is its ability to manage and automate minor tasks using AI. It leverages advanced algorithms to streamline pull request processes by providing automated reviews and responses. Additionally, Gitya offers ticket automation, allowing you to label a ticket with 'gitya' and let the AI handle minor tasks swiftly.Users have reported significant improvements in productivity after implementing Gitya into their workflows.
