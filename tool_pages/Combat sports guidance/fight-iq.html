About Fight IQ

The Fight IQ is a comprehensive AI-based application designed specifically to revolutionize combat sports training. Serving as a personal coach, the tool allows users to document their journey through disciplines like MMA, boxing, muay thai, judo, BJJ, karate, or equivalent martial arts. It enables users to log every sparring session, tracking significant factors such as opponents' details, age, weight, height, strengths, weaknesses, and favorite techniques. Fight IQ provides a comprehensive analysis of every sparring session. It includes documenting every technique executed, scored, or countered; further providing insights and learning moments. The application's AI coach feature emphasizes recognizing patterns in your fights, tracking progress, and provides personalized advice on a wide range of aspects from optimal strategies, mental preparation, to body conditioning. This application makes sharing your progress easy; users can present their scorecards and AI coach's advice through various social media platforms. The tool offers a seamless experience by providing iCloud backup access across devices. The Fight IQ app has a free version available, along with a subscription version offering premium features. It's noteworthy that the developer does not collect any data from this application. Whether you're just starting out or a seasoned combatant, Fight IQ is well-equipped to enhance your combat sports journey.
