About SHIELD

SHIELD is a device-first fraud prevention and risk intelligence tool powered by AI technology and cutting-edge device fingerprinting. Its primary aim is to empower online businesses to combat fraud, build trust, and drive growth. The tool offers several solutions to address different aspects of fraud prevention, including Device Intelligence, AdShield, and Compliance AI. It caters to a variety of industries such as ride-hailing, social media and networking, superapps, media and streaming, online delivery, e-wallets, e-commerce and marketplace, digital and neobanking, gaming, and online casinos.SHIELD's Device Intelligence feature provides real-time identification and intelligence to prevent fraud at its root. It utilizes accurate device identification through device fingerprinting to make precise decisions about the trustworthiness of devices, users, and accounts.With real-time actionable device signals and continuous risk profiling, SHIELD helps businesses gain a comprehensive view of each user, eliminate risk blind spots, improve user experiences, and accelerate growth. Additionally, its plug-and-play SDK enables instant insights without requiring additional codes or compromising personally identifiable information (PII) data.The tool employs explainable machine-learning algorithms, allowing users to configure self-configurable risk thresholds and obtain the full picture with transparent intelligence. To stay ahead of emerging fraud attacks, SHIELD leverages a global intelligence network that synchronizes real-time attack patterns worldwide.Trusted by leading mobile apps and organizations, SHIELD is renowned for its ability to deliver trust, fairness, and security. It ensures user trust and protects against fraud, emphasizing its commitment to trust as a service across various industries.
