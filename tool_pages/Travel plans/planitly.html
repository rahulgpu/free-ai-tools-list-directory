About Planitly

Planitly is an AI-driven platform that aids users in crafting personalized travel plans. The user specifies their preferences, such as preferred destinations, activities, budget, and travel dates, and the AI generates a travel itinerary in seconds. Planitly facilitates the creation of completely customizable travel plans, providing users control over every aspect of their trip. This includes accommodations and local experiences, all revamped according to the users' style leading to a tailor-made experience, boosting user convenience by helping them evade the often overwhelming travel planning process.The AI recommends suitable flights, hotels, local attractions, and dining options based on user preferences. As part of these recommendations, Planitly presents detailed insights on local attractions, reviews of dining options, and cost estimates. This data is built to enhance user experiences by helping them discover the best places according to their tastes and budget. Planitly also equips users with the ability to effortlessly make modifications to their detailed day-by-day itineraries 'on the go', thus offering an easily adjustable and efficient planning tool. Furthermore, the platform allows users to save their favorite itineraries for future reference. With Planitly, users can bypass traditional travel planning hassles such as extended research across multiple websites, rigid travel packages, hidden costs, limited knowledge of local insights, and time-consuming itinerary adjustments. With a personalized, detailed itinerary from Planitly, users can book all their trip components in one place, offering a seamless and intuitive planning experience.
