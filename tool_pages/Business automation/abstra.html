About Abstra

Abstra is an AI-driven workflow engine designed for business process automation and management. Users can combine programming techniques with AI capabilities to gain a comprehensive insight into their business operations. This Python-based tool aims to automate entire business processes with full transparency, rather than just basic tasks, eliminating the need for overhead programming. The versatility of this platform enables a broad range of applications including credit analysis, finance, account onboarding, and revenue operations. Abstra provides various features such as smart forms, jobs, hooks and several others. Smart forms are dynamic and support custom logic with advanced integrations. Jobs are time-based automations, while Hooks are event-based automations. The platform also ensures security through advanced, custom granular control for user authentication and permission management. Further, it provides compliant logging capabilities with access and export of auditable logs for compliance investigations. Its 'Connectors' feature facilitates easy integration with databases, internal APIs or external services. Apart from cloud-based deployment, it offers on-premise hosting options for superior data control. In addition, it features options to integrate with 'Tables', its own relational database, for storing and querying data from inside the projects.
