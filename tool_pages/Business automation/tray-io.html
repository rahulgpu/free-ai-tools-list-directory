About Tray.io

Tray.io is a unified, AI-powered integration platform (iPaaS) aimed at automating business processes and facilitating ecosystem integrations. It offers a broad range of connectivity options allowing integration of systems whether they are cloud-based, on-premise, or spread across customer and partner ecosystems. Furthermore, Tray.io facilitates data integration, maintaining data consistency throughout an organization's evolving software environment. It also allows for custom process automation beyond the limitations of traditional out-of-the-box tools. Another notable feature is its ability to activate ecosystem integrations across varied systems for the workforce, customers, and partners. Tray.io adds flexibility to API Management with its low-code and code-optional development approach. Its 'Merlin AI' provides an intelligent platform-level layer that enables faster and improved building of business processes with natural language instructions. In terms of enterprise support, Tray.io provides a secure and scalable infrastructure that caters to large-scale automation needs. Its governance features include role-based access control, log-streaming, workspace partitioning, and audit trails. Security measures include encryption at rest, site-to-site VPN, single sign-on, and compliance for sensitive data. Scalability capabilities include serverless architecture, big data processing, and zero hardware maintenance. Transparency is maintained via robust logging, error notifications, and multi-layered process analytics. These features collectively help drive an organization's integration strategy while ensuring data security and system protection.
