About Aayde

Aayde is an AI-based tool that acts as a virtual employee, assisting with various tasks related to administration, accounting, project management, and other professional roles. The tool is designed to simplify the operations of a company by automating routine tasks. Aayde can be communicated with like an assistant and integrates well with other platforms such as Slack, Discord, Email, Quickbooks, CRMs and Project management tools. A common use case is instructing Aayde to compile all invoices received via email and pre-populate them into QuickBooks as expenses, subsequently preparing the accounting work. Being AI-driven, Aayde's operations are not bound by human working hours, leading to efficiency and time savings in executing administrative tasks. Its versatility makes it ideal for roles like a secretary, an accountant, an administrator, and even a scrummaster. This gives corporations a wide range of utilization options based on their individual needs.
