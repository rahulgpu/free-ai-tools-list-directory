About NexusBots

NexusBots AI is an automation agency offering a range of services aimed at businesses looking to automate processes and improve efficiency. Services include the development of chatbots through platforms like Botpress and ChatGPT, implementation of automated workflows with tools like Make.IO, data analysis using advanced AI techniques, and creation of landing pages and blogs with Framer. The agency operates on a subscription model that allows customers to make regular requests as per their needs. Some of the key features offered in the subscription are unlimited automations, chatbot development, Framer development, ability to pause or cancel anytime, and dedicated customer service. Service requests are managed through Trello, enabling clients to track progress in real time. The agency's focus is on delivering robust, scalable automation solutions tailored to the specific needs of businesses across various industries. The agency also offers ongoing development, updates and maintenance services on customer request, enabling businesses to reduce operational costs. Although it is described as an 'agency', NexusBots AI consists of a single person, ensuring a personal, dedicated service for each client.
