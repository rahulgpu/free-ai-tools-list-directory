About Pipefy

Pipefy is a low-code business process automation tool designed to streamline various operations across different teams such as HR, Procurement, CS, etc., by managing and automating workflows. It allows users to build effective workflows without any coding skills, extending workflow efficiency across the enterprise operations. The Pipefy platform facilitates the creation of custom workflows that bridge processes, teams, and tools for an optimized business environment. The solution is equipped with Pipefy AI, which generates optimized workflows based on your inputs, driving productivity and efficiency. It also analyzes complex data, making it easier for teams to gain insights and make informed decisions. Pipefy provides native integrations, APIs, and custom connectors for seamless interaction with your existing tech stack, unifying data sources and dissolving silos for comprehensive, real-time data visibility. It is designed to control costs and reduce complexity by eliminating the need for expensive add-ons and unsanctioned workarounds while maintaining enterprise-grade security protocols. Pipefy is recognized as a reliable choice for businesses looking to automate both simple and complex processes, increase productivity, and promote data-driven strategies.
