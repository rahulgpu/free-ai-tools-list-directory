About UiPath

UiPath is a Business Automation Platform designed to automate all knowledge work to accelerate human achievement. Leveraging artificial intelligence (AI) and automation, it enables continuous innovation within business operations. The UiPath platform is composed of various tools serving a wide range of automation needs, from identifying high-return opportunities for process optimization to deploying mission-critical automation programs at scale.The platform follows a three-phase method: Discover, Automate, Operate. The 'Discover' phase includes identifying high-ROI opportunities for process optimization and automation using AI. Key parts of this stage are process mining, task mining, and communication mining. The 'Automate' phase involves building AI-powered automation that collaborates smoothly with people and systems to transform work facets. This features UI and API automation, low-code development, intelligent document processing, and majorly, AI. Finally, the 'Operate' dimension aims to run and optimize an automation program at a significant scale with real-time analytics, continuous testing, unified management, and cloud-first flexibility.UiPath also provides a Product Suite that comprises some prominent tools like Process Mining, Test Manager, and Orchestrator. Furthermore, UiPath offers specialized AI solutions in a single enterprise-grade platform. Other services include free online automation training, customer support, and a community forum for discussions and problem-solving.
