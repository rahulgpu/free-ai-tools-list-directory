About My-Legacy.ai

My-Legacy.ai is an AI-enabled platform aimed at making the intricate process of estate planning user-friendly, safe, and affordable. It's designed to provide quick answers, personalized recommendations and useful resources to guide the user through what necessary legal documents they should have. My-Legacy.ai uses a conversational approach to assist users at different stages of life, from young adults to those over 60 years old, ensuring an all-encompassing experience for estate planning. A standout functionality of this tool is the 'LegacyScore'. It is a unique feature which provides users with a measure of their legal preparedness. The tool also puts a high emphasis on user privacy and ensures data remains confidential and is never sold. Finally, and importantly, the platform was built with global applicability in mind enabling it to offer personalized advice to people in different countries and states. Notably, My-Legacy.ai has received recognition for innovation and user-friendliness from various platforms and user reviews, suggesting its effectiveness and reliability for estate planning.
