About SendCall

SendCall AI is an AI-powered calling agent designed to execute lifelike phone conversations, capable of managing over 5,000 applications. With its ability to operate 24/7 without requiring a pause, retraining or motivation, it effectively simulates the role of a full-time employee. Its primary function includes conducting sales calls, interviews, surveys, scheduling appointments, managing data entries, and other customer service tasks. Users can instruct SendCall AI on what information to retrieve from a call and receive a targeted report via email once the call is concluded. It boasts an uninterrupted memory and perfect recall, negating the need for note-taking or concern over forgetting important information. SendCall AI offers infinite memory and flawless recall, making it an efficient tool for phone interactions, making it a beneficial tool in revolutionising phone-based customer interactions. Furthermore, it's capable of autonomously managing tasks efficiently across multiple applications, reducing the need for manual oversight. The AI's deployment is straightforward and requires no training, making it an accessible tool for varied businesses looking to enhance efficiency and minimize operational costs.
