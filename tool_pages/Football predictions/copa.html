About COPA

COPA is an AI-powered football companion app designed to provide comprehensive football match predictions, player performance analysis, match statistics, and real-time score updates. Its core functionality is to make predictions on various match events such as match outcomes, both teams to score, total team goals, player performances, corners, and cards. These predictions are powered by advanced algorithms that analyze a rich variety of historical and current data points to forecast potential outcomes. Alongside these predictions, COPA highlights the most probable outcomes. This information can be valuable for fans who want not only to keep track of ongoing match events but also for those interested in sports betting as it may help them make more informed decisions.In addition to the predictive capabilities, COPA ensures that football fans never miss a moment of their favorite games, providing access to match highlights from games around the globe as soon as they're available. Score Centre allows witnessing match events in real time, offering up-to-date scores, statistics, lineups, and detailed analytics for matches across the globe. The Hub feature presents team and league pages containing the latest news, standings, player stats, and fixtures from various international teams. Thus, COPA assembles a well-rounded repository of football information, offering insights into team performances and leagues worldwide.
