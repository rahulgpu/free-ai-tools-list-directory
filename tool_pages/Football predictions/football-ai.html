About Football AI

Football AI is a machine learning based football prediction app developed by Cookie App Factory. The app uses a unique algorithm that analyses over 1000 data points to predict football match outcomes. The algorithm analyzes data ranging up to 7 years, including form, past meetings, squad quality, injuries, whether the match is played on the home court or as guests, formation of the team, scored goals, rookies and current points in the table. The app provides tips in 1, X, 2 formats along with full slip predictions. It is constantly updated to improve its prediction accuracy and has an 85% success rate in predictions. Football AI claims to compete against the bookmakers' prediction system and aims to provide users with better predictions. The app has over 1 million downloads with a PEGI 18 rating and includes in-app purchases. The developers declare that no personal data is shared with third parties and the app encrypts data during transit. The app collects personal info, messages, and app activity, which can be deleted upon request. The ratings and reviews of Football AI are verified and are mostly positive, with users claiming that the app provides reliable and accurate football match predictions.
