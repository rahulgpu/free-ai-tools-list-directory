About Xound

Xound.io is an AI-based Sound Enhancement System aimed at enhancing the audio quality of various content types such as podcasts, videos on YouTube and TikTok, and more. Its primary purpose is to elevate audio quality, boosting engagement and reducing listener churn. The tool is user-friendly, evident from its drag-and-drop functionality for video uploads and instant analysis of uploaded media for potential audio improvements. The software employs advanced techniques such as cepstrum analysis for pitch detection and Mel-Frequency Cepstral Coefficients (MFCC) for feature extraction, ensuring high accuracy and performance. One key feature is natural pitch correction that delivers in-tune vocals and enhances voice clarity. Another notable feature is background noise removal that prioritises crystal-clear communication. Xound.io also offers dynamic range compression contributing to a more consistent volume level, thus minimizing listener fatigue. For improving user interaction, it brings in high-frequency presence by adding brightness to the voice, helping it stand out against a background score or effects in a mix. Xound.io is committed to high security, ensuring your media never touches their servers and offers a WhatsApp integration for sending media files directly to the system.
