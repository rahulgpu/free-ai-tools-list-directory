About Sensey

Sensey.ai is a software-as-a-service (SaaS) platform, specifically tailored to assist startups, particularly first-time founders and B2B AI startups, along their journey to product-market fit. It primarily guides startups by providing in-depth market research, data analysis, and financial modeling. The platform employs advanced artificial intelligence to enrich and further analyze market and competitor data, thereby offering a deeper understanding and facilitating smarter startup decisions. Sensey.ai not only provides the capability to observe data but also an interactive experience, where AI helps illuminate the timeline from data collection to insight generation. Based on these insights, the startups can personalize their market perspective and customize their strategies. In addition to publicly available information, Sensey.ai provides analysis based on a wide variety of firmographics, marketing strategies, and product lifecycle analytics. It aims to fill gaps in startups' understanding, keeps their knowledge up-to-date, and assists with the development of effective strategies informed by advanced AI analysis.
