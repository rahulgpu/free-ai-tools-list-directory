About LaraFast

LaraFast - Laravel Starter Kit is a Laravel boilerplate designed to streamline SaaS app development. By providing essential, ready-to-use components such as payments systems, an admin panel, a blog feature, and SEO tools, LaraFast can accelerate an entrepreneur's journey from idea to product launch. The toolkit is powered by technologies such as Jetstream, Tailwind, Inertia.js, and Vue.js. The built-in features aim to eliminate repetitive coding tasks, saving both time and money each time a new project is started. Some of the specific features include user registration and logins, SEO meta tags, built-in admin panel with user and blog article management, payment plan checkout sessions, and automatic sitemap builders among others. However, the stand-out value of LaraFast lies in the efficiency it brings: by handling much of the 'boring stuff', it allows entrepreneurs to focus more on their ideas rather than the implementation, providing a faster route to launching their projects.
