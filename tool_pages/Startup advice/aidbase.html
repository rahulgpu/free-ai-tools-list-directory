About Aidbase

Aidbase is an AI-Powered eco-system designed to enhance customer support in start-ups. The eco-system features various products including an AI Chatbot that acts as the customer support agent, an email support component that handles customer support emails, and an AI-powered ticket system that resolves customer issues efficiently. Aidbase also offers 'Elements', a feature that allows to create a fully embedded AI-powered support experience in the startup's own app. The platform helps in training AI models through a multitude of sources like website content, YouTube videos, and other documents to understand and emulate the brand's offerings accurately. In addition to autonomous AI solutions, the platform also includes AI-assisted areas acknowledging when human intervention may be necessary. With Aidbase's No Code Form Builder feature, custom ticket forms can be created intuitively. The AI also learns from the startup's knowledge base and previous tickets, making it smarter with time. Aidbase also provides a 'Smart Suggestions' feature where the AI suggests solutions from users with similar issues. Aidbase's compatibility extends to a broad range of third-party tools and services. Aidbase Elements allow for integration, design, and implementation of support pages in different ways. The platform also has features like FAQ & Knowledge Hub, invitation for team collaboration, identification of knowledge gaps, quick integrations, and triggering of actions using webhooks.
