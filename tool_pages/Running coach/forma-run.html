About Forma Run

Forma is an AI-based tool designed to enhance and empower users' running journey. It harnesses the power of artificial intelligence to provide expert coaching feedback based on running videos uploaded by users. Once a video is uploaded to the platform, Forma analyzes the user's run style, providing personalized feedback and coaching advice to optimize running performance and minimize the risk of injury. This tool is designed with a user-friendly interface and is accompanied by a privacy policy and terms of service ensuring user information is kept confidential and secure. Forma offers a unique blend of technology and fitness information thus providing a personalized fitness solution. It's an excellent option for runners looking to improve their technique, receive expert-level feedback without hiring a personal coach, and understand better how they can push their limits safely and efficiently.
