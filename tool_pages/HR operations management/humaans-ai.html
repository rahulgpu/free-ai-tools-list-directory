About Humaans AI

Humaans AI is a comprehensive tool designed to enhance efficiency and allow professionals to focus on what truly matters in their operations. As a core HR employee database, it consolidates people data and documents in one place. It also features workflow automation to optimize processes, and absence management and tracking tools compatible with over 300 global regions. Secure permission driven data access ensures that all responses are dictated by user permissions without compromising privacy. In addition, it provides timesheets for tracking employee working hours and includes functionalities to connect with external apps. The open API allows users to create custom integrations, adding flexibility and adaptability to the tool. Furthermore, it offers a suite of data and analytics features. From generating workforce diversity insights to facilitating access and extraction of company data, the product enriches HR processes. It allows real-time editing via spreadsheet data tables and offers headcount planning to assist in forecasting company workforce. Compensation management features help in planning pay structures and equity. Moreover, Humaans AI assists teams by readily answering repetitive, time-consuming questions related to company policies and documentation, thus saving valuable time. Users can create data views using natural language, and significant documents can be used to give AI powered answers to team questions. This tool evolves as a team's companion, capable of answering wide-ranging inquiries around people, policies, among other topics.
