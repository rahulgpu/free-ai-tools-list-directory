About Edde AI

Edde.ai is a platform designed to assist and enhance academic writing using artificial intelligence. It provides a range of tools to facilitate the writing process and optimize learning outcomes. Among the available features is an AI Writing Assistant, which aids in improving text through real-time suggestions, grammar checks, and stylistic enhancements. A Title and Outline Generator uses AI to provide examples for starting a writing project. The platform also includes a Research Helper, which simplifies the process of engaging with scholarly articles, journals, and citations. To further support academic research, the platform offers Citation Management, which can automatically format citations in a variety of styles. Additionally, Edde.ai provides Subject Guides to assist in developing understanding of particular academic fields, and a Question Solver to explore detailed topic explanations. Users can also utilize the Homework Helper feature to aid in the academic journey. The service is designed to be user-friendly and guides users through steps to write more efficient and effective academic papers. Edde.ai offers different pricing options, including a free basic plan and a professional plan to accommodate varying user needs.
