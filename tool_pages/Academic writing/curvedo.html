About Curvedo

Curvedo is an AI-powered toolset designed primarily for students to streamline academic writing tasks. The tool suite includes a range of distinct yet interconnected tools. The 'Essay Writer' functionality enables users to create unique, high-quality academic essays according to given topics. It promises plagiarism-free delivery with well-cited references. The 'Essay Grader' function automates essay grading by providing instant feedback, scoring essays, and offering improvement tips, similar to having constant access to a tutor. Further reinforcing the essay writing process is the 'Paragraph Generator', capable of creating well-structured individual paragraphs suitable for essays or other text assignments. In addition to writing and grading essays, Curvedo also provides a 'Math Solver' tool, designed to aid in deciphering complex mathematical equations by providing detailed step-by-step solutions. Suitable for different academic levels, Curvedo aims to act as an all-in-one platform for educational support, facilitating smarter studying practices through AI-backed tools.
