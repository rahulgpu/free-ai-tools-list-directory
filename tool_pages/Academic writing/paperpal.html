About PaperPal

Paperpal is a generative AI-powered academic writing tool designed to enhance the quality and efficiency of academic writing by offering real-time, language-specific suggestions. In particular, it provides guidance in selection and use of language, grammar checking, paraphrasing, and academic translation. It is also equipped with a text reduction feature to help users streamline their work. Furthermore, Paperpal includes a free essay checker to ensure an error-free manuscript and a plagiarism checker to avoid accidental duplication of contents. The tool is designed to be context-sensitive in providing recommendations, making it a value-added companion for academic writers. Another feature includes its ability to assist writers in performing research, where it provides science-backed answers derived from a vast number of research articles. This research feature also allows users to save relevant citations directly into their library. This tool is compatible with web and MS Word and has been widely adopted by leading universities, publishers, and academic writers worldwide.
