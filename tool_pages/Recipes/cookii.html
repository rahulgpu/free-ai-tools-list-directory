About Cookii

Cookii is an AI-powered tool designed to assist users in finding recipe ideas based on their preferences and available ingredients. With its user-friendly interface, Cookii allows users to select one or more ingredients from a comprehensive list to form the basis of their desired recipe. Additionally, users can specify basic details such as the number of people being served, dietary restrictions, cuisine preferences, and meal type. The tool offers a wide variety of options, including the choice of serving 1 to 6 people or more, and the ability to cater to different dietary needs, such as vegetarian, vegan, pescatarian, flexitarian, gluten-free, lactose-free, kosher, halal, or a raw food diet. Users can also choose from popular cuisines like Italian, Chinese, Indian, Japanese, Mexican, French, Thai, Spanish, Mediterranean, and Greek, ensuring a diverse range of recipe options.Furthermore, Cookii allows users to input the ingredients they already have at home, providing a helpful inventory tool. This feature enables the tool to suggest recipes that incorporate the selected ingredients, assisting users in making the most of what they have available. With its comprehensive database and intuitive interface, Cookii simplifies the process of finding recipe suggestions and enables users to explore diverse culinary options effortlessly.
