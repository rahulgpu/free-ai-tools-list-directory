About FoodAI.app

FoodAI.app is an AI-driven web application that facilitates the generation of cooking recipes. It leverages the capabilities of the GPT-3 Model for creating recipes. Users input their preferred ingredients and the application produces recipes incorporating those ingredients. In addition to providing ingredient-based results, the FoodAI app also boasts the ability to filter results based on dietary preferences and regions. Dietary categories such as vegetarian, vegan, and gluten-free options are accommodated for. Moreover, users can select recipe origin like Asian, American, European, African, Australian, Indian, Middle Eastern, and Latin American. The platform also provides a feature to ban certain ingredients. The application creates an interactive and user-friendly method of discovering new recipes, which are based on a large and diverse dataset. This tool can be particularly useful to those looking for creative meal planning, exploring diverse cuisines or dealing with dietary restrictions. Note that the use of this tool includes a subscription fee after a trial period.
