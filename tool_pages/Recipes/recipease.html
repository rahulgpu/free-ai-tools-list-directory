About Recipease

Recipease is an AI-powered cooking app designed to assist both novice and seasoned home cooks in discovering new dishes to cook. The tool is engineered for individuals confronted with 'culinary block', aiding them to utilize ingredients already in their pantry and tailor recipes according to user preferences. User's have the option to input on-hand ingredients into the app, after which the AI component generates a recipe incorporating those ingredients. Recipease also allows for user-specific cravings to be met by inputting the desired dish, with the AI capability providing a recipe within seconds. Recipease includes a save feature, allowing users to easily store, revisit and search through their favorite recipes at any given time. The app also has the functionality to import recipes from external sources via copying and pasting, or using a built-in scan text feature. Users generally begin with a supply of tokens which can be used to obtain new recipes. When the original token supply is consumed, users have the option to purchase more tokens for continued culinary exploration. The application prides itself on simplifying the cooking process and enhancing culinary creativity through the use of AI technology.
