About Tomaito

Tomaito - Recipe Generator is an AI-powered mobile application available on the App Store that provides users with personalized recipe recommendations based on their dietary restrictions, preferences, and ingredients on hand. The app has a user-friendly interface with features such as inputting dietary preferences, pantry and fridge inventory, and serving size requirements. It then generates recipe suggestions, including the recipe name, ingredients, instructions, and nutrition information. Users can also save recipes for future use and share them with others. Additionally, the app includes reviews and ratings from other users, so users can make informed decisions on what recipes to try. One unique feature of the app is a shopping list generator, which allows users to create a grocery list of the ingredients they need to buy based on the recipes they select. Another feature is the ability to modify and adjust the recipe according to their taste preferences. Overall, Tomaito - Recipe Generator offers a convenient and personalized approach to meal planning and recipe discovery, which can save users time and take out the stress of deciding what to cook.
