About YouGotCooking

YouGotCooking is an AI-based tool designed to assist in meal planning by providing recipe suggestions based on ingredients already available at home. Its main objective is to simplify meal time while promoting a healthy and zero waste approach to cooking. Despite its reliance on AI for generating recipes, users are advised to apply their own judgment when following the suggested recipes, as the tool cannot account for individual taste preferences or physically assess the outcome. The AI function is modelled on the GPT-3 model. The tool supports entries in English for optimal results but is compatible with other languages as well. Additionally, YouGotCooking operates with a daily recipe request limit to prevent misuse and ensure the tool remains accessible without charge for users. It incorporates a 'Buy me a coffee' feature, allowing users to make contributions towards maintaining its free service. While YouGotCooking encourages creative culinary exploration with the aid of AI technology, it upholds a user-guided approach towards the final cooking process.
