About RecipeBox

RecipeBox is an AI-based tool designed to streamline the process of meal preparation. It acts as an integrated platform to pull recipes from various websites and create interactive shopping lists. The tool leverages artificial intelligence to read the recipes and extract rich details including ingredients required and the steps involved in the cooking process. Following this extraction, RecipeBox generates a clear and straightforward shopping guide that provides users with the ingredients needed for the recipe. This tool affords a level of customisation, allowing users to alter the recipe to personal preference, edit ingredients and cooking instructions, or add their own recipes. The interactive shopping list offers convenience, enabling users to keep track of purchased items. RecipeBox primarily operates on a pay-as-you-go system, with no tie-in to monthly subscriptions. It maintains stringent security measures, ensuring users' data is encrypted and held in a secure environment without any sharing to third parties.
