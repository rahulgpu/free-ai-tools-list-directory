About Flavorish

Flavorish is an AI-powered platform that simplifies the cooking process by offering personalized recipes based on users' available ingredients and dietary preferences. The platform allows users to input the ingredients they have on hand, and it then recommends recipes accordlingly. It also accommodates specific dietary needs ensuring recipes are tailored accordingly, and caters to global cuisines, with easily adaptable recipes. Additionally, Flavorish includes a feature for smart shopping lists where it auto-generates and organizes grocery lists from the selected recipes, ensuring an efficient shopping experience. Users can also save their favorite recipes, be it ones generated by the AI, or personal favorites from the web, and also offers the ability to modify stored recipes to suit individual taste. One of the platform's distinguishing features includes an offline mode that allows users to create and manage shopping lists and access recipes without internet connectivity, synchronizing any changes once reconnected. Future features include grabbing recipes from social media, accessing nutritional information for each recipe, suggested beverage pairings for each meal and a grocery app sync for a seamless online shopping experience. A basic version of Flavorish is available for free with premium features being accessible through a subscription.
