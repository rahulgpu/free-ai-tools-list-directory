About FoodWiz

FoodWiz is a Google Chrome extension that utilizes AI technology to modify online recipes based on your dietary preferences and requirements. It allows you to edit recipes to make them vegan, gluten-free, and adjust serving sizes. With its advanced AI algorithms, FoodWiz analyzes and modifies recipes to cater to your specific needs. This lightweight and user-friendly Chrome extension seamlessly integrates with your browser, providing a convenient and hassle-free recipe editing experience. It offers endless customization options such as ingredient substitutions and modification of serving sizes. To use FoodWiz, simply create a free account and download the Chrome extension from the Chrome Web Store. After logging in, you can navigate to any recipe webpage and customize it by selecting popular presets or inputting your own custom instructions. FoodWiz has garnered positive feedback from users who appreciate its ability to make exploring new diets and recipes easier. Ensure the privacy of your personal data as sign up is free and FoodWiz pledges not to sell your information. If you want to enhance your recipe editing experience through AI-driven technology, sign up for FoodWiz today and enjoy your favorite dishes the way you want them.
