About Kiti

KITI.AI is an innovative platform that combines artificial intelligence and culinary creativity. By harnessing the power of AI, it provides a unique service of creating food kits based on users' submitted recipes or trending culinary posts. The AI interprets the recipe from the given link and packages all necessary ingredients into a convenient food kit that can be ordered and shipped directly to the user. In addition, KITI.AI has a feature that allows visitors to view and order trending recipe kits. It has been designed to primarily serve food content creators, chefs, and home cooks who regularly source recipes from social media. Aside from providing a user-friendly platform for ordering food recipe kits, KITI.AI aims to enhance the cooking experience by minimizing difficulties of ingredient sourcing and recipe interpretation. This platform also offers a unique opportunity for content creators to make their recipes more accessible to a wider audience. Whether you're a seasoned chef looking to elevate your home cooking experience, or a food enthusiast seeking to recreate viral culinary creations, KITI.AI promises a convenient, AI-enhanced experience that simplifies the journey from recipe to plate.
