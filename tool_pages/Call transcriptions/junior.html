About Junior

Junior is an AI Copilot tool specifically designed for consultants. It leverages AI technology to extract and structure insights from expert calls. With Junior, consultants can benefit from client-ready transcripts, eliminating the need for manual note-taking. The tool includes advanced note-taking capabilities that remove filler words, informal language, repetitions, and interruptions from transcripts. It also offers the ability to boost spelling capabilities by specifying proper nouns.One of the standout features of Junior is its instant key takeaways. It generates key points from each call, which can be linked to interview guides and the scope of work. These takeaways can be automatically shared with the team, ensuring everyone is on the same page.Searching for specific quotes is made effortless with Junior. Its search functionality understands synonyms and context, expanding the scope of relevant answers. Users can save their favorite quotes to custom quote banks for later synthesis.Junior also allows for the automation of quantitative data entry. It transforms unstructured voice and transcript data into meaningful structured insights. Users can import questions from interview guides and let Junior handle the rest, including generating customizable charts.Furthermore, Junior offers workflow tools specifically tailored for the consulting industry. It provides features like call note-taking and cleaning, transcript review, call tracking, interview workstream analytics, and interview guide generation.Overall, Junior empowers consultants by streamlining their projects with the help of AI, enabling faster and more efficient decision-making.
