About Devzery

Devzery is an AI software testing platform designed to streamline quality assurance (QA) workflows by enabling faster, more efficient tests. It leverages algorithms to create and automate test cases from product URLs, providing comprehensive coverage and simplifying the process of software validation. This AI-powered tool also marks a significant advancement in regression testing, as it identifies and prioritizes the most critical test cases based on code changes. It is specifically built to seamlessly integrate with popular Continuous Integration/Continuous Deployment (CI/CD) pipelines, assisting agile development workflows by automatically identifying the impacted test scenarios. One of Devzery's key benefits is its contribution to reducing the testing cycle time, which in turn accelerates the product's time to market. The platform also facilitates rapid onboarding, aiming to train testers on the product within a week. Furthermore, it aids in minimizing costs by preventing late-stage bugs that could affect the product's lead time, quality, and ultimately revenue.
