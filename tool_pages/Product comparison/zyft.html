About Zyft

Zyft is an AI-driven shopping tool designed to help users find the best online prices for products. Available as a free-to-use application for iOS devices and as an extension for the Chrome web browser, Zyft aims to simplify and optimize online shopping. Users can utilize Zyft for immediate price comparisons from multiple websites, aggregated in one location for convenience. No sign up is required to use the tool, highlighting its user-friendly approach. Zyft additionally offers the ability to check whether a product is available in stock and where, providing real-time results to users. It also aids in tracking price history and searching for the latest deals, further helping consumers make informed purchasing decisions. A feature known as 'ZyftZap' also exists for in-store shopping. It allows users to scan a product barcode to compare prices on the spot. Zyft covers a wide range of products across a vast number of retailers, thus offering a comprehensive look at available prices. It primarily caters to Australian users as it browses through Australian retailers. This tool is suitable for those seeking to maximize savings when shopping online, offering the ability to quickly and easily compare prices and find the best deal.
