About 20paths

20paths is an AI tool designed to facilitate the creation of quick and easy how-to guides and documentations. It is suitable for technical writers, documentation specialists, and instructional designers who aim to develop concise and compelling guides. Many users have found it beneficial for creating standard operating procedures (SOPs), offering an intuitive interface and powerful features that enable efficient production of professional-level content. It can also be used as a support tool to address customer queries through instant sharing of how-to guides. 20paths offers a multi-format guide creation, allowing users to weave together SOPs across different tools and procedures. The tool even extends its use to sales and marketing by tailoring interactive demos to particular customer profiles and stages, helping to convert leads and interactively showcase product features. A convenient feature of this tool is the Chrome extension which allows users to start and finish a guide or demo with just a click, auto-generating highlighted elements and basic text for the guide. Customization options are available, and the generated content can be shared instantly via a link or embedded into various platforms. The tool further aids in creating auto-generated help centers with demos and guides, offering SEO benefits, customizable aesthetics to match brand identity, and an option to use a custom domain link.
