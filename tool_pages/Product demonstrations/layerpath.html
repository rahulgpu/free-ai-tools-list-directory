About Layerpath

Layerpath is a digital tool designed to create interactive product demos in an efficient and effective manner. This tool allows users to capture screen recordings and convert them into various formats such as step-by-step guides, product tours, and videos. Its aim is to enrich product understanding and accelerate the buying and adoption decisions of potential customers. Notably, Layerpath offers its users the capability to organize and measure engagement through features like Lead Forms, Playlists, and Analytics. Users can create personalized tours, sales demonstrations, and onboarding processes, providing a richer, interactive alternative to static screenshots. These tours can be customized to enhance the viewer's learning experience, by adjusting various aspects including colors, voiceovers, layouts, fonts, and more. In addition to this, Layerpath leverages AI technology to assist in the creation of these tours, with features to improve text and add engaging voiceovers. Further, it provides data capture functionality within tours to capture viewer information, and assist in driving conversions. Layerpath offers different subscription plans, accommodating the needs of individuals and professional teams alike, each offering a diverse range of features and capabilities.
