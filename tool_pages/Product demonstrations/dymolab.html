About Dymolab

Dymolab is a tool specifically designed for creating interactive product demos. The primary aim is to transform all aspects of customer engagement, ranging from first impressions and conversion rates to onboarding and customer support. It allows users to record demos using a Chrome extension that automatically captures the screen interactions and permits addition of guides with every click. Users can further customize their demos by adding their unique touch. It offers a selection of guiding tools to make demos more interactive, such as incorporating audio and video, or attaching files.Users can share their custom demo via a live link, embed on their own website, or preview directly on the Dymolab website. Furthermore, Dymolab provides the function to create tailored links for different recipients, facilitating personalization at scale.In addition to these features, Dymolab includes advanced analytics to monitor what's effective and who's engaged, facilitating better marketing strategies. For better integration, Dymolab can be easily connected with existing marketing tools to sync engagement data effectively. This allows a seamless operation between platforms and provides a comprehensive view of marketing engagement and impacts.
