About Trood

The Trood Business Development Manager is an AI-driven software product management platform designed for business owners, product creators, and industry visionaries. This tool empowers users to launch and maintain control of their software products, promising agility and speed in product development cycles. The platform provides guided scenarios for product and business development, reducing reliance on technical teams and minimizing risk associated with loss of key technical personnel. Besides, it also extends individuals and companies access to a supportive community, best practices, and cutting-edge technologies. With its AI CTO and AI Board of Directors feature, Trood Business Development Manager enables users to fulfil technical requirements smoothly and manage product execution, ensuring that the users' vision is respected and implemented. The platform's features are useful for both startups and corporations, providing templates for different development environments, such as in-house and community-driven development, outsourcing, and outperformance. Moreover, the platform facilitates connections within a virtual community, removing the need for physical networking hubs.
