About IPscreener

IPscreener is a web-based AI tool designed for conducting intelligent intellectual property research, making patent information accessible to all. Developed in Sweden, this tool assists users in exploring and processing the wealth of information embedded within patents. With just a text input, it presents an instant analysis of the innovation landscape, highlighting similar solutions, active competitors, and global trends. The IPscreener AI tool is integral to various aspects of innovative workflow such as validating innovation concepts and understanding technological trends and specific domain issues. Features include a highlighting tool for easier review, smart filters for focused analysis, API integration abilities, automated reporting, and a passage finder that zooms in on the most relevant sections of documents for users. IPscreener's AI learns from user interactions, optimizing and improving future iterations. The system supports the translation of over 25 languages to English, allowing for global accessibility and use. Geared towards innovators, IP professionals, and anyone interested in harnessing the power of AI for thorough IP exploration, IPscreener aims to democratize patent information and to facilitate efficient innovation.
