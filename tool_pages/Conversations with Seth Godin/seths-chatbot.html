About Seths Chatbot

The "Ask an AI trained on Seth’s writing" tool on Seth Godin's blog allows users to ask a question and receive an AI-generated response based on Seth's writing. The AI has been specifically trained on Seth's writing style and content and aims to provide helpful and informative responses to user queries. This tool can be useful for those seeking information on marketing, tribes, and respect, topics that are commonly covered on Seth's blog. The tool is accessed by clicking on the "Ask" button on the blog's homepage, and users can submit their questions using a text box. The AI processes the query and generates a response that is presented on the same page. Users can provide feedback on the response using thumbs up or down symbols, and even submit additional feedback if they wish. The tool is designed to provide an additional layer of engagement and interaction for visitors to the blog. Overall, the "Ask an AI trained on Seth’s writing" tool is a unique and innovative resource for those interested in Seth Godin's work and seeking quick and reliable answers to their questions. It provides a way for users to engage with the content of the blog in a new and interactive way, and may be particularly beneficial for those seeking practical advice on marketing-related topics.
