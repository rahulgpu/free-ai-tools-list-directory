About Wordsmith AI

Wordsmith is an AI-powered tool designed for legal professionals, providing an easy-to-navigate workspace for legal tasks. It enhances legal operations by facilitating the creation of custom legal assistants to respond to legal queries, screen and review contracts, handle document generation, and manage workloads. The system can respond to complex legal questions in any language, being instantly available 24/7. It offers a legal document kit that includes AI chatbot assistance for drafting responses to legal queries. Wordsmith also features functionalities which grant the legal team full command over responses and approvals, and allows the setting of custom policies for more controlled legal actions. The system's capacity to understand and respond to requests from the team empowers users to keep control of legal operations. Moreover, Wordsmith facilitates swift contract analysis by automatically marking and redlining documents. It offers an feature to instantly provide feedback on NDAs, customize reviews on DPAs, and allows procurement to have an initial pass on third-party paperwork. Furthermore, Wordsmith offers integrations for seamless connection with other essential tools for team workflows. The platform prioritizes security and data privacy, being fully SOC2 certified without training on user data.
