About LeaseLens

LeaseLens is an AI-based lease abstraction software designed to identify and extract relevant data from real estate or commercial lease documents. The software utilizes machine learning technology to quickly and accurately abstract information from the entered content. The complex process of lease abstraction, which can often be error-prone and expensive, is seamlessly and efficiently automated by LeaseLens, saving both time and resources. Users can view abstracted data for free or opt to export the results to Excel or Word for a fee. LeaseLens supports both single and multiple lease extraction, making it a versatile solution for diverse needs. The software can cater to specific requirements including property management and equipment leasing. Additionally, LeaseLens maintains the confidentiality of the data uploaded. The software processes the lease documents through optical character recognition (OCR), then uses machine learning to identify and extract relevant lease data. It can identify over 200 standardized fields to form the lease abstract. The softwares versatile nature allows users to customize their lease abstracts according to their individual needs. There is also an option for human review of the extracted data if required. Users interested in streamlined lease administration, accelerated due diligence, and enhanced contract management may find significant value in LeaseLens.
