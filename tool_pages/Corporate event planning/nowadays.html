About Nowadays

Nowadays is an AI-powered platform designed to streamline corporate event planning. Its features enable users to negotiate and secure the ideal venues for their events via email and phone communication. The tool considers unique user requirements to find and suggest the best locations, activities, event venues, and hotels that match the criteria defined by the users. As a result, it facilitates the organization of events varying from offsite partner meet-ups to larger corporate gatherings. To assist users, the platform leans on AI technology to negotiate with venues on a global scale, aiming to ensure cost-effectiveness. It operates as an IATA certified AI travel agency, making the planning process efficient and time-conscious. The platform has been utilized by a diverse range of companies, from startups to larger corporations, to simplify the intricacies of event planning and negotiation. Despite the level of automation, it boasts offering uniquely tailored recommendations taking into account specific tastes and needs of individual companies. While the systems clear-cut, user-friendly interface makes event planning less complex, its broad-reaching and smart venue search capability ensures that events can be organized in diverse global locations according to user preferences. This tool is particularly useful for HR managers, event planners, and corporate executives looking to organize efficient, cost-effective corporate events.
