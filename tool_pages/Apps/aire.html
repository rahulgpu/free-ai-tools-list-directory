About Aire

Aire is an AI-powered, no-code application builder designed for building custom web applications for business processes and data management. Rather than traditional coding, applications are built from text prompts with the AI doing most of the heavy lifting in the initial development. Aire also provides a low-code environment, the Aire Hub, for users to add complexity, define roles and permissions, craft workflows, and set up third-party integrations. The platform is capable of building a wide range of management applications such as Customer Relationship Management (CRM), Asset Management, Project Management, Inventory Management, and many more. Applications built with Aire specialize in managing records for any type of organization in any industry, with examples ranging from Administrative Records to Volunteer Records. Aire is suitable for users across the full range of technical expertise, catering to non-IT citizen developers, no-coders, and businesses. However, please note that Aire builds records-based web apps and does not support the creation of apps such as chat/messaging apps, mobile apps, and user portals among others.
