About Appomate

Appomate is a platform that allows users to define, refine, and execute their unique AI automation needs using custom-built AI applications and agents. The platform functions by providing users with a suite of AI-powered tools that help them describe their specific requirements, which can vary from idea automation to workflow streamlining. The tool's dedicated AI team then processes these requests, setting up a comprehensive workflow that is adaptable to the user's preferences. Additionally, Appomate provides a flexible 'Try before you buy' framework, enabling users to test the apps or agents within a sandboxed environment to ensure optimum functionality before proceeding with payment. Post payment, the AI and development team creates and deploys the app or agent, which can then be accessed either from Appomate's dashboard or via a public URL. Appomate also ensures seamless integration with websites and promises free support. To encourage repeat usage and commissioning, a revenue-sharing feature is in place that provides users with a percentage of every sale generated from their commissioned app or agent.
