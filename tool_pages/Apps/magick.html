About Magick

Magick is a cloud-based software platform offering accessible and user-friendly AI tools to build and deploy AI applications and services without the need for code, infrastructure management or hosting model. The software offers built-in templates and simple drag-and-drop visual builder interfaces for easy creation and customization of AI components. Users can access a full suite of modern machine learning providers, including OpenAI, AI21, and Cohere through Magick Key to avoid vendor lock-in and switch between models as per business needs. The AI platform supports easy deployment of complex AI components across various services, making it highly adaptable and scalable. Designed to make AI development accessible to everyone, regardless of their technical background, the platform empowers experienced developers and novices. Additionally, users can deploy Magick Spells to multiple services such as Discord and Twitter to build apps that can talk across all channels. Magick is suitable for creating anything from simple chatbots to sophisticated multi-agent systems and generative pipelines. The platform's flexibility, powerful features, and user-friendly interface enable users to create world-class AI applications in just a few clicks, saving hours of time and effort.
