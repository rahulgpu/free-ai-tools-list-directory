About ZBrain

ZBrain is an enterprise generative AI platform specifically developed to improve business workflows, increase operational proficiency, and accelerate decision-making processes. Through the use of proprietary data, ZBrain aims to optimise business operation, allowing for a seamless and more productive execution of tasks across an organization. ZBrain provides a suite of AI tools catering to different needs. This includes AI-based regulatory monitoring, which delivers real-time insights into regulatory changes relevant to a business, thus mitigating compliance risks. Its AI Copilot for Sales generates summaries of deals, identifies issues, and suggests the next best actions. ZBrain's AI Research Solution enhances due diligence assessments, facilitating data-driven decisions. Finally, the platform's AI Customer Support Agent streamlines customer support processes, offering precise, multilingual assistance across multiple channels to reduce support ticket volumes.Beyond these, ZBrain extends its generative AI capabilities across several diverse industries, including retail, manufacturing, logistics, HR, real estate, legal, finance, healthcare, IT, hospitality, and automotive sectors. The platform's ability to streamline tasks and optimise workflows is intended to empower businesses to focus on their core priorities and achieve strategic objectives more efficiently.
