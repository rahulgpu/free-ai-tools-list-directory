About FormWise

FormWise is an AI-powered tool that enables end-users to design and build their custom AI prompts and forms without coding skills. It uses OpenAI's latest GPT language models to generate AI responses and integrate them with custom prompts, resulting in powerful forms that can be embedded anywhere. The tool offers a variety of use cases such as customer service, sales, and marketers. Users can build onboarding forms, digital marketing planners, Q&A generators, travel advisors, and more by customizing the prompts to match their industry needs easily. FormWise has three pricing plans: Lite, Creator, and Pro, starting with a free plan that includes ten responses. The pricing scales up as the use case increases, with the Pro plan offering unlimited AI responses/generations. The tool allows embedding forms on unlimited URLs, theme building, customization, webhook/API integration, and has a marketplace of AI templates for users to explore. FormWise's prompt builder course and knowledge base help users unlock their creativity to build forms with endless potential. The tool is known for its scalability, fully customizable prompts, enterprise-level features, and no OpenAI API key required, making it easy and intuitive to use. FormWise is powered by OpenAI, the world's most potent artificial intelligence language models and technology.
