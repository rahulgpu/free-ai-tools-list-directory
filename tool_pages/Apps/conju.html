About Conju

Conju provides an artificial intelligence (AI) solution designed to turn missed calls into opportunities for sales. It operates like a virtual assistant that works 24/7 to engage missed callers with personalized text responses, turning them into potential leads. Conju aims to ensure that no prospective customer inquiry falls through the cracks and enables businesses to generate sales from missed calls. When a call is missed, the AI receptionist sends a text to the caller and is capable of performing bookings on behalf of the business. Callers can arrange appointments and secure services via these texts. Beyond handling missed calls, Conju also qualifies leads, saving businesses from spending unnecessary time on leads that wont convert. The solution provides a chat terminal where businesses can monitor all conversations and intervene if necessary. Furthermore, Conju equips businesses with a feature to start SMS conversations directly from their website, offering continued engagement even after customers have left the site.
