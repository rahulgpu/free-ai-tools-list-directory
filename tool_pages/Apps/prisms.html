About Prisms

Prisms AI is a no-code platform designed to facilitate the creation of AI-powered applications. Instead of requiring deep knowledge of artificial intelligence (AI) or coding, Prisms allows users to 'stack' together data sources, user inputs, and pre-existing building blocks to make their apps. This versatility helps Prisms cater to a range of usage needs. An integral part of the Prisms creation process is writing AI promptsyet the platform is constructed with user-friendly features that simplify this aspect, making it more accessible to those without specific AI expertise. The tools that Prisms is built upon include Large Language models such as GPT3, DALL-E, and Stable Diffusion, which demonstrates the platform's serious AI credentials. Furthermore, Prisms offers pre-built user interfaces (UIs), meaning users can deploy their AI apps straight from the platform. Alternatively, users have the option to create their own frontend and simply use Prisms as the backend for their AI logic. Thus, whether you're deploying directly or using Prisms as an AI backend, the platform promises flexibility to suit a variety of creation contexts.
