About Retune

re:tune is a comprehensive platform designed for building AI applications without the need for coding. The platform facilitates the creation of custom AI applications to optimize various aspects of a business, from customer support to sales. Its core features include bespoke chatbot creation and training them with any conceivable data source, ranging from website-generated data to personalized customer information. Furthermore, re:tune can be integrated into a wide range of existing tools to enhance their functionality with AI capabilities. Its platform is intended to be as accessible as possible, with plans to match the needs of hobbyists, startups, small businesses, and larger agencies with unique security and performance demands. For flexibility and customizability, the platform supports unlimited messages and users, web crawling, file uploads, memory allocation for chatbots, and even custom white-labeled branding. The commitment to adaptability also extends to application integration and custom trained fine-tuned models. The platform promises early access to new features and prioritized feature requests as part of their offering. In summary, re:tune provides a user-friendly, efficient, and customizable solution for building AI applications that can help drive a business forward universally.
