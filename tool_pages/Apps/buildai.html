About Buildai

Build AI is a no-code platform designed to enable users to create AI web applications with a focus on nutritional planning services. This platform allows individuals, especially those without technical skills, to automate and customise their services. Notably, Build AI offers tools to generate individualised experiences for users, such as personalised meal plans sourced from user's own recipe collections. Another tool, known as 'Fridge Fusion', uses image recognition technology to identify food ingredients in refrigerator pictures and suggest recipes accordingly. Users can also create a 'MacroFIT Calculator' which provides estimates of daily macro goals. Additionally, Build AI provides options for monetization, enabling users to give exclusive access to paid members. Aside from the offered features, the platform also gives the flexibility to incorporate your own data and create new AI tools that align with your service needs. Another valuable feature would be the ability to understand audience trends by analysing tool usage. Lastly, the platform allows embedding AI-created applications directly onto the user's website, creating a seamless experience for website visitors. While the focus seems to heavily orient towards nutritional service applications, it's mentioned that AI tools for other topics can also be created.
