About Klu

Klu.ai is an all-in-one platform designed for AI teams to build, deploy, and optimize Generative AI applications. It allows users to easily design, develop, and iterate Generative AI features and apps using its intuitive interface called Klu Studio. The platform offers a range of integrations including connections to CRM systems, databases, knowledge bases, ticketing systems, and more.With Klu, AI teams can prototype and iterate over time, leveraging existing systems and data to create generative actions with on-demand context. The tool supports various features such as dynamic and generative prompts, GenAI chat with context and memory, connecting multiple actions together, and using external services through skills.Klu also provides an advanced data engine that allows users to build and scale on any major LLM (Large Language Model) providers. It offers insights into usage, cost, and performance and enables observation and improvement of features through state-of-the-art optimization techniques. The platform supports fine-tuning of models like Davinci-002, GPT-3.5 Turbo, and GPT-4 with user data, while ensuring data ownership and portability.Developers can benefit from Klu's developer-focused tooling for Python, TypeScript, and React UI, which accelerates development and offers flexibility in building and scaling AI applications. The platform caters to a wide range of use cases, including generative or analytical actions, ML policy prototyping, conversational chat and coaching experiences, and customer feedback analysis.Klu offers different pricing plans tailored to the needs of hobby projects, small teams, businesses, and enterprise-scale applications. The platform aims to empower AI engineers and teams to work more efficiently, iterate rapidly, and create AI systems that assist and enhance human productivity rather than replacing humans.
