About Lingtual

Lingtual is an AI tool that allows you to build AI business tools and chatbots. It enables you to connect these tools and chatbots to various applications and services that you already use, such as calendars, emails, company APIs, documents, and databases. The tool does not require coding, making it accessible to users without programming skills.With Lingtual, you can turn text into actionable commands and create tools to control, modify, and observe your business operations using your existing messaging platforms like Slack or SMS, or through a custom interface.The tool offers fast setup and supports OAuth2 for secure authentication. It supports APIs, custom tools, and messaging as primary building blocks, allowing you to integrate with internal or external REST APIs, databases, and other services. Lingtual includes built-in connectors for popular services like Gmail and Slack, or you can create custom connectors for your internal services.Lingtual provides a drag-and-drop interface for assembling your desired tools quickly. It offers features such as language models, vector stores, browsing tools, and more to enhance the functionality of your interface. The tool leverages the provided tools and APIs to answer your questions effectively.Enterprise users have the option to self-host Lingtual, offering full control and ensuring data security within their own infrastructure. The tool supports HIPAA compliance and offers self-hosted models for enhanced privacy.Lingtual offers a 1-month free trial, an always free tier, and enterprise features. It is designed to be adaptable, allowing users to build tailored solutions as long as data is available through APIs. Paid users can also call flows via Lingtual's API.
