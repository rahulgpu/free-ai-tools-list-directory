About Frontly

Frontly is a platform that empowers users to build AI-powered SaaS applications and internal tools without requiring any programming skills. With Frontly, users can create intuitive and highly functional apps with AI features, such as automating repetitive tasks and generating content. It also allows users to transition spreadsheets into visually appealing interfaces by utilizing tools such as Tables, Charts, and Forms. Frontly ensures data security by giving control over which data users can access, facilitated by granular access controls. Additionally, the platform enables users to personalize their applications with their own branding elements, including a custom logo, brand color, and domain. In terms of support, Frontly provides a community where users can network and share experiences. Users have praised Frontly for its user-friendly nature, intuitive drag-and-drop interface, and efficiency in building and deploying apps.
