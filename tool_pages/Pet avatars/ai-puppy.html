About AI Puppy

AI Puppy is a revolutionary mobile application designed to use artificial intelligence to generate unique images of dogs. The primary feature of this application involves creating up to a hundred and twenty distinctive photos of the user's dog from photos uploaded by the user. The process follows a simple three-step procedure. Step one involves the user uploading 10-20 images of their dog. The user then selects their preferred package in step two. The App's AI autonomously processes the images in step three, generating 120 new photos in an array of styles. Designed by Wonderlus and available on the App Store, this iOS-friendly application is compatible across several devices such as the iPhone, iPad, and Macs with the Apple M1 chip or later. Notably, this application holds a high regard for privacy as it has policies in place that prevent the linkage of user data to their identities.
