About Vibe AI

Vibe AI is an AI-powered cybersecurity tool designed to help protect and secure digital assets of businesses. The product features real-time alert notifications and developer-friendly APIs, aiding in the detection and vigilance of any suspicious activity or potential breaches. The tool constantly monitors app data and issues immediate alerts whenever suspicious activities are detected. It integrates AI-based algorithms to enhance data security and safeguard important business information. Furthermore, the easy-to-use API allows developers to swiftly and effortlessly integrate Vibe AI into their existing apps or systems, ensuring a wide-scale protection across platforms. Vibe AI operates on a subscription model and offers flexible plans to fit various business needs. In addition to providing a comprehensive security solution against malicious actors, it also supports businesses in adhering to industry standards and improves trust. Users can set up Vibe AI via a straightforward sign-up process.
