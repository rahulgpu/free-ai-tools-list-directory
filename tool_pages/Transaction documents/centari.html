About Centari

Centari is a secure legal AI platform designed to enhance the efficiency of legal teams involved in dealmaking. It combines secure language learning models (LLMs) with trusted legal precedents, offering a range of features to accelerate transaction processes. Users can access Centari through an intuitive platform, allowing them to draft documents without leaving Microsoft Word. The platform includes an extensive repository of precedent language and clauses, which can be easily searched using Centari Search, a powerful AI-powered search function. Additionally, users can activate Augmented Drafting to benefit from generative AI and enhance contract negotiations.Centari also facilitates knowledge sharing within legal teams through its cloud storage integrations, enabling the transformation of scattered documents into a searchable library of trusted precedents. The platform offers an efficient way to locate relevant information in lengthy agreements with its Document Q&A feature, eliminating the need for manual searching. Quick Compare redlining allows users to compare changes and identify issues quickly.Furthermore, Centari automates time-consuming tasks such as contract abstraction and high-volume document generation, enabling legal teams to focus on strategic work. Security is a priority, with Centari employing enterprise-grade measures to ensure privacy and confidentiality. Customer data is siloed and not shared with other users, and all data on the platform is encrypted at rest and in transit.Centari caters to a variety of users, including law firms, in-house legal teams, solo attorneys, freelancers, and students. Its generative AI capabilities offer valuable support for drafting contracts, improving deal outcomes, and increasing productivity.
