About Beducated

The Beducated AI Sex Coach is a conversational chatbot designed to provide information and advice on sex and relationships. Combining Artificial Intelligence (AI) with a vast library of courses on intimacy, the chatbot offers users actionable insights based on expert knowledge. The technology behind the AI Sex Coach incorporates Large Language Models (LLM), including Open AI's ChatGPT, to process user requests and generate responses. One of its distinguishing features is its data source, which is trained on thousands of lessons and educational materials, ensuring that the chatbot produces insightful responses that prioritize pleasure. User interactions with the Beducated AI Sex Coach are completely confidential, and data is not stored for marketing purposes. The chat history and responses are only stored on the user's local device and remain inaccessible to intermediaries, including Beducated. However, it is worth noting that while the chatbot is trained on reliable information from sexual wellness and health experts, it is still in the prototype stage and may occasionally generate inaccurate responses, commonly referred to as "hallucinations." Therefore, it is recommended to consult with a healthcare professional before implementing any suggestions provided by the chatbot.Currently, the AI Sex Coach is free to use, with no registration required. Non-paying users are limited to 10 messages per day, while subscribers of Beducated have unlimited access. It's important to note that the AI Sex Coach is not intended to replace the guidance of a sex coach or therapist, but rather to provide additional information and support.
