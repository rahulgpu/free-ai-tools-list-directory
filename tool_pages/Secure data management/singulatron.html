About Singulatron

Singulatron is an on-premise AI software tool that focuses on data privacy and security. It is developed to run AI models locally on a user's PC or laptop, ensuring all data remains on-device and private. The platform offers comprehensive data protection measures, including role-based access control which allows permissions for data access to be precisely defined and managed. Singulatron provides a vast library of AI models that users can seamlessly use for different tasks, all within the platform. Another feature of Singulatron is its capability to provide private chat functions where no conversations leave the user's computer, offering an added layer of data security.In addition to on-premise AI capabilities, Singulatron offers a business search feature built exclusively for corporate use. This feature helps users to navigate the internet using query capabilities that are enhanced by AI. It allows industry-specific searches which can be further refined by criteria such as business size, location, and industry. This feature can be beneficial for sales teams, market researchers, B2B marketers, and investors who want in-depth information about businesses.Singulatron presents an opportunity for users who want to enter the world of AI, offering a secure and user-friendly platform to operate a personal AI on their local devices. In addition to these, the platform also enables users to explore the latest and best AI models, making cutting-edge AI technology more accessible and secure.
