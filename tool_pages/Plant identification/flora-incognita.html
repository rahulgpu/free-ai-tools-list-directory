About Flora incognita

The Flora Incognita App is an AI-driven mobile application that helps simplify the process of plant species identification. Leveraging the potential of machine learning and artificial intelligence, the app is capable of recognizing an extensive array of plant species. Beyond identifying plant species, the app allows users to save their observations, contributing valuable data to scientific endeavors. In addition, the application provides a rich set of facts regarding each plant species, including salient characteristics, protection status, and distribution patterns. The app is entirely free of charge and devoid of invasive advertisements. It also functions efficiently even without a continuous internet connection, making it eminently suitable for educational use in various settings - schools, universities, and nature conservation initiatives.A noteworthy aspect of this tool is its incorporation of citizen science. It allows users to learn and contribute at the same time, creating a community engaged in the expansion of common knowledge. The researchers project, 'Flora Incognita++', is an extension of this endeavor that further combines AI plant identification with citizen science initiatives.
