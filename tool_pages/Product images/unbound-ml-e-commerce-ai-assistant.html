About Unbound ML E-commerce AI Assistant

Unbound ML's E-commerce AI Assistant is designed to automate the management and growth of online businesses. It provides AI tools intended to enhance efficiency and save time for business owners and teams. Various business operations catered by the platform include outreach, ad campaign creation, content calendar building, product description writing, SEO optimization, and e-mail campaign management among others. A key feature of this tool is the Product Photo Generator. Users can upload a picture of their product or subject with the background removed, choose or describe a desired environment as the new background, and the AI will provide high-quality, unique product pictures. Such images can be used in digital marketing, social media and websites. Unbound ML also integrates with business data, analyzing trends to provide insights aimed at revenue growth. Available in three pricing plans: Free, Plus and Pro, the tool can be customised with specific features depending on the needs - be it individuals, professionals or larger teams. The tool can be integrated with applications such as Slack, Google, Zapier, Shopify, Discord, Stripe, and Amazon.
