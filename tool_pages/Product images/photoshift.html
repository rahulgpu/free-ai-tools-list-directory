About Photoshift

Photoshift is a commercial AI tool that enhances product presentation by seamlessly blending products into any stock image or mid-journey scene, resulting in strikingly realistic visuals. The tool offers instantaneous transformations with just one click, eliminating the need for prompts. The process of using Photoshift is simple: users can upload a background scene where they want to feature their product and add the product image, and Photoshift automatically removes the background. By clicking "shift," the product is instantly merged into the uploaded scene. Photoshift is tailored for various types and designs of product images, ensuring a hassle-free experience customized to individual needs. It seamlessly integrates with any chosen stock backdrop, allowing for a seamless and customizable user experience. The tool also effortlessly adjusts to preferred angles and sizes, enabling users to present products in the most visually appealing way. Photoshift offers different pricing plans, including a free account with three welcome credits, a starter plan for $20 per month with 50 credits, and a standard plan for $99 per month with 400 credits. The tool supports resolutions up to 1024x1024 and offers upscaling up to 2x. Customer support is available, and users can contact the team via email. Photoshift grants users full ownership and a commercial license over their photos. A trial period is available with the free credits, and subscriptions can be canceled at any time. Templates and pre-set backdrops are provided to assist users in getting started quickly.
