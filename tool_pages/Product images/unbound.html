About Unbound

The AI Product Photography tool offered by Unbound ML allows users to generate high-definition product photos with AI backgrounds of their choice. It offers a three-step process for creating these photos. First, users need to upload a high-definition photo of the product with the background removed. Then, users can either select a preset scene or describe their own desired environment for placing the subject in. Finally, users can generate endless unique variations of their product photos, which can be used in digital ads, social media, and websites.Unbound ML provides different pricing plans for users to choose from. The Free plan is suitable for those who want to try out the tool and experiment with AI features. The Explorer plan is designed for individuals working on small projects, offering additional features like live search and dedicated support. The Plus plan is targeted at individuals and teams who need increased productivity, with higher upload limits and team features. For enterprise-level users, Unbound ML offers custom pricing based on specific requirements, with additional benefits such as secure company data, integrations, and unlimited users.Overall, Unbound ML's AI Product Photography tool aims to provide an efficient and versatile solution for generating high-quality product photos with customizable backgrounds, catering to a range of individual and business needs.
