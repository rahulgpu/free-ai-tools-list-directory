About Flair

Flair is an AI-powered design tool for branded content. It enables users to create visually stunning content with just a few clicks. With a drag and drop interface, users can upload their own branded assets onto a flexible canvas and build a scene around their product with the help of accessories. Flair also features smart prompt recommendations and an autocomplete feature to help users communicate with the AI. Tutorials are also available to help users learn how to make the most of the tool. Furthermore, users can explore examples of user-generated content and watch a demo video to quickly get started. Flair is currently in private beta and offers a free trial.
