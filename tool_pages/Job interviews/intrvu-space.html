About Intrvu Space

Intrvu Space is an Artificial Intelligence (AI) based tool aimed at streamlining the interviewing and reporting processes in personnel recruitment and evaluation. This solution lets you conduct fully automated interviews; it uses AI interviewers that can conduct interviews in an unbiased and standardized manner, thus potentially bridging gaps in human decision making. Moreover, Intrvu Space does not merely stop at conducting the interviews, it also generates comprehensive reports based on each interview conducted. These reports can provide a robust and objective scrutiny of each candidate, which can aid in making informed recruitment or staff evaluation decisions. Intrvu Space hence appears to offer a multi-prong approach to automate and enhance the objectivity of the interview process and subsequent reporting.
