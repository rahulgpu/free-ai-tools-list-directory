About NoteMD

NoteMD is an AI Medical Scribe designed to streamline documentation processes within medical practices. It's fully HIPAA compliant, offering secure and efficient functionality. The tool aims to reduce time spent on paperwork by automatically transcribing healthcare visits, generating notes, and preparing follow-up instructions. These documents can be adjusted to match a provider's writing style and can be reviewed and edited before being transferred to electronic health record (EHR) systems. NoteMD also facilitates direct patient-provider interactions, eliminating the distraction of note-taking during consultations. Further, its advanced technology strives to enhance the accuracy of medical correspondences by capturing every detail of patient encounters. It is accessible across all devices and focuses not only on efficiency, but also on improving the quality of patient care by allowing physicians to focus more on their patients rather than on record-keeping tasks. NoteMD is trusted by a wide range of cliniciansincluding pediatricians, chiropractors, orthopedists, therapists, ophthalmologists, and primary care physiciansfor its seamless integration into their workflows and the accuracy and detail of its transcriptions.
