About Document Crunch

Document Crunch is an AI-assisted platform designed to simplify and streamline contract review in the construction industry. Notably, the tool helps in the identification of critical risks within construction contracts, thus reducing the time usually spent on contract reviews. With this system, construction professionals can become empowered with a thorough comprehension of their contracts, ensuring clarity and eliminating guesswork with regards to contract specifics. The platform provides a 'Project Playbook', a condensed reference guide designed to demystify legal language and make contract terms easily understandable. It also offers a standardized review process intended to make contract review consistent, measurable, and repeatable. Additionally, Document Crunch includes a 'Chat with Your Contract' feature, which presumably provides interactive contract understanding and clarification. The platform integrates with other key software in the construction industry, like Procore, enhancing its collaborative capabilities. Document Crunch assists in advancing contract risk review and compliance in construction projects, which contributes to more effective project management and potential profitability protection.
