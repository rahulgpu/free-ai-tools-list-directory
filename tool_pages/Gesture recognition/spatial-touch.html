About Spatial Touch

Spatial Touch is an application that enables users to control their digital devices using hand gestures, ensuring remote control without physical contact with the screen. The AI-driven hand gesture remote control application supports media apps such as YouTube, Netflix, Disney Plus, Instagram Reels, Tiktok and other apps. The utility of Spatial Touch is especially notable when the user might not want to touch their device, such as during meals, when their hands are wet, or simply for convenience. Some key features include air gestures for controlling media playback, volume adjustment, navigation, scrolling, and more. It offers remote control capability at a distance and includes state of the art gesture recognition technology to minimize inaccurate gesture detections. The app also has a 'background auto-start' feature enabling it to get activated and run automatically whenever supported apps like YouTube or Netflix are launched. Concerning security, the app reassures that while it uses the device camera, it does not store or transmit any images or videos external to the device. Spatial Touch grows more accommodating with time, planning to add more apps to its compatible list in the future.
