About RikiGPT

RikiGPT is an AI-powered tool designed primarily for academic research and writing. Leveraging a comprehensive database of scholarly sources, it enables users to access a wide range of academic references. It offers advanced writing features, providing deep, thorough, and highly detailed writings for research purposes. Users can customize their research according to specific reference lists and study the most relevant and influential documents related to their field of interest. RikiGPT incorporates the latest GPT technology, enabling it to produce cutting-edge writings. To streamline the research process, users can simply input their requirements, including topic, research question, writing level, and language, and the tool will generate a full academic piece with references. It includes in-text citations and bibliographies in multiple styles such as APA, MLA, and Chicago. In addition to research papers, RikiGPT offers tailored writing services, allowing users to create customized outlines perfectly fit for their research needs. It can deliver continuous and connected paragraphs forming full-length chapters, accommodating comprehensive research projects. Furthermore, RikiGPT provides an automatic translation feature, making it accessible to a global audience. The tool is intuitive, making research writing easier and more convenient. It caters to a variety of disciplines and can be employed by academics, students, writers, and researchers in different parts of the world.
