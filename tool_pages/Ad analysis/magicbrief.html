About MagicBrief

MagicBrief is an AI tool designed to assist content creators, video editors, motion designers, and clients in their creative workflow. The tool provides the capability to save ad references from Facebook, Instagram, and TikTok ads, break them down into pieces, and rework them in one's own storyboards. Additionally, MagicBrief offers the ability to build a library of winning creatives, create unlimited storyboards, and collaborate with team members in real-time. The tool provides support for various export options, including images, videos, scripts, and subtitles.One feature of MagicBrief is the MagicAI beta, which analyzes video ads and highlights winning keyframes to help users build a viral ad of their own. The tool also includes an automatic asset chop up feature to break existing ads into their main scenes, ready to be reworked into new variants. MagicBoard allows for the creation of unlimited storyboards, complete with captions, soundtrack and visual recommendations, reference links, videos, and images to each frame to make it easier for creators and video editors to collaborate effectively.The tool offers different pricing models, including a free viewer option, a $29/month standard user plan with access to all features, and a custom enterprise plan. MagicBrief is used by leading brands and agencies, and users have reported that the tool has transformed their creative planning and execution processes. MagicBrief's Chrome extension enables users to save ads from the Facebook Ads Library and the TikTok Creative Center to get access to more data about the ads while keeping their data safe and secure. MagicBrief is a sophisticated tool that empowers content creators to add magic to their creative workflow.
