About Taplio

Taplio is a comprehensive AI-fuelled tool designed to enhance personal branding efforts on LinkedIn. It offers an array of features aimed at making LinkedIn use more effective and efficient. One of its key offerings is the AI-Powered Content Creation feature, which aids the user in ideating, improving, and creating LinkedIn posts, including posts and carousels. It also offers inspiration by providing access to a database of viral posts. Taplios Scheduling function allows users to plan and schedule posts, including those containing text, images, videos, and carousels, and preview them before they go live. It also provides advanced options to boost post performance. For growing, nurturing, and converting LinkedIn networks, Taplio facilitates interaction with post-likers and commenters, and assists with bulk Direct Messaging. It provides access to a database of enriched LinkedIn leads that can be used for networking and business development. Taplio incorporates an analytics suite that monitors key performance indicators (KPIs) and provides insights on what is working and what can be improved. There is also a Chrome extension that provides direct analytics and high-performing post suggestions, straight on LinkedIn. Taplio serves the multiple needs of professionals, creators and agencies looking to enhance their LinkedIn presence and personal brand.
