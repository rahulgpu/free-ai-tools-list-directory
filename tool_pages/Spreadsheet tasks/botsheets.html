About Botsheets

Botsheets is an AI tool designed to automate lead generation and customer interaction processes through the creation of AI chatbots that read and capture data in Google Sheets. This no-code tool enables the generation of personalized responses through querying of user data, along with capturing leads and insights by extracting data from conversations and analyzing through AI. It offers the capability to understand and respond in multiple languages, and integrates easily with external platforms through widgets, embedded chat, or direct links. Users can customize chatbots to match their brand, set engagement rules, and ensure privacy by controlling the data shared with the AI. It keeps AI in sync with any changes to your datasets, providing a dynamic, responsive system for managing and delivering customer interaction. It also offers features such as multilingual compatibility, reading and writing to spreadsheets, auto-sync of data sources, and customizable rules of chatbot engagement. Furthermore, Botsheets allows for seamless integration into a website or standalone application via direct sharing of links, embedding into a page, or connecting with their API.
