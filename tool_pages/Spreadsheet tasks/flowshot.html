About Flowshot

Flowshot is an AI plugin for Google Sheets that uses artificial intelligence to streamline work within the sheet, automate tasks, generate images and even create custom AI models. Flowshot utilizes AI prompts which can be implemented in any part of Google Sheets. These prompts form instructions that can be as specific or as generic as needed, and can include queries, references to cell data, or replication of writing styles. Flowshot also offers an Autocomplete feature that can instruct the AI to perform repetitive tasks, enabling large scale operations. Bulk copywriting, information extraction, and simultaneous text based tasks are made easier through Flowshot. Additionally, Flowshot provides the ability to train custom AI models which are bespoke, scalable, and integrate easily with Autocomplete, Zapier, and API. These models are generated from user guide data and custom tuned to specific tasks for efficiency. Lastly, Flowshot has the capability to generate Google Sheets formulas and AI images from simple short descriptions. This plugin is adaptive in nature, making it highly suitable for organizations of all sizes for diverse tasks.
