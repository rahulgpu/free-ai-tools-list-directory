About Cargo

Cargo for Sheets is an AI-powered tool designed to optimize and automate tasks within Google Sheets. Offering the ability to work with pre-built templates, the tool focuses predominantly on sales, marketing and content creation functions. It enables users to manipulate and generate content such as sales emails, follow-ups, Google Ads copy, and blog posts, bringing a level of scalability and efficiency to these areas. The AI capabilities of the tool allow it to take user inputs and automatically populate the spreadsheet, significantly reducing manual data entry and streamlining business processes. Produced by GetCargo Inc, Cargo for Sheets is available as a free addition to the Google Workspace, connecting directly to your Google account. User reviews and further information can be found within the Google Workspace Marketplace.
