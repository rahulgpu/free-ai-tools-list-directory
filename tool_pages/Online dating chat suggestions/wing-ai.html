About Wing AI

Wing AI is an AI-powered tool designed to assist users in initiating and maintaining engaging conversations on dating apps. The tool uses AI technology to generate flirty and engaging opening messages based on the text and images from screenshots of other users' bio or profile captured on any dating app. To use it, users capture a screenshot of another user's bio, upload it onto Wing AI, and the AI system crafts a message based on this information. In other words, it carefully checks out all the text and even recognizes what's in the photo, then uses it to craft fun, engaging responses that aim to amplify user's chats. Users may also press 'Generate More' for additional lines. Wing AI can be used with any dating or chat platform that uses text or pictures.
