About Xspiral

Xspiral is a hybrid design visualization tool that incorporates advanced 3D modeling and 2D sketching capabilities, facilitating seamless collaboration and real-time visualization. This platform allows for the creation, management, and sharing of 3D work, and integrates familiar 2D design principles to help designers with 3D tasks. Xspiral provides additional features to bolster productivity, comprising intelligent writing tools, mind mapping, and the assisted creation of workflow applications. The platform allows for efficient team collaboration with real-time online editing and built-in comment features, promoting the timely and streamlined exchange of ideas. Xspiral offers support for the designing of VR/AR applications using built-in components and resources. Users can publish creations effortlessly as 3D websites, with options available for image exporting, video recording, and 3D file conversion. Notably, no coding knowledge is required to fully utilize these capabilities. Xspiral targets a broad user base, including UX/UI designers, product managers, educators, and freelancers, among others. The platform supports a wide range of tasks and activities, such as prototyping, brainstorming, teamwork, animation design, website production, and the creation of marketing materials. This reinforces the potential of Xspiral as a comprehensive tool that can address various creative and collaborative needs in a single workspace.
