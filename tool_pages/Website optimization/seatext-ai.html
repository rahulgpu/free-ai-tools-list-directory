About Seatext AI

Seatext AI is an advanced tool aimed at improving the effectiveness of a website in terms of sales and user engagement. It harnesses AI technology to auto-craft multiple text variants and use A/B testing to determine the best-performing versions for your target audience. In doing so, it personalises and optimises your website's text content for every visitor, leading to potentially improved conversion rates. Furthermore, Seatext AI boasts an automatic translation feature, capable of localising a website's content into 107 different languages. This enables businesses to effortlessly reach global traffic, improving SEO in the process. Aside from text generation and translation, Seatext also focuses on content personalisation. It uses visitor data (from sources like LinkedIn profiles, CRM records, and chat transcripts) to rewrite the website content for each visitor based on their specific language, industry, and company needs. This ensures that the website content is tailored specifically for every individual user, offering a personalised online experience to the visitors. To summarise, Seatext AI focuses on enhancing website performance and conversion through personalised content, multi-language translation, and A/B testing.
