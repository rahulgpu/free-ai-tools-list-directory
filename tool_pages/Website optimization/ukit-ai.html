About uKit AI

uKit AI is an artificial intelligence tool that upgrades websites to the latest trends quickly and easily in just 10 minutes. The system analyzes thousands of SMB websites and identifies what works and what doesn't to implement the best practices for your website all at once, fixing all typical mistakes along the way. With uKit AI, users can get a free design and technology upgrade that includes HTTPS, mobile-friendly layout, easy-to-use CMS, SEO tools, responsive layouts, unique and attractive design, and HTML5 and CSS3. The AI system provides a simple and powerful CMS with over 30 tools for business optimization. The tool is easy to use and the process involves giving uKit AI the link to the website, sitting back while the service analyzes the website and creates an upgraded version, reviewing the new website version on the uKit CMS, and finally publishing it at the custom domain or playing around with its look. uKit Group is a leader in DIY website builders in Russia and CIS.
