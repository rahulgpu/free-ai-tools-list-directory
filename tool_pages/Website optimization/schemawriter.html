About Schemawriter

Schemawriter.ai is an AI-based software platform designed for optimizing webpage elements through automated schema and content generation to enhance on-page SEO. To do this, it relies on a target search term and a URL. Based on these inputs, it generates lists of Google-recognized entities and produces optimized schema files and content. Schemawriter.ai uses a variety of external APIs, including several from Google, in conjunction with complex mathematical algorithms to manufacture entity lists and content correlated with high rankings in Google. The entities, all recognized by Google, are populated in the schema along with their corresponding Wikipedia description, page URL, Wikidata page URL, and Google knowledge panel URL. This interconnectedness aids Google's comprehension of your webpage's topic. The software is equipped with an intuitive, user-friendly interface that enables the generation and editing of advanced schemas within minutes and without the need for manual schema code edits. It also offers the capability to analyze competitor entities, create advanced webpage schemas, identify missing entities on your website and produce complex schemas, including but not limited to local business schema, FAQ schema, Product schema, and Organization schema.
