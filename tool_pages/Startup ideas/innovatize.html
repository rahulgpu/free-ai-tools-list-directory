About Innovatize

Innovatize is an AI-powered platform designed to assist users in generating personalized startup ideas based on their interests and experiences. The user experience starts with filling out a short form detailing their interests, experiences and preferences. Once this is complete, the platform's AI analyzes this data, using it to produce startup ideas uniquely tailored to the user's profile. The generated ideas are then emailed to the user, providing them convenience and privacy. Innovatize is particularly useful for individuals or groups who are finding it difficult to formulate viable startup concepts. The platform aims to be a valuable tool in the initial stages of the startup journey, serving to inspire and guide budding entrepreneurs towards developing ideas that align with their passions, skills and experiences.
