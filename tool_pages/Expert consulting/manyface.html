About Manyface

Manyface is a tool designed to provide affordable access to high-quality expert consultation for small and medium enterprises as well as individuals. Addressing the issue of expensive consultancy fees, this AI-powered application offers vital services at a fraction of the market cost. Manyface employs a variety of expert AI consultants that guide users, providing advice ranging from lifestyle to career decisions. The tool uses an innovative approach, the Mixtures of Experts (MoE) model, to optimize for the best expert for each consultation task, in turn boosting quality and efficiency. Their MoE model is being fine-tuned to surpass traditional Long Lifetime Models in performance and resource utilization. Additionally, Manyface offers services such as personalized videos, voice cloning, and video translation. The Manyface team consists of AI researchers and engineering professionals associated with Cambridge University and leading tech firms such as AWS, Microsoft, and Google. The platform is designed with a focus on augmenting decision-making capabilities and presents a robust AI consultancy app utilized by a growing user base.
