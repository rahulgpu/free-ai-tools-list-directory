About Atlas.org

Atlas is an AI-based assistant designed to help students improve in their academic performances by providing support based on their course materials. The tool is designed to act as a copilot for students, not only assisting with academic tasks but also aiming to reduce the stress often associated with studying and course work. Atlas is adept at a range of tasks including homework assistance, studying support, writing aid, review and summarization. This AI assistance extends to a variety of subjects, as seen in examples involving economics, sociology, business administration, writing skills and more. Besides providing answers to specific coursework problems, it is also capable of creating tailored study guides and offering key insights for coursework texts and narratives on missed lecture content. Its functions also include assisting in creating drafts for specific assignments, offering insightful analyses on case studies, and helping provide quick summaries on readings. Atlas' range of capabilities, accuracy, and its training based on personal class materials make it a comprehensive AI assistant for students across divergent fields of study.
