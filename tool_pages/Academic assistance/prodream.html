About ProDream

ProDream is an AI-powered academic writing assistant intended to improve speed and writing skills, specifically designed for academic texts. The tool provides comprehensive assistance, from drafting and editing to citation and proofreading. It aims to streamline the essay writing process by organizing scattered thoughts, promoting clear and organized writing, simplifying citations, and preventing plagiarism. It offers a 'Continue Writing' feature to help students craft suitable sentences when they are stuck in the middle of writing. The 'Auto Cite' feature automatically generates relevant sources and adds accurate in-text and reference citations according to specified citation style. The tool also checks for potential plagiarism, providing paraphrasing tools to ensure the originality of the submitted papers. It provides a database of scholarly publications for reference purposes, ensuring the novelty of students' research. The tool aids in overcoming language barriers by offering multilingual support, allowing users to write in their native language and then adapting the text to English. ProDream ensures content components meet academic style and requirement compliance. Developed by a team of experts from renowned universities, it is utilized by academic writers worldwide.
