About ClipDrop Replace Background

ClipDrop Replace Background is an AI-powered tool that can teleport anything, anywhere, with incredible accuracy. It can remove backgrounds, remove objects and text, relight images, upscale images, generate images from text, and remove text from images. All of this is done without any complex image editing software. It also has an app for both iOS and Android, as well as plugins for Photoshop and Figma. The tool is easy to use: just drop your image and describe where you want it to be, and the AI will do the rest. With ClipDrop Replace Background, the only limit for your creativity is your imagination.
