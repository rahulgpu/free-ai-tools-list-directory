About ContractCrab

ContractCrab is an online AI-powered tool designed for summarizing contracts. It extracts key points from lengthy legal documents, providing users a quick and convenient summary. This tool enables users to make well-informed decisions and saves time by significantly reducing the effort spent on reviewing comprehensive contract details manually. Using AI technology, ContractCrab swiftly extracts and presents essential details from contracts in a structured and clear format. Security is a key aspect of the service. It operates peer-to-peer arrangements, eliminating intermediaries, logging, or human factors. User privacy is prioritized, and users have full control over who can access their Contract Crab account. The tool is able to process information swiftly and turns unstructured data into organized summaries. It supports multiple file formats for contract uploads including .docx, .txt, and .pdf. The summarized contract results are then sent to the user's email for ease of access and review.
