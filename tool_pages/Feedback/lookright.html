About LookRight

LookRight is an AI tool that provides instant, intelligent feedback on images using advanced computer vision technology. It serves as a second pair of eyes, helping users evaluate and enhance their visual content. Users can choose from a range of prompts, such as "Does this look right?", "Rate my outfit", "Roast this!", "Say something inspiring", "Complete my look", or "Write a product caption".To use LookRight, users simply need to click on the camera icon and upload the desired image. The tool then leverages cutting-edge computer vision algorithms to analyze the content and provide feedback based on the chosen prompt. By utilizing AI techniques, LookRight offers a quick and objective evaluation of visual elements.LookRight.ai BETA aims to assist users in various contexts, such as fashion, design, photography, or social media content creation. It leverages the power of AI to provide constructive criticism, inspiration, or guidance in the form of captions. The tool's ability to generate intelligent feedback instantly saves users the time and effort of seeking external opinions or manually analyzing their images.Overall, LookRight is a valuable tool for anyone seeking a reliable AI-powered solution to get objective feedback and improve the quality and impact of their visual content.
