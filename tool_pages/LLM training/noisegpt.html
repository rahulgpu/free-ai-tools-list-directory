About NoiseGPT

NoiseGPT is a cutting-edge generative artificial intelligence tool that offers decentralized AI capabilities without any censorship. It allows users to train and run noiseGPT models while ensuring freedom of speech and avoiding hidden biases. The tool includes various features such as hyper-realistic text-to-speech generation, dialogue bots for realistic conversations, and single-shot voice cloning. NoiseGPT integrates into different platforms, offering web applications, Telegram bots (both free and premium), a Twitter bot, and a Discord integration. It also has plans for an Android/iPhone app and an API for future use. The tool has gained attention in the media for its ability to turn text into celebrity voices and its potential impact on deepfake technology. The noiseGPT token plays a central role in the ecosystem by ensuring value accrual and fostering sustainable growth. Token integration allows for dynamic fee structures, governance, and control over the ecosystem. The token is available on Uniswap V2 (Ethereum) and Uniswap V3 (Arbitrum), with liquidity locked and distribution fairly launched. A 5% tax is active on Ethereum until December 2023. The token is listed on CoinGecko and CoinMarketCap.NoiseGPT prides itself on its commitment to total freedom of use, freedom of speech, and resistance against hidden biases and censorship.
