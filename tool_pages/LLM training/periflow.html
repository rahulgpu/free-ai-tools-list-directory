About PeriFlow

FriendliAI is a leading provider of a Language Learning Model (LLM) serving engine designed specifically for generative AI. Their primary objective is to aid clients in deploying their large language models with higher efficiency. Generative AI, enabled via FriendliAI, serves as the backbone for the creation of complex AI models that are capable of predicting or generating new content based on the input they receive. This caters to numerous applications across diverse sectors, from chatbots and virtual assistants to content creation and more. Their LLM serving engine streamlines the process to boost productivity, implying a significant reduction in time and resources typically dedicated to this task. As a result, organizations can focus on fine-tuning their AI solutions, moving swiftly from production to implementation. By fuelling innovation and simplifying the complex process of managing AI models, FriendliAI has positioned itself as a major player in the AI domain.
