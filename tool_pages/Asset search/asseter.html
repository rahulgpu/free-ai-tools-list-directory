About Asseter

Assets Scout by asseter.ai is an AI-powered tool designed to streamline the process of finding assets by picture across various 3D stocks. With Scout, users are able to easily assemble a styleframe by quickly searching for and selecting assets that match their desired image. This tool supports different search methods, allowing users to drop an image, paste it from the clipboard, or browse for one. Once an image is provided, Scout utilizes its AI capabilities to locate assets that are visually similar to the given image.The search functionality of Scout is seamlessly integrated with continuously updated 3D stock platforms such as 3ddd.ru, 3dsky.org, CGTrader, and Quixel's Megascans. This ensures that users have access to a wide range of high-quality assets to choose from. By leveraging the power of AI, Scout simplifies the asset scouting process, making it more efficient and convenient for users.It's important to note that this website, created by the asseter.ai development team, aims to facilitate asset scouting within the CG community and does not seek any commercial benefits.
