About ACCELQ

ACCELQ is an AI-powered Codeless Test Automation tool designed to facilitate and accelerate continuous testing in software development. It's a versatile platform capable of managing tests for various environments including web, mobile, and API. ACCELQ offers a unified platform for comprehensive quality management across multiple tech stacks. It transcends traditional coding barriers with its 'no-code' feature, which makes it capable of handling complex real-world tests while remaining intuitive and scalable. In addition to its AI-empowered test automation, its capabilities extend to packaged apps automation through its transformational technology, ACCELQ LIVE, designed specifically for cloud and packaged app testing and automation. It further provides unified management for the entire test design lifecycle. ACCELQ integrates seamlessly into contemporary Agile development environments while empowering users to manage and maintain quality throughout the development process. Training and certification programs are also available for users to enhance their skills on the platform.
