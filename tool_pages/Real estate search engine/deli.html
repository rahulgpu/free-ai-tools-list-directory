About DELI

DELI is an AI-powered tool that transforms the process of finding homes for real estate clients. By offering instant access to multiple listings, neighborhood insights, and comprehensive property details, DELI aims to accelerate home buying decisions. It is designed to match clients' criteria with suitable home listings and equip agents with in-depth neighborhood data and insights. The AI tool integrates with MLS listings and scans these extensive real estate data sources in just a few seconds, pulling detailed property and neighborhood analytics that would typically take hours to research manually. It includes features such as free-form text search, lifestyle search, AI-powered property drip campaigns, and expanded criteria to search. The primary goal of DELI is to automate tedious tasks like searching through listings and researching property details, enabling real estate agents to focus more on building client relationships and closing deals. The data sourced by DELI is consistently up-to-date, ensuring that new listings, pricing, days on market, and listing status are refreshed in real-time to better serve clients. Moreover, DELI provides the latest community data, keeping users informed about new openings and developments.
