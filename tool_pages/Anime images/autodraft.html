About AutoDraft

AutoDraft is a generative AI tool designed to assist with visual storytelling and help bring creative ideas to life. The tool utilizes advanced techniques such as text-to-image, image-to-image and AI-paint to generate stunning visuals that are consistent with natural language descriptions. With AutoDraft, users can easily generate multiple images in seconds and modify small details or entire visual features on any image. Text-to-image functionality allows users to describe desired images in natural language and seamlessly blend different artistic styles to produce stunning visual masterpieces. Image-to-image optimization is made easy with the help of AI in-painting, which allows users to erase unwanted objects or elements from the picture, with the AI rendering new content in the empty space. Users can also easily edit images with AI paint and transform any image to a customized version with subtle tweaks or dramatic modifications.Overall, AutoDraft is a powerful tool for any creative workflow and offers users complete creative control to produce exciting comics and other visual content. The tool is engineered for speed and accuracy and is designed to assist with character consistency, ensuring that images are generated with consistent characters. Users can easily create an account and take a tour of the product to see how AutoDraft can revolutionize their creative process.
