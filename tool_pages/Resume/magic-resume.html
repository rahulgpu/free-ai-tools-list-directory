About Magic-Resume

Magic-Resume is an AI-powered tool that facilitates the creation of optimized professional resumes. By tailoring each CV to the user's unique skills, it aims to enhance their chances of securing their dream job. The tool avoids the time-consuming nature of manual resume creation and the generic approaches of conventional resume builders. Instead, it uses AI to enrich basic information about individual's achievements and knowledge, ensuring a perfect final result that adheres to best practices, uses correct keywords, and meets industry standards. It provides for an efficient, cost-effective solution, with users able to create an optimized resume in just approximately 10 minutes. The tool is also designed to be ATS-friendly, meaning it optimizes resumes for Applicant Tracking Systems, increasing their visibility to employers. A fundamental feature of this tool is its user-friendly interface, guiding users through each step of the resume creation process. Another essential feature is the instant PDF generation, which allows users to easily produce a clean and professionally formatted copy of their resume. In terms of pricing, there are different plans available, including a free option, which alters in terms of the number of professional CVs and AI-powered enhancements available. The top-tier plan also offers lifetime access to edit and download.
