About Blackshark

Blackshark.ai is an AI-powered platform that generates a real-time, accurate, and photorealistic 3D digital twin of the entire planet. This platform utilizes satellite data and artificial intelligence to extract insights about the planet's infrastructure from current satellite and aerial imagery. It enriches the data using AI to create a photorealistic, geo-typical, or asset-specific digital twin.The generated digital twin can be used for various purposes such as visualization, simulation, mapping, and mixed reality environments. The platform offers a massive cloud-computing capability, enabling rapid updates at any time.One of the key features of Blackshark.ai is SYNTH3D, a synthetic, realistic 3D replica of the Earth's surface. This includes terrain elevation, buildings, vegetation coverage, and infrastructure. It provides a region-specific, geo-typical representation of every country, city, building, and detail on the entire globe.The platform also offers digital airports, powered by specialized airport generator technology. This technology recreates airports with high accuracy, including runways, taxiways, airport lights, buildings, hangars, towers, obstacles, and more. The digital airports can be used for night scene and weather simulations, as well as sensor simulations.Additionally, Blackshark.ai provides synthetic training data, which includes regionally specific, geo-referenced, synthetic training images for machine learning. These training images come with necessary annotations for ML-ops integration and neural network training. The platform offers efficient training for AV edge cases, UAV geo-intelligence, and remote sensing.In summary, Blackshark.ai is a comprehensive platform that utilizes AI and satellite data to generate a realistic 3D digital twin of the planet. It offers various solutions for visualization, simulation, training, and analysis in industries like aviation, urban planning, insurance, and more.
