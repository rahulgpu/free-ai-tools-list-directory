About Blur On

BlurOn is a plug-in specifically designed for After Effects, aiding in the process of video editing. The primary feature of this tool is its ability to automate the masking process in videos. It leverages AI to identify certain elements within the footage, such as faces, heads, full bodies, and license plates, which it can then apply a blur to, reducing the need for manual mosaic work. This technology can significantly enhance efficiency, as it aims to lessen the manual time and effort spent on this detailed editing work. Primarily, it is utilized by TV stations and post-production companies. With its automatic detection and blurring capabilities, BlurOn streamlines the video editing process, making complex post-production tasks more manageable while maintaining a consistent level of quality in the final outputs.
