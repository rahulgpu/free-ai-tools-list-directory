About My Training Plan

My Training Plan is an AI-powered tool primarily designed for runners preparing for marathons and half marathons. The tool focuses on providing tools and resources to help users in crafting personalized running training plans, leveraging their recent activities data. To initiate, users link their Strava accounts, and the AI uses this information to understand their training history and establish their current fitness levels. From this data, a tailored training plan is made, which users can adjust flexibly according to their needs.A standout feature of My Training Plan is the ability for users to compete against their previous training cycles. This competitive aspect is aimed to uphold motivation throughout the training period. Moreover, the tool assists users with race day preparation, providing countdowns and pace calculators to fine-tune expected performance.Additional features provided by the tool include displaying total mileage, and options for viewing countdowns to races. Two pricing options are available for users - 'Single Race' and 'Unlimited Races', each providing access to the aforementioned features. The 'Unlimited Races' plan allows users to create multiple custom training plans for numerous races.It's important to note that if unsatisfied, users have a week-long window after their purchase where they can request a refund. Support is available through email for any concerns or additional questions.
