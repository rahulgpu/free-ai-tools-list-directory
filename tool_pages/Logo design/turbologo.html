About Turbologo

AI Logo Maker is an AI-powered tool designed for generating logos, primarily aimed at personal and business use. This tool provides an interface easy enough to be used by individuals without any design proficiency. Using AI Logo Maker, users insert their company name and industry to begin constructing their logo. Pre-established fonts, colors, and symbols are available for users to select from, employing AI to deliver customized logo designs based on the users selection. In addition to logos, AI Logo Maker also provides the functionality for users to design business cards, social media kits, favicons along with several other brand assets. Upon purchasing, the tool provides high-resolution PNG, JPEG and print-ready vector SVG or PDF formats of the user's design. It also delivers logos with varied color and background options, issuing users exclusive ownership rights to all designs created with AI Logo Maker. The application is available on multiple device types including desktop, mobile, and tablet. Subscribers can make unlimited changes to their logo post-purchase. AI Logo Maker additionally offers live customer support.
