About AutoCaption

AutoCaption is an AI caption generator that offers automatic transcription and animated emojis for videos on Instagram, TikTok, and YouTube. It saves users hours of editing time by utilizing Artificial Intelligence technology. Users can generate captions with ease and customize them fully by editing the subtitles, adding animations, fonts, colors, and more. Additionally, AutoCaption provides a one-click feature to automatically add emojis and allows users to customize their size, position, and animations.The tool supports more than 56 languages, giving users a wide range of options for generating captions. It offers ready-to-use templates, as well as the ability to create personalized templates to save individual settings. The rendering speed of AutoCaption's technology is extremely fast, ensuring quick results for users.AutoCaption is optimized for vertical content, with a resolution of 1080x1920 (FULL HD) and a frame rate of 60 FPS. It offers multiple pricing plans, including a Starter plan for personal use and short videos, an Expert plan for mass publishing and larger videos, and a Pro plan for professional use and teams.The tool also has a frequently asked questions section, where users can find information about video duration and resolution limitations, supported languages, and pricing details.AutoCaption has an affiliate program that allows users to earn 35% of the chosen plan's price for every referral they make. Users can reach out to the tool's support team via email and can find the tool on Twitter as well.
