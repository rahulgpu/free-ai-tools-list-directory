About AI-Media

AI-Media is a globally oriented AI-powered platform capitalized on delivering a comprehensive suite of captioning and translation services. Its services are majorly employed across various sectors including broadcast, corporate, government, and education arenas. The firm leverages advanced AI technologies to deliver cost-effective, high-quality, and seamless caption delivery. Notable among the technology solutions is the LEXI toolkit which is utilized for different captioning tasks such as translation, display, archiving, and search. The software also provides automated captioning services for both live and recorded content which achieves quality rivalling human captions. For more secure and localized services, AI-Media offers the LEXI Local On-Premises Solution that delivers live, automatic captions on-premises and off the cloud. AI-Medias services also encompass translation, transcription, and human captioning to increase global reach with multilingual captions and subtitles. In addition to these, the platform is home to the iCap Cloud Network, one of the largest captioning and subtitle delivery networks globally. To enhance user experience, AI-Media offers the LEXI Viewer, a tool designed to output live captions to event screens while keeping presentation content fully visible.
