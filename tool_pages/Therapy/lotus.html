About Lotus

Lotus is a free online AI tool designed to serve as a personal therapist, aiming to empower mental well-being. It's capable of providing compassionate guidance, insights, and support around various life challenges. The AI system behind Lotus has been built with the goal to help transform personal struggles into strengths. The tool strives to mimic the process and techniques of therapy in a digital setting. However, users should note that this is ultimately an AI tool and not a replacement for professional mental health services. For optimal use of Lotus, users would need to enable JavaScript. As it is a fully digital offering, Lotus is available for use at any time and from any location with an internet connection.
