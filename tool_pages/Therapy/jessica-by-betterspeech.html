About Jessica by BetterSpeech

Jessica is an AI Speech Therapist developed by Better Speech. This tool utilizes cutting-edge artificial intelligence and natural language processing to provide personalized speech therapy. It leverages speech recognition and large language models to accurately assess speech patterns, identify problems, and deliver feedback to improve speech. Jessica is available 24/7 and can be accessed from any device. The tool offers the option to choose an avatar for a more engaging and customizable experience.Better Speech's AI Speech Therapist aims to make speech therapy more convenient, effective, and affordable. By utilizing AI technology, it provides a convenient alternative to traditional speech therapy sessions. Users can access Jessica's services anytime and anywhere, eliminating the need for in-person appointments.The tool's speech recognition capabilities allow it to analyze and evaluate speech patterns, identifying areas for improvement. It then provides personalized feedback to help users enhance their speech abilities. This feature enables individuals to work on their speech skills independently, at their own pace.Overall, Jessica offers a convenient, effective, and affordable solution for individuals seeking speech therapy. With its AI-powered capabilities, users can benefit from personalized assessments, feedback, and support, all accessible through a user-friendly interface.
