About Deepen

Deepen is an AI-based therapy and counseling tool aimed at providing emotional support to users in a setting that encourages open conversation. This user-friendly tool offers a platform for users to articulate, monitor, and better comprehend their emotional state and mood changes over time. The AI aspect mimics the interaction users would have with human therapists, offering user-responsive dialogue and gentle engagement. Deepen works by utilizing AI capabilities for ambient conversation, achieving a striking balance between automated response and user-led dialogue. Over time, this interactive AI models a users emotional trends, thus creating a personalized emotional map. While Deepen provides AI-based therapeutic engagement, it does not replace professional psychological help. Its purpose is to supplement therapy by providing additional engagement and emotional tracking for users looking for mental health support. It is also important to note that the tool prioritizes its users' privacy by ensuring all conversations and interactions are stored securely. The tool offers an innovative approach to therapy, combining tech and mental health to improve emotional understanding and mental wellbeing.
