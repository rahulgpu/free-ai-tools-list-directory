About AutoInfra

AutoInfra is an AI-based tool that automates DevOps processes and infrastructure management. It allows users to control their AWS infrastructure through Slack, making it possible to communicate with an "always-online infra team" in a chat-like manner. The tool offers fast performance, precise control over AWS resources, and ensures privacy for users. It also provides AI log analysis capabilities to monitor and resolve issues within the infrastructure. AutoInfra generates automatic daily reports on the state of the AWS infrastructure, alerting and helping users resolve any detected issues. Additionally, it offers a feature that gives a rundown of events that occurred in the last 24 hours. If infrastructure-related problems are identified, AutoInfra provides fixes to be approved in Slack, simplifying the resolution process. The tool unifies AWS management by allowing users to easily configure and manage their cloud infrastructure through Slack. It claims to enable companies to accelerate feature release timelines by allowing multiple users and teams to have their own permissions and autonomously ship and maintain features. AutoInfra integrates with Slack and AWS and is considering expanding to MS Teams, Google Cloud, and Microsoft Azure. It ensures the full security of the user's AWS environment and offers a one-month free trial period along with an "Always Free Tier" for users to explore its functionality. AutoInfra is headquartered in San Francisco and provides support through their support center.
