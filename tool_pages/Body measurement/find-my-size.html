About Find My Size

Find My Size is a web-based tool created to help online shoppers find the correct sized clothing from various stores. The tool provides personalized size recommendations for clothing items from numerous top retailers, which ranges from categories like active wear and outerwear to maternity wear and plus-size apparel. By utilizing AI and camera scanning technologies, Find My Size generates size recommendations for users to ensure fitting clothes when buying online. This aims to alleviate common online shopping issues related to sizing confusion. The tool is also accessible via platforms like Product Hunt. As a feature-rich solution, it offers additional utilities for retailers. The size recommendation tool can be used by ecommerce platforms to enhance their customer shopping experience by giving them confidence that what they purchase will fit correctly. This tool helps both consumers, by providing a more enjoyable shopping experience, and retailers, by reducing returns due to size issues. It demonstrates a practical application of AI technology in enhancing ecommerce experiences.
