About Wilowrid

Wilowrid is an AI-based blog post-generation platform that allows bloggers, media companies, and regular users to quickly convert video content into text articles. The tool offers a simple three-step process: users sign in and input their video link, the tool automatically converts the video into a blog post, and users can export the article as an HTML-based file or a PDF.One notable feature of Wilowrid is its ability to include images in the generated articles, automatically extracting and incorporating them from the original video. This makes it particularly useful for interviews, podcasts, and vlogs. The tool also offers a mobile version called Wilowrid Reader, which allows users to manage transcriptions and access them from anywhere.Wilowrid offers additional features such as an interactive editor, allowing users to make on-the-fly changes to their articles, and an AI-based summarizing feature that prepares a summary of the text. The tool supports YouTube and Vimeo links, making it compatible with popular video platforms.Privacy and security are addressed by Wilowrid, as it promises full privacy during the transcription process and deletes all files after 60 days, in compliance with GDPR policy.Overall, Wilowrid provides a convenient and efficient solution for transforming video content into text articles, catering to the needs of bloggers, media companies, and individuals who prefer to read rather than watch videos.
