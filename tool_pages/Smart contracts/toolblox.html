About Toolblox

Toolblox is a no-code AI tool for creating custom on-chain workflows to tokenize real world assets. Use Toolblox Contract Maker to move from pure text to purpose-built smart contracts and deploy them on blockchain, all while using pre-validated easily auditable code (not AI generated) and audited base contracts. Just describe your asset, its lifecycle and all the stakeholders that participate in the process to tokenize it on blockchain. Toolblox also offers pre-audited smart-contracts, example templates, and an AI assistant to facilitate a smooth start for users. Toolblox is integrated with many blockchains like Aurora, Near, Ethereum, Polygon, Evmos, Taraxa and Avalanche. Its functionality extends to different user categories including founders, agencies, and businesses, covering various aspects from business process transformation into on-chain workflows to the creation of ad-hoc on-chain ERP workflows. Toolblox DApp Studios versatility combined with additional integration options such as Toolblox API, DApp Widgets, and plugin for Bubble ensures its usability across varied scenarios. Designed with a user-friendly interface, users can visually configure the flow of screens and widgets for their DApps. Toolblox aims to simplify complex processes, making the creation of purpose-built smart-contracts and DApps as straightforward as possible.
