About Dreamcard.ai

Dreamcard.ai is an AI tool that allows users to create personalized postcards using their own photos and a list of hand-crafted presets. Using advanced AI technology, the tool automatically processes photos uploaded by a user and generates a list of preset images to choose from. Users can choose from various preset options like astronauts, cyborgs, and scientists to create a unique postcard. Dreamcard.ai automatically selects the appropriate aspect ratio, crops the image, and formats the front and back of the printable postcard depending on the user's region. Once the user completes the order, the postcard is printed and delivered worldwide in just a matter of days.The tool comes with a credit system, where users receive credits to generate images upon registration. Users can also top up their credits for continuing to generate images. The generated images are of high-quality, thanks to the tool's market-leading expertise in AI-generated printable art and state-of-the-art upscaling algorithms. Dreamcard.ai also promises to add more features in the future, such as the ability to draw someone onto the card using uploaded images, support for different types of cards, including greeting and anniversary cards, and even wall posters. Dreamcard.ai is a simple and streamlined tool that provides a fun and creative way to send personalized postcards to loved ones around the world.
