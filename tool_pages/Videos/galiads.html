About Galiads

Galiads is an AI-powered video creation tool that utilizes AI actors to generate user-generated content (UGC) video from text. Ideal for various advertising and content creation needs, this platform is aimed at marketing teams in brands and agencies, particularly those focused on digital content. The actors in these videos are AI-generated and do not exist in reality. Users can write scripts, customize audio effects, and create context for their content, after which the platform renders their content video. The final video can then be edited to match the user's preference. Galiads can be used for a wide variety of video styles, ranging from tutorial, training, and demo videos to product review, podcast, and marketing videos. All videos created on Galiads can be used for commercial purposes, making it a valuable tool for any marketing or advertising strategy. The tool also comes with a 'pay-as-you-go' pricing model, uses a credit-based system, and provide support for multiple languages. Galiads provides easy, fast, and cost-effective video production while enhancing ad performance.
