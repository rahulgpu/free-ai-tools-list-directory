About Maverick

Maverick is an AI tool designed for generating personalized videos on a large scale to optimize the eCommerce revenue and lifetime value (LTV) of customers. Maverick offers brands a way to interact personally and effectively with their customers throughout their journey, with a focus on increasing conversions and boosting customer LTV. The AI is used to generate videos that are customizable for each customer, promoting a personalized experience. This provides a unique opportunity to establish tangible connections between brands and their audience, improving customer service by presenting the faces behind the brands in a meaningful manner, and driving customer satisfaction and loyalty. The tool delivers these personalized videos through your email marketing platform. The unique selling proposition of Maverick lies in its 'record once, personalize forever' feature where only one video recording is needed which the AI tool then personalizes for each customer. Maverick integrates well with Shopify, Klaviyo, Omnisend and other Email Service Providers (ESPs), making it a seamless addition to the existing eCommerce frameworks.
