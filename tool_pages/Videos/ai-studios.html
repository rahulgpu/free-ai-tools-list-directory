About AI Studios

AI Avatar Video Generator at AI STUDIOS by DeepBrain is an artificial intelligence-driven platform that simplifies the traditional video production process for a variety of content creation needs. The AI-based tool can transform text sources such as blogs, PDFs, PPTs, and URLs into videos in minutes. It offers different AI avatar options, providing an opportunity to choose from multiple realistic digital avatars or even create custom avatars. The platform supports multiple languages, featuring over 80 text-to-speech (TTS) languages for creating natural-sounding voiceovers for videos. The collaborative environment provided by AI STUDIOS allows multiple users to engage together in producing videos. It also offers video translation features, which can instantly translate video content into multiple languages. Other offered features include auto captions, topic to video, URL to video, and a screen recording tool. The AI Avatar Video Generator also offers a wide array of video templates, catering to different needs from social media to business presentations, news broadcasts and more.
