About Invoke

Invoke is an AI-based generative creation tool and custom AI model manager designed for professionals in various creative industries. It is specifically targeted at studios, enhancing their ability to create visual content. Users can train AI models on their intellectual property and oversee every step of the production process, maintaining total control and ownership of their data permanently. Invoke is versatile and applicable to a range of sectors including gaming, film, television, retail, architecture, and product design. It enables streamlined asset creation using a user's private IP, allowing the production of stunning, IP-appropriate visuals with customized workflows and models. The platform also provides features for comprehensive control over the creation process. It combines AI image generation technology with a secure, practical user interface. Warriors have a single platform to create, refine, iterate, and store images and production workflows. Invoke allows users to easily manage multiple projects and permissions with its multi-user capabilities. They can create studio spaces for distinct projects and workflows, and manage access and permissions. The platform also allows secure management of custom models trained on user content. Users have ownership and flexibility over these models, without constraints from proprietary platform lock-ins or commercial licenses. Invoke also offers solutions for enterprise-level AI deployment. It helps safeguard against legal and compliance risks, providing single tenancy hosting, Single Sign-On integration, and an audit and activity log. In summary, Invoke is a comprehensive solution for professionals seeking to leverage AI in the creative process, offering a powerful toolset to generate custom imagery and manage AI models securely and effectively.
