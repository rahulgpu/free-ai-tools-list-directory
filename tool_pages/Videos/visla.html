About Visla

Visla's AI Video Generator is a comprehensive tool designed to create, edit, and share videos with ease. It allows users to convert different types of content, such as blogs, text, scripts, and audio, into engaging videos. The system offers AI-powered features including an automatic video editor for seamless editing, auto-subtitle generator for adding captions, and an AI screen step recorder for creating walkthroughs or tutorials. It also affords the ability to transition a blog into a video or narrate visual stories effectively. Videography options extend to a variety of sources ranging from private to free stock videos, with AI recommendations for footage to aid the creation process. Additionally, the tool provides multiple editing features like background music addition, filler word removal, auto-cutting, text-based video editing, scene-based editing, among others. Users can record content through screen, video, meeting, or multi-camera recording setups, and even have the option to use their phones as a webcam. Collaboration features, such as the option to share videos, review them, and add comments, calls-to-action, or incorporate auto transcription make it an effective tool for essential functions within organizations such as marketing, communications, sales, training, and product development.
