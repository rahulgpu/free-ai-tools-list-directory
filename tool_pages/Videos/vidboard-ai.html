About Vidboard AI

vidBoard.ai is a powerful AI tool used to create videos from plain written text. It has a user friendly interface and a variety of pre-designed templates and AI presenters to choose from. It can generate speech in 125+ languages and allows users to add multimedia assets and export the video based on their needs. Examples of templates include video resumes, online education, youtube intro videos, pitch decks and fashion videos. vidBoard.ai is a great time-saving, money-saving and effort-saving solution for anyone looking to create videos for any purpose. It is easy to use and provides an all-in-one solution for video presentation problems.
