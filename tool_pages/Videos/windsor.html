About Windsor

Windsor.io is an AI marketing platform designed specifically for e-commerce brands. The AI tool aids in customer acquisition, retention, and growth by automating and scaling the production of personalized videos, aimed to enhance customer communications. Users can create a single video, and Windsor.io's AI platform will enable millions of personalized iterations for delivery to customers. This approach can significantly boost the effectiveness of email and SMS campaigns. Windsor.io is also equipped to generate personalized videos for specific scenarios such as abandoned cart recovery, user acquisition, and post-purchase follow-ups, thereby aiding in increasing repurchase rates and brand loyalty. The platform is designed to seamlessly integrate with existing tools commonly used in e-commerce like Klaviyo, Zapier, and WooCommerce. It's important to note that this tool's services will discontinue in February 2024, due to its acquisition by Front, a leader in customer operations.
