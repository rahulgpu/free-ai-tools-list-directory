About Ozone

Ozone is a video creation tool that leverages artificial intelligence to automate and streamline the video editing process. Touted as an efficient solution for producing engaging short-form videos, Ozone aims to simplify traditionally tedious editing tasks allowing creators to focus more on their narrative. It provides a range of automated features including auto animation, color correction, silence removal, intelligent transitions, and speech enhancement. Its smart image recognition capability enables quick retrieval of required clips, making the video production process smoother and faster. In addition to its innovative editing features, Ozone also promotes collaborative work by offering a unified workspace that facilitates editing, commenting, and sharing ideas in real-time. It employs cloud video streaming technology which supports video playback at high-resolutions. Another distinguishing feature of Ozone is its auto resize functionality that automatically adjusts content to fit different platforms, ensuring flexibility for cross-platform video sharing. It also incorporates features for workspace, team and asset management to foster efficient workflow and collaboration among team members. The overarching aim of Ozone is to transform content creation into a more seamless storytelling experience, facilitated by its advanced AI-driven tools.
