About Fliki

Fliki is an AI-based video generator that enables users to create stunning videos, transforming text and ideas into visually captivating content. It is equipped with dynamic AI-powered features, including a user-friendly text-to-video editor, lifelike voiceovers, and AI clips. Its editor simulates writing an email, aiming to make the creation process as simple as possible. For convenience, Fliki offers script-based editing and AI-generated voiceovers, promising a fast creation process. Fliki's features are geared towards efficiency and accessibility, generating high-quality content at a fraction of the usual cost. Fliki's 'idea to video' tool further allows conversion of blog articles, PowerPoint presentations, tweets, and even product listings into engaging video content. It also provides a feature to create avatar videos. The platform supports over 75 languages in more than 100 dialects, facilitating content localization and translation. Additionally, Fliki's AI-driven text-to-speech feature converts written text into natural-sounding speech, exhibiting human-like speech patterns and tonalities. The tool is fully web-based, requiring just a device with internet access and a compatible browser to operate. Users can create a variety of videos to adhere to diverse requirements, making it a flexible tool for content creators, marketers, and educators. Although Fliki offers a free tier, certain advanced features and premium AI functionalities may require a paid subscription.
