About WowTo

WowTo is a comprehensive video knowledge base software, enabling users to build and host video libraries for a range of uses such as support, training, and educational content. Its toolkit includes a video creator, specifically crafted for how-to and instructional video content, and knowledge base builder which presents a YouTube-like experience to visitors. The in-app video widget allows seamless access to the video content either on a website or inside an application. Additionally, it converts screen recordings, PDFs and PPTs into video format, offering a diverse range of video creation methods. The software also boasts a chrome extension and integrations with various platforms such as Intercom, Zapier, Jira, Confluence, Shopify, and Zendesk. Along with being a robust tool for creating support videos, WowTo has the capability of translating videos, making them suitable for global consumers. Noteworthy features also include AI-powered voiceovers that can be tailored to different accents and localizations. Customisation options are available to ensure the video layout aligns with a users brand. Videos can be constantly updated, ensuring their relevance in rapidly evolving environments.
