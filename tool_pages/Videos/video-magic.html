About Video Magic

Video Magic is an AI-powered platform that allows users to effortlessly generate high-quality videos for various purposes. With its user-friendly interface, users can create customized videos in as little as 5 minutes, with costs as low as $5 per video. The platform offers a wide range of templates tailored for different use cases, including e-commerce, real estate, music, and more.Video Magic has gained recognition for its ability to drive higher conversions, with over 4000 sellers and products from over 300 major brands reporting improved conversion rates. In comprehensive A/B tests on Facebook and Instagram comparing Amazon product listings and automated product listings, Video Magic consistently outperformed the competition. The platform provides a comprehensive suite of features, including scriptwriting, image and video selection, text superimposition, background score integration, video resizing, and the application of various effects.To get started, users simply need to select a template and enter their product, property, or business URL, with Video Magic accepting links from popular platforms such as Amazon, Zillow, Google My Business, Walmart, and Shopify. While the platform generates the video, it creates a storyboard, writes a script, selects appropriate images and videos, applies text and effects, and adds a background score. Ultimately, Video Magic aims to help businesses and individuals improve user engagement, increase conversions, and deliver a compelling message through fresh and immersive video content.
