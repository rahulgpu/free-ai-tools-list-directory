About Waymark

Waymark AI Video Creator is designed to simplify the process of creating professional sample commercials. With a unique AI-driven system, it enables users to readily produce tailored adverts for a variety of media sectors, including broadcast & cable, radio, publishing, and agencies. Users initially provide the AI with business information, following which Waymark's AI scans the web for relevant local business data, and uses these insights to auto-generate a video that aligns with the specified brand. The produced content can then be finalised with any necessary adjustments, before it is ready to be shared, downloaded, or aired. Additionally, all commercials created via Waymark are optimised for airing on TV, CTV, and digital platforms, providing businesses with broad distribution opportunities. Notably, the Waymark AI Video Creator is compatible with a range of adtech platforms, making it a versatile tool for advertising creation. While Waymark does offer a range of plans and pricing options, these might be subject to change, and it is recommended to check the official website for the most accurate and updated information.
