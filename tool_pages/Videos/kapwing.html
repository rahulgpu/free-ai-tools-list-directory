About Kapwing

The AI Video Generator by Kapwing is a tool that allows users to create high-quality videos using AI technology. Users can generate videos by providing descriptions of the desired content, and the AI Video Generator will generate videos based on these descriptions. The tool utilizes deep learning techniques to generate videos from the provided descriptions, which users can then refine and polish.Unlike other video generators, Kapwing's AI Video Generator offers full creative control to the users. They can make edits to the AI-generated videos using over 100 features available in the built-in video editor. Users can customize the video by changing the background music, adding their own video clips, and even recording voiceovers.The AI Video Generator can be accessed through the Kapwing platform. Users can start a new project and open the AI tools by clicking on the lightbulb icon in the editor. They can describe the video topic and elements in detail, select the size, text style, and duration of the video, and then generate a video. After generating the video, users can make any necessary edits.In addition to the AI Video Generator, Kapwing also offers other AI-powered tools such as Document to Video AI, B-Roll Generator, and Text to Speech features. These tools allow users to convert written content into engaging videos, access stock footage and graphics, and generate AI voices for voiceovers.Overall, the AI Video Generator by Kapwing provides users with a user-friendly and customizable platform to create high-quality videos using AI technology.
