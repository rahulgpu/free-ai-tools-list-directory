About Align AI

Align AI is an analytical tool designed for AI-native products. It specializes in understanding and analyzing conversational data generated from language model-powered applications. The tool is designed for product teams to easily convert conversational data into actionable insights. Align AI provides features like powerful search functionality that enables specific conversation tracking in natural language, thus simplifying the understanding of user conversations and unearthing points of friction. Additionally, it offers conversational data overview, critical updates on user experiences, and categorization and tracking of similar conversations. The platform comes with an intuitive dashboard to access high-level insights on user satisfaction and allows for custom insight reports. Align AI emphasizes on data security and compliance to high-quality standards, with SOC II Type 1 Certification. It has a straightforward SDK for easy integration and compatibility with a wide variety of data sources such as AWS S3, GCP Bigquery, PostgreSQL, and MySQL. A popular choice among AI-native products globally, Align AI is geared toward helping businesses maximize user satisfaction and enhance product performance.
