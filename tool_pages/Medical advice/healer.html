About Healer

Healer is an AI tool intended to act as a virtual clinic, providing access to artificial intelligence specialists. The Healer is designed to be a health and wellness resource that users can access at any time, making AI care available around the clock. The tool provides guidance and assistance across a range of health areas. The varied list of AI clinicians includes an allergist for asthma and skin allergies, a beautician for makeup and fashion advice, a dermatologist for skin conditions, an endocrinologist for metabolism and hormonal issues, and a general practitioner for a broader range of health concerns. Additionally, Healer encompasses a nutritionist to guide lifestyle and dietary choices, a sexologist for sexual health and intimacy issues, and a therapist for help in managing self-esteem issues. These AI professionals aim to support users in maintaining a healthy and mindful lifestyle. The primary user interface for Healer appears to be through TryHealer, enabling seamless integration and easy access to professional AI advice. While the description points towards a pricing structure, the specifics aren't mentioned. The tool is multilingual, supporting many languages including English, Spanish, French, Italian, Japanese, Kazakh, Korean, Russian, and Turkish.
