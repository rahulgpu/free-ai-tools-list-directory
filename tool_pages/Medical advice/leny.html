About Leny

Leny.ai is an AI-powered medical assistant designed to provide instant, 24/7 responses to medical inquiries. It has the capability to generate differential diagnoses from symptoms and draft treatment plans. Aimed to assist healthcare providers, the tool efficiently manages tasks such as drafting pre-authorization letters, appeal letters, referral letters, and discharge instructions. The system is built on innovative technology and medical knowledge for reliable and accurate information. Leny.ai also offers a symptom checker for preliminary assessments, medication management, and appointment scheduling for increased simplicity and efficiency in healthcare journey. It can help in drafting patient assessment plans and assisting with discharge instructions and referral letters. Please note that while Leny.ai is focused on data security and privacy, it is not currently HIPAA compliant, hence it is advisable not to use patient identifiers while utilizing its service. The tool is constantly being improved, with plans to soon introduce a registration process exclusively for medical professionals and to become HIPAA-compliant.
