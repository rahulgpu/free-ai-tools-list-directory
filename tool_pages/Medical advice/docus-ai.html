About Docus AI

Docus is an AI-Powered Health Platform designed to assist in personal health management and preventive care. The platform uses AI algorithms to provide personalized, data-driven health advice based on user's health profile and health-related queries. Docus has versatile features such as a personal AI Doctor, lab test interpretation, tailored supplements, an AI symptom checker, and access to top doctors. Users can customize their personal AI Doctor by completing a health profile and asking health questions to receive personalized advice. The AI doctor provides tailored insights, personal checkup plans, evaluates health risks, and generates health reports. The lab test interpretation feature interprets biomarkers and offers detailed lab test reports. Another salient feature includes a symptom checker which allows users to describe their symptoms, answer targeted questions, and identify potential health issues. Users can also connect with a network of over 350 doctors from the US and Europe for expert second opinions. Personalized supplement kits are also offered based on a user's checkup quiz results and can be delivered to their door. The key focus of Docus is to enable proactive preventive care and provide in-depth health insights that allow for better informed health decisions. Docus ensures user data confidentiality and security through compliance with regulatory standards like HIPAA and GDPR.
