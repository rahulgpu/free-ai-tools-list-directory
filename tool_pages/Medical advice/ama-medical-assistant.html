About AMA – Medical Assistant

AMA - Medical AI is a health, fitness, and nutrition inquiry tool designed to help users understand their health better and make informed medical decisions. This AI-powered application serves as a reliable source of health-related information by accurately answering user queries in a more precise, faster, and personalized manner than traditional web searches. It is specially designed to handle inquiries related to health, fitness, nutrition, and diet, providing users reliable and accurate responses aimed at promoting improved personal well-being. AMA - Medical AI has been designed for Apple devices and is optimized for the iPad. The app also underwent recent updates to enhance its performance and ensure user convenience, such as fixing anomalies that caused it to crash. Privacy-wise, it handles data as per policies detailed in the Developer's Privacy Policy, including usage data and diagnostics both linked to your identity and those not linked to your identity.
