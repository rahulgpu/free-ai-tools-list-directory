About YourDoctor

YourDoctor AI is a generative artificial intelligence assistant designed to provide health advice. It operates by first asking users to describe their current situation, prompting them to respond with detailed descriptions of their symptoms or conditions. The tool is developed by Sambo Chea and it employs advanced AI algorithms to generate personalized health advice. It is important to note that while YourDoctor AI aims to provide general health advice and guidance, it is not meant to replace a consultation with a professional healthcare provider. The system does not make diagnoses or offer prognoses but rather educates users about possible health issues based on information provided. It is an easy-to-use tool suitable for anyone looking for quick health-related advice based on AI analytics.
