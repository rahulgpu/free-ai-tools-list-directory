About Emusion

Emusion is a bespoke music recommendation tool developed by Freshly.ai. The tool utilizes artificial intelligence to suggest new music to users based on their current musical preferences. To generate these recommendations, users are asked to provide three song titles. The tool's AI then takes these entries and explores their musical characteristics in an attempt to derive songs with similar qualities. Emusion brings up the concept of the 'Musi-psyche' type, which represents an individual's specific musical mood and preferences. This tool aims to discover and provide insight into this mood, enhancing users' music discovery process by aligning new recommendations with their existing tastes. It's an innovative solution for users looking to expand their music libraries while maintaining a specific taste or mood in their music selections. Note that Emusion, like any AI tool, is designed to learn and improve its recommendations over time based on continual user inputs.
