About Swapper

Icons8's Face Swapper is an AI-powered tool that allows users to swap faces on photos without reducing image quality. It is designed to provide exceptional results that can't be told apart from real photos. Face Swapper is available as a 3-day free trial with no limits and paid plans include priority processing, email support, and 60 day storage. Face Swapper uses artificial intelligence to detect faces on photos and replaces them with other faces of your choice. It can modify photos of up to 5 MB with faces of up to 1024*1024 px in size and returns images of the same size and quality as the source image. Face Swapper also offers an API for developers to integrate the tool into their own projects. The API offers access to the Face Swapper, Smart Upscaler, and Background Remover tools. Face Swapper is easy to use, secure, and provides exceptional results.
