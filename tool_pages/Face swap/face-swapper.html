The tool then automatically swaps the faces in the photo or video, giving the user a realistic result. The user can then hover the mouse on the face to see the difference. FaceSwapper is easy to use and does not require any technical skills. It is also secure, with a Privacy Policy and Terms of Service in place to protect user data.
