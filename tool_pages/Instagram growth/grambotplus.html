About GramBotPlus

GramBot+ is an AI-powered SaaS tool designed to help automate Instagram growth using Gary Vee's 1.80 and 502 strategies. Its main feature is the use of the ChatGPT model family, specifically the GPT-3.5 Turbo technology, which provides faster and more accurate personalized insights for user's Instagram accounts and goals. With GramBot+, users can automate both growth strategies, engage with their niche and followers, save time and focus on creating content. The platform enables users to extract top or recent posts by searching for specific hashtags or locations or posts on their explore page. Furthermore, it allows users to engage with their target audience daily through its automated Likes and comments feature, extract Instagram user account details from top, recent, and explore page posts, grow their Instagram audience on autopilot through automation of repetitive tasks such as liking, commenting, and following potential followers. GramBot+ also saves users' time and resources by automating the customer acquisition process and prospecting for new customers by extracting data from Instagram and doing outreach. It has a user-friendly interface, and users can export extracted data into CSV or Google Sheet.
