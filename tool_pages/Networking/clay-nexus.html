About Clay Nexus

Nexus, created by Clay, serves as a AI-based navigator to your entire professional and personal network. It aims at providing a collaborative networking experience for different roles including teams, executives, chiefs of staff, recruiters, assistants, freelancers, and creatives across various industries like hospitality, healthcare, education, tech, and nonprofit. The functionality of Nexus is expansive, providing context for the entirety of your relationships and seamlessly understanding requests about your network. These could range from generating ideas for reconnecting with someone, providing suggestions for crafting the perfect outreach email, or even coming up with a gift idea for that special touch. Furthermore, Nexus is helpful in minimizing stress and saving time by acting as a guide to valuable networking opportunities. This assistance can be particularly beneficial for freelance or small-business navigating multiple vendor relationships, or salespersons aiming to deepen relationships with key clients. Nexus also seeks to streamline event hosting and introductions, making it a convenient tool for managing and optimizing networking strategies. This tool not only connects favorite tools but also integrates with popular platforms. It is available on macOS, web, Windows and iOS, and brings state-of-the-art artificial intelligence techniques into network management.
