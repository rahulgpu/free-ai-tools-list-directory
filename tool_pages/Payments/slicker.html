About Slicker

Slicker is a payment orchestration platform designed to optimize your payment processors and create seamless checkout experiences. It enables you to monitor transactions, generate financial reports, and reduce vendor dependence. With Slicker, you can integrate multiple payment service providers through a single integration, simplifying the onboarding process. The platform offers global coverage, allowing your customers to pay in their preferred currencies and supporting local payment methods. Slicker employs smart routing capabilities to select the best payment service provider for each checkout session based on conversion trends and custom rules. It also facilitates reconciliation and settlement by consolidating financial reports and providing a standardized financial data stream for easy comparison. With Slicker, you can orchestrate refunds, manage disputes, and handle complex payment scenarios using a customizable portal. The platform offers comprehensive reports and alerting features to help you monitor essential payment metrics and proactively identify issues. It seamlessly integrates with your existing payment setup, including widely used providers such as Stripe, Adyen, PayPal, and Worldpay from FIS.Slicker caters to different business sectors, providing tailored solutions for retail, digital businesses, and marketplaces. It aims to enhance payment flows, improve conversions, reduce fraud, and highlight relevant payment methods globally. The platform is suitable for businesses looking to expand their subscription services, embed payments into their platforms, and streamline complex payment processes.Overall, Slicker offers businesses an AI-powered payments infrastructure that enables them to route and optimize payments globally, report finances, combat fraud, and enhance the overall payment experience.
