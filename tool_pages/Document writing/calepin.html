About Calepin

Calepin is an AI writing assistant tool designed for professionals in various fields such as founders, managers, engineers, researchers, and analysts. It aims to boost productivity and simplify the writing process for these specialists, allowing them to create professional documents effortlessly.The tool works in three simple steps. Firstly, users can access over 500 meticulously crafted writing templates tailored to different industries and professional roles. Secondly, users provide the context and information to the AI, which generates the content accurately and coherently, ensuring industry-standard quality. Lastly, users have the option to edit and polish the generated content to add their personal touch.Calepin's AI algorithms can deliver documents and reports in over 30 languages, enabling users to address multiple global audiences. The tool also offers data security, with encryption technologies used to securely store the generated content, and access restricted only to the user.Users have the flexibility to export their generated documents in various formats such as PDF, DOCX, or plain text for further use or sharing. The platform regularly updates its library with new templates based on user feedback and industry trends to provide relevant content.For new users, Calepin offers a free trial that includes 10,000 words generated upon sign-up. Additional word generation can be purchased through token packs, which come in different sizes to match users' specific requirements.Customer support is available via email for any questions or issues that may arise.
