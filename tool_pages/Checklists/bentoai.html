About BentoAI

BentoAI is a tool that allows users to generate activation experiences from existing content such as video recordings and help articles. It offers customizable onboarding and activation components, including checklists and tooltips, to create cohesive and native user experiences. The tool also provides analytics capabilities to understand user behavior, allowing users to drill into details by user and account. BentoAI supports experimentation with A/B tests that can be split automatically by account, promoting growth and optimization. Furthermore, BentoAI enables users to reuse data across their ecosystem for targeting, automation, and an integrated user experience. It offers a customizable resource center, acting as a centralized hub for help and guidance, incorporating guides, links, and a help center. The tool also allows for fine-grained segmentation, enabling users to show the right experience to the right user or account.In addition to these features, BentoAI offers a Figma template to jumpstart activation design work, allowing users to customize popular components to match their brand. Users can browse through inspiration and templates created by other Bento customers for their own product experiences.BentoAI is suitable for various use cases, including unlocking product-led growth (PLG) onboarding, boosting trial conversion, driving product adoption, and facilitating no-code UX experimentation for product teams. It can also be used by customer success teams to scale customer onboarding effectively.Overall, BentoAI is designed to help users transform existing content into effective guides and flows, generating better activation experiences for their users.
