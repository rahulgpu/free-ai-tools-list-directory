About TypeflowAI

TypeflowAI employs AI technology to redefine user engagement through dynamic, intuitive forms. These next-gen forms transform simple queries into high-level prompts, helping businesses build sophisticated AI tools for data collection and user interaction. The tool allows users to convert form questions into dynamic merge tags and seamlessly integrate their tools with existing sites or share them via email. One of the main features of TypeflowAI is its ability to deliver custom contextual responses, thereby enhancing productivity and enabling scalability in businesses. Users can build these forms either from scratch or by leveraging TypeflowAIs extensive library of over 350 pre-configured templates tailorable to specific needs. The platform offers integrations with popular applications like Google Sheets, Google Docs, Airtable, Notion, Zapier, and Webhooks. Additionally, TypeflowAI promotes ease of use and confidentiality, ensuring all questions, responses, and prompts always remain private.
