About Deformity

Deformity is an AI-powered tool designed to create distinctive, personalized forms. Its main objective is to enhance user interactions by generating forms that initiate personalized conversations, providing an interactive and engaging way to gather information. Its AI capabilities enable it to understand user inputs and generate meaningful, context-aware responses that help in instigating a dynamic two-way communication. Users can utilize Deformity to gather diverse information ranging from personal details to specific preferences or needs. The tool is specifically designed to be flexible and stand out, providing a unique approach to form creation that breaks the conventional static mold. Deformity might be a valuable asset for businesses or individuals looking to elevate their customer engagement, capture data in a more conversational manner, or provide a more immersive and personalized experience in their data gathering processes.
