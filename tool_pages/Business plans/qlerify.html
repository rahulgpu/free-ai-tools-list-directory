About Qlerify

Qlerify is an AI-powered business process modeling tool primarily utilized for IT transformation teams. The platform facilitates defining and documenting a business domain, offering a remote co-editing workspace that encourages team collaboration. This tool supports various agile models like Event Storming, BEAM (Business Event Analysis and Modelling), User Story Mapping and Business Process Modelling. Qlerify aids in creating clarity in record time, empowering teams to visually align on critical business processes and document them in a readily-accessible single source of truth online. Also, it accelerates the delivery of IT system implementation projects by shortening the agreement period of project requirements. One of the notable features of Qlerify is that it enables the transformation of process models into data models within minutes thanks to its generative AI feature. In addition, users can create detailed process and data model artifacts that can be exported in various formats like CSV, JSON, PDF. For enhanced accessibility, it accommodates integration with Jira and anticipates additional integrations in future updates.
