About Briefly

Briefly is an AI-powered briefing platform primarily designed for enhancing creativity and efficiency in marketing efforts. Its key focus is to reduce budget wastage linked to poorly crafted briefs by facilitating the creation of standout marketing briefs significantly faster than traditional methods. To achieve this, it uses a range of features such as Fastdrafts, which aids in producing the first draft in significantly less time. Built on marketing theory and best practice, Fastdrafts also utilize a vast array of innovative case studies. Furthermore, tailored feedback is provided to transform good briefs into great ones, thereby achieving more effective work outcomes. In order to inspire and challenge their agency partners, users can also set creative and effectiveness expectations using thousands of personalized effectiveness case studies through the Inspiration feature. Additionally, ensures that each brief aligns with strategic goals by seeking feedback based on key documents, plans, and priorities. Effective briefs and compliance are two other significant priorities inherent to the platform. Enterprise-grade security, performance analytics, fine-grained admin controls, and personalized onboarding are further capabilities offered by Briefly. The tool is trusted by marketers globally, ranging from startups to Fortune 500 companies.
