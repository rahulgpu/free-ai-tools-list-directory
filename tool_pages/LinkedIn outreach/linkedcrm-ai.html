About LinkedCRM AI

LinkedCRM AI - The Sales GPT on LinkedIn is an Artificial Intelligence powered Chrome extension designed to enhance sales workflows through LinkedIn. It functions as a robust AI assistant, bringing a new edge to lead creation and management on the LinkedIn platform. Leveraging Generative Pretrained Transformer (GPT) capabilities, it helps streamline sales processes and provides valuable insights. With this tool, users can ditch the limitations of manually searching for leads on LinkedIn. Instead, it provides refined insights without the research hassle, taking users straight to core customer data. An exciting feature of this extension is its ability to enrich emails. This feature named 'Prospect Reach Pro', effortlessly discovers emails, paving the way for efficient outreach. LinkedCRM AI also supports instant syncing with various CRM platforms, including Salesforce, catering to the valuable need for simplified data management and inter-platform compatibility. Overall, LinkedCRM AI is a tool geared towards simplifying complex tasks and enhancing productivity, while adding innovative AI-driven functionalities to your sales workflows.
