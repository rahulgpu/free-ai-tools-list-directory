About Bypeers

BYPEERS.ai simplifies, automates and enriches the process of B2B prospecting, improving efficiency and making buyer engagement more meaningful. It combines the power of generative AI, buyer intent signals and reliable company data to build efficient, high-yield sales pipelines for businesses. The AI tool serves to streamline the sourcing, qualification, and prioritization of leads, delivers research-based, dynamic messaging, and automates buyer engagement. At its core, the tool uses multi-source buying intent signals to find prospective leads who are actively seeking solutions. It leverages the power of AI, referred to as the Sales Co-Pilot AI, to analyze these signals, determine the readiness of accounts to purchase, construct personalized in-context messaging, and determine appropriate actions based on a buyers stage, preferred channel and engagement level. BYPEERS.ai also enriches your understanding of each prospect by providing significant context, such as over 300 company attributes and full details about an account's buying team, drawing from a database of more than 350 million company records. By reducing the need for cold prospecting and offering a more personalized, engaging experience to potential buyers, BYPEERS.ai aims to deliver a sales-buying experience that benefits both the selling businesses and their prospective customers.
