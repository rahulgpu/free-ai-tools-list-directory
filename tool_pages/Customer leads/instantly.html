About Instantly

Instantly.ai is a tool that helps users expand their business by enabling them to engage in effective email outreach campaigns. The tool offers unlimited email sending accounts, meaning users can send thousands of emails every day without damaging their sender reputation. Instantly.ai provides the largest warmup pool on the market, consisting of 200k+ real human accounts, to ensure that emails stay out of spam and improve sending reputation. Additionally, the tool allows users to clean and verify lead lists and check if their domains are correctly set up for cold emailing. With the use of campaign builder and integrations, users can personalize and automate emails to optimize deliverability and response rates. Instantly.ai also offers inbox rotation, which allows users to add all of their sending accounts and rotate sending emails between them for a more natural approach. Moreover, the tool includes campaign analytics to analyze the success rate of campaigns and optimize them accordingly. Instantly.ai also offers access to various resources such as 50+ docs and SOPs, 600+ cold email templates, and a private Facebook community. Overall, Instantly.ai is a comprehensive email outreach solution designed for entrepreneurs, recruiters, and agencies to effectively expand their business.
