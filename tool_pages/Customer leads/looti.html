About Looti

Looti I AI for B2B Growth is an AI-powered software designed to supercharge B2B growth. It uses the latest AI technology to analyze customer data from CRMs, create personalized lists of qualified leads, and provide industry insights. It has a three-step process to save time and resources - connecting the CRM, getting lists based on ICPs, and creating smart lists. It also provides customers with AI-generated ideal customer profiles, qualified leads, and custom personas. There are three pricing plans available - free, Pro, and Business - with different features and benefits. Additionally, Looti offers a newsletter with product updates, news, and promotions to join their community.
