About Qolabot

QolaBot is an AI-powered tool designed to boost engagement and conversion by automating lead management and customer service tasks. QolaBot continuously engages with inquiries across multiple channels like Google My Business, Facebook, Instagram, TikTok, and Web Chat. One of the unique selling propositions of QolaBot is its instant follow-up feature that significantly increases the odds of initial contact with leads. By handling mundane and repetitive tasks, QolaBot helps businesses focus on their core activities. Beyond its functioning hours, QolaBot provides consistent and unlimited conversation management round-the-clock, making it a cost-effective alternative to hiring additional personnel. Businesses can enhance the performance of QolaBot over time by training it using their unique datasets for personalized and efficient query resolution. Additional features include rapid response guaranteeing replies are reviewed and sent in under a minute, 24/7 customer care, and a customized setup process that allows tailoring QolaBot according to your specific business requirements. A point to note is QolaBot's advanced appointment booking framework that assures meticulous prompting for better accuracy and customer satisfaction.
