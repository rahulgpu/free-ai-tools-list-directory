About Skyla

Skyla is a customer service chatbot powered by ChatGPT and tailored for Shopify. The tool aims to streamline customer support and provide a superior customer service experience for online retailers. The sophisticated AI is designed to deliver real-time 24/7 customer support, responding to customer queries instantly and aiding with tasks such as product recommendations. Skyla is easily installable with a one-click option available in the Shopify App Store, ensuring straightforward setup and integration with your storefront. It allows you to customise its interactions to match your brand image, ethos, and customer service style, which aids in maintaining brand consistency across platforms. It comes with an easy-to-use dashboard that provides intuitive insights into the chatbot's performance and interactions with customers. This allows for effective monitoring and a better understanding of your customers' needs and buying habits. The technology behind Skyla's intelligence enables the chatbot to understand your shop's inventory from the onset, becoming an adaptive and vital part of your customer service team. Skyla is designed for scalability, versatility and simplicity, aiming to take care of customer queries so the vendor can focus on business growth and other crucial aspects of their Shopify store.
