About Bottled Wisdom

Bottled Wisdom is an AI tool designed to provide quotes that aim to uplift or inspire users based on their current emotional state or situation. Users can interact with the interface by expressing their feelings or situation to the tool, such as feeling great, unmotivated, or intending to take a risk, for instance. Following these prompts, the tool presents suitable quotes to help inspire, advise or motivate the user. An important aspect of Bottled Wisdom is its ability to respond to a variety of emotional cues, thus making it versatile in the kind of support it can provide. In addition to providing quotes, Bottled Wisdom also offers an avenue for users to express their feelings, effectively serving a dual functionality of listening to users' concerns while providing enlightening quotes.
