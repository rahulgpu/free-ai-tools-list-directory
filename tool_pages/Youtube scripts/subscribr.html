About Subscribr

Subscribr is an AI-powered scriptwriting platform designed specifically for YouTube creators. It aims to facilitate the process of writing engaging YouTube scripts, helping users develop and grow their audience. Users input information about the video topic, unique angle, audience and goal, and Subscribr guides them through the script creation process. It proposes options for titles, thumbnails, hooks, and the script itself, all trained and modelled on successful YouTube videos. Subscribr also features a built-in research assistant to support high-quality research in seconds. Users can enter a topic, and Subscribr generates questions to explore, aiding in the finding of answers to these questions with its built-in web search tool. Users are able to add research from external sources, making it easier to create scripts that are accurate and backed by reliable sources. With an integrated editor, Subscribr provides instant feedback and suggestions to improve the script's storytelling and engagement. Moreover, it features an option to remix proven viral video scripts, offering an innovative approach to generating new content. Subscribr offers a glimpse of its capability by showcasing script drafts that it has produced.
