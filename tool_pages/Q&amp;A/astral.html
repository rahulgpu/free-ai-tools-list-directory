About Astral

Astral is an AI tool that allows users to transform their courses, books, articles, and podcasts into personalized conversations with clients. With just a few clicks, users can convert their content into tailor-made recommendations for clients based on their unique circumstances. This tool aims to help professionals reach more people without requiring additional time and effort.Astral enables users to scale their expertise by being available 24/7, delivering personalized responses to clients even when the user is not physically present. It allows professionals to free themselves from answering repetitive questions, giving them more time to focus on work that brings them joy. The tool is designed to help people who may not have access to or can afford the services of the professional.Astral creates a trusted source of information by answering questions in the user's voice and words. It provides ease of use by allowing users to upload various types of content, including articles, videos, podcast episodes, books, courses, and coaching materials.
