About Amanu

Amanu is a chat assistant designed to transform team chats into an intelligent knowledge base. It analyzes and learns from messages, documents, and meetings to provide reliable answers to user queries. Amanu aims to replicate the expertise of experienced colleagues without the judgmental tone. By organizing and indexing information, it helps users quickly retrieve relevant knowledge and eliminates the need to search through outdated documents or meeting notes. Amanu remembers every message, thread, and snippet, making it a valuable resource for team members searching for specific information.Regarding privacy, Amanu only accesses messages in channels to which it is invited and remembers the ones users react to with a specific emoji. Therefore, it does not retain sensitive information if the user removes the corresponding emoji. Amanu can be installed on the Slack platform and offers simple pricing tiers: a free trial, as well as professional, business, and enterprise plans with varying memory and query limits. Users can integrate Amanu with their existing subscriptions and access human chat and email support. Amanu also plans to introduce additional integrations in the future.The FAQ section offers answers to common questions such as installation, privacy and sensitive data concerns, compatibility across multiple platforms, plan changes, and support inquiries. To ensure customer satisfaction, users are encouraged to provide feedback and suggestions to enhance Amanu's functionality.
