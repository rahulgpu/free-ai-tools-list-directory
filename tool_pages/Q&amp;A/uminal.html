About Uminal

Uminal is an AI assistant built with capabilities to surf the web and extend its functionalities through the use of custom applications or skills, without requiring any code. AI operates in a similar vein as ChatGPT, but with added utilities. The tool can be extended with custom applications or skills, presenting a broad spectrum of potential functionalities that users can explore. Key features include internet surfing capabilities, which broadens its scope of operation beyond standard conversational abilities, allowing Uminal to access and extract online information as required. Applications are handled without any necessity for coding skills on the user's part, thereby making the tool accessible to a wide audience. Users also have the opportunity to connect and interact with a community via platforms such as Discord, providing support and fostering an environment for sharing and improvement. All these make Uminal a versatile AI assistant that can be used across various domains and purposes, enhancing efficiency and productivity.
