About Aispect

Aispect is a groundbreaking tool designed to enhance the experience of events by transforming live audio into striking visual representations. It works by taking a live audio feed, such as a speech from a webinar or meeting, and using AI technology to distill and turn this into a unique visual representation. A prime use case for Aispect is during events where it can effectively translate key points from speeches or conversations into thought-provoking visuals. Aispect supports over 30 languages and operates on a credit system, where each generated image costs a credit. There are various packages available tailored to meet different user needs. Moreover, the credits do not expire, providing the flexibility to use them at any time. Users can easily manage their subscriptions and have the autonomy to cancel at their convenience through the dashboard. Aispect ensures data privacy by not storing any recorded audio and it only retains the generated images, which users are free to use as they wish. The tool also ensures secure payment processing through its partnership with Stripe. New users are typically offered a small number of credits for free to get started.
