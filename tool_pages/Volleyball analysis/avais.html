About Avais

Avais is an advanced volleyball training application designed to revolutionize the way players and coaches approach the game. Leveraging state-of-the-art AI technology, Avais offers real-time tracking of a volleyball match using a built-in camera system. The data gathered is utilized to analyze each play, providing insightful feedback and coaching tips tailored to the user's performance. The platform has a feature which develops personal coaching recommendations, suggesting specific drills to help users improve. A unique feature of Avais is its capability to create highlight clips automatically after every game. This offers a hassle-free review of key plays without manually sifting through hours of footage. Amidst the friendly competition amongst users, the app also serves as a platform for users to be scouted, making athlete scouting a seamless process. Unlike other training applications, Avais places heavy emphasis on growth and progress. It presents an intuitive, easy-to-setup interface that prompts users to mount their device on a tripod, detect the court and athlete, and commence tracking and analysis. The goal of Avais is to enable athletes to unlock their full athletic potential, guiding them in achieving their goals, all from the convenience of their mobile device.
