About Divi AI

Divi AI is an artificial intelligence tool designed to facilitate the creation of WordPress websites through the Divi builder. Integrated into Divi, this tool offers the functionality to generate written content, code, and images inside the builder. Because it has knowledge of each Divi element, Divi AI can provide contextually relevant content tailored to a website's specifics. Divi AI is able to generate new content, provide recommendations for next additions based on existing content and website details, and can automatically generate images that are relevant to the page and its context. Divi AI also provides the ability to customize existing images as needed, offering the flexibility to retain or alter composition and style aspects. One of the key features of Divi AI is its capacity to write code, generate custom CSS, and modify the website's design within the Visual Builder. This tool is trained on the Divi codebase, granting it an advantage over other language models. As it learns from the website's name, description, and the content of the current page, Divi AI consistently delivers relevant content. With unlimited text, code, and image requests, Divi AI empowers its users to work without interruptions while accelerating the website creation process.
