About Re-View

Re-View is an innovative tool transforming the way surveys are conducted and analyzed. Leveraging AI, it supplies video survey forms, enabling users to understand emotions and uncover insights more effectively. Through its user-friendly interface, its users can collect video responses to survey questions - thus it allows for qualitative feedback via seeing and hearing what people think. This feature is designed to capture critical non-verbal cues such as body language and voice intonation promoting a deeper, more authentic emotional connection with surveyss. Streamlining the process of data collection, it expects higher response rates, particularly among diverse audiences, and ensures efficient research at scale. Users can simply share a survey link, and Re-View's AI analyses the responses, providing transcripts and research reports. Therefore, it helps users to save time while maintaining a steady flow of potent inputs for their studies or business strategies. Re-View is designed to be accessible and visually appealing, aiming at simplicity, and easy response collection. Significantly, it extends its affordability by offering practical pricing, emphasizing a modern solution. It is suitable for a wide variety of use cases, including UX research, product management, HR, marketing, and content creation.
