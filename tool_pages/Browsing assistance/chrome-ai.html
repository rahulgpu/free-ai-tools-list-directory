About Chrome AI

AI in Chrome introduces a new generation of AI features designed to enhance usability while maintaining personalization. These AI advancements span across performance, productivity, accessibility, privacy, and security. New generative AI features aim to make browsing more efficient. Specific features include 'AI Themes', which allows users to quickly generate custom themes for their Chrome browser based on their preferred subject, mood, visual style or color. The 'Gemini Shortcut' enables users to engage in chats with Gemini through the desktop address bar to assist with more complex tasks such as planning trips or researching new topics. Another feature, 'Help me write', streamlines the process of producing well-articulated texts by offering assistance when writing reviews, RSVPs, or inquiries. 'Tab Organizer' is a useful tool for managing open tabs, with Chrome automatically suggesting and creating groups. Significantly, these features can be synced across devices, allowing for a uniform browsing experience.
