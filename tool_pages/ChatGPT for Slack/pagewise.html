About Pagewise

PageWise AI is an AI-driven tool that provides an intuitive and conversational approach to accessing Confluence pages in Slack. It aims to eliminate the time spent on repetitive queries and searching for information that is somewhere in the Confluence pages. PageWise AI offers many features that streamline discussions and enhance team communication. It effortlessly integrates with Slack and Confluence accounts, without requiring technical knowledge. The tool also eliminates the need to sift through multiple pages by bringing the Confluence data to the Slack workspace, allowing access to hidden insights and connections through its intelligence. PageWise AI offers flexible pricing plans that cater to every team. There are three payment frequency options, which are monthly, annually, and enterprise. The plans vary in the ingestion tokens and the number of questions asked per month. Additionally, the tool can scale with the business needs, ensuring efficient access to information as the business grows. PageWise AI also provides a FAQ section that answers common queries on its features and functionality. Users can quickly get started with PageWise AI and give their team a virtual AI expert on their Confluence knowledge base. Finally, the tool offers a subscription to its newsletter, keeping users up to date with the latest developments.
