About GPTChat

GPTChat for Slack is an application that combines the communication capabilities of Slack with the cognitive intelligence of OpenAI. The app provides real-time communication and collaboration within Slack, offering AI-powered assistance to enhance productivity. It enables the generation of emails and articles, finds lists and best practices for various items, and supports code-writing. It utilizes Slack and OpenAI API keys to achieve this functionality. To improve the user experience and provide error-handling support, the app also stores conversation logs temporarily for up to 30 days, a process that adheres to stringent data protection policies. GPTChat for Slack requires users to register using the same email as their Slack organization. By integrating with Slack and OpenAI, this tool is subject to their respective privacy policies and terms of use. The app's primary goal is to offer users a secure and trustworthy experience while utilizing its advanced AI capabilities.
