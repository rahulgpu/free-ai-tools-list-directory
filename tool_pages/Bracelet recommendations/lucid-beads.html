About Lucid Beads

Lucid Beads is an AI-powered tool that allows users to create custom gemstone bracelets and offers rewards for their creativity. With the help of an AI assistant and live 3D technology, users can design their bracelets by choosing from a wide variety of beads. The tool provides a range of options, including different gemstone colors, birthstones, and chakras.Citrine, a radiant yellow gemstone associated with abundance, prosperity, and success, is one of the available options. It is believed to carry the energy of the sun, bringing warmth, joy, and positivity into one's life. Citrine is also known as the "merchant's stone" and is believed to bring good fortune and success in business ventures. It is a birthstone for November.The tool offers a user-friendly interface that allows users to manually rotate and adjust the design bead by bead. It also provides information on sizing and pricing. The actual bead sizes may vary slightly, and the pricing is based on a base price of $29.99, with additional costs for premium beads.Lucid Beads emphasizes the use of all-natural gemstones and handmade craftsmanship. It offers free shipping and rewards for the user's creativity. The tool aims to provide a personalized and meaningful experience for individuals interested in creating their own unique gemstone bracelets.
