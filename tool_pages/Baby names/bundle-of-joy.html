About Bundle of Joy

Bundle of Joy is an AI-powered tool designed to aid prospective parents in discovering and curating names for their newborn. The app collects parents preferences for their baby names, including the babys gender, preferred origin or religion of the name, theme, starting letter, and its meaning. These preferences assist the application in making tailored recommendations that align with the user's specific needs and tastes. As parents browse through the recommendations, the app learns from their likes and dislikes, further fine-tuning the suggestions provided. The first 50 name recommendations are free, providing users an opportunity to assess the service without any financial obligation. If parents find the service helpful, they can upgrade to a paid plan for continued access. Bundle of Joy, thus, simplifies the overwhelming task of sifting through countless of websites and thousands of names to find the perfect match for your baby. The tool is user-friendly, does not require an additional mobile app from Android or Apple Store, and can be used with a web browser on mobile devices.
