About Textrics

Textrics is an AI-powered text analysis suite that can interpret, translate, and analyze data from text. It is capable of detecting emotions and sentiments, performing Name Entity Recognition, and doing tagging on given text. The tool uses Natural Language Processing (NLP) and advanced text processing techniques such as sarcasm detection algorithms to extract and classify data.Textrics can handle inputs from a wide range of sources, from social media to feedback forms, and can decipher the intent of the input text. Its proprietary framework allows users to retrain the model according to their specific industry needs, allowing for a more customized text analysis.Moreover, Textrics offers data visualization that simplifies the analysis process. Users can see a dynamic visualization of their data, with multiple options available for customization. Depending on the user's preference and requirements, Textrics offers flexibility in its deployment, whether through a subscription model or opting for a secure private cloud deployment.This AI system applies machine learning to automatically learn from experience and deliver accurate solutions. It also implements a neural network that emulates the human mind to understand the relationships in the data. Textrics applies text mining to analyze large volumes of unstructured data, extract useful information and gather insights.Data privacy is a paramount concern for Textrics, with the tool strictly complying with GDPR guidelines. Lastly, no matter the field or source, Textrics is designed to analyze contextual data with high accuracy.
