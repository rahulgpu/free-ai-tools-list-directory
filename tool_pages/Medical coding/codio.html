About Codio

Medicodio's CODIO is an AI-powered medical coding assistant that combines automation with the expertise of medical coders to simplify the process of medical coding. The tool leverages advanced technologies like artificial intelligence, machine learning, robotic process automation, and natural language processing to develop a code search function. This function provides a list of relevant codes based on patient charts, which medical coders can then verify and select as appropriate. The aim is to streamline the medical coding process and make it more efficient. The tool is designed to be used with any EHR, EMR, and Physician's Notes through RPA/API integration, eliminating the need for tedious data entry. It offers suggestions for the most suitable codes after analyzing medical records, helping in decision-making. A team of professional medical coders reviews and chooses the recommended medical codes to assure maximum accuracy and compliance. Medicodio's tool is created with the purpose of reducing the burden on medical coders, preventing burnout, and enabling them to concentrate on their proficiency. It is also designed to improve revenue cycle management by reducing the chances of claim denials and enabling the management of a larger number of records with minimal errors.
