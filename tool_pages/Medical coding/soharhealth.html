About Soharhealth

Sohar Health is a technology solution mainly designed for behavioral health companies. It offers an API-based platform that automates patient eligibility verification and insurance discovery processes. This automation primarily helps to increase patient intake, by fast-tracking the verification of eligibility for behavioral health coverage. It also aids these organizations in focusing their resources more on delivering quality results rather than manual operations. As a digitally innovative tool, Sohar Health effectively helps to reduce preventable claim denials through providing detailed information on patient's insurance coverage. It serves as a trusted tool for behavioral health providers for its advanced yet easy-to-use capabilities. This platform is particularly designed to ease developers' tasks with its powerful APIs, giving them access to API documentation, libraries, and technical support. Additionally, the Sohar Health platform ensures accuracy in patient eligibility verification for behavioral health reimbursements across a vast range of insurance providers.
