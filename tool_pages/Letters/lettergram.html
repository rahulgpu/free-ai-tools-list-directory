About Lettergram

Lettergram is an interactive AI tool designed to simulate pen pal conversations via physical letters. It facilitates the creation and reception of personalized letters to a user's address in a cyclic manner. The tool provides users with a unique experience of traditional letter writing, offering a variety of AI personas with unique backstories that users can choose from as their pen pal, such as a Civil War soldier, a life coach, or even a moon colonist. The experience unfolds as users receive letters written from these chosen personas' perspectives and reply in return, maintaining an ongoing engagement. For additional user convenience, Lettergram offers the ability to change or cancel the service at any time. Users are also given the flexibility to select a suitable plan according to the frequency and volume of their desired correspondence.
