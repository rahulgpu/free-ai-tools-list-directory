About LedgerBox

LedgerBox is an AI-powered tool designed to simplify the process of converting bank statement PDFs to Excel CSV files. The tool uses AI and computer vision to instantly convert documents, making it a convenient tool for individuals, as well as industries such as banking, accounting, and personal finance. Its fundamental purpose is to provide fast and accurate conversions while eliminating the need for manual data entry. Beyond converting standard bank statement PDFs, LedgerBox also accommodates documents from thousands of banks around the globe. LedgerBox prioritizes user data security, complying with leading encryption standards, and uses secure connections. The tool is crafted for precision and deploys frequent testing and improvement schemes to ensure the consistency of the conversions. Another notable aspect of LedgerBox is its scalability. Thanks to cloud infrastructure, it is always available, capable of handling any load in a timely manner. Users are able to convert a select number of pages for free every 24 hours, with additional document conversion needs catered for via its paid plans available in a variety of ranges to suit various user requirements.
