About DaVinciFace

DaVinciFace is a software platform based on the Deep Learning approach to generate Leonardo Da Vinci-style portraits from any photo of a human face. Developed by Mathema, the platform uses Generative Adversarial Network (GAN) technology, with more than 500 million parameters for training the network. The process of generating a portrait takes less than two minutes and the photo submitted is not shared with any third party. The portrait generated can not be used for commercial purposes and it is forbidden to remove the logo from the portrait generated for free. If the user wishes to have the portrait posted on the official Instagram page, they can send a DM at @DAVINCI_FACE. The platform is registered with the Italian Authors' and Publishers' Association and is compliant with GDPR.
