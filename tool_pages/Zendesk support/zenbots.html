About Zenbots

Zenbots is an AI-powered chatbot designed to assist with Zendesk Support operations. This dedicated Zendesk Support AI chatbot can access all Zendesk support articles, potentially speeding up operation times, aiding agents in troubleshooting, and even permitting customers to resolve issues autonomously. Features of Zenbots include an easy integration process into existing workflows which can be completed expeditiously, usually within a 24-hour timeframe. Also, the AI solution grants the flexibility to be embedded anywhere as required, fostering seamless adaptation to various operations and processes. Zenbots illustrates meaningful results, with testimonials indicating improvements in client satisfaction and a decrease in incoming tickets. A trial period is available, allowing users to install the app from the official Zendesk Marketplace and test its functionalities on the user's own data free of charge for the initial seven days. To ensure the AI-driven solution is the right fit, users are encouraged to use it to get a firsthand experience of Zenbots' potential.
