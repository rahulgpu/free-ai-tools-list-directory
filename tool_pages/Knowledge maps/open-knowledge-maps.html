About Open Knowledge Maps

Open Knowledge Maps is an AI-based tool designed to enhance the visibility and accessibility of scientific knowledge for both science and society. It is available as a search engine for scientific literature, wherein users have the ability to visually map a research topic, discover relevant papers and distill key concepts. Underpinned by the principles of open science, Open Knowledge Maps is a non-profit organization that aims to provide an inclusive, sustainable, and equitable infrastructure usable by a wide range of users. The platform functions as more than a discovery tool for current existing knowledge; it is also an effort to collectively co-create a more robust platform for future scientific research and findings. The software includes features that allow organizations to embed Open Knowledge Map components into their own discovery systems. This tool has been recognized for its vision in the field of discovery in open spaces, which propels its mission to revolutionize the way research findings are discovered and shared.
