About AskAIForm

Askaiform is an artificial intelligence tool designed to simplify the creation of feedback forms based on product descriptions. This tool holds the ability to ingest a product description and subsequently use it to generate a custom feedback form. The AI-based assistance extends beyond form creation and into response analysis. Received responses are analysed and summarised by Askaiform, which also provides sentiment analysis. This indicates whether user responses carry a positive, negative, or neutral sentiment. Users have full control over the generated questions and can modify the content or order of questions, accommodating their evolving requirements. This caters for a customisable experience enhancing the precision of collected data. Aside from form creation and management, Askaiform also includes robust analytics capturing daily response amounts for tracking engagement.Askaiform supports multiple languages for form creation and analysis, ensuring broader usability and accessibility. This tool is not limited to any specific region and can cater to a global user base.Regarding data storage and sharing, Askaiform caters to various needs, providing options for exporting data collected. It supports formats such as CSV and Excel, aiding users in an effective analysis of the feedback, and easier sharing with stakeholders.
