About Solvemigo

Solvemigo is an AI tool that provides access to several AI-powered chatbots, including ChatGPT, Whisper, and Dall-E, through the messaging app Telegram. With Solvemigo, users can receive personalized advice and insights on a variety of topics, such as marketing, fitness, coding, writing, diet, photography, and more. Solvemigo can be used on any device that supports Telegram, including iOS, Android, macOS, Windows, and more. Solvemigo offers several features that help users save time and effort, such as sending voice inputs that support over 60 languages, generating HD photos and artworks, and eliminating typing. Solvemigo also allows users to get expert help 24/7 and solve any problem without hiring multiple professionals. Solvemigo's subscription costs $9.99 per month or $99.99 per year, which includes access to ChatGPT, Whisper, and Dall-E, as well as other features like fast response times, fast access to ChatGPT using voice notes in 60 languages, and upcoming features like prompts. Solvemigo assures users of their privacy by deleting old messages and retaining only the last 10 required for maintaining chat context. Uploaded audio files, voice notes, and images generated for users are immediately deleted from Solvemigo's servers.
