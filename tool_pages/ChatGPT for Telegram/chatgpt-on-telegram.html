About ChatGPT on Telegram

ChatGPT on Telegram is an AI powered bot that brings the functionality of ChatGPT into the Telegram environment. It's designed to provide the benefits of AI conversation to any Telegram discussion. The functionality of the ChatGPT bot ranges from responding to queries to aiding in ideation and problem-solving. It can also assist in tasks such as decision-making and writing emails. It's versatile and can be used in various contexts, whether in a one-to-one chat or deployed in a group setting. An added feature of the bot includes the ability to generate images using DALL-E 3. The bot relies primarily on GPT-3.5 version for faster responses, but also provides the option to use GPT-4. Initial access to the bot is free with charges applying afterwards for continuous use. The bot ensures ease of setup and operation, aiming to enhance conversations, maximize thinking capacity, and increase productivity without the requirement to leave the Telegram application.
