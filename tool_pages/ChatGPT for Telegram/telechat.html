About Telechat

Telechat is an AI tool that allows users to build custom chatbots powered by ChatGPT. With this tool, it's possible to create AI chatbots, customize them according to individual preferences, and subsequently make them available on the Telegram app and other similar platforms. Telechat provides a no-code interface, making it user-friendly and accessible to various user groups. Users can import data to the tool in various forms such as PDFs or website scraping to build the knowledge base used by the bots. They can also choose the AI's personality, which provides a more personalized experience to the end-users. Connecting the AI to Telegram enables the chatbot to become available anywhere, anytime. Telechat is used by a broad range of clients to improve engagement and response times in customer interaction scenarios and has found significant applications in customer support and employee knowledge base. The tool emphasizes user-efficiency and has been lauded for its helpfulness in creating intuitive and fast-responding chatbots.
