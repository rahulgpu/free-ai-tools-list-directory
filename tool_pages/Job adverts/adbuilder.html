About AdBuilder

AdBuilder is an AI-engineered tool designed to significantly simplify the process of writing job adverts for recruiters. It generates structured, fully optimized job adverts that enhance applications to any given job role. The intent of the tool is to increase the efficiency of recruiting processes by minimizing time in advert creation, hence allowing recruiters more time for building relationships. AdBuilder leverages AI technology to enhance the quality of job advertisements, subsequently driving superior application quality and quantity, thereby maximizing the possibility of effectively filling job roles and increasing agency revenue. With AdBuilder, the process of creating job-board ready adverts becomes a few seconds' task, leading to time savings and an improved recruitment process.
