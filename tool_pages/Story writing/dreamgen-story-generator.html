About DreamGen Story Generator

You define the "scenario" -- a short plot idea, or an elaborate outline together with a cast of characters, locations and other important lore. 2. Then you either sit back and let AI generate the story for you, or you take the reigns and use the AI as your helpful side-kick 3. In either case, you are the director of the story, and can give the AI instructions along the way to steer the plot where you want it ## Architect Rich Worlds Define the plot, characters, locations, and writing style in an intuitive “wiki”.
