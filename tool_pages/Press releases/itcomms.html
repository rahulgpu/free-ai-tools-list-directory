About ITComms

ITCOMMS's AI-Powered Press Release Generator is a tool aiming to streamline the creation of press releases. Leveraging advanced artificial intelligence capabilities, this tool helps in crafting unique texts that meet user's specific requirements. The process, which traditionally takes between 1-2 days with the assistance of a copywriter and editor, is significantly enhanced, with results delivered in minutes, thereby saving both time and resources. The functionality of this press release generator involves a form that requires the user to input details such as the date and location of issuance, press release topic, notable product features or event details, unique selling proposition, target audience, a quote, speaker's position, company description, media type for distribution, language of the press release and press contacts. Once these details are submitted, the AI tool generates a custom press release provided to the user's email, making the process remarkably convenient and catered to the users' demands. The AI application presents the potential advantage of adding to the progressive image of the user firm, as using AI in the content creation process could be highlighted as a progressive business approach. This tool is developed by ITCOMMS, an agency focused on integrating artificial intelligence into communication solutions.
