About Newswriter

Newswriter.ai is an OpenAI powered tool designed to assist in the creation of press releases. It offers two main functionalities: the ability to generate a brand new press release or to enhance an existing one. To generate a new press release, users input a brief description of their news announcement, and the AI will create a press release draft accordingly. To improve an existing press release, users simply paste their current press release into the tool, which will then suggest enhancements and alternative headlines. Furthermore, users can utilize the tool to write press releases for a variety of purposes, including new product announcements, events and conferences, speaking engagements, awards, business growth or milestones, partnerships, new hires or promotions, funding news, and content announcement such as books and research. Once the press release is completed, it will be sent to the user-provided corporate email address. Newswriter.ai also integrates with Newsworthy.ai, a press release distribution platform.
