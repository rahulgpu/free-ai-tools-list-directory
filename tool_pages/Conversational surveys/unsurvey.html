About unSurvey

unSurvey is a tool designed to facilitate the creation of personalized conversational agents for collecting and analyzing information on a large scale. It merges the concepts of Artificial Intelligence and survey creation, resulting in a unique AI-powered survey tool. unSurvey aims to accelerate and simplify the survey creation process, helping users save time and increase productivity. Once conversations are initiated via the conversational agents, users can then turn these conversations into detailed insights and structured data shortly after. In line with its user-friendly design ethos, unSurvey also simplifies the signing up process, enabling new users to commence utilizing this tool shortly after registration. In essence, unSurvey represents an innovative blend of conversations and structured surveys, translating unstructured data into valuable insights and actionable intelligence.
