About HomeHelper

HomeHelper is an AI-driven tool that offers virtual assistance and guidance for home improvement projects. The tool features LEO, an AI assistant, who acts as a personal in-home construction expert, providing valuable insights and information. Users can ask LEO questions about their projects, such as project costs, required tools, and level of difficulty. LEO also offers step-by-step instructions to perform tasks and helps users become informed buyers for larger projects by providing guidance and recommendations.One of the main features of HomeHelper is its limitless understanding of users' homes. The system learns to cater to specific home needs, making processes of maintenance and home improvement easier. Additionally, HomeHelper includes a Home Journal feature, powered by AI, that allows users to track their progress, set goals, and receive personalized tips from LEO.The tool aims to assist both homeowners and professionals in their home improvement projects. It offers different pricing plans, including a basic plan with AI analysis and advice, an advanced plan with access to the Home Journal and local contractor support, and a pro plan with advanced AI logic and additional features like monthly quotation reviews. Overall, HomeHelper offers a comprehensive platform for users to enhance their knowledge and capabilities in home improvement, providing reliable assistance and innovative solutions for a wide range of projects.
