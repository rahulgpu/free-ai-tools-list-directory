About Autoname

Figma Autoname is a powerful Artificial Intelligence (AI) tool that allows users to automatically rename all of their Figma design layers in one single click, eliminating the need to manually rename each layer. It is smart enough to detect and prevent renaming of components and instances layers, while still being fast and efficient. The plugin is completely free and open-source, released under the MIT License, and is powered by the Svelte framework. It has been praised by thousands of designers, including Edgar Navarro, Charles Patterson, and Vitaly Friedman, who have all been amazed by the time-saving and efficiency benefits of the tool. The plugin is sponsored by Hugging Face and is available for download on the Figma Community website.
