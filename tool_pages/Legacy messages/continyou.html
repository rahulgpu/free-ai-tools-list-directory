About ContinYou

ContinYou is an application designed to provide a platform for individuals to share personal stories or messages that are intended to outlast them. It caters to people facing situations such as terminal illness or military deployment who wish to leave lasting mementos for their loved ones. The app allows users to create, tailor, and schedule messages for delivery on future dates or specific life occasions, thus ensuring their sentiments endure beyond time. Messages can be tailored for specific events such as anniversaries or birthdays. The application delivers these messages via email and securely stores them, aiming to preserve the user's personal narratives and intimate stories. Beyond its messaging feature, it also provides the option to transform the content into AI-powered personal characters. These characters can foster communication between generations, implying potential for a system of interactive storytelling or roleplaying. The content created can turn into dynamic personalities that adapt and respond based on user input, which can lead to engaging conversations and unique adventures. Overall, ContinYou is an emotional connectivity and storytelling tool with a focus on legacy building.
