About Levytation

Levytation is a Business Intelligence and Analytics platform tailored specifically for small businesses. The tool places an emphasis on providing real-time insights and actionable analytics to help businesses drive growth. It is designed to enhance data-driven decision making for businesses, particularly focusing on sales data. Levytation allows businesses to convert their sales data into valuable, actionable insights quickly, which can inform and guide important business decisions. The platform appears to be user-friendly and easily accessible, offering functionalities such as data cards to help businesses explore their data efficiently. In addition to data analysis, Levytation provides a range of services including Pricing Analysis and Inventory Analysis. These services help businesses identify low selling items, predict future revenue, determine optimal pricing for maximum profit, manage inventory levels, and evaluate supplier performance, among other things. Future services from Levytation will include features like conversational analysis for past sales data, social media analysis for understanding customer base, and sentiment analysis to improve customer retention. Overall, Levytation aims to help small businesses enhance their profitability by making smart, data-driven business decisions.
