About Wolfe

Wolfe by Slideworks is a generative AI designed to serve as a personal management consultant. It boasts a comprehensive understanding of business principles garnered from extensive study of business books, slides, and reports. By leveraging sophisticated language models and consultant-like understanding, Wolfe acts as a co-pilot to help solve a wide scope of business problems. The AI enables users to tackle complex challenges with agility and structure and simplifies tedious tasks such as research, drafting, analysis, and communication. It offers structured guidance and frameworks for creating presentations and managing projects, approaches for solving business-specific problems such as reducing churn, or optimizing pricing cycles. It also aids in content creation and editing, providing services such as compressing presentations into executive summaries, suggesting headlines, and refining language. Moreover, Wolfe is capable of analyzing datasets to identify patterns, trends, and potential reasons behind certain business phenomena. It can even conduct market research to understand key trends and perceptions, thus delivering essential insights to users. Wolfes impressive capabilities are a result of a fruitful partnership between seasoned consultants and developers, and Slideworks, a company specializing in business strategy tools and templates. While Wolfe isn't available for public preview yet, it is accessible to select companies and partners.
