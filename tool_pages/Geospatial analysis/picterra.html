About Picterra

Picterra is a cloud-based platform that harnesses the power of AI for geospatial solutions, contributing to a sustainable future. Leveraging AI, Picterra offers object detection, change monitoring and pattern discovery features to optimise geospatial workflows and facilitate quick action. It serves various industries including Forestry and Carbon Markets, Agriculture and Regenerative Farming, Mining and Quarries, Infrastructure, and FMCG & commodities. For each industry, specific functionalities are provided. For example, tree detection for forestry, multi-class crop detection for agriculture, and mine expansion/deforestation detection for mining. Customers can use Picterra's platform to automate their data analysis and generate insights efficiently. Their services extend to providing professional solutions adhering to EUDR compliance. The application of Picterra spans across several sectors, enabling businesses to manage and interpret their data more effectively, thereby driving sustainable action.
