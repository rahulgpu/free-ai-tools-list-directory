About Canvas AI

Canvas AI is a digital tool designed to empower and stimulate business strategy through artificial intelligence. Positioned as an innovation catalyst for strategizing, it offers assistance in filling gaps in your strategic frameworks. The AI supports users by generating AI-driven suggestions, and turning these into actionable strategic pathways. Additionally, Canvas AI boasts image generation capabilities, meaning it can produce vibrant imagery to accompany and provide context to your strategic insights. Central to Canvas AI is its function for business model innovation. It can generate insights tailored specifically for both Business Model and Customer Value Canvases, transforming vision into implementable strategy. It also offers product management features, providing AI-powered insights to redefine your product strategy. Canvas AI facilitates a deep dive into customer desires and needs to shape standout products with precision. The tool supports a range of canvases, including Business Model Canvas, Customer Value Canvas, Lean Canvas, Kano Model Canvas and Customer Interview Canvas. This array of options ensures you can find the perfect tool for your specific ideas and business requirements. Compatible with IOS and MACOS on Apple Silicon (M1), Canvas AI can be an integral part of shaping and illuminating your business strategy.
