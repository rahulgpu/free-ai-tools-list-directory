About B7Labs

Converse is an AI-based reading companion designed for enhancing the user's interaction with web articles, PDF documents, and YouTube videos. It allows users to save these materials and generate tailored summaries or too-long-didn't-read (TLDR) versions. Key insights from these resources can be extracted quickly helping to reduce reading or watching time. One of the distinctive features of this platform is an intuitive document chat that enables users to converse with their saved documents and videos, obtaining precise answers to their questions cited with source references. All saved web articles or uploaded PDFs are stored permanently in the user's library, which can be organized into separate collections. It further facilitates better organization and ease of reference.Besides, it also promotes social sharing and interaction, allowing users to share their saved articles and interactions with their friends and social networks. Other users are provided with the ability to interact with shared documents.In essence, Converse is about redefining the reading experience, making it more engaging, intuitive and efficient, while also encouraging knowledge sharing and social interaction.
