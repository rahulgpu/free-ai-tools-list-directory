About TinkerTell

TinkerTell is an AI-powered tool designed to create customised, captivating and educational stories for children. The user has the freedom to provide inputs such as their childs favourite people, things, and places, or can choose to be surprised with a completely unique AI-generated storyline. TinkerTell uses this information to craft personalised narratives which not only entertain, but also nurture self-esteem and reading abilities. Moreover, it facilitates quality time between parents and children, transforming traditional story-telling into a more interactive and engaging experience. Available as a mobile application, TinkerTell is designed to accommodate varying user preferences, allowing communication through text, voice messages, videos, emojis, or annotations. End users can enjoy these stories as text or fun animated videos, providing a versatile tool for nurturing a child's imagination.
