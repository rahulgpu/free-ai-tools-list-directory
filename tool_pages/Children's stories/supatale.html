About Supatale

Supatale is an AI aided tool that allows individuals to review their life journey in the form of personalized fairy tales written by professional authors. The tool uses the information provided by the users, including their social media links, personal articles, Instagram photos, LinkedIn posts and more, to draft a unique and insightful fairy tale about their life. The story is characterized with a deep research into the user's social networks using AI technology, bringing out personal insights, accomplishments, career development and life transitions into a charming narrative form. It aims to highlight the users' strengths and achievements, which often gets lost in the busyness of life, by providing a new perspective through a fairy tale lens. Moreover, for every story, the tool also creates a personal fairy tale avatar based on facts scoped from the user's life. While each tale is precisely aimed to be favorable to the user, there are no refunds issued due to the high-quality nature of the work. The order placement is straightforward and the customers typically receive their tailored fairy tales within 3 to 5 business days via email. Supatale is available for adults as well as kids and it works in English language, with potential plans of adding more languages soon.
