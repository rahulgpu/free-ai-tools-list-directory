About Practica

Practica is a tool designed to assist in career development through personalized coaching, with a choice between AI or human domain experts. It offers a platform to tackle professional challenges and aims to enhance individual skills, providing support for various career fields including but not limited to engineering, product management, data science, marketing, sales, and customer success. The AI coach analyzes user inputs related to career goals and encountered challenges and proposes tailored solutions based on a vast library of expert resources across numerous disciplines. The platform ensures accountability, offering follow-up sessions and keeping user goals at the forefront of discussion. Alternately, users can engage 1:1 with a human domain-specific expert for recurring sessions. The tool guarantees a comprehensive coaching methodology that aligns career goals with the challenges encountered in everyday work. A unique feature of the tool is its commitment to confidentiality, creating a safe space for growth, and the implementation of frequent check-ins to monitor progress.
