About Compas AI

Compas AI is a web-based solution designed to guide users through their technology-related career paths by using artificial intelligence. The platform works as a personalized AI assistant, with which users can interact to answer questions about their preferences, strengths, and interests. It then leverages AI to make tailored recommendations, suggesting the top five skills that would be a perfect fit for each user. In addition to these insights, users gain access to free crash courses, a robust library of resources including industry insights and educational materials, and a vibrant community of other learners. The platform offers a gamified learning experience, encouraging learners to earn rewards through weekly contests, hackathons, and even uncovering potential job opportunities. Compas AI also equips learners with a plethora of skills ranging from programming to data science. The platform has a freemium model where an account creation grants users with initial credits and skill tokens which can be replenished through subscription packages or a pay-as-you go model. The platform is accessible on different devices and there are plans for Android and iOS mobile applications.
