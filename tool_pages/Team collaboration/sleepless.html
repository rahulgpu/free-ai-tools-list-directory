About Sleepless

Sleepless is an AI-enhanced service designed to provide 24/7 support to businesses with creative tasks. This platform is integrated directly into Slack, making it easily accessible for team communication and management. Sleepless provides a myriad of services including design tasks, video tasks, and even landing page-building. Their team comprises designers, animators, and video editors trained to deliver high-quality, performance-driven assets. Utilizing AI-training, Sleepless equips its team to be ahead in modern marketing practices. Its unique selling point lies in its proprietary video library, which customers can leverage in their marketing initiatives to develop compelling ads. Sleepless prides itself on providing a rapid turnaround time between 12 to 48 hours, ensuring businesses never have to wait for creative outputs. Additionally, Sleepless operates on a monthly subscription model that allows unlimited execution of tasks. This means, with a single subscription, customers can request any number of design-related tasks without any extra charges. Furthermore, the service emphasizes the ability for unlimited revisions, allowing customers to make an unlimited number of changes to ensure the final product is perfect. In essence, Sleepless functions as an extension of your internal creative team, expanding its bandwidth to handle more tasks efficiently and proficiently.
