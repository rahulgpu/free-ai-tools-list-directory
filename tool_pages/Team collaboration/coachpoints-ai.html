About CoachPoints AI

CoachPoints AI is a tool designed to assist people managers with on-the-job coaching. The platform provides a single view from where managers can add team members, note their current performance, and make necessary adjustments. The interface allows easy entry of coaching inputs and offers a centralized place to consolidate feedback. CoachPoints AI streamlines the coaching process by allowing managers to generate custom coaching plans for each teammate based on specific inputs. These plans are realized using the latest language model AI technology to convert rough points on a teammate's trajectory, areas that are going well and those that need improvement, into a tailored guidance note and coaching exercise. This technology also suggests practical mini exercises based on teammates' areas of improvement.Moreover, the platform enables managers to make quick additions to the team and adjust their fellow teammates' performance trajectory with ease. They can consistently note and save coaching inputs for their team in one spot and create AI-powered guidance and coaching plans personalised for team members.CoachPoints AI offers several pricing plans, including a limited free plan and options for increased features, making it accessible and scalable according to the size and needs of your team. It can be used in various people management roles, such as business, product, or engineering management, providing a valuable tool to optimize team performance, enhance individual growth, and foster a growth-oriented work environment.
