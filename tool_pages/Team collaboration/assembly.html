About Assembly

Assembly's AI-powered Intranet is geared towards enhancing internal communication within an organization. The platform is designed to elevate teamwork, streamline communication, and foster meaningful engagement across all departments of an organization. It features a suite of solutions aimed at improving operational efficiency such as fostering employee engagement, appreciation programs for recognition and rewards, fast and informed decision-making capabilities, better communication and collaboration tools, an AI workplace assistant for fast access to organizational information, and tools for more effective knowledge management. The interactive intranet facilitates knowledge sharing, comprehensive search of documents and presentations across all tools and applications, hop-to-it feedback capturing, easy culture-building, and much more. With its built-in integrations, Assembly's intranet is a complementary tool to the existing software in your organization. It showcases strong compatibility, as it allows for integration with SharePoint, Workday, SuccessFactors, Paychex, BambooHR, and the Slack Bot amongst others. Designed for varying levels of use, the platform is beneficial for both individual professionals and larger work departments or teams. Additionally, it can be scaled up to satisfy the requirements of entire organization regardless of its size.
