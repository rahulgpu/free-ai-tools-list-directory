About Joia

Joia is a ChatGPT platform designed for team collaboration. It allows its users to manage team access to Large Language Models and centralize payments into a single bill. Joia offers an alternative UI to the ChatGPT, focusing on speed, simplicity, and usability. It supports not just OpenAI models, but also open source models like Llama, Mixtral, and more. Users can create personalized chatbots for repetitive tasks, share them with their team, and manage the access and payments for these chatbots through a centralized platform. Joia directly links to Large Language Model providers via an API, allowing for more cost-efficient usage based on actual activity, not flat fee subscriptions. With Joia, teams can boost their productivity and collaboration, specifically built for organizations, the platform is also capable of supporting individual users. In addition to the functionalities, Joia also provides an accessible support system through an established contact system for user assistance.
