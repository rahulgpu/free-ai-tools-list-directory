About Spike

Spike is a unified platform designed to streamline team collaboration by providing a range of communication and productivity features in one space. The tool combines chat, emails, meetings, and collaborative documents, offering a unified feed for all team communication. Key features include the Magic AI, an AI-backed assistant that aids in handling emails, replies, and ideas, and Channels that allow for public team conversations on any topic. Spike also enables private group conversations and prioritizes important messages via the Priority Inbox. The platform supports file management, task tracking, and calendar features to ensure never missing an important meeting. Spike also enhances email experience by allowing users to use their domain or obtain one from the suite. Clients can easily switch between text conversations and video or audio calls, and the platform supports collaborative document creation and editing. The platform is suited for various industries, including professional services, creative agencies, technology, and IT & Support. It aims to enhance team communication and productivity by reducing the need to switch between multiple apps and platforms.
