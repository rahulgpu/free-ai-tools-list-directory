About Chattermill

Copilot AI is an innovation built to reshape the way team members collaborate and work. The tool leverages the power of generative artificial intelligence to provide answers to questions related to customer experience (CX), product, and marketing. Its primary aim is to enhance team alignment, efficiency, and productivity. Users have the ability to ask any type of question and quickly receive business insights, fostering a higher level of cross-team collaboration and prioritization of quality work. Copilot AI is equipped with a user-friendly interface and auto-generated visualizations making it straightforward for everyone to use. This tool quantifies results, measures the scope of customer issues, and predicts business impact, functioning like a dedicated team of experts providing insights 24/7. The tool goes an extra mile to ensure its insights are trustworthy and verified, courtesy of direct customer quotes that offer solid evidence for every detail. It also comprises enterprise-level security, compliance measures, and automated personal identifiable information (PII) removal. Finally, Copilot AI integrates with popular tools such as Slack, Jira, and Notion, facilitating seamless communication and sharing of insights without disturbing ongoing work.
