About Shotstack Workflows

Shotstack Workflows is a media automation tool targeted towards marketers, developers and industries focusing on sectors like real estate, automotive, sports, travel, media & entertainment, education & training, and health & wellness. The platform enables users to create videos and images using generative AI and third-party media, providing a no-code, visual platform for designing and automating media workflows. It comprises several APIs for different aspects of the media automation process. The 'Video Editing API' facilitates batch generation of videos, while the 'Ingest API' is used for ingestion, storage and transformation of videos and images. The 'Create API' employs generative AI for media and content creation, and the 'Serve API' deals with video and asset distribution, storage, and hosting. Shotstack Workflows' solutions are adaptable to different roles, such as marketers, agencies, product managers and startups, offering sector-specific tools for video automation and workflow integration.
