About SupaClip Pro

Developed by KwikTwik Technologies Pvt Ltd, SupaClip Pro is an AI-powered tool that facilitates the creation of a website from a video. The tool provides an array of features suitable for different user groups. For content creators, it increases the organic reach of videos by facilitating content distribution. Students can get more out of long lectures using the summary and AI assistant features. Podcast hosts can offer show notes, transcripts, and chatbot for episode recaps and engagement. Researchers can extract key insights, quotes, and clips from interview videos and documentaries.SupaClip Pro allows users to quickly comprehend the main points through a short video summary. It also provides an AI assistant trained on video content to answer your queries. Additionally, it offers a complete transcript with timestamps for easy video navigation. This tool presents plans tailored for different needs, including 'Free Plan' and 'For Creators'. All plans offer video summary, transcript, timestamps, AI chat assistant, and customization of your domain. The 'Creator plan' specifically targets content creators while a more advanced 'Premium Plan' is also available for a fee.SupaClip Pro's ultimate aim is to turn any video into a knowledge base. Through its summarization, transcripts, timestamps, and AI assistant features, essential information from your videos is just a click away.
