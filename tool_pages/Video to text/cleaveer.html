About Cleaveer

Cleaveer is an AI tool designed to turn YouTube videos into different types of text-based content including blog posts, LinkedIn posts, Twitter threads, and summaries. It utilizes the power of GPT-4 - a highly advanced language model developed by OpenAI. The primary function of Cleaveer is to simplify content creation by generating text from video content. Users can convert videos into blog posts, edit them to match their style and share with their audience. The tool also allows creating Twitter and LinkedIn posts from videos with advanced regeneration options. It provides users with the ability to get a text summary of the video, saving their time. Cleaveer aims to create a more seamless content creation process, optimizing the way content creators interact with video-based content and transform it into shareable written formats. The platform offers different pricing tiers, each including a certain number of conversions per month, catering to different usage frequencies. With a focus on transparency, the pricing plan does not have hidden fees and comes with an option to cancel anytime.
