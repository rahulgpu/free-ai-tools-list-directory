About Trip Tunes

Trip Tunes is an app that creates customized playlists for your road trips based on your specific inputs such as the trip details and your musical preferences. The AI of the app curates a playlist that syncs with the local music scene at your location and harmonizes with the overall mood of your trip. Users can preview and edit the playlist within the app itself and then save the tracklist locally or create the playlist directly on Apple Music. The app provides an initial number of 'tapes' which can be seen as opportunities to create playlists. Once these tapes are exhausted, more are available through in-app purchases. To avail the full features of the app, you need to be an Apple Music subscriber, else, you can access the track list and play preview clips but won't be able to listen to the full playlist. The app provides a quick solution for music lovers looking to explore local music during their trips and having playlists tailored to their preferences. Please note that this app is not affiliated with Apple.
