About AppFlowy

AppFlowy is an AI-powered workspace, prioritizing privacy and user control over data. This workspace tool offers cross-platform functionality and offline mode, centralizing tasks, notes, and project organization, with data visualization across tables, boards, and calendars. AppFlowy stands out with its extensible and customizable knowledge base created through a community-driven collection of plugins, themes, templates, and more. It features customization options for themes, colors, fonts, and user interface between dark and light modes. The AI's capabilities are integrated into AppFlowy's stunning rich-text editor, helping users achieve more without tool switching. Users have the option to select the AI service, like OpenAI or Stability AI, which best fits their needs. A standout feature is its commitment to user data privacy, allowing users to retain control and ownership over their data, with the choice to not send data to AI service providers. AppFlowy can be hosted as per user preference, avoiding vendor lock-in. It also offers end-to-end encryption and is available 100% offline, keeping data secure and available on demand. The active community assists in the development of secure workplace tools, creating a versatile collection of add-ons. User customization drives the system, encouraging individual tweaks and modifications to fit unique needs. AppFlowy is available for download on multiple platforms including iOS, Android, macOS, Windows, and Linux.
