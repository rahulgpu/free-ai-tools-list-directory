About Kidgeni

Kidgeni is an educational platform committed to introducing children to the world of artificial intelligence (AI). Their AI-centered app, built specifically for the younger audience, provides a variety of AI learning tools. These tools allow children to engage in activities such as generating AI art and crafting unique stories aided by AI, promoting an interactive and hands-on learning experience. Children can also get an understanding of practical AI applications, such as using AI to print their generated art on various items like shirts and hoodies. Further, Kidgeni provides opportunities for children and parents to co-create AI-powered books, introducing another level of interactivity and collaborative learning. The platform aims to nurture the next generation of tech-savvy innovators by equipping them with essential AI knowledge and skills, therefore deepening their understanding of this rapidly evolving technology. By joining the Kidgeni community, children are not only learning about AI, but are being encouraged to let their imagination explore the wide-ranging potential of AI, hence preparing them for a future increasingly shaped by this technology.
