About AI Stickers

AI Stickers is a platform powered by artificial intelligence designed to enable users to create bespoke stickers with relative ease. It provides two distinctive methods for creating stickers. The 'Text Prompt' feature allows users to convey a concept in text that the AI tool will then transform into a sticker. Conversely, the 'Face-to-Sticker' feature gives users the capacity to convert any selfie or frontal facial photograph into a custom sticker, thus giving a personalised touch to their creation. A range of styles and themes cater to diverse tastes, including but not limited to cute themes, game-themed stickers, anime styles, car stickers, and a variety of other categories. AI Stickers also features advanced algorithms that enable the creation of high-resolution, print-ready stickers and ensures a user-friendly creation process. The platform further facilitates easy sharing and downloading of custom stickers, allowing stickers to be shared across digital channels. Currently, the tool is in a research preview phase and is not intended for commercial use.
