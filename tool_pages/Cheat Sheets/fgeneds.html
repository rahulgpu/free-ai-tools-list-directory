About FGenEds

FGenEds is an AI tool developed by Sphere Labs designed to transform lecture slides into concise cheat sheets. The objective of the tool is to distill lengthy and potentially monotonous educational materials into more digestible and manageable content. FGenEds is especially beneficial for general education courses that one might not necessarily be interested in, but are compulsory for their academic journey. To use the tool, users simply need to upload a PDF of their lecture slides. Leveraging advanced AI algorithms, FGenEds parses through the content, generates, and presents information in a condensed format, often referred to as cheat sheets. Despite its informal term, these cheat sheets are essentially summaries that maintain the core content and meaning of the original lectures. Aimed at enhancing learning efficiency and user convenience, FGenEds thus catalyzes the comprehension of academic materials by making it more targeted and concerted. Privacy and user terms are fully addressed with guidelines accessible via the platform's Privacy Policy and Terms of Service respectively.
