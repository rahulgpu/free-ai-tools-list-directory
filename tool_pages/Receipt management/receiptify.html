About Receiptify

Receiptify is a receipt management software that caters to the organization and management needs of businesses. The tool is proficient at extracting receipts directly from your mailbox and keeping them in one repository, eliminating the worry of lost receipts. Users can retrieve receipts conveniently from their Gmail or Outlook, speeding up the task significantly using AI technology. Receiptify allows users to upload receipts from their phones, thereby eliminating the requirement for a physical scanner. The software also features a QuickBooks integration for matching transactions and receipts, ensuring seamless record-keeping. A notable aspect is the software's ability to ensure comprehensive data extraction from every receipt, including details like purchase amounts, categories, merchants, and sales tax. Receiptify also comes with an automatic receipts backup option that lets users link Dropbox or Google Drive accounts to save all receipts. Moreover, the tool permits users to generate reports by downloading all their receipts to a ZIP, CSV, or PDF file at a moment's notice. The software also guarantees the safety of user info, not saving any emails in its database and using only message IDs to track processed items.
