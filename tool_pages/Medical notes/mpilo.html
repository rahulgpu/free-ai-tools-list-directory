About Mpilo

mpilo is an AI-driven solution designed to revolutionize patient notes for healthcare providers. This tool automatically transcribes, analyzes, and produces clinical documentation using advanced AI technologies, allowing medical professionals to focus on patient care. mpilo listens and learns from doctor-patient consultations in real-time and auto-generates accurate SOAP notes. Custom templates for various specialties ranging from General Medicine, Cardiology, Psychiatry to Diet are available. Once the notes are generated, they can be reviewed and edited before seamlessly integrating with preferred EHR systems. mpilo is committed to security and compliance, adhering to strict HIPAA regulations and taking robust measures to safeguard sensitive medical data. It also operates entirely within the user's browser to ensure sensitive information remains under their control, significantly reducing the risk of data breaches. Specially designed to alleviate administrative burdens, mpilo streamlines the process of clinical documentation. By accurately transcribing and summarizing consultations, it aids in eliminating errors and inconsistencies in medical records. Additionally, mpilo adapts to various medical specialties, mimicking the user's writing style and managing multi-speaker scenarios.
