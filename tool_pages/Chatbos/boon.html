About Boon

Boon is an AI-powered solution designed to help businesses create custom chatbots. The tool is designed to boost customer engagement, streamline lead capture, and enhance customer service operations by delivering round-the-clock interactive support. Boon sets your business active even when you're offline, ensuring that no opportunity for interaction with leads is missed.The system allows you to train the AI using your own data derived from uploaded documents like PDFs, Microsoft Word, and TXT files, enabling it to learn from your knowledge base. After training, the AI chatbot can be customized to your liking to reflect the unique aspects of your brand. This includes key company information such as headings, logo, brand color, and messaging. Once set up, the chatbot can easily be embedded on your website to engage with visitors. Boon not only provides answers to user queries, but it also captures visitor information and converts these interactions into valuable leads. This functionality can be conveniently adjusted according to your privacy needs.Further, Boon allows for a seamless integration of captured leads into other business tools via Zapier, ensuring effective follow up of leads. The tool also includes a feature for storing chat interactions securely, allowing for data-driven insights and in-depth analysis. Boon is geared towards affordability, making it suitable for start-ups and smaller businesses looking for advanced features without a large investment. This makes it a cost-effective solution for businesses seeking to optimize their customer service without the need for a large support team.
