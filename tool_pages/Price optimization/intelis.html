About Intelis

Intelis is an AI-driven pricing optimization tool designed for online stores functioning on platforms like Shopify. It offers capabilities for dynamic pricing and competitive analysis, allowing merchants to tune their product pricing to the ever-changing market trends efficiently. Intelis works by continuously monitoring and tracking prices of your competitors on different sales channels like Google Shopping, Amazon, eBay, and more. It then used AI to optimize your pricing strategies based on these insights, thereby helping to increase sales and profits. Not only does it help with pricing strategies, but it can also reveal the user's store positioning at a glance and offers insights on competitors, thus providing a deeper understanding of market positioning. Additionally, Intelis supports multiple marketplaces like Google Shopping, Amazon, Ebay, Walmart, and Target. Its other notable features include competitor auto-reveal, real-time price tracking, and the provision of meaningful insights into each competitor's strategies. This collectively helps merchants to adapt quickly to market shifts and keep up with their competitors.
