About Pikzels

It replaces the need for unreliable freelancers or expensive agencies by offering a single monthly fee. Users can access Pikzels' services through a subscription, which grants them access to Trello and Discord platforms. With Pikzels, users can submit an unlimited number of thumbnail requests, even for previously uploaded videos. They can also monitor the progress of their requests in real-time, eliminating the need for constant follow-up. Pikzels guarantees the delivery of thumbnails within 48 hours, eliminating the frustration of waiting for days or weeks for designers to deliver the finished product. The tool has a simple and efficient workflow, ensuring a smooth and hassle-free experience. Pikzels prides itself on providing top-tier workflows and unbeatable pricing. According to their clients, using Pikzels has resulted in an average click-through rate increase of over 5.9%. Users can also utilize their Pikzels subscription to improve the performance of underperforming videos. The tool only employs expert designers who have gone through a rigorous application process, ensuring high-quality thumbnails. Communication between users and designers is direct, with no middlemen involved. Pikzels has received positive feedback from customers and talent, highlighting its professionalism, quality, and timely communication.
