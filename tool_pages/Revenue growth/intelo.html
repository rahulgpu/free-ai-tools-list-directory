About Intelo

Intelo.ai is an AI-based value management platform designed to enhance revenue generation by effectively managing a product's value. The tool centers on collaboration between internal teams and customers with customer value being the main focal point. Intelo's AI engine leverages data from various sources such as customer interactions, feedback, tickets, and external sources to identify potential opportunities for revenue growth. The primary aim of this AI tool is to maintain and grow the net dollar value from existing customers by proactively maintaining their accounts. The platform significantly enhances account interactions with customers, teams, and the product by harnessing comprehensive intelligence. With it, one can identify pain points and seize opportunities for optimal revenue growth. The platform is also designed to proactively alert and manage potential risks, thus eliminating surprise customer escalations. Furthermore, Intelo.ai facilitates the detection of potential upsell or cross-sell opportunities buried within your customer and product interactions. Finally, this tool enables seamless customer engagement, note generation and enables monitoring of product-market fit at a customer level, all integrated with your Customer Relationship Management (CRM) for maximum revenue impact.
