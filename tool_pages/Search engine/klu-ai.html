About Klu AI

Klu is an AI-powered internal search engine designed to help teams locate information across various cloud-based applications quickly. The tool allows users to connect their different apps for seamless searching and organizing of data and documents. By using natural language processing (NLP) and machine learning, Klu can scan and search through all files, messages, and conversations for information that teams need. Its intuitive design and advanced search functionality make it easier for teams to collaborate and improve productivity by finding relevant information faster. Klu ensures the privacy and security of all data by encrypting data in transit and at rest. Additionally, all integrations connected to Klu are only visible to the user, and disconnecting an app immediately removes any data attached to it. The tool is available as a Google Chrome extension and can be downloaded on MacIntel Processor and Apple Silicon devices.
