About Alpharesearch

AlphaResearch is an AI-powered search engine designed for investors and corporates to extract insights from unstructured texts, including company filings, transcripts, press releases, and reports across global markets. AlphaResearch delivers effective data analytics by applying machine learning and natural language processing techniques to these texts and traditional fundamental data. It assists researchers in understanding market perceptions and company executives' views on future company direction. The platform functionality includes Document Search, Document Alert & Filings Screener, Trending Topics & Sentiment Analysis, News Watch, and Insider Transactions & Institutional Ownership Analysis. Users can precision search through millions of documents in seconds, highlight, reference, and write personal notes directly in the platform, and share research with colleagues. AlphaResearch offers Basic and Enterprise pricing plans, the latter of which expands features to include global fundamentals & estimates, and a worldwide equity screener. Free limited access is available on a trial basis, while premium services require a subscription.
