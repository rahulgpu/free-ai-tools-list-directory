About Turbo Seek

Turbo Seek is an open-source Artificial Intelligence (AI) powered search engine. Aimed at enhancing the standard search experience, Turbo Seek leverages AI to deliver intelligent search results. It is designed to provide users with precise and relevant answers to their queries, increasing the speed and efficiency of information retrieval. The AI system is especially useful for complex queries that would require a typical search engine to spit out a sea of links, rather than direct responses. The AI component of Turbo Seek is powered by Together AI, a software company known for their advanced AI solutions. As the project is fully open-source, it invites developers from all walks of life to contribute to its codebase, fostering a collaborative effort to enhance and refine the product. Active involvement of the developer community aids in constantly improving the AI capabilities of the search engine, adapting it to new user needs and interests. The tool is hosted on Github, a platform known for facilitating open-source collaboration, thus making it an accessible project for developer contribution.
