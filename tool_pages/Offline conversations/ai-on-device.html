About AI on Device

AI on Device - Local Assistant is an app that offers advanced AI capabilities with an emphasis on privacy and ease of use. Leveraging the power of large language models, the app operates locally on your personal device removing the need for an internet connection, making it ideal for remote locations or secure environments without internet access. The app is compatible with iOS, macOS, iPad Vision Pro devices, and is free to use. Users can customize system prompts and model temperature settings for a tailored experience. The app can engage with PDFs directly for quick information extraction and detailed analysis. The user interface is interactive, includes a new feedback form, and stop generation button for enhanced control.Apart from performance and capacity enhancements for smoother interactions, the app can also be used as a tool to write essays, answer a vast array of questions, analyze content from documents, language tutoring, and for creative writing. Since no data is collected from the users, the app prioritizes users' privacy.The versatility of the AI on Device - Local Assistant app makes it useful for developers, researchers, or the generally curious mind as it provides a flexible and innovative platform to interact with state-of-the-art language models locally.
