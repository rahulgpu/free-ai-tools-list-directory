About Ivee

Ivee is a B2B influencer marketing platform that enables businesses to collaborate with industry experts to increase revenue, generate leads, and improve brand visibility. It leverages a digitally declining landscape, wherein traditional channels of outbound marketing, advertising, and SEO have become increasingly saturated. The advent of influencer marketing, which promotes engagement with opinion leaders for expertise, transparency, and a sense of community, is presented as a key future revenue channel.Ivee's features include an AI-powered search engine. This centralizes data from various platforms, such as LinkedIn, Youtube, Substack, and Apple Podcast, making it easier for businesses to identify relevant industry leaders aligned with their brand's DNA and goals.Ivee also offers advanced analytics to assess audience quality and relevance. It incorporates several KPIs, including engagement rate, performance trends, and sponsorship history, providing greater insight into audience behavior and promoting goal-oriented collaborations. Another feature is its ability to facilitate business-specific relationship building. Ivee provides collaborative messaging capabilities, streamlining the negotiation and closing of partnerships, enhancing operational efficiency. All in all, Ivee combines influencer identification, audience engagement analysis, and efficient partnership management into one platform, providing an effective solution for B2B influencer marketing.
