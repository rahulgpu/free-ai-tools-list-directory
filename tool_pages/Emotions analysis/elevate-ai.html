About Elevate.AI

Elevate.AI is a tool designed to 'read the room' in video calls or streaming video applications, offering insights on communication effectiveness by identifying and analyzing participants' sentiments through facial expression recognition. It recognizes eight primary emotions and computes an overall sentiment score, with scores representing positive, neutral, or negative expressions displayed in color-coded on-screen circles next to each person's face.The app continuously updates each participant's sentiment score during a call and applies a weighted moving average considering their recent activity. It also shows an average sentiment score for all participants, useful for gauging the overall sentiment of the call.Elevate.AI tracks sentiment of the active screen, which means it switches to scoring faces displayed in the current active window regardless of the previously tracked application.The facial expression analysis presented by the tool is designed to be inclusive and non-discriminatory, regardless of age, gender, or skin color. While the number of participants whose sentiment can be analyzed is subject to the processing power of your computer, an average computer can nominally support multiple participants on a video call. All facial images used for emotion recognition are processed and stored locally, illustrating the tool's commitment to user privacy.
