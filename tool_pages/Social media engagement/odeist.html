About Odeist

Odeist is an AI-powered social media discovery and engagement tool designed to establish and manage your brand's social media presence. The tools AI capabilities work by identifying and engaging a highly targeted audience on your behalf, allowing businesses to connect effortlessly and more effectively with relevant audiences. After the social media accounts are linked to Odeist, it operates continuously to filter and engage with your target audience, thereby promoting brand discovery. The AI system understands your business and its target audience by learning from its interactions, enabling it to identify and engage with tweets that resonate with your brand. Though currently mature for Twitter, plans are underway to roll out solutions for other platforms like Facebook, LinkedIn, and Instagram. Odeist operates by adhering to official APIs provided by platforms, ensuring that it doesn't violate any terms and policies of the social media platforms. It uses a vast training set from content moderation samples across social media, which contributes to its efficiency. Odeist aims to offer straightforward and flexible financial and policy arrangements.
