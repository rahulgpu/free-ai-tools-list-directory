About Chopcast

Chopcast is an AI-driven content repurposing platform designed for B2B teams. The tool employs natural language processing (NLP) to identify interesting topics and segments in long-form recordings like webinars, live streams, video podcasts, and meetings, and automatically clips them for use on social media platforms such as YouTube Shorts, TikTok, and Reels. Chopcast offers various use cases, including marketing, employee engagement, training and development, sales, product, customer success, and done-for-you content repurposing service. Chopcast is entirely editable and allows users to create their own intelligent clips based on speaker detection, topic selection, and more. The tool offers automatic video subtitling with 95%+ accuracy, and users can also edit, burn to video or export the subtitles as .SRT. Additionally, users can turn text transcripts into articles or their videos into audio podcasts, and the tool supports MP4 and SRT export formats. Chopcast has a free-to-use version and paid plans come with monthly minute bundles per user. The tool helps teams save time on manual content creation and repurposing, resulting in a more extensive reach and engagement, lower customer acquisition costs, and increased revenue. The platform is entirely customizable and provides cross-department use cases for marketing, sales, events, HR, and more.
