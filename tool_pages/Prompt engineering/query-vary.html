About Query Vary

Query Vary is a comprehensive test suite designed for developers working with large language models (LLMs). It provides tools to design, test, and refine prompts in a systematic manner. By using Query Vary, developers can ensure the reliability of their prompts, reduce latency, and optimize costs.The tool aims to free up developers from the burden of maintaining testing tools, allowing them to focus on innovation. It offers a professional testing suite to ensure brand integrity and agility, without the need for constant testing tool updates.Query Vary promises to save developers up to 30% of their time with its accelerated testing environment. It streamlines the prompt engineering process, boosting productivity by 80% with its streamlined design interface. The tool also includes built-in safeguards to reduce the chance of application abuse by 50%. It prioritizes security with advanced measures to mitigate the risks of unauthorized access.With Query Vary's structured testing infrastructure, developers can improve the quality of their LLM application outputs by 89%. The platform enables extensive evaluation under diverse scenarios, resulting in high-precision performance.The tool is trusted by top companies and offers features such as the ability to compare different LLMs, track cost, latency, and quality metrics, version control for prompts, and the capability to embed fine-tuned LLMs straight into JavaScript.Query Vary offers flexible pricing plans for individual developers, scaling businesses, and large corporations, catering to different budget and need levels.
