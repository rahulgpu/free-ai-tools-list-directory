About Pet Genius

Pet Genius is an AI-powered pet health assistant designed to help pet owners take optimum care of their animals. The tool provides pet care insights, immediate care advice during pet health uncertainties, and timely guidance for different species. It caters individual advice to the unique dietary needs of pets and offers relevant grooming tips. It is also equipped to understand breed-specific traits and help owners navigate any sudden behavioral changes in their pets. With a feature for creating detailed profiles for each pet, Pet Genius uses this data to provide guidance matched precisely to the pet's individual personality and wellness requirements. Moreover, the tool provides real-time support during common pet emergencies, aiding in making necessary decisions while seeking professional veterinary care. The tool has free and premium plans, which includes features like endless pet profiles, file uploads, and chats. It also enables pet owners to store important records such as vaccinations and medication schedules. Lastly, the tool is accessible on both desktop and mobile for easy access, and users can provide feedback for continuous improvement of the platform.
