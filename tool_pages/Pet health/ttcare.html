About TTcare

TTcare is an AI-powered health care app for pets and their owners. It provides an easy-to-use solution to monitor and check a pet's health, helping pet owners to take preventive care of their pet. The app can detect abnormal symptoms early with an accuracy level of over 90%. It also helps pet parents find and book vet services and provides timely notifications for their pet's health. The app offers two membership plans: a free plan with three scans, unlimited profiles for pets and timely notifications, and a monthly or annual subscription plan with unlimited scans, storage of health check results, and personal care made right for pets. The app also provides helpful information and advice on pet health, such as how to take care of pets, disease information, health updates, weight tracking, nutrition management, and behavior management. It also includes periodic reminders and hot tips relevant to pet owners. TTcare is developed by AI For Pet, a company registered in the Republic of Korea and approved by the Korean Ministry of Agriculture, Food and Rural Affairs. The app is available on both iOS and Android devices. The company offers a user guide and instructional videos to help users learn how to use the app.
