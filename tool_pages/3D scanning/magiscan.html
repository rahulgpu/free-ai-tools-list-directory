About Magiscan

Magiscan is a 3D scanning application designed for iOS and Android platforms. This innovative tool utilizes artificial intelligence to scan objects and environments, allowing users to generate high-quality 3D models with their phone or tablet. It is trusted and used by professionals and ordinary people for its simplicity and performance. The app features multiple export formats, thus enhancing compatibility with other platforms and softwares. Formats supported include USDZ, GTLF, GLB, OBJ, STL, FBX, PLY, among others. Compatible with NVIDIA Omniverse, it is a valuable tool in the field of virtual and augmented reality applications. Magiscan also provides an API feature, which allows external platforms or applications to create 3D models using Magiscan technology. Furthermore, the app is frequently updated and improved based on user feedback and is reputed for its functionality and affordable access to 3D scanning technology. Its utility extends not only to individual users, but also to businesses, especially those in eCommerce. Lastly, developers of Magiscan prioritize erasing the boundary between the real and virtual worlds, thus enhancing the user experience and providing a practical tool in the growing demand for 3D content.
