About Algorithma

Algorithma is a new-generation strategic monitoring software that allows businesses to easily collect, organize, and share strategic information. The software boasts a simple interface and intelligent features that make collecting and analyzing information easy and effortless. The software utilizes necessary cookies for the proper functioning of the site, and other categories of cookies may be used to personalize the user experience or to analyze website performance. Algorithma offers all the necessary features to meet the demands of strategic monitoring including collecting, analyzing, organizing, and sharing data. The platform is designed with an easy-to-use interface to ensure a seamless user experience. Additionally, the software leverages Natural Language Processing, machine learning, and AI to simplify and speed up the data analysis process.Algorithma offers a varied and customizable store of sources, including RSS feeds, social media, forums, and internal documents, to ensure a comprehensive data collection process. The software also provides reliable and high-quality technological tools, allowing users to focus on their daily business operations without any interruptions. This software requires no technical expertise, and businesses can easily configure and customize the platform's features to suit their specific needs. The software also includes a semantic classification feature that simplifies the organization of information, freeing up more time for data analysis. Overall, Algorithma is a powerful strategic monitoring tool that offers businesses unique and innovative ways to collect, analyze, and share valuable information across their organization.
