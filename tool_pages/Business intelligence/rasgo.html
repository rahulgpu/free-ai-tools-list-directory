About Rasgo

Rasgo is a generative AI tool that aims to revolutionize self-service analytics by providing users with the ability to make data-driven decisions more efficiently. It goes beyond traditional chatbot capabilities and offers features such as natural language query, data visualization, and automated intelligent insights.By leveraging the power of GPT-4, Rasgo creates autonomous agents that deliver insights directly from the Enterprise Data Warehouse (EDW), eliminating the need for users to manually analyze data. The tool reads and understands data, providing tailored insights and rich business context, including visualizations. It offers a natural language interface, allowing business users to interact with AI analytics effectively.Rasgo prioritizes data security, as it securely utilizes GPT to process EDW data without moving or copying any source data. Every AI-enabled interaction is logged in the EDW and can be tracked within the application, ensuring transparency and trust.The tool aims to provide generative insights that create lasting intellectual value within an organization. It enables proactive decision-making by utilizing its specialized knowledge of the enterprise and continually seeking valuable insights.Rasgo envisions a connected future, where it can generate strategic analytical products based on a wide range of data sources. It highlights GPT-4's capabilities in complex reasoning tasks, self-learning, and self-teaching, allowing for a collaborative and perpetually learning AI system.Backed by substantial venture capital funding, Rasgo strives to transform enterprise analytics and offers a demo to showcase its potential in self-service analytics powered by GPT.
