About Structured Labs

Structured is an AI-powered tool designed to simplify data operations across organizations. Its primary feature is to offer a unified view of all data, eliminating data silos and providing a single source of truth for the entire organization. The intuitive platform allows users to easily connect and streamline their data sources. Whether data resides in warehouses, CRMs, or point solutions, Structured ensures that users have access to the most recent and relevant information.With its plug-and-play analytics, users can generate alerts, queries, and reports, providing deep insights into all facets of the business. Its user-friendly design encourages practical and accessible analytics, enabling users to extract insights with just a few clicks.Structured also aids in building a data glossary, allowing users to set associations and relationships between data from various tables and sources. The system learns these connections over time, shifting the focus from tedious data wrangling to more strategic tasks.Moreover, Structured promotes self-service insights, allowing users to gain the information they need independently from one central hub. Its features extend to real-time log search and analysis, aiding in maintaining system performance and resolving issues promptly. Its natural language query capabilities make it possible for non-technical users to unlock data insights easily. By breaking down silos and enhancing data visibility, Structured plays a significant role in fostering cross-team collaboration for revenue operations. The platform is designed for easy onboarding and seamless integration into an existing tech stack.
