About CueTap

CueTap is an AI and Competitive Intelligence (CI) powered platform targeting sales and product marketing management (PMM) professionals. It assists them in creating and maintaining evergreen sales Battlecards. The platform aids in the extraction of data points about competitors in an automatic fashion using AI, which then forms the basis for Battlecards. This process supports PMMs by contributing towards impactful sales Key Performance Indicators and actionable knowledge sharing capabilities. Sales professionals benefit by gaining confidence and expertise in their sales pitch and positioning which fosters increased sales success. Through CueTap, users are able to add competitors they wish to track, after which the platform extracts data-points about each competitor. The information collected gets updated and fine-tuned regularly based on new intelligence and feedback from the sales team. The final product is a Battlecard that the sales team can use in their onboarding process and for competitive positioning. CueTap helps in maintaining a single source of the truth that is always up-to-date providing actionable insights to its users. The platform also offers additional tools for users such as a Battlecard Generator and Cold Email Generator.
