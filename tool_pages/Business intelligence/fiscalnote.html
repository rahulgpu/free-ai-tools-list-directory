About FiscalNote

FiscalNote is an AI tool that operates primarily in the legal, political, and regulatory domains. It represents a broad suite of applications and services designed to navigate and manage the complex terrain of policy issues, advocacy, and constituent services. The offerings extend globally, capturing federal, state, and local government relations, as well as public and external affairs, with distinct solutions designed specifically for varying roles including congressional offices, government agencies, and legal and compliance executives. Additionally, FiscalNote possesses a variety of specialized tools for public policy and issue management, allowing users to track legislation, monitor risks, and even utilize advocacy campaigns. Moreover, the tool provides Geopolitical & Market Intelligence, offering services for geopolitical analysis, market monitoring, security risk analysis, and third party threat detection. The AI-driven intelligence component of FiscalNote specifically focuses on extracting insights from datasets to guide business decisions, addressing management quality risk as well as offering support in different industries including but not limited to cannabis, cryptocurrency, cybersecurity, data privacy, and education. In sum, FiscalNote leverages AI to offer comprehensive solutions for diverse aspects of public policy, government relations, market intelligence, and data-driven decision-making.
