About SageBrush

SageBrush is an innovative AI-based application that enables the transformation of basic sketches into visually detailed and impressive imagery with the assistance of cutting-edge artificial intelligence technology. Designed to simulate an experience as familiar as using a box of crayons, SageBrush aims to empower users of any skill set, whether they are seasoned artists or untrained novices. It enables users to guide the AI in adjusting the style and details of their artistic creations with a customizable prompt editor. Depending on the strength of their prompts, users can stir the AI's creativity to produce detailed and imaginative interpretations of their sketches. Furthermore, SageBrush gives room for flexibility and user-friendliness by supporting both left and right-handed drawing modes. It also allows input via Apple Pencil or even directly from the user's fingers. SageBrush also has a function to randomize the AIs interpretation of the user's drawing, offering a freestyle mode of creativity. Additionally, the tool offers both light and dark modes, accommodating creativity at any time of day or night. It is worth noting that the use of this AI tool may necessitate the purchase of in-app tokens. Overall, SageBrush leverages artificial intelligence to transform simple sketches into sophisticated and imaginative artwork.
