About Drawings Alive

Drawings Alive is an AI powered tool that breathes life into children's sketches by transforming them into vibrant and dynamic artworks. It offers an intuitive platform that requires users to upload their child's drawing and write a short description or submit an art reference image. These details assist the AI to effectively turn the sketch into artwork. The user-friendliness of the platform is reflected in the ability to use either scanned images or pictures of the kid's drawings, as long as they are in JPG or PNG formats, with the tool giving users full commercial license and ownership over the generated images. Moreover, users have the liberty to save or print the transformed artwork as per their need.The AI is capable of understanding most drawings, and in instances where the children's drawing is too abstract, the use of a good art reference image or a prompt in the form of a written description can lead to better results. An important consideration though is that quality of the drawing submitted does affect the output, and so decently clear and mildly realistic drawings yield better results. Upon payment, users are given immediate access to the platform and the tool ensures the security and confidentiality of the payment details. Drawings Alive offers various subscription tiers to cater to different users' needs.
