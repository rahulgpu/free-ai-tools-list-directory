About Vansec

Vansec is an advanced platform powered by Artificial Intelligence technology, designed to simulate spear phishing attacks and provide comprehensive security training. The tool seeks to establish a solid line of defense against phishing threats by preparing users to identify, understand, and respond effectively to such attacks, helping improve the overall security posture. The AI system has the capability to mimic realistic spear phishing attempts, which aids to enhance the experience and applicability of the training. This kind of phishing simulation can help organizations understand how their employees might be targeted, and provide guidance on how to respond to such threats. Vansec also offers a security training component, making it a holistic solution for enhancing an organization's cyber resilience against spear phishing attacks. This results in a well-informed staff and a more secure business environment. The platform's AI-driven approach ensures constant updates based on new phishing techniques, making the tool continuously relevant and effective. To use the Vansec app, JavaScript needs to be enabled.
