About AdSpawn

AdSpawn is a User Acquisition (UA) creative AI marketing platform specifically designed for mobile games. It automates the generation of tailored ad copies and headlines with no prompting required, with a core ambition of optimizing ads performance and growing mobile games visibility and reach. The tool relies on a three-step automated process: First, it analyses a game based on information from the app store to identify its most compelling selling points. Second, it conducts market research by scanning direct competitors and processing their publicly available data. Lastly, it generates tailored content for the game, utilizing the data and insights gathered. This systematic approach aims to save time and money by minimizing the budget and hours spent on market research and copywriting, while increasing brand awareness with content specifically designed to set a game apart from its competition. The platform self-develops and adjusts to the latest market trends, reflecting its continually evolving AI technology. The tool was created by experts in UA and mobile gaming marketing, crafted to provide effective ad copies for mobile games of various genres.
