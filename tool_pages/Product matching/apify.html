About Apify

Product matching AI is an all-in-one tool that utilizes web scraping and machine learning to compare and match products from multiple e-commerce websites. It is designed for market research, competition analysis, and automated pricing intelligence. With this tool, users can extract and match data, saving time and effort by using the same provider.Scalability is a key feature of the product matching AI. Whether matching 1,000 or 1 million product pairs, the system can handle it efficiently. It offers the ability to scale up or down as needed, providing flexibility for different project sizes.Speed is another advantage of this tool. Users don't need to spend time adjusting to and learning the website's catalog. Mapping a new category can be done within a day, enabling quick access to updated data.In comparison to manual mapping, the product matching AI offers several benefits. Manual mapping can be slow and complicated, requiring additional resources and time to process a large number of products. Additionally, manual mapping may suffer from deteriorating quality over time, leading to the need for increased quality monitoring.Data update rate is also improved with AI mapping. Instead of relying on limited man-hours, AI mapping can provide faster updates, ensuring that users receive the most up-to-date product data.Overall, the product matching AI tool provides an efficient, accurate, and scalable solution for comparing and automatching products across e-commerce websites, offering valuable insights for market analysis and pricing intelligence.
