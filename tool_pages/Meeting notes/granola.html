About Granola

Granola is an AI-enhanced notepad application designed to help manage and optimize the process of taking notes during meetings. It does so by transcribing the meeting audio and enhancing the resultant raw notes to create clear and concise summaries. Granola works seamlessly on all platforms and requires no additional meeting bots. It directly transcribes your device's audio into textual format. The application comes with customizable templates, allowing meeting notes to be generated in the exact format that suits your team's needs. Granola also incorporates insightful features for post-meeting action items, including question tracking, action item lists and extensive budgeting tools. This makes it easier for users to capture key takeaways, next steps, and decision-making insights. Granola is additionally built with sharing in mind, and hence it simplifies the process of sharing the meeting notes across various platforms. A notable feature of Granola is its utility for individuals with a heavy schedule of back-to-back meetings. It assists such users by improving note-taking efficiency and lessening the burden of manual transcription. In addition, Granola has capabilities built on GPT-4, ensuring a superior transcript quality and enhancing post-meeting action item documentation.
