About Sticky App

Sticky App is an AI-powered assistant designed to streamline the organizational elements of meetings by taking notes and capturing action items automatically. It records and transcribes meetings, creating searchable transcripts and extracting key points of discussion. Following a meeting, the tool sends meeting minutes and action items directly to users, allowing for an easy review and sharing of content through email, Slack, Google Docs, Notion, and more. Sticky App also offers the ability to schedule and prioritize tasks within its workspace, providing pertinent context by linking back to the original notes that they came from. The app integrates with several platforms, including Google Meet, Teams, and Webex, and can be invited to any meeting, regardless of whether the user is hosting or not. Sticky App aims to enhance productivity by taking over note-taking responsibilities, freeing users to focus entirely on the content of meetings. The app is lauded for its ability to increase engagement within meetings as users are less distracted by note-taking.
