About Unity

Unity AI is a suite of products powered by AI designed for real-time 3D experiences. Its solutions enable developers to create and operate interactive, real-time 3D content for AR, VR, mobile, desktop, and web. Unity AI offers products that cater to different industries, such as Unity Pro for professionals, Unity Industry for industry developers, Unity Enterprise for large organizations, Unity Reflect for creating AR and VR experiences from BIM models, and Pixyz for importing and optimizing CAD, mesh, and point cloud models for real-time visualizations in Unity. Unity AI also provides Asset Store, which offers pre-made building blocks that reduce costs and development time. Several solutions are available for different industries, such as Automotive and Transportation, Government and Aerospace, Live Entertainment, Architecture, Engineering, and Construction, and Gambling. Unity AI also offers Case Studies that showcase the creative use of Unity technologies. Unity AI also provides solutions for accelerating innovation across enterprises, delivering real-money games, revolutionizing visual effects, and accelerating innovation across AECC. Unity AI has its own AI-powered characters, Unity Muse, and Unity Sentis, which is easily integrated with Unity Runtime. Unity AI also provides an ecosystem of solutions for AI-driven game development and gameplay on the Unity Asset Store. In summary, Unity AI is a set of AI-powered products designed to provide creators with a flexible real-time development platform that allows them to create and operate interactive, real-time 3D content for different industries. It offers several solutions that cater to different industries and provides an AI ecosystem for game development.
