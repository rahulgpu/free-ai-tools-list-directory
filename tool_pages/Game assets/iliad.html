About Iliad

Iliad is an advanced AI tool that focuses on the generation of visual art assets. Using expressive controls like brushstrokes and prompts, it provides users with a heightened control over the creation and composition of images. In addition to this, Iliad supports inpainting, a technique that fills in the area of an image with plausible values, making it an especially useful feature for touch-ups or final edits. It enables users to precisly edit and control transformation strength, further enhancing the aspects of customization. One notable feature of Iliad includes its ability to transform 2D images into 3D voxel art, a process that adds depth and dimension to a standard image, giving it a unique and creative touch. Iliad also offers texture authoring capabilities, which allows users to generate unique and seamless textures instantly. This tool is particularly beneficial for concept art, where users can effortlessly iterate on characters and environments. Overall, Iliad combines a variety of robust features and controls to deliver a comprehensive and intuitive tool for generating and manipulating digital art.
