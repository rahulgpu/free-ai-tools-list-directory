About G3D

G3D.AI {Jedi} is an AI powered tool intended for the gaming industry. Its primary role is to facilitate game creators in generating games with the assistance of artificial intelligence. The tool is focused on bolstering creativity while eliminating the burden of manual tasks in the process of game creation. G3D.AI {Jedi} uses text prompts as instructions to create in-game assets, helping game developers manifest their imagination directly into game levels. Capabilities of Jedi include the creation of 3D assets to match your required art direction, generation of unique levels from text prompts, and the production of gameplay mechanics based on user needs. It also offers the ability to create narratives for games. It goes beyond a mere creative tool by optimizing its outputs for practical use in gaming workflows. The developers further express an intention for this technology to contribute to the construction of the metaverse. Also, there is emphasis on creating a community around this tool, with users encouraged to join a waitlist or a Discord server to stay updated about developments in game creation.
