About Verticle

Verticle is a Generative 3D platform that empowers users to create assets for games and computer graphics (CG). Through its conversational interface, Verticle introduces a novel approach to generating 3D assets by incorporating AI co-authorship, revolutionizing the asset creation process. This platform offers an inclusive and accessible solution for individuals looking to design and develop assets for their projects.With Verticle, users can expect a unique experience that goes beyond traditional methods. By leveraging AI technology, this tool provides a collaborative environment where users can interact with the AI system to generate 3D assets. The conversational interface serves as an intuitive medium for users to co-create assets, enhancing creativity and efficiency.By offering a platform that simplifies the asset creation process, Verticle democratizes the world of game development and CG, enabling individuals without extensive technical knowledge or expertise to participate in asset creation. This accessibility broadens the scope of creators within the industry and fosters a more diverse and inclusive community.Developed by Temporal, Verticle ensures a user-friendly experience that encourages exploration and experimentation. Through its generative capabilities and the integration of AI co-authorship, users can expect a streamlined asset creation process that enhances productivity and delivers high-quality results.Overall, Verticle is a game-changer in the field of generative 3D asset creation. By harnessing the power of AI and introducing a conversational interface, this platform opens up new possibilities for creators, making asset creation more accessible, collaborative, and exciting.
