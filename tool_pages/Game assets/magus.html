About Magus

Magus.gg is a platform that offers AI tools for generating game assets, specifically 3D models, videos, animations, and more. The platform currently supports the generation of 3D models using text or images, providing Blender-compatible .glb files as output. Users can access a free library of model generations and can also request API access or watch a demo for further exploration. In addition to 3D models, Magus.gg is expanding its capabilities to include video generation with sound using text prompts. Users can watch a demo and request access to the private beta for this feature. The platform also has plans to introduce animation generation from text in the near future.Magus.gg offers ImagineAI, VideoAI, and DreamAI as specific tools for generating game assets. ImagineAI allows users to create 3D models from text or images, while VideoAI focuses on video generation. DreamAI, although coming soon, will enable animation generation from text. The platform offers pricing plans for its tools, including ImagineAI and ScripterAI, with different features and limitations.Magus.gg also provides access to a library of high-quality generations for platforms like Roblox, Unity, and Unreal Engine. Users can access generations from other advanced AIs like ChatGPT and GPT-4, and they can explore AI-generated 3D models and generation videos.Overall, Magus.gg offers a range of AI tools for game asset generation, aiming to provide developers with efficient and accessible solutions for their creative needs.
