About NFT Creator – Create Your Art

NFT Creator - Create Your Art is an AI software application that allows individuals to create Non-Fungible Tokens (NFTs) from their mobile devices, with no registration required. With this tool, users can design their NFTs from static images or Gifs, deciding the background, size, and importation of images. It also provides an opportunity to use artificial intelligence for styling these images, and customize them further with the app's embedded icons, images, texts and varied color schemes. After creating a piece of digital art, the application enables users to store it on the InterPlanetary File System (IPFS), ensuring it remains unaltered thanks to the Content Identifier (CID) system. Users can then mint their NFTs directly on blockchains from the app, either on some testnet Blockchain like Binance, CELO, Matic or for free on the Polygon mainnet. Once the NFT is minted, users will receive a transaction hash from the blockchain and they can view the minted NFT in their personal crypto wallet. Plus, the NFT Creator provides an option to explore new NFTs using single words or Addresses. The application also offers a gallery for viewing and storing saved NFTs. This tool enables both novices and experts to bring their crypto art visions to life conveniently.
