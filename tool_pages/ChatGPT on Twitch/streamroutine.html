About Streamroutine

StreamRoutine offers a feature-packed platform including ChatGPT Twitch ChatBot. This tool ties directly into an existing StreamRoutine account and provides functionalities like chat logs, custom commands and more. One of the unique aspects of this bot is that it is powered by ChatGPT, an advanced language learning model. For StreamRoutine subscribers, the ChatGPT module can be enabled in the Chatbot section of the site. However, this tool is also available free of charge for non-subscribers. Users can sign up and access the ChatGPT feature using the platform's intuitive interface. The application of this bot can be diverse and creative, and while it helps facilitate user interactions during streaming, it can also be used by the chat to brainstorm ideas or even for fun activities like organising a rap battle. Additional detailed information on functionalities can be obtained from the platform's documentation.
