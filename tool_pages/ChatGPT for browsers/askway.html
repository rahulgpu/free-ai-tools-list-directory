About ASKWay

ASKWay is a browser extension AI assistant built on advanced AI models including GPT-4. It is designed to seamlessly integrate into any browser, offering users a comprehensive suite of features that extends their browser capabilities. Key functionalities provided by ASKWay consist of AI writing, which generates high-quality text content such as articles and titles, and is ideal for academic papers or reports. Users can also utilize it for coding purposes. ASKWay can translate documents, making it ideal for web translation and PDF translation tasks. It offers an email response feature, generating apt responses to emails at a much faster pace compared to human effort. ASKWay also provides AI internet search feature. It simplifies the search process by delivering accurate results for queries. The extension, touted as an all-in-one AI assistant, has highly favorable reviews from users across the globe highlighting its effectiveness as a personal assistant. It's also mentioned that the app doesn't limit the length of the content generated. With regular updates promised, there's potential for greater functionalities such as word to image features for creating visually engaging content.
