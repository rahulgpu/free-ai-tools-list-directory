About Spot AI

Spot AI is a browsing tool available on the Chrome Web Store that aims to provide users with answers to their most relevant questions. It functions by reading webpages to extract information and deliver answers based on their content. Spot AI is designed to assist users in researching and uncovering information effortlessly. It is compatible with Chrome, Brave, and Arc browsers, and can be launched with a keyboard shortcut or pinned in the browser for a better user experience. The tool is offered by Spot and has a version number of 0.0.3, last updated on October 15, 2023. It has a file size of 331KiB and is available in English language. It also collects personal communications like emails, texts, or chat messages, as well as website content, including text, images, sounds, videos, or hyperlinks.
