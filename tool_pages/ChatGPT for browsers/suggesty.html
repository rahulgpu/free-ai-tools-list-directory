About Suggesty

Suggesty is a Chrome Web Store Extension that uses GPT-3 artificial intelligence technology to provide users with human-like answers to their Google searches. The extension eliminates the need to scroll through all results pages by providing users with the answers they need straight away. It works by sending the anonymized Google search request to GPT-3 and displaying the answers on the Google page. This tool can be used for grammar correction, translation, creating recipes, and making any other request such as movie recommendations, step-by-step guides, and more. Suggesty is a safe and secure extension that follows recommended practices for Chrome extensions and does not collect or use any user data. It has been positively reviewed by users who appreciate its ease of use and ability to summarize information quickly.
