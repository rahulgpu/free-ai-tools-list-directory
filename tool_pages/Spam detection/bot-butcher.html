About Bot Butcher

Bot Butcher is an API tool designed to classify spam messages submitted through contact forms on websites. By accurately categorizing incoming messages, Bot Butcher helps reduce the influx of spam messages that reach website owners' inboxes. The tool follows a four-step process to handle incoming messages. In Step 1, a human or bot visitor posts a message through the website's contact form. In Step 2, the message is sent to Bot Butcher for classification using a finely-tuned large language model. In Step 3, Bot Butcher responds with a JSON message, passing non-spam messages along to the user's CRM or inbox, while dropping the spam messages. Additionally, Bot Butcher provides the message ID for users to update their records accordingly. Step 4 allows users to audit and access past messages, including their classifications, either through message IDs or by logging into the system. Bot Butcher offers simple and user-friendly documentation to assist users in easily understanding and implementing the tool. By leveraging Bot Butcher, users can effectively protect their inboxes from spam messages and improve the efficiency of handling contact form submissions.
