About Owly

Advanced Spam Blocking by Owly is an AI-powered tool designed to efficiently filter out unwanted messages, ensuring your focus remains on important texts. Leveraging GPT and AI technology, Owly offers an intuitive and fully automated experience, eliminating the need for manual configuration. Its adaptive filtering system categorizes messages into Transactional and Promotional categories, resulting in a clutter-free inbox based on content and importance. Owly provides a free plan with essential spam blocking features, while Owly Pro offers increased daily filtering quota and enhanced spam protection for those requiring more robust capabilities. Its clean and user-friendly interface seamlessly integrates with SMS settings, simplifying message management. In summary, Owly empowers users to take control of their messaging experience by providing unparalleled spam protection and advanced AI-driven filtering. By offloading the task of spam detection and categorization to Owly's GPT and AI technology, users can focus on their priorities without the disruption of unwanted messages.
