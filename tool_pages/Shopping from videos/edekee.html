About Edekee

Edekee is an app platform designed to make online shopping more interactive and personalized. Through the app, users can watch shoppable videos from their favorite brands and creators and easily click through to purchase featured products. The platform uses AI to recommend products that match users' tastes and preferences based on their viewing history. Users can sign in with their Google account to access their saved videos and shopping history on any device. The app also prioritizes data privacy and security, encrypting all data in transit and allowing users to request the deletion of their data. Edekee's content is verified and rated by users, with regular bug fixes and performance improvements. The app can collect data on users' locations and personal information but does not share any data with third parties. In summary, Edekee offers a unique, interactive shopping experience that combines video content with personalized recommendations, making it easy for users to discover and purchase products they love.
