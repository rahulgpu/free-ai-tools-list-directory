About Novacene

The NovaceneAI Platform is an AI-based tool designed to automatically organize unstructured data. It focuses on transforming raw data into actionable insights at scale, thereby minimizing time spent on data cleaning and organization. The tool substantially reduces the manual work typically associated with data organization, allowing data professionals to dedicate more time to analysis and insights. The NovaceneAI Platform uses theme-based analysis to extract specific sentiment from open-ended comments and measures the sentiment in context. The platform is equipped with a flexible RESTful API and a powerful interface giving data engineers and data scientists complete control over the platform. The platform also provides a user-friendly web-based experience for business analysts. NovaceneAI is trusted by a range of organizations across different sectors for its ability to efficiently process, analyze, and derive insights from large amounts of data. The platform upholds the safety, privacy, and security of user data, and can be deployed as a single-tenant hosted solution or on-premises in the users infrastructure.
