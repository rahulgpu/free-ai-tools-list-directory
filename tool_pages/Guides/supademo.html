About Supademo

Supademo is an AI-powered tool that allows users to quickly create interactive demos and guides without the need for coding. It aims to simplify customer success, close deals, and accelerate onboarding processes. The tool offers features such as AI voiceovers, enabling users to demonstrate more effectively.Supademo allows users to create interactive demos with hotspots and text instructions by simply turning on the extension and clicking through a flow. Customizations like custom domains, logos, blurring, autoplay, and CTAs can be applied to tweak the demo flow and content. The created demos can be instantly shared as public links or embedded in support docs, onboarding materials, websites, or other existing tools.The tool also offers features like shared team workspaces, advanced analytics, recording of video and clicks, zoom and pan, and exporting to GIF and video formats. It supports enterprise-grade single sign-on (SSO) solutions for increased productivity and security.Supademo caters to a range of use cases, including customer success, customer support, sales enablement, product marketing, and training and onboarding. The tool claims to increase team productivity, user engagement, and the speed at which support tickets are resolved.Supademo has gained the trust of thousands of users, including companies such as Metaphacts, Billetto, Ledger, Triggerbee, Freshlearn, and more. It offers showcases where multiple Supademos can be grouped together into a learning module or onboarding guide. The tool also provides testimonials and showcases of trending Supademos for inspiration.
