About LastApply

LastApply is an AI-powered tool designed to streamline and enhance the job application process. After registering and uploading their current resume, users can add job applications by supplying the relevant links. LastApply utilizes its AI capabilities to extract pertinent details from these applications, enabling it to dynamically shape the user's resume to align closely with the job in question. Beyond bespoke resume creation, the tool also offers a cover letter generator, crafting custom letters attuned to the specific job and company a user is applying to. The tool additionally features job market insights to supplement users' job search efforts. A premium tier is available that offers unlimited job application tracking, resume tailoring, cover letter creation, more extensive job market insights and analytics, and AI-powered interview preparation. Thus, LastApply functions as a multi-faceted aid for job applicants, combining AI expertise with an intuitive interface to enhance users' prospects and efficiency within their job search.
