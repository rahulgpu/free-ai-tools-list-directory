About JobCopilot

JobCopilot is an AI-powered tool designed to help job seekers automate their job applications. Its main function is to automatically apply to job postings from numerous companies worldwide based on user-defined parameters. This tool offers convenient features like a Job Application Tracker which allows users to manage and track their job applications in one simple dashboard. JobCopilot also utilizes an AI Resume Builder which assists in improving or rewriting resumes. In addition to that, there is a AI Cover Letter Builder to generate professional cover letters tailored for different jobs. The tool is designed with a focus on time efficiency and improving the job hunting experience by enabling automatic application to suitable jobs using multiple filters and options. It continuously looks for new jobs posted daily on over multiple company career pages. This eliminates the risk of missing out on job opportunities or applying too late to be considered. JobCopilot aims to reduce the time spent on searching for jobs and filling in forms by handling these processes on the user's behalf.
