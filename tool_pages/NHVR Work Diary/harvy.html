About Harvy

HARVY is an AI-powered portal designed to automate and streamline the processing of daily work diary sheets and improve operational efficiency for heavy vehicle compliance and NHVR driver work diary reporting. The platform automates data entry, reduces errors, and accelerates compliance reporting, ultimately saving time and costs for transport businesses.By automating data entry, HARVY frees up valuable time for employees to focus on strategic activities that drive growth. It ensures consistent adherence to NHVR laws and promotes a culture of regulatory compliance by rapidly reporting breaches, reducing the risk of fines and penalties.The platform also provides quick access to historical work diary sheets, eliminating the need for physical filing cabinets and allowing users to generate compliant reports by driver and date range. HARVY's user-friendly interface makes it easy for staff to automate consistent NHVR work diary data entry and logbook compliance.Additionally, HARVY offers best practice insights and analytics tools to monitor efficiency gains and turnaround times, facilitating continuous improvement. The platform's AI-driven smarts scan and extract information from work diary sheets, flagging breaches and potential errors for action. Its intuitive workflow enables efficient review and management of work diary data.HARVY can be easily integrated into transport operations through a simple onboarding process, which includes personalized demos, discovery calls to understand business needs, staff onboarding, and training. The aim of HARVY is to simplify complex processes, reduce operational costs, improve the bottom line, and help businesses move faster in the transport industry.
