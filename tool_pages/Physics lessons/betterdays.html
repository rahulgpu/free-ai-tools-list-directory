About BetterDays

BetterDays is an AI-powered educational tool designed to assist students in mastering physics concepts. Intended to supplement traditional learning methods, it operates by offering clear explanations, fostering understanding, and thereby improving academic performance. The tool leverages artificial intelligence to adapt in real time to the unique educational needs and progress pace of each user. Its unique personalized tutoring approach aims to alleviate the struggle and frustration students commonly encounter while studying complex physics concepts. This makes it an effective aid for learners seeking to enhance their understanding and performance in physics. Notably, the tool also provides users with the convenience of logging in using their Google account, and prioritizes user privacy as outlined in its privacy policy.
