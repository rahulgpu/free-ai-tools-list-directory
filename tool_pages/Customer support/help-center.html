About Help Center

Help.center is a customer support solution leveraging artificial intelligence to offer robust knowledge base services. It empowers businesses to provide instant responses to their customers' inquiries, improving customer satisfaction. It hosts features such as AI search and a chat widget, both geared towards increasing the speed and efficiency of customer interactions. The platform allows a business to launch a personal 'help.center' website, where customers can use the AI-enabled services. It also accommodates the deployment of an AI chat widget on the business's primary website, delivering assistance in a conversational format. Help.center is not just directed to cater to customer queries but also reduces the number of support tickets by making use of artificial intelligence functionality, thus streamlining the issue-resolution process and saving resources. In addition, users can import existing knowledge into the platform, and it can be easily used in tandem with established customer interaction tools, providing multilingual support for seamless assistance. The AI component of Help.center learns with each addition or modification of an article, maximizing its effectiveness with use over time. The platform is secure and trusted by a wide array of companies, affirming its versatility and user-friendly interface.
