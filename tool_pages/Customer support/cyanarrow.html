About CyanArrow

CyanArrow is an AI-powered tool designed to enhance customer service through the utilization of AI chatbots and a ticketing system. Aimed at elevating efficiency and reducing costs of customer support functions, CyanArrow operates round the clock offering quality support to its users. Its features allow for instant responses to Frequently Asked Questions with high accuracy.By using CyanArrow, businesses can save significant time and resources dedicated to customer support tasks. The tool is successful in minimizing the volume of support tickets by offering proactive solutions. It includes a user-friendly setup process where users can upload their data for AI training, customize the appearance of their chatbot and ticket forms, and simply embed the chatbot or ticket form script onto their website.In addition, it allows tracking of active conversations, captured leads, and raised tickets, providing a comprehensive oversight in dealing with customer queries and concerns. It offers capabilities such as quick training on data, lead collection using custom forms, fine-tuning responses using conversation history, and quick and accurate response delivery.Moreover, CyanArrow includes a feature to customize the interface of the chatbot to fit the style of a user's website. Overall, it aims to streamline customer support tasks, saving time, and reducing the workload of managing customer service.
