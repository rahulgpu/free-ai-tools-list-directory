About Brainfish

Brainfish is an AI-powered help center and content platform designed to enhance customer experience. Its primary function is to provide instant answers to customer questions, serving as an automated self-service interface. This AI tool is designed to learn your content, enabling it to deliver accurate and personalized responses to customer queries. The aim is to reduce ticket volumes, improving overall customer satisfaction. In addition, Brainfish offers real-time translation capabilities, serving global customers in their native language by translating your content to suit their language preferences. One of the compelling features of Brainfish is its functionality that provides insights into customer concerns in real time, assisting customer experience teams enhance the efficacy of the content in their knowledgebase. Furthermore, Brainfish enables the majority of customers to self-serve, reducing the need for extensive customer service interactions. The platform is designed for seamless integration with various human-support software such as Zendesk, Intercom, Salesforce and others, ensuring an improved and efficient customer service experience.
