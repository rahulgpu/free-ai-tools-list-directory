About Chatling

Chatling is a no-code AI chatbot tailored for websites. Its primary application includes customer support, lead generation, and knowledge base search, which are essential for business operations. Users can create advanced AI chatbots in a visual manner with no coding skills required, using AI models such as GPT and Claude. The chatbots can be added to any website to automate processes like customer support and sales. Chatling features a drag-and-drop builder for design ease, and allows you to train the AI chatbot automatically using your data sources. Customization options are available, enabling users to align the chatbot's appearance with their brand. The platform further aids in monitoring and analysis of the chatbot's performance, thereby contributing to its improvement over time. A unique feature of Chatling is its multilingual support, as it can communicate with users in over 85 languages. Other impressive capabilities include accuracy in responses, privacy and security of data, scalability to handle any number of users and the provision for integration with other tools.
