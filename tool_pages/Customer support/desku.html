About Desku

Desku is a robust help desk software that aims to boost customer support and retention. It offers seamless communication by integrating live chat, email, and social media platforms into one centralized platform. This streamlines messages and enhances customer loyalty by providing a unified and efficient support system.Desku provides a range of features and solutions tailored to different industries, including retail, education, and NGOs. The tool offers shared email inboxes, social media inboxes, live chat software, and a customer knowledge base powered by AI.
