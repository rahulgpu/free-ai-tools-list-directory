About GaliChat

GaliChat is an AI-powered, no-code chatbot designed to provide 24/7 customer support. The tool is built to facilitate both lead generation and customer service for businesses across diverse industries such as healthcare, finance, retail, hospitality, and more. GaliChat primarily aims to reduce customer support costs and response times by offering personalized responses to customer inquiries around the clock. The chatbot can be conveniently trained on user website data in three steps, requiring the user to simply add their website link or files, train the chatbot on this data and finally deploy it on their site. The chatbot is capable of adapting to every industry owing to its training on a variety of formats like text, PDFs, and links. Key features include lead generation, accuracy of answers, customer support and being privacy-focused. The chatbot interface can also be customized to match the branding style of the business. Furthermore, GaliChat supports more than 50 languages, ensuring accessibility and ease of use for global customers. Importantly, the data import process is straightforward and quick, and the chatbot bases its responses on advanced AI and Natural Language Processing technologies.
