About Userdesk

Userdesk is an AI assistant platform that allows businesses to build and train AI chatbots resembling ChatGPT. These AI chatbots can be tailored and trained with data from various sources including websites, Notion, PDFs, and Google Drive. Userdesk simplifies lead generation and customer support by delivering instant replies to user queries, without the need for human intervention. The AI analyzes content from your documents and selects the most relevant information to provide answers to users' questions. As an added functionality, Userdesk collects data on user questions, allowing businesses to identify common issues and patterns, which can be instrumental in improving products and services. The platform supports over 52 languages, enabling interactions with customers in their preferred language, regardless of the language of your knowledge base. Additionally, Userdesk provides cross-platform support and can ideally be integrated with platforms such as WordPress, Webflow, Wix, Squarespace, and Shopify. Furthermore, Userdesk continuously learns and updates itself when you update your data, ensuring it provides the most recent and relevant information to your customers, thereby reducing manual effort.
