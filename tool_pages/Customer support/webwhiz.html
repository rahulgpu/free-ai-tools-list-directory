About WebWhiz

An artificial intelligence (AI) tool, WebWhiz functions as an AI-powered assistant integrated into your website to deliver instant, precise responses to support queries. Capabilities akin to chatGPT are used within the tool to answer questions specific to the data on your website, meaning responses are accurate and relevant.WebWhiz features an easy integration process; the tool can be created, trained, and added to your website in minutes, requiring no coding skills. The chatbot relies upon the data from your website to form its responses, with the website regularly crawled for updates to keep the chatbot's information up to date.Beyond its practical implementation, the chatbot can also be customized to match the aesthetics of your website, with additional fine-tuning options available to improve its function. Translation into over 100 languages allows visitors from various regions to engage with the bot comfortably, increasing its accessibility.WebWhiz upholds data privacy policies, strictly maintaining GDPR compliance. The tool has potential to contribute significantly to lead generation by collating visitor email addresses and efficiently reducing support volumes, freeing up your support team to tackle more complex queries.It must be noted that all the code for WebWhiz is available for review on GitHub, emphasizing its transparency and security.
