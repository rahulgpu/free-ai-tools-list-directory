About Galeby

Talknotes is an AI-powered tool that facilitates the transformation of voice notes into coherent, structured, and actionable text. The user can record voice notes in more than 50 languages, which are then transcribed by the AI. The transcriptions can be further formatted into various styles including task lists, transcripts, blog posts, and more, thereby turning raw thoughts and ideas into useful content. Users can customize the content with the provided markdown editor, organizing it into folders and adding tags. Talknotes also offers the functionality to export notes to various applications via Zapier or download them in PDF or Markdown formats. Furthermore, quick sharing is enabled by generating a public link to the notes. This tool is available on Web, iOS, and Android platforms, making it accessible across multiple devices. This is helpful for a variety of use cases including brainstorming sessions, content creation, voice journaling, interview transcription, and during meetings or study sessions.
