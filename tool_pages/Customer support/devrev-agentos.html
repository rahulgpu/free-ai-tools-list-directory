About DevRev AgentOS

DevRev AgentOS is a cloud-based platform designed to help in improving the efficiency and coordination among customer support and software development functions. The tool uses artificial intelligence technology to analyze and cluster customer feedback, helping product managers gain valuable insights. DevRev AgentOS features an in-app search function and 'nudges' - both AI-powered - to enhance the customer experience without compromising the self-service aspect. It also supports live chatting and fine-tuning for continuous learning. The tool is designed to help align technical support with product engineering, streamline tier 2 support processes, and decrease debugging and root cause analysis time. Its AI agents can reduce time taken in customer problem-solving by optimizing priority setting. DevRev AgentOS also includes analytics capabilities that offer a 360 view of customer and product analytics and help in making informed decisions, tracking team performance, and standardizing business processes. It has a serverless workflow engine to define business processes guiding agents and prompt objectives to automate tasks. Various connectors allow actions to be specified for agents to complete across systems. An advanced AI-powered knowledge graph connects your work to its impacts on the customer. DevRev AgentOS also simplifies data exploration and maintains data access permissions across different sources. Altogether, it aims to provide an intuitive and social experience for its users through its beautifully designed apps and enhanced engagement with seamless in-app and portal interactions.
