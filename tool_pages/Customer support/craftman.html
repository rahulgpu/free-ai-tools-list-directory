About Craftman

Craftman is an AI chatbot builder called Custom ChatGPT. Its main purpose is to provide a chatbot solution for sales and support on websites. By training ChatGPT on your specific data, Craftman enables you to create a custom chatbot widget that can be easily integrated into your website for faster customer support.The tool emphasizes increasing customer engagement and understanding user needs. It allows visitors to ask questions and provides AI-driven accurate responses, ensuring immediate and high-quality interactions. Craftman claims that using their chatbot can speed up response times by up to 10 times compared to manual handling.Craftman also highlights its ability to improve user satisfaction and boost revenue by creating a positive customer experience. The chatbot is designed to make website interactions easy and enjoyable, with the aim of converting visitors into customers.Additionally, Craftman offers features such as effortless feedback collection, direct feature request channels, and use cases for customer support, sales, and wiki purposes. The tool allows customization of responses to align with your brand and voice.Craftman offers different pricing plans based on the number of messages per month, with a free trial option available. It also offers a 30-day money-back guarantee.
