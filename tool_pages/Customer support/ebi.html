About Ebi

EBI.AI offers an AI assistant designed to handle a significant portion of your routine enquiries. Unlike basic AI chatbots, this tool is capable of providing support across your entire organization. The AI assistant is easy to set up and is aimed at improving customer happiness while reducing costs. The offerings are comprehensive, including AI SaaS that can go live swiftly, AI Supervision where the AI is managed for you, and AI Services that apply AI across your entire organization. The tool features options for human in the loop, natural language processing, security and compliance, along with integration capabilities. EBI.AI also offers a variety of support depending on the size and industry of the company, with options suitable for enterprises, SMEs, and startups across fields like retail, e-commerce, travel, finance, insurance, and more. The AI assistant offered by EBI has proven to increase capacity, deliver ROI rapidly, and it's scalable to accommodate growth. It operates on a pay-as-you-grow model, giving you freedom to tailor the service to your needs. The assistant also features personalization and automation capabilities that increase customer loyalty, streamline administration, and enhance efficiency.
