About Tended

Tended.ai is a tender automation platform that utilizes AI and ChatGPT technology to streamline the tendering process. By connecting data sources, Tended.ai produces data-enhanced responses in natural language, allowing users to answer RFP questions efficiently and with confidence. The platform features AutoFill, which fills in tenders automatically, saving valuable time. Team collaboration tools and notifications further enhance productivity. Tended.ai aims to allow users to complete tenders faster and more accurately than ever before, helping businesses win more contracts. The platform's design philosophy is centered around simplicity, with a three-step process that includes connecting data sources, importing tenders, and fine-tuning responses with help from AI. Tended.ai offers real-time product updates, industry news, and educational resources on its blog. The platform's value proposition is based on its ability to free up users' time, allowing them to focus on growing their business. A 7-day free trial is available, and users can sign up without providing credit card details. Tended.ai emphasizes compliance and features clear policies on privacy and cookies.
