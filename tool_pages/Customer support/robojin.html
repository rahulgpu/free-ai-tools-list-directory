About Robojin

RobojinAI is an AI-powered tool designed specifically for customer support. It seamlessly integrates with popular customer support systems like Crisp Chat, Intercom, and Freshdesk, offering enhanced response capabilities. Using RobojinAI, businesses can provide prompt, accurate, and helpful responses to customer queries, leading to reduced wait times and increased customer satisfaction.One of the key benefits of RobojinAI is its ability to harness ChatGPT technology, which allows it to provide consistent and high-quality answers based on various sources such as website content, knowledge base, PDF documents, SOPs, and FAQs. This ensures that customers receive accurate information and support.Additionally, RobojinAI is capable of handling a high volume of inquiries simultaneously, making it suitable for businesses of all sizes. It also supports multiple languages, enabling inclusive and accessible customer service on a global scale.RobojinAI offers different pricing plans to cater to varying requirements. These plans include features like the number of chatbots, web/PDF pages, languages supported, and ChatGPT response limits. There is also a custom plan option for businesses with specific needs.It is important to note that RobojinAI requires users to provide their own OpenAI API key for training the chatbot and answering customer inquiries. The tool ensures the security of API keys by encrypting and storing them safely in the database.The integration of RobojinAI with popular chat providers like Crisp Chat is already available, with integration options for Intercom, Zendesk, and Freshdesk coming soon. Overall, RobojinAI empowers businesses to improve their customer support capabilities by leveraging AI technology.
