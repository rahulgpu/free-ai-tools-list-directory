About CronbotAI

Users hate "Usual response time is hours/days" for a chatbot. Thus, we decided to solve this problem by building CronbotAI, a tool that can reply to your users queries in seconds using AI trained on your data, such as website content. With CronbotAI, you can: - Deploy a customisable AI live chat widget to your website/dashboard in two minutes. - Have a CRM to manage leads and the sales cycle. - Connect your chatbot with tools you use every day, such as Slack & Email. - Have analytics to pinpoint common user pain points, enabling you to enhance your product.
