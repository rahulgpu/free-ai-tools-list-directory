About Norby AI

Norby AI is a web assistant designed to make websites smarter by providing prompt and correct responses to customer queries. Users simply copy-paste their product or service descriptions, documentations, or frequently asked questions, and Norby responds to customer inquiries round the clock. The solution is particularly useful for startups, personal brands, and small companies. With no coding knowledge required, Norby AI is accessible to everyone and demands no setup to get started. Users only need to create an account, insert a snippet of code into their web page, and upload their FAQs into the automation system. As a result, this user-friendly tool becomes a readily-available web page assistant, making it an easy, swift, and practical solution for those aiming to improve their user experience and customer service on their website.
