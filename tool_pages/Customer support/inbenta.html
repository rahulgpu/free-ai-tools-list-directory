About Inbenta

Inbenta is a comprehensive Enterprise AI Platform known for its capacity to automate answers to customer queries, thereby optimizing operational efficiency. The platform leverages proprietary AI technology, which has been trained over a decade using data from billions of customer interactions across various industries. The Inbenta suite includes a Chatbot, Search, Benti, Knowledge, and Digital Instructor. The platform and its constituent tools are used in a diverse range of applications including Customer Service, IT Helpdesk, Marketing & Sales, HR, and Digital Transformation, signifying its broad-ranging use cases. Additionally, it caters to an extensive range of industries encompassing Banking, Government, Healthcare, E-commerce, Utilities, and more. Inbenta promotes the enhancement of customer experience and the reduction of costs through intelligent automation. It is trusted by companies around the globe, with the option for partnership and reselling opportunities. The platform continuously evolves, as evidenced by regular updates and innovations.
