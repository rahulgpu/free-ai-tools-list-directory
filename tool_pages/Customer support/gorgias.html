About Gorgias

Gorgias is a helpdesk tool designed specifically for e-commerce stores. The tool provides an all-in-one support inbox that allows businesses to easily centralize and manage customer interactions across various channels, including email, social media, live chat, voice, and SMS. With its automation features, businesses can provide instant resolutions to common customer service enquiries. Utilizing AI, Gorgias is able to automate tasks such as prioritizing, assigning, and tagging support tickets, thus improving efficiency and productivity. Another key feature is the ability to automatically process returns, send tracking information, generate discount codes, update subscriptions, and more, without the need to open Shopify or other e-commerce platforms. Gorgias also offers customer insights, giving customer service teams access to each customers order history, loyalty status, subscriptions, reviews, and more. This information can be used to provide more personalized and helpful responses. The tool is compatible with popular e-commerce platforms such as Shopify, BigCommerce, Magento, and WooCommerce.
