About Chatwoot

Chatwoot.ai is an open-source AI-powered customer support platform that offers an alternative to Intercom. Its main goal is to enhance the customer support experience without compromising user experience. With the help of AI, Chatwoot.ai can distinguish between support requests and simple questions, resulting in a more efficient support system.One key feature of Chatwoot.ai is the provision of self-service tools for customers, allowing them to find answers to their questions independently. This empowers customers and reduces the workload on support teams, resulting in improved customer satisfaction and team efficiency.The platform provides a universal search interface called Chat-GPT, which allows customers to interact with articles and find information without the need to contact a support agent. If assistance is required, the live chat SDK ensures that customers can easily reach the support team for immediate help.Chatwoot.ai also offers enhanced AI tools to supercharge agent productivity. It provides recommendations for replies, priority, labels, and more based on the context of each conversation, which speeds up response times and improves accuracy. AI is also utilized to suggest responses based on conversation context, enhancing communication efficiency and accuracy.Additionally, Chatwoot.ai prioritizes incoming conversations through auto triage based on content and can identify and resolve duplicate or similar conversations to improve response times. The platform offers natural language search capabilities and smart compose for help center articles, simplifying the creation process and reducing errors.Security is a priority for Chatwoot.ai, as it supports self-hosted installations and is SOC 2 Type II compliant. Users have control over their own customer data and can ensure compliance with regulations like GDPR, CPRA, and HIPAA. The platform also allows for seamless switching between various vendors or self-hosted Large Language Models, ensuring data privacy and control.
