About Creatus

Creatus.AI is an AI-native workspace designed to enhance productivity and optimize resource expenditure within small-to-medium businesses. The platform integrates AI capabilities and tools with daily office tools such as Canva, Notion, Airtable, and Zapier to offer custom solutions tailored to enterprises' unique needs. With Creatus, users have access to a suite of autonomous capabilities including video generation, task organization, and the creation of a conversational avatar. The platform positions itself as a complementary digital co-worker, providing independent functions similar to a private Google workspace. Creatus.AI also offers a range of AI models and tools, including text-to-video, image editing, AI avatars and face swap options, image generation, and image animation, among others. Their offerings are diverse, from chat capabilities with PDF files to image-to-HTML transformations. Moreover, Creatus specializes in custom AI integrations and solutions, utilizing a range of AI expertise areas like Generative Adversarial Networks (GAN), Natural Language Processing (NLP), Conversational AI, Machine Learning, Computer Vision, and others, to solve unique business challenges and implement sector-specific solutions. Their commitment extends to identifying if an AI solution exists and ensuing its implementation from the initial concept to the final execution stage.
