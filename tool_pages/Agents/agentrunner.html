About AgentRunner

Capably is an Enterprise AI Management Platform designed with the aim to boost productivity by integrating artificial intelligence into company operations. This platform allows the creation, onboarding, training and deployment of AI employees across organizations. Capably simplifies the process of AI adoption by offering a plethora of tools that provide visibility, and control over the use of AI within an organization, without needing specialized knowledge in the area. Capably's platform features include a simplified process of integrating AI into existing workflows, seamless connection with company databases, documents, and information resources, advanced user management controls, permissions, and dedicated user support. Using Capably, businesses can integrate AI employees into their teams following a straightforward onboarding process. It is designed to review the performance of these AI employees, develop their capabilities, and train them according to company requirements. Moreover, Capably also provides robust control over the behavior and use of AI employees within an organization, ensuring safety and compliance. Ease of adoption and integration, coupled with a user-friendly interface, makes the platform particularly suitable for users with no AI expertise.
