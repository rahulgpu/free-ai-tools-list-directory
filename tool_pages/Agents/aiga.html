About AIGA

AIGA provides custom-built AI agents intended to enhance and streamline business operations across various departments. The agents are designed to automate repetitive tasks, aid decision making, and support strategic business activities. Specific agents include the Lead Manager Agent, which uses advanced data analysis techniques to manage sales leads, and the Executive PA, which helps schedule meetings and arrange travel. Also available is the Sales Rep Assistant Agent offering comprehensive support in handling objections, and the Employee Onboarding Agent facilitating smooth and frictionless integration of new employees. Other prominent AI agents are the Product Knowledge Agent offering detailed product information, and the Leadership Coach Agent that provides insights to improve leadership skills and decision making. AIGA's AI agents can be integrated with other applications and systems for enhanced operational performance. The offering includes 24/7 support and ensures immediate results and visible growth, optimized to unique business needs.
