About BetterFriendAI

BetterFriendAI is an AI-powered assistant designed to transcribe, summarize, and remember key points from your voice notes, enhancing your networking abilities. Primarily targeted towards busy individuals who partake in frequent meetings, calls, and networking events, the tool aims to alleviate the cost of lost time, money, and relationships due to forgetting important details. The primary feature provides an efficient way to capture conversations by recording voice notes which are then transcribed and summarized by AI, eliminating the need for manual note-taking. The tool also offers a 'Smart Follow-Ups' feature that suggests personalized follow-up questions for future meetings or social events based on previously recorded interactions. Lastly, the 'Energy Boosters' feature analyses individual interactions to identify connections that provide motivation, aiming to optimize the user's networking channels and experience. The tool offers a free version with voice-to-text transcription and basic note organisation, and a paid version which extends functionalities to include energy mapping and advanced note tagging.
