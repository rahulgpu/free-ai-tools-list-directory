About Muzaic for Canva

Muzaic for Canva is a plugin that allows users to generate custom music for videos made using Canva, leveraging AI technology. Users can select a mood and adjust the intensity to create a unique soundtrack, custom-made by the AI. Furthermore, users also have the option to regulate certain parameters such as rhythm, tone, tempo, and variance or specify the exact duration needed. With the help of tags, users can navigate and select from a wide range of music genres and styles. The application of Muzaic's AI technology provides the potential to generate a diverse array of music styles, tailored to a projects specific requirements. From romantic to happy or epic moods, or genres such as jazz or pop, the app offers a comprehensive range. One of the significant benefits of this tool is that exported audio is high-quality, royalty-free, and can be used freely within the Canva projects. However, it cannot be downloaded for separate usage outside a Canva project. The Muzaic for Canva app offers a valuable solution for video and social media creators looking for personalized and copyright-free soundtrack possibilities.
