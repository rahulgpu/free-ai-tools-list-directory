About Color Fuse AI

The AI Chat Color Palette Generator is a tool designed to assist designers in generating color palettes for their projects. With the help of an AI chatbot, users can easily and quickly create and visualize color schemes. The tool offers a One-Click Apply feature, allowing users to apply the generated color palette directly to design projects in Illustrator.The AI Chat Color Palette Generator is compatible with popular design tools such as Illustrator, Photoshop (2022 or later), and Figma. It offers features such as AI-generated palettes, color scheme customization, and the ability to save palettes for future use.The tool is available in both free and pro versions. The free version provides basic functionality, including the AI chatbot, color scheme generation, and palette customization. The pro version, available for a monthly fee, offers additional benefits such as the ability to save and access palettes from anywhere.By utilizing AI, designers can save time and effort by avoiding the manual selection of color schemes from pre-existing palettes. The AI Chat Color Palette Generator aims to simplify the color selection process, making it more efficient for both designers and non-designers alike.Please note that exact numbers and pricing information have been purposely excluded from this description to avoid potential changes in the future.
