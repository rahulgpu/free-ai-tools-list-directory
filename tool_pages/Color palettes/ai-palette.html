About AI Palette

AI Palette is a Figma plugin that simplifies the process of creating a different set of color palettes for your designs using AI technology. With this innovative tool, designers can quickly and effortlessly generate unique and diverse colors without the need for tedious manual color selection. By clicking on the generate button, the plugin quickly detects the current color palette and generates new suggested palettes every time you click. The designer can apply the generated color palette in their design by clicking on the apply button, which changes the color in the artboard. The tool also features a chat GPT AI model that designers can use to generate unique color palettes from text input. To use this feature, designers simply have to enter the color they want in the input box and click on the search button to generate a new color palette. The suggested color palette can then be modified easily by clicking on the suggested color and choosing other colors from the window color palette. AI Palette saves designers time by streamlining the color selection process and bringing greater efficiency to their workflow. Overall, it is a user-friendly and innovative tool that simplifies the process of creating unique and diverse color palettes for your designs.
