About Extracurrify

Extracurrify is a college preparation platform that utilizes artificial intelligence to offer personalized advice to high school students, aiding in their college application process. Its primary functionalities include an AI-guided counseling system, designed to help students navigate the selection of extracurricular activities, choice of major and other critical decisions. The platform also encompasses a comprehensive database of extracurricular activities and scholarships, which are tailored to students' interests and academic focus. These resources are connected with students' chosen majors, offering access to a range of opportunities such as internships, competitions and volunteering experiences. Furthermore, this platform is designed to support students from diverse backgrounds, including international students and those from low-income backgrounds, accommodating their unique needs and aspirations. In addition to its free offerings, Extracurrify provides premium features at student-friendly pricing plans. Though primarily accessed via a web interface, inquires about mobile application versions can be handled by their support team.
