About Mediachance

AI Photo & Art Enhancer, developed by Mediachance, is an artificial intelligence tool designed to enhance the clarity, detail, and resolution of photos and artwork. This tool can dramatically increase the resolution of photos, potentially transforming general graphics and artwork into a quality suitable for gallery printing. The tool is efficient for enlarging vector-type illustrations and digital art and can also enhance the resolution of 3D rendered images, making partially rendered images appear as if they have been completed. A powerful feature is its ultra-efficient Noise Reduction model, meaning it could also be used for the removal of noise from images more effectively than traditional noise reduction models. This tool can help digital artists in preserving the artistic qualities of their work by adding an astonishing amount of small details to their digital paintings, graphics, and other images. The software is designed to operate on recent Windows versions and requires a reasonably powerful graphics card for best performance. It also has the capability to process batches of images, a feature that can save time for users with bulk image requirements.
