About Eazy Editor

Eazy Editor is an AI-powered tool that offers a range of features to streamline the editing process for images and videos. With its automatic background removal technology, users can easily remove backgrounds from their visuals, allowing for easy addition of new elements or changing the background altogether. The object eraser tool enables quick and effortless removal of unwanted objects or elements from images or videos, saving time and effort in the editing process. Eazy Editor also provides the capability to add or remove shadows, making objects stand out and adding depth to images. Furthermore, the tool offers an automatic text eraser feature, allowing for easy removal of text from images with just one click. Resizing images or videos to specific dimensions for social media or e-commerce purposes is made simple by Eazy Editor. The tool supports batch editing, enabling the application of changes to multiple images simultaneously, saving time and streamlining workflows. Eazy Editor prides itself on a user-friendly and intuitive interface, making it accessible to users of all skill levels. It also offers a cost-effective solution, providing all the necessary editing tools at a reasonable price. Users who join the beta program get exclusive free access to the software, the opportunity to provide feedback that influences the final product, early access to new features, and valuable learning opportunities.
