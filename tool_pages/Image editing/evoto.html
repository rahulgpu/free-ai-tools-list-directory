About Evoto

Evoto is an advanced AI-powered photo editor that revolutionizes your photo editing workflow and empowers your creativity. This next-generation photo retouching tool combines all your editing work into one powerful app, streamlining your process and eliminating repetitive tasks. With Evoto, adding cutting-edge effects to your photos is made easy.The tool offers precise face, gender, and age detection, allowing you to touch up your photos and achieve a natural glow on all skin types. It also provides face sculpting and body reshaping tools, along with realistic digital makeup options. Additionally, Evoto offers advanced color editing features, giving you complete control over your photo adjustments, including exposure, white balance, and color correction settings.Evoto simplifies background adjustments with accurate sky replacement in seconds and the ability to change or remove backgrounds effortlessly. The tool also includes exclusive presets designed by experienced designers, enabling you to effortlessly edit your photos with just a few clicks. You can even save and share your own custom presets to showcase your unique style.To enhance efficiency, Evoto offers batch processing capabilities, allowing you to edit multiple photos simultaneously. Simply select the photos you wish to edit, choose your presets, and let the software do the rest.Stay up-to-date with the latest techniques and features by being the first to try out new additions to Evoto. Experience the future of photo editing and unleash your creativity with this intuitive AI photo editor.
