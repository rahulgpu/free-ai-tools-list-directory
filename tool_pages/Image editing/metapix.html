About Metapix

Metapix is a powerful photo and video editing tool that allows users to express themselves creatively. With the help of generative AI, users can redefine the art of editing and achieve stunning edits with just a single click. The tool is designed to cater to all editing needs, offering an intuitive interface and a range of powerful features.One of the standout features of Metapix is its face swap capability, allowing users to reimagine reality by swapping faces in photos. Additionally, the tool provides the option to revamp one's look with AI-based hair color changes. Users can also enjoy a rich collection of virtual characters and spice up their creativity with fun stickers.Metapix offers a variety of editing tools for different purposes, including blur, cropping, resizing, watercolor effects, and zoom background adjustments. The tool also includes a range of templates and options specifically tailored for social media platforms like Facebook, Instagram, Twitter, and YouTube, enabling optimal presentation and engagement.For marketing purposes, Metapix provides tools for creating brochures, business cards, flyers, gift certificates, logos, posters, and wallpapers. The tool also offers smart filters, such as duotone effects, double exposure, and image upscaling.Metapix extends its functionality beyond photo editing, providing a video editor that allows users to remix music, adjust video backgrounds, enhance videos using AI, apply makeup filters, add subtitles, resize videos, and more.Overall, Metapix is a versatile tool that empowers users to express their creativity and enhance their visual content through advanced AI-based editing features.
