About NeuralPix

NeuralPix is an AI-powered photo editing and enhancement application developed by NeuralCam. This app offers various features to improve the quality and aesthetics of your photographs. With it, you can import or capture photos for enhancement or scan physical photos that need improvement. The app's features include an AI Bokeh function, which works great especially on portraits and product photos, adding a professional DSLR feel by creating a depth map and selectively blurring the background. There is a Magic Eraser that can be used to remove imperfections from photos like people in the background, blemishes on faces or scratches on old photos. A Photo Enhancer is also included, providing a two-step process to enhance faces in the image using a special face restoration model, and to remove noise and deblur the context around it. The Unblur feature utilizes the latest image deblurring models to sharpen blurry sections of images. The AI Styles feature enhances your photos by applying styling models trained on professionally edited photos. Other features involve adding a white background to product or portrait photos, colorizing black and white photos, brightening underexposed areas of the photo, and upscaling photos to a higher definition. The application is free to download and use, and it does not contain ads or require subscriptions.
