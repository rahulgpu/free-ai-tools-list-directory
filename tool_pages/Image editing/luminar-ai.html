About Luminar AI

Skylum's Luminar Neo is an AI-powered photo editing software designed to serve both macOS and Windows users. This intuitive software uses AI to automatically perform a number of editing functions. Features include 'EnhanceAI' for enhancement of photo details, 'RelightAI' for lighting adjustments, 'Erase' for removal of unwanted objects, and 'AI Masking' for precise selection in photo manipulation. It also offers a unique 'SkyAI' tool that can seamlessly replace the skies in images and generate realistic reflections. The software utilizes automated scene relighting that considerably enhances landscapes and portraits. Further, 'AccentAI' allows control of diverse parameters like Shadows, Highlights, Contrast, Tone, Saturation, Exposure, and Details with a single slider. Adding a unique mood is made easy with 'AtmosphereAI' that places fog, mist, or haze in the images using content-aware masking. The software also enables fixing backlit photos & simulating out-of-focus backgrounds with tools like 'Relight AI' and 'Portrait BokehAI'. Skin retouching capabilities are provided by 'SkinAI', that automatically removes skin imperfections. The software can operate as a standalone application as well as a plugin, making it versatile for varying usage requirements.
