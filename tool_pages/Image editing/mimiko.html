About Mimiko

Mimiko is an AI-based mobile app available on Google Play that offers a variety of features for image manipulation. With Mimiko, users can upscale and restore old photos, manipulate images based on their inputs, get a glimpse of how they might look in the future, remove backgrounds from pictures, generate high-resolution graphics from detailed descriptions, and obtain answers from specific aspects of images. The app promises additional features to be added in the future.Mimiko emphasizes data safety and privacy. According to the developer, the app does not collect any user data and does not share data with third parties. The developer provides information about data privacy and security practices but may update it over time. The content rating is PEGI 3, indicating it is suitable for users of all ages.
