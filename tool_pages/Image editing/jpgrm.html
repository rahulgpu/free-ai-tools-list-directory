About jpgRM

AI Image Magic Cleanup is a tool for the removal of unwanted objects from images, leveraging the power of state-of-the-art AI models. This AI solution can automatically fill the background after an objects removal, reducing the need for manual refinement. The tool supports the removal of objects from various settings including landscapes, and it can erase dense crowds and smartly remove watermarks and product logos. It provides particular functionality for brush customization, which enables meticulous removal of specific contents. The tool's processing speed depends on the image's resolution, with higher resolutions taking more time. The platform is also mobile-friendly for on-the-go image processing, and provides a VIP service for high-resolution 4K image downloading, available to users who choose to upgrade. Free users have a 720px download limitation. The AI Image Magic Cleanup's algorithm is based on advanced generative adversarial network (GAN) technology, that carefully identifies content and background for precise alterations. The tool ensures user privacy by deleting user images immediately after processing. The tool is also backed up by a committed support service, available for any arising issues or further queries.
