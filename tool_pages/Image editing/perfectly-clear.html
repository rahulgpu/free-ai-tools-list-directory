About Perfectly Clear

EyeQ is a tool for automatic photo and video corrections and enhancements. It is designed to give businesses a competitive edge by providing efficient and accessible technology. EyeQ is powered by Cookiebot, which uses cookies to personalize content and ads, provide social media features, analyse traffic and share information with social media, advertising and analytics partners. Necessary cookies enable basic website functions like page navigation and access to secure areas, while preference and statistic cookies enable the website to remember language and region preferences as well as collect statistical data anonymously. Marketing cookies track visitors across websites and are used to display relevant and engaging ads. EyeQ also uses cookies to detect if the visitor has accepted the marketing category in the cookie banner, detect which server-cluster is serving the visitor, distinguish between humans and bots, determine optimal video quality, register data on visitor behaviour, and track individual sessions.
