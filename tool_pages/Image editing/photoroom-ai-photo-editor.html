About Photoroom: AI Photo Editor

Photoroom is an AI-powered photo editing app designed for suite of functions such as creating, editing and organizing product shots. The tool uses artificial intelligence to offer varied features like Background Removal, Magic Retouch, Instant Backgrounds, and Batch Editor which help to edit photos quickly and accurately while saving time. The built-in app tools help to create professional images for e-commerce websites, customize profile pictures and create marketing materials using built-in templates suited for multiple social media platforms. Additionally, the app allows users to remove unwanted objects in photos, refine edges of photos and resize them for any social media or selling platform. The tool offers Smart Templates, which provide recommendations best suited for your needs. Catering to the needs of creators and online sellers, the tool aids in digitizing their offerings and increasing sales. The tool is built for usage on platforms like iPad and iPhone and is accessible through the Apple App Store.
