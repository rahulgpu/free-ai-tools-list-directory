About PhotoKit

PhotoKit is an AI-enabled online photo editor designed for simplicity and accessibility. The tool offers a suite of photo editing features available from basic operations such as cropping, resizing, and rotation, to more advanced processing like clarity refocus, exposure adjustment, cutout, and the AI-patented 'Inpaint' tool, designed to remove unwanted elements from an image. User privacy is respected, with the system designed not to store user images or data. Online usage through modern browsers eliminates the need for download or installation. This tool also includes batch image processing, enabling multiple image adjustments at once across several formats including png, jpg, gif, bmp, tiff and RAW. PhotoKits features extend beyond image correction to include enhancement tools. For example, it enriches the quantity and interpretation of image data, optimizes brightness, and corrects exposure for an overall improvement in image quality. Its simple, user-friendly interface positioned alongside advanced functionalities make PhotoKit suitable for users with varying levels of photo editing skills.
