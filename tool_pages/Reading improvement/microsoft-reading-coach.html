About Microsoft Reading Coach

Reading Coach is an AI-powered educational tool designed to engage students and improve their reading fluency through AI-enabled stories and personalized practice. The platform can be accessed via web or through a dedicated Windows app. It is suitable for home use by parents and guardians, as well as for incorporation into schools by educators and IT administrators. The tools features include personalized learning where learners can co-create their stories with an AI to keep them engaged, even reluctant readers. It has targeted practice features where Reading Coach detects learners' challenging words and automatically embeds opportunities for additional, independent practice. The solution is inclusive by design, providing built-in support from Immersive Reader for emerging readers and learners with dyslexia. It follows the principles of fairness, reliability, safety, privacy, security, inclusiveness, accountability and transparency, ensuring the safety and privacy of learners using the platform. Readings achievements and progress tracking mechanism are also embedded in the tool, encouraging continuous engagement as learners unlock new story elements the more they read.
