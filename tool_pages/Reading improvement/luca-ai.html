About Luca AI

LUCA is an AI-powered reading tutor designed to improve reading skills, confidence and engagement in children. It employs speech recognition technology to build personalized stories, providing targeted interventions to enhance reading proficiency. With its word adventures, LUCA also improves vocabulary, turning learning into an engaging experience. The proprietary AI technology creates personalized learning plans to assist children in their success, generating student-centered content to inspire and engage readers. LUCAs platform is valuable for parents aiming to enhance their children's reading skills, becoming a key tool in childrens educational journey. It also includes a specific program to assist children overcome dyslexia challenges. The platform's Reading Solutions feature provides personalized lessons tailored to each student's instructional needs, fostering efficient learning and information retention. The technology also includes other modules such as StoryLabs for creating customized stories, LUCAListens for phonetic-level automatic speech recognition, and ProfessorAI for evaluating progress and delivering targeted intervention strategies. LUCA's methodology is underpinned by research by experts in dyslexia and reading.
