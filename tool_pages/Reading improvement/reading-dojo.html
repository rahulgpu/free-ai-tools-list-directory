About Reading Dojo

Reading Dojo is an AI-powered tool designed to simplify the process of creating and assessing reading comprehension exercises. The tool allows users to generate tailored questions that examine comprehension, with the flexibility to either create a comprehensive reading passage or use your own custom text. Reading Dojo caters to a variety of needs by producing up to 20 questions in multiple-choice or short answer formats, and supporting more than 10 languages. Teachers and parents can utilize the tool's multi-participant quizzes feature to administer reading checks to an entire class or a group of children, while students can self-assess their reading comprehension. The tool also offers the ability to save questions and reading passages as PDFs or printable worksheets, and allows users to work on particular reading comprehension skills such as inference and identifying the main idea. Furthermore, Reading Dojo enables users to edit the AI-generated content, export questions and answers to a CSV file, and offers explanations to understand the reasoning behind the correct answers. The developers of Reading Dojo are committed to continuously improving the tool, with plans for adding more features in the future.
