About Pitches.ai

Pitches.ai is an automated service designed to assist businesses and startups in creating investor and sales pitch decks. Its primary goal is to assist founders and sales leads in securing investments and closing deals by leveraging artificial intelligence. Pitches.ai offers a better design interface with light and dark themes, AI-generated images, integrated charts, and a cost-effective pricing model. Users can start the generation process by either uploading a draft or answering roughly 20 guided questions. In addition, the platform also features a 'Freestyle Mode' that allows users to create a pitch with minimal input. Pitches.ai also provides regular blog posts with valuable insights and strategies to help users improve their pitches. Improved features in its latest update include upgraded design, freestyle mode, automatic charts, and token-based pricing.
