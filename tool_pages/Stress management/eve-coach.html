About Eve Coach

Eve Coach is an AI tool developed as a personal assistant for dealing with workplace stress. Its primary function is to act as a coach, providing guidance and support midst stressful work situations, utilizing an action plan approach to combat stress. The tool's emphasis revolves around assisting its users to manage their stress better, contributing towards their overall wellbeing and productivity at work. Eve Coach values user privacy, as indicated by their privacy policy, pledging to adhere to high standards of data protection and user confidentiality. While Eve Coach primarily targets workplace stress, individuals may find its services helpful for managing stress arising from other aspects of life as well. Terms and conditions apply to its use. The app is currently accessible through the Apple App Store, and impending releases are expected on other platforms.
