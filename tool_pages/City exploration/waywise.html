About Waywise

Waywise is an essential and user-friendly city guide application for iOS, designed to assist travelers to explore any city on their own terms. Its core utility lies in providing guided assistance related to transport, money management, language, and understanding neighbourhoods rather than promoting restaurants or personalized itineraries. From navigating the quickest route from the airport to the city, to advising on appropriate tipping etiquettes, and even helping you figure out shopping districts in unfamiliar places, Waywise has a broad spectrum of helpful features. Each user's experience is personally catered towards them which allows for more accurate and personalized guidance. The application does not push any tours or tickets, but rather provides objective information to help a traveler independently explore and experience a city from their own perspective. It facilitates you in unveiling a city's character in a non-prescriptive manner echoing the ethos of deeply personal exploration. Furthermore, Waywise offers support and maintains a thorough and clear privacy policy and terms of service to ensure user's data safety and alleviate any potential concerns. The app is available for download on the App Store.
