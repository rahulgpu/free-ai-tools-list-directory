About Quazilla by Squad

Quazilla is an AI-powered personal coaching tool that helps users set and achieve personalized goals and develop positive habits. The tool is powered by ChatGPT, an AI conversational agent that can offer emotional support and assistance at any time of the day. Quazilla aims to keep users accountable and motivated by sending daily check-in reminders to track progress and offer encouragement. The tool is designed to be user-friendly and engaging, featuring a friendly AI monster as the coach. The tool is accessible through WhatsApp, making it easy to use and integrate into daily routines. It is also part of Squad Habits, a group accountability app and habit tracker. Quazilla requires users to engage in conversations with the AI-powered coach, where it asks questions and provides personalized tips and recommendations. These conversations are based on the user's goals, habits, and preferences, making for tailored coaching sessions. Quazilla's goal is to guide users towards developing a healthy and positive lifestyle by creating habits that are both sustainable and enjoyable. The tool is available for iOS devices and can be downloaded via the App Store. Overall, Quazilla is a useful tool for individuals looking to develop healthy habits, establish and achieve goals and receive positive encouragement along the way.
