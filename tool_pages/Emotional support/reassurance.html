About Reassurance

Reassurance AI is a mental health support tool featuring an Emotional Support AI chatbot named Sai. Sai is designed to provide emotional support, guidance, and advice when needed. It is interactive and remembers users' most recent interactions for seamless conversation continuity. Beyond the chatbot, the tool also provides a personal journal where users can log their thoughts, feelings, or concerns for future reference or self-reflection. If users prefer, they can choose to engage the journal feature independently of Sai, using it as a tool to express or vent without a response from the chatbot. Customization options are available to enhance user's experience, including multiple fonts, font sizes, and text-to-speech voices. Additional customization features, like journal themes, are planned for future versions of the service. Importantly, while Reassurance AI aims to provide a safe space for emotional support, it is not a replacement for therapy or professional mental health services.
