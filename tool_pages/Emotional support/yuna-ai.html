About Yuna AI

Yuna AI is an AI-powered tool used to offer mental health support to individuals. It acts as a digital health companion designed to assist with self-discovery and empower self-worth. The AI's features include emotional clarity and anxiety alleviation through delivering timely calming techniques, which can be accessed any time of the day. It also offers transformative thinking, helping users change their thought process positively. Yuna AI caters to its user's privacy keenly, ensuring that conversations within the tool remain confidential and the user's data is safe. Furthermore, it involves PhDs in clinical psychology in its development, ensuring the delivery of research-driven conversations. The application is also equipped with wellness tracking capabilities that monitor user's emotions and connects them with mental health professionals if necessary. For safety, Yuna AI employs real-time safety monitoring to identify signs of trauma or heightened emotions and immediately links the user to a healthcare professional if such signs emerge. A distinguishing trait of Yuna AI is that it offers quality mental health conversations at an affordable price.
