About X Beast

X Beast is an AI-driven tool designed to fully automate your X account posts. The tool majorly comprises two core functionalities - automatic AI text and image post generation, and automatic scheduling of different post types within a specified time range.The automatic post generation function uses Artificial Intelligence to develop unique and interesting text and image posts for your X account. This aspect of the tool caters to the need for consistent content generation, helping to keep your account active and engaging without the need for manual input at every juncture. The second key feature of X Beast is the ability to automate the scheduling of posts. Once a time range has been defined by the user, the tool automatically schedules the posting of different types of content within that range. This feature aims to increase workflow efficiency and enables consistent online presence, even during non-working hours.Together, these functionalities make X Beast a valuable tool for anyone looking to streamline their X account management activities. However, its applicability can extend beyond solely managing X accounts to potentially being useful for broader social media management. The tool supports the user in maintaining active engagement on their account through regular, varied content posting while also allowing them the luxury of pre-determined scheduling.
