About Ubique

Ubique is an innovative AI tool designed to boost levels of engagement by creating compelling, personalized video messages. Using advanced AI algorithms, it effectively mimics the user's voice and lip movements to customize video templates. This allows for personalized videos that give the impression the user is delivering fresh content each time. Ubique offers help with video scripting and advice to ensure maximum impact and audience resonance. Users also have the flexibility to customize numerous aspects of their contacts' information. This tool integrates smoothly with the most utilized sales tools, as well as supports data import through Excel or CSV files. Ubique provides users with key metrics to track the effectiveness of their video campaigns by showing who engaged with the content and the duration of their engagement, offering valuable insight on audience interaction and interest levels. It currently supports English language only. The tool is also complemented by an onboarding and sales video specialists team, providing expert advice and guidance throughout the setup process, and ensuring that the best possible videos are created to meet specific needs.
