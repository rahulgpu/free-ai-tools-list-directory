About Wallu

Wallu Bot is an AI chatbot designed to answer frequently asked questions (FAQs). It is easy to set up and use and is free to add to your Discord server. Wallu Bot uses modern language models to understand your members and automatically answer their questions. It can also learn from feedback given by members through the use of üëçüëé reactions. In addition, Wallu Bot features an easy to use admin panel that allows you to manually add FAQs, as well as suggest FAQs to add. Wallu Bot is also multilingual, supporting over 50 languages, with varying performance depending on the language. Wallu Bot also includes stats, such as the number of servers, members, FAQs answered, and total FAQs. Get started by adding Wallu Bot to your server and typing !wallu.
