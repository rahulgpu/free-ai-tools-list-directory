About Cyanite

Cyanite.ai is a tool designed to leverage artificial intelligence in order to enhance the way we search and discover music. It provides music recommendation services with the ability to generate comprehensive metadata for songs or music catalogs in a matter of seconds. The system can automatically tag various aspects of a song, such as its genre, mood, instruments, and tempo, freeing users from manual tagging. Metadata generated by Cyanite not only enriches the information about the music but also significantly simplifies management and search activities. With one of its features, users can input a reference song and discover similar music, thus providing valuable insights for music supervision, content creation, curation and more. The tool also offers a flexible keyword search, allowing the combination and weighting of different keyword to fine-tune the search process. A holistic Free Text Search enables the users to search for music using their own choice of words and descriptions. It also includes powerful visualization tools to identify patterns and show music data graphically, supporting various strategic applications like pitching and marketing. The AI framework and search technology can be integrated into current systems via an API, and is already compatible with various Catalog Management Systems.
