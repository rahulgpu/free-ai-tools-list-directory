About Autron

Autron is an AI-powered software that specializes in Amazon ad optimization and management designed for both brands and sellers. It features programmatic solutions for Amazon DSP and PPC. The software leverages artificial intelligence to automate ad campaign management, relieving users from the complexities of manual management and ACoS control efforts. Autron facilitates different ad types including Sponsored Products, Sponsored Brands, and Sponsored Display, aiming to boost sales, build brand awareness, and reach relevant audiences both on and off Amazon. Using its advanced decision-making engine, it simulates, predicts and delivers advertising decisions akin to having a personal data scientist and machine learning specialist. Besides campaign automation, Autron offers users features such as keyword and ASIN expansion negating the need for manual, lengthy keyword research. To add a layer of transparency, the software comes specified with advanced analytic tools, providing insights into total and organic sales growth, along with the blended ACoS. This enables users to understand their holistic financial performance and measure incremental sales lift from advertising spend.
