About AI Rubric Generator by Coursebox

The AI Rubric Generator is an automated tool designed to facilitate the process of assignment evaluation in the field of education. Its core functionality lies in generating practical assignment questions and marking criteria swiftly using artificial intelligence (AI). An integral part of this tool is creating a marking rubric which is subsequently used to instantly assess the paper submissions and deliver feedback to the learners. This tool boasts a feature that seamlessly converts media such as files and videos into rubric tables for simplicity and smooth operation. Compatibility with various formats ensures the tool's versatility when it comes to educational resources. The AI assessment feature provides learners with instant grading as well as feedback based on the parameters set in the marking rubric. The AI Rubric Generator also includes a chatbot function, serving as an AI tutor, offering real-time guidance to learners. The tool further offers specifications to allow for white-label branding for a seamless user experience along with the capacity to brainstorm and decide on course structures before creation. Additionally, the AI Rubric Generator can produce quizzes and offers support for mobile-friendly assessments for a convenient learner experience. The platform also allows for integration with existing learning management systems or HR platforms adding to its adaptability.
