About Robust Intelligence

Robust Intelligence is a platform designed to offer security and validation to AI models and data. This tool stands as an end-to-end solution to prevent potential adverse outcomes that can result from machine learning model failures. The platform provides real-time protection of machine learning models, notably against security threats, operational threats and ethical threats. Additionally, it offers an AI Firewall that secures production models against attacks and undesirable responses by validating inputs and outputs in real time. Another significant offering by Robust Intelligence is the Continuous Validation feature that ensures AI models and data are validated throughout the AI lifecycle. This is achieved by surfacing weaknesses via automated red teaming, while also establishing a standardized audit record for their health, and tracking adherence to standards and regulatory compliance. Furthermore, the platform supports Generative AI and other major modalities, while effectively managing AI risk in compliance with significant AI regulations, guidelines, and frameworks.
