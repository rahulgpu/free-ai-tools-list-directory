About Helpmee

Helpmee.ai is an AI-guided tech support and assistant, designed mainly to assist seniors or those with limited tech skills navigate and use their computers independently. The AI companion leverages OpenAIs latest GPT-4o model to facilitate patient, voice-enabled conversations and provide step-by-step guidance through tasks. This includes screen sharing and AI vision for more comprehensive and accurate assistance. Additionally, the service can cater to a wide range of users, as it offers support in over 50 languages and is available 24/7.For a better user experience, helpmee.ai also includes multilingual support and is accessible round-the-clock to ensure continuous assistance, whenever needed. Moreover, helpmee.ai handles personal data with prudence. It works by using voice to text transcription and AI-driven responses to help users, but does not store any personal data. Instead, all processing occurs in real-time, solely to assist the user.Subscription plans offer access to this personal AI-tech assistant with a set amount of monthly interaction time, suitable to seek personalized advice and solutions according to the user's unique tech challenges. It's versatile enough to discuss any topic of interest. Lastly, the tool works seamlessly across both macOS and Windows operating systems.
