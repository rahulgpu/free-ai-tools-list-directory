About Auditive

Auditive is an AI-powered platform that facilitates third-party risk management. The platform empowers businesses and their vendors to carry out security reviews in a speedy and efficient manner, without the need for repeated processes. Auditive is a multi-purpose tool that not only allows you to manage your stakeholders at an impressive scale, but it also helps you continuously monitor your vendors against your specific security requirements. The platform houses a network feature that promotes increased connectivity, allowing you to share security posture with customers and monitor vendors in one place. It is the first of its kind to streamline security reviews for both vendors and buyers. Key features include real-time notifications of changes to vendor risk posture, option to skip questionnaires and promote sales efforts with security profile, and the ability to manage risk and build customer trust on a singular platform. This tool provides an innovative approach towards automating security reviews, accelerating sales, and enabling continuous risk monitoring. Auditive is popular amongst businesses seeking to understand their true vendor risk in relation to company controls and requirements.
