About SWMS

SWMS AI is a safety and compliance tool that leverages artificial intelligence to devise comprehensive job-specific Safe Work Method Statements. This tool has the ability to identify potential hazards, assess corresponding risks, and propose relevant control measures, aiming to enhance both the safety and efficiency of workplace operations. It works by taking user input on project specifics such as activities involved, specialized equipment utilized, and existing hazards, from which it generates a safe work sequence, recognizing hazards, scoring risks, and suggesting suitable control measures. SWMS AI offers customizability, allowing conformity to a companys particular safety policies and standards. It services a wide array of industries including construction, mining, oil and gas, transportation, logistics, facilities management, and warehousing. Additionally, SWMS AI hosts 'Oscar', an AI assistant providing access to a considerable knowledge base of safety information from globally reputable sources. Oscar's functionality includes aligning document generation and query responses to the safety requirements, guides, and best practices of the company. The end result is universally accessible expert safety knowledge available in multiple languages. Together, these features present SWMS AI as a comprehensive tool for dynamically ensuring safety while meeting compliance requirements in various job environments.
