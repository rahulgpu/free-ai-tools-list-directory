About FertLi

FertLi is an AI-powered app designed to boost the chances of having a healthy baby and pregnancy. The tool provides data-driven fertility guidance, taking into account factors like irregular ovulation, low sperm quality, PCOS, thyroid issues, genetic issues, lifestyle and hormonal factors, as well as potential underlying health conditions. Its AI capabilities analyze user-provided health data and lifestyle information to predict the likelihood of pregnancy and potential risks. The platform's comprehensive approach encourages viewing couples as a unit and explicitly acknowledges that male factors contribute to infertility in up to 50% of cases. This data is then used to provide personalized insights and a unique fertility roadmap, as well as a lab test plan tailored to each user's specific needs. Designed by doctors and fertility specialists, it is a useful tool for both couples and individuals on their path to parenthood. The platform can also help those planning for egg freezing or considering Assisted Reproductive Technology (ART) procedures, including Intrauterine Insemination (IUI) and In Vitro Fertilization (IVF).
