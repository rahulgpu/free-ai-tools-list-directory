About Dealize AI

Dealize.AI is an AI-enhanced tool specially designed to provide assistance to account executives and sales managers in closing deals faster and achieving accurate revenue forecasts. Its features are created with the intention of increasing the deal size, shortening sales cycles, and providing precision in revenue forecasts. This is achieved by enabling the users to run multiple sales scenarios. It allows users to access their AI sales playbook and to integrate with existing sales tools for seamless alignment. The tool also facilitates the review of revised contract terms. It offers a conversational AI feature that encourages chat experiences to uncover various sales scenarios, forecast revenue, and discover potential cross-sell or upsell opportunities, assisting users in boosting their deal size. Furthermore, it has a precise revenue forecast feature that analyzes key sales metrics and pipeline data to predict accurate revenue forecasts, ensuring alignment and target achievement. Another feature, the AI Sales Playbook, uses sales collateral and past deals to boost the deal size. The platform is designed with a privacy-first approach, and customer data is isolated with SOC2 compliance and GDPR-compliant deletion options.
