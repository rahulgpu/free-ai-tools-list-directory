About Franz AI

Franz AI Text Rewriter is a browser extension designed for content creators and developers who seek to rewrite text on any webpage using advanced AI models like GPT-3.5 Turbo and GPT-4. Users can select the text, right-click, and choose the rewriting option from the context menu. This tool enables content transformation with AI-powered text rewriting, allowing users to preview the results in place. Franz AI Text Rewriter provides a reliable and efficient solution for anyone working with text looking to leverage advanced AI models. The tool's use of OpenAI API makes rewriting text seamless and efficient. The extension is available on Chrome Web Store, and the size of the installation is relatively small (22,41Kilobites). The privacy policy of the developer assures users that their data is not sold to third parties outside the approved use cases, and their information is not being used or transferred for purposes unrelated to the item's core functionality or for lending purposes. Overall, Franz AI Text Rewriter is a helpful tool for anyone looking to streamline their content creation process by leveraging powerful AI models.
