About Read Easy.ai

Read Easy.ai is an AI tool designed to make text more accessible to users with low literacy skills. It simplifies text, making it easier to read and understand, and it supports multiple languages including English, Spanish, German, Dutch, and Portuguese. This tool is beneficial for both developers and editors. For developers, it offers an API and is compatible with a wide range of popular platforms and technologies, including React, React Native, Swift, Vue.js, WordPress, and custom development solutions. For editors, it allows them to check if their text is simple enough for people with a reading disability, and provides feedback on which words or sentences need to be changed and why they are too difficult. Additionally, the tool has a Cookie Policy, Terms of Use, and Privacy Policy. Read Easy.ai is part of Accessibility Desk and Digitaal Toegankelijk.
