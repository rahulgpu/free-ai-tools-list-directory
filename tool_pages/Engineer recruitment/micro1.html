About micro1

micro1 is an Artificial Intelligence recruitment engine that leverages AI to systematically vet engineers. It facilitates a comprehensive process of candidate screening that results in matching clients with the top engineering talent. The tool executes AI-based interviews of a substantial number of candidates every month, a system which certifies only the top 1%. These rigorous processes include AI interviews, soft skill evaluations, one or two human technical interviews, and thorough background checks. The platform also provides its certified engineers with continuing training on AI technologies. In addition to these functionalities, micro1 manages international hiring complexities such as employment laws, global payroll and benefits, making the hiring process seamless for clients. It offers products like Talent, gpt-vetting, and microLab - enabling the hiring of world-class engineers, deploying the AI-based interviewer, and extending your engineering team respectively. Given the capabilities, businesses can potentially hire top-tier engineers at significantly lower costs.
