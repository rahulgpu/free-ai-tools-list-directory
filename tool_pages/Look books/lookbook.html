About LookBook

LookBook AI is an AI tool that allows users to create AI-generated look books to express their creativity. With LookBook AI, users can easily generate beautiful and photorealistic look books with just one click. The tool offers a wide range of options for customization, with over 100 different attributes to choose from, such as mood, model gender, skin color, hair color, model ethnicity, age, body type, and eye color.Users can select their desired attributes to create their perfect style and generate a unique and personalized look book. The generated look books include 30 high-definition (HD) professional photo shots and one complete look book.LookBook AI provides users with inspiration and a convenient way to explore different styles and aesthetics. The tool eliminates the need for manual selection and coordination of various elements involved in creating a look book, saving time and effort.To use LookBook AI, users can make a one-time payment of $5 with no subscription required. The current turnaround time for generating the look books is 12 minutes.Overall, LookBook AI is a handy tool for individuals seeking quick and AI-powered solutions to create visually appealing look books, allowing them to unleash their creativity and find inspiration for fashion, styling, and photography projects.
