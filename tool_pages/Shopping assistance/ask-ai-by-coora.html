About Ask AI by Coora

Ask AI is a software solution that enhances the shopping experience by using artificial intelligence to effortlessly clarify product queries. It seamlessly guides visitors and addresses their doubts about products, ensuring a delightful shopping experience. The software's Question Answering feature connects users with AI-powered assistance, providing guidance and clarifying product-related queries. By instantly providing product information, Ask AI efficiently addresses customer inquiries, ensuring a seamless and delightful shopping experience.The software is available in the Shopify App Store, with more integrations coming soon. It offers customization options to match your website's branding and design, allowing you to create a cohesive and seamless user experience. Ask AI supports multiple languages, making it suitable for businesses with an international customer base.Ask AI improves customer satisfaction by providing quick and accurate answers to product-related questions, helping customers make informed decisions. This reduces confusion and leads to a better overall shopping experience and higher conversion rates. The software prioritizes data security, adhering to strict industry standards. All data collected is securely stored and encrypted to ensure the privacy and protection of customers' information.Ask AI offers two pricing options, including a basic free version with limited features and a usage-based plan. The software is suitable for businesses of all sizes, adapting to your product catalog and scaling with your business growth. A dedicated support team is available to assist with any questions or concerns and comprehensive documentation is provided to help users make the most of the software.
