About Accordian

Accordian, a time-saving Voice AI tool, streamlines your team's workflow by providing instant and credible responses to common queries. Designed for busy sales and support representatives, as well as experts, Accordian ensures efficiency by offering prompt answers to product inquiries, sales collateral requests, solution explanations, and objection responses. Distinctively, Accordian relies on direct quotes sourced from your company's experts and existing content, including case studies, white papers, and your help center. This approach eliminates blind trust in AI, assuring your representatives that the information provided is reliable and traceable to its original source. One standout feature of Accordian is its ability to find collateral swiftly, outpacing the time spent searching or bothering colleagues. The tool not only locates the right piece of collateral but also extracts and quotes the most relevant content while providing a link to the original page.
