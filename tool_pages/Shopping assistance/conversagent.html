About Conversagent

Conversagent is an AI-based shopping assistant specially designed for ecommerce platforms like Shopify. The AI assistant operates 24/7, providing round-the-clock support to help mitigate cart abandonment and increase sales conversions by promptly answering customers' queries. Conversagent uses advanced technology to offer personalized responses to each unique customer query, directly from product pages and engages customers proactively. The communication style is human-like, designed to help customers purchase the items they want. Conversagent aims to take care of repetitive customer enquiries, freeing up time for the store owner to concentrate on more complex tasks. Its implementation requires minimal setup and can integrate seamlessly with a Shopify store. In addition to providing effective customer service support, it gives ecommerce owners control over the assistant's tone of voice and responses to ensure it aligns with their brand. Testimonials suggest that it significantly reduces email volumes containing frequently asked questions, thereby increasing efficiency.
