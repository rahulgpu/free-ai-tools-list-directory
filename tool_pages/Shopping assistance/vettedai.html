About VettedAI

Vetted is an AI-powered shopping assistant that supports users in product discovery and price comparison. It facilitates shoppers in finding the most recommended brands and products based on trusted reviews from popular platforms like Reddit, YouTube and other recognised expert websites. Vetted employs AI to analyze several factors like price history, product reviews, and brand reputation, ensuring users get the best deal possible. Offering more than just a discount finder, it provides concise insights on trustworthy expert and user reviews along with brand reputation analysis, which aids users in deciding whether to continue with a brand or explore other options. Furthermore, it implements price tracking and comparison features, helping users avoid overpaying. Vetted is available for use on various marketplaces including Amazon, Sephora, Google, and Best Buy. The tool is appreciated for its non-intrusive, user-friendly interface and efficiency in helping users make quick purchasing decisions.
