About Cognosys

Cognosys is an artificial intelligence tool designed to simplify workflows and improve productivity. It accomplishes this by assigning tasks to AI Agents, leaving users free to focus on more important matters. The tool has a broad range of functionalities such as compiling comprehensive market reports, sending weekly trend report summaries, summarizing daily emails, and drafting responses to customer support inquiries. It has a unique capability of breaking down complex objectives and creating tasks for itself which it then accomplishes autonomously. Research, automation and email management are main use cases of Cognosys. The tool also offers a function for automating workflows, which can be scheduled or triggered. For example, users can set up a task for Cognosys to send them a daily summary of news every morning at 8AM, or to automatically sort new emails into 'work' or 'personal' categories. Cognosys acts as a central hub for your work, seamlessly integrating with your favorite apps such as Google Calendar, Google Drive, Gmail, Notion, Google Maps, and Outlook to provide actionable insights and streamline communication. Moreover, it allows users to connect their Notion and Gmail accounts for even more functionality, such as drafting emails in Gmail based on information retrieved from Notion.Not just limited to professional tasks, it can be used for curating personalized newsletters as well. It facilitates productivity improvement and enables a shift from production to strategy in operations. Cognosys aims to reimagine productivity by allowing users to automate the hard work so they can make time for what matters.
