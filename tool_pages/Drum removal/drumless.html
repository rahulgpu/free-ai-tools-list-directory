About Drumless

Drumless is an Artificial Intelligence (AI)-powered tool designed to aid drummers by facilitating the removal of drum sounds from songs. This tool provides drummers with the opportunity to practice and improve their skills by playing along with their favorite band's music in a personalized and creative manner. By allowing users to isolate the drum segment within any given song, Drumless presents a new way to engage with music, enabling drummers to produce recreate their favourite tracks in their unique style. The tool supports MP3 and WAV files and offers an AI feature that extracts drum sounds from any uploaded track. Drumless not only offers the capability to remove drums from music but also provides cloud storage for the processed files. Aimed at students, teachers, hobbyists, and streamers, this tool offers a blend of AI technology and music, redefining traditional practice methods for drummers.
