About iMocha

iMocha is an AI-driven platform designed to streamline skill assessments and talent management processes. Its range of solutions is intended to augment workforce productivity, underpinning a data-driven, skills-first approach. The platform provides a wealth of utilities, including Skills Intelligence Cloud, Talent Acquisition, Talent Management, and Campus Hiring tools. With what is claimed to be the world's largest skills assessment library, the platform offers a holistic view of an organization's skills inventory and allows for skill-gap analysis and skills matching in order to anticipate future requirements and address skill gaps. iMocha also provides resources for recruitment and career development, boasting features designed to support remote hiring, diversity hiring, campus hiring, and workforce agility. The software is designed to integrate seamlessly with existing HR systems, offering comprehensive, data-driven talent decisions for a strategic approach to talent acquisition and management. Additionally, iMocha delivers skills benchmarking tools to gain actionable insights into workforce proficiency, enabling recruiters and HR professionals to make informed talent decisions.
