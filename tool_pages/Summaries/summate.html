About Summate

Summate.it is an AI-driven tool designed to quickly summarize web articles. Users can insert the URL of an online article into Summate.it to automatically generate a concise summary. As an on-demand service, it offers immediate access to brief overviews of longer texts, from news articles to blog posts. The tool can be particularly useful for those needing to quickly understand key information from an article without reading the full text. Additionally, Summate.it features an API for developers, allowing them to integrate Summate.it's Web article summarization functionality into other applications or services. Summate.it is made by FiveFilters.org, a company known for creating tools such as Feed Control, Push to Kindle, and Simple Print.
