About Context Clue

Context Clue is an AI-powered tool that aims to accelerate document research by summarizing multi-format content, simplifying complex topics, and extracting desired information. It can be utilized in various scenarios such as research companion, communibot for internal communications, customer aid bot for enhancing customer service, contract navigator for understanding legal agreements, and IT intellect for managing and improving tech infrastructure. The tool allows users to upload and analyze files in formats like PDFs, docs, or xls, ensuring the confidentiality of the data. It is flexible in terms of language models and supports any large language model, not just GPT-based models. Context Clue processes documents internally to eliminate the risk of data leakage. One of the benefits of using Context Clue is time-saving, as it helps users sift through large volumes of text and grasp the main points without reading the entire document. It also enhances comprehension by summarizing and extracting numeric and textual information in an accessible form. The tool prioritizes data privacy as it works directly on the user's device, ensuring confidentiality. Additionally, it provides multilingual support, breaks down language barriers in research processes, and can be customized and modified to suit specific needs. Context Clue was developed by Addepto, a renowned AI and data science company that focuses on building practical AI solutions to address real-life challenges faced by businesses across different industries.
