About Gist AI

Gist AI is a multi-purpose Chrome extension designed to generate summaries from various online content including websites, YouTube videos, and PDFs. Utilizing ChatGPT, Gist AI simplifies a user's content consumption by extracting key points from these sources, offering a comprehensible summary in a single click. A unique feature of the tool is its ability to deep dive into the summary source for greater clarity and directly access specific segments in a YouTube video that are pertinent to the user. The tool is also designed to summarize PDFs found online as well as those saved on a user's device, irrespective of the size or number of pages. Following research-backed techniques, Gist AI enhances comprehension and focus by breaking down long articles, PDFs, or videos into smaller parts. Easily manageable through its integration as a Chrome extension, it assists a wide range of professionals in acquiring fast insights from voluminous documents. Gist AI does not require API keys for operation, thus ensuring a user-friendly and efficient summarization process.
