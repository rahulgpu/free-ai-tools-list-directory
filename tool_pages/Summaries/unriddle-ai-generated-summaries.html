About Unriddle: AI-generated Summaries

Unriddle: AI-generated Summaries is a tool designed to simplify your reading experience by generating compact summaries of articles, blog posts, and other web content. It is particularly valuable when dealing with lengthy texts, allowing users to understand the key points in a fraction of the time. Beyond simply summarizing content, the AI has the potential to offer clarity on certain topics when requested. To use the extension, visit any webpage with a body of text, click on 'summarize', and a short paragraph detailing the main points will be produced. This feature makes reading more engaging and manageable, helping users to focus on the most essential parts of the text. The developers have released updates to ensure faster generation of responses and a new name as Unriddle, formerly known as Squish. This tool makes the reading process more efficient, especially for individuals with extensive reading lists. Note: The privacy policy discloses handling of personal data, with assurances that user data will not be sold, used, or transferred for reasons unrelated to the tool's core functionality.
