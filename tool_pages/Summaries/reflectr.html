About Reflectr

Reflectr is an AI-powered notebook designed for reflection, growth, and productivity. Its main purpose is to help users declutter their minds by allowing them to post thoughts, ideas, observations, and notes without feeling the pressure of social media or sharing. Reflectr provides instant feedback through AI-generated tags, comments, mood indicators, and emojis, eliminating the need for manual effort. Additionally, Reflectr generates a daily recap and writing tips based on the user's posts to help them stay on track. Reflectr's mood calendar allows users to review their daily mood indicators, while recent topics are auto-generated for easy access and review. Reflectr is available for both iOS and Android devices and is a product of Daily Labs, LLC. It provides privacy protection by allowing users to keep their thoughts and notes private. The tool is designed to help users unlock the power of their minds and become more productive by allowing them to organize their thoughts, track their moods, and review their progress effortlessly. Reflectr's simple yet powerful design makes it a valuable tool for anyone looking to improve their productivity, maintain focus, and stay organized.
