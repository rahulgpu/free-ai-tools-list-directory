About GistAI

Gist AI is a free Chrome extension that provides a convenient way to summarize various types of content including websites, YouTube videos, and PDF documents. Powered by ChatGPT, Gist AI extracts key points from long articles, YouTube videos, or PDFs with just one click, allowing users to save time and quickly understand the main ideas. The extension also offers a "Read More" feature, enabling users to deep-dive into the source of the summaries that interest them, without any fluff or distractions. Additionally, Gist AI allows users to jump to specific segments of a YouTube video, making it easy to focus on the relevant parts.One of the notable features of Gist AI is its ability to summarize PDF documents, whether they are online or saved on the user's device. The extension enhances comprehension and focus by breaking down long articles, PDFs, or videos into smaller parts, using a research-backed technique. Gist AI imposes no length restrictions, making it suitable for summarizing longer documents such as books or research articles.The extension is user-friendly, with installation from the Chrome Web Store and a toolbar icon for easy access. Gist AI respects user privacy by not collecting any personal data and only reading content for the summarization process.Customer testimonials highlight the usefulness of Gist AI for students, professionals, and anyone who wants to quickly extract important information from lengthy articles. The extension is praised for its precision, time-saving capabilities, and ability to streamline content consumption.Gist AI is a beneficial tool for individuals who regularly engage with online content and seek efficient summarization methods.
