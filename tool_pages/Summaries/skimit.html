About SkimIt

SkimIt.ai is an AI-powered summarization tool that enables users to quickly get a summary of any article. Simply email the article link to [emailÂ protected] , and within 10 minutes the user will receive an AI-generated summary of the article along with drafts of shareable tweets and a LinkedIn post. The AI-generated summary is powered by OpenAI's GPT technology, and is meant to provide users with key takeaways from the article without requiring them to read the full article. All generated content is for entertainment purposes only, so users should not rely on its accuracy. SkimIt.ai was created by Karthik Sridharan and Alex Furmansky as a fun little hackathon project to practice AI text processing, email handling, and web scraping.
