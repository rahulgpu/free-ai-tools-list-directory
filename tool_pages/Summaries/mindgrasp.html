About Mindgrasp

Mindgrasp is an AI-powered tool for generating notes and answers to questions about any document or video. It uses essential and non-identifiable cookies to protect user data. The tool offers a range of features, including AI Note Generation, Video Summarizer, Student Discounts, Ambassador Program, Help Center, Terms of Service, Policies, and Blog. Mindgrasp is designed to save time and help users learn more quickly. It is capable of analyzing multiple media types, including documents, textbooks, article links, and video/audio lectures. People using Mindgrasp have reported decreased reading time and improved comprehension. It is available on iOS devices and can be used by students, professionals, and avid readers. It is currently being used by over 40 universities and K-12 institutions across the US.
