About Shortwave

Shortwave is an intelligent email client powered by AI, designed to help professionals streamline their workflow and maintain inbox zero. The platform offers a variety of features to help users save time and increase productivity, such as AI-generated summaries, natural language processing, auto-labelling, drag-and-drop task management, and more. Shortwave also provides users with a modern chat experience, with blazing speed and agility, keyboard shortcuts, and Markdown support for easy composition. The platform is trusted by leading professionals, with an intuitive UI and reliable Gmail sync. It is available on desktop, iOS, and Android, and is free to download.
