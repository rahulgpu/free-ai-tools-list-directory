About Endel

Endel is an AI-powered tool that creates personalized, real-time soundscapes to help users focus, relax, and sleep. Its algorithm generates sounds that adapt to varying factors, such as time of day, weather, heart rate, and location, to create a user-centric audio environment. The tool is backed by neuroscience research, suggesting consistent use can improve users' focus, reduce stress, and assist in achieving a restful sleep. The platform's applications support a range of user states including focus, to boost productivity, sleep, to aid in restful slumber, and relaxation, to help calm the mind. Additionally, its technology can create sound accompaniments to power activities, keeping users present and grounded during their physical movements. Endel has formed strategic partnerships with companies like Universal Music Group and Sony, enhancing its offerings and reach.
