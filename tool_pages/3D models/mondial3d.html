About Mondial3D

Mondial 3D is an advanced platform that offers a combination of a marketplace for a diverse range of 3D models, alongside an AI-powered tool for creating 3D models. This platform is versatile and enables users to create, explore, and engage in digital domains without any coding skills. Special emphasis is put on AI-driven interior and exterior design where users can transform and visualize living spaces and outdoor environments with ease and precision. The 'No Code Metaverse Designer' is another key feature that allows crafting of immersive virtual worlds. For future advancements in Extended Reality (XR) design, Mondial 3D seeks to redefine immersive experiences by bringing together the virtual and real world in never seen before ways. This platform also offers architectural solutions, 3D educational content creation and production, medical education modules, and a Metaverse and game design tool that works without the need for any coding. Apart from its AI features, Mondial 3D is also a hub for networking where businesses can connect and collaborate with talent for projects. With a vast library of assets reaching over a million in count, users have the option to explore and select resources that best suit their needs.
