About Muze One

Also known under the former name Muze, OTTO is an advanced web application designed for providing tailored music playlists through AI-driven analysis of conversational prompts provided by the user. OTTO harnesses artificial intelligence to interpret user inputs such as mood, desired activity, concept, or artists and music styles to generate personalised music streaming experiences. This could range from creating a music playlist for a romantic evening to a high-energy mix for a workout session. The quality of the results produced by OTTO directly correlates with the amount of information provided by the end-user. Moreover, OTTO allows users to ready a personal music curator capable of setting a personalized soundtrack for whatever situation a user might find themselves in. OTTO's interface incorporates album art and previews and it also offers an option for users to share their input and thoughts on the application.
