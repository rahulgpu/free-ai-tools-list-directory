About PassportMaker

PassportMaker is an online AI-powered tool designed to automate the creation of passport, visa, and ID photos that adhere to government guidelines. It allows users to choose their country and the type of document they need a photo for, ensuring the image meets the defined requirement such as 51x51mm or 40x60mm. In addition to passport photos, PassportMaker can standardize photos for a range of documents including visas, travel cards, driving licenses, and ID cards. Users can upload their self-portrait, and the AI tool handles tasks such as background removal, image clarification, and size standardization in line with official guidelines. Users can further edit the photo background as required and download the passport photo as a single printable file or sheet of multiple images. It's user-friendly on any device. The tool also ensures data security as it does not require account sign-up and does not save users personal information or photos. PassportMaker also offers a mobile app for iOS and Android for user convenience.
