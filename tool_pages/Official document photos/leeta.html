About Leeta

LEETA ID Photo is an application that generates ID, passport, and other regulatory photos using advanced AI technology. The application automatically resizes photos to meet varying global standards. It allows users to produce passport, visa, identification card, driver's license, qualification exam, document application photos and more. The application uses artificial intelligence for proper positioning and framing of the photo to match specific requirements. Besides, it has a feature that allows users to automatically change and choose the background of the image. It also provides an auto-layout setting to ease the printing process. The app stores the electronic data for 180 days, and users can download it at any time for use. LEETA ID Photo also offers a broad range of regulatory photo sizes from more than 150 countries. The app is designed to produce high-quality results, ensuring a smooth application process for various needs. Furthermore, users can generate these photos from the convenience of their home or office, making it a suitable choice for busy individuals. The application operates on a user-friendly interface and requires no registration or ads. It ensures fast and efficient production of regulatory photos.
