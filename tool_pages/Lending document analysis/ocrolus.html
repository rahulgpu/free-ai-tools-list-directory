About Ocrolus

Ocrolus is an intelligent document automation software specifically designed for the fintech industry. It utilizes AI-driven technology to expedite and enhance lending decision-making processes by transforming physical documents into digital data and performing data analytics. By automating document processing, Ocrolus enables customers to make faster and more accurate lending decisions.The tool offers several capabilities, including document classification, data capture, detection of relevant information, and data analysis. It supports various use cases such as cash flow analysis, income verification, address verification, employment verification, and identity verification.Ocrolus caters to multiple industries, including small business lending, mortgage lending, consumer finance, and property management. Its automation capabilities streamline workflows and improve operational efficiency.For developers, Ocrolus provides a developer program and comprehensive documentation to facilitate integration and customization of the tool.To assist users, the platform offers a resource hub containing informative content like whitepapers, eBooks, guides, and videos related to intelligent document processing, fraud detection, cash flow analysis, and business process automation. Subscribing to their newsletter provides access to additional resources. Overall, Ocrolus is an AI-powered document automation solution that empowers financial institutions to expedite lending processes, improve decision accuracy, and transform physical documents into actionable digital data.
