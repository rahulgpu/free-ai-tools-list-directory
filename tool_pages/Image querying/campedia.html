About Campedia

Campedia is an Artificial Intelligence (AI) powered camera application designed to respond to user inquiries about various objects and scenarios based on images provided. It has capabilities in identifying diverse items such as plants, animals, coins, wines, and landmarks as a part of its offerings. Furthermore, it excels in assisting with more complex tasks, for instance, creating a recipe from ingredients available in a user's refrigerator. Campedia operates utilizing GPT-4 Vision, an advanced technology deemed capable of interpreting images and providing answers pertaining to them. This represents a significant advancement in enabling AI to 'see'. User interaction with the application is enabled through a simplified interface which converts the entire screen into a single button, facilitating ease of use. The process involves tapping and holding to snap a picture, asking a question while holding, and releasing to get an answer. Alongside these, Campedia offers multilingual support, including but not limited to English, German, French, Italian, Spanish, Japanese, Korean, Portuguese, and Chinese languages.
