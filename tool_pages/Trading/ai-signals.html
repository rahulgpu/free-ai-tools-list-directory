About AI-signals

AI-Signals is an AI-powered trading indicator designed for traders on TradingView. It provides real-time buy and sell signals for stocks, forex, and crypto. The indicator uses machine learning algorithms to analyze various parameters and generate accurate trading signals on the user's chart. With this tool, traders can identify optimal entry and exit points for their trades.AI-Signals aims to simplify trading by eliminating complexity and overwhelm, especially for beginners. By signing up, users can access the AI-powered indicator directly in their TradingView account. The tool offers features such as auto risk management, auto order blocks, and real-time alerts.In addition to the indicator, AI-Signals provides a supportive Discord community of traders, insights, educational content, and more. Traders can also engage with an AI trading chatbot trained on the platform's data to get answers to their trading-related queries.AI-Signals prides itself as the world's first AI-powered trading community and is committed to helping traders enhance their knowledge and skills using AI technology. The tool offers different pricing plans, including a free beta version with simple buy/sell signals and a full version with advanced features such as AI-powered buy and sell signals, risk management, order blocks, VIP Discord access, and invitations to exclusive events.Overall, AI-Signals is a comprehensive AI-powered trading indicator aimed at simplifying and improving trading strategies for stocks, forex, and crypto traders on TradingView.
