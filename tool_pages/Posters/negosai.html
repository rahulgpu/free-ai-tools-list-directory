About NegosAI

NegosAI is an AI-powered tool designed to efficiently generate high-quality poster designs. It harnesses AI to translate user input on audience, purpose, visual style, and color scheme into personalized posters. The created designs are of high-definition quality, making them look crisp, vibrant, and professional regardless of where they are displayed. Moreover, the tool enables easy conversion of generated posters to SVG format for further editing. Users can download the design for use in an alternate editor or utilize NegosAI's integrated editor for making instant adjustments. NegosAI is designed to be user-friendly and does not require prior design experience, making it an accessible tool for both beginners and professional designers. Users have the ability to create a variety of posters ranging from promotional and advertising posters to inspirational or educational ones. NegosAI ensures data security with advanced encryption and security practices. It offers a simple, affordable subscription plan that includes unlimited poster generations, HD quality images, and unlimited uploads.
