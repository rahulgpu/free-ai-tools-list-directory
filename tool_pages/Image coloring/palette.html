About Palette

Palette is an AI colorizer that enables users to colorize anything from old black and white photos to modern images. It is as simple to use as Instagram, and no sign-up is required. The process is automatic and no personal data is stored. It is free to use, and comes with a 3-minute tutorial. The Palette community is composed of original creators and more than one million people around the world. The platform also includes an affiliate program, job opportunities, and testimonials from users. It is connected to Twitter, GitHub, and YouTube to provide users with the most up-to-date information. The platform is also compliant with all privacy and terms of service regulations. Overall, Palette is an AI colorizer that enables users to quickly and easily colorize photos with the added benefits of a supportive community and up-to-date information.
