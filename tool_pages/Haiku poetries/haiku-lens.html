About Haiku Lens

Haiku Lens is an innovative AI-based tool that crafts exquisite haiku poetry from your photos. The app uses advanced AI to identify the critical elements in your photos such as colours, textures, shapes, and uses this information to create a haiku, reflecting the mood and ambiance encapsulated in the image. This tool is uniquely designed to nurture appreciation and mindfulness towards the present moment; it could be used while exploring nature, spending time with loved ones, or even during mundane day-to-day tasks. In addition, the Haiku Lens app provides a voice feature that recites the developed haiku for an auditory experience. It also enables users to share their personalized haiku videos on social media platforms. To analyze a new photo and create a haiku, an internet connection is required as the chosen photo is sent to a server. However, previously created haikus can be viewed offline. Your photos and generated videos are stored locally on your device, while the photo is transmitted for content analysis and haiku creation, used solely for these purposes. Haiku Lens is available for free download on the App Store and Google Play Store. After an initial free period, users may opt for in-app purchases to continue creating haikus.
