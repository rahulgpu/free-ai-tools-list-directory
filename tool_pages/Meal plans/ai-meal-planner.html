About AI Meal Planner

Utekal Media Ltd.'s AI powered meal plan generator is a great tool for those looking to achieve their fitness goals. It provides users with healthy, delicious meal options and a free 1-week training plan as a bonus. The AI model is trained on data from the best fitness coaches in the country, making it a trusted tool for many fitness enthusiasts. The meal plan generator is easy to use and requires users to provide some basic information such as age, gender, exercise frequency, and fitness goals. Additionally, users can input any foods they do not like eating, such as gluten-free or sugar-free items. Once the information is provided, the generator will generate a personalized 1-week meal plan within minutes. After payment, users should leave the page open so the generator can finish generating their plan.
