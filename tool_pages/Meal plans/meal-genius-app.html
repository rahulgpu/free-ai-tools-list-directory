About Meal Genius App

The Meal Genius App is designed to enhance the user's culinary experience. This AI-powered tool aids in the discovery of new meals, helps to preserve and organize favourite recipes, and offers handy access to ingredients and instructions anytime, anywhere. One of the app's standout features is its ability to accept custom requests, thereby enabling users to tailor generated meals to their personal preferences. This customizability provides a unique, user-centric approach to meal planning and preparation. Central to its design is an emphasis on usability and adaptability, with the tool developed to be compatible with a range of Apple devices, from iPhones, iPads, and iPod touch to Macs with Apple M1 chips. The app is also designed to require minimal data access, according to the developer's privacy practices. Offered in English, the app caters to users who are 4 years and above, and falls under the 'Food & Drink' category. While free to download, it may offer in-app purchases.
