About OH, a potato!

OH, a potato! is an AI-powered iOS meal planner tool, leveraging the capabilities of GPT to support users in curating their weekly meals. Its main functionality revolves around discovering, saving, and planning recipes based on the ingredients users currently have on hand. The tool offers a way to create a weekly plan and grocery list, enabling users to optimize their shopping trips by focusing only on the essentials. Also, it provides a feature where users can save their favourite recipes from various sources such as websites, social media platforms, or even by scanning recipes from cookbooks. The tool's engine allows for efficient searches based on ingredients, making it easier for users to find suitable recipes. Additional functionalities include a feature to track the grocery list and the food currently in the fridge, enhancing organisation and reducing food waste, aligning with the overall zero-waste philosophy of the tool. The intuitive design of OH, a potato! aims to ease the mental strain of planning meals while promoting a sustainable cooking habit.
