About Wholesum

WHOLESUM is an AI-powered tool designed to streamline meal planning, ingredient substitution, and shopping lists, catering to various group sizes and dietary restrictions. It is an ideal solution for several applications, including planning meals for camping trips, organizing a catering event, or managing food planning for bed and breakfast establishments. With an emphasis on reducing food waste and saving time and money, WHOLESUM allows for easy adjustments based on the size of the group and the duration of the event. This tool also addresses dietary restrictions by offering the option to add or exclude meals accordingly, enabling automated recipe adjustment to suit specific dietary needs. Additionally, users can create and share meal plans equipped with respective recipes. WHOLESUM also plays a significant role in cost estimation, allowing users to monitor expenses per recipe, per group, or per serving. This tool uses artificial intelligence to generate recipes and instructions. Furthermore, it offers features like calendar planning that organizes meals by date and category. To enhance its usability, WHOLESUM provides mobile shopping lists for both iOS and Android devices, enabling users to take their lists along with them. The tool offers thorough support including user stories, help advice and contact information.
