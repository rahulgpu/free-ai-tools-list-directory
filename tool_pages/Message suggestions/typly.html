About Typly

Typly is an AI-powered writing assistant designed to enhance conversation and correspondence. It utilizes Large Language Models such as GPT, ChatGPT, LLaMA, among others, to auto-generate contextual suggestions suitable for your conversation. Typly also works as an intelligent correspondence analyzer and distribution system tailored for public and business sectors, with software based on advanced AI solutions including neural networks. It features integration with email platforms, the EPUAP system, and the EDZ RP system, thus making the entire correspondence more efficient and manageable. Further, it boasts features such as AI response suggestions, decoding of complex text, summarizing articles, and perfecting emails making communication easy and effective. Added functionalities in the platform include a dating function for compatible match suggestions, Typly Translate for language flexibility in your suggestions, and a user profile creation for personalized sentence recommendations. To address the issue of overwhelming notifications and time-consuming response drafting, Typly generates contextually relevant sentences for seamless conversation flow. Other notable features include a state-of-the-art software keyboard and Typly's Reply function, an AI-powered chat assistant that offers a plethora of response suggestions.
