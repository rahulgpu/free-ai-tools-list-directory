About Stormz

Stormz AI Brainstorming tool is designed to help facilitators run brainstorming sessions enhanced with AI features. The tool includes a variety of capabilities to support ideation sessions. Within the 'Workshop V2' feature, users can brainstorm ideas, cultivate them into robust concepts and make informed decisions. The 'Quiz & Survey' capability enables facilitators to conduct live surveys or arrange engaging quizzes for interactive sessions. The main highlight of this tool is the 'AI Boosters' feature which harnesses the power of artificial intelligence to augment brainstorming activities. This includes AI-formulated questions and idea suggestions, one-click clustering of ideas, refining ideas with listing of pros and cons, and generating variations of an idea. The AI tool also assists in formulating 'How Might We' questions to broaden perspective, and creates creative illustrations of intangible ideas. It also provides help in clustering and labelling similar ideas for easier understanding and recognition. The intended use of this AI tool is to assist facilitators by simplifying and enhancing the brainstorming process, promote better participation and formulate more rigorous ideas.
