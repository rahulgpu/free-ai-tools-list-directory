About Mynd

Mynd is a novel journaling app designed for self-care that utilizes Artificial Intelligence (AI) to identify previously inaccessible patterns and insights. Developed by B612, Inc., Mynd aims to make it easier to know oneself. With the app, users can effortlessly identify key words, emotions, and themes in their journal entries in real-time, giving them a better understanding of their reactions or experiences.
