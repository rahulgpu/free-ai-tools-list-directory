About Pagefelt

Pagefelt is an AI-powered journal that provides users with custom feedback based on daily entries. The tool uses chat-generated GPT (Generative Pre-trained Transformer) to analyze entries and generate personalized feedback. This feedback takes the form of bookmarks, which are non-intrusive and only display after daily sessions are complete. Bookmarks are where Pagefelt displays all AI-generated content and provide users with tips and content recommendations to help them succeed. The tool is designed to be a judgment-free space for users to write about their thoughts and feelings. The tool's website includes a web app and a privacy page that outlines how user data is collected and used. Users can get started today by visiting the website and creating an account. Pagefelt appears to be a useful tool for individuals who are looking for a way to track their thoughts and receive helpful feedback without judgment. However, it is important to note that the tool's efficacy depends on the accuracy of the AI-generated feedback, which is subject to change.
