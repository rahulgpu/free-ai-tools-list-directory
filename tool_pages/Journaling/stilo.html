About Stilo

Stilo is an AI-powered journaling tool designed to facilitate personal growth through wisdom and recommendations. Unlike traditional journals, Stilo goes beyond repetitive prompts and clich√©s by asking probing questions that delve into the philosophy of happiness and literature. As users continue to journal, Stilo learns about their preferences, generating increasingly insightful questions and recommendations. Additionally, Stilo connects the dots in users' entries to provide actionable insights on a daily basis, transforming it from a basic repository of thoughts into a valuable source of guidance. The tool goes even further by offering personalized recommendations for high performers, suggesting relevant books, podcast episodes, and insightful quotes to encourage further exploration and self-discovery. Stilo believes in creating moments of magic, allowing users to analyze dreams and pull tarot cards to gain a deeper understanding of their subconscious. Privacy is a top priority, as all journal entries are encrypted both in transit and at rest, ensuring that only the user has access to their data.Stilo has garnered positive feedback from users who appreciate its ability to help organize thoughts and provide an interactive journaling experience. As the tool is currently in beta, it is offered for free, and users can join a waitlist to participate in shaping its future direction.
