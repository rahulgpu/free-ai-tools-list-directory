About Rosebud Journal

Rosebud is an AI-enhanced journaling tool that utilizes OpenAI's GPT-3 model to provide personalized prompts to users based on their entries. This product aims to help individuals speed up their journey of self-discovery and manifest the life they want through the popular Rose Bud Thorn framework. Every evening, users are asked three simple questions about their day, and the AI-powered system suggests follow-up questions to help them dig deeper with minimal effort. Rosebud AI also suggests goals based on users' answers to help them live their best life and automatically builds a vision board. Weekly summaries are provided to help users gain insights into their thoughts, emotions, and behaviors by reflecting back patterns in their journal entries.The tool offers a safe and secure feature where each user's journal is privately stored in a browser database, which only they can access. Additionally, Rosebud promotes a quick and easy journaling habit with just 5 minutes of reflection time per day. It aims to provide an easy and rewarding way for users to reap all the benefits of self-reflection without dedicating too much time or energy to the practice.
