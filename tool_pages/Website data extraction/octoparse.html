About Octoparse

Octoparse is a no-code web scraping tool designed specifically for web scraping and data extraction. This scraper enables users to collect structured data from any website, providing a platform to drive business development with accurate and relevant data. The tool is relatively easy to use and does not require any coding ability. With Octoparse, you can create your custom scrapers in a distinct workflow designer that effectively visualizes everything in a browser. Octoparse includes an AI-powered assistant, designed to help users get started faster with features like Auto-detect. The AI assistant also provides useful, timely tips at every step of the data scraping process. To enhance the efficiency of data scraping, Octoparse includes features for automation, scheduled scraping, automatic data export, and OpenAPI support. Octoparse also offers features to overcome typical data scraping challenges, such as IP rotation, CAPTCHA solving, and proxy support. Users have access to an extensive template gallery, which offers pre-set templates for popular websites for instant data extraction with no setup. Popular templates include Google maps scraper, Twitter search and email extractor. The platform offers use cases in various industries, such as Lead Generation, E-commerce, Price Monitoring, Higher Education, News & Content Curation, and more. Furthermore, Octoparse offers resources like blog articles, case tutorials, and a beginner's guide to assist users in navigating the platform and to enhance their website scraping skills.
