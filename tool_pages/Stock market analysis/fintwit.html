About Fintwit

Fintwit is an artificial intelligence-driven platform designed to deliver insights and recommendations for stock market trading. It encompasses several functionalities allowing users to access real-time stock market quotes and perform sophisticated stock analysis driven by AI. The platform also enables users to screen stocks according to their preferences using the Free Screener feature. Arguably one of its key features is the AI Top Picks, where the platform's proprietary AI technology scrutinises vast amounts of stock market data to provide suggestions for trading. Fintwit provides trading signals which are automated prompts, generated by the platform, indicating the opportune moments to execute trades. It is also a source for market news, consolidating the latest happenings from the financial world to keep users informed. Note, users require JavaScript enabled on their device to properly utilise this application.
