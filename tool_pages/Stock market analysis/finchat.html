About FinChat

FinChat.io is a comprehensive AI-powered platform designed for investment research. The core functionality of the platform integrates high-quality financial data alongside advanced analytics, supplemented by a conversational AI feature. The intent behind FinChat.io is to streamline detailed investigation of companies, offering data that's been verified for its accuracy. This solution provides relevant information about a multitude of global public companies, including revenue and profit segments, KPIs, analyst estimates, and price targets. The platforms AI capability enables users to generate summaries, models, and visualizations simplifying complex data. FinChat.io also includes features for reading transcripts and filings, tracking dividend histories, seeing insider and institutional ownership, screening stocks and more. The platform is designed with customization in mind, allowing users to track information based on personal preferences and auto-save their research. The platform is designed to not just present stable, institutional-grade financial data, but also to ensure the data gets translated into meaningful and actionable insights.
