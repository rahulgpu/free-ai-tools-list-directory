About Stonks GPT

StonksGPT is an AI-powered tool by Stonks News that allows users to search for financial information and insights. It features a search bar that suggests popular queries when users start typing, such as the last 50 insider transactions for Apple or the top 10 technology stocks by marketcap. Users can also input their own search terms and receive relevant results.StonksGPT provides users with a range of financial data, including trading volumes for Apple, Google, and Amazon over the last 30 days, and a chart for Uber, Lyft, and DoorDash for the past three months. The tool also has a random search option that presents users with a random search result.The tool is part of the Stonks News platform that includes other features, such as calendars, news, insider trading information, and a heatmap of stock performances. Stonks News is powered by an AI system, and the StonksGPT tool appears to utilize natural language processing technology to understand user queries and provide relevant results.Overall, StonksGPT is a useful tool for investors and traders who require quick access to financial information and insights. Its AI-powered search engine provides a convenient and efficient way for users to find the financial data they need, and its integration with the other features in the Stonks News platform makes it a comprehensive resource for financial data.
