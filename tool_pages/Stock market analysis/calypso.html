About Calypso

Calypso is an AI-First public equities tool designed to function as a co-pilot for investors. It utilizes Artificial Intelligence to assist in tracking all of your coverage in one place, combining public data with professional buy-side training. Calypso offers intelligent assistance with interpreting transcripts, financials, headlines, and case studies. The tool's primary functionalities enable users to enhance their analysis as it can extract management quotes, debates, news and financials to maintain comprehensive coverages. AI-powered insights offered by Calypso guide investors in their testing of investment theses with AI feedback on the narrative driving their names. Calypso can help to keep track of personal investment theses and earnings events. Calypso's framework is trained on case studies by professionals and can respond to questions through an AI chat feature powered by a GPT-4 class model. The platform integrates various data sources to provide a holistic view to make the best investment decisions. It allows tracking of alternative data through custom notes and searching across all public transcripts for specific terms. Calypso aims to provide a jargon-free and straightforward interface while maintaining high-grade analytical capabilities.
