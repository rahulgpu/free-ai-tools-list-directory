About Alfie Invest

Alfie is an AI investment analyst and assistant app that provides instant information and analysis on stocks, companies, ETFs, and the market. It offers real-time data, insights, and recommendations by querying financial, news, and research data sources. The app generates investment research reports on the fly for specific companies and provides analysis based on over 42 technical indicators.Alfie combines the power of GPT (Generative Pre-trained Transformer), real-time data, and customized financial models. It allows users to receive concise answers without the need to search through multiple charts, figures, articles, or reports. The app covers a wide range of investment topics, from general stock market information to detailed investment analysis.Some example questions that Alfie can address include understanding current stock prices, market trends, dividend payments, and company-specific news and sentiments. Users can also explore the differences between ETFs, receive recommendations for portfolio optimization, and access personalized savings and investment planning.Additional features and functionalities are expected to be added in the future, such as stock and sector screening, personalized recommendations, and support for portfolio management. The app is available for free download on both the App Store and Google Play.Alfie Invest is developed by a Singapore-based AI-driven financial research team that specializes in applying advanced AI/ML technologies to investment, trading, and portfolio management.
