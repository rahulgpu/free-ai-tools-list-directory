About IP Author

Dolcera IP Author is a patent drafting tool that implements artificial intelligence to streamline and automate the patent drafting process. It primarily serves IP law firms and corporate patent teams. As a key player in AI-powered patent drafting tools, IP Author provides facilitated patent drafting services by generating patent claims, comprehensive reviews of factual patent prior art references, and concise backgrounds and descriptions of the invention. It's designed to expedite the patent filing process, reducing the duration from weeks to a few hours of work. In addition, the software includes a feature that enables users to integrate prior art references from Dolcera PCS, a respected patent search engine, into their workflow. This feature allows users to swiftly analyze prior art references and modify their patent draft applications within a short timeframe. The IP Author software aids in producing a well-articulated and effectively drafted patent application by providing unlimited inventions and including a prior art module in its tools. The Dolcera IP Author tool helps in empowering IP law firms and corporate patent groups by providing a streamlined and efficient process for drafting patents.
