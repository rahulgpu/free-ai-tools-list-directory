About Frame.so

Frame is an all-in-one collaborative platform designed to improve the focus and productivity in a working environment. It acts as a central hub where all tasks, wikis, whiteboards, goals, notes, and focused activities are connected. Frame aims to address the issues resulting from constant task switching and multitasking, it facilitates deeper focus and cross referencing of every aspect of your work. With Frame, notes are used not just for documenting thoughts but also for initiating collaboration with the team. Tasks and deadlines are tracked efficiently ensuring no detail is missed, allowing project management to be simpler. Frame also functions as a unified knowledge center where a team can share, learn, and find relevant information quickly. Additionally, the platform assists in setting, tracking, and achieving goals as a team. The digital whiteboard is an effective tool for brainstorming and sharing solutions, plus it can be cross-referenced anywhere. Frame also features AI intelligence to generate suggestions that yield potent results, aiding fast navigation between applications. Furthermore, it provides instant templates for straightforward project initiation, and it enables the categorization and sorting of contacts based on project requirements and interactions. Frame is also designed to offer memory support, making ideas quickly accessible and easy to reference at any time.
