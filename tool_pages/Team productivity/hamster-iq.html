About Hamster IQ

Hamster IQ is an AI-powered tool aimed at enhancing productivity for small businesses and teams. It provides 24/7 online assistance and comes with over 30 pre-built applications and tools. The service essentially serves as an AI assistant, learning user specifics over time such as industry details, team size, nature of profession, helping improve the accuracy and relevance of its responses. This makes it a tool that adapts to the user and their business over time. Users can ask the AI questions about various aspects of running a business, including sales, marketing, copywriting, IT, HR, legislation, and regulation. It also allows the quick creation of comprehensive corporate documents customized to the user's business, including strategies and policies. Another key feature of Hamster IQ is its ability to share its memories or data across a team account, ensuring the AI tools relevance and functionality remain consistent across team interactions. A unique characteristic is its avoidance of predefined responses. Instead, it adapts to who the user is and what they do, providing tailored solutions. Scalable plans are also available to suit varying business needs.
