About Typeright

Typeright is an AI-powered writing assistant designed to enhance writing skills by checking for grammar, spelling, and punctuation mistakes. It also provides style and synonym suggestions to help refine and polish your text. Previously known as Grammatica, Typeright not only helps with correcting errors but also aids in remembering them to avoid repetitions in the future. It handles multiple languages including English, German, and Spanish, making it useful for a diverse group of users. Typeright includes a suite of features such as simple spelling or punctuation corrections, finding missing words, providing style suggestions, and predictive typing. It is a versatile tool, available for use on iOS, Android, and desktop via browser extensions, allowing users to improve their writing on a range of devices. There is also an additional Typeright Plus feature which, when unlocked, grants access to all features across all supported platforms. This platform-independent approach makes Typeright a comprehensive AI-powered writing assistant, accessible wherever one needs it.
