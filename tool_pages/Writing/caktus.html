About Caktus

Caktus is an educational artificial intelligence (AI) tool that automates school work, allowing students to save time and do the things they love. It offers a range of AI-driven services such as an Essay Writer, Paragraph Generator, Discussion Questions, Personal Statement Writer, YouTube to Essay, Text Summarizer, and a range of coding writers for Python, Java, C#, Javascript, and Go. It also features a Career section with Cover Letter and Resume Bullet Points writers. Caktus also has a Scholarship program to help students enrich their education. All of these features are designed to make school work easier and help students make the most of their time.
