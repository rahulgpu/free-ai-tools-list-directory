About Auri.AI

Auri AI is a comprehensive productivity tool designed to enhance writing and transcription tasks using artificial intelligence. Available across several Apple devices, this tool comprises features including a multilingual AI keyboard, an enhanced AI chat, smart notes, and advanced transcription systems. The AI keyboard serves multiple functions including writing, translating, paraphrasing, checking grammar, and replying to emails, thus enhancing multilingual communication across various platforms. The AI chat enables brainstorming of ideas, setting reminders, as well as facilitating learning experiences. It boasts a robust voice-recording feature that simplifies transcription, transforming thoughts into coherent textual content. The smart notes feature doesnt just facilitate notetaking, but also aids in summarizing, paraphrasing and correcting grammar and spelling errors. The tool also provides features such as text-to-speech capabilities and privacy-centered design. Auri's assistance is available across various devices including iPhone, iPad, Mac and Apple Watch, helping users streamline tasks and enhance productivity.
