About Bogar

Bogar.AI is an AI-powered tool that helps users create content quickly and easily. Users can choose from more than 40 templates and use the AI chat assistant, called Bogar Chat, to enter instructions and create engaging content. Bogar.ai can generate blog titles, blog outlines, email subjects, Google Ads copy, social media posts, YouTube titles, blog intros, and more. The chat assistant uses GPT4, a powerful and creative natural language processing technology, to create high-quality content that is engaging and optimized for SEO. Bogar.ai offers different pricing plans, including a free plan that allows users to create up to 2,000 words per month. The Starter plan, which is designed for solopreneurs, social media agencies, and influencers, offers 50,000 words per month, access to priority email support, multi-language support (coming soon), and access to the newest features. Bogar.ai generates original and plagiarism-free content based on user input and the selected tool. The tool currently supports only English, although the company plans to include more languages in the future. Bogar.ai offers discounts for large volumes and non-profits and has an API for integration with other tools. Users can also try Bogar.ai's free tools, including the blog title generator, evergreen content generator, and switch case tool.
