About AutoTextGenie AI

AutoTextGenie AI is an innovative app enhanced with AI technology to streamline the writing process in multiple languages. It is a browser extension that optimizes online text creation with simplicity and speed. You can integrate it smoothly into any text input on any website and it collaborates well with almost all popular browsers and writing platforms. A unique feature is the ability to generate full names with connotations in different languages. Additionally, you can create custom commands and shortcuts to match your unique writing style, utilizing advanced GPT-3 and GPT-4 technology. The tool is able to instantly translate any text in any language with high accuracy, offering an added command shortcut for all languages. Customizable commands facilitate the swift and efficient production of high-quality content including blog posts, emails, and professional responses. The user has the option to buy a lifetime license or purchase the source code. AutoTextGenie AI can be used on multiple devices that support the browser extension. The tool is designed to improve productivity by saving time on text generation, improving the quality of writing and offering lightning-fast translation services.
