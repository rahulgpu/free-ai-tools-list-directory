About AI Author

AI Author: Write like Writers is a sophisticated app that utilizes artificial intelligence to assist users in book writing, article writing, content writing, and story writing. The application provides a unique way to improve your writing style by modeling over 1000 renowned writers. Users can choose their preferred writer and the AI-powered tool will analyze their selected writer's style, grammar, and tone, applying them seamlessly to their text. The application offers an innovative way to create texts that read as if they were drafted by the chosen writers themselves. An added benefit is the app's ability to convert the text into a professional-looking PDF document that can be easily shared. By offering wide-ranging functionality, this tool ensures adaptability and flexibility for its users. Moreover, it enables easy text copying to GoodNotes and seamlessly integrates with this widely used note-taking app. The tool is designed to be user-friendly and easy to navigate, reducing the learning curve for new users.
