About INK

INK is an AI-powered content marketing and SEO tool designed to help users create optimized text and visual content. It offers a suite of features including an AI Writer for crafting original articles, an SEO Optimizer that scores content for SEO, an AI Content Shield to protect your site from AI penalties, and an AI Keyword Research tool for automated keyword exploration. INK also provides an AI Keyword Clustering feature to create topic clusters with one click, an AI Assistant for optimized copy creation, and an AI Images tool that generates visual content from text prompts. Aimed at improving content performance, INK delivers real-time audience insights for more effective copywriting. The tool is built with patented SEO and user engagement optimization technologies intended to significantly increase reach and improve conversions. To support users, INK offers free learning resources, certification through a bootcamp, and a community for bouncing ideas off seasoned marketing experts. Aimed at many users including marketers, writers, and entrepreneurs, INK focuses on providing safe and effective AI for marketing.
