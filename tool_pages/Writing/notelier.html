About Notelier

Notelier is an AI-powered platform designed to help students write and study smarter. It offers a range of tools built-in that helps to read, study, and write better notes and essays. With Notelier’s smart AI, students can summarize, paraphrase, and generate paragraphs in the matter of seconds, saving more time for editing and perfecting the work. The platform allows users to catch and rectify all kinds of grammatical errors while keeping the writing concise.Notelier offers an automatic flashcards generation tool to help memorize notes efficiently. The app claims to revolutionize note-taking by using AI to help users understand their notes and write more effectively. Notelier’s tools are built to make studying and writing more efficient by saving time and improving the quality of work. Notelier offers a monthly subscription priced at $5.99/month. It is loved for its innovative tools by the community and beta testers who claim the paragraph generator saved them hours of writing. Notelier's AI is designed to help write notes better, faster and smarter, offering a new way of studying and writing notes.
