About Machined

Machined.ai is an AI-powered tool that automates the process of creating content clusters. Users can define the topic and target audience, and the tool takes care of everything else, including keyword research, content writing, and interlinking the articles.The tool offers features such as SEO strategy on auto-pilot, automated keyword research, automated keyword clustering, and automated internal linking. It uses advanced AI models to produce human-like and high-quality articles. Users have the ability to control the perspective and tone of the content to align with their intended audience.Machined.ai supports over 100 languages, making it suitable for global audiences. It is built for scalability and speed, utilizing parallel processing and background processing for efficient content generation. The tool is cost-effective and offers unlimited usage at a competitive price point. Users only pay for access to the software and there are no markups on tokens.Machined.ai has a roadmap for future developments, including features like Wordpress integration for easy publishing, search integration for real-time search results, and SERP analysis for enhanced keyword research.The pricing plans are simple and transparent. The Unlimited Plan offers unlimited clusters and articles, automated keyword research and clustering, automated internal linking, and supports multiple connected Wordpress sites. Users can choose between monthly or annual billing options, with a 7-day free trial available.Overall, Machined.ai is an efficient and comprehensive AI tool for generating content clusters, helping users save time and effort in content creation while maintaining quality and SEO relevance.
