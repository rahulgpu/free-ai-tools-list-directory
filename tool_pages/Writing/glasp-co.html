About Glasp.co

Glasp is an AI-powered tool that allows people to highlight and organize quotes, thoughts, and ideas from the web. It provides an easy way to capture and store these snippets, as well as connect with other users to access their learning. Users can curate their highlights to create their own AI models, which can be used to write with the AI models or export to note-taking apps. Glasp also provides a “Wall of Love” feature, where users can share their highlights, notes, and resources with their friends and the community. With Glasp, users can quickly refer to important parts of articles and discover interesting content from other users’ highlights. This helps users become more organized, productive, and creative.
