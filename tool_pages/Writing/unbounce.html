About Unbounce

Smart Copy is an AI powered copywriting and content generation tool from Unbounce. It is designed to help businesses create engaging and effective content quickly and easily. It uses natural language processing and machine learning to generate copy that is tailored to the user's preferences and needs. It also includes features such as automated content rewriting, summarizing and expanding existing copy, a Chrome extension, a desktop app, and an AI optimization feature. Smart Copy is accessible to users of all levels, with a free plan and affordable pricing options. It is a great way to save time and money while producing creative and high-quality content.
