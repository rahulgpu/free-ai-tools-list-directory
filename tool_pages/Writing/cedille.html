About Cedille

Cedille.ai is a natural language processing (NLP) platform that uses artificial intelligence (AI) to assist users in generating, summarizing, and rewriting text. It provides a toolbox for text processing that includes text generation, rewriting, summarizing, and extraction. Cedille.ai offers a unique platform that enables users to seamlessly integrate the AI tool into their projects. The platform is easy to use and requires no installation, as it operates online. Cedille.ai uses the best market-leading language models available and even created its language models, which are also open-source.Cedille.ai has been rated as a top-performing model for text generation, and its Cedille technology can rival the multilingual GPT-3 model developed by OpenAI. Additionally, Cedille.ai allows users to rework high-quality content within seconds, and it has already generated over one million texts. Cedille.ai has been featured in various news outlets, with users praising the tool for its exceptional performance.Cedille.ai provides API access and a blog with useful information. The tool's website also includes a contact page, privacy policy, and terms of service. Cedille.ai was created by Coteries, a digital agency based in Switzerland, at the EPFL innovation park. Finally, Cedille.ai was made possible through the generous support of the Google TRC program. Those interested in the tool can test its full potential by trying Cedille for themselves through the website.
