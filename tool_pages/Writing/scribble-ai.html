About Scribble AI

Scribble AI is an automatic text generator that leverages the power of OpenAI's GPT-3 Language Model to create diverse content swiftly. Users can use Scribble AI to produce various types of content, including LinkedIn posts, poems, and more by simply specifying the type of content, the topic, desired word count, and style options such as professional, flirty, funny, etc. After inputting their preferences, the user can proceed to generate their text and if unsatisfied, they can regerate a new version. It enables users to craft content in different styles and tones, offering flexibility and adaptability in content creation. Users have commended this tool for crafting personalized messages with purpose and tone, perfect for diverse needs including social media posts, work emails, and various communication needs. This text generating app provides an efficient way of ensuring that all messages are well-written and on-brand. Despite its current mobile-only interface, users have expressed interest in a desktop version suggestion for improved workflow.
