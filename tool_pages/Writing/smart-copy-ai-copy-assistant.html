About Smart Copy – AI Copy Assistant

Smart Copy, offered by Unbounce, is an AI-powered copywriting tool designed to generate on-brand, creative content effectively. This tool is designed to eliminate writers block and facilitate smooth content creation for various kinds of materials such as documents, emails, messages, posts and others. Smart Copy allows users to generate sentences, paragraphs, and even entire stories with minimal input, making it a practical solution for diverse writing projects. Users can also construct blog posts, emails, poems and more within a matter of minutes. Smart Copy is designed to ensure high-quality writing while saving time, enhancing productivity, and fostering understanding of different topics at an expert level. The tool also supports translation into over 30 languages, allowing users to cater to audiences worldwide. The AI writing assistant is equipped with a library of over 45 templates to suit different content needs. Whether brainstorming ideas for viral content or writing for various platforms, Smart Copy is designed to generate original ideas and content. The tool is available as a Web App and Chrome Extension for streamlined copywriting, and its desktop app offers a distraction-free workspace. Smart Copy can be incorporated into favorite applications for a seamless writing experience.
