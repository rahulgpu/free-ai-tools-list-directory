About Contents

AI Writer by Contents.com is an AI-powered tool designed to automate content creation for individuals struggling with writer's block. Users can provide simple and clear instructions to the AI, which generates up to 600 words of content effortlessly. The tool relies on cutting-edge software in the field of Natural Language Generation to ensure high-quality and credible output. Users can edit, add or delete subheadings generated by the AI before obtaining their completed piece of content. AI Writer offers three pricing options for its users: Starter, Best-seller, and Premium, with the latter catering to businesses with complex content needs. AI Writer provides access to thousands of copywriters, translators, and proofreaders on the Contents.com platform, as well as access to AI copywriting, SEO copywriting, product descriptions, translations, and audio text conversions. Users can also utilize the tool's plagiarism checker to avoid duplicate content. Additionally, AI Writer offers a 1-click WordPress export feature for easy integration with websites. Overall, AI Writer is a useful tool for individuals or businesses looking to enhance their content creation process and save time.
