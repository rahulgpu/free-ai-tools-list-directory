About ES.AI

ES.AI is a suite of AI-powered tools designed to assist college students in writing essays, cover letters, and email copy. The tools can be accessed on-demand and in real-time by subscribing to the complete ES.AI collection for $39.99 per month. Alternatively, users can purchase access to individual tools for a one-month use period at less than a quarter of the price of a single session with a human essay tutor. At present, the ES.AI suite includes tools for college application essays, college class essays, cover letters, and email copy. The ES.AI mission is to make higher education and professional opportunities accessible to all, regardless of background or resources, by leveraging the power of generative AI to help students communicate their unique stories more effectively. Payment methods accepted include American Express, Diners Club, Discover, Mastercard, PayPal, Shop Pay, Venmo, and Visa. Overall, ES.AI is a useful tool for students looking to improve their writing skills and receive AI-powered assistance in a cost-effective and efficient manner.
