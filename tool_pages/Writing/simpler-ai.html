About Simpler.ai

Simpler.ai is an AI-powered tool that aims to simplify the writing process. It offers users the ability to co-write content with the assistance of artificial intelligence. The tool does not require a monthly fee and users have the option to pay as they go. Upon registration, users receive 6,000 free words and access to free Grammarly spellchecking. One of the key features of Simpler.ai is the Magic Button, which allows users to seamlessly continue text, give commands, write text in the middle, complete lists, and enhance various parts of their content simply by moving the cursor and pressing the button. This feature enables effortless writing and increases productivity. Simpler.ai also offers a distraction-free Google Docs-style editor with a minimalist user interface, allowing users to focus solely on the writing process. The tool is designed to work with any language, as the AI model has been trained on a significant portion of Internet content.In addition to assisting with content creation, Simpler.ai functions as a personal assistant, allowing users to write notes, emails, blogs, taglines, and product descriptions. It also offers an "Ask Anything" feature, enabling users to seek ideas, summaries, or even draft suggestions from the AI.To facilitate ease of use, Simpler.ai offers a one-minute setup process, and account creation involves a simple registration and email confirmation. Users can start with as little as $1 or take advantage of promotional offers to begin with 10,000 free credits. With its ability to automate content creation, Simpler.ai is a valuable tool for those looking to streamline their writing process.
