About Pro-Writer

Pro-Writer is an AI-powered copywriting platform that generates fast, high-quality content for marketing, social media, articles and ads for businesses. It acts as a co-writer for content producers and teams, reducing time, cost and human efforts for drafting and content creation. The AI writes the requested texts within seconds based on the existing texts of the company, using a combination of few-shot-learning and finetuned GPT3+ models to create robust and safe workflows. Pro-Writer also offers customized components, which allow writing in 135 languages, more than any other platform, and produces tailor-made style according to the user's requirements. With Pro-Writer, businesses can brainstorm new ideas and overcome writer's block, unleashing their creativity, and write 10 times faster. Furthermore, the platform provides workflow modules for different situations, such as Press Release Writer, Market Review Writer, and Action Classifier, allowing businesses to tailor the language model according to their unique needs.Overall, Pro-Writer offers a practical solution for businesses to streamline their communication workflow and improve their marketing content efficiency by using AI to automate and augment the tasks of copywriting. Pro-Writer's customized features and modules enable users to create better-outlined content in less time than traditional manual experiences.
