About Textok

Textok is a sophisticated tool designed to automate the development of writing guides and to ensure brand-specific tone, style, and compliance rules. This tool enables the creation of custom automated text-writing guides, defining brand-specific tone, style, spelling, and inclusivity rules. With one click, it enables users to ensure compliance. Textok aids in generating and rewriting text with AI in your specific tone, rather than a generic style. It offers innovative tools to create quality texts, and can detect the tone of any piece of text - be it as cool as a cucumber or as warm as Sundays at your grandma's house. The tool supports the automatic production of precise prompts for AI and allows custom tone creation. Moreover, it aids in the development of term glossaries, facilitates the making of inclusive language work, and provides specific tips. Textok lets you set more than 80 parameters to fine-tune your brands tone of voice, or choose the best suited tone from their comprehensive style book, thereby reducing repetitive errors. The tool ensures consistency in language even with multiple writers and automates brand book corrections. It is designed to help brands and companies strike the right tone in their communication with more consistency, enhancing their brand's growth.
