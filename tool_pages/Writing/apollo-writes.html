About Apollo Writes

Apollo is an AI tool that offers affordable brand copywriting services for blog posts and articles. It allows users to easily and quickly write content in their brand's unique voice using artificial intelligence (AI) and machine learning (ML) capabilities. The tool aims to help brands effectively communicate and elevate their online presence through compelling narratives.With Apollo, users can maintain consistency in their brand's messaging while producing content at lightning speed, eliminating writer's block. The tool's AI-powered platform simplifies the creation of blog posts and articles, allowing users to focus on their brand's message and creativity.Apollo offers a 30-day free trial, which includes one new article and one rewrite, without the need for providing credit card details. After the trial period, users can avail themselves of the tool's services at $10 for 10 new articles and 5 rewrites. These articles and rewrites range from 600 to 1200 words each.The tool emphasizes the importance of maintaining a brand's voice and identity throughout the content creation process. It aims to empower brands by leveraging the power of AI and ML to craft engaging and on-brand blog posts and articles.To further aid users, Apollo provides terms and privacy information, as well as a status page for technical updates and support.
