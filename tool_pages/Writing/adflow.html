About Adflow

Adflow is an AI-powered ad copy generator that helps marketers and businesses quickly create high-conversion ads and campaigns across a variety of platforms, including Google Ads, Facebook for Business, Linkedin Marketing Solutions, Youtube, Amazon Advertising, TikTok, Outbrain, and Taboola. Powered by OpenAI and GPT-3, the largest Natural Language Processing AI, Adflow is capable of generating unique and original ads in English and Italian with just a few words. It also provides users with images, translations and other features to help create a finished ad ready to convert customers. Adflow is free to use on its standard plan, or can be upgraded for additional features. It is an ideal solution for startups, marketers, and agencies to boost their campaigns and save time.
