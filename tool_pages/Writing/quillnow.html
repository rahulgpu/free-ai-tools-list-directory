About Quillnow

QuillNow is an AI-powered writing platform designed to help bloggers, authors, copywriters, and content writers streamline their workflow and produce high-quality content. The platform offers a range of features such as instant feedback, trending topic ideas, content statistics, and a readability score for both native and foreign speakers. Users can adjust their tone and style, store their ideas, and showcase their work in a personalized portfolio. QuillNow provides a simple and intuitive interface that helps writers make their writing process effortless while saving a lot of time. QuillNow offers three plans with different features, Essential, Standard and Business, to cater to writers at different stages of their careers. The platform has a responsive customer support team that addresses users' queries efficiently. QuillNow enables users to write better, faster, and with confidence. The platform has a vibrant writer community consisting of book writers, content professionals, copywriters, students, academics, and others. QuillNow's amazing AI capabilities make it a fantastic choice for writers looking for an all-inclusive writing platform. The platform is an excellent solution that helps users overcome writer's block and allows them to focus on their creative ideas. With QuillNow, users can take creative control over their writing and grow their writing careers while pursuing their writing goals.
