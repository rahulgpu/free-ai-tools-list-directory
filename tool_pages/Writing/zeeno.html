About Zeeno

Slate is a next-generation mobile keyboard powered by Artificial Intelligence. It is designed to transform a smartphone into a device optimized for creation and productivity. The primary goal of Slate is to make creation and productivity tasks as efficient on a mobile device as they are on a laptop. This application can be accessed from any other application, making its services available on-demand everywhere. The application challenges the traditional idea that mobile phones are only meant for consumption and asserts that they have unfathomed creative capabilities. The primary feature of Slate is its innovative, AI-powered virtual keyboard interface that represents a leap towards maximum efficiency and productivity. The solution aims to eliminate the need for a laptop for performing productivity tasks, thereby presenting a technology that enables you to maintain productivity regardless of your location. Rather than forcing you to plan your tasks around your computer, the Slate interface helps make your mobile phone the ultimate device for creation.
