About Cohesive AI

Cohesive is an AI-powered editor designed to boost productivity by optimizing content creation and editing processes. The technology brings together human creativity and AI to facilitate seamless creation, refinement, editing, and publishing of work. Cohesive offers a wide range of applications including SEO, ad copywriting, marketing copywriting, and social media content. It features over 200 handpicked curated templates designed to speed up the content production process. The tool also aims to increase collaboration within teams, offering real-time collaboration features designed to keep everyone updated and on schedule. Editing capabilities extend to text, images and language translations. In addition, the platform generates content ideas as users write within the editor, providing a constant flow of inspiration. Cohesive addresses various needs across personal and professional realms, delivering high-converting AI content to maximize marketing ROI, conversion-focused emails for sales, fast customer support conversations, and AI-powered enhancements for personal writing.
