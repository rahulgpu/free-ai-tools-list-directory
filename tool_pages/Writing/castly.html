About Castly

Castly is an AI-powered tool that aims to enhance content creation and knowledge acquisition for bloggers, marketers, learners, and writers. With Castly, users can easily generate content by engaging in a conversation with the AI or gaining insights from uploaded documents. The tool allows users to select and transfer appealing segments of the conversation into an advanced text editor, enabling them to refine and enhance their content seamlessly. Castly's feature-rich text editor empowers users to craft polished and engaging content with ease.Additionally, Castly offers a streamlined process for sharing content. Once users have finalized their content within the text editor, the platform facilitates quick and effortless publishing, eliminating delays and ensuring effective dissemination of their message.Castly provides both free and paid pricing plans. The free plan includes unlimited conversations, up to 5 PDF file uploads with a maximum of 10MB per file, and publishing of up to 5 posts. The Castly Pro plan, priced at $17.99 (with a promotional price of $12/month), offers unlimited conversations, unlimited PDF file uploads (up to 50MB per file), unlimited published posts, and upcoming features such as question answering over YouTube videos and audio files.Furthermore, Castly encourages users to join their community, where content creators can connect with others, fostering creativity, collaboration, and personal growth.
