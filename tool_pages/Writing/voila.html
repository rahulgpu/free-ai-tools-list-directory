About Voila

Voila is an AI browser assistant powered by ChatGPT. It's designed to streamline user's online interactions, making them more intelligent and efficient. With Voila, users can ask any question, and the AI tool will provide a response via chat. This tool enables users to respond to emails, answer common questions, summarize and translate text, and brainstorm ideas, amongst other things. Voila is available on various platforms including Chrome, Edge, Firefox, Safari, iOS and Android. It features a library of expert, battle-tested prompts and can transform web pages into different kinds of content. Voila also supports research with web access and can work in any language. To help content creation and writing, it provides prompts tailored to aid in crafting various types of professional content such as ad campaigns, landing page copy, blog posts, resumes, LinkedIn posts, email replies, and more. It boasts AI-powered Instant Reply functionality for drafting email responses, which users can review, edit, and send. Furthermore, Voila offers comprehensive web research capabilities. It has features for copy editing, improving writing, fixing spelling and grammar, translating text, and summarizing or extracting key information from any document. Users can also streamline their browser experience with convenient shortcuts for common browser and work-related actions, speeding up daily tasks.
