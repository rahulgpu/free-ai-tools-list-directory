About Shy Editor

Shy Editor, once known as Pencil, is an AI-empowered writing environment that assists various types of writers: novelists, non-fiction writers, students, academics, bloggers, content creators, and journalists. The platform utilizes advanced AI technology to help improve writing speed and quality, enhance writer productivity, and instill more confidence in the writing process. This AI ensures the writer's style remains unique while aiding in the writing process. The platform includes a suite of tools that allows users to customize their working approach, which includes lists, toggles, markdown, and blocks. Users can integrate text, media, and tables to create a seamless work experience and organize their workflow, whether writing a report, creating code, or strategizing a plan. Features such as the versatile Markdown editor, AI assistant for feedback and brainstorming, quick note-taking, knowledge base for research organization, bibliography and citation management, progress tracking stats, version control, intelligent search, grammar check, and content export options are available. Available in basic and professional versions, this platform is designed to evolve and cater to different writing needs.
