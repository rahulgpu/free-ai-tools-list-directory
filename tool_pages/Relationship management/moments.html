About Moments

Moments is a Personal Relationship Manager (PRM) tool designed to help users deepen their personal and professional relationships. The tool aims to assist users in being thoughtful at the right moments in a personal way. With Moments, users can organize, prioritize, and strengthen their social connections effortlessly. The tool provides features such as managing social to-do lists to reduce cognitive load and stress, organizing contacts into different groups based on importance levels, creating events and reminders for contacts and getting timely reminders for tasks like sending messages and buying gifts.Additionally, Moments offers a social feed that consolidates social updates and activity data from the user's CRM contacts, allowing users to focus on the people who matter most to them. The tool also provides resources to improve social skills, including articles and courses covering topics such as improving friendships and effective networking strategies for introverts.One of the standout features of Moments is the AI Composer, which enables the tool to compose personalized messages and emails tailored to the unique relationship and specific interests of each contact.Overall, Moments aims to simplify relationship management, enhance communication, and provide users with the tools they need to maintain meaningful connections, both personally and professionally.
