About Frequentli

Frequentli is an AI-powered tool designed to streamline the process of generating chatbot intents for businesses. It offers a solution to the traditional time-consuming method of manually creating intents by automating the process with the help of artificial intelligence. Users can leverage Frequentli to automatically generate FAQs, intents, and training phrases for their existing chatbots. The platform is positioned as a time-saving alternative, allowing users to generate chatbot intents in under 30 seconds. It eliminates the manual effort involved in pulling data from various sources by delegating the task to the power of AI. Users can upload relevant resources, and the AI-driven system takes care of the intent generation. Key features of Frequentli include collaboration tools, enabling users to invite people from different departments to collectively gather data and manage intents. The sub-link extractor automatically recognizes all sublinks of webpages on a website, feeding them into the intent generator. The phrase generator further expands generated intents by creating related keywords and phrases. Frequentli supports efficient export capabilities, allowing users to export chatbot intents in CSV format for integration with their chatbot builder. The platform offers different pricing plans, including a free basic plan, a pro plan with additional features, and an enterprise plan catering to larger organizations with advanced security and customization needs.
