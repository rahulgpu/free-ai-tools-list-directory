About Giftpack AI

Giftpack AI is a corporate gifting platform that leverages artificial intelligence to automate and personalize the process of bulk gifting. It provides tailored gifts to individual recipients, facilitating data-driven appreciation and engagement of employees and clients. The platform handles all steps of the gifting process; from idea generation to delivery, saving users time and effort typically spent in selecting and acquiring suitable gifts. Giftpack AI also features a user-friendly dashboard that enables users to track the progress of their gifting initiatives, recipient interactions, as well as key return-on-investment metrics. It uses modern technology to analyze the digital footprints of individuals to suggest the most suitable gifts for them. Additionally, the platform offers a diverse catalog of physical goods, vouchers and experience gifts allowing its use globally. Giftpack AI is also designed to support branding efforts and offers an extensive catalog for producing branded gifts. It is used by companies to digitize their gifting workflow and enhance relationships, especially in remote working environments.
