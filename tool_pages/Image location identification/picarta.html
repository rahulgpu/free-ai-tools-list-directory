About Picarta

Picarta is an AI-based tool that predicts the geographical location of a photo worldwide. Users need to upload an image, and the software will use Artificial Intelligence algorithms to analyze and detect potential locations where it might have been captured, providing GPS coordinates. Credited for its accuracy and reliability, this image geolocalization solution streamlines the identification of photo locations for exploration, research, and decision-making. Moreover, it also maps the latitudes and longitudes onto Google Maps, giving users a visual reference of the image's origin. Picarta extends its services by offering EXIF data detection and Landmarks detection. Consequently, a user gains a significant insight into the captured image's intricate details, such as the timestamp and camera details used for capturing the image. By transforming image data into actionable information, Picarta can serve various spheres, including individuals, businesses, researchers, and explorers.
