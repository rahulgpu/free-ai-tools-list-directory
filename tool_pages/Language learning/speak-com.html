About Speak.com

Speak is a language learning application that utilizes advanced AI technology to provide real conversational practice, eliminating the need for a live language tutor. The core philosophy of Speak revolves around encouraging learners to practice speaking the language out loud. The apps distinctive feature lies in its AI-developed character, the Speak Tutor, which is positioned as a personal language tutor. The Speak Tutor designs a tailored teaching curriculum based on learners' individual preferences, thus ensuring a personalised and engaging learning experience. It is designed to be an on-the-go conversational partner, allowing users to practice speaking on any topic of their choice, anytime. Moreover, the Speak Tutor keeps users motivated and accountable in reaching their language learning goals. The fluency-focused curriculum aims to make learners proficient speakers as quickly, but effectively as possible. The application also includes the ability to generate study plans based on users available time and teach through a very interactive system, thus offering a diverse and robust approach to language learning.
