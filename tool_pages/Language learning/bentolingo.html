About Bentolingo

Bentolingo is a language learning application with a twist, combining artificial intelligence to provide a personalized learning journey. It acts as a 'daily language bento box', serving learners with a variety of language skills to practice on a regular basis. The application is designed to mitigate decision fatigue by offering a pre-set clear structure of lessons and activities for different linguistic abilities. The primary skills catered to by bentolingo are spread over six domains of language learning. The AI element is implemented to adjust the flow and content of lessons based on a user's progress and achievement. Thus, it adapts the learning process to match each user's individual pace and proficiency level. To access and use bentolingo, JavaScript needs to be enabled on the device, ensuring proper functioning and optimal user experience. The application could be best suited for language learners seeking a structured, varied and personalized approach in their journey towards language acquisition and mastery.
