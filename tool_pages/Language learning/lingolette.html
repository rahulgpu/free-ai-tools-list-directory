About Lingolette

Lingolette is an advanced language teaching tool that utilizes AI techniques to enhance the learning experience. Faced with addressing the issue of student dropout rates in language learning, Lingolette employs a blend of interactive features to maintain student engagement. Through the use of a chat-based system, powered by a personalized neural network, Lingolette allows users to practice the language in real-time, encouraging an immersive learning experience. It also includes a unique learning adaptation functionality that adjusts the learning materials according to the user's knowledge and interests. This ensures the new words and phrases presented are relevant and challenging, fostering steady language progress. Moreover, Lingolette works to improve both the user's pronunciation and verbal skills by interacting as a real teacher. The teaching machine provides clear explanations every step of the way, making new knowledge easily accessible. Focusing on intensive and personalized language learning, Lingolette also includes a motivational feature to keep students committed to their language journey. Furthermore, Lingolette offers daily articles, adding variety to the learning resources. For companies interested in integrating Lingolette, an API manual is accessible.
