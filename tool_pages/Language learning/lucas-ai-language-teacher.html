About Lucas – AI Language Teacher

Lucas is a personal AI language teacher designed to help individuals learn and improve their language skills in English, Spanish, German, Italian, Portuguese or French. It operates as an audio-based program that can easily be integrated into your daily routine. Rather than just focusing on vocabulary or grammar tests, Lucas emphasises on enabling users to learn to speak the language. This AI tool creates personalized lesson plans tailored to the user's proficiency level, from beginners to professionals. The program is conceived to adjust to your needs, by allowing you to request specific topics and adjust the speech speed. Furthermore, the tool complements the learning process by offering the capability to send materials via WhatsApp for further reading. The service operates on a fair monthly subscription model, offering a flexible term with a cancellation option at any time. Lucas also allows potential users to try out the service for free before deciding to subscribe. The tool is part of a phone app, thus providing an accessible, convenient and portable learning resource.
