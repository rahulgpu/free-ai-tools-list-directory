About Tutor Lily

Tutor Lily is an AI-powered language learning tool that aims to help users become fluent in another language by practicing real-life conversations. It offers a friendly AI companion that can engage in conversations with learners. Powered by ChatGPT and GPT4, Tutor Lily is available for download as a mobile app. The tool supports 18 languages and has received positive reviews from over 25,000 language learners. Tutor Lily's key features include real-life conversations, instant feedback, and corrections. Users can choose their desired language and proficiency level, and then engage in conversations with Tutor Lily in their native language or the language they're learning. The AI companion provides instant feedback, corrections, and detailed explanations to assist learners in improving their language skills. Tutor Lily is designed to be available 24/7, allowing learners to practice at their convenience.The tool emphasizes affordability, claiming to be 30x more affordable compared to traditional language tutors. Tutor Lily offers both free and subscription-based plans. The free starter plan includes limited daily messages and mistake explanations, while the monthly and annual subscriptions provide unlimited messages and mistake explanations, access to premium features, and early access to new features. User testimonials highlight the engaging chatbot format and the effectiveness of real-time corrections in enhancing language learning. Users have reported significant progress in a short period of time.Overall, Tutor Lily offers an AI-powered solution for language learners seeking a convenient and affordable way to practice conversations and improve their fluency in a new language.
