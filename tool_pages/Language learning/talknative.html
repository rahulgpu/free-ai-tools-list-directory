About TalkNative

TalkNative is a responsive language learning tool that provides users access to native speakers across a range of languages, available around the clock. It eliminates the problem of time zone differences and conflicting schedules, making it possible for users to connect with a language tutor at any time. The unique aspect of this tool is its ability to adapt to the user's language proficiency level, providing tailored assistance that supports progressive learning. The interactive platform creates a friendly and engaging environment, simulating casual conversations that make language learning feel less rigid and more enjoyable. TalkNative supports a spectrum of languages, including English, Chinese (Mandarin), Spanish, French, German, Portuguese, Italian, Russian, Japanese, Korean, Turkish, Hindi, Dutch and Polish. The tool is available on both the Apple App Store and Google Play Store, making it accessible for a wide range of gadget users.
