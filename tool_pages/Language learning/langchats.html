About Langchats

Langchats is an AI-powered Language Partner that helps users improve their fluency in over 30 languages. The tool supports multiple major dialects for each language and uses AI technology to offer translations, voice responses, corrections and suggestions for phrases. Users can practice speaking anytime and anywhere, making it flexible and convenient to fit language learning into their daily routines. The tool encourages spoken language from the beginning to help users emulate native speakers and improve their accents. It also offers a credit system based on usage, allowing users to send and receive messages that count towards their subscription. Langchats claims to be 15 times cheaper than a normal language tutor, and subscribers can benefit from a 30-day money-back guarantee. The tool is powered by AI technology from ChatGPT which is a world leader in consumer Artificial Intelligence, and it is the brainchild of Jay, a language learning enthusiast who aims to create the best language learning resources on the internet. The tool promises to help users gain invaluable experience in any situation, improve their speaking skills through regular practice, and build confidence in public speaking, job interviews and other scenarios. Langchats offers a 7-day free trial, and users can choose between monthly and yearly pricing plans.
