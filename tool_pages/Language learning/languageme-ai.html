About LanguageMe AI

LanguageMe AI is a revolutionary language-learning app designed to provide a customizable experience for users. It adapts generative AI technology and combines it with conventional language-learning strategies to assist users in mastering 65 different languages. Features include personalized learning that allows users to select content and difficulty, combining key teaching methods of comprehensive reading materials and flashcards for optimal learning. It uses established language learning principles like comprehensible input (learning words in context) and spaced repetition (reviewing material at increasing intervals). The tool includes Text-to-Speech in 65 languages for perfecting pronunciation and includes support for romanization for character-based languages like Chinese and Japanese. An integral feature of the tool is its user-friendly interface designed to make the language learning engaging and fun. Supports Albanian, Amharic, Arabic, Armenian, Bengali, Bosnian, Bulgarian, Burmese, Catalan, Chinese, Croatian, Czech, Danish, Dutch, Estonian, English, Finnish, French, Georgian, German, Greek, Gujarati, Hindi, Hungarian, Icelandic, Indonesian, Italian, Japanese, Kannada, Kazakh, Korean, Latvian, Lithuanian, Macedonian, Malay, Malayalam, Marathi, Mongolian, Norwegian, Persian, Polish, Portuguese, Punjabi, Romanian, Russian, Serbian, Slovak, Slovenian, Somali, Spanish, Swahili, Swedish, Tagalog, and more languages.
