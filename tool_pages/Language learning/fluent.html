About Fluent

Fluent is an AI tool designed to streamline data insights extraction for businesses. It offers a user-friendly interface where users can simply type in their data-related questions and get instant insights. Fluent works to understand the question and provide nuanced, relevant data insights. The tool lets users explore their data, helping to uncover critical questions that could further enhance their business understanding.Fluent facilitates a shared comprehension of a company's data by promoting real-time collaboration and a unifying data dictionary. This feature reduces the potential for confusion, disagreements, or discrepancies in revenue definitions or other business metrics.Through integration with popular communication platforms like Slack and Teams, Fluent enables users to gain data insights without the need to leave chat interfaces. The tool's features include easy-to-understand SQL outputs, enhanced data integrity through curated datasets with clear usage guidelines, and seamless embedding of company-specific metrics, definitions, and terms, ensuring consistent responses for business users.Moreover, Fluent is compatible with seven common data warehouses and four typical file formats. For data security, it uses industry-standard AES-256 encryption for warehouse credentials and offers deployment options for on-premise, in data centres, or Virtual Private Clouds (VPCs). Fluent also offers multi-factor authentication with major identity providers and lets users opt for their storage solutions.
