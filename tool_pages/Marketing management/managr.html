About Managr

Managr is a generative AI platform principally designed for PR and marketing teams. It allows its users to keep up with the latest news swiftly by summarizing real-time news coverage and spotting trends on social media platforms. One of the key features of Managr is its capacity to create personalized content that reflects the users unique writing style. This platform is not only centered around news summaries, but also caters to content creation, engagement optimization, and newsjacking - brainstorming creative and timely ideas based on the latest news. An additional feature of the platform is its ability to find journalists and influencers along with providing pitching tips. Managr also offers unique workflow automations that result in time savings for marketing and PR teams. The platform has a wide coverage, as it taps into a large number of news articles and social media content delivering meaningful, real-time insights for PR professionals. Managr also respects user privacy, ensuring that data is not used to train commercial AI models, providing personalized and accurate results without compromising privacy.
