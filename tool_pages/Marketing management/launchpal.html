About LaunchPal

LaunchPal is an AI-enhanced marketing tool designed to streamline the marketing workflow for brands, agencies, and consultants. Key features of this platform include training the AI on new brands in a short time, delivering fast results, and managing clients with ease. Users can plug in their client's URL and assets and train the platform, making it adaptable to different brands. It also offers automated content delivery which is continuously improved over time. The platform also includes a client portal designed to elevate the brand experience and save time, making it possible to share assets and reports, and manage approvals. The platform offers different pricing models to cater to individual marketers, agencies managing multiple brands, and larger enterprises seeking tailored solutions. LaunchPal also allows users to run a free trial before commitment. It emphasises control for its users, enabling brands to manage asset libraries, schedule posting across platforms, and review advanced analytics. For agencies, unique features include a white-label client portal, an agency dashboard, and additional approval workflows.
