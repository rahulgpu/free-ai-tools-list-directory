About Hawke AI

Hawke AI is a digital marketing insight tool built to analyse digital marketing platforms and deliver data-backed insights. The primary features of Hawke AI include monitoring and alerts, allowing users to be notified of performance issues, generating detailed reports and trackers for effortless monitoring of marketing efforts. With Hawke AI, users can analyze the performance of their budgets and Key Performance Indicators (KPIs) across all their accounts and track them to ensure pace. The tool provides the ability to optimize marketing budget allocation between different platforms based on performance, and benchmark performance against other businesses in the same industry. It also offers the ability to gain insights via ChatGPT. Alerts functionality allows early identification of potential risks and problems. Hawke AI is notably operational for a diverse range of industries and business types such as Agencies, Businesses, Multi-locations, and Investment Firms, providing a single source of truth for data-driven decisions and universal transparency across all major marketing channels. User feedback suggests Hawke AI is easy to navigate, time saving, and offers valuable insights for growth strategies.
