About BOSCO

ASK BOSCO is an AI-powered marketing platform designed to aid agencies and retailers in making informed marketing decisions. ASK BOSCO's key functions include consolidating various marketing data and utilizing algorithmic modelling to produce personalized reporting dashboards. Hence creating a single source of truth and facilitating marketers to make effective digital marketing spend decisions utilizing data-driven insights. Additionally, it aids in planning budgets and forecasting future performance using advanced machine learning techniques to offer data-based predictions. Not only can it analyze and monitor your organic and paid media performance, giving you insights for quick revenue growth and footprint addition, but it also has a feature that allows you to benchmark your digital marketing performance against competitors. Its Index makes use of third party data to rate the digital interactions of you and your competitors, where the scores range from 0 to 1000. ASK BOSCO streamlines digital reporting without requiring coding and provides marketing support as well.
