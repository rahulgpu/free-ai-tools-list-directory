About Humanic

Humanic PLG OS is a tool designed to help businesses transform free users into paying customers by leveraging AI technologies. It offers features for handling lifecycle marketing and automating workflows, powered by real-time user activity data and profile information. Humanic's AI capabilities extend to creating personalized emails for users, offering a blend of product usage data, real-time segmentation and AI to drive conversion. Moreover, the tool enables businesses to track the performance and impact of their marketing campaigns and hypotheses in real-time, providing insights into what is working and what is ineffective for users. Additionally, it automates content generation in response to user actions within a product, eliminating the need for consistent manual content creation. The platform integrates with tools such as Stripe, Segment, Intercom and HubSpot, fostering an interconnected marketing ecosystem. The aim of the tool is to foster growth while providing users with deep insights into their campaigns performance, ultimately assisting businesses to drive meaningful, personalized experiences at scale.
