About Swifty

Swifty is an AI business travel assistant designed to simplify the process of planning and organizing business trips. With Swifty, users can create their travel itineraries and book flights and hotels quickly, saving time and effort.The tool enables users to book flights and hotels simply by chatting with the AI assistant. This means that users can provide their travel preferences and requirements through conversation, allowing the assistant to handle the booking process on their behalf.In addition, Swifty provides easy access to trip details, confirmations, and business-related documents such as invoices and receipts. Users can retrieve this information with just a click, making it convenient to stay organized and manage expenses.Users can also ask Swifty any questions related to their trip, and the AI assistant will provide relevant information and assistance. This feature ensures that users have access to all the necessary details and can get answers to their queries in a timely manner.Swifty is backed by Lufthansa Innovation Hub, providing credibility and expertise in the field of travel. By using Swifty, users can streamline their business travel process, making it more efficient and hassle-free.Overall, Swifty's AI travel assistant offers a convenient solution for business travelers, allowing them to plan, book, and manage their trips with ease.
