About Avataar’s GenAI Creator

Avataar's GenAI Creator leverages Generative AI to enable spatial storytelling, ushering in a seamless integration of digital and physical reality. This platform is designed to make the user the protagonist, enhancing decision-making capabilities via immersive interactions and a immersive reality context. It empowers users to effortlessly create and manipulate 3D models using a suite of intuitive tools. These tools include a 3D modeller that can generate 3D objects from single 2D images, a feature that allows users to import, export, and edit 3D models, and an incubator application that can easily turn objects of various kinds into 3D models. Avataar's offering also includes a platform to craft 3D rooms, bringing digital spaces to life interactively. The platform has an application across various consumer touchpoints, ensuring cost-effectiveness through its neural-driven technology. Its broad utility spans diverse industries like furniture, electronics & durables, footwear, marketplaces, bedding, bags and luggage. One of the defining aspects of this platform is its emphasis on fostering a community of creators, innovators, and enthusiasts who lead advancements in spatial storytelling.
