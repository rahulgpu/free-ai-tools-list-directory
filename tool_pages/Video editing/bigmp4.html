About bigmp4

bigmp4's AI Video Enhancement is a cutting-edge tool for lossless video enlargement, enhancement, and colorization. It uses a 2022 AI model to increase the resolution of videos up to 2K, 4K, and 8K with enhanced quality and reduced noise. It also supports black and white video colorization, smooth slow motion, and batch mode. The tool supports a variety of file types, including mp4, mov, mkv, m4v, mpg, mpeg, avi, jpg, png, bmp, webp, and gif. It can also process a large number of images in a zip file. AI interpolation increases video frames per second (FPS) to 60, 120, and 240 FPS, making the video vivid and life-like. The tool also keeps the video FPS unchanged after AI interpolation for silky smooth slow motion. Users can purchase the service to prioritize their tasks and upload larger files. The uploaded files and results files are automatically deleted within a week. If a processing failure occurs, the GPU points deducted are refunded after deleting the failed task. The tool provides a FAQ section to explain how it works and answer any other questions. It also offers contact information for further support.
