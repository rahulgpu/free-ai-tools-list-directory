About Nova A.I.

Nova A.I. is an online video editing platform that leverages artificial intelligence for a variety of tools and features. It provides multiple video editing tools such as clipper, trimmer, merger, and cropper. Furthermore, it facilitates video content enhancement through features like sticker, text and image addition, speed control, and addition of various video elements and transitions. The platform is also integrated with a computer vision video search engine and video analyzer tools such as automatic video cutter and video recognition. It carries a range of functions for generating subtitles, translating them, and hardcoding them into videos. Nova A.I.s audio capabilities include an AI voice generator for video dubbing and audio translation. The platform provides easy content creation for a variety of purposes including corporate, promotional, educational, social media, and other videos. In partnership with iStock, Nova A.I. also offers access to a range of digital assets such as stock footage, royalty-free audio, and studio-quality images. In addition to video editing and creation, it provides video management functionalities like video annotation, archiving, search, and moderation.
