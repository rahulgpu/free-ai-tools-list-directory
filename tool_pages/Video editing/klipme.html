About Klipme

Klipme is a powerful visual AI video editor that automates the video editing process using sophisticated Visual AI algorithms. It offers a hassle-free web app that allows users to easily clip and edit their videos. With top-of-the-line Visual AI algorithms, Klipme guarantees comprehensive video summaries and reels without the need for complex menus or a videography degree.The tool is designed to simplify content generation by automatically creating clips from videos. It is especially useful for creators who need short clips for platforms like TikTok, Instagram, and YouTube. Klipme generates up to 25 prepped and ready-to-publish clips from a single AI run, making daily production streamlined across more than 30 content categories.Klipme excels at two main tasks: creating promotional clips and summarizing long-form content. The Visual AI video editor utilizes a unique technology stack powered by cognitive science to analyze individual frames of footage, identifying points of interest similar to the human brain. This ensures that the generated clips include the most important quotes and memorable moments from the original videos.Customization options are available to users, allowing them to set parameters such as regular or visual-driven promos and summaries, audio-driven clipping, music-driven clipping, duration, smart video cropping for landscape to portrait aspect ratios, visual styling, and the ability to add background music.With Klipme, users can save hours on manual video editing and have the Visual AI video editor handle the process up to 20 times faster. Overall, Klipme offers a user-friendly solution for effortless video editing and content creation, leveraging the power of Visual AI algorithms.
