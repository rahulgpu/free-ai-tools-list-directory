About Vidnami

Vidnami Pro is an AI-driven online creation platform that enables businesses to leverage the power of video marketing, irrespective of their industry. With this tool, users can create all types of video content, including content videos, sales videos, influencer videos, e-commerce ads, course videos, real estate videos, Facebook and Instagram ads, and more. The program's Artificial Intelligence feature automatically splits video into appropriate scenes and selects thematically matched movie clips from the well-known Storyblocks website, making content creation a lot easier and faster. Users can adjust everything perfectly to their content, either with the help of AI or manually, and can also record their own voice track or upload pre-recorded voice tracks to use with scenes. Users can choose between several male or female voices with various accents. Besides, the tool provides access to a huge database of video clips, static images, and audio tracks. The program comes with tons of features, including automated captions, automatic aspect ratio adjustment, and easy video sharing on social media platforms. Vidnami Pro is easy to use and offers a free trial version, and discounted versions for full purchases, giving users the chance to put the program to the test before making any financial commitment. Overall, Vidnami Pro is a powerful video marketing tool that can significantly boost businesses' online visibility and engagement.
