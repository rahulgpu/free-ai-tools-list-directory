About Smartrazor

Smartrazor is an AI-based tool designed to streamline and expedite the video editing process for content creators, particularly YouTubers. This tool significantly reduces the time taken for first pass edits by automatically cutting out mistakes from raw footage. It works by enabling users to upload raw footage, full of potential errors or bad takes, and within a few minutes, Smartrazor applies its auto-clip function to trim the uploaded content. A unique feature is its transcript editor, which can be employed to make any quick changes before exporting the cleaned file to the user's preferred primary video editor. Therefore, the user does not have to painstakingly listen through footage to make basic cuts. Smartrazor is thus an essential tool for creators, entrepreneurs, editors or content agency owners who primarily make 'talking on camera videos'. It allows creators to push out more content efficiently while freeing up their time to focus more on content creation, storytelling and other high ROI tasks. The tool currently supports English language only and provides export flexibility as users can export a zip file of all their clips to import into their preferred editor.
