About Peech

Peech is an AI video platform developed to support marketers. It possesses streamlined video editing, enhanced with advanced NLP technology, aimed to control and enhance content marketing. Peech helps generate high-quality branded videos to strengthen marketing strategies in alignment with established publishing strategies. The AI platform is designed for automatic video post-production, capable of sourcing videos from a user's content and then transforming them into branded videos via a one-click, fully automated process. Peech's AI empowers media companies with solutions like organizing and categorizing large volumes of video footage, maintaining brand consistency using automatic branding element integration, reaching international audiences via real-time transcription and translation in over 20 languages, easy video editing, video adjustment for multiple platforms, and short-form video content generation for social media. Peechs suite of tools also includes a smart content analysis that reduces time and effort required for organizing large volumes of video content, an AI video editor that allows easy tweaks and edits, and a frame cropper that helps with automated resizing and positioning of videos. With Peech, teams can enjoy scalable video output without compromising on quality while saving time and cost, and increasing productivity.
