About Smudge.ai

Smudge.ai is a Chrome extension that enhances your right-click menu with AI-powered shortcuts. It integrates ChatGPT technology to provide various commands directly in your browser. With smudge.ai, you can perform tasks such as summarizing lengthy articles, refining your writing, or translating messages without leaving the page.The extension offers popular commands like summarize, correct (spell check), bulletize (convert text into bullet points), refine (improve the readability of your writing), and translate. These commands are intended to help students, professionals, and anyone looking to improve their productivity while browsing the internet. Students can use the tool to polish their papers, generate study notes, or solve complex word problems. Professionals can create and edit content, draft emails, or overcome language barriers with AI-powered translations. Smudge.ai is also suitable for everyday browsing, allowing you to summarize any text, fix writing mistakes, or even transform any content into a limerick for a fun reading experience.Furthermore, smudge.ai allows users to customize their toolset by choosing from 16 built-in commands, creating their own commands, or importing commands from the gallery. The tool is compatible with Google Chrome, and a single license can be used on multiple devices via Chrome profile syncing. Importantly, user data privacy is maintained, as the extension does not log or save input or output text, and third parties do not have access to the text. The extension also does not employ tracking/analytics cookies or scripts.Overall, smudge.ai is a practical AI tool that enhances browsing efficiency and productivity by providing AI-powered commands accessible through the right-click menu in Chrome.
