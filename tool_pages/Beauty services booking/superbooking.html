About SuperBooking

SuperBooking is an AI-powered tool designed to make the booking of beauty services simple and efficient. Integrated with the AVAX blockchain, SuperBooking provides a platform for secure transactions and ensures all reviews on their platform are 100% verified, eliminating fake reviews. Users can book various beauty services including hair salons, nail salons, skin treatments, day spas, and more directly from the tool. The tool also features 'Zippy', a friendly AI booking assistant that can provide recommendations on businesses and services, or provide information on businesses that have 5-star ratings. Users can also engage with Zippy to get insights about specific types of services. SuperBooking offers a variety of payment methods for its users including credit/debit cards, crypto, or a GBP deposit. For ease of use, the tool also supports prepayment methods and a feature to convert user points into $SN, the SuperBooking token. This feature allows users to fund their in-app wallets and make transactions from the same. The tool is poised for early access launch in 2024. It's worth noting that SuperBooking also offers a referral program where users can earn points by referring the tool to others.
