About Tootler

Tootler's SOP Writer is an AI-based online tool designed to assist users in writing a statement of purpose (SOP) for various purposes such as study visas, student visas, and tourist visas. The tool offers several key features to enhance the SOP writing experience.One notable feature is the inbuilt plagiarism checker, which allows users to verify the originality of their content. This is particularly beneficial for students, teachers, and writers who want to ensure the integrity of their work.The tool also offers an inbuilt editor that enables users to customize their SOPs on the go. Users can apply text formatting, such as bold, italics, and underline, to enhance the visual appeal of their SOPs.Another valuable feature is the Autofill Inputs functionality, which automates repetitive data entry. This feature saves users time by allowing them to enter information with just a few clicks.Tootler's SOP Writer provides a personal library feature, enabling users to create and manage a collection of customized SOPs for their personal and professional needs. This feature makes it easy for users to access and search for SOPs whenever they need them.Additionally, Tootler's SOP Writer offers affordable pricing, making it accessible to a wide range of users. The tool supports various types of SOPs, and new ones are regularly added.With the ability to download SOPs in .docx format with all formatting intact, users can conveniently use the downloaded files without worrying about formatting issues.Overall, Tootler's SOP Writer is a comprehensive AI tool that streamlines and simplifies the SOP writing process, providing users with the tools they need to create compelling and personalized statements of purpose.
