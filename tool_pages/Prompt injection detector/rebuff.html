About Rebuff

Rebuff AI is a sophisticated tool designed for detecting prompt injection, a common vulnerability in AI systems. The underlying technology is self-hardening, meaning it evolves and strengthens its capacity to identify potential threats over time, becoming more resilient with continuous usage. Rebuff AI's uniqueness lies in its transformative approach to threat detection - rather than maintaining a static level of defense, it learns and adapts from the attacks it counters, thereby amplifying its protective function. This tool also provides an interactive area known as the 'Playground' where users can test and observe its capabilities in real-time. For developers and users looking for detailed information on how to use the product, comprehensive documentation is available. Moreover, the tool is openly hosted on GitHub, indicating transparency and an openness for improvements from the developer community. This tool is of particular interest to AI developers, cybersecurity professionals, and organizations that seek to fortify their AI systems against prompt injection attacks. Rebuff AI stands as a resilient layer of defense in the continually evolving landscape of AI threats.
