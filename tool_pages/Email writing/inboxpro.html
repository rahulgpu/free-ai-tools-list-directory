About InboxPro

InboxPro is an AI-powered tool that is designed to streamline the process of acquiring and nurturing clients. It incorporates various features such as AI Email Assistant that composes and summarizes email content with a single click, and a Calendar Schedule that allows clients to schedule meetings. The tool is equipped with capabilities to devise automated follow-up sequences and track email activities such as email opens and link clicks. Additionally, it provides facilities for creating and saving email templates and generating email activity statistics. It also offers a selection of professional signature templates and features an Email Checker for verifying the existence of an email address. Users can effortlessly transfer their email contacts to InboxPro or add new ones manually as needed. The platform is compatible with Gmail Contacts and allows for a follow-up email sequence setup based on business objectives. Using the merge tags feature, personalization of communication is made possible by including client's name or company details. Integration with Google Calendar aids in setting availability and scheduling appointments. Additional features like Email Tracking, Templating, and professionally designed Signatures empower control over the sales funnel. The tool also includes extended features such as email performance insights for informed decision-making and additional security with RGPD, HIPAA, ISO certification.
