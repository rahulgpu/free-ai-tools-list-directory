About Persana AI

Persana AI is a prospecting tool aiming to enhance outbound efforts for businesses of all sizes. It provides a platform for identifying, enriching, and personalizing prospects with the support of AI technology. Leveraging over 75+ data sources, Persana AI aids in building high quality lead lists from popular platforms such as LinkedIn, Apollo, Salesforce, ContactOut, and GitHub, among others. The tool further provides dynamic data enrichment features, helping improve the find rate for valuable prospect information such as emails. In order to stay ahead of competition, the tool also offers exclusive features such as tracking job changes within target accounts to spot promotions and important movements. Businesses can also monitor potential leads who are hiring, as these companies are likely to require their service. Additionally, Persana AI makes it easier to track technographics across companies for finding those that are using related technologies. The tool is designed to simplify the prospecting process, significantly saving time and improving the efficiency of outbound efforts. Persana AI offers a Chrome extension for further ease of use, providing its service directly on the LinkedIn platform.
