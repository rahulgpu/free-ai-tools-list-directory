About Flowrite

Flowrite is an AI-powered communication tool that helps users save time and hit the right tone when sending emails and messages. The Flowrite Chrome Extension works across popular web browsers, allowing users to quickly generate ready-to-send emails and messages. It provides a library of smart templates for common emails and messages, and users can also create their own custom templates for frequently used messages. The Flowrite Tone Selector allows users to choose the right tone for their message, from casual to formal, friendly to polite, and respectful. Flowrite also provides users with an AI-powered assistant that can help them generate unique drafts with one click, making it easier to write emails and messages. Finally, Flowrite's intuitive user interface makes it easy to use and enables users to quickly generate emails and messages.
