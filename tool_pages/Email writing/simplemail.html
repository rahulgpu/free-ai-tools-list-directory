About SimpleMail

SimpleMail is an AI-powered tool designed to streamline the email writing process, summarizing lengthy emails into concise notes, crafting grammatically error-free responses from a few bullet points and offering options for the tone of replies. The technology aims to free up time spent on email management for users, allowing more focus on essential tasks. SimpleMail interfaces with Gmail and main features include AI-powered summarization, which turns extensive emails into key points, and the AI reply function, with which the AI drafts an email depending on a selected tone, including positive, negative, and neutral options. It also supports an AI compose function, interpreting point forms and grammar-less input into grammatically correct and professional emails. The tool provides frequent users with the option to produce alternative versions of generated emails with a refresh button. According to the provider, SimpleMail does not currently support Outlook, Yahoo and other platforms beyond Gmail, but work is in progress to extend its compatibility. The tool operates as a Chrome extension and is available in open beta with plans to implement a mobile app in 2023. After the open beta period ends, a pricing model will be applied.
