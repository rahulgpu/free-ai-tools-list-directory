About IntelliMail

IntelliMail is a Chrome extension that helps users generate emails in a fraction of the time it would take to write them manually. It is designed to save users time and effort while still providing them with an accurate and personalized email. The extension does not store any of the user's email data, preserving their privacy. Furthermore, IntelliMail offers users the opportunity to provide feedback to the developers and to support them with a 'Buy me a coffee' donation. IntelliMail is an easy to use, safe and secure Chrome extension for those who want to speed up their emailing process.
