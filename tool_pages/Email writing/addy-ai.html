About Addy AI

Addy AI is an AI-powered email assistant designed to boost efficiency and aid in managing and prioritizing emails by learning user preferences. The tool allows users to train it to sound like them, enhancing the personalization of emails. It has the ability to not only write emails quickly, but also mimics the unique style and tone of each user. It provides an organized approach towards email management by scanning, summarizing, and highlighting key details in the inbox. It brings attention to important tasks for the day, schedules events, and alerts about any follow ups - relieving the burden of constant email checking. Addy offers the feature to draft replies and follow-ups with minimal input required, creating a more streamlined email experience. The tool also enables users to go through email attachments such as Excel spreadsheets and text documents in a conversational manner. Users just have to ask Addy to analyze them. This, along with the tools ability to automatically highlight the main points, such as action items and deadlines in long emails spark considerable time savings. Addy's search features allow users to find specific emails in their inboxes using natural language requests. Finally, to eliminate language barriers, Addy is capable of writing in the language of the recipient. It supports integrations with email clients, making the import of emails into Addy straightforward.
