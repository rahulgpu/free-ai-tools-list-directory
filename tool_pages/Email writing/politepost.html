About Politepost

PolitePost.net is a powerful AI tool that can help you write professional, workplace appropriate emails in seconds. It works by taking a draft of your email written in any language or style and using AI to clean and refine the text, removing any slang or expletives. PolitePost.net is available as a Google Chrome browser extension and can be used with Gmail, Outlook, and other sites. To use the tool, simply write a quick draft of the email and click the ‘Fix my Email’ button. The AI then takes the email and sends it to OpenAI who process it and provide a cleaned version of the email to be sent. The AI-powered tool is economical, costing only ~$0.002 per email. The fees are used to keep the site running.
