About Scribbly

Scribbly is an AI-powered email writing tool designed specifically for busy professionals. The tool offers content suggestions that are context-specific, written professionally, and personalized to sound like the user. With Scribbly, users can write emails ten times faster than they would without the tool. This is made possible through the use of advanced AI technology that takes into account the user's writing style, communication patterns, and email content. The tool is available as a free Chrome extension that seamlessly integrates with Gmail. Once installed, users can simply compose emails as usual and Scribbly will suggest context-specific content to complete the email. Scribbly's AI technology ensures that the suggested content is highly relevant and matches the user's writing tone and style. Overall, Scribbly offers an efficient way to optimize email communication, saving time and improving productivity for busy professionals who need to correspond via email regularly.
