About Leeway

Leeway is an AI-powered tool that aims to assist individuals in finding legal solutions in the United States. By simply entering a description of their problem, users can access a list of potential solutions tailored to their specific situation. It is important to note that Leeway is a proof of concept and does not provide legal advice but offers general information relevant to the American legal system.To obtain relevant results, users are requested to provide their zipcode. This enables Leeway to narrow down the available options and present them with the best advice near their location. Additionally, Leeway offers a choice of three specialized lawyers who possess expertise in solving the specific problem at hand.The process of utilizing Leeway involves three steps. First, users must clearly identify and explain their problem in their own words, allowing for sufficient time to provide concise and accurate information. Secondly, entering their zipcode allows Leeway to source localized options. Lastly, users are presented with a selection of three specialized lawyers who can potentially address their unique situation.Leeway provides a user-friendly experience that can guide individuals towards potential solutions for their legal concerns. To ensure user privacy, it is recommended to review their privacy policy before engaging with the platform.
