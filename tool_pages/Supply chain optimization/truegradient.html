About TrueGradient

TrueGradient AI is an AI tool designed to unlock revenue growth by optimizing supply chain operations. The platform allows users to combine their business intuition with advanced AI capabilities in a self-serve manner. It offers several solutions tailored to specific business needs.One of the key features of TrueGradient AI is its demand forecasting capability. By analyzing historical data and relevant factors such as demographics and seasonality, the tool can predict future product demand accurately. This helps businesses plan their inventory levels and ensure that products are available when and where they are needed.Another solution provided by TrueGradient AI is pricing and promotions optimization. The tool enables businesses to determine the right price and promotional strategy for a given timeframe by predicting the net impact on sales. This can help maximize revenue while maintaining competitive pricing.Additionally, TrueGradient AI offers inventory optimization capabilities. By analyzing predicted demand, the tool helps businesses optimize inventory levels, ensuring that products are not overstocked or out of stock. This helps businesses meet customer demand efficiently while reducing costs associated with excess inventory.Lastly, the assortment optimization feature of TrueGradient AI allows businesses to optimize product assortments in stores based on various attributes, including product and store characteristics. It also takes into account the halo and cannibalization effects of products, ensuring an effective product mix that maximizes sales.Overall, TrueGradient AI provides businesses with valuable insights to enhance supply chain operations, optimize pricing and promotions, and improve inventory management, ultimately unlocking revenue growth opportunities.
