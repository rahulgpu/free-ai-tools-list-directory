About Floutwork

Floutwork is an all-in-one productivity application, constructed specifically to cater to the needs of freelancers. Devised to be a resourceful work system, it integrates various tools for task management, calendar management, email management, goal management, notes management and time tracking into a single desktop application. The app enables users to add tasks and reminders effortlessly, and to set and track goals automatically. Floutwork utilizes intelligent algorithms to find optimal times for task execution within busy schedules. Users can switch to 'Focus Mode', which minimizes distractions to promote focused work, while gaining quick access to their web applications from within Floutwork, thus further enhancing productivity. The app helps in email categorization to streamline inbox management and includes a Notes app for convenient note-taking and organization. A notable feature is its ability to track time spent on various web apps, eliminating the need for a separate time-tracking tool. Additionally, Floutwork promotes workplace gamification by keeping a record of focus, task execution, and work hours. Furthermore, in an attempt to maintain user privacy, the app ensures users emails and calendar data are retained only on their device and all user interactions within the app are securely encrypted.
