About Ema – Universal AI Employee

Ema is an Artificial Intelligence (AI) tool built to boost productivity across various roles within an organization. The AI can integrate with hundreds of different apps and interact with any form of data. Ema employs a proprietary Generative Workflow Engine to automate complex workflows through straightforward conversations. A distinct feature of Ema is the EmaFusion model, blending outcomes from large public language models and custom private models into a unified, highly accurate outcome. The AI aims to resolve the shortcomings of Gen AI, such as fragmentation, inadequate security measures, and unreliability. It offers data governance, abiding by international standards including SOC 2, ISO 27001, HIPAA, and GDPR, and ensures that sensitive data is redacted from audit logs. The EmaFusion model strengthens over time by continually adding new models to enhance precision. Ema stands as a conversational interface, enabling users to intuitively engage it to automate tasks across multiple business applications, maintaining efficiency and data security. It comes with standard personas for common roles such as Customer Support, Sales, HR, Data and the ability to create new specialised personas instantly.
