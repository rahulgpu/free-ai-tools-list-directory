About Hunch

Hunch is a no-code app that harnesses the power of the leading AI models, providing a platform to visually build workflows and improve both productivity and creativity. With its user-friendly interface, Hunch facilitates the chaining of AI tasks in a visual workspace, thus creating powerful and reusable workflows. In a paradigm shift towards making AI accessible and efficient, the app includes a range of pre-existing AI models from text and code such as OpenAI GPT-4 Turbo and Anthropic Claude 3, to image like OpenAI DALL-E 3. It also extends its reach to include other models such as transcription, and knowledge models like Perplexity Sonar Online. The intuitive nature of the Hunch platform also ensures that these AI tasks can be shared across teams and organizations, encouraging a more efficient and collaborative work environment. Importantly, Hunch gives the option to publish these workflows as easy-to-use tools for the team, helping the entire team leverage the power of AI to work smarter. The mission of Hunch is to make AI accessible to every user, seamlessly bridging the gap between intricate AI functionalities and daily tasks.
