About Chaturji

Chaturji is an AI-assisted platform aimed at simplifying the collaboration and sharing process in teams. It offers a unified interface to access a variety of world-renowned AI systems such as Claude 3, Opus, Haiku, Sonnet, GPT4, and Gemini Pro, amongst others, eliminating the need for switching screens. This tool facilitates productivity by allowing users to write, save, organise, and share prompts. It also encourages collaboration with private AI workspaces, where shared knowledge remains secure. Chaturji provides valuable analytics to monitor tool usage and optimize team's AI adoption, along with customizable per-user usage limits that control costs. An additional feature is an AI-powered analysis of various document types such as PDF, Excel, Word, and PPT, generating insights from these sources at users' fingertips. Through a curated prompt library, it further augments efficiency by providing predefined templates to jumpstart business processes. Chaturji places a strong emphasis on data privacy and security, ensuring that users can utilize AI's full potential in a secure environment.
