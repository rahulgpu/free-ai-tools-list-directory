About Kroolo

Kroolo is an AI-powered productivity management software designed for enhanced project, task, and goal management, along with document handling. The software leverages artificial intelligence to streamline operations, automate tasks, and boost productivity. Kroolo supports the creation and management of personal or team goals with aid from its AI system. It assists users in creating and handling documents, enabling conversations with documents or PDFs for streamlined task and process management. Kroolo also offers features to create and manage to-do lists, as well as projects with the help of AI, and manage team roles. It provides a platform for communication and collaboration through direct messages and channels. Its artificial intelligence element, known as Kroo AI, helps users spend less time on manual work and operate more efficiently. Kroolo integrates with various external applications and provides features such as chat, summarizing, and generation facilities. With a wide range of functionality, Kroolo seems poised to serve as a comprehensive workspace solution integrating tools for projects, tasks, document management, and team collaboration into one location. Notably, it's an adaptable tool, allowing users to customise its elements per their requirements using text or voice prompts. Furthermore, it supports the creation of project or goal templates, task management, and effective team management, facilitating collaboration and communication among team members.
