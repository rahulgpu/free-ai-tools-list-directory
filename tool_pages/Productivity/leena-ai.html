About Leena AI

Leena AI serves as an enterprise-level conversational AI platform designed to enhance the employee experience through automation and engagement. Its primary focus lies in the automation of HR helpdesk tasks and fostering improved employee engagement. The AI work assistant component within Leena AI streamlines knowledge management across systems, in addition to providing intelligent analytics, with the goal of increasing overall productivity and efficiency. The assistant integrates with an organisation's tech stack to perform complex tasks, and has abilities such as creating, accessing, and updating enterprise knowledge bases. It is equipped with a feature for managing information scattered across different systems and platforms, aimed at equipping teams in the fast-paced, information-driven environment. Furthermore, Leena AI can uncover patterns and trends providing data-driven insights to assist in informed decision-making and strategic initiatives, which potentially optimise operations and lead to better business outcomes. Leena AI also facilitates the automation of routine tasks, simplifying approvals, and taking action through seamless, enterprise-wide integrations. It offers potential applications across different business functions including HR initiatives, IT efficiency, and sales success among others.
