About CoPilot.Live

Fynd CoPilot is an AI-driven technology designed to augment productivity across multiple digital platforms. The tool is versatile, offering solutions to enhance web and app experiences, streamline design processes, promote efficient software development, and facilitate organized messaging on WhatsApp. Additionally, it proposes an all-in-one office suite for mobile devices along with task management capabilities. CoPilot integrates well with popular platforms such as OpenAI, PaLM2, and Boltic AutoML. The tool aims to reshape the way information is accessed and interacted with, thereby empowering businesses with swift, precise, and context-aware support. Its functionalities cater to various industrial verticals, offering tailored AI-powered assistance for enhanced performance. The tool integrates seamlessly with commonly used design and development tools, thereby maintaining a consistent and productive workflow. Unique attributes of Fynd CoPilot include its ability to generate, correct, and complete codes using Natural Language Processing, offer hyper-personalized assistance based on user preferences, and deliver data-driven insights for improved customer engagement. Its content creation feature simplifies the generation of diverse content types, while the design aspect promotes effective collaboration within teams and efficient design iteration.
