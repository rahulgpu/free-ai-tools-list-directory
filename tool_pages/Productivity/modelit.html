About Modelit

Modelit is a personal AI work assistant designed to increase productivity by facilitating co-writing, searching, and chatting in connection with a user's knowledge base. It interacts with user data from various apps to comprehend a companys ethos and assist in the generation of business documents. With a central design point of decluttering the user's space, Modelit helps locate necessary information across multiple apps.Through integrating with apps such as Google Drive, Notion, Confluence, JIRA, Github, Dropbox, Slack, Email, and Figma, it brings all the users knowledge into a uniform ecosystem. Users can upload their knowledge from different formats (PDFs, URLs, text files) and interact with AI assistants connected to a specific knowledge folder or subset. Modelit allows personalizing the AI system with knowledge, custom templates, workflows, and the user's tone of voice. This personalization feature helps build an AI that is as unique as the user, providing a tailored experience and automating daily tasks that suit the individual or company's needs.Handling user data securely is also a key attribute of Modelit. They follow industry-grade best practices for data security with a zero-retention data policy and ensuring data encryption both in transit and at rest. The tool has a range of pricing options available depending on user requirements.
