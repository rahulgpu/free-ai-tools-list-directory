About Rehance

Rehance is a tool that allows you to add AI assistants to your SaaS platforms. With this tool, users can provide instructions to your SaaS products in plain English and have the tasks completed for them. It provides a seamless integration with your existing SaaS applications and offers a refreshing alternative to traditional chatbots that can only answer queries or redirect users to support channels. Instead, Rehance can perform any task that a user can, including solving complex tasks and answering questions. In addition to enhancing user experience and productivity, it also provides insights into user behavior within your software, allowing you to make necessary improvements for better user satisfaction. A notable feature is its ability to unlock productivity data which can be a valuable asset for improving your SaaS offerings. Despite its advanced functionality, Rehance is easy to set up and deploy. Its founders aim to make AI practical in real software and to create a tool that adds value, similar to how self-driving technology has improved car functionality.
