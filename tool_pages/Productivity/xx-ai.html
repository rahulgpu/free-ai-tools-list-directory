About XX.AI

XX.AI is a comprehensive desktop AI tool designed to amplify productivity and creativity across various tasks. It integrates seamlessly across multiple apps and websites, providing universal support for writing, editing, and translation tasks powered by GPT-4 and Claude 3.5 AI models. It offers functionalities on-demand without any location restrictions, enhancing your writing and communication workflow regardless of where you are typing or highlighting text. It is designed for Windows and macOS, providing support for a plethora of apps and websites. Apart from its writing assistance, XX.AI excels in proofreading by identifying errors and refining the text, making it a viable tool for professionals striving for perfection in their written communication. It also provides brainstorming assistance by offering innovative ideas and solutions, thereby augmenting the creativity process. As an added advantage, the application has multilingual abilities, supporting over 200 languages. This allows users to draft questions and receive responses in their native language, thereby ensuring better localization of responses. It stands as a one-stop solution, providing features similar to various specialized tools yet being more affordable and versatile. The tool is easy-to-use with a simple activation system and interface.Lastly, it values its users and offers easy cancellation policies along with customer support for all user related queries and issues through email or Discord.
