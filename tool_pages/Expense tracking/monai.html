About MonAI

MonAi is an AI-powered expense tracker designed to streamline the tracking process of your financial expenses. The tool primarily works as a voice-activated system. You enter your expenses in a conversational manner, much like telling a friend about your spending, and the AI parses this into a concise summary, including a brief description, the spent amount, and the relevant category. Once the AI has categorized the expenses, users just need to confirm and save the information. An added convenient feature of MonAi is that it doesnt require a login, storing user data securely within the users private iCloud account. This, however, would necessitate the presence of an iCloud account on your system with iCloud-Drive enabled. The application also offers sharing and collaboration features, potentially useful for shared expenses or for those using it professionally. MonAi can be accessed via Apple App store. Please note the features and requirements may change over time, and it's always best to visit the app's official page for the latest information.
