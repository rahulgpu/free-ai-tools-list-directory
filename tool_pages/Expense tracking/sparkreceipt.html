About SparkReceipt

SparkReceipt is an AI-powered tool that simplifies the process of tracking and managing receipts and other business documents. With SparkReceipt, users can scan and digitize various types of documents, including receipts, invoices, bank statements, and more. The tool extracts key information such as merchant, date, total, and taxes automatically, eliminating the need for manual data entry. Additionally, SparkReceipt's AI categorizes expenses and income intelligently by reading the contents of the receipts, making it easier for users to track and manage their financials. The tool offers collaboration features, allowing team members to centralize scanned receipts under one account and invite others to collaborate on expenses. Users can also provide read-only access to their accountant for better financial oversight and more efficient pre-accounting.SparkReceipt offers cloud access, enabling users to access their scanned receipts from any device, whether through the web browser or the mobile app. It supports international usage with 150 currencies and accurate conversions between them, and it can read receipts in any language and dates & prices in any format. The tool also provides the option to forward e-receipts from email inboxes, which will be automatically read, extracted, and categorized using AI.The tool offers a free tier for light use cases, without any trial periods or lock-in. For those requiring multi-user features and powerful collaboration tools, there is a subscription option called SparkReceipt Pro.
