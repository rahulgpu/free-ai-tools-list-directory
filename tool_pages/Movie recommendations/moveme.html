About Moveme

Moveme.tv is an AI-driven tool that assists users in finding movies to suit their mood across a multitude of streaming services. The platform's intelligent AI learns user preferences to provide emotion-led recommendations. By capitalizing on the power of emojis and sentiments, moveme.tv offers a unique approach to movie recommendation. Users simply choose how they want to feel from a list of emotions presented as emojis, empowering the tool to suggest movies tailored to their desired mood. In addition, the tools AI technology goes beyond typical genre-based categorizations by learning what kind of movies the user loves, allowing for more personalized recommendations. moveme.tv also offers the ability to filter results by users streaming services. This means users can select their subscribed streaming platforms and the system will return only movies that are available on those platforms. Furthermore, the service prides itself in popping the filter bubble, providing a balance between user preferences and exposure to new content. With this, it can expose users to a wider range of cinematic options, including classic, indie, and foreign films. Usable across multiple devices, moveme.tv does not require any app download and is offered free of charge.
