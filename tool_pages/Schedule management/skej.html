About Skej

Skej is an AI scheduling assistant designed to handle scheduling tasks that would typically be done by a human assistant. It is intended to eliminate the need for manually coordinating any meeting schedules. Skej integrates with existing workflow systems and does not require any new software installations. It uses your existing calendar and email client right from the initiation. It offers meeting times based on the user's calendar and preferences, coordinating with contacts to find the perfect time to meet for both one-on-one and team meetings. With its ability to reschedule and cancel meetings with ease, the user simply informs Skej about any changes and the rest is taken care of. Skej also has a feature to manage Calendly links, checking for slots with mutual availability when received. It also works well with human assistants, recognizing who they represent. When scheduling between Skej users, the tool automatically books mutual time. Users can also set custom availabilities for different contacts. Skej handles time zones, keeping track of who is where, and making it easy to coordinate meetings across multiple time zones. It supports over 50 languages, enabling assistance in the user's native language. In essence, Skej aims to streamline scheduling tasks, reducing the need for manual intervention and aiming to make the process easier and more effective.
