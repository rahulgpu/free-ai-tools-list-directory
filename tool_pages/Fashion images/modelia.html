About Modelia

Modelia is an artificial intelligence-powered tool that offers a platform for visual content creation with customization options. It's primarily designed for transforming various types of fashion imagery. The primary function of the tool is converting flat lay clothing or ghost mannequin images into dynamic visuals of models wearing the attires. It also allows for a model-to-model transformation to match the aesthetics of different campaigns.In addition to these, the users can effortlessly adjust the age, ethnicity, and gender of their models. This feature enhances the inclusivity of fashion lines and can resonate with diverse markets and demographics. Modelia also offers the option to manipulate the facial expressions of models to match the mood and context of campaigns. The background of images can be customized to align with the theme or setting of marketing campaigns, further enhancing visual impact. It even supports back dressing to provide a full showcase of clothing from all angles. It covers a wide variety of clothes, with features such as logo, text, color preservation incorporated into the transformations. Future updates promise more types of clothes and additional refinements to image transformation features.This tool offers various subscription plans, from start-up to enterprise-level packages, with each offering unique benefits suited for distinct users. Also accessible is a custom plan for non-standard requirements, and an on-demand AI image-generation service for those needing dedicated, specific attribute fulfillment.
