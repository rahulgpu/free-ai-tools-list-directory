About Madgicx

Madgicx is a cloud-based tool primarily aimed at Ecom brands and agencies. It provides an integrated platform to manage and optimize Facebook and Google ads to boost ad return on investment. The tool comprises several features tailored towards streamlining the process of ad creation and management. Madgicx's AI Marketer acts as a personal AI media buyer, providing insights and highlighting actions you need to take based on your current account data for result optimization. To improve Facebook ad attribution reporting, the tool includes a tracking feature that helps users gain more confidence and clarity about their campaign performance. For autonomous ad management to beat competition, the platform offers features including budget optimization across all areas of an ad account and ad account management automation. A Custom Automation feature is also included which helps streamline every ad-buying workflow. Creatives constantly generate converting ads in a seamless workflow, facilitated by an ad library and AI-based creative insights. It provides detailed insights to learn how your ad copy resonates with your audience, coupled with an AI copywriter that aids in composing effective ad copy. The Targeting feature helps identify the most profitable audiences on Facebook and Google for more focused ad campaigns. It includes tools for audience insights, ad launching, bid testing, and account management. To stay updated with ad campaign performance, it provides automated reporting, data performance segmentation, and a collaborative platform. Additionally, a feature called One-Click Report brings instant multi-channel reporting to users. Beyond these, Madgicx comprises several other features aimed at enriching ad experience and campaign effectiveness.
