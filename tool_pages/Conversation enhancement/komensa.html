About Komensa

Komensa is an Artificial Intelligence powered tool that aids in improving communication. Utilizing the principles of Nonviolent Communication (NVC), a globally adopted communication framework, Komensa systematically transforms rough, confrontational communication into constructive, non-confrontational messages. The tool works in a step-by-step process. The user first writes their message as they normally would. The tool's AI algorithms then analyse and transform the message into a more constructive and effective format upon the click of the 'magic button'. The refined message is then ready to be reviewed and sent to the recipient. Komensa also offers multi-language support, allowing users to express in their preferred language which is then translated into the language in which it was written. Assuring user's security and privacy, Komensa safeguards conversations, protecting personal information and preserving communication history. This tool, thus, helps in smooth navigation through difficult conversations, potentially resulting in more effective conflict resolution and stronger relationship building.
