About Pebble

PebblePage is an AI-based tool designed to support and enhance the learning process. It emphasizes on transforming learning from a daunting task into a series of smaller, achievable steps, hence the name 'Pebble'. In essence, it leverages Artificial Intelligence to create a personalized learning path, thereby enabling learners to progress at their own pace. Its key functionality lies in breaking down complex learning materials into smaller, manageable units, which can be understood and assimilated more easily by the learner. It also provides feedback and support throughout the learning journey to ensure the effectiveness of the learning process. The tool aims to create a seamless, integrated learning experience that aligns with individual learning styles and capabilities. Ideal for both students and professionals, PebblePage is all about simplifying learning and adapting it to the learner's pace and preference. Its adaptability and personalized approach makes it a flexible educational tool for a variety of learning environments.
