About Similarix

Similarix is an AI-powered tool designed to optimize the management and organization of digital assets stored in S3 buckets. By adding a 'thin AI layer' to your storage, it enhances search, organization, and management capabilities. Similarix facilitates efficient and intelligent management of digital assets by making your files searchable and more accessible using advanced algorithms. Users can locate any file instantly due to its semantic search feature which understands the context of your images and delivers highly relevant search results. It also identifies and manages duplicate assets thereby ensuring efficient storage organization. With Similarix, one can also find visually similar images with its image-based search feature. Offering multilingual support in 133 languages, it caters to a global audience guaranteeing seamless asset management. It incorporates API availability allowing Similarix to integrate smoothly into existing systems and workflows. Similarix operates with read-only access, safeguarding the integrity and safety of your stored files. The company also provides independent AI models and enterprises solutions that offer custom-fit SLAs and additional configuration options for large-scale operations and extensive redistribution rights.
