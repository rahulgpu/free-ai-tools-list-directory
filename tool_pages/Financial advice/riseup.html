About Riseup

For UK only - RiseUp Money Insights is a new AI engine from the financial wellbeing app RiseUp, which gives you free personalised money insights and saving tips based on your past spending. What do you spend the most money on? Where can you save? Money insights and tips tailored specifically to YOU, which can improve your money management, make it easier to save and enhance your financial wellbeing. Simply connect your bank account with the most transactions and wait to receive your tailored money insights and savings hacks that you can put into practice today. We use Truelayer to connect to your accounts securely through open banking.
