About Fina

Fina is a finance management tool that provides users with the ability to track and analyze their finances according to their preferred methods. With Fina, users can create custom calculations tailored to their specific needs or rely on AI to answer any financial question and track any metric. By leveraging the power of AI, Fina enables users to make informed decisions about their financial futures.One of the key features of Fina is its flexibility. Users have the ability to create custom calculations that are perfectly suited to their particular financial goals and needs. Fina also leverages AI-based analytics that can provide users with insights and recommendations for optimizing their financial strategies.The app's user interface is designed to be intuitive and easy to navigate, making it accessible to both novice and experienced users. Additionally, Fina is a web-based application that requires javascript to function properly.With Fina, users can gain a comprehensive understanding of their current financial situation, identify areas for improvement, and develop personalized strategies to achieve their financial goals. Through its combination of customizability, AI-powered analytics, and user-friendly design, Fina provides a powerful tool for transforming your financial future.
