About FinanceGPT Chat

FinanceGPT Chat is a tool designed to allow users to build custom AI co-pilots for personalized financial insights and market analysis. These AI co-pilots are designed to understand unique investment goals, risk tolerance, and market preferences of the user. Functions include generating and writing reports to help the system comprehend the user's financial landscape and provide personalized insights. Users can interact with their AI co-pilots by posing questions about market trends, investment opportunities, and financial strategies; the AI co-pilot analyzes these queries and provides tailored insights. These co-pilots are intended to help users make informed financial decisions. FinanceGPT Chat is also equipped to allow creation of custom co-pilots with different specialties such as investment, risk management, financial analysis, management consulting, investment banking and financial law. The tool employs the advanced technology of Retrieval Augmented Generation (RAG) to deliver precise insights. RAG works by retrieving relevant documents based on user queries, then generating responses influenced by the retrieved information. This ensures the AI co-pilot's responses are tailored to the specific financial problem the user is intending to solve.
