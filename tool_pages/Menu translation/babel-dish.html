About Babel Dish

Babel Dish is an AI-driven tool designated for helping users understand foreign menus. The tool allows you to take a photo of any foreign menu, and it will then translate each dish into your native language. This tool makes it simple and easy to understand what ingredients make up unfamiliar dishes. To use the tool, you need to capture a photo of the menu using a smartphone, or upload an existing photo, and then tap on the highlighted text that you want translated. The tool swiftly analyzes the picture, recognizes the dish names and translates them, helping you make informed decisions about what to eat. Babel Dish aids in removing language barriers that can inhibit culinary exploration. The tool is designed to be user-friendly and can be installed on mobile devices. It is aimed at individuals who travel frequently, enjoy trying new foods, or live in multicultural areas where menus may be written in different languages. It puts an end to menu confusion and opens up a world of international cuisines to the user.
