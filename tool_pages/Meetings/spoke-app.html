About Spoke App

Spoke is an AI-powered tool that summarizes video meetings with transcripts, video clips, and AI-generated summaries. It integrates with 30+ other apps, including Slack and CRM. With Spoke, users can capture key insights from each meeting in a sortable, shareable format. Additionally, Spoke features hands-free note-taking and real-time agenda tracking for greater efficiency and productivity. It also offers tailored AI meeting summaries and insights, streamlining communication. Spoke is free for individual users, with pricing for teams and integrations. The tool works in over 100 languages and directly integrates with Zoom, Microsoft Teams, and Google Meet. Users can personalize their meeting companion with a name and image that reflects their brand. Privacy and data security are prioritized with Spoke, utilizing AWS facilities with certifications for ISO 27001, PCI DSS Service Provider Level 1, and SOC 1 and 2 compliance. Spoke adheres to coding standards based on the OWASP Top Ten, utilizing NodeJS for front-end development and Rust for back-end design. All connections to Spoke are encrypted using SSL, and any attempt to connect over HTTP is declined. Lastly, Spoke offers Single Sign-On to streamline authentication and a vulnerability bounty program to address any privacy or security concerns.
