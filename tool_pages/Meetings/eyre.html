About Eyre

Eyre is an all-in-one AI-powered platform designed to facilitate and enhance collaborative meetings. It incorporates a virtual whiteboard interface where teams can brainstorm ideas, visualise concepts, assign tasks, and track progress in real-time. One of Eyre's key features is its AI assistant, Jane, which is powered by GPT-4 and can create a whiteboard template for the meeting. This helps users easily share and access meeting materials, organize ideas, and visualize concepts. The platform also allows for interactive meetings with functionalities like sketching diagrams, annotating, and adding sticky notes to the digital canvas. Task management can be integrated within the meeting environment, enabling users to create action items directly from the whiteboard, assign tasks, set deadlines and track progress. The platform also has the capability to synchronize with other task management tools for seamless workflow integration. Users also have the option to save and revisit whiteboard sessions, and capture important insights and action items for future reference. Eyre is ideal for creating a personalized meeting room experience, with secure content distribution and assignment of granular permissions. Another powerful feature is its ability to generate highly visual meeting content, including meeting agendas, summaries, files, and tasks.
