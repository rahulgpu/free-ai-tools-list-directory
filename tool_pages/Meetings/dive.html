About Dive

Dive is an AI-driven tool designed to improve the productivity and efficiency of meetings. Utilizing advanced AI, Dive automates several aspects of the meeting process. With a key focus on automation, Dive builds meeting agendas, takes comprehensive meeting minutes, and sends them out to relevant participants. It caters to different use-cases including meetings for team leads, one-on-one sessions, marketing teams, town halls, and agencies. The automation extends to meeting preparation, recording and transcription, generating meeting summaries, and creating action items. It also allows users to track action items for follow-ups. Dive seamlessly integrates with various popular platforms like Google Meet, Zoom, Slack, Google Calendar, and Google Docs. In addition, it provides a range of tools and features to streamline the meeting process, such as automated note-taking, AI-generated summaries and action items, and the easy tracking of progress. Dive's capabilities enhance user focus on discussions and allow for efficient capturing of key insights from meetings.
