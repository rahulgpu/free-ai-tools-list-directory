About Chadview

ChadView is an AI-powered tool designed to assist users during technical interviews. It employs ChatGPT for real-time meeting assistance, providing instant, accurate responses to queries on programming, analytics, and mathematics. The tool can be utilized with different online meeting platforms, including Zoom, Google Meet, and Microsoft Teams, provided that the calls are conducted within the browser. Users interact with ChadView through a Chrome extension, which captures audio directly from your browser during the call. By pressing the 'Ask' button, the extension reviews the previous 30 seconds of conversation, identifies the question asked, and generates a suitable response within seconds. This feature saves time for users, as they do not have to manually type out or research responses. The answers generated by ChadView are varied and cater to positions such as developers, analysts, and similar technical roles. Furthermore, ChadView supports a range of languages, including English, Spanish, Portuguese, Ukrainian, Russian, and Chinese. Subscription to the service provides unlimited monthly access. The tool welcomes community engagement and feedback, with a dedicated Discord server for customer support and feature requests.
