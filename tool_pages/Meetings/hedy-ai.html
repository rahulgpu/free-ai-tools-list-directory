About Hedy AI

Hedy AI is an AI-powered tool designed to assist users in their business meetings and educational classes. The tool operates in real time, providing customised insights and recommendations to facilitate enhanced performance in these interactions. Hedy's main functionalities include real-time assistance, interactive insights and the generation of transcripts and summaries. In operation, the user can start a meeting or class in the usual way, and Hedy will listen and analyze the unfolding conversation. A user may set a 'perspective filter' to ensure the insights provided are specifically tailored to their context. In response to this real-time analysis, Hedy delivers thought-starters, insights, and recommendations via an unobtrusive interface. A user can interactively ask Hedy questions during meetings for immediate, relevant responses to help navigate complex topics. Additionally, after the meeting, Hedy offers the user the ability to review and reflect upon the identified insights and takeaways which can serve to inform future discussions. Hedy also creates an automatic summary of the meeting, highlighting key points, action items and generating transcripts. This tool serves to support users as they navigate their professional and educational interactions, providing timely and invaluable assistance in real-time.
