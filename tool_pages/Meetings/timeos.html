About TimeOS

timeOS is a Time Management AI tool designed to enhance your time management skills and productivity. It focuses on automating meeting-related tasks such as note-taking and scheduling. Compatible with popular meeting platforms like Zoom, Google Meet, and Microsoft Teams, timeOS seamlessly integrates with your calendar to generate accurate and comprehensive meeting notes and summaries.One of its key features is the ability to capture meeting notes, follow up on action items, and delegate tasks using artificial intelligence. This not only saves time but also ensures that important action items are not overlooked. timeOS also assists in writing follow-up emails, drafting them in a way that is personalized and aligned with your writing style.The tool offers multi-language support, making it suitable for users who speak different languages. It can integrate with various productivity tools such as Notion, ClickUp, Asana, and Slack, allowing for easy synchronization of action items and tasks.timeOS prioritizes user data privacy and security. It encrypts data during transit and utilizes secure hosting on AWS. The tool does not train its AI or OpenAI with meeting content and ensures that customer data is protected. Users have control over their data and can request its deletion at any time.Highly praised by professionals and teams, timeOS is praised for its ability to improve meeting efficiency and productivity. By automating note-taking and follow-up tasks, it allows users to be fully present in meetings and saves hours each week.timeOS is available for free, with the option to add it to your browser for convenient access.
