About Neuralangelo by Nvidia

Neuralangelo is an innovative Artificial Intelligence (AI) model developed by NVIDIA research that is primarily designed for 3D reconstruction using neural networks. The main functionality of Neuralangelo lies in its ability to convert 2D video clips into detailed 3D structures, thereby creating highly realistic virtual replicas of real-world objects such as buildings, sculptures, etc. The intricate details and textures of these 3D structures mimic the precision of lifelike visions carved from marble blocks. Creative professionals can import these 3D objects into various design applications and further edit them for diverse use cases in art, video game development, robotics, and industrial digital twins. One of the significant strengths of Neuralangelo is its capability to translate textures of complex materials from 2D videos to 3D assets, outperforming traditional methods. Thus, Neuralangelo makes it easier for developers and creative professionals to quickly create usable virtual objects for their projects using footage captured by smartphones. Furthermore, Neuralangelo can also recreate both interior and exterior building structures, potentially transforming the way creators recreate real-world experiences in digital realms.
