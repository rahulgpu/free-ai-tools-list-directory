About Lugs

Lugs.ai is an AI tool that allows users to accurately caption and transcribe all audio on their computer and microphone. This tool does not require an internet connection, ensuring privacy and eliminating the need for streaming data to the cloud. Lugs.ai is available for download on MacOS and other platforms.Built by the hearing impaired, Lugs.ai aims to provide the most accurate results by deeply understanding the context of conversations, allowing it to adapt to the dialogue and deliver unmatched accuracy. The tool is constantly improved based on real experiences rather than perceived ones.With Lugs.ai, users can seamlessly generate live captions for their conversations. The tool offers best-in-class accuracy and provides lifetime updates for continuous improvement. Simply plugging in a microphone enables Lugs.ai to work effortlessly, making it user-friendly and convenient.By offering offline functionality, Lugs.ai ensures that users never miss out on important conversations. Its ability to transcribe audio quickly and accurately directly on the device eliminates the reliance on an internet connection.In summary, Lugs.ai is an AI tool that enables users to accurately caption and transcribe audio on their computer and microphone. It prioritizes privacy, does not require an internet connection, and is constantly improved based on real-world use cases.
