About Drippi.ai

Drippi.ai is an AI-powered tool for automating highly personalized Twitter DM outreach messages and managing your inbox with filters and auto-replies. Drippi uses ChatGPT, an AI algorithm, to analyze each lead's profile and create personalized messages for higher reply rates and better engagement. The tool also provides comprehensive analytics, sorting lead responses and tracking reply rates to improve campaigns and increase ROI. Drippi offers powerful lead discovery solutions, allowing you to find leads that match your target audience's occupation, industry, and interests to engage more and search less. The AI message generator can personalize messages based on Twitter profile information, recent tweets, and website data, making them highly relevant and engaging for your target audience.Drippi also offers powerful inbox management tools, such as filters, auto-replies, and quick responses, for streamlined communication. The tool provides detailed insights into the performance of your Twitter DM campaigns, allowing data-driven decisions to improve campaigns and increase ROI. Drippi supports up to three Twitter accounts in the Pro plan, and users can choose to sign up for private training. The pricing is simple and transparent, ranging from a free basic plan with up to 50 messages per day to custom pricing for unlimited Twitter accounts with over 12,000 credits per month. Overall, Drippi.ai is a useful tool for automating personalized Twitter DM outreach messages, managing your inbox, and improving your Twitter DM campaigns' performance.
