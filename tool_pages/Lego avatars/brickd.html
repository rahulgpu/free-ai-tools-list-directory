About Brick’d

Brick'd by Reez Avatar is an online tool specifically designed for generating custom LEGO minifigure avatars from any photo. The user simply uploads a photograph of their choice and, within minutes, the tool transforms it into a stylized representation akin to a LEGO figurine. Besides individual use, its wide application range makes it appealing to a variety of users like designers looking for unique avatars or illustrators seeking distinctive graphic elements. The tool also features a gallery for users to view creations generated by others and draw inspiration. Overall, Brick'd by Reez Avatar offers an efficient, innovative and enjoyable way to create personal and stylish LEGO figurine avatars.
