About ContractPower

ContractPower is an AI-powered platform designed to simplify the understanding and management of renewable energy contracts including PPAs (Power Purchase Agreements), LGIAs (Large Generator Interconnection Agreements) and more. It specifically helps IPPs (Independent Power Producers), utilities, financial institutions, and corporate buyers to navigate complex contractual language. The platform provides a contract-specific approach and addresses industry nuances, enabling efficient document-specific summaries and compliance extraction. In addition to that, ContractPower is tuned to identify and track compliance items, thereby reducing the liability in contract review and increasing the accuracy of understanding complex topics and clauses. The tool also employs semantic search capabilities to isolate relevant sections of contracts, saving time in contract review. It offers thought models designed for renewable energy's needs, crafting AI agents to specifically identify compliance items, risks, and ISO (Independent System Operator) costs for commercially relevant information. The technology is available as a service or via a SaaS (Software as a Service) platform.
