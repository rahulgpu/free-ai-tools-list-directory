About Xinva

Xinva is an AI tool aimed at designers, freelancers, editors and business owners who want to easily generate innovative and stylish designs for print-on-demand stores. Powered by machine learning and artificial intelligence technology, Xinva's designer tool can create customized designs in moments, boosting productivity and saving on designing costs. The tool offers a range of AI generators, including T-shirt design, sticker design, furry art, and pattern design, and uses prompts and user input to generate visual designs. Xinva can also send users well-researched prompts to their inbox every week. Users can easily download high-resolution versions of the generated designs for use in their print-on-demand business. Xinva markets itself as a cost-effective and efficient way to engage customers and increase sales and revenue. The tool provides an auto-background remover, and generates super high-resolution designs with no credit card required. Xinva claims to be used by popular print-on-demand brands such as Redbubble, Printify, Printful, Teelaunch, Merch by Amazon, and Customcat. The tool offers a range of pricing plans to suit different business types, and a free trial to allow users to test the tool's capabilities. In summary, Xinva is an AI-based designer tool that utilizes machine learning to generate innovative and stylish designs for print-on-demand businesses, allowing users to boost productivity and save designing costs. The tool is available in a range of pricing plans and aims to improve engagement with customers and increase sales and revenue.
