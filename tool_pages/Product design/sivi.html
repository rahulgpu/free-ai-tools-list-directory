About Sivi

Sivi is a generative AI tool that enables users to quickly create visually engaging designs for their ad campaigns, websites, banners, email headers, social posts and more. Using natural language processing and AI algorithms, Sivi is capable of generating unique designs from text content within a minute or two. It also provides infinite variations for each design, allowing for greater flexibility and creativity. Additionally, Sivi has been recognized by NASSCOM 10K Startups, Top 5 NTLF Super Startups, KTech Elevate Winner, and TiE Women Bangalore Finalist. It provides a free version with 30 free requests, and users can start creating their designs in just a few clicks. With Sivi, users can compose their own layouts for any custom content and create design options for their brand in a matter of minutes, making it the perfect tool for businesses looking to save time and money.
