About Prism

Prism is a generative AI platform designed to enhance the creative process for designers. It offers a visual control interface where designers partner with the AI to design and animate in real-time. The powerful features it boasts allow swift iterations, with the capacity to guide the AIs generation of designs using animation as a medium. The collaboration is not one-sided, with the AI also working in tandem to reduce the waiting time, interpreting instructions precisely as delivered by the designer. Prism grants the leverage to experiment with different styles, moods, and prompts through a feature known as 'Lenses'. The Lenses retain the designer's control over the AI and facilitate fast and creative iterations without tampering with the original work.Moreover, Prism establishes itself as an all-inclusive creative studio, catering to all stages of the creative process from inception to completion. The tools capabilities extend to motion design systems for larger creative organizations seeking control, cohesion, and consistency across teams. The platform empowers its users to complete meaningful work by ensuring an intuitive, polished, and unmatched creative workflow.
