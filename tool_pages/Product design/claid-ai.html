About Claid.ai

Claid.ai is an AI tool focused on improving product photography to enhance commerce experiences. The tool can generate product photos, remove the background, and provide image enhancement and editing capabilities. It's designed to empower businesses by transforming basic product images into photoshoot-quality visuals, creating an engaging experience for consumers. A notable characteristic is its ability to create consistent product images, achieved through the use of standardised backgrounds and frames in sync with brand aesthetics. Through its robust API, Claid.ai enables instant access to the edited images, contributing to a smooth content creation process. It offers several image enhancement features such as Smart Frame for background removal, AI Color Correction for vibrant images, and Upscaling for high-resolution visuals. Users have reported improved image perception, increased conversion rates, and higher purchasing intent associated with its enhanced images. Its API is easily integratable and can automate the improvement of image quality, making it a powerful tool in managing visual content for businesses.
