About BlazeSQL

BlazeSQL is an AI-powered tool that helps you turn questions into data insights from your SQL database. It writes SQL code based on your input, runs the queries, and visualizes the results. BlazeSQL is for anyone in a company who needs data insights, whether they're technical or non-technical Ideal for teams of 10+ users, including product managers, marketing, sales, and operations staff, users can comfortably interact with this AI chatbot without needing any codes or technical skills.
