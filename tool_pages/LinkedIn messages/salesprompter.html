About SalesPrompter

SalesPrompter is a chrome extension that leverages OpenAI's GPT-4 model to automate the process of writing hyper-personalized LinkedIn messages. It is designed for frequent LinkedIn users across various professional roles and industries to enhance networking efficiency.With SalesPrompter, users can benefit from several features to amplify their outreach efforts. The tool offers automated messaging to save time drafting messages and allow users to focus on building valuable professional relationships. Additionally, it provides AI-driven personalization, tailoring outreach messages based on each recipient's job title and professional background. It also generates smart connection request notes or messages based on the degree of connection, enhancing the first point of contact.SalesPrompter maximizes networking potential through its seamless integration with LinkedIn profiles, providing a user-friendly experience. Users have full control over AI-generated messages with an editable field for customizations. The tool also supports multi-lingual messaging, breaking language barriers by detecting and using the recipient's preferred language.The advanced features of SalesPrompter include adaptive functionality to adjust to changes in LinkedIn's layout, ensuring a smooth user experience. It utilizes AI to quickly analyze a person's career trajectory, current role, and potential value to the user's network. The tool crafts effective follow-up messages based on initial responses to maintain conversation momentum. It also offers flexible subscription management directly from the browser popup, aligning with users' networking needs.SalesPrompter enables users to join the AI revolution in professional networking, providing a free trial to experience its power in supercharging outreach strategies, fostering meaningful professional relationships, and saving valuable time.
