About Spinify

Spinify is an AI-powered sales gamification platform that aims to make sales performance management engaging and transparent. It is designed to give teams visibility into sales data, drive performance, and foster a positive company culture through gamified competition. The platform creates an environment where performance is visible, and achievements are recognised to boost team morale. It also offers a range of features including leaderboards, competitions and visibility, recognition and fun, metrics and targets, coaching, and multiple device functions. Spinify can be used across various departments and industries, with a special focus on remote teams. Additionally, it comprises an AI entity, SpinifyGPT - Sidekick AI, built to aid the product features. By leveraging data-rich insights, the platform helps organizations in making informed decisions and tracking their Key Performance Indicators (KPI) effectively. Furthermore, their gamification strategies transform sales targets into achievements, quotas into conquests, and challenges into opportunities leading to a dynamic and enjoyable journey.
