About MobileGPT

MobileGPT is an AI assistant that operates through WhatsApp. Implementing OpenAI's GPT technology, this tool offers various functions to the user in one integrated service. Notably, users can interact with MobileGPT in a conversational manner to gather information, conduct translations, solve mathematical problems, and even generate content. This service also encompasses language processing capabilities and the ability to write code. MobileGPT's features include the creation of AI images and documents. Using the Stable Diffusion XL 1.0 model, the chatbot can generate photorealistic images based on the prompts given. Users can benefit from an AI teaching assistance function that offers lessons on a range of subjects, paired with practical examples and quizzes. The chatbot extends its capabilities to understand and analyze images with the help of the GPT4 Vision Model. Using this tool, items in an uploaded image can be identified and described. Another significant feature of MobileGPT is the ability to generate a range of document types. The AI assistant can create documents like business plans or resumes based on the user's input. Lastly, MobileGPT provides a note-taking and reminder system, where users can save important information or set reminders.
