About WizAI

WizAI is a chatbot tool designed to generate human-like smart replies on any topic directly in WhatsApp. With natural language processing and a vast knowledge base, it engages in back-and-forth dialogue, making it easy for users to ask questions, get information, or chat. WizAI works with ChatGPT and understands both text and audio messages. The tool can be accessed by sending a simple text message, and users can access all ChatGPT features right in WhatsApp. WizAI has been trusted by thousands of people worldwide, and their free version allows users to try things out with up to five messages using ChatGPT 3.5. The tool's pricing is transparent, with a monthly fee starting from $2, which allows users to enjoy unlimited messages with the flexibility to cancel anytime. WizAI is still working on an entirely free ad-based free tier that they hope to implement soon. The tool helps businesses and individuals to provide efficient customer support, minimize response time, and improve user engagement in their WhatsApp accounts. WizAI is available for use worldwide, and users can access support and reach the team through the company's Twitter account.
