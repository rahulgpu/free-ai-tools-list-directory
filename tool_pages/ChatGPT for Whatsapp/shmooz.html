About Shmooz

Shmooz AI stands as an AI assistant, developed with the aim to provide support in the execution of your everyday tasks. It offers an array of functionalities that cater to a diverse set of needs. Key features are content creation and email writing, giving users an edge in efficiently managing their day-to-day written communications. Built on top-of-the-line GPT tech, Shmooz AI boasts a broad spectrum of AI capabilities. It operates with web access functionality in tandem with Google and possesses image creation abilities in conjunction with Midjourney. Notably, Shmooz AI also incorporates advanced listening features. These enable users to send voice notes, providing an alternate, convenient mode of communication. Moreover, its capability to summarize articles adds value for users seeking quick information extraction from large textual data. The underlying objective of Shmooz AI is to streamline your routines, enhance productivity and offer assistance in keeping tasks organized. With its suite of features, this tool rightfully positions itself as a comprehensive AI-based utility that can effectively simplify everyday tasks.
