About WhisperBot

WhisperBot is an AI-powered transcription tool that turns WhatsApp voice messages into text. It works directly within WhatsApp and requires no additional installations, making the experience seamless. WhisperBot leverages OpenAI technology to transcribe messages accurately and quickly, supporting over 57 languages. Once the transcription is sent back to the user, the audio file (voice message) and its text content are erased from the database for data security reasons. WhisperBot's AI powers allow users to request key takeaways from long voice messages, making it easier to understand the most important points. The transcription tool has a high level of accuracy, ensuring that at least 95% of the voice message is accurately transcribed. WhisperBot is a one-time payment tool with no subscription required, though users can also subscribe for its additional features.
