About Glyphic

Glyphic is an AI-enabled tool designed to assist sales teams in customer interactions, sales call preparations, and deal insights. It serves as a copilot for sales teams, aiding in streamlining sales processes by automating routine tasks, thereby allowing sales teams to focus on the core aspects of their roles. Glyphic's power lies in its ability to analyze customer interactions, providing valuable insights into sales pipelines that can help businesses shape their revenue strategies and improve conversion rates. It offers automated lead qualifications, automatically syncing all key points and insights from sales calls and deals with your CRM system. In addition, Glyphic aids in call preparation by surfacing relevant data and suggested talking points before a call, drawing from its strong partnership with databases like Crunchbase. The tool also provides resources for effective follow-ups post-call, using key points and agreed next steps to generate personalized emails. Another main feature of the platform is its ability to process unstructured data from customer interactions across all channels and repurpose this into digestible, actionable insights, including suggestions for next steps. This allows sales teams to stay on top of their deals and calls while having immediate access to essential information.
