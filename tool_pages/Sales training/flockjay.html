About Flockjay

Flockjay is a unified Learning and Content Management System catered towards sales enablement needs. Its core functions include managing sales team's content, facilitating their learning, offering deal insights, and enabling real-time coaching, powered by Generative AI. Flockjay's AI-driven content management feature enables rapid semantic search, content tagging, and optimization. The platform has been designed to provide an intuitive user interface, automation, and peer learning functionalities to scale your training efforts. It fosters real-time, automated coaching for every member of the sales team, and every deal they are working on. The system assists in bringing the right enablement to the suited representative at the right time to help close the deal. Furthermore, Flockjay aims to do more with fewer resources, scoring calls, grading sales pitches, and surfacing the most relevant collateral swiftly. The system is customizable for teams of every size and is utilized to build effective, future-ready sales teams.
