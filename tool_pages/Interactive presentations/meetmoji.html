About MeetMoji

MeetMoji is an AI-powered tool designed to simplify and enhance presentations by making them more engaging and interactive. The tool offers several features that aid in audience inclusion and active engagement, converting typical presentations into interactive experiences.MeetMoji's wide array of features include the ability to launch word clouds, get open feedback, conduct live Q&A sessions, and conduct live polls. Concurrently, users can embed web applications into their presentations, share their content, and livestream emojis reflecting audience reactions and sentiments.AI-powered sentiment analysis is another significant aspect of MeetMoji that helps users in gaining insights about audience responses across categories, understanding overall moods and efficiently evaluating open feedback.Users can also generate engaging polls and open-ended questions for their audience, thereby fostering active participation and entertainment.MeetMoji's seamless integration capability, allowing combination of users' existing slides with audience engagement elements overlaying on top of the slides, innovatively transforms the presentations and eliminates disruptive transitions.Suitable for both online and in-person use, this tool can be utilized to bring life and interactive experiences to hybrid meetings, thereby combatting issues such as shorter attention spans and lack of audience engagement during presentations.
