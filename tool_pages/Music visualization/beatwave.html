About Beatwave

Beatwave is an online tool designed to assist musicians, beatmakers, and artists in creating audio and music visualizers for their tracks, beats, audios, and podcasts. It aids in transforming these into interactive videos that can help in growing listener base, although its compatibility with social media platforms makes it easier to publish the content directly. The Beatwave allows users to choose a template that suits their aesthetic, upload their tracks, and personalize the resultant visualizer as per their preferences. Furthermore, it offers a variety of professional video templates, which are regularly updated to give a fresh look to the videos. Users can fully customize the visualizers appearance, effects, colours, and other elements. Beatwave also offers a service of publishing the music videos directly from its interface to any social media platform. The tool is also mobile optimized, so creating music videos can be done anywhere, anytime.
