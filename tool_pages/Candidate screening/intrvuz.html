About Intrvuz

Intrvuz is an AI-powered platform specifically designed to streamline the process of hiring. The tool employs advanced AI technology to enable efficient screening of resumes in bulk. The core competency of the tool lies in its ability to match the details in resumes with job descriptions precisely, reducing manual efforts of filtering through multiple CVs. Intrvuz is exceptionally beneficial for HR professionals, helping to unlock the power of Contextual AI for candidate screening. It features a CV screening capability driven by its AI engine, designed to eliminate the hassle of manual screening and ensuring only the best-qualified CVs are engaged with by companies. The AI also analyzes job descriptions when they're uploaded, performing an in-depth analysis to extract valuable insights and benchmark against industry standards. This feature helps to provide clarity on hiring requirements and ensures a perfect fit for open positions. This tool envisages transforming the hiring landscape, streamlining candidate screening, elevating decision-making, and enabling organizations to make exceptional hires with ease.
