About PoweredBy

AI Powered Candidate Screening is a cutting-edge platform, primarily intended for enterprises, designed to streamline the process of candidate assessment. It gauges the relevance between skills of a candidate as per his/her resume and the requirements specified in a job description. The tool is integrated into an Applicant Tracking System (ATS) to provide a seamless experience. Whether it's for job seekers or employers, the technology provides a simplified and efficient approach to analyze compatibility. It uses a proprietary model, that's being enhanced continually, and it's API can be integrated in a few easy steps. This technology stands out in the industry by using advanced screening methods, which help find the right job match for candidates and ensure employers make impactful hiring decisions. The uniqueness of this tool lies in its use of artificial intelligence for candidate screening which reduces manual effort, eliminates bias and significantly speeds up the recruitment process.
