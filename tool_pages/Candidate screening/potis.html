About Potis

Potis.AI is an AI-powered tool designed to optimize the hiring process by automating candidate screening. The platform evaluates candidates' real-world skills through automated behavioural interviews, using actual situations to gain insights into how a candidate would handle certain scenarios. Its unique approach makes it difficult for candidates to prepare for the interview, thus aiming to offer a clearer view of their actual abilities. Beyond automating interviews, Potis.AI also provides clear and bias-free scoring of candidates, offering detailed insights into their strengths and weaknesses, and highlights the best fit candidates for a job. The tool also utilizes a 'clear scores' feature, providing a comprehensive rating of all candidates, including a detailed breakdown per candidate, indicating their skill tree and strengths or weaknesses. Potis.AI offers personal, customized feedback to applicants, advice, and scoring based on their skills to enhance the candidate experience. Additionally, the platform supports fair and equitable hiring processes by giving all applicants an unbiased opportunity to showcase themselves. Primarily aimed at HR managers and recruiters, Potis.AI helps in reducing hiring related costs and saves significant recruitment hours by automating routine pre-screening tasks.
