About MachineTwin AI

MachineTwin AI, powered by Bevywise Networks, is an artificial intelligence-driven tool set designed to optimize manufacturing processes through effective planning and prediction capabilities. Its specialty lies in its ability to predict shop floor schedules, enabling a comprehensive insight into the manufacturing timelines and processes. Using past performance data, MachineTwin AI can effectively analyze and forecast potential outcomes, providing you with focused areas to ensure adherence to customer promises. The AI engine not only delves into the past, but also provides insightful analysis that informs thoughtful and actionable decisions in planning and other areas. This is accomplished through a precision-focused forecasting algorithm, encouraging optimal planning for improved execution and outcome. Furthermore, MachineTwin AI implements a data-driven approach, continuing to refine its learning algorithms in line with data growth.One of the fundamental components of MachineTwin AI is PlanInsight, a predictive planning tool that offers users the ability to input their own manufacturing data for analysis, taking into account both historical performance and future requirements. This prediction engine processes this information and provides an achievable manufacturing schedule. Furthermore, MachineTwin AI also offers integration functionality with any Manufacturing Execution System (MES) Solutions. This allows for a streamlined process, enabling more effective output without increasing overheads. Taken together, MachineTwin AI aims to bridge the gap between static planning and dynamic demands of modern manufacturing, enabling efficient control over plan changes.
