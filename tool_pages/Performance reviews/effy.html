About Effy

Effy AI is an AI-powered performance management and review software designed to enhance productivity and engagement within teams. This tool provides a streamlined and user-friendly platform for conducting diverse types of reviews such as 360 feedback, formal performance reviews, and evaluations in cases of role change, promotion, or major project milestones. Effy AI makes it easier to run reviews in three simple steps and offers a ready-to-use template along with a bank of curated questions to simplify the process. The platform supports flexible reviewer nominations, enabling evaluations from various sources including self, peer, manager, and subordinate. One of its unique features is empowerment of employees to suggest their reviewers, ensuring diversity of feedback. The tool also prioritises timeliness and efficiency by setting deadlines for response collection and automatically following up to ensure high response rates. Furthermore, Effy AI leverages AI to distill reviewers' responses into comprehensive reports for each employee, saving significant time and effort in analysis. Integration with communication platforms like Slack enhances accessibility and convenience, allowing users to receive review notifications and submit reviews directly within Slack. The tool's commitment to GDPR & CCPA compliance makes it a reliable choice for performance management.
