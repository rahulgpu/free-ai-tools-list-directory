About Hypercontext

Hypercontext is an AI-powered tool that automates the process of performance feedback for individuals and teams. It generates performance reviews based on meeting notes, goals, and other relevant data. With the tool, users can set and track shared goals, collaborate on shared agendas, and document decisions and next steps. Hypercontext also offers AI-powered insights and real-time feedback to help users conduct productive and impactful meetings. The tool is integrated with various platforms such as Slack, Microsoft Teams, Google Suite, and Outlook, allowing users to manage and run meetings from these platforms seamlessly. Additionally, Hypercontext provides templates for various meeting types such as 1:1 meetings, team meetings, remote meetings, and agile meetings, which users can choose from to enhance their meeting experience. Hypercontext also offers a library of over 360+ role-specific and personal development goal examples, which users can use as inspiration to set goals for their teams. The tool's pricing plan includes an unlimited number of guest users with free and smart share settings for collaboration. The tool provides resources such as a blog, reports, and a newsletter that offer management tips, resources, leadership trends, and best practices to directly improve users' leadership and management skills. Overall, Hypercontext simplifies performance management and meeting organization, enabling users to focus on achieving their goals and improving their team's productivity.
