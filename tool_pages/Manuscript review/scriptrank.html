About ScriptRank

ScriptRank is an AI-powered tool designed to provide developmental edit feedback for writers. This tool analyses manuscripts and offers insightful feedback on aspects including plot, character, thematic cohesion, and genre. ScriptRank serves as an efficient 'beta reader,' supporting authors by providing in-depth, comprehensive feedback to enhance their work before publication. It produces a Developmental Manuscript Report which includes an accurate storyline synopsis, insights on character development, detailed plot analysis, benchmarking against genre essentials, and evaluation of central themes. ScriptRanks technology draws on the expertise of experienced authors, editors, and publishing industry professionals, supported by advanced language processing technologies. This helps to deliver nuanced analysis adaptable to the evolving landscape of literary works across various genres, both fiction and non-fiction. ScriptRank's contribution does not just end with authors; it also caters to editors, agents, and publishers, helping streamline their processes with AI-generated insights. Authors submit their manuscripts via a secure portal and can expect to receive their reports within a day. These reports serve as guides to inform their editing strategy without compromising their narrative voice and vision.
