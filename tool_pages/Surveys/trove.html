About Trove

Trove is the first AI-powered conversational survey tool. By converting boring and unappealing traditional surveys into a unique conversational format, it makes the surveys 10x more engaging and powerful. Here're a few benefits of Trove: - By leveraging AI, Trove enables users to boost interaction, increase participation, and inspire genuine responses from survey participants. The tool emphasizes the power of natural dialogue, allowing users to harness its capabilities to gain a better understanding of their audience. - Trove uses GPT-4 to automatically generate your survey questions, make it 5x more efficiently to create an amazing survey - Trove automatically generates an AI-powered summary for each conversation, making it super easy to understand and get a gist of each response.
