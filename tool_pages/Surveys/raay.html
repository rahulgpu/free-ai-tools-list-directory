About Raay

Raay is an AI tool that allows users to create, share, run, and analyze data with forms and surveys. It aims to simplify the process of gathering insights by utilizing artificial intelligence technology. With Raay, users can quickly design questionnaires and obtain actionable insights without the need for extensive manual work.One notable feature of Raay is its high level of integratability. Users can collect responses on any device and embed the forms on their websites or mobile apps through APIs. This flexibility enables seamless data collection across various platforms.To ensure accuracy and fairness of results, Raay incorporates a bias detection system. This feature identifies and corrects potential biases in the responses, enhancing the reliability of the collected data.The power of AI in Raay enables users to generate meaningful insights. Clustering, visualization, and outcomes are used to create insights from the collected data. These AI-driven features assist users in gaining a deeper understanding of the information gathered through their forms and surveys.Raay offers different pricing plans to cater to various needs. The starter plan is free and provides 10 AI units and 500 entries with a 15-day data retention period. The professional plan costs $10 per month and includes 100 AI units, 50,000 entries, and a one-year data retention period. For more extensive usage, there is a custom business plan available that offers over 500 AI units, unlimited entries, and a three-year data retention period.Overall, Raay provides a user-friendly and efficient solution for creating and analyzing forms and surveys with the assistance of AI technology.
