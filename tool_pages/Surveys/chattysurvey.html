About ChattySurvey

ChattySurvey is an AI-powered survey tool that enables businesses to conduct personalized and natural conversations with their customers. The tool uses context-aware questioning and adaptive follow-ups to collect detailed feedback, automating the survey process and providing real-time analysis. ChattySurvey also allows businesses to fully customize their survey and design to fit their specific needs. It captures respondents' attention and yields higher response rates by transforming traditional surveys into interactive, chat-based experiences. The platform adapts to respondents' answers, revealing deeper context and understanding through relevant, dynamic follow-up questions. With ChattySurvey, businesses can expect to receive objective and unbiased results, as the conversational AI is designed to ask relevant follow-up questions based on respondents' answers. The platform allows for dynamic, adaptive questioning that generates more meaningful insights, uncovering additional context, motivations, and pain points. Businesses can query their results using natural language to get exactly the insights they need. Using AI-powered surveys not only saves time and money but can also significantly improve engagement and response rates. ChattySurvey helps businesses make data-driven decisions faster and with greater confidence. The tool offers a free basic plan that includes one survey, 50 responses, and basic analytics. More advanced plans include custom trend tracking, integrations with email and Shopify, custom branding, customer alerts for the team, and up to 10 team members. The tool is available in over 30 languages.
