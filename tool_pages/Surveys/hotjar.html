About Hotjar

Hotjar AI is a powerful tool that enables researchers to streamline their research process and generate insightful reports automatically. With Hotjar AI, users can create surveys for any research goal in just seconds, eliminating the need for manual work. The tool allows users to align with their team faster by reducing the analysis of survey responses with summary reports, making it easier to get the entire team on board. Hotjar AI also takes on the heavy lifting in research, allowing users to focus on what works best for their users. The tool enables users to create surveys quickly by telling the AI their objectives. Moreover, Hotjar AI eliminates the need to spend hours analyzing open-text responses by generating summary reports with a single click. This allows researchers to quickly identify key findings, quotes, and insights. Hotjar AI also offers seamless integration with various applications, enabling users to sync survey responses to their favorite tools such as email, Slack, or more than 1,000 tools via Zapier. The tool is available on all Hotjar plans, including the free Basic plan, making it accessible to all researchers. Hotjar AI is designed to simplify and accelerate the research process, enabling users to gather meaningful insights efficiently.
