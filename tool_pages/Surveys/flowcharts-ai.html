About FlowCharts.ai

FlowCharts.ai is an AI-powered tool designed to facilitate the creation and dissemination of surveys, forms, questionnaires, and workflows in an intelligent manner. The platform empowers users to distribute these items via multiple channels such as SMS, email, website link, and an AI ChatBot. It assists businesses in collecting valuable data from customers, patients, clients, and other segments of their audience. The surveys or forms authored with FlowCharts can feature interactive and adaptive elements, directing respondents through personalized decision trees or workflows depending on their exact responses. The tool presents a variety of response formats, including yes/no, free text, and single or multiple-choice answers. It also allows users to probe follow-up questions driven by the respondents' previous answers. FlowCharts.ai stands out thanks to its smart engine that generates dynamic and adaptable workflows depending on respondents' behaviors. In addition, the tool offers an intuitive and clean user interface alongside speedy performance. Finally, FlowCharts.ai provides mobile applications on multiple platforms, enabling users to manage and execute their surveys and forms on smartphones or tablets.
