About Clickable

Clickable is an AI-powered tool designed to facilitate the generation of ads for various marketing channels. It aims to create aesthetically pleasing, brand-consistent, and effective advertisements. The tool is user-friendly and does not require prior design experience, making it accessible to a broad user base. Clickable supports a variety of marketing channels, ad formats, and sizes. Utilizing AI, it streamlines the ad generation process, eradicating the need to hire and manage additional designers or copywriters. Users retain full control over the content and presentation of their ads. The consistency of brand presentation is a core focus of the tool; it retrieves brand details from a provided URL to ensure uniformity across all ads. Users can generate graphics and copy simply by adding keywords from existing campaigns or website content. Further customization is offered through editable elements such as taglines and calls-to-action. Clickable is devised to leverage AI advancements to boost sales and promote brand awareness effectively.
