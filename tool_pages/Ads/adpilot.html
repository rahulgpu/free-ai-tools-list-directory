About Adpilot

Adpilot by Picsart is an AI-powered ad maker tool designed to help businesses create on-brand and personalized social ads in a fast and efficient way. With Adpilot, users can create brand-aligned ads using AI-generated imagery, backgrounds, and text all in one place, without needing multiple tools. Adpilot offers various use cases for marketers, e-commerce businesses, social media influencers, and small business owners. Adpilot combines visuals and copy in about 1 minute, making it an all-in-one platform to streamline the workflow, save time and resources, and enable customization based on specific business goals. Adpilot offers mobile preview options for creating mobile-optimized ads. The AI-generated ads created by Adpilot are optimized for various platforms such as social media, websites, display ads, and chatbot ads. Adpilot is a free tool, but there is a premium plan expected to be released soon with additional benefits such as unlimited text and image generation and auto-resizing to different platform sizes. Overall, Adpilot simplifies the ad creation process for businesses with its AI technology, providing versatility, efficiency, and customization options to create high-quality ads that drive results.
