About 30characters

30characters is an AI-driven tool designed specifically to streamline the process of creating effective search ads. It assists in overcoming writer's block, aiding in the generation of high-quality headlines, descriptions among others with minimal input. The tool features a user-friendly interface where users input ad group details such as landing page, keywords, and calls to action. 30characters subsequently generates relevant content which can be edited and refined according to the user's preferences. It understands character limits, allowing it to produce apt search ad copy. The option to bulk import an entire ad into the Google Ads Editor simplifies the ad creation process greatly. The AI used in this tool has been trained on live Google Ads, which results in performance-oriented, accurate copy with proven frameworks to drive not just clicks but conversions. The tool supports multiple campaign types including Responsive Search Ads (RSAs), Performance MAX (PMAX), and Demand Gen campaigns. Additionally, it offers multilingual support, further widening its scope and making it an adaptable solution for various markets. It's important to note that as opposed to other AI tools that could be overwhelmed by larger projects, 30characters can handle multiple campaigns and ad groups without any decrease in its performance.
