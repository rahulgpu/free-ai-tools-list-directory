About AdswithAI

AHILAB is an advertising network designed specifically for developers and marketers interested in leveraging AI apps. It enables marketers to run contextual ads over AI-generated content, potentially reaching users engaging with a wide array of generative AI apps. The tool is distinguished by its quality control for ad placements, minimizing low-quality leads and providing more valuable exposure for marketers' ads. Furthermore, AHILAB equips marketers with customized analytics, allowing them to monitor business-significant metrics closely. Developers who utilize AHILAB can monetize their GenAI apps using AHILABs RESTful API with just a single line of code, which displays relevant ads to users and provides full control over advertisers. This method can remove potential frictions, allowing everyone to use the apps for free, and it opens up a network of advertisers for developers. AHILAB also empowers developers to choose which advertisers can promote in their apps and provides the ability to quickly earn and monitor performance right from day one.
