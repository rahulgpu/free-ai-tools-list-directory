About RoastMyLinked.In

RoastMyLinked.In is an AI-powered tool designed to optimize LinkedIn profiles. It does this by analyzing various elements of a users profile, including LinkedIn headline, summary, experience, and skills in comparison to industry best practices. Upon inspection, the tool identifies areas of strength and aspects in need of improvement. Based on these findings, it then provides users with actionable recommendations designed to enhance their online presence and visibility. To assist users in their understanding and utilization of the tool, an interactive chat feature is included that answers specific questions about the provided report. This assistive chat facilitates detailed explanations and offers examples to help users implement the recommended changes effectively. After adjustments are made, users can submit their updated profile, upon which they receive a follow-up review and a report of their optimized profile. RoastMyLinked.In also offers personal career coaching in some of their service plans. These services are not exclusive to individuals but can be used by businesses or teams trying to optimize multiple LinkedIn profiles simultaneously.
