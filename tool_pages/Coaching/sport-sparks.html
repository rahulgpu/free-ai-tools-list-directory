About Sport Sparks

SportSparks is an innovative application tailored for sports coaches aiming to integrate advanced problem-solving techniques into their coaching methods. The app provides an interactive platform for coaches to transition seamlessly from identifying the issue to finding the solution. It includes a research feature, which utilizes artificial intelligence and the expertise of high-level coaches to facilitate exploration of numerous problem-solving possibilities. The app offers various tools and resources to enhance a coach's knowledge and accelerate their performance. These include a wide array of case studies from expert coaches, AI-generated insights, and a repository of online resources for quick learning. A unique feature of SportSparks is its personalized suggestions that it offers based on the users needs. This feature helps coaches discover varied perspectives and strategies for the enhancement of their athlete's performance. The app also encourages coaches to create their own playbook of derived ideas, solutions, feedback which they can further share within their network. Clearly, SportSparks is more than just a coaching app; it's a comprehensive solution for coaches to redefine their coaching strategies with creativity and evidence-backed informational resources. Note that the tool development is enabled by the National Centre for Creativity, a project funded by City, University of London and UK Research England.
