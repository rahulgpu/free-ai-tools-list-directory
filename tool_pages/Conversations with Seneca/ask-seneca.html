About Ask Seneca

Ask Seneca is an educational tool focused on the teachings of Seneca, who is widely recognized as one of history's greatest philosophers and one of the most popular proponents of Stoicism. This tool is designed to allow users to interactively learn about Stoicism by asking questions directly, to which they receive advice and insights derived from Seneca's philosophical beliefs and teachings. The tool aims to create an engaging and interactive learning platform, making philosophical learning more accessible and enjoyable. It has been developed with a focus on user engagement, integrating a question and answer format to facilitate an interactive learning experience. Built by Dylan, Ask Seneca is aimed at those interested in philosophy, particularly Stoicism, offering them the ability to gain knowledge directly from one of the guiding lights of this philosophical school.
