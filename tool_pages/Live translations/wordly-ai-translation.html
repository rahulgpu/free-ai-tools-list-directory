About Wordly AI Translation

Wordly AI Translation is an AI-enabled tool designed to facilitate multilingual communication in various event formats. Its primary function is to provide audio translation, captions, subtitles, and transcription for in-person and virtual meetings, webinars, and on-demand videos. The utility offers vital accessibility and inclusivity options for diverse audiences, enhancing engagement during events. Wordly provides simultaneous, two-way translation, making it applicable for 'one to many' and 'many to many' sessions. Attendees can access translated audio and subtitles alongside same-language captions, allowing them to engage in their preferred languages. Additionally, the tool offers high-quality translation optimization and language testing for reliable output. Database security and privacy protection are integral parts of Wordly's design, fulfilling SOC 2 Type II compliance requirements. The tool is compatible with popular platforms, including Zoom, Teams, and Cvent, providing flexible integration options catered to different event setups. Wordly's video captioning and subtitling feature is beneficial for extending the usability of webinars, training sessions, and product demos to global audiences. The tool performs language translation using AI algorithms, including rule-based translation, statistical machine translation, and neural machine translation.
