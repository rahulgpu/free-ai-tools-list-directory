About Viva Translate

Viva Translate is an AI-powered tool designed to aid real-time communication across multiple languages. The software employs a powerful AI engine to provide quick and accurate translations, allowing users to translate online meetings and various other forms of content in real-time. It aims to break language barriers that may cause slowdowns in communication processes. Beyond basic translation, Viva Translate incorporates advanced AI-features that result in improved productivity. This includes automatic meeting summaries, action items, transcriptions, and more, streamlining users' workflows. The software offers a quality post-meeting experience, allowing users to revisit portions of their meetings. Viva Translate supports seamless integration with popular video platforms like Google Meets, Microsoft Teams, and Zoom, enabling users to access translations and other meeting information on the platform of their preference. The tool can be used globally, and it has been implemented by numerous companies to enhance their cross-language communication. In addition, Viva Translate also offers desktop applications for Windows and Mac, along with a Chrome extension for additional utility.
