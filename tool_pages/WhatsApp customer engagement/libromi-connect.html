About Libromi Connect

Libromi Connect is an AI-first Whatsapp cloud-based business platform, specializing in providing various tools and features for businesses to utilize WhatsApp more effectively. The core features of this platform include an AI-enabled no-code chatbot builder that allows businesses to automate interactions and provide real-time responses to customers. Additionally, Libromi Connect offers automation capabilities, bulk broadcasting to send large volumes of messages at once, and a team inbox which enables multiple agents to manage incoming chats. Further features support eCommerce within WhatsApp through integration with platforms like Shopify and WooCommerce. This allows businesses to create a fully integrated shopping experience within the chat platform, which includes automated checkout processes and recovery options for abandoned carts. Libromi Connect also enables the development of multi-agent chat systems with access control and performance monitoring functionality, improving sales and support chat management. The platform offers native integrations with various other platforms for increased functionality, including OpenAI for AI assistance and Salesforce and Zapier for extended customer relationship management capabilities. Furthermore, it provides a service to get businesses officially verified on WhatsApp by acquiring a green tick verification mark, adding to credibility and authenticity of the business presence on WhatsApp. Libromi Connect is suitable for small to large scale businesses looking to leverage WhatsApp for customer service, marketing, commerce and more.
