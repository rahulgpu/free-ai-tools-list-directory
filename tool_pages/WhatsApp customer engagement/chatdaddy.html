About ChatDaddy

ChatDaddy is an AI-powered, WhatsApp-dedicated platform designed to automate customer interactions, manage prospects, and elevate business growth. The tool has a range of features including AI chatbots that autonomously answer support inquiries, instant message reply suggestions, and tone alterations to better suit client needs. Cloud-based team inbox allows efficient collaboration among teammates, while assignments prioritizes tasks and optimizes workflow. The platform also offers multi-agent access and ticketing for complex problem resolution; internal note for sharing customer-specific information among team members; and omnichannel for integrating all conversations into a single platform. Another key feature is the no-code chatbot builder that creates and deploys powerful chatbots without requiring technical skills. The customizable flow tailors chatbots to specific business need with keyword reply ensuring swift and relevant responses. ChatDaddy also offers WhatsApp-specific features including broadcasting tailored messages to large audiences and sending real-time shop notifications. Lastly, the platform seamlessly integrates with numerous popular eCommerce, CRM, automation, and productivity tools.
