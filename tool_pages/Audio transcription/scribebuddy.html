About ScribeBuddy

ScribeBuddy is a versatile AI tool that provides free transcription, translation, and subtitle generation services. By converting speech into text, it can automatically transcribe audio and video files, podcasts, voice memos, and live speech in 120+ languages. It is designed to function seamlessly on iPhone, Android, Mac, and Windows devices. A key feature of this tool is its high transcription accuracy, making it a reliable tool for all types of media. Additionally, ScribeBuddy can translate podcast, interview, meeting transcripts and the like into 100+ languages. The summary feature, powered by AI, helps distil key information from transcripts, consequently providing concise overviews for quick insights. It can generate subtitles for videos, podcasts, or meetings, thus improving accessibility and enhancing user engagement. Apart from transcription, translation, and subtitle creation, ScribeBuddy also provides a utility to transcribe live speech into text instantly, which is useful for live events, lectures, or meetings. Efficient transcription of meetings, interviews, lectures, voice notes, etc., into text furthers documentation and collaboration. Furthermore, the software also offers unlimited free transcriptions for audio, video, and podcasts, with over 98% accuracy, along with speaker recognition and time stamps for easy navigation.
