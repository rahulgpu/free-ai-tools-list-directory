About Tely AI

Tely AI is an artificial intelligence-driven tool designed to effectively automate B2B content marketing. Tely AI's fundamental methodology involves understanding a product in-depth, building domain expertise, and conducting thorough SEO optimisation. It gathers details from a broad array of resources including the product's website, API documentation, demo videos, and blogs along with external data sources like books, research papers, podcasts, authority websites, and others. This comprehensive approach allows Tely AI to produce content that resonates with the target audience and addresses their problems in a knowledgeable and engaging manner. The tool has an integrated functionality for SEO optimisation which includes keyword research, article performance tracking, and content rewriting for improved rankings and conversions. Tely AI also creates and manages monthly content plans, relieving businesses of regular posting ordeals. This includes article generation, social media syndication, creating lead magnets such as e-books, and designing infographics or code snippets based on article context for personalized content creation. Tely AI stands out by adding quotes from market experts to articles for credibility and adding contextual call-to-actions to convert readers into leads. It aims to help businesses optimise their content marketing strategies while reducing their customer acquisition cost and dependence on paid channels. Tely AI affords startups the opportunity to establish trust with potential customers through consistent and valuable content, thereby driving more sales and conversions without the need to expand their team.
