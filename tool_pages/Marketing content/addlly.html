About Addlly

Addlly is an advanced AI writing tool optimised for creating a wide range of marketing content, such as blogs, social media posts, ad copy, press releases, and newsletters, in a brand's unique voice. This tool employs multiple AI models for targeted, high-impact content creation, and leverages a decade-worth of SEO expertise for crafting content likely to rank better in search results. One inherent benefit of Addlly is its brand-focused content development scheme which allows the content to resonate with the brands personality. Addlly also offers real-time data and boasts trends analysis to keep the content relevant. It further ensures complete data privacy. Additionally, this tool can generate fact-checked, real-time content. Addllys unique composition includes different AI models - OpenAI, PaLM 2, Stability AI, Llama, and Hugging Face, to deliver optimum results. Major Addlly's products include: 1 Click Blog Writer for creating engaging and SEO-friendly blogs, SEO Blog Co-Pilot for long-form content and keyword research, Social Media Post Generator for crafting viral posts for various platforms, and Ad Copy Writer for effective Google Ad copies.
