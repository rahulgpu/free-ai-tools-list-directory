About Sequens

Sequens.ai is an AI tool designed to generate and manage B2B marketing content at scale, with a special focus on SEO. It facilitates the production of custom and tone-of-voice oriented content suitable for different channels, drawing from disciplines like journalism, copywriting, social media management and web design. The tool also includes expert review stages to ensure the quality of its AI-generated content. It helps to increase audience engagement and the lead pipeline by providing value across all channels. The AI system embodies roles to deliver high-quality content, which is later reviewed by experts using user feedback. After content creation, the tool provides the option to publish the content on various channels and watch the audience's interaction. Sequens.ai aims to provide a blend of human expertise and AI-driven efficiency, reducing time-consuming tasks and improving content quality. Some of its specific features include SEO focus, project management capabilities, custom prompts for easy content editing and brand voice customization. Security and confidentiality are vital aspects of the platform, committed to ensuring the protection of client data.
