About Vox by Marr Labs

Vox is a highly advanced AI-voice agent developed by Marr Labs. With the capacity to be almost indistinguishable from a human, Vox is designed to support sales, customer service, and various other business functions, providing round-the-clock conversational intelligence. As a powerful aid for businesses, the tool takes care of standard operations, enabling the workforce to concentrate on crucial issues. The team behind Vox includes pioneers and leaders in AI and speech recognition technology, with previous experience in groundbreaking projects such as Siri and Samsung's S-Voice. Vox's abilities include scheduling meetings, pre-qualifying leads, acquiring data, and supporting sales processes, providing a human-level customer service. It can also serve as a personal assistant, managing calls and appointments.The foundation of Vox's capabilities is Symphonia, Marr Labs' proprietary conversational intelligence platform that enables Vox to learn your business, deploy its skills, and handle conversations flawlessly. At its core lie two components: ML Turbo and ML Reason. ML Turbo leads Vox's listening capability, ensuring a smooth back-and-forth conversation. The additional module, ML Reason, is a custom Large Language Model that allows Vox to quickly absorb and apply a wealth of knowledge in various fields, offering specialized skills similar to expert-level understanding in Sales, Customer Service, and Scheduling. Overall, Vox represents the future of conversational AI, offering businesses an efficient, intelligent tool to handle various tasks, seamlessly integrating into business functions, and enhancing customer interactions.
