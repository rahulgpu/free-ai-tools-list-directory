About Pixel-Art.ai

Pixel-Art.ai is an AI-powered tool designed to generate pixel art imagery. This tool embeds artificial intelligence to change regular visions into pixel-perfect artwork, a feature that makes it an optimal solution for game developers, graphic designers, and pixel art aficionados. Pixel-Art.ai provides a seamless platform for users to create striking and vibrant pixel art for free. Via the tool, users can generate a diverse array of aesthetic creations, ranging from cosmic turtles, night cityscapes to underwater caverns and winter village scenes. The tool also boasts a built-in pixel art studio which assists users to manage their work with multiple canvases and provides privacy to their creations. In a bid to make user designs pixel-perfect, Pixel-Art.ai not just allows export of work to PNG but also arms users with an equipped pixel art editor. This editor offers numerous features such as copy/paste, layers, brushes etc, to superimpose personal elements to the AI art. Lastly, the tool supports AI inpainting allowing users to choose the area they intend to alter and apply their favourable result to the canvas simply by typing in a prompt and letting the AI automatically carry out the rest.
