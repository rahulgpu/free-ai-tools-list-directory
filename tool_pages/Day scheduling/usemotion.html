About Usemotion

Motion Calendar is an Artificial Intelligence (AI) based tool used in planning work schedules, managing calendars, tasks, meetings and project management. The tool provides an automatic schedule based on the user's tasks and priorities, by taking into account all projects and tasks and displaying them in a prioritized time-block manner. The tool dynamically optimizes schedules dozens of times in a day to keep plans up-to-date. The tool is especially useful for enhancing productivity as it assists busy individuals and work teams. The proprietary algorithm of the tool called 'The Happiness Algorithm' aims to maximize user happiness and satisfaction while increasing productivity. It automatically integrates work and personal calendars into a master calendar. The tool helps users to manage tasks by computing across all projects and tasks, their deadlines, priorities, dependencies, etc. The tool also has workload reporting that advises users if they are taking too much work or setting impossible deadlines. In terms of meetings, Motion creates meeting booking pages, shows availability, and schedules meetings at ideal times. Motion provides services for various businesses such as IT Service providers, Marketing agencies, Design agencies, Law Firms, Consulting Businesses, Startups, Product Teams, Marketing Teams, Sales Teams, Executive Teams, and others in a wide array of sectors.
