About Cosonify

Cosonify is a tool suite designed for songwriters and music producers to help them create, brainstorm and develop song ideas. The tool suite consists of three main tools: Researchboard, Ideaboard and Taskboard. The Researchboard is an audio mood board which allows users to research reference songs and find their song vision. The Ideaboard allows users to turn their song ideas into whiteboards with text and visuals. The Taskboard enables users to organize tasks and collaborate with others. The Cosonify tool suite is designed to improve the creative process and professionalize the workflow of music creators with easy-to-use tools. All data collected by Cosonify is subject to the privacy policy and terms of use.
