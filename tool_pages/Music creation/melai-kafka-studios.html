About MelAI – Kafka Studios

MelAI is an AI-powered tool developed by Kafka Studios that leverages artificial intelligence to create original and captivating melodies. Packaged as a Virtual Studio Technology (VST) plugin, it's designed to seamlessly integrate with your favorite Digital Audio Workstation (DAW) for a streamlined composition process. The tool provides an expansive range of melody styles, covering an array of 11 distinct musical genres that cater to a wide spectrum of musical tastes. With MelAI, users can generate unique tunes, fueling their inspiration as they compose music. MelAI transforms the way users ideate and produce music, doing away with the generic tunes and instead, offering a more personalised musical brainstorming experience. The tool is not just for professional musicians; it's also suitable for anyone interested in creating songs, regardless of their musical expertise. As such, MelAI has wide applications in music production, composition, and entertainment. Through the MelAI website, users can access and download the VST plugin and engage with the community via their Discord server.
