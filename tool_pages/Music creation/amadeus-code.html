About Amadeus Code

Amadeus Code is a company that provides a range of AI-powered music creation tools for artists, music producers, and other creatives in the music industry. The company's main product of the same name, 'Amadeus Code', is a platform that aids in songwriting and music production. They also offer a selection of other products including 'Amadeus Topline', 'Evoke Music', 'MusicTGA-HR', and 'OTOKAI', each serving unique needs in music creation and production. 'Amadeus Topline' is an app specifically designed for writing and recording lyrics, 'Evoke Music' is an AI music creation service, and 'MusicTGA-HR' and 'OTOKAI' are specialized tools for music arrangements. Overall, the company's suite of tools aim to blend artificial intelligence with human creativity to efficiently streamline the music production process. The company provides detailed information about its products, business operations, hiring, news updates, and investor relations on its official website.
