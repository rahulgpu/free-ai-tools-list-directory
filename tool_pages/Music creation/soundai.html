About SoundAI

SoundAI is an AI-based instrumental web service designed for sound creation and generation in various formats, such as samples, MIDI files, and presets for virtual synthesizers. This AI tool enables musicians and sound engineers to quickly and easily generate musical components, allowing for increased creativity and efficiency in the music creation process. Beyond simple generation features, SoundAI offers an AI melody generation function, enabling the creation of new melodies and MIDI notes based on provided parameters. This is particularly useful for exploration of new musical ideas and variation on established musical phrases and riffs. The tool also supports the synthesis and modification of sounds, with capabilities including the synthesis of new sounds and musical instruments, and the modification of audio file characteristics, such as tempo, pitch, and timbre. SoundAI also provides the ability to import and export sound files and projects for integration with other Digital Audio Workstations (DAWs) and software tools. For developers, an API is provided, allowing for the integration of SoundAI's functionality into other applications and platforms.
