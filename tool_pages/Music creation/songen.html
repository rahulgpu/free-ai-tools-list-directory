About Songen

Songen is a highly innovative artificial intelligence powered music generator application, available on the iOS platform. It's primary function is to create an unlimited string of unique music loops and songs which are royalty-free. These musical creations can be exported in both MIDI and audio, allowing you to easily complete the piece on your preferred digital audio workstation (DAW). Apart from generating music, Songen allows customization features such as the option to change tempo, key, and instrumentation. It also provides you with the capability to create various aspects of popular genres, such as chord progressions, melodies, basslines and drum loops. Songen is designed to cater to music creators of various kinds, including songwriters, artists and music producers. The app focuses on helping them succeed by offering a constant stream of music ideas, synthesizing the essence of different music genres through high-end technology and algorithms. Another essential feature of Songen includes the provision for users to export their work as MIDI, audio, or video. It's constantly updated to stay in line with the latest music trends making it a valuable tool for any music professional.
