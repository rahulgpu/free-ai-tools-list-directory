About AISong.Fun

AISong.Fun is a platform designed to harness AI technology for music composition. Users can create their personalized music tracks by choosing a prompt or instrumental and generating a song. The platform can generate a wide range of music styles, including melodies, rhythms, and lyrics, which is particularly helpful for songwriters or enthusiasts looking to explore new creative possibilities. The 'Custom Modes' feature gives users the option to input their own lyrics, song style, and song name, allowing for further personalization of the AI-generated music. This feature enables users to have increased control over the creative aspects of the song, ensuring the generated music aligns with their specific needs and preferences. Despite the platform powering its service free-of-charge, users have the opportunity to subscribe to premium plans to get enhanced features and higher song generation allowances. In addition, users can download the AI-composed music to listen offline or share with others. The platform supports multiple languages, widening its accessibility for users internationally.
