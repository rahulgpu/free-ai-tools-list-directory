About Wavtool

WavTool is a professional-grade, AI-accelerated music and audio production tool that operates within your browser. It is designed to assist in music projects, regardless of location, facilitating easy collaboration through URL sharing. WavTool offers services like creating, editing, and remixing tracks. It allows importing of songs for editing and also supports starting from scratch. WavTool provides instrumental assistance through the Conductor, a multi-modal music chatbot, capable of generating audio and MIDI, transcribing recorded music, and offering more creative services. It also features a Composer to help combat writers block, understanding the musical context of a project and suggesting continuations and variations instantly. WavTool allows for the creation of unique sounds and instrumental passages of music across various genres. It also facilitates the isolation and extraction of individual instruments from any input audio. It allows interactive extraction of notes from audio and provides real-time control over the transcription process. WavTool also offers MIDI recording, MIDI exports, and audio recording along with a growing directory of free samples and loops. There's the possibility of cloud saving for seamless work across different machines. It also supports VST & AU plugins with customizable down-to-code synthesisers.
