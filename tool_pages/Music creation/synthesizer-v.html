About Synthesizer V

Synthesizer V is an advanced music production tool developed by Dreamtonics. The tool excels in producing realistic vocals with the nuance and expression of the human singing voice, aiming to revolutionize the way music is produced. It leverages a deep neural network-based synthesis engine to replicate warmth and tonality, characteristics synonymous with the human singing voice. This easy-to-use, convenient, and efficient tool allows users to experiment with various voice notes and tones without hindering their musical vocabulary. Synthesizer V operates offline, providing users with the freedom to generate vocals even without an internet connection. It includes functions like Live Rendering for real-time waveform visualization of changes being made, and Cross-Lingual Synthesis to break through language barriers with the feature to produce music in English, Japanese, and Chinese. The Synthesizer V Studio Pro also adjoins with your workflow through interfaces like VST3 and AudioUnit. It allows users to import a MIDI file, simply type in lyrics, enter notes, and achieve a high-quality vocal output. The tool offers a variety of voice databases sung by professional musicians. Synthesizer V comes in two variants: Basic and Pro Edition, each with its distinct features, ensuring suitability for both hobbyists and professionals.
