About Emergent Drums

Emergent Drums 2 by Audialab is an AI-powered music plugin that generates unique drum samples from scratch. By using state-of-the-art artificial intelligence, the plugin can generate an unlimited number of drum samples, all of which are royalty-free. The plugin is ideal for music producers and composers who want to create unique and high-quality drum sounds. It is also great for artists looking to get creative with their music and explore new sounds. The plugin also comes with featured artist demos and sample packs, so users can get inspiration from the work of other professionals. Additionally, users can join the Audialab mailing list for access to the latest patch notes and announcements. Emergent Drums 2 is $149 and offers free 7-day trial!
