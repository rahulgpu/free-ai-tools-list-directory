About Beatoven

Beatoven.ai is an AI music generation tool that uses advanced AI techniques to generate unique, mood-based music that fits the specific needs of a video. It collects and stores data on user preferences and behaviour to personalise the experience and to generate content that is tailored to the user. The tool also uses cookies to track user interactions and to remember user preferences. It is GDPR compliant and uses cookies to detect if the visitor has accepted the marketing category in the cookie banner. It also uses cookies to collect data on visitor behaviour from multiple websites, in order to present more relevant advertisements. Overall, Beatoven.ai is a powerful and intuitive AI tool that can generate unique and personalised music for video production.
