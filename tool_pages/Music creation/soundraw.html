About SOUNDRAW

SOUNDRAW is an AI-powered platform designed to generate unique songs for creators and artists. This solution allows users to create, edit, and personalize music aligning with their specific needs. With easy-to-use AI tools, it makes manipulating song structures including shortening intros, rearranging choruses a seamless process. The platform offers unlimited generation of royalty-free music for various applications such as projects, videos, and personal songs. It enables the distribution of user-generated music across various platforms where the creator can earn 100% of the royalties. SOUNDRAW also possess a permanent licensing model, which allows users to keep the license even if they unsubscribe from the service. For businesses, SOUNDRAW provides an API that can be integrated with their products for generating and customizing songs at a faster speed, allowing users to download an unlimited amount of content. Moreover, the platform ensures that all music created through their proprietary algorithms and data sets is legally sound, thus retaining full copyright ownership of the AI-generated music.
