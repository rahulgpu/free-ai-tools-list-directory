About Moises App

Moises App is an AI-powered musical practice tool that enables musicians to interact with songs in various groundbreaking ways, enhancing their practicing experience. The app offers AI-driven audio separation, allowing users to isolate vocals and instruments in any track, making it easier for musicians to focus on specific aspects of a song. A smart metronome and audio speed changer enables users to control the pace of a song, offering a great way to practice at different speeds. Additionally, a pitch changer and AI key detection allows musicians to easily adjust the pitch of songs to meet their vocal range or to play without a capo. Simultaneously, the app provides insight into the key signature of any song. Moises App also features a chord detection tool that enables users to see chords in real time. This versatile tool appeals not only to individual musicians such as drummers, guitarists, singers, and bassists, but also to producers who would find the audio separation and music modulation features useful. The app is available on various platforms including iOS, Google Play, and Desktop.
