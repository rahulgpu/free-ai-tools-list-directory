About Tagify

Tagify is a tool designed for Webflow developers, designers, and marketeers to automate alt-tagging tasks. Created by Webflow professionals, Tagify uses advanced AI capabilities to analyze images and generate reliable and SEO-friendly alt tags. Through this automation, it removes the requirement for manual input, leading to significant time savings. Users can review, improve, or rewrite the generated alt tags at any given time, allowing for maximum control over their projects. In addition to generating new alt tags, Tagify will search for existing alt tags within a project and check if they need corrections for optimisation. Users are also presented with an overview of all images and assets within their Webflow project. Furthermore, the alt-tags generated are known for their high degree of accuracy and contextual relevance, thanks to the integration with Azure AI. The initial release of Tagify will have the capability to generate alt-tags for an unlimited number of images and in English.
