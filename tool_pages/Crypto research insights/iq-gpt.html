About IQ GPT

IQ GPT is an AI-powered search engine and assistant developed by BrainDAO, which specializes in extracting and providing cryptocurrency and blockchain insights. It is a tool designed for traders, researchers and individuals interested in cryptocurrency. IQ GPT retrieves information from the IQ.wiki database and provides answers to prompts about crypto and blockchain-related topics. The integrations with CoinGecko, InvestHK, DeFiLlama, Etherscan, CoinNess, Frax, Debank and Vector Query Tool facilitate this process, accessing comprehensive and up-to-date market data, infrastructure data, decentralized finance (DeFi) data and more. IQ GPT is customizable, with the ability to train it for a specific community. There is a curated collection of tools and plugins to personalize responses and deliver contextually relevant information. It can also be integrated with social platforms for specialized info delivery, such as Telegram and Discord, and can be used as a chatbot to enhance user experience on DApps, DEXes, DeFi protocols, Crypto Projects, NFT Marketplaces and Data Platforms. The tool is part of the larger IQ ecosystem and provides regular updates through the IQ Ecosystem Report.
