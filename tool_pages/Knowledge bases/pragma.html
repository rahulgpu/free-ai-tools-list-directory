About Pragma

This tool aims to centralize your organization's knowledge base, making it easily accessible and manageable across various platforms. Key features include instant access to information right when you need it, even within apps like Slack. The AI tool can be trained on your data, allowing it to provide relevant and precise responses. It also integrates with multiple platforms, creating a single command that initiates multi-platform actions. It offers personalized privacy options to protect your information and can store data based on your preference. User feedback is utilized to constantly refine your knowledge repository and improve future responses. It also has features targeted towards sales and support teams. Your sales team can retrieve important details and even gain insights from competitor websites. Support can be automated with an AI chatbot on your website. This tool also encourages the use of organizational knowledge to generate resonating and updated content. Moreover, it champions a continuous learning approach and is designed to gradually improve over time based on user feedback and added content.
