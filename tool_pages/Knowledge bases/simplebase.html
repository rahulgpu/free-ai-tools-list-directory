About SimpleBase

SimpleBase is an AI-powered knowledge base platform designed to streamline knowledge management. With a user-friendly interface, it allows users to effortlessly create and publish articles in 34 languages, enabling global reach. The platform simplifies article creation through the power of AI, eliminating the need for time-consuming manual writing. Key Features: 1. **Multilingual Support:** SimpleBase supports translation into 20 languages, facilitating communication with customers worldwide. Users can add new languages with ease, leverage a side-by-side translation editor, and automatically translate articles. 2. **Customizable Knowledge Base:** Create a visually stunning knowledge base with custom branding, templates, and the option to connect your own domain. Reflect your brand's identity with powerful branding customization. 3. **Article Management:** The platform offers efficient article and category management based on extensive user experience research. Users can create visually appealing articles using the powerful rich text editor. 4. **Unlimited Content Creation:** SimpleBase allows the creation of an unlimited number of categories and articles without restrictions on viewers or bandwidth, providing flexibility for diverse content. 5. **Analytics and Integrations:** Gain insights into knowledge base usage through in-depth statistics. Additionally, integrate Google Analytics and Intercom seamlessly into your knowledge base. Pricing: - SimpleBase offers a transparent pricing plan of $49 per month, encompassing all features, with a 14-day free trial and no credit card requirement during the trial period. - The subscription includes unlimited articles, categories, languages, file upload storage, author seats, and features such as machine translation, custom domain, and advanced analytics. Migration and Support: - SimpleBase offers migration assistance for transitioning knowledge base content to their platform. - Nonprofit organizations can apply for a free Supercharged account by contacting SimpleBase. FAQs: - Users can cancel their subscription at any time, retaining access to content through the dashboard. - Multiple payment options, including credit cards, Google Pay, and Apple Pay, are supported via Stripe integration. - A 30-day refund policy is in place, with case-by-case handling after the initial period. Enhance your knowledge base experience with SimpleBase's AI-driven features, offering customization, multilingual support, and seamless content management.
