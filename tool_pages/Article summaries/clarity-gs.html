About Clarity.gs

Clarity is an AI-driven tool designed to provide a layered, depth-first reading experience. The tool has the primary purpose of enabling users to gain a deeper understanding of complex topics. Conceptually, users begin with summary-level information and can choose to delve into more details as needed. Clarity's interface allows you to interactively 'tap' to explore further details on the subject matter. The platform can be used to dissect a wide range of topics, including but not limited to business strategies, online articles, reference material like Wikipedia, and topics from various fields such as sports analytics. It provides a unique approach to consuming information, shifting from the traditional linear reading format to a more dynamic, user-controlled exploration of content. This makes it an ideal tool for researchers, students, and anyone aiming to achieve a greater understanding of layered informational structures. The depth-first reading approach aims to foster comprehensive comprehension and critical thinking by allowing users to control the level of detail they wish to explore.
