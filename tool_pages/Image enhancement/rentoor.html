About Rentoor

Rentoor is an AI-driven tool that offers image enhancement solutions. It serves to improve the quality of blurred or old face photos, enabling users to keep their memories alive with better clarity. Rentoor focuses on restoring details and ensuring stunning visuals with its super-resolution feature. In addition, it helps users to easily download their processed images. Rentoor also comes equipped with a noise reduction feature designed to clean up photos for a more professional outcome. This tool also boasts an image sharpening utility intended to deliver sharp and crisp results. The AI provides a basic plan with limited credits and image generations alongside a Pro Plan which includes more features. Rentoor has garnered positive responses from users, including the ability to restore low-quality images, and easy accessibility. Catering to personal and commercial usage, this tool offers early access to new features and the opportunity to store photos in a dashboard for its Pro Plan users.
