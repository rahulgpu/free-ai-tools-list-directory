About SuperID

SuperID is an AI photo enhancer focusing on detailed and high-quality facial upscaling. It serves to improve and perfect facial details in images, making it a suitable tool for professional photography or capturing family moments. SuperID has particular strengths in enhancing photos where facial details are critical, proving useful for a variety of industries including print, graphic design, and e-commerce. It offers the capacity to upscale low-resolution images, honing in on facial details like skin and beards to generate a strikingly clear output. The tool is compatible with any image format and provides both fast and enhanced download options. It has its own Clear Resolution Enhancer and an advanced batch editing feature, allowing for the upscaling of multiple images simultaneously. The tool also integrates easily with other platforms through its comprehensive API documentation. As it is cloud-based, SuperID is scalable and assures data protection. Along with one-time bulk upscaling services, SuperID offers subscription plans and custom solutions.
