About Pixalto

Pixalto - AI Photo Enhancer is a powerful app that utilizes artificial intelligence to enhance and improve your photos. With its "Remove Background" feature, you can easily isolate the main subject of your image and place it on a transparent or customized backdrop. This process saves time and effort, allowing you to create eye-catching visuals without the need for complex photo-editing skills. Pixalto offers a range of AI-powered tools for image enhancement, including fixing blurry images, reducing noise, and enhancing facial features for a natural and polished look. It effectively eliminates red-eye and blemishes, transforming your portraits. The "Separate Face" feature is particularly valuable for group photos. It allows you to effortlessly extract individual faces from the group, enabling various applications such as crafting social media profile pictures or enhancing facial attributes. You can convert a group photo into a collection of distinct portraits, expanding its versatility and creative potential.Additionally, Pixalto provides image coloring capabilities, allowing you to turn any photo into a coloring page or Colorscape. You can create PDF or hard copy coloring books to send and share as gifts. Furthermore, Pixalto's image upscaling feature can transform low-resolution images into stunning, high-quality ones.Overall, Pixalto - AI Photo Enhancer offers a comprehensive set of AI-powered tools for image editing and enhancement, making it a valuable app for anyone looking to improve their photos effortlessly and efficiently.
