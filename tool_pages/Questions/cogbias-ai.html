About CogBias AI

CogBias AI is an Artificial Intelligence platform primarily focused on detecting cognitive biases in conversations. The backbone of its technology lies in its ability to aid users in asking effective questions, assessing answers through the right perspective, and thereby facilitating informed decision-making processes. Specific features of CogBias AI include question rephrasing and AI-powered recommendations that help identify potential biases in audience responses. This aims to avoid leading customers to biased or influenced answers, driving towards genuine insights. Apart from singular question analysis, the platform also evaluates entire interview systems, scrutinizing how the sequence of questions might influence the responses. CogBias AI goes one step further with its functionality, featuring the provision to calculate a 'Quality Score'. This score allows users to comprehend how their questions might affect the caliber of insights they gain, thus improving the efficiency of their research. In simpler terms, CogBias AI acts as a cognitive bias co-pilot, steering users through the process of question creation and bias detection, all while offering one-click rephrasing solutions to mitigate bias and maximize question impact.
