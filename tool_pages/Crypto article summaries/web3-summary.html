About Web3 Summary

Web3 Summary is an AI-driven summarization tool designed to help users make sense of complex crypto projects. It utilizes ChatGPT and Artificial Intelligence (AI) to break down projects into easy-to-understand explanations, eliminating the need to decipher jargon. With Web3 Summary, users can gain insights into Non-Fungible Tokens (NFTs), DeFi projects, tokenomics, economic models, and performance metrics, all without having to deal with confusing language. The tool was created by Manav Kalra, who continues to work on improvements. It is available as an extension and users can leave reviews if they like it. Web3 Summary also uses cookies from Google to deliver its services and analyze traffic.
