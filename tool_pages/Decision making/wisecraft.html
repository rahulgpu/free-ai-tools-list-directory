About Wisecraft

Wisecraft is an AI tool designed to provide instant critical feedback, aiding in the improvement of thinking, writing, and reflecting. Available as a Google Chrome extension, it utilizes selected mental models to analyze highlighted text on web pages. The tool is equipped with six key mental models: First Principles Thinking, Second-Order Thinking, Inversion, Occam's Razor, Opportunity Costs, and 6 Thinking Hats. Users are encouraged to apply these models as part of critical self-reflection or to generate insightful feedback for others. Every interaction with text can thus be leveraged as an opportunity to create and transmit great ideas more effectively. The tool requires a personal OpenAI Key to operate as per the current version. Additionally, Wisecraft maintains a collaborative stance, inviting users to suggest additional models via email, potentially expanding the range of mental models accessible in the tool. The overarching vision of the tool is geared towards democratization of access to the best frameworks, principles, and mental models to empower everyone in their thinking and communication processes.
