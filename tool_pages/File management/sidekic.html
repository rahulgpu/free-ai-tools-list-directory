About Sidekic

Sidekic AI Extension is a dynamic tool designed for web users to effectively manage and organize web information resources. As a Chrome extension, Sidekic AI aids users in saving everything from everywhere on the web through its smart bookmarking feature. It is developed with AI capabilities to tag, sort, and organize your resources, thereby making it easy for users to find and share saved resources. One of the standout features of Sidekic AI is the creation of 'Starter Pacs', auto-assembling collections that users can share their best resources in. Users can also capture screenshots and save them to Sidekic. With its Sidekic Vision, users can automatically tag and extract information from images. A dark mode feature is also available for an alternative viewing experience. The tool also has capabilities to automatically scrape, tag, and save any visited webpage into Sidekic. This extension can significantly enhance a user's productivity and efficiency in managing web resources. The developer of Sidekic AI is committed to privacy, ensuring that user data is not sold to third parties or used for unrelated purposes.
