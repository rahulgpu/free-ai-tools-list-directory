About ViableView

ViableView is an AI-powered tool designed to facilitate data-driven decision making for entrepreneurs. Its main function is to analyze market and product data and provide metrics projecting profitability and competition in various niches. Focused on providing actionable insights, ViableView collects data on numerous products and services, which it then feeds into a Viability Simulation. This simulation mimics real-world processes to estimate potential profits and margins within current market conditions.In addition to forecasting revenue and cost margins, the tool tracks historical trends, offering insights into the market dynamics over time. It can produce reports that display the total revenue, the total addressable market (TAM), and the serviceable addressable market (SAM), among other factors, enabling deeper, more precise market analysis. ViableView also offers real-time market tracking, opportunity-finding tools, and comprehensive market overview features. These capabilities allow users to stay aware of current trends, identify new product opportunities, and gain a detailed understanding of the market dynamics, respectively.The tool is equipped to analyze both digital and physical products and has future plans to extend its capabilities to SaaS, mobile apps, and real estate sectors. This expansion aims to extend the tools benefits to a wider range of entrepreneurs and industries.ViableView's data is sourced from publicly-available information and proprietary algorithms, improving the accuracy of projections over time. While the tools predictions are based on ideal market conditions, the reliability of these predictions is influenced by the individual actions of the seller.
