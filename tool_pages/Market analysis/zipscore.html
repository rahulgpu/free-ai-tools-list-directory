About Zipscore

Zipscore.ai is an AI co-pilot tool for market analysis and location planning. It provides the ability to explore comprehensive census data and ZIP Code maps across various geographical details. The tool is built on OpenAI's GPT-4, utilizing recent data from 75,000 US locations.One of its key features is the ability to draw polygons or paths for target locations, which can be shared among team members for collaboration in real-time. It also offers fast filtering capabilities, allowing users to drag and drop the target audience on the map and obtain ZIP codes with the highest percentile of chosen demographics for a specific country.Zipscore.ai also enables users to understand demographics by geo-fencing the ZIP codes within a certain distance of their physical store location or point of interest. This can be particularly useful for retail outlet chains or businesses catering to specific age groups, household types, or income levels.The tool provides insights and functionalities for targeting ZIP codes in advertising platforms like Google Ads and Facebook Ads, allowing marketers and advertisers to customize their content or ads to specific audiences based on ZIP codes.Overall, Zipscore.ai offers comprehensive market analysis and location planning capabilities, powered by AI technology and extensive demographic data. It is designed to assist businesses in identifying target market areas efficiently and effectively.
