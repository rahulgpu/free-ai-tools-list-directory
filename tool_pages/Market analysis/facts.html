About &facts

&facts is a market insights platform primarily constructed for consumer brands. It conducts deep analysis on a variety of market and product signals, providing real-time updates about shifting consumer demands. Used as a tool for anticipating trends, &facts aids brands in identifying product opportunities, streamlining product launch plans, and optimizing marketing strategies. Featuring detailed market and product insights, the platform enables brands to decipher market dynamics and consumer trends with elaborate insight features. It aids not just in new product development but also in business optimisation, competitor analysis and international expansion by providing access to expansive and fresh data. A significant aspect of &facts is its real-time data and its ability to uncover vital information about customer preferences, needs, and pain points without the need for complicated research processes. The platform also validates innovative ideas and reduces risks related to investment. Businesses can use &facts to comprehend their position in the market landscape, thereby allowing them to design strategies that differentiate their brand while also identifying growth areas with data-driven analysis. The platform's data engine conducts AI-driven processing to deliver actionable insights quickly and efficiently. These insights facilitate understanding of consumer behaviour and preferences, presenting potential opportunities. With an advanced AI, &facts surfaces insights that other tools might miss, continually monitors the market with real-time data, and the user-friendly, self-serve platform avoids the need for consultants.
