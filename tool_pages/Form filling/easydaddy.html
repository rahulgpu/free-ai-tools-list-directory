About EasyDaddy

EasyDaddy is a tool designed to significantly simplify the process of filling out online forms. To do this, users create a profile containing their personal details. This profile is then used by the tool to automatically fill out forms on the web, saving users time and hassle. The AI technology utilized allows for personalized and intelligent form completion, with answers tailored to match the values and interests of each individual site. EasyDaddy is not static, but evolves with every form submission, enhancing the accuracy and relevance of future responses. The tool also has the unique feature of understanding the specific contexts of each website, allowing it to perfectly tailor user responses to match these individual requests. An additional feature allows users to attach a file with the necessary information for form fills, simplifying the process further. Users also have the capability to track their history, where they can review, update, export, and share the status of an application. This tool is beneficial for professionals and other individuals who deal with frequent online form submissions, providing a streamlined and efficient process. While there is a cost associated with using EasyDaddy, it offers remarkable convenience, making it a valuable tool for managing online forms.
