About Unifill AI

Unifill AI is an AI-powered tool designed to enhance online form-filling. It aims to simplify workflows by learning from the initial manual data entry to automate repetitive tasks. The tool handles the entire form-filling process, improving efficiency, and reducing errors. To use Unifill AI, one needs to create an account and download the extension. Once installed and the suitable form identified, the tool fills the form on behalf of the user. Unifill AI's key features include quick form filling, custom form creation, secure and reliable operation, and a capability for bulk upload, useful for enterprise users. Unifill AI has a range of subscription plans tailored to meet the needs of various users from individual users to small businesses and enterprises. For user convenience, Unifill AI offers a free trail during its beta phase. The tool underscores data security employing robust encryption protocols and stringent privacy measures. Though unavailable on mobile devices, Unifill can handle various forms including job applications, exam registrations, and surveys. Customization options allowing creation of forms with fields tailored to specific user requirements are also available. The tool also supports forms from different parts of the world. The tool accepts a variety of payment methods for subscriptions and the number of forms a user can fill depends on their chosen subscription plan.
