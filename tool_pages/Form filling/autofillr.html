About Autofillr

Autofillr is an Artificial Intelligence (AI) powered Chrome extension designed to automatically and accurately fill out web forms, thus boosting efficiency and minimizing manual entry errors. AI technology is used to understand and learn the structure of various web forms, generating filling rules in real time to ensure precise fill-out. While traditional autofill tools focus mainly on basic fields like names and addresses, Autofillr strives to fill a wider range of fields across diverse web forms. The tool is continuously upgraded to deliver an increasingly streamlined and efficient online experience for users. In addition, Autofillr demonstrates a commitment to user privacy. In particular, it does not send personal data to its servers, and no one at Autofillr can access any user data stored locally on individual devices. This makes for a secure experience, as well as a user-friendly one. Suitable for a variety of online tasks, this tool proves to be a reliable and efficient companion for seamless browsing experiences.
