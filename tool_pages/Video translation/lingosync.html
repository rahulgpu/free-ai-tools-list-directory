About Lingosync

LingoSync is an AI-powered tool built for translating video content into multiple languages effectively. It provides seamless translations with voice-overs for an engaging experience across diverse audiences. The tool has been designed to unlock new markets for businesses and ensure broader international reach. It supports translations in over 40 languages, making it a versatile solution for global content creators. The handling of LingoSync is straightforward as it only requires the user to upload their video, select the target language, and download the translated video. The video translation process is tailored to save costs, time and extend a user's reach. Plus, LingoSync provides customization options, such as selecting from over 220 voices and adjusting the translated text before it is converted to speech. The translation process is automatic, but users have the option to manually adjust any text inaccuracies due to automated translation. LingoSync also ensures data security, not evaluating or passing your video content on to third parties. Developed by FYYN.IO, its fundamental aim is to simplify the conventional video translation process and make it more efficient, aiming to dominate the global market.
