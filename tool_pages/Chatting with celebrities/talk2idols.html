About Talk2idols

talk2idols is an AI chatbot available on Google Play that allows users to engage in conversations with famous personalities or fictional characters. Powered by ChatGPT, it utilizes advanced text-to-speech and speech-to-text features to facilitate natural voice conversations. With talk2idols, users can immerse themselves in dialogue with their idols, whether they are real-world celebrities or beloved characters from movies or books.The app incorporates a custom memory feature, ensuring that conversations remain in context. The chatbot can recall previous messages, allowing for continuity and an authentic experience. It stays in character, enabling realistic and engaging interactions.To use the chatbot, an API key from OpenAI is required, as indicated in the app's instructions menu. This key comes with an associated cost per 750 words. Additionally, to enable the optional text-to-speech feature offered by Google Cloud, users must follow detailed steps provided in the instructions (free tier - no cost).It is worth noting that the talk2idols app does not collect or track user data. For added functionality, users have the option to enable internet access using a Google custom search engine API Key, which is stored locally on the device.Overall, talk2idols offers a unique opportunity to engage in voice chats with beloved idols and cherished fictional characters. Users can explore extraordinary conversations and fulfill their dream of directly connecting with their idols.
