About Yourfriends

yourfriends.ai is an AI-based tool designed to foster interactive communication with AI WhatsApp contacts. Users can ask queries without any charges, facilitating learning in an innovative manner. The platform provides a selection of AI personalities, which are simulations of well-known personalities to chat with. AI characters range from personalities such as Einstein, Elon Musk, to Santa. Users can have lifelike and in-depth conversations with these characters, adding an entertaining and educative angle to the communication. Each AI character has its unique responses, providing users with different perspectives for their questions. They can be used as personal chat bots in a privacy-compliant messenger. It is important to note that these characters are unofficial AI chatbots and are not officially connected with the real-world personalities they are named after. Also, it's emphasized that the tool does not hold any liability for any potential damage due to the advice attained from the chats, particularly in relation to health, finances, and politics. The tool is available in various languages including English, French, and German.
