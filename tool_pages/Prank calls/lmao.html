About LMAO

LMAO is an Artificial Intelligence powered prank calling application aimed at injecting humor into daily interactions. The cornerstone of this is the comprehensive range of customizable prompts which allow users to create unique scenarios, ensuring each prank call is both convincing and entertaining. It offers dynamic text-to-speech options and voice modulation features for greater customization. A highlight of the app is its large selection of different voices, including various accents, tones, and character impersonations, further enhancing the authenticity and amusement of the prank calls. To help users keep track of their antics, it provides a feature in the form of comprehensive call logs. Users can review, manage, and replay each prank call with ease. Moreover, the application includes a voice recording feature which allows for the documentation of prank calls which can be saved, replayed, and shared for continued amusement. User testimonies suggest that the voices used in the app are highly realistic which helps to maintain the believability of the prank calls.
