About Privacy Observer

Privacy Observer is an AI-powered tool designed to automate the scanning of privacy policies on websites. Its purpose is to make privacy more accessible by saving users the time and effort of manually reading lengthy privacy policy documents that may contain up to 5,000 words. By using Privacy Observer, users can easily determine if websites require excessive amounts of personal information from their visitors.The tool operates as a browser extension, constantly observing and analyzing the privacy practices of the websites users visit. It provides users with a score and detailed information about the privacy practices of each website, ensuring that users are well-informed about the potential implications of sharing their personal data.Privacy Observer offers unlimited background scans, allowing users to continuously monitor privacy policies without any restrictions. The tool also includes anonymous checks by humans, ensuring that the evaluation process remains objective and unbiased.The pricing for Privacy Observer is set at $9.99 per month and comes with a money-back guarantee. Additionally, users who support the project on BuyMeACoffee will receive a 50% discount on the premium extension once it is launched.Overall, Privacy Observer aims to empower users to make informed decisions about their privacy by automating the process of scanning privacy policies and providing detailed insights into website practices.
