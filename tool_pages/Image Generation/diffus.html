About Diffus

Diffus is a Stable Diffusion image generator designed for both professional creators and enthusiasts. It offers precision and control for unique image creation, enabling users to effortlessly tailor and refine their artworks. Users can segment the canvas into distinct areas and apply different prompts to each section, offering precise control over the composition. Diffus also provides easy editing features for manipulating lighting, shadows, and color schemes. Users can leverage a library of over 10,000 Checkpoints and Loras as well as popular community extensions to refine the style, character, and objects in their images. In addition, a feature called ControlNet allows users to design lighting using masks and easily change the image's color with straightforward prompts. This tool is designed to elevate creativity and streamline the artistic process by providing tools to craft stunning images with precision and ease.
