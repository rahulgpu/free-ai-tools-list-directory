About SoulGem

SoulGem is an AI-based tool that allows users to transform their personality into art. Through a brief personality test, which takes cues from the Myers-Briggs Type Indicator, users are provided with an interpreted presentation of their personality. As a next step, they can generate a unique piece of AI-generated art, called a SoulGem, which visually represents their personality traits. Shapes, colors, and styles used in the artwork correspond to users' characteristics as interpreted from the test results. Users can choose to share their artworks and insights with their friends, further enhancing the interactive aspect of the tool. Additionally, users can personalize their spaces with the art they created, such as using the images for wallpapers. Though the personality test is intended to be fun and entertaining rather than for professional or scientific use, it offers an engaging way to express oneself. The initial offer includes generating up to two images for free. Supporter packages are available for additional or higher resolution images.
