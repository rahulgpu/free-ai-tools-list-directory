About PromoAI Content Planner

PromoAI Content Planner is a tool designed to automate the process of creating, publishing, and scheduling brand-consistent videos for various social media platforms. Offering a wide scope of functionality, it caters to different industries, marketing needs, and e-commerce platforms. It includes features for creating videos focused on a daily calendar, and for platforms like Facebook, Instagram, and YouTube, including YouTube intro videos. Industries that can benefit include Real Estate, HR, Fitness, Fashion, Restaurants, and Travel sectors. PromoAI Content Planner also supports a variety of marketing video types, such as Facebook Story Ads, Instagram Ads, LinkedIn Video Ads, YouTube Ads, and Promo Videos. For e-commerce platforms, it offers tools for creating e-commerce and Amazon video ads.Moreover, the tool comes with a selection of templates that are currently trending. These range from holiday templates to motivational and inspirational templates, birthday greetings, and listicles. Particularly fitting for sales and promotions, it includes templates for short ads and slideshows across various industries.Compatibility with social media formats is extensive, accommodating Facebook, Instagram, TikTok, YouTube, and vertical formats for stories. It provides different formats like meme videos, 6-second videos, YouTube intros, and quote or inspirational videos.PromoAI Content Planner is not only a promotional video maker, but it also serves as a video ad maker and a YouTube Intro maker.
