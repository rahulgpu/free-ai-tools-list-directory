About Aivoov

AiVOOV is a text-to-speech generator tool that allows users to convert their text into speech using realistic AI voices. The tool offers over 900+ voices across 125+ languages, making it suitable for various global users. Users can easily download their converted text as MP3 or WAV files in a matter of seconds. AiVOOV is designed to provide a professional and captivating audio experience without the expenses and hassle of traditional voiceover services. The platform utilizes cutting-edge text-to-audio technology powered by AI voices, ensuring that projects sound high-quality and engaging. The tool supports a wide range of languages and accents, allowing users to create natural-sounding speech in more than 125 languages and accents. This feature eliminates the need for a separate translation tool, saving time and effort.AiVOOV offers several use cases, including audio articles, YouTube videos, IVR systems, marketing content, IoT, and podcasts. The tool aims to enhance accessibility, audience reach, and user engagement across different media platforms.Furthermore, AiVOOV stands out with its user-friendly interface and easy-to-understand functionalities. It offers powerful features such as text-to-speech, audio-to-text, SRT generation, project management, audio file merging, and background voice customization.In terms of pricing, AiVOOV provides flexible plans, allowing users to pay monthly or yearly based on their usage needs. The tool offers different package options with varying character limits, voice options, storage capacity, and additional features like podcast hosting and commercial use.Overall, AiVOOV is a cost-effective and efficient solution for generating high-quality AI voices for various applications, catering to a diverse range of languages and accents.
