About TTSAI by ENTD

TTSAI (Text to Speech AI) is an AI tool developed by ENTD that converts textual content to voice. It features multiple languages and voices, supporting over 80 languages with various voice types. Both standard voice and AI voice, also referred to as Neural Voice, are available. The standard voice tends towards a lower cost conversion while the AI voice is designed to produce more fluent voices. The TTSAI platform caters to individual and enterprise needs through a flexible pricing model, offering pay-as-you-go, package, and subscription options. Users can utilize this tool for diverse purposes including e-learning, telephony and call center applications, content creation, support for disabled individuals, voiceovers and video creation, as well as internationalization of text content. This could be especially useful in fields such as online course creation or customer service. Continuing updates for the tool are mentioned, indicating an emphasis on consistent growth and improvement. It aims to facilitate more immersive and accessible content across various mediums, supporting individuals who have difficulty speaking and hearing, and it offers opportunities to bring unique voiceovers to video content.
