About Voicer

Voicer is a browser plugin that uses Text-to-Speech (TTS) technology to convert web text into audible speech. Once installed on your browser, Voicer can help alleviate reading fatigue by orally presenting the content of any web page. The user interface is simple to use. Users only need to highlight the text they desire to listen to and right-click to select the 'Read This' option in the menu. The extension will then translate the selected text into natural-sounding speech through a neat player interface. Voicer offers a groundbreaking method for individuals to consume online content in a relaxing and immersive manner. As a reputable browser extension with a record of no violations, Voicer is also committed to respecting users' privacy. It is important to note that the use of Voicer involves the handling of personally identifiable information and authentication information.
