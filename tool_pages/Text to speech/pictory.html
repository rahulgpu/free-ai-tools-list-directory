About Pictory

Pictory is an AI-powered tool designed to facilitate easy and efficient video creation and editing. It allows users of all skill ranges to create professional-quality videos using text, with no need for software downloads or technical skills. The service targets various professions, including course creators, business professionals, content creators, educational professionals, social media managers and marketing teams. Pictory features script-to-video functionality, enabling the conversion of scripts into videos replete with AI voices, matching footage, and music. It also offers a blog-to-video feature, transforming written blog posts into engaging videos to enhance SEO and decrease bounce rates. Additionally, Pictory can transform long-form videos, such as webinars and podcasts, into digestible, branded clips ideal for social media. The platform also incorporates automatic captions, improving reach and watch time. Pictory is also designed to encourage collaboration, with features that enable sharing of assets and ideas within teams or with external partners.
