About TTSLabs

TTSLabs is an AI Text-to-Speech service dedicated to streamers, particularly those on Twitch. It gives streamers the ability to enhance their streaming experience by customizing their Text-to-Speech alerts. Key features include custom voices and the option to add unique sound clips to Text-to-Speech notifications, which can considerably enrich viewer experience. A dedicated desktop application facilitates seamless management and playback, and there is easy customization of prices, voices, sound clips and other parameters. Furthermore, the service allows for faster than real-time audio processing. There is a handy guide for viewers where they can check enabled alerts, voices, and minimum values for Text-to-Speech. The application can be synchronized with Streamlabs or StreamElements, thereby easing the control of Text-to-Speech donations through the user's dashboard. Additionally, TTSLabs enables streamers to manage which donations come through with preset levels of profanity and custom filters. The service offers a variety of voices created using speech synthesis to artificially produce human-like speech. It allows streamers to elevate their content creativity and offer an interactive platform for their viewers.
