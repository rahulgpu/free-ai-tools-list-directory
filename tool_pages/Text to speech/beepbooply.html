About Beepbooply

beepbooply is a text-to-speech platform that uses Artificial Intelligence to convert written text into realistic and natural sounding speech. The platform supports over 80 languages and offers a choice from more than 900 voices. Potential applications range from voiceovers for videos, podcast narration, to multilingual customer service interactions. The service is designed to be scalable, enabling creation of high volume audio content rapidly. The voices are sourced from leading tech companies including Google, Microsoft, and Amazon. beepbooply allows users to adjust settings such as pacing, pitch, volume, and speaking styles to fit individual needs and preferences. Users select a voice, type in the text they want converted into speech, and generate the audio with a single click. The audio can be subsequently saved, listened to, or downloaded. The platform also offers different pricing tiers to meet various user needs, from free limited access for casual or hobby use, to different levels of prepaid monthly and yearly subscriptions for more extensive usage or commercial purposes. The higher the subscription level, the more characters per month can be converted.
