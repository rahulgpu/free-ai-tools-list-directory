About Woord

Woord is a Text-to-Speech (TTS) tool that allows users to convert text into natural-sounding audio. This technology is able to read any website content aloud, turning web content into accessible speech. It offers users a variety of voices to choose from, that vary by language, gender, and accent. Woord supports over 30 languages, including regional variations such as Canadian French or Brazilian Portuguese. Users have the flexibility to convert any text content they wish, ranging from blog posts to research papers. Woord's smart voice technology utilizes AI to create high-quality, human-like speech, which can be used for a range of purposes, from YouTube videos to e-Learning modules. This tool also provides opportunities for redistribution, supporting MP3 download and Audio hosting with an HTML embed audio player. This allows the creation and sharing of audio files for commercial purposes. Users can start the conversion process by sharing a URL or uploading their text content directly to the platform, choose the preferred voice and listen to or download the resulting audio.
