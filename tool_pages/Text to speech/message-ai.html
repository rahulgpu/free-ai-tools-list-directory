About Message AI

Message AI - GPT TTS is an AI tool available for download on the App Store for use on iPhone, iPad, iPod touch, or Mac OS X 13.0 or later. The tool provides text-to-speech capabilities, powered by the GPT (Generative Pre-trained Transformer) model. This allows users to translate written messages into spoken audio, providing a convenient alternative for those who prefer not to read.
