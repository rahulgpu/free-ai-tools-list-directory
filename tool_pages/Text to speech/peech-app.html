About Peech App

Peech is an AI tool designed to transform text into immersive audio, narrated with a human-like quality. Its main audience extends from individuals to publishers. For individuals, Peech can convert texts from web articles, e-books, or other resources into captivating audiobooks. It is particularly useful for individuals with dyslexia, ADHD, vision disabilities, or those who prefer auditory learning. Its AI-powered detection capability supports multiple languages and diverse input formats. For publishers, Peech offers an efficient solution to convert written content into high-quality audiobooks in a fraction of time and cost than traditional production. It supports various languages and provides an immersive listening experience with diverse voice characters. The tool can also analyze the content for voice selection and is capable of narrating the content from images. Peech also extends its use to businesses, helping to broaden their audience reach by turning textbooks, web novels, fanfictions, and more into easily accessible audiobooks.
