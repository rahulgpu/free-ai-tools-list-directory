About TTSMaker

TTSMaker is a free online text-to-speech tool that provides speech synthesis services in over 100 languages and a wide variety of voice styles. It is powered by a powerful neural network that makes the speech sound more natural. It can be used to read text aloud and convert text to audio files that can be downloaded in mp3, wav format. The tool has a character limit of 20000 characters per week, with some voices offering unlimited free use. It also provides the option to adjust the speed and volume of the speech and to insert pauses between paragraphs.
