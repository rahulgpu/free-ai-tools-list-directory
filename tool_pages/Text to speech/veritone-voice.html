About Veritone Voice

Veritone Voice is an advanced AI voice solution that offers various services to create and manage lifelike synthetic voices. The tool allows users to produce text-to-speech and speech-to-speech voice content smoothly and swiftly, catering to various industries' needs. Veritone Voice's functionalities include creating custom voice models and automating voice efforts by integrating AI in enterprise workflows. The voice models feature empowers users to generate voice-over content without the need for studio time or scheduling concerns. They can clone any voice, including celebrities, sports announcers, and public figures, given they have their consent. Moreover, the solution supports creating content on demand using either text-to-speech or speech-to-speech inputs, available in localized languages.For enterprise workflows, Veritone Voice assists in optimizing voice automation output, enhancing metadata, and generating dialogues using state-of-the-art AI capabilities. The platform also provides an API for integrating its realistic, real-time AI voice feature across various products and projects. Besides, Veritone Voice offers stock and premium synthetic voices to initiate text-to-speech projects more readily. Users can choose from an extensive range of voices, customize intonation, gender, dialect, and accent, and translate content into over 150 languages. Moreover, the tool has proven its effectiveness in expanding the reach of content to global audiences and maximizing the scale of voice content. Clients have used Veritone Voice to increase the production speed of audio descriptions, enter new markets, and reduce time and resource costs. In summary, Veritone Voice is a comprehensive solution for creating, managing, and optimizing lifelike AI voices.
