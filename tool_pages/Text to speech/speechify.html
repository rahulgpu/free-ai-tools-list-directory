About Speechify

Speechify is an artificial intelligence-based application focused on text-to-speech conversion services. The tool allows users to transform written text into spoken words across multiple platforms such as Chrome, iOS, and Android. It offers services for personal use, business and educational purposes. Users can interact with Speechify through several applications including browsers, iPhone & iPad apps, Android apps, and desktop applications for Mac users. In its key features, Speechify allows users to listen to Google docs, web articles, Gmail, Twitter, and more directly from their preferred device. For business and educational needs, it provides voice-over studio services and educational resource access. For content creators, there are tools for automatic translation and dubbing videos. With a feature like 'Voice Cloning', the tool enables creating AI-based clones of human voices in a matter of seconds. Speechify also offers transcription services where users can transcribe any video into written format. Its AI Avatars & Video feature allows users to create AI-generated videos. The tool also provides an API for developers looking to incorporate natural-sounding text-to-speech functionality into their applications or platforms. The application also offers a variety of other features, including the capacity to convert content into a voice-over, and download it in various formats such as .MP3, .OGG, or .WAV.
