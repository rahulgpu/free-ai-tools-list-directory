About Readvox

Readvox is a Text to Speech reader tool which leverages AI to produce naturally sounding voices. It is designed to convert written text into speech, helping users consume content in an audible format. The tool can be installed as a Chrome extension and it provides the functionality to listen to any text on web pages, enhancing user experience and accessibility for those who prefer audio content. In terms of features, Readvox allows users to choose their preferred voice for the text-to-speech conversion, and it offers both a 'Natural Voice' option and a standard 'System Voice' option. Furthermore, Readvox provides users with the ability to select specific text for conversion into speech, giving them more control over what they want to listen to. This tool could be particularly beneficial for users who have visual impairments, dyslexia or other conditions that make reading text challenging. It could also be useful for multitaskers who want to listen to text content while focusing on other tasks.
