About Listen2It

Listen2It is an AI-powered voice generator primarily designed to convert text to speech, generating lifelike audio files. Boasting advanced deep learning technology and visual audio editing tools, Listen2It allows users to create high-quality voiceovers, aiding in various content creation tasks. The wide range of uses includes professional voiceovers, converting articles into audio format for better engagement, integrating voice into applications through APIs, and generating podcasts without the need for physical recording. Geared towards content creators, marketers, agencies, writers, customer support managers, educators, and more, Listen2It provides advanced customizations to build a unique audio brand with lifelike pronunciation styles and natural accents. With the assistance of its studio-like editing features, voice profiles can be fine-tuned in terms of speed, pitch, emphasis, and volume. Additionally, Listen2It allows adding background audio tracks, custom pronunciation for words, multilingual voiceovers, subtitle generation for videos, and saving audio settings for each profile for future reference.
