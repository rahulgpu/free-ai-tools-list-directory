About Umbrellabird

Umbrellabird is a tool designed for product teams that simplifies the process of creating product documents from meeting recordings. It automatically generates drafts of product documents based on user-interview recordings, saving time and effort for the team. The tool integrates with popular platforms like Google Meet and Google Drive, allowing users to leverage the tools they already use for meetings and document storage. Umbrellabird's automated document generation feature transforms user interviews into actionable insights. It automatically extracts transcripts, summaries, and identifies key insights such as pains, gains, and desired outcomes. Users can also combine multiple sources to create comprehensive drafts, including product one-page briefs for stakeholder review. Exporting these documents is made easy, streamlining the workflow for product teams. The highlight of Umbrellabird is its speed. It enables users to synthesize meeting recordings into product documents within seconds, significantly accelerating the document creation process. Overall, Umbrellabird provides a practical solution for product teams to streamline their workflow by automating the generation of product documents from user-interview recordings.
