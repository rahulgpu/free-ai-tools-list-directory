About Apployal

APPLOYAL Localize is an AI-powered tool designed to help mobile app developers localize their applications for global audiences. It specializes in translating app content, descriptions, and screenshots into more than 80 languages and enhancing user experience with native language interfaces. This solution aims to boost app downloads across different regions, enhance revenue through region-specific monetization strategies, and improve store conversion rates with a localized app page. It supports both Android and iOS app localization and provides an AI-driven solution for translating release notes and app store pages. The platform also includes a Figma plugin for localizing ad creatives and logos as part of app screenshot translation. It integrates with GitHub, App Stores, Google Play, enabling streamlined workflows. The tool offers different pricing models, offering flexible solutions according to the needs and size of businesses.
