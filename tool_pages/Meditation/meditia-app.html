About Meditia App

Meditia is an Artificial Intelligence (AI) powered meditation application. It utilizes AI capabilities to deliver personalized meditations, catering to the user's specific mood and preferences. The application is designed to aid the users in relaxation and enhancing their focus. Considering diversity in users' meditation skills, Meditia offers a range of courses that caters to beginners through to advanced meditators. The highly personalized AI tool embedded in the app helps in creating custom meditations, ensuring optimal user experience and effectiveness. The app is available for download on Google Play and the AppStore. User personal information privacy is assured with a dedicated privacy policy.
