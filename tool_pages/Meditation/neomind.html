About Neomind

Neomind is an AI-powered tool that allows users to create their own personalized meditation sessions for free. By leveraging the capabilities of AI, Neomind aims to help individuals achieve a desired quality of life by reducing stress, enhancing emotional resilience, boosting focus and concentration, and promoting mental clarity. The tool offers users the opportunity to navigate and process their emotions effectively.To begin the meditation creation process, Neomind asks for the user's real name, emphasizing the importance of authenticity for a meaningful meditation experience. The tool then prompts the user to choose a goal for their meditation from options such as dealing with stress, improving sleep quality, practicing self-love, enhancing career success, forgiving oneself or others, and boosting confidence.Users can also customize the length of their meditation session, selecting either a short session lasting up to 3 minutes or a longer session lasting up to 7 minutes. Neomind allows users to choose between a male voice (Joey) or a female voice (Ruth) to guide them through their meditation practice.Before accessing the personalized meditation, users are presented with an opportunity to join the waitlist for an upcoming meditation app. They can benefit from perks such as early access, a free Emotional Empowerment book, and life-changing meditation tips and advice.The AI-generated meditation created by Neomind lasts approximately 1 minute and can be listened to directly on the website or downloaded for offline use.
