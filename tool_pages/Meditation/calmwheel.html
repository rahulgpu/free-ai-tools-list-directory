About Calmwheel

CalmWheel is an innovative application available on Google Play that aims to reduce stress and promote inner peace. It provides a safe and anonymous platform for users to share their thoughts, emotions, and experiences, creating a supportive community. The app offers various features to help users find balance and nurture their well-being, including mindful journaling and soothing guided meditations.One of the noteworthy aspects of CalmWheel is its "Talk to People Therapy," which allows users to engage in real and honest conversations without judgment or stigma. Through this AI-powered system, users can connect with empathetic AI peers who understand their struggles. This feature can be particularly beneficial for individuals feeling low, stressed, anxious, or lonely, as well as those dealing with work-life balance struggles, relationship woes, pandemic stress, and more.CalmWheel emphasizes data safety, stating that no data is shared with third parties and no data is collected from users. The app promotes a humanistic approach to mental health and well-being, empowering individuals to take control of their mental health journeys.With positive user feedback indicating that 94% of users feel better after a single chat, CalmWheel combines mental health support with AI technology to create a feel-better platform for all. Users can download the app and experience empathy, respect, and unconditional support while maintaining their autonomy and choices.CalmWheel is a startup focused on creating a safe space and matching users with relevant AI peers. The app continues to release updates to enhance the user experience and emphasizes its commitment to data privacy and security.
