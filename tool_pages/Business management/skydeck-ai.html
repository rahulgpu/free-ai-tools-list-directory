About SkyDeck AI

SkyDeck AI is a secure, business-centric AI productivity platform that facilitates the use of generative AI across organizations. It aims to enhance creativity and productivity by providing the ability to maintain control, monitor use, and protect data within businesses. The service permits the application of large language models such as ChatGPT, GPT-4, GPT-3.5, Anthropic Claude, Google Vertex AI, or even custom models.Its user-friendly chat interface is designed to be easy to navigate by both non-technical users and power users alike for enhanced productivity. Notably, SkyDeck AI enables collaboration, allowing multiple colleagues to work together on an AI conversation, build context, and invite others to participate in projects.For businesses that prioritize security, the platform provides data loss prevention, logging, single sign-on, analytics, and options for private models. It comes equipped with advanced tools including a SQL Assistant, Pair Programmer, and a feature for reviewing legal agreements.To build efficiency into operations, it allows users to schedule business processes and share results with colleagues via integrations with channels like Slack and email. SkyDeck AI also provides for the creation of custom tools tailored to specific business needs. Moreover, it provides administration, curation, and customization features to safely and securely deploy generative AI across your business.
