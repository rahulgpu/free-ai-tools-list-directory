About Wurkzen

Wurkzen is an AI-powered revenue performance and business management software designed for companies of different sizes, from small to large businesses. This platform offers a variety of features intended to optimize business operations and revenue performance. These include automated online marketing, an AI-enabled CRM for revenue management, sales management tools, and a website and sales channel builder. In addition to these, Wurkzen provides specific AI solutions like Business 360, a tool for running business operations with AI-powered insights; Deal Compass, aimed at increasing sales and enhancing sales teams' performance; Lead Magnet, a tool for boosting online presence and lead generation; and Emotion Engine, an AI tool that provides insights into customer sentiment to prevent customer churn and lost revenue. It also includes project management tools, quotes and payments handling, time management solutions and customer insights. Wurkzen aims to provide data-driven decision making with the help of AI, intending to enable businesses to run more efficiently and to increase sales value. Furthermore, Wurkzen provides online AI agents to engage with customers on websites and social media, driving them to sales channels.
