About Aginsi

Aginsi is an artificial intelligence-powered tool designed to enhance and streamline research processes. Its primary function is to assist users in summarizing, processing, and organizing their research in a single application, eliminating the need for many different tools and complex workflows. Aginsi caters to a variety of users, including academics, consultants, and journalists, addressing different types of research questions across disciplines. With features such as saving and reading all items in one app and managing and organizing research materials using AI-process tagging, users gain an efficient and effortless workflow. Aginsi allows the user to start their research within the tool and culminate in their preferred text editor, including MS Office, Notion, Docs, amongst others. Robust privacy protections are integral to Aginsi's ethos as it employs industry-standard encryption to protect users' data and maintains strict compliant with GDPR and CCPA regulations. The collected data won't be sold or shared with any third-party entities, and the tool refrains from storing or tracking any user's browsing activity.
