About GrowASO – AI App Store Optimization (ASO)

GrowASO is an AI-driven platform designed to optimize App Store Optimization (ASO) for iOS and Android applications. The platform includes a range of features geared towards improving app downloads, revenues, and rankings in app stores. This includes generating keyword optimized app-store listings which incorporate keywords related to your app and those used by competitors and similar apps. GrowASO also recommends similar apps and keywords curated from various sources for your app, as well as providing estimates for keyword traffic and keyword difficulty in any country available on the App Store or Google Play. The platform features keyword rank tracking capability, enabling you to monitor your app's keyword position in both iOS App Store and Google Play stores across numerous countries. Additionally, GrowASO supports cross-platform optimization complying with Google Play and iOS App Store guidelines such as character limits and rules. A unique feature of the platform is its 'ASO Copilot' that auto-analyzes your app's listing from its store URL and recommends optimized listing texts, descriptions, and store-specific fields to enhance visibility. Through this tool, you can discover and delve into specific keywords, monitor your app's rankings, and identify growth areas to outpace competitors. GrowASO supports both the App Store and Google Play Store. The listings generated by GrowASO are fully resellable for further commercial purposes.
