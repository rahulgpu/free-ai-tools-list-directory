About GliaStar

GliaStar is an AI-powered tool designed to animate brand mascots using text input. The tool enables users to transform the design of their mascot, either in 2D or 3D, into an animated character. The system offers a set of features that includes auto-animate facial expressions and body gestures, lip synchronization, and 360-degree rotations for a realistic overall effect. By interpreting the text input, GliaStar animates the mascot to express emotions nonverbally through a variety of facial expressions and body gestures. Additionally, the tool can identify the tonal and emotional nuances of up to five languages - English, Chinese, Japanese, Korean, and Vietnamese. It allows the creation of animations that can be employed in various applications such as presentation slides, educational and training videos, commercials, social media content, and web-based advertisements. The GliaStar system aims to facilitate in-house creation and customization of animated mascots, potentially simplifying the animation process and reducing costs for businesses.
