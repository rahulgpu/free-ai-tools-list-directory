About TheDream

TheDream.ai is an AI-powered tool that enables users to create their perfect profile picture or avatar. The tool offers a range of styles to choose from, including Fashion, Artistic, Fantasy, Movies, Futuristic, History, Illustrations, Beauty, Vision, Sculpture, Fun, and Travel. The tool utilizes state-of-the-art Dreambooth technology, which is an open-source deep learning generation model developed in 2022 that allows users to train AI models on their own photos to generate any type of image they desire. TheDream.ai requires users to upload portrait photos of themselves from their existing gallery to achieve the best results. The tool offers a video tutorial to guide users on how to upload photos and achieve the best results. The photos uploaded are deleted from the servers within 24 hours, and the AI model is deleted after ten days to ensure data privacy. TheDream.ai offers different pricing plans starting from $3.99 and allows users to share the resulting photos on social media or use them for various purposes, including personal branding and creating custom items like business cards. The platform ensures secure payment using Stripe, which uses secure encryption technology to protect user information. TheDream.ai offers a 14-day refund policy, provided users haven't trained the AI model yet.
