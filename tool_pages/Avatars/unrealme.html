About Unrealme

Unrealme FR/EN is a tool that uses AI to generate images of you. After you click the get button, you will be redirected to a secure payment interface where you can choose from two options - Get 50+ AI images surprise for 22€ or Choose my themes for 15€ (this can be offered as a gift for someone else). After you upload your photos, you will receive your images at the end (it might be in spams). The images are 512*512px, jpeg images and usually about 70% of pictures generated look like you. AI costs are expensive and refunds are not available if the error is on your side. It usually takes a few hours to get your generated images, but if you don't receive it after 24 hours, you can contact the team. If you have specific needs and suggestions, you can contact the team to discuss. Your photos will only be used to generate your images and will be deleted after 30 days.
