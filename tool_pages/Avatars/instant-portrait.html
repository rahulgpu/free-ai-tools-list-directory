About Instant Portrait

Instant Portrait is an AI-driven app that enables users to create a portrait painting of themselves. It is available for download on both Google Play and the App Store. To get the best results, users are encouraged to upload a well-lit, close-up selfie and choose the theme they prefer. New themes are added weekly, and the app is free and requires no sign-in. Additionally, users have access to a contact page and a privacy page.
