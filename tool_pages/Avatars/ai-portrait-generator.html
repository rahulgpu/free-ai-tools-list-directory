About AI Portrait Generator

The AI Avatar Generator is a robust tool that allows users to generate their own distinct art portraits in a flurry of styles. The AI-powered system requires users to upload five well-lit photos of themselves from various angles. The platform then uses a trained neural network, specifically designed for each user, to create a model based on the uploaded photos. Afterward, the tool cultivates 200 unique avatars in over 50 diverse styles, all accessible via the dashboard. The avatar images are 1024x1024 pixels, but it features the promise of an upcoming 'Upscale' function where avatars can be converted into 2048x2048 pixels. Additionally, a photo enhancer tool is in the pipeline, aiming to boost avatar quality. Users are also granted the liberty to create custom avatars by merely using prompts, text descriptors that facilitate unique avatar design based on the user's specifications. Despite this, the system discourages the use of hats and glasses in photo uploads as they hinder the efficacy of the AI model creation. Given its proprietary model, computations, and privacy, only the user has access to their trained neural network and the resulting portraits. It doesn't create dipped or masked appearances, instead, it embodies the person's features and portrays them as avatars from scratch. This tool doesn't follow a subscription model but a one-time payment making it a seamless and straightforward tool to generate appealing, personalized avatars.
