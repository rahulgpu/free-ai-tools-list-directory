About Artguru

The AI Avatar Generator, developed by Artguru, is an online tool that utilizes advanced AI models to generate highly realistic and expressive avatars and portraits. The tool offers users a variety of avatar styles to choose from, including Nostalgic Art, Cyberpunk City, Impressionista, Futura Palette, and more. Users can select their preferred style and follow a simple four-step process to create their own unique and trend-setting AI avatar.To get started, users can choose their gender and select up to 10 styles that match their preferences. They are then prompted to upload between 6 to 30 selfies showcasing different facial expressions and backgrounds. After uploading the photos, the AI model begins generating unique avatars.The AI Avatar Generator boasts automatic image enhancement, and users can generate up to 100 avatars at once. Additionally, the tool is user-friendly and accessible to all. The online platform allows users to explore dozens of AI avatar and portrait styles, giving them the freedom to personalize their avatar to their liking.Although the price is not specified, users can create AI avatars using their own selfies for free. The tool also provides answers to commonly asked questions, such as what kind of photos should be uploaded for accurate AI avatars and how long it takes to generate them. The AI Avatar Generator can be accessed online and through the Pica AI Magic Avatar App, available on the App Store and Google Play.
