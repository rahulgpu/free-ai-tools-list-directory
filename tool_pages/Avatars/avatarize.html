About Avatarize

Avatarize is an AI-powered tool that enables users to convert their images into unique avatars. The solution utilizes GPT-4 Vision and DALL-E 3 technologies to deliver this functionality. Users can choose from more than 50 distinctive avatar styles, and have unlimited access to their avatar's historical data. The tool generates unique avatars and allows users to save and download their creations in high resolution. Users can obtain a specific number of 'credits' to generate new avatars. A point of consideration is that the outputs' accuracy may vary depending on diverse factors. The technology being relatively new, the output quality is not guaranteed. As it concerns data security, the system deletes user images within 24 hours and assured that they are not shared with any external entity. The platform does not offer refunds but recommends using free credits for testing before a purchase is made. Support can be reached via email.
