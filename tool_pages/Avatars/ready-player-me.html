About Ready Player Me

Ready Player Me is a comprehensive developer tool that enables the rapid integration of a customizable avatar system into digital games or applications. The tool is highly valued by developers for its capability to provide users with high-quality, personalized avatars, leading to increased user engagement and retention. Its robust Avatar Creator allows the creation of unique avatars, through the use of extensive customization options, starting from a base character designed from a user selfie. The tool facilitates the design of custom themes, allowing for further personalization. The offering also includes Open Source SDKs to facilitate the seamless integration of the avatar system into live or under development apps or games, accommodating different platforms such as Android, iOS, Unity, Unreal Engine, WebGL, and React. The Developer Studio feature of Ready Player Me allows developers to configure the Avatar Creator, create unique designs using in-house AI content tools, and access hundreds of assets, including those from leading brands and IP. The platform also offers monetization tools for developers, allowing avatars and skins to be sold to users with ease of integration into existing payment systems. Further, it extends an opportunity to reach a broad audience of players across thousands of games.
