About AiShot

AiShot is an AI-powered tool designed to transform everyday selfies into professional-grade headshots. The tool operates within WhatsApp, utilizing a chat-based interface where users engage in a simple conversation with an AI assistant. Users initiate the process by sending basic photos to the WhatsApp bot. The artificial intelligence then begins training, learning from the shots provided by the user. Once training is completed, the tool lets you generate enhanced images by simply sending text commands, such as 'imagine me as a supermodel'. If a user is not satisfied, AiShot offers a full refund guaranteeing an almost perfect match between the original and the generated image. Its pricing and delivery model is both straightforward and efficient with custom and default image options. No separate application download or signup is required; the entire procedure takes place within the user's existing WhatsApp platform. In addition to the AI features, AiShot also places emphasis on customer service, with a dedicated support system for inquiries and clarifications.
