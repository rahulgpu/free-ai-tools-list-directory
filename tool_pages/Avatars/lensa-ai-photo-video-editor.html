About Lensa AI: photo & video editor

Developed by Prisma Labs, Lensa AI is an intuitive photo and video editing tool, designed specifically for perfecting selfies. The tool is available for iOS as well as MacOS, granting users a simple, yet effective way of beautifying their images. Capabilities of the app include facial retouching, skin smoothing, face highlighting, and background blurring to focus more on the subject. It offers an impeccable eyes feature to eliminate eye bags and enhance eyebrows and eyes for a more distinguished look. The app is also equipped to adjust the background blur and depth to complement the subject and add dynamics to selfies. Extra perks include an Auto Adjust feature for users short on time and various elements related to altering exposure, color intensity, saturation, sharpness, fade effects, and temperature for creating mood-specific and personalized selfies. The Lensa AI tool does have Premium Subscriptions for additional features and functionalities. Users can refine their selfies to their preference with this app, although the app's automatic detection of features including faces, foreground, and background may be limited in certain scenarios.
