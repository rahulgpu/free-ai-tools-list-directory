About Mayo Studio

Mayo Studio - AI Headshot is an AI-powered application designed to transform selfies into stunning, studio-quality headshots. This tool allows users to create professional profile pictures suitable for LinkedIn profiles, generate business portraits for corporate accounts, and produce casual headshots for personal social media accounts. Users simply select 10 photos from their camera roll, specify their preferred style ranging from professional to casual, and let the AI technology of the Mayo Studio generate high-quality headshots. Once the images are processed, users can apply filters to enhance the results and keep the image they find most appealing. Moreover, the app offers an option to remix their photos to create unique images by combining an initial portrait with an inspiration picture. The platform features an intuitive interface and provides high-quality results straight from your smartphone. A purchase may be necessary within the app to generate a batch of headshots. The app is a powerful resource for anyone looking to upgrade their professional or personal photo presence on digital platforms.
