About beb.ai

beb.ai is an artificial intelligence (AI) tool designed to create dynamic creative content for brands. With an emphasis on accessibility for those without design skills, the tool facilitates the creation of a variety of content types including social media posts, pattern interrupt ads, marketing campaigns, dynamic marketing materials, and holiday cards. beb.ai utilizes a simple, two-step process to generate content. Users begin by uploading a collection of 20-30 photos, which could include a variety of locations, angles, facial expressions, and emotions. The AI generator then takes the uploaded content and undergoes a learning process to understand the unique features of your product. This learning process could take up to 24 hours and once completed, it can generate a substantial batch of curated photos in nine different themes on various backgrounds every week. beb.ai boasts endless creative possibilities, simplifying and enhancing the process of content creation for users of all skill levels.
