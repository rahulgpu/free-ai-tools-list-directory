About Blockode

Blockode AI Photo Studio is a tool that enables users to generate custom photoshoots using artificial intelligence. Users start by uploading a range of selfies from different angles, which the tool uses as a basis to create a unique 'studio'. Users can then exercise their creativity by writing prompts that the AI uses to generate a variety of images. This tool caters to a vast array of styles and themes, as indicated by the range of prompts available, such as 'viking', 'hobbit', 'cyberpunk', 'zombie' and many more. Images generated can be exported in 4K resolution and are available for commercial use under the terms of the licensing agreement. Besides generating images, the tool also provides AI prompt assists, enhancing user creativity. The resulting images can be shared publicly in a gallery, with the option for sponsorship. Note, while using this tool, users may be required to purchase services to cover the computational resources required for creating a custom-trained model and generating images.
