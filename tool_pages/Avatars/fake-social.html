About Fake Social

Fake Social is a unique AI tool that enables users to create and share AI-generated content based on their personal images, allowing for a new and interactive kind of social experience. The tool requires inputs such as a selfie, username, gender, and friends list from its users to create distinctive profile pages and generate daily content.Users need to upload a good quality selfie with a clear, neutral expression, which acts as the basis for the daily AI-generated content. The user's personal information, including their chosen username and selected gender, helps in developing a unique profile page. Adding friends into the platform not only allows users to view their friends' daily content but also provides an opportunity to create collaborative content. Every day, users can look forward to new content, following a theme set for a week and changing after midnight Pacific Time daily. The theme ends every Sunday at midnight PT, creating an exciting and dynamic environment on the platform. Fake Social extends beyond typical social media applications by leveraging AI in generating inventive, custom content and offers a new enjoyable way for friends and family to interact.
