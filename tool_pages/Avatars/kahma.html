About Kahma

Kahma.io is an AI-powered tool that generates digital portraits and avatars from source images, such as real-life photos. By providing at least five source images, users can create personalized, lifelike digital twins of themselves, their pets, or loved ones, even ones who have passed away. The AI algorithm generates unique portraits and avatars based on the provided source materials, capturing the unique personality and style of the subject. The resulting images are available in 8K quality—a high definition resolution standard—and can be used for personal keepsakes, social media profiles, or professional projects. In addition to generating digital twins, Kahma.io also generates stunning selfies that are transformed by AI technology.The service is available through a membership subscription, with various pricing plans and options. Users can sign up for a free trial with the option of free cancellation, and membership fees are charged on a monthly or yearly basis. Users can easily download their generated images once they are created, and there are no limitations on the use of the images. Overall, Kahma.io offers a unique and accessible way to create digital twins or bring loved ones back to life through a combination of AI technology and source images.
