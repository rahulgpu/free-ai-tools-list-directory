About Avaturn

Avaturn is a tool designed to convert 2D images into realistic and recognizable 3D avatars. It leverages generative AI technology to create life-like avatars from photos, with applications found in games, apps, and digital marketing campaigns. These avatars can then be customized further with different options for body types, hairstyles, clothing, and accessories. The tool provides a wide range of options for avatar customization, ensuring a unique look for each avatar created. Avaturn also allows users to export their created avatars as 3D models which can then be loaded into various 3D environments such as Blender, Unity, Unreal Engine, Maya, and Cinema4D. Additionally, these avatars are animation-ready and come with standard humanoid body rig, ARKit blendshapes, and visemes, making them compatible with Mixamo animations and VTubing software. For developers, Avaturn offers SDK integration for use within their applications or computational universe. This unique AI technology assists in turning gamers into in-game characters while retaining their actual identity.
