About Partly

Partly is an AI-based tool that facilitates the transformation of user photos into unique art pieces. Using advanced AI techniques, it modifies and elevates ordinary photos into digital artworks. Initially designed and made in Sweden, it has crafted thousands of artworks and has a significant gallery of featured work, demonstrating its proficiency and wide usage. Beyond just applying visual filters, Partly seems to leverage more sophisticated AI operations to create artworks which are a combination of user inputs and AI generation. Partly also entertains art commissions, suggesting a level of customization beyond automatic transformations. Another notable aspect of this tool is its pronounced focus on digital privacy and security, adhering to the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). It follows secure payment protocols and ensures user data encryption. However, the exact specifics of its operational process are not spelled out, invoking curiosity about how it generates magical art from user photos.
