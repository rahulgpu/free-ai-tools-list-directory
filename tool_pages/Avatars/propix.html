About ProPix

The ProPix: AI Profile App is a profile photo creation tool designed to allow users to stand out with unique, trendy and stylish designs. Aimed at creating stunning profile photos, the app essentially offers 'PC or Photoshop level' editing capabilities with a user-friendly one-tap feature. Users simply select the template that appeals to them and choose the photo they wish to edit. This app is completely ad-free with a small fee required to unlock all available effects in the Pro version. Computing and processing for the photo effects occur within the users device, not on the cloud, making it a secure and privacy-friendly tool. An overarching feature of the ProPix app is its dedication to offering users modern and constantly updating templates, enabling users to easily refresh their profile photos across all their social media accounts. Beneficially, no data is collected by the developer from the app, a privacy policy that ensures secure usage. This app is available in English and is compatible with devices that operate on iOS 14.0 or later.
