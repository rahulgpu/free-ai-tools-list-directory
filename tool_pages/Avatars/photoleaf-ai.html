About PhotoLeaf AI

PhotoLeaf AI is an online tool designed to generate photorealistic AI-based avatars. Users upload photographs of themselves from multiple angles, and the AI model processes these to generate striking profile photos suitable for use in various contexts like social media profiles, dating apps and professional networks. The AI offers a selection of over 50 styles to choose from, such as Color Sketch, Black and White Drawing, Futuristic City, and more. The final result consists of more than 300 images, allowing users to select their preferred ones. If not satisfied with the generated images, PhotoLeaf AI offers a retraining option on a new set of images. The entire process takes between 2-6 hours, depending on processing power availability. Notably, the platform puts a great emphasis on user data protection, deleting all uploaded photos and generated models after 48 hours unless asked by the user to keep them for future use.
