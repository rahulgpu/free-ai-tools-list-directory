About AI Profile Pictures

AI Profile Pictures offers the ability to generate personalized profile pictures using artificial intelligence. The whole process begins with the purchase of credits that enable the processing and creation of over 200 images. Users are then prompted to upload between 10 to 20 photos of themselves or the intended subject of the pictures. A broad variety of photos including close-ups, different poses, facial expressions, backgrounds, and lighting conditions are preferred to ensure the diverse and realistic output. However, the tool advises against using identical expressions, settings or the inclusion of multiple people per photo for optimal results. Nude or sexually explicit content is also expressly prohibited. Following the photo submissions, the AI processes the images and creates the finalized profile pictures within an estimated timeframe of 2 to 3 hours. The generated photos are then sent to the user via email. Users who are unsatisfied with the result obtained after the initial generation can request for further iterations within a 7-day period to ensure satisfaction. Due to the costs associated with training the AI model, refunds are not extended once photos have been submitted.
