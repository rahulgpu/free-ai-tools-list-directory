About Formula Dog

Formula Dog is a tool that transforms textual instructions into Excel formulas or VBA Code. It utilises artificial intelligence to process the instructions and generate the required formula or code instantly. This tool effectively turns complicated spreadsheet problems into simple solutions by generating formulas. In addition to creating Excel formulas, this tool also excels in the generation of VBA code in accordance with the user's text instructions. The tool is accessible for trial purposes and offers features laid out on a publicly accessible roadmap. This roadmap provides transparency on planned updates and improvements. Support options are available for the users in case of any issues or queries including various contact methods. The tool is developed and maintained by Real Craft Tech Private Limited.
