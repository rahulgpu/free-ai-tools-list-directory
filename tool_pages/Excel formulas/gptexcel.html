About GPTExcel

GPT Excel is an AI-powered tool designed to simplify spreadsheet processes for individuals and businesses. It streamlines various automation tasks in Excel, Google Sheets, and Airtable by generating formulas, scripts, and more. One of its features includes an Excel formula generator AI that aids in the creation and automation of complex calculations and analyses across different spreadsheet platforms. It also has a script generator AI designed for creating VBA scripts for Excel, Apps Scripts for Google Workspace, and Airtable scripts, thereby leveraging automation in these platforms. It has an SQL query generator AI that eases craft, debug, and modifier complex SQL queries, removing the need for manual coding. Additionally, there's a regex generator AI aimed at creating and understanding regular expression patterns for data validation and filtering. Users can also generate spreadsheet templates via the template generator AI. This AI solution aims to boost productivity by taking care of repetitive tasks and enables users to focus on strategic activities.
