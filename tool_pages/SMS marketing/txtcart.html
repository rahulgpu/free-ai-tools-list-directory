About TxtCart

TxtCart is a conversational SMS marketing platform, particularly designed for ecommerce brands on Shopify. The system combines human empathy and artificial intelligence to transform website visits into valuable interactions. One of its prominent features is the ability to optimize SMS marketing through conversation, providing a personalized way for brands to reach their audience. TxtCart offers an AI-supported Campaign Planner, helpful in planning marketing strategies and ensuring marketing dates are never missed. The tool is enriched with analytics that yield robust insights into subscribers and SMS marketing performance. Another essential feature is Cart Recovery, which enhances revenues by converting abandoned checkouts into completed purchases through genuine interactions. TxtCart allows users to create advanced SMS flows and offers one-click SMS automations, designed to maximize return on investment (ROI). Additionally, TxtCart encompasses an AI Assistant to assist in cart recovery processes and campaign copywriting. Considering various industries, such as fashion, beauty, food, pet care, health, fitness, home decor, and dropshipping, TxtCart aims to serve a range of ecommerce sectors.
