About Brief News

Brief by Upnext is an AI-powered tool that delivers a personalized news digest via email. It caters to an extensive range of topics, including technology, artificial intelligence, electric vehicles, financial markets, worldwide news, gaming, science, venture capital, and others. The tool curates top stories of the day according to the user's area of interest and summarises thousands of articles from various sources, resulting in a comprehensive daily briefing. Users can choose the format of their brief between a curated set of top stories or a list of all stories. The tool is designed to keep users informed and updated with relevant news, removing the need to sift through multiple sources. Moreover, Brief by Upnext employs AI technology to make this curation and delivery process more efficient.
