About Newsprint

Newsprint is an AI-powered tool designed to provide personalized daily news briefs, tailored to the user's specific interests. This tool is envisioned to assist users to stay updated with the latest news relevant to them without having to scan through numerous headlines. Utilizing AI technology, this tool analyzes thousands of stories daily and includes only the most pertinent articles in its briefs. The news briefs are filtered, grouped, and summarized based on each user's unique preferences.The personalized news briefs are delivered daily via email and can be tailored to cover the news of specific interest to you, your business, or your clients. It is designed to enhance your news consumption experience by learning from your interactions such as clicks, likes, and replies, refining its briefs over time based on your behavior. If you want to make a change, simple feedback mechanisms are in place to further guide the AI in curating your news. In essence, Newsprint is a tool designed to maximize the utility of news consumption for its users, making it ideal for busy professionals who require a concise yet comprehensive news roundup. By utilizing AI capabilities, it aims to take the noise out of the news and provide a personalized service unique to each user.
