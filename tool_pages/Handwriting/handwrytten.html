About Handwrytten

Handwrytten is an innovative AI-driven service that enables users to send personalised handwritten notes and cards online. A notable feature of the service is its focus on automation, allowing users to craft custom event-driven messages such as thank you and birthday cards. The service offers a broad market solution and can be easily integrated into different sectors including automotive dealerships, insurance agencies, nonprofit organizations, political campaigns, real estate, retail, eCommerce, and solar panel installation. A key aspect of Handwrytten is its extensive integration capabilities with multiple platforms such as Blackbaud Raisers Edge, CDK/Fortellis, Hubspot, Integrately, Salesforce, Shopify, SyncSpider, Tekion, and Zapier. Such integrations enable a seamless automated workflow, allowing the service to fit smoothly into existing systems. This tool is ideal for businesses looking to add a personal touch to their customer engagement, with AI technology effectively replicating human handwriting. The platform is built for scalability, allowing it to support varying volumes of outreach campaigns, helping to foster positive impressions and establish lasting bonds.
