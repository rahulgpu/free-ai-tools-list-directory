About Knowt

Knowt is a comprehensive AI-based educational tool offering a range of study methods to aid learning. Its primary functions are to create flashcards and practice exercises from your academic content, such as notes, lecture videos, and slides. The application includes modes for learning, spaced repetition, matching, multiple-choice, writing, and true/false questions. Undoubtedly, the distinct feature of Knowt lies in its ability to generate flashcards directly from your lecture videos, a feature synchronous with the ability to summarize video content into notes. Further interactive features include AI-led dialogues for follow-up discussion on video content, providing an immersive learning experience. Knowt also offers the option to generate practice tests to quiz yourself on video material, offering versatility in studying. The app is not just restricted to computer use; the Knowt mobile application allows flexible on-the-go learning. Knowt also provides an easy import feature from Quizlet, allowing the accessibility of flashcards across platforms.
