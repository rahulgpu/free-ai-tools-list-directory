About Heyday

Heyday is an AI-supported tool designed to distill insights from the user's various sources of data - documents, notes, and conversations. This tool is utilized by professionals to automate various tasks such as creation of meeting notes, extraction of insights from research and drafting content which is influenced by past readings and conversations. Users upload their data input and Heyday creates a searchable database, draws insights from the information, and provides summaries. Beyond its core capabilities, Heyday offers different functionality options, namely - Universal Assistant, Writing Assistant, Topic Assistant, and Call Recaps. Heyday can also draft personalized content based on the user's writing style and it has a feature that connects to conversations and generates notes automatically. Another important feature is its ability to provide important context about clients in the user's calendar. It should be mentioned that user data is safely stored, encrypted and can be deleted at any time. Furthermore, Heyday has undergone thorough security reviews by companies like Zoom and Google. This tool is especially beneficial for professionals who do a lot of reading and research, as well as those who depend heavily on recalling important data from their digital files.
