About JagodaAI

jagodaAI is an artificial intelligence powered online tutoring tool designed to assist students in over 30 school subjects. The platform aims to promote self-directed learning by not only providing the solutions to homework tasks but also explaining the steps to get there. JagodaAI tailors its responses according to each student's school and grade level, continually learning with each new request. This ensures its tutoring content is adjusted to individual needs, promoting understanding of topics by answering additional queries from the students. The system also includes a feature called 'MathpixScansTotal task' that allows you to photograph your homework, upload the image, and specify an area you want help with. JagodaAI has the capability to translate its responses to over 20 languages, which can be particularly beneficial for non-English speaking learners. There are several plans available on the platform, including free options for individuals or teams, with varying levels of access depending on the chosen plan.
