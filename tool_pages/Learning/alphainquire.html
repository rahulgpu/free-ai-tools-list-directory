About AlphaInquire

AlphaInquire.com is a personal AI-powered research assistant that helps users gather relevant information on topics they care about. This tool scans various data sources to find relevant information and generates a daily digest with summaries for the user. Users can customize the AI agents through the tool's user interface, specifying the topics they want the tool to focus on. The tool then sends an email summary to the user, including links for further exploration if desired. AlphaInquire.com also offers integrations with popular news sources like Google, arXiv, Hacker News, and Financial Markets News, allowing the AI agents to learn from these sources as well. The pricing for this tool includes a simple monthly subscription with a one-day free trial, costing less than $10 per month. With the subscription, users receive a daily personalized research summary, Alpha Inquire emails delivered to their inbox, and access to manage their AI agents by setting knowledge sources and interests. Additionally, API access is available for power users.This tool is built by Aster, a small team that is passionate about generative AI and the future of work. AlphaInquire.com aims to assist users in automating their research process, saving them time and delivering relevant information on their specified topics.
