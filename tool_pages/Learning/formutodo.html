About FormuTodo

FormuTodo is an app designed to assist students in their academic pursuits by providing a comprehensive library of formulas in mathematics, physics, and chemistry. It goes beyond formula references by offering a range of powerful tools to simplify calculations and enhance academic performance. The app features a user-friendly interface and does not require an internet connection.Whether you're a beginner or advanced student, FormuTodo covers various disciplines from basic levels to university level. It provides formulas and fundamental concepts in areas such as algebra, geometry, calculus, probability and statistics, mechanics, fluid mechanics, thermodynamics, electromagnetism, stoichiometry, solutions, thermochemistry, and organic chemistry. The app is a reliable resource for tackling complex equations and detailed analysis.In addition to formulas, FormuTodo offers intelligent tools to support students. These tools help simplify challenging calculations, verify results, and instill confidence in studies. The app also includes brain-training games and engaging puzzles to further enhance learning.FormuTodo is regularly updated to improve user experience, and user feedback is taken into account. The app promotes interactive learning, exam preparation, and serves as a study guide for students in mathematics, exact sciences, engineering, and related fields. It also functions as a scientific calculator. With FormuTodo, students can unlock their full potential and excel in their academic endeavors.
