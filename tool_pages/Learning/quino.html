About Quino

Quino AI is an AI-powered study tool package specifically designed for university students. Its primary function is to provide instant AI-driven summaries of documents to facilitate faster learning. Users can upload files, such as PDFs, DOCXs, DOCs, and TXTs which the AI then summarizes, potentially saving the user hours of reading time. Quino AI also allows users to interact with their documents by asking questions or clarifying definitions based on the content. Additionally, learning cards can be generated from these documents for quick testing of knowledge. Quino AI also fosters collaborative learning by enabling users to share projects and learning materials, making it a useful tool for forming study groups. It also promotes affordability with a range of plans to accommodate different users' needs, including a free plan for basic features and a more comprehensive pro plan. The platform is highlighted as user-friendly and intuitive, offering clear, consistent, and in-depth summaries. It is designed to enhance both solo and collaborative learning experiences for students.
