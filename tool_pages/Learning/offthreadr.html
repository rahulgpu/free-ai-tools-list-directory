About Offthreadr

Offthreadr AI is an AI-enabled platform that facilitates the creation and delivery of courses, aimed at enhancing the learners' completion rates. It emphasizes on effective 'on-the-go' learning, enabling users to interact directly with course materials and get their queries resolved instantly. A unique feature of this tool is the provision to engage directly with course content, elevating the traditional learning experience. Learning is promoted through compact, manageable snippets of information, which can be delivered directly to learners' mailboxes, increasing the accessibility and convenience of learning. It also fosters a sense of community among learners, providing them an avenue to stay motivated and engaged. For course creators, it presents an opportunity to design AI-powered courses that can be delivered across different segments of learners. Through what Offthreadr calls 'offthreaded learning', course reach is expanded to various places, making education more universal. Finally, for the end-users, it helps in achieving course completion through instant query resolution, and enhances adaptability of learning by supporting learning even during commute or travel.
