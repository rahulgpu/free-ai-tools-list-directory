About LearnQ

LearnQ.ai is an AI-powered learning platform aimed at transforming the way we learn by unlocking a learner's full potential. The tool primarily focuses on data-driven learning methods and real-time insights to measure and drive growth. It targets both students and tutors/institutes. The key areas where AI is being used in this platform are detecting and addressing learning gaps in real-time and providing educators with AI-powered student analytics. For students, it helps in boosting their confidence through data-driven learning strategies and 24/7 access to an AI-Tutor named Mia who offers personalized guidance. The platform is designed not just for learning as a destination, but as a journey, providing tools and resources for continuous growth. This includes a mix of features such as diagnostic tests, a play & practice section with game-based learning modules, and full-length SAT practice tests. For educators, it offers a suite of powerful tools and insights to facilitate informed decision-making by providing AI-Driven real-time actionable insights, making learning more data-driven. It also provides a comprehensive analytics platform for instant visibility into student progress. The main goal is to put students and their needs at the center, provide teachers with timely data and insights to drive learning forward, and improve learning outcomes.
