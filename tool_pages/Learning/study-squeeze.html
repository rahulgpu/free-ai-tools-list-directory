About Study Squeeze

StudySqueeze is an AI-powered learning platform that expedites the creation of study materials, adding efficiency to your learning experience. It simplifies the conversion of files and documents into flashcards, diagrams, and summaries. The platform supports numerous types of documents for upload including PDF, Word, PowerPoint, JPEG, and PNG files. It offers the option to transform notes to various formats like Question and Answer, multiple choice, fill-in-the-gap style flashcards, and summaries. Thus, it allows customization of learning materials according to personal preference. Thence, converted materials can be reviewed on the platform's study page, making the learning process highly accessible and streamlined. In addition to viewing on StudySqueeze, note-takers have the freedom to export converted files to other study platforms such as Quizlet and Anki, or to file formats such as CSV, PDF, and Markdown. By automating the note-taking process, StudySqueeze enables learners to spend less time on preparation and more time on actual study.
