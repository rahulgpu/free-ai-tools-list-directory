About Tutorly

Tutorly.ai is an AI-powered tool designed to help students with their homework. Users can paste their question, assignment, or prompt into the app and get an answer. The app offers multiple choice, short answer, and other question formats. Elite users can access additional features like ‘Explain’ and ‘Show Steps’ to gain a more comprehensive understanding of the material. Tutorly.ai also allows users to share links with each other for further study. The app is still in development and users are encouraged to fact-check the information provided.
