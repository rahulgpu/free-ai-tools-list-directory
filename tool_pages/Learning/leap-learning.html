About Leap Learning

LEAP by Thoughtjumper is an intelligent learning tool designed to enhance the learning experience. It aims to guide individuals in effective learning across various domains such as business, data science, technology, design, and more. The tool offers learning quests in a wide range of subjects, allowing users to select their desired topics such as web development, digital marketing, data science, finance, and more.LEAP is focused on helping users learn faster and better. It provides an intelligent guidance system that adapts to individual learning preferences. By decluttering distractions, LEAP allows users to solely focus on their learning, leading to a more immersive experience. The platform also offers multiple pedagogies, allowing users to explore a topic through different content types such as videos, asking questions, or embarking on their own quest.Managing learning goals is made easier with LEAP, enabling users to take control of their progress and keep track of their true progress. The tool is constantly evolving, with upcoming features being shaped by user feedback and requests.Users have found LEAP beneficial in various ways. It has streamlined research processes, enriched understanding of complex subjects, supported ongoing education in areas like mental health coaching, facilitated exploration of engineering topics, and provided context-based learning for PhD research.LEAP is accessible through a web application for desktop and laptop devices, with mobile and tablet versions under development. It caters to learners of all levels and backgrounds, including students, professionals, entrepreneurs, and lifelong learners. During the closed beta phase, access to LEAP is currently free, and users can contact the support team for assistance via Discord or email.
