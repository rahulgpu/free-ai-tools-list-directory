About Synapse Tutor

Synapse Tutor is an educational AI tool designed to provide a personalized and interactive learning experience. Its main focus is on fostering a solid understanding of mathematics. Unlike standard video courses available on platforms such as YouTube, Coursera, or Udemy, Synapse Tutor aims to move beyond passive watching to a more engaging form of learning. It offers a user-customized approach that tailors the learning experience to the specific needs of the individual student. To fully utilize its features, JavaScript must be enabled. Synapse Tutor shows potential for users who prefer an active learning approach and value personalization in their educational resources. However, please note the nature of the Synapse platform is digital, and users must comply with the terms of service and privacy policy.
