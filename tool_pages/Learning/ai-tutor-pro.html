About AI Tutor Pro

AI Tutor Pro is developed by Contact North | Contact Nord, offering an artificial intelligence-powered personalized digital assistant designed to help users enhance their knowledge and skills across a multitude of topics. This tool guarantees privacy and confidentiality, and it is versatile with a multilingual support, enabling learners around the globe to gain knowledge regardless of language barriers and geographical restrictions. The tool is capable of assessing a user's existing knowledge and skills on a particular subject, thereby tailoring its assistance to effectively facilitate learning and improvement. This includes a wide range of disciplines, from business and history to science and technology, and psychology. Despite its comprehensive offerings, AI Tutor Pro is a free resource, making education accessible to everyone.
