About Hire AI

Arc is an AI-powered remote jobs marketplace specifically designed for hiring software developers. The platform utilizes its AI tool, HireAI, to match companies with top remote developers at a rapid pace. Companies can choose between hiring full-time or freelance developers. HireAI eliminates the need for manual screening and eliminates the time-consuming process of outreach and resume screening. By implementing AI technology, Arc ensures that businesses can quickly identify the most suitable candidates from a global pool of over 250,000 developers. This saves valuable time and resources by providing instant candidate matches.With HireAI, companies can identify top applicants without the need for extensive manual screening. Hiring managers can focus on high-quality candidates rather than reviewing hundreds of resumes. As HireAI learns and adapts to the preferences of the company after every review, personalized results are generated for a better hiring experience.Arc boasts significant benefits for companies using the platform. It enables them to save time by instantly finding the best match candidates, improve accuracy by automatically identifying relevant profiles, reduce bias by evaluating candidates with the same criteria, and enhance the candidate experience by streamlining the process.The platform has a proven track record, as over 800 companies have successfully hired developers through Arc. It provides access to a diverse pool of engineers worldwide, covering a broad range of technical skills. By utilizing Arc, companies can efficiently and effectively hire top remote developers, leading to cost savings, time efficiency, and improved hiring outcomes.
