About Globify

Globify is an AI tool designed to rapidly localize your app by utilizing GPT-4 technology. The software aims to streamline and simplify the localization process, enabling developers to translate their applications into multiple languages effectively and efficiently. With Globify, several key features allow a high degree of customization and control in the localization process. Developers can manage target languages, edit individual localizations, and work on multiple projects simultaneously. The software also provides the option to add custom tones and styles while defining glossaries for projects, providing better context and accuracy for translations. One of the main features is the 'smart button' that performs an auto-localization function that translates entire applications with a single click. This feature drastically enhances the global reach of the apps by making them accessible in multiple languages. It also maintains a sync with your string catalog file, ensuring consistency and coherence across your various software strings and eliminating the hassle of manual management. The service is available for download on Mac. The platform is committed to promoting productive dialog between its users and the developers, valuing feedback and using it to continuously update and improve its offering.
