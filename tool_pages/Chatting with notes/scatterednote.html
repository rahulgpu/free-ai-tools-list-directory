About ScatteredNote

scatteredNote is a simple and intuitive tool that helps you grab content and take notes effortlessly. Its user interface is designed to ensure a seamless note-taking experience without distracting you from your main task. The tool emphasizes the concept of note-taking as a by-product of your main activity, such as research or reading, to prevent switching modes and maintain focus.With scatteredNote, you can easily jot down and organize your notes, ensuring that no worth knowledge is lost or forgotten. The tool features a straightforward workflow and user interface, minimizing any potential for feeling trapped while taking notes and allowing you to quickly return to your main task.One of the notable aspects of scatteredNote is its ability to capture information atomically and back it up with your insights, promoting effective knowledge management. The tool also offers features such as organizing notes per website or YouTube videos, enabling easy access to your archived knowledge.Additionally, scatteredNote leverages AI capabilities to enhance your note-taking experience. You can query, chat, and even create flashcards with your archived knowledge for quick insights and efficient learning. The tool also supports AI chat integration to effortlessly access information from your extended memory.scatteredNote requires little effort for note organization, minimizing the need for manual rearrangement. It offers various capture modes, including YouTube, VS Code, web, and PDF, allowing you to capture perspectives based on timestamps, functions, paragraphs, or images.Please note that scatteredNote offers a subscription-based model for its upcoming version 1.0, which will include AI and database enhancements.
