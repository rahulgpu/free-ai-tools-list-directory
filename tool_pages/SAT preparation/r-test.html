About R.test

R.test is an AI-powered platform built for practice ACT and SAT tests. Providing an adaptive, accessible, and reliable method, it assesses test readiness quickly and efficiently. The platform features a diagnostic test that presents a limited set of questions across multiple sections, resulting in a rapid evaluation of the test-taker's capabilities. R.test also delivers a comprehensive analysis report, powered by AI analytics, that offers a detailed breakdown of the user's performance to pinpoint areas requiring improvement. Notably, the test environment is engineered to simulate the real SAT and ACT test setting, offering features such as a test navigator and a test timer to familiarize users with the actual test process. Attention is also given to facilitating digital SAT practice, making R.test a useful tool for both students and instructors preparing for the new digital SAT format. The platform has found affirmations from a wide variety of users, including students, tutors, educators, and evaluators.
