About Appified.ai

Appified.ai is a no-code platform designed to transform an OpenAI Assistant into a Web App. The platform allows users to embed the OpenAI assistant on their own website, share it with others, or even sell it as a product. User data and the AI assistant remain under the ownership and control of the user as Appified.ai only acts as a bridge for connection. The web app that is developed is fully featured, enabling end-users to interact with the AI Assistant on a variety of devices and refer back to previous conversations. This tool goes beyond simply building an AI chat app, by facilitating the turning of the AI assistant into a potential business. It achieves this by integrating with Stripe to handle payments and user management, thereby creating a revenue stream for users. Additionally, Appified.ai also provides an admin dashboard, where users can monitor the performance of their AI Assistant and manage their users. The tool prides itself on its simplicity, allowing users to develop, monetize, and embed their chat assistant without having to write a single line of code.
