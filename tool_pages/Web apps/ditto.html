About Ditto

Ditto is an AI-powered tool designed to democratize app building for non-technical users. Leveraging a user-friendly document editor interface, it simplifies the process of building web apps. Familiarity with commonly used tools such as Google Docs or Notion eases the learning curve, allowing users to focus on the content and business logic of their apps. These apps range from knowledge-sharing platforms, to forms for information collection in a conversational way, AI-based chat applications, online course apps which can be gated behind user logins for exclusive resources, and various apps based on extensive databases. A notable feature of Ditto is that it takes care of the coding and design aspects, thus removing technical barriers for users. It offers guidance through various resources such as blogs, a get-started guide, YouTube tutorials, and a community on Discord. Additionally, Ditto provides a service to help users build their app ideas free of charge.
