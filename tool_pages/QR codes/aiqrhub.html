About AIqrhub

AIQRHub is an online tool that utilizes AI technology to generate QR codes. Unlike standard QR codes, the QR codes generated by AIQRHub are aesthetically pleasing, artistic, and more appealing to users. This makes them easier to spread and attract more attention. The tool offers a user-friendly interface where users can create QR codes by entering a URL and clicking the "Get QR Code" button. Users can also choose from a selection of preset templates to customize the style of their QR codes. Once the customization process is complete, users can click the "Generate" button to generate a beautiful artistic QR code. AIQRHub offers a free version for new users, with each new user receiving 200 free diamonds. Diamonds are used to generate AI art QR codes, with each generation consuming 10 diamonds. Users also have the option to purchase additional diamonds on the Pricing page using various payment methods such as credit cards or PayPal. Pro users have the option to request a full refund if they have used less than 10% of their diamonds within 10 days of purchase. AIQRHub provides a contact email for refund requests. Overall, AIQRHub is a useful tool for creating visually appealing and artistic QR codes using AI technology, making them more engaging and attractive to users.
