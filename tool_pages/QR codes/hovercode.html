About Hovercode

The AI QR code generator by Hovercode is a tool that allows users to create unique and artistic QR codes. While still in the prototype phase, this tool offers a fun way to generate QR codes with an artistic touch. Using the tool is simple. Users can add their desired link or dynamic link, enabling them to shorten the URL, track scans, and edit the scan destination. They can then select from a variety of example prompts for the artwork of the QR code or modify one of these prompts to their liking. Users can specify detailed descriptions of their desired artwork, such as a painting of a cottage in the British countryside on a summer day, complete with a sky background, birds, and trees.The generated QR codes can be customized to match the user's brand and can be designed in different shapes, including round QR codes. The tool also supports dynamic QR codes, allowing users to update the destination URL without changing the code itself. Tracking and analytics are available to monitor the performance of the QR codes.The AI QR code generator is offered by Hovercode, a company that provides a range of QR code-related services and features, including branded QR codes, custom domains, bulk QR code generation, and PDF QR codes.
