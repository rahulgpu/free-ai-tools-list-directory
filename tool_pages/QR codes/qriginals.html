About Qriginals

Qriginals is an AI-powered tool that allows users to create and share unique QR code art on Qriginals.com. With the AI Art Generator, users can customize and beautify QR codes to create visually appealing designs. The tool aims to offer a new and innovative way of experiencing QR codes.The platform offers a gallery of QR code art creations, showcasing various designs such as Cosmic Space Garden, Warrior Princess, Watercolor Cityscape, and more. Users can explore these beautiful creations and get inspired to create their own QR code art.To use the tool, users can sign in or create a new account on Qriginals.com. It is worth mentioning that new users can benefit from a limited-time release offer, receiving 30 free credits instead of the standard 15.One of the highlighted features is the ability to join the Qriginals community, allowing users to share their QR code art creations with others. The tool emphasizes simplicity by offering a free trial with no requirement for credit card information.Qriginals also provides support through a community forum and credits information. Additionally, the platform offers account settings and a collection feature for organizing QR code art creations.Overall, Qriginals aims to provide users with a creative and visually appealing way to interact with QR codes, allowing them to explore, create, and share their unique QR code art on the platform.
