About mai.bio

mai.bio is an AI tool powered by OpenAI ChatGPT designed to convert social media channels into a fully operational and responsive one-page website. It facilitates the integration of various social networks by merely providing usernames; it supports a wide array of platforms such as Instagram, Facebook, TikTok, LinkedIn, and more. This tool leverages AI to generate optimized texts based on the information it retrieves from the user's social networks. These texts can be easily edited, especially when subscribed to the Pro plan that offers more editing options. In the process of creating a website, users can choose from multiple pre-designed, SEO-optimized themes that are compatible across all devices. After setting up the theme, users simply provide an email address to create an account, select a subdomain and publish the website. The dashboard provides the functionality to edit or update the website as per requirement. Opting for the Pro plan also lets users utilize a custom domain, check view analytics, and enjoy unlimited functionalities like site creation, social channel incorporation, and making revisions. The tool is designed to be user-friendly and efficient while prioritizing the ease of website creation.
