About Helper.im

Helper is a free desktop client for ChatGPT, available for MacOS, Windows, and Linux. It is specifically designed for entrepreneurs, developers, and marketers who are looking to integrate AI assistance into their daily tasks. With Helper, users can seamlessly access ChatGPT directly from their favorite applications, eliminating the need for context switching. The tool boasts convenient built-in AI commands and a powerful prompt library, allowing users to build their own artificial intelligence assistant on their computer.Helper prioritizes user safety and privacy, focusing on individual security. Users have the option to use their own OpenAI API key with unlimited credits, ensuring a secure and customizable experience. The tool also offers shortcut keys to quickly access the AI Helper app, enhancing productivity.Helper caters to various use cases, including content generation for marketing purposes, AI coding assistance within integrated development environments (IDEs), AI work assistance for various work scenarios, and AI social assistance for quick replies on social media platforms.By downloading the Helper app and setting up an API key, users can enhance their productivity by generating high-quality posts, receiving coding assistance, getting work-related support, and efficiently managing their social media interactions.Helper.im, the official website, provides additional resources such as documentation, a changelog, a Discord community, and a FAQ section. The tool ensures compliance with privacy policies, terms of use, and an end-user license agreement.
