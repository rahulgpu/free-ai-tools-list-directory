About Text GPT

TextGPT is an innovative texting app that enables users to access OpenAI's GPT (Generative Pre-trained Transformer) and Dall-E 2 through text messaging. With TextGPT, users can quickly and accurately receive answers to any question they may have, and generate realistic images with just a few words. TextGPT is designed to be more than just a Q&A app, and is a powerful asset for expanding knowledge and understanding of the world. After a free trial of 15,000 tokens, users can purchase additional tokens as needed, with the minimum purchase being 150,000 tokens. Each token is approximately 4 characters or 0.75 words for English text, and will also be applied to the cost of the text message service at a rate of 800 tokens per exchange. The cost to generate an image from text is 1,900 tokens. Coming soon, TextGPT will enable users to generate images using the DALL-E API, use past conversation for better results, back up conversation to the cloud, and access advanced tuning and configuration.
