About MultiOn

MultiOn AI is a technology and design group that develops innovative ways to apply AI to improve human capabilities. The core offering of MultiOn AI is its Agent API. This API allows the embedding of AI agents into various applications, helping users to automate tasks on the web. A significant illustration of its capabilities is the Agent app, built leveraging the Agent API. This app is designed to plan and book gatherings on behalf of users, permitting users more time and energy to focus on social connections. Beneficially, this means enhanced productivity as users can rely on these AI agents to accomplish daily responsibilities more efficiently. Moreover, MultiOn AI updates its services and products regularly to maintain the platform's effectiveness and accessibility to its clients. Harnessing the power of AI, MultiOn AI is committed to building a brighter future for humanity by augmenting what people can do and making day-to-day tasks simpler.
