About Myai.ninja

My AI Ninja - GPT-4 is an AI language model tool that provides access to the latest GPT-4 model without requiring a subscription. It offers the convenience of generating content without having to start from scratch. Instead of being locked into expensive monthly payments, users only pay for the actual usage of the tool and can choose when to utilize it.The tool operates on a credit-based system, with all credit purchases having a validity period of 6 months. Users have the flexibility to make one-time payments based on the desired word count. The pricing tiers include a small package that offers 20,000 words for £5, a medium package with 50,000 words for £10, and a large package with 100,000 words for £15. Additionally, the tool allows users to try it for free without any payment required for the first 500 words.My AI Ninja - GPT-4 emphasizes time and cost savings, enabling users to easily generate content without the need for extensive manual writing. The tool offers a user-friendly interface and is powered by GPT-4, a powerful AI model developed by OpenAI. It ensures that users have control over their usage and expenses, making it suitable for users of varying needs, from beginners to power users.For additional information or inquiries, users can contact the provider through the provided email address. Detailed terms of use and consent preferences are also available on the website.
