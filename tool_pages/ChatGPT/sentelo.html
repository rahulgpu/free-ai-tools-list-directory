About Sentelo

Sentelo is an AI tool powered by Google and OpenAI, designed to facilitate faster reading, writing, and learning. It leverages Information Retrieval and Generative Pretrained Transformer (GPT) models to provide users with fact-checked and up-to-date information, supplemented with links to relevant resources. Available 24/7, Sentelo can handle heavy loads and serve as a reliable browsing assistant. It's a browser extension easily accessible across various platforms, including Docs, Overleaf, PDFs, and websites. Sentelo offers personalized functionality, where users can select their favorite tasks from its prompt repository. It assists in tasks like content creation, providing inspirations when users are stuck with creating content. It also helps put ideas into words using its elaborate function. In addition, Sentelo aids in understanding new topics by creating control questions and using the 'Explain to a Child' function for intuitive explanations. Users can use Sentelo to generate simple or bulleted-point summaries from an entire chapter for note-making. Moreover, Sentelo can provide real-world examples of certain topics.Start leveraging Sentelo for free, exploring its writing and summarizing capabilities, essentially becoming your ultimate AI learning copilot.
