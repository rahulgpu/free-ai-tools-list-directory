About Ask AI

Ask AI is an AI tool developed by Sindre Sorhus that allows users to utilize the powerful capabilities of ChatGPT on their wristwatch. With this tool, users can get instant answers to any question they have, whether it's explaining complex topics, translating languages, providing recommendations, solving math problems, improving their writing, and much more. Users can ask questions in multiple languages, including English, Spanish, French, German, Chinese, Japanese, Korean, Portuguese, and many more. It's important to note that the app has data only prior to 2022, so it won't be able to answer questions about recent events. Ask AI uses OpenAI's official ChatGPT API, with Sindre Sorhus paying for every question. The app is available for one-time purchase, and users do not need to worry about subscription fees. Although Ask AI is only available on Apple Watch and does not support iOS and macOS, there is a QuickGPT option available for users who want to use the app on macOS. Finally, Sindre Sorhus also provides a version history of the app for users who want to stay updated on recent changes. Overall, Ask AI is an excellent tool for individuals who want to have quick and easy access to a powerful AI assistant right on their wristwatch.
