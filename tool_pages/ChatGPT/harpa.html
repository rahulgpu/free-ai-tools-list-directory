About Harpa

HARPA AI is a Chrome Extension and NoCode RPA platform that automates web tasks to save time and money. It uses a hybrid AI engine built on ChatGPT and Machine Learning to perform tasks such as providing AI answers to search queries, summarizing web pages, extracting data, tracking product prices and stock availability, monitoring articles and legislation, and detecting changes in competitor sites. It runs locally in the browser, does not send data away, and is optimized for speed and performance. It is free to use, with the option of a paid plan for running ChatGPT queries. It supports various search engines such as Google, Baidu, Bing, DuckDuckGo, Brave, Yahoo, Naver, Yandex, Kagi, and Searx. It stores cookies for the purposes of accessibility and security, and can be configured to accept only necessary cookies.
