About Tooltips AI

Tooltips.ai is an AI-Augmented Reading Extension which employs the capabilities of OpenAI's language model, ChatGPT. It is designed to enhance and expedite the process of reading and understanding various documents on the web or in PDF formats. This tool offers features that can provide explanations for highlighted words, jargons, phrases, or concepts, and can also answer follow-up questions to offer a well-rounded understanding of the context. This is accomplished by it utilizing ChatGPT's natural language processing abilities to comprehend and interpret complex texts and then provide user-friendly explanations or answers. In addition, Tooltips.ai supports over 100 languages, making it an effective tool for multilingual users or for readers in need of language translation assistance while reading. This extension requires JavaScript to be enabled in order to run effectively. Users can seamlessly integrate Tooltips.ai with their browser or document reading workflow, enabling a more interactive and engaging reading experience. However, it's worth noting that the effectiveness and comprehensibility of the explanations and answers provided may depend on the complexity of the text and the accuracy of the language model. Overall, Tooltips.ai is a robust tool for anyone looking to streamline their reading process and wanting context-specific insights while navigating through complex or lengthy texts.
