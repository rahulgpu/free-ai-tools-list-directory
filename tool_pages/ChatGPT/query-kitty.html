About Query Kitty

Query Kitty | ChatGPT Chrome Extension is a powerful tool designed to overlay various functionalities on top of web content. Its main feature is the quick creation of compelling content. In addition, it allows ChatGPT to function on top of different document types: PDFs, Word documents, articles, YouTube videos, or anything on the web. This feature expands the scope of applicability for end users, who can use it even outside of conventional writing or drafting. The extension also aids in efficiently designing writing prompts, a process traditionally time-consuming. A unique attribute is its ability to translate web content into contextual data for AI prompts, adding a layer of personalization to the results. Furthermore, the tool finds utility in diverse fields like marketing, ad campaigns, social media, sales, and research. It's also set to accommodate different levels of usage with basic and pro plans, each offering a range of features including GPT-3.5 and GPT-4 Support respectively, and access to unlimited translations. This versatile Chrome extension is an asset for any professional seeking assistance in generating, revising, or customizing web content.
