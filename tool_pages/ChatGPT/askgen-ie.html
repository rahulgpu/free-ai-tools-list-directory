About AskGen.ie

askGenie is a versatile tool that enables users to create AI agents from their existing data. With just a simple upload of files or linking of websites, users can generate a ChatGPT-style bot. The AI agent can be embedded onto a website or interacted with through the API. askGenie supports a wide range of data sources, including files and websites, with plans to expand further. Integration and deployment options are flexible, with current support for website widgets and web iframes, and upcoming support for channels such as Slack, Discord, Teams, Telegram, Whatsapp, Zapier, and API. The tool allows for customization and branding, allowing users to personalize the agent's name, color, logo, welcome message, sample questions, and prompts.Users have praised askGenie's exceptional versatility and reliability, finding the setup, chat, integration, and usage intuitive. The tool consistently produces coherent and adequate responses, meeting user expectations. Customers have expressed anticipation for future enhancements. askGenie's interface seamlessly combines and presents information from various sources in an organized and convenient manner. It has been a valuable tool for showcasing AI expertise, enhancing customer service operations, and revamping interactions between clients' customers and website visitors.askGenie offers different pricing plans, including a free option with limited features and paid plans for increased agents, knowledge bases, message credits, and text processing capabilities. The tool has garnered positive reviews from users who appreciate its quick setup, powerful performance in multiple languages, and seamless operation with uploaded data.Overall, askGenie is a reliable AI agent builder that empowers users to leverage their existing data and enhance their interactions with customers and website visitors.
