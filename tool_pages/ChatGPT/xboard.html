About Xboard

Xboard is a keyboard app available on Google Play that utilizes OpenAI's chatGPT and gpt3.5 API. It enables users to effortlessly search and write messages, emails, and content based on tone and prompts. The app aims to enhance typing experience through efficiency and personalization.One notable feature of Xboard is its intelligent search function. Users can search for contacts, files, and online content directly from the keyboard, without switching to other apps. The search function is powered by advanced algorithms that learn from the user's typing behavior, providing relevant suggestions in real-time and minimizing spelling errors and typos.Additionally, Xboard offers message and email generation capabilities. Users can generate pre-written messages and emails tailored to specific tones and prompts. The app provides a range of templates users can customize for various communication purposes.Furthermore, Xboard includes advanced content generation capabilities. Ideal for users in creative fields such as writers, bloggers, and social media influencers, the app allows for quick content generation based on prompts and keywords. It also provides users with writing prompts and templates to assist in content creation.Xboard prioritizes data safety and does not share user data with third parties. Users also have the option to request data deletion.The app is regularly updated, with the latest version being the fifth stable release. Support for Xboard is provided through email, and the developer is located in New Delhi, India.Xboard is comparable to similar apps in the market that offer custom keyboards, text-based communication, AI chatbots, and AI assistants.
