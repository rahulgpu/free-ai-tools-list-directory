About Jarvis Tel

Jarvis AI is an open-source artificial intelligence platform that can be accessed through text messaging. It is marketed as an AI assistant that can provide answers to any questions users may have. The platform is powered by ChatGPT from OpenAI, which is a state-of-the-art natural language processing algorithm. It is designed to be accessible from anywhere, making it ideal for users who are always on-the-go. Users can easily add Jarvis to their contacts by using their phone's camera, and they can initiate text conversations with Jarvis through voice commands. Jarvis is intended to make AI more accessible to the general public. It is a user-friendly platform that can be introduced to family and friends who may be curious about AI technology. The platform offers a variety of functionalities, including generating creative ideas, providing travel suggestions, and even delivering funny jokes. Users can try out Jarvis for free by sending ten text messages, after which they can upgrade to a monthly subscription that comes with 100 messages. Overall, Jarvis is a convenient and accessible AI tool that allows users to engage with AI technology in a fun and interactive way.
