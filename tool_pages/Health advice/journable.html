About Journable

Journable is an application that uses advanced artificial intelligence to make tracking diet and exercise as easy as having a conversation. Through a user-friendly chat interface, users can log their meals, workouts, and track their progress towards personalized health goals. The application simplifies the process of keeping a food and fitness journal by allowing users to chat their way to health and fitness. Journable's unique feature is its AI-powered meal and exercise analysis, which provides instant nutritional and exercise insights based on users' inputs to the chat. This feature enables effortless tracking of caloric intake and burn, essential for weight loss, muscle gain, or maintaining a healthy lifestyle. Additionally, users can set and track their individualized health goals within the app, offering a personalized touch to their health journey. Journable's AI is also capable of understanding a wide variety of foods, from homemade meals to restaurant dishes, facilitating tracking of local and international foods.
