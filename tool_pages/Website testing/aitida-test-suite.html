About Aitida Test Suite

The AI Web Test Aitida Test Suite is designed to perform automated tests on websites to ensure their functionality, appearance, and user experience meet specified standards. This AI tool imitates the user experience by navigating through website pages and performing functions that a regular visitor to the site would. It can effectively evaluate the layout of landing pages and the functionality of log-in processes. In cases where the website does not meet the expected standards or errors are encountered during the evaluation process, the tool highlights these issues to aid in the site's improvement. It can be used for routine checks and quality assurance to uphold the website's standard and usability. For any issues or inquiries related to the tool, there is support available via email to provide solutions or clarifications.
