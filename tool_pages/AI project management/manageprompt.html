About ManagePrompt

ManagePrompt is a platform designed to assist in the acceleration and simplification of AI projects. It supplies the necessary tools and takes care of the backend infrastructure to enable users to focus on their main objectives and innovation. The platform offers flexibility in its usability by providing access to multiple AI models, including those from renowned developers such as OpenAI, Meta, Google, and Mixtral. ManagePrompt offers unique features like workflows that enable users to swiftly modify prompts, update models, and implement changes that can be instantly delivered to users. Additionally, the platform provides security controls to filter and limit malicious requests, augmenting its reliability. A remarkable feature of ManagePrompt is its billing system, where users only pay per use with no hidden charges. This 'Pay as You Go' system is calculated per 1,000 tokens, resembling pieces of words, providing an economical solution for project deployment. The platform encourages team collaboration, allowing up to five members per organization. Lastly, alongside its primary service, the platform offers additional free AI tools on its website for tasks such as proofreading, summarising text, photo colorizing, and more.
