About ViralKit

ViralKit is an AI-powered contest, giveaways, and sweepstakes tool that allows users to quickly and effortlessly generate and manage various types of contests. With the aim of growing followers, likes, subscribers, customers, and sales, the tool uses artificial intelligence to automate the process of creating engaging contests. The AI-powered contest builder allows users to create a variety of giveaways in seconds, while the viral giveaways feature helps to supercharge growth by driving engagement. The tool offers integration with over 60+ social platforms, allowing users to collect likes, follows, shares, and leads from a wide range of channels. Custom form builder and visual editor functionalities enable users to customize the design and collect additional information from participants.ViralKit also provides over 25 API integrations and a native API for creating custom workflows. The proprietary AI contest builder allows users to take their contests to the next level, offering versatility and adaptability for contests of any scale. The tool prioritizes security and employs enterprise-level security measures to block cheaters and fake entries, ensuring a fair and transparent contest process. ViralKit is suitable for content creators, social media managers, small business owners, and agencies/freelancers who want to increase their audience, engagement, and sales on various social media platforms. The tool is user-friendly and offers a range of pricing plans, allowing users to choose the option that best suits their needs and budget.
