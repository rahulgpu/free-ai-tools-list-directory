About March Health

March Health is an AI-driven health and wellness solution specifically designed for women. This tool offers comprehensive health support with particular attention to pre-pregnancy care, menstrual pain, and endometriosis. March Health utilizes a holistic approach, incorporating science-based care that extends beyond pharmacological methods by offering lifestyle guidance, symptom tracking, and pain management, all aimed at enhancing the quality of life for women. The tool utilizes a non-invasive screening and diagnostic solution that accurately screens for endometriosis and severe period pain. This is accomplished through symptom analysis, risk assessment, medical history evaluation, and the interpretation of digital biomarkers. If endometriosis is detected, March Health connects users with healthcare professionals for further care. The platform is also positioned as a resource for workplace wellness, aiming to create habitable working environments and support women's health issues often overlooked in the workplace. Additionally, March Health provides users with personalized recommendations for symptom management, lifestyle adjustments, and pain management strategies. It has a dedicated medical supporter feature that offers tailored strategies from medical professionals, helping to provide tried and tested recommendations for users. These features aim to ensure that users can manage and control their symptoms, access expert advice, receive personalized recommendations, and support their overall well-being. The tool can be accessed via a user-friendly app available on both Apple App Store and Google Play.
