About AskEllyn

AskEllyn is a conversational AI tool designed to provide support for individuals diagnosed with breast cancer. It is developed by Gambit Technologies in collaboration with Ellyn Winters-Robinson, a breast cancer survivor. The AI's responses and advice are built on the lived experiences of Ellyn which gives it a personal, empathetic touch. This AI is specifically designed to answer questions regarding breast cancer, sharing detailed information, insight and experiences of Ellyn's journey. It offers guidance on what kind of questions to ask oncologists, advises on managing treatment side effects and shares inspiring stories of hope and recovery. In order to cater to the diverse user base, it also supports multiple languages. Users can engage with the tool via a chat interface. The tool is designed for engagement and informational purposes only and encourages users to seek professional healthcare advice for medical matters. Regarding user privacy, AskEllyn adheres to strict data management policies, ensuring that personal data of the users is not shared or stored.
