About CodeMonk

CodeMonk is an all-in-one Artificial Intelligence-backed platform designed to cater the needs of businesses in hiring and managing the top vetted talents. It provides its users the flexibility to build high-performing full-stack tech teams on demand, thereby accelerating their technological delivery without straining their budgets. The platform facilitates hiring on multiple arrangements such as full-time, freelance, onsite, hybrid or remote basis. It offers comprehensive services that cover the complete life-cycle of talent acquisition and management. This includes a talent marketplace, application tracking, interview scheduling, and a global job board for sourcing talent; KYC, AML checks, digital contracts for onboarding; global payouts, simple invoicing systems, and other perks for rewarding talent; and skills evaluation, psychometric analysis, and cost analysis for team optimization. The platform's capabilities and resources cater to many roles such as engineering managers, human resources, operations, finance & procurement, suitable for companies of various sizes, from SMEs to enterprises. Besides, CodeMonk coordinates the delivery of projects on a fully managed basis, providing hands-off project management for businesses.
