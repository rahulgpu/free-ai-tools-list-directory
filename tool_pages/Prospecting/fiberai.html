About FiberAI

Fiber AI is an AI-powered tool designed for startups to automate prospecting and outbound marketing. It functions similarly to Marketo but with AI capabilities specifically catered to startups. By leveraging AI, the tool automatically finds prospects, conducts thorough research on them, and generates hyper-personalized outbound messages. These messages have proven to achieve an 80% open rate, which is four times higher than the industry standard.Fiber AI has been acknowledged as part of Y Combinator's Summer 2023 batch, showing its recognition in the startup community. The tool addresses the issue of poor email deliverability rates by utilizing various techniques to avoid common pitfalls that result in emails getting marked as spam.Using data sourced from over 50 providers, including BuiltWith, Crunchbase, LinkedIn, and Gartner, Fiber AI allows users to precisely target companies that meet specific criteria, such as monthly visitors, employee count, funding stage, platform usage, geographical location, and fundraising date. Moreover, the tool assists users in identifying ideal prospects within these companies based on their job titles, tenure, LinkedIn connections, and previous employment history.Fiber AI also offers verified contact information for prospects, rigorously vetting data from multiple sources and providing accurate details like names, LinkedIn profiles, email addresses, phone numbers, and addresses.Additionally, the tool empowers users to create ultra-personalized outreach messages by leveraging data such as blog posts, educational background, previous workplace, and social media activities. These messages can be automatically drafted using the latest-generation language models (LLMs) and can be easily reviewed and approved by users.Furthermore, Fiber AI offers features for AI auto-responses in CRM systems, answering customer inquiries and scheduling meetings through the user's preferred calendar app. The tool has demonstrated success in closing deals with prominent individuals, including well-known business figures and CEOs/VPs of notable companies.By booking a demo, users can gain valuable insights into their growth strategies while discovering how Fiber AI can enhance their marketing efforts.
