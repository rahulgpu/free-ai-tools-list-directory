About Spline AI (Alpha)

Spline AI is a 3D design tool that helps users create 3D objects, animations, and textures using AI prompts. With the power of AI, users can generate objects and scenes simply by describing what they want to create, allowing them to design faster and expand their creative processes. Spline AI is equipped with an AI Textures feature, which generates seamless textures for objects using text prompts. The tool also enables users to modify objects, apply materials, add lighting, and experiment in real-time with teammates. Spline AI is currently in alpha and is available on a waitlist basis. The platform emphasizes its focus on community and resources, including tutorials, examples, and a customer support section. The FAQs section answers common questions about the waitlist, AI features cost, data usage, and current limitations. Spline AI aims to improve and evolve its service through user feedback on its early preview version. Spline AI is suitable for designers looking for a way to optimize their workflow and expand their creative process by incorporating AI into their 3D design projects.
