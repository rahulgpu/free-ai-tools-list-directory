About Depthforge

Depthforge is an innovative application designed to create AI-generated 3D immersive images utilizing the capabilities of Apple Vision Pro. The core function of this application is to transform ordinary text prompts into stimulating visuals, making 3D image generation accessible to all users. Depthforge features a hassle-free procedure for 3D image creation, with simple text prompts producing unique and high-quality images. The app includes a 'Prompt Inspiration' feature, a randomized prompt generator to spur creative ideas. Moreover, prompts can be improved with the app's advanced language model for optimized results. The app also provides flexible orientations with a choice between landscape and portrait modes. Users can fully immerse themselves in viewing their creations in a 3D space exclusively available on Apple Vision Pro. Interactive controls allow users to efficiently manage positioning and zoom functionality of their images. An integrated image library assists users in managing and accessing their generated images. As a supplement to its functionality, Depthforge seamlessly incorporates Apple Vision Pro's spatial computing capabilities, thus enabling users to interact with their 3D images in an innovative manner.
