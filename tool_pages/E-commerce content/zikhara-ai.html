About Zikhara AI

Zikhara AI is a content creation platform designed for e-commerce applications, using proprietary AI models. Its primary function is the automatic creation of diverse e-commerce content such as Amazon A+, product images, infographics, and product text. Users can generate this content easily in three steps: inputting their product URL, confirming their product details, and creating AI content. The tool's AI engine collects necessary data from the product page and creates customized content, which can then be easily edited. This includes compelling visuals and text for listings on various platforms such as Amazon. Aside from automatic content generation, Zikhara AI also features advanced editing capabilities, allowing users to fine-tune specifics such as text, fonts, images, and color adjustments to align with their brand identity. The tool also supports the creation of infographics to highlight key product features in an engaging and effective manner. Additionally, Zikhara AI provides visually rich and contextually relevant product images. Moreover, Zikhara AI is designed with the understanding of user convenience. It enables bulk uploading for multi-content creation and provides secure storage for product information, media, and generated content.
