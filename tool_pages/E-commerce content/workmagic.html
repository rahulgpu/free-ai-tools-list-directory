About WorkMagic

WorkMagic offers a suite of AI-powered tools, designed primarily to cater to the needs of eCommerce businesses. At its core, the application facilitates the automatic generation of creative content, including product or fashion photography, alongside AI-generated copy for multi-channel marketing purposes. This provision allows users to source original content and eliminate the need for stock photos hence, reducing expenses related to creative outputs and photoshoots. The tool features a special creative generator that can create content and post it directly to all specified channels. Measurement and attribution tools provide data-driven insights that could help optimize brand growth, making understanding incremental profits more accessible. Features for profitability analysis and incrementality testing provide further depth to the analytical capabilities of the platform. WorkMagic also remains user-friendly, enabling users to achieve business goals quickly and efficiently. Customers have found WorkMagic particularly useful for tasks such as generating social media content, improving user engagement and shaping image branding styles. The platform has also been credited for its contribution to cost reduction and improving eCommerce growth.
