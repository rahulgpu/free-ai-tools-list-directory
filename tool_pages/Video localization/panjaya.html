About Panjaya

Panjaya is an AI-powered tool designed to localize or tailor video content for any audience or language. It aims to preserve the speaker's natural voice and lip movements while adapting video content to cater to different settings and audiences. This tool can be utilised in various scenarios such as presentations, product announcements, customer education, promotional ads, educational content, interviews, employee training, and social media content creation. The tool works by users uploading their video and choosing whether to adapt the content in the same language or localize to a different language. Users can edit the script text and fine-tune the audio performance, ensuring it syncs well with the visual aspect. The tool will then apply lip matching to the new audio performance, which can be downloaded as a lip-synced video.As an all-in-one solution, Panjaya covers the full adaptation process, from applying script translation and voice cloning, to adjusting vocal performance and lip synchronization. It is highly flexible and can be adapted to match specific use cases. It can translate scripts to different languages, and customers can fine-tune the style and timing of the generated vocal performances. The tool is designed to ensure the adapted content retains all the connective power of the original human performance, capturing the speaker's voice and style, and matching the lips to the new vocal delivery.
