About Puzzle Labs

Puzzle Studio by Puzzle Labs is an AI tool designed to create private chatbots for teams. The tool integrates AI with a peer-to-peer knowledge base, enabling users to break knowledge silos. This solution is designed to help teams bring context to their work and maintain data ownership.Puzzle Studio supports a 'prompt-first' approach where the emphasis is on issuing the right prompts to provide your team with the necessary context rather than focusing on delivering the perfect document. This tool provides an advantage of data storage on the user's devices, reinforcing data ownership and security. Moreover, it facilitates local-first operation as their desktop app also works offline.In terms of model integration, Puzzle Studio is 'LLM Agnostic', allowing users to integrate their own models. Further features include the omission of storage fees and the ability to import web content, such as sitemaps and RSS feeds. Puzzle Studio contrasts with traditional SaaS knowledge bases that generally require an online-only access, centralized data storage on vendor servers, and provide limited options for model and web content integration with consequent billing for storage. Puzzle Studio is designed to be a flexible and cost-effective tool, encouraging user control over data storage, AI model selection and integration of various web contents.
