About Sonny9

Sonny9 is an artificial intelligence (AI) tool designed to assist Certified Public Accountants (CPAs), tax preparers, and auditors with the data collection process. Its primary function is to streamline customer information and document gathering, thereby reducing repetitive tasks and back-and-forth communications. Users can incorporate Sonny9 into their emails, and it will generate necessary checklists, validate documents, and ask follow-up questions based on the received documents. The AI tool works to ensure that all required documents align with the user's checklist, flags any missing or incorrectly submitted files, and seeks clarifications from clients in real-time if needed. These features help elevate the customer experience by making the data collection process more efficient. Additionally, Sonny9 automatically extracts information from documents into various formats such as CSV or directly integrates into QuickBooks, providing a comprehensive report containing all client responses. Sonny9 also offers a unique feature of document and answer analysis. This analysis works to unlock new business opportunities by providing tips and high-level consulting services opportunities, such as Chief Financial Officer services or tax auditing, based on the client's collected information. This efficient, proactive, and insightful approach to data collection aims to enhance customer service and maximize productivity.
