About Suit Me Up

Suit Me Up is an AI-based tool that generates ultra-realistic pictures of you wearing a suit. All you have to do is upload 2 to 5 classic pictures of yourself and let the AI work its magic. In just 25 to 45 minutes, you can have 20 pictures of yourself in a suit. The pictures can be used for any purpose, including LinkedIn, resume and Tinder photos. The pictures you upload, the AI model and the generated pictures are stored for 30 days before being deleted. The tool was created by a French solopreneur, Alex, who used Astria, Firebase, Vue.js and Bulma.
