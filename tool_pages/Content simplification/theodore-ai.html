About Theodore AI

Theodore AI is an artificial intelligence tool designed to simplify the understanding of complex content. This tool can be used to break down complicated paragraphs and technical jargon into manageable, clear, and comprehensible content enriched with practical examples. The technology behind Theodore AI allows users to reduce the complexity of various sources such as technical documentation, academic papers, and smart-sounding tweets in just a few clicks. Features include the capability for unlimited simplifications and tracking of history of simplifications. The tool is available through a Chrome extension, enabling users to easily incorporate it into their browsing experience and enhance understanding of complex language on the web. Developed by Tim Wheeler, the primary aim of this tool is to make it really easy for its users to understand confusing topics and to turn complexity into simplicity.
