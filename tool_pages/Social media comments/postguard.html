About PostGuard

PostGuard is an AI-powered tool designed to help automate and moderate social media comments. It is targeted at assisting businesses in managing their social media presence more effectively while reducing manual labor. With the primary function of crafting responses to social comments, it operates on an auto-reply system where responses can be automated or manually reviewed before publishing. Additionally, PostGuard features an Auto Comment Management system, proficient in identifying and hiding inappropriate comments, responding to potential engagements, thus promoting a healthier and more engaging digital space. Further, the PostGuard AI system offers advanced sentiment analysis by meticulously analyzing every comment, providing a comprehensive understanding of audience sentiment. In the context of ROI maximization, the system helps guide audience interaction towards desired actions like clicking on tracked links or using strategic language to increase engagement. PostGuard operates 24/7, constantly managing and optimizing social media comments, thus leading to efficiency improvement. It allows customization according to specific brand tone and social media strategy which ensures a consistent and personalized experience for the audience. PostGuard's services can be utilized by businesses of various sizes, from newcomers to large enterprises, each allowing a certain number of supported social media pages and comment management limits. The tool currently supports Facebook and is expected to extend its services to Instagram. It is multilingual, supporting an extensive list of languages. Note that usage of PostGuard requires a subscription, which varies in feature availability and price.
