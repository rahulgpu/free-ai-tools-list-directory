About Jogg

Jogg is a leading artificial intelligence-based platform designed to generate commercial videos from a URL quickly and effectively. It offers a user-friendly interface and a range of diverse templates to cater to various user needs. This system is capable of turning website URLs into engaging video advertisements, contributing to an increase in website traffic and sales. Jogg provides diverse AI avatars, creating a realistic representation of the brand, and enriching the interaction of the advertisement. Furthermore, Jogg also features an AI script writer, capable of crafting compelling narratives that make for captivating and conversion-optimizing ads based on data from successful social media ads. It additionally offers a selection of 40 unique voice characters for delivering brand messages, with a wide language range. It also includes utilities for merging b-roll footage from the user's website or Jogg's stock media with user uploads to create tailored advertisements. Jogg supports a varied selection of industry sectors including home and kitchen, beauty and personal care, electronics, clothes, shoes, and jewelry. Users can control video outcomes in numerous ways, such as modifying speech speed and intonation and selecting from a library of AI actors with different backgrounds, emotions, and energies. Moreover, it offers a free trial, a fast generation time, and the potential to use the created videos for commercial purposes.
