About RideAI

RideAI is an AI tool that allows users to check surf conditions at various surf spots. With a simple and intuitive interface, users can search for specific surf spots such as Waikīkī, Huntington Beach, or Cocoa Beach. The tool provides a map where users can navigate using arrow keys and zoom in or out using the plus and minus keys. Additionally, RideAI offers a toggle feature between metric and imperial units for distance measurements. The map displays terrain and satellite views, with the option to show labels for better orientation. One of the key features of RideAI is the ability to view street-level perspectives through the Street View Pegman Control. By dragging and dropping the Pegman icon onto the map, users can access the Google Street View imagery of the selected surf spot, enhancing their understanding of the location. Users can also benefit from keyboard shortcuts to navigate the map more efficiently. These shortcuts include moving left, right, up, and down, as well as jumping to specific positions on the map using the home, end, page up, and page down keys. Overall, RideAI provides a convenient and user-friendly platform for surf enthusiasts to stay updated on surf conditions. By combining AI technology with mapping capabilities, it offers a valuable tool for surfers to plan their activities and make informed decisions based on real-time data.
