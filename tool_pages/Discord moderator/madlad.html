About Madlad

Madlad is an AI-powered bot designed to function as a community moderator within Discord communities. It provides the ability to train the bot, add it to chat, and witness its human-like behavior, which the creators claim is better than that of human moderators. To utilize Madlad, users can invite the bot to their Discord communities with just a few clicks. The bot can then be trained with specific information or links, allowing it to learn and respond appropriately to community queries. The tool offers various pricing plans to cater to the requirements of different types of communities. These plans include different levels of question asking capabilities and web page training options, ranging from limited to unlimited access. It is worth noting that pricing details are subject to change, so users are advised to verify the most up-to-date information.Madlad is built to provide users with a personalized community moderator that operates 24/7, presenting itself as a convenient alternative to human moderation. It claims to offer quick setup, promising users their own personalized community bot in under 15 minutes.For further information, users can access the Madlad website, book a demo call, follow Madlad on Twitter, join their Discord server, or contact their support team. The tool was developed by Rajat and Anushka, according to the provided information.
