About Jurny

Jurny is a Property Management System (PMS) designed to automate and elevate the guest experience in the short-term rental industry, powered by AI and automation. With Jurny, users can manage multiple properties through an all-in-one owner's dashboard. Jurny's key features include an advanced AI virtual assistant, Nia, designed to automate guest communication, review management, and content creation. In addition, Jurny offers a mobile guest app that allows guests to have full control over their stay and access features such as smart locks and thermostats. Jurny also offers an IoT Hub that allows users to integrate with third-party providers. Jurny's pricing plans are customizable. The basic plan is free and includes access to Jurny's PMS, Airbnb Connect, guest mobile app, and Nia AI. The plus plan, starting at $29 per month, includes additional features such as channel management and a website booking engine. The pro plan, starting at $69 per month, includes even more advanced features like a review tracker, dynamic pricing, and cleaning management. Finally, Jurny offers an enterprise plan, which is customizable for users with more than 10 units. Jurny aims to simplify the process of short-term rental management by providing an all-in-one solution, eliminating the need for users to spend thousands of hours researching and integrating with different providers. Overall, Jurny is a convenient and user-friendly system that can help streamline the management of short-term rentals through the use of advanced AI and automation technology.
