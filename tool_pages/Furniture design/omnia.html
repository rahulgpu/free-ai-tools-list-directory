About Omnia

Omnia 3D Model Generator is an AI tool designed to generate 3D models based on text prompts or uploaded images. It is currently optimized for the furniture industry, allowing the generation of models for items like kitchen tables and armchairs. However, it is important to note that the tool is still under development to expand its capabilities to other industries such as food. Therefore, while it may not be perfect for non-furniture model requests at the moment, the tool shows potential for future improvements. The tool offers the option to generate AR assets or upload images, although the latter feature is marked as "coming soon." It supports file formats such as .PNG and .JPG, with a maximum file size of 50 MB. To use this tool, users are advised to review the provided terms and conditions. Overall, Omnia 3D Model Generator presents an AI-powered solution that simplifies the process of creating 3D models based on text prompts or uploaded images, particularly for the furniture industry.
