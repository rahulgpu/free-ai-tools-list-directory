About Swing Savvy

Swing Savvy is an artificial intelligence-based coaching platform designed specifically for golfers aiming to improve their game. Utilizing advanced AI algorithms, the platform provides custom swing analysis and tailored drills to help golfers of any skill level fine-tune their skills. Its major functions include detailed swing analysis, which helps to identify and correct potential issues, and personalized drills developed based on this analysis, which enable golfers to focus on specific areas for improvement. In addition, Swing Savvy offers real-time progress tracking to help users measure and monitor their improvement over time. The application of data-driven insights coupled with expert guidance allows users to better understand their games and enhance their skills effectively. Overall, Swing Savvy provides a comprehensive technological solution for golf practice, providing users with an opportunity to elevate their golf game.
