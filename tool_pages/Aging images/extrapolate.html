About Extrapolate

Extrapolate is an Artificial Intelligence (AI) powered application designed to predict how individuals will age over time. It uses advanced algorithms to realistically age-process user's photos, offering them a preview of their potential appearance in future decades. By uploading a photo, users are able to see their transformed facial appearance for the next 10, 20 years, or when they are 90 years old. The primary function of Extrapolate is to provide a simulation of the aging process, illustrating the possible visual effects of aging on an individual's face. As an AI-driven tool, Extrapolate performs these transformations while ensuring the privacy and safety of user data by providing features such as account deletion. The application also offers a gallery feature wherein processed photos are stored for user access. This tool is the result of a collaboration between creators, Steven and Ajay.
