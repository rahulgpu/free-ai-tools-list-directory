About Storefries

Storefries is a comprehensive social media management and automation platform catering to the needs of brands and agencies. It facilitates multiple facets of social media management including content discovery, content curation, scheduling, and real-time engagement analytics, helping businesses to enhance their online visibility and engagement.Its content discovery feature enables users to find, collect, and distribute the most relevant and data-driven content, ensuring that businesses stay ahead of their competition. With Storefries' built-in RSS feed reader, users can control their news stream based on keywords, regions, and languages. The platform's composer tool enables efficient creation of posts across multiple social media channels, and through the visual calendar functionality, users can schedule posts ahead of time and obtain real-time engagement data. Storefries also offers a media library for storing and organizing digital assets and a post-planning tool, allowing businesses to curate content relevant to their brand and schedule it for publication.Storefries' social media automation tool paves the way for businesses to schedule and automatically publish posts across major social networks such as Facebook, Twitter, LinkedIn, and Instagram. The tool streamlines the management process, saving time and enhancing engagement across all social media platforms. The platform is integrated with various popular social media networks and is adaptable to different business types, industries and use cases such as geo social marketing, social media engagement, social media analytics, and more. It also supports multiple languages and tones of voice to tailor the content as per the target audience.
