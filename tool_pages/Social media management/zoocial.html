About Zoocial

Zoocial is a comprehensive tool designed for managing and optimizing social media marketing efforts. The platform features AI powered automations such as a Facebook and Instagram chatbot capable of conversing like a human for customer engagement, storing information and initiating personalized dialogues. Zoocial allows users to configure and schedule posts on most major social media networks, fostering continued online activity and conversations. Additionally, Zoocial comprises a full e-commerce platform letting businesses create online stores quickly to sell their products directly on their social networking pages or through a browser. The system also includes marketing solutions for email and sms, and helps manage leads. Users can set up automated AI responses to comments and replies on their posts, providing a more reactive online presence. Ideal for businesses aiming to grow their digital footprint, Zoocial simplifies social media management while aiming to convert followers into customers.
