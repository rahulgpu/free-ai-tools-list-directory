About CREaiD

CREaiD is an AI-powered chatbot assistant specifically designed for the Commercial Real Estate (CRE) sector. It utilizes ChatGPT technology to enhance efficiency and decision-making precision in CRE transactions. This tool offers real-time data insights, streamlines transactions, and generates essential sales and marketing content. In terms of financing, CREaiD provides up-to-the-minute verified information on CRE lender terms such as financing programs, preferred asset types, and average interest rates. It also offers access to high-level loan officer and lending executive contacts, ensuring accurate and up-to-date contact information. Additionally, it provides insights into financing activity nationwide and offers information on CRE asset types, financing types, average interest rates, loan-to-value ratios (LTV), capitalization rates (CAP), and recent closing data for deals nationwide.One of the key features of CREaiD is its content generation capabilities, powered by ChatGPT 4. This tool is capable of generating various types of content including sales emails, financial templates, flyers, legal document templates, ads, blogs, website content, and property listing descriptions.Notably, CREaiD aims to automate and expedite property and mortgage data searches, similar to platforms like CoStar and CREXI. It provides access to a wide range of verified commercial real estate lenders, loan officer contacts, and debt financing insights.The data provided by CREaiD is derived from actual term sheets and closed deals from lenders and commercial mortgage brokers across the nation. All lender and contact data undergoes independent verification and is refreshed bi-monthly to ensure accuracy and timeliness.Overall, CREaiD offers a comprehensive AI solution tailored to the needs of the commercial real estate sector, enabling users to access real-time data insights, streamline transactions, and generate essential sales and marketing content.
