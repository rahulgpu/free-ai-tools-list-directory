About Archistar

Archistar is an all-in-one property development tool designed to maximize profit and minimize risks associated with property development. It offers functions for fast site assessment, simplified design and analysis, and risk minimization. The platform consolidates crucial planning rules from numerous sources, allowing users to understand factors such as slope, bushfires, floods, transmission lines, heritage and environmental layers affecting potential development sites. Information on the market, median prices, sold properties and nearby developments is also provided, giving users insight into local precedents and market trends. Archistar offers a high-resolution aerial imagery feature, which allows users to survey a site remotely and identify potential issues. It also includes a design generator that creates building options while respecting site rules and restrictions, plus options for design analysis and running static feasibility analysis on designs. Specific tools include development potential assessment, an efficient dashboard for portfolio management, feasibility checking, and generating a due diligence report. This platform is meant for both professionals and businesses involved in property development.
