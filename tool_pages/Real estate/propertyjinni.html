About PropertyJinni

PropertyJinni is a Generative AI-powered chatbot specifically designed for UK Estate Agents. Its primary function is to transform customer communication and engagement to secure more landlords and ensure business success. The AI operates around the clock as a virtual agent, interacting with visitors, capturing required details and filtering motivated vendors and landlords through fully customizable conversations. The chatbot is equipped to automatically schedule property valuations, callbacks, resolve automated queries, perform sentiment analysis, and manage dynamic FAQs, thereby increasing operational efficiency and customer service. The PropertyJinni chatbot is multi-functional, designed to save time and effort in locating inquiries from vendors and landlords. It can seamlessly integrate with Facebook Ads, create an automatic collection of feedback to improve sales and support processes, and help increase listings. One feature property agencies might find particularly useful is the bot's ability to automatically schedule valuations and callbacks based on the clients' interactions. PropertyJinni also provides a platform for data collection and analysis, ensuring relevant and rapid access to essential information. This AI solution not only operates on your website but also across all social networks, enhancing the opportunity for lead acquisition and customer engagements.
