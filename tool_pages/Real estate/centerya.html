About Centerya

Centerya is an AI-driven real estate intelligence and sales automation platform. It aims at enhancing the operational efficiency of real estate businesses by offering systematic ways to interact with clients, manage leads, and streamline sales processes. The tool provides a centralized system, referred to as 'Clients Center', rendering a comprehensive overview of all clients, inclusive of their personal and property-related data, and past interactions. Another component, called 'Intelligence Center', performs analysis on the collected real estate data, capturing and summarizing each interaction for better operational efficiency and increased revenues. The platform is tailored to tackle common challenges in the real estate sector, such as the lack of tech for analyzing interactions data, manual sales operations, and struggles maintaining the CRM. Centerya is noted for its strong commitment to transform interactions into efficient automated tasks, thus helping businesses in closing more deals and building stronger relationships with clients.
