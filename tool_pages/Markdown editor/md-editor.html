About MD Editor

MD Editor is an AI-powered markdown editor designed specifically for tech writers. This tool aids in creating and managing articles, drafts, and ideas in a consolidated location. It also supports the organization of these items into folders, tagging, and searching for ease of use. Additionally, it draws upon AI to provide intelligent suggestions, formatting assistance, and code highlighting, streamlining the writing process. Furthermore, it bolsters productivity, quality, and efficiency in the technical writing sphere. The editor is especially dedicated to the needs of developers, offering features such as syntax highlighting, code blocks, and support for Jupyter Notebook and Mermaid diagrams. It allows for easy cross-device article synchronization and exportation to various Pandoc formats, such as HTML, PDF, and DOCX. Users can customize their writing experience with a variety of themes, fonts and editor settings. The MD Editor also lets users bring their own API keys to connect to their OpenAI account and publish to their Medium or Dev.to blog. The platform also provides capabilities for importing articles from local files or the web, including from markdown, HTML, and Jupyter Notebooks. Additionally, tech writers can utilize AI to generate first drafts based on article title and writing style.
