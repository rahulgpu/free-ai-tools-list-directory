About PrevessAI

PrevessAI is an AI-powered tool called ChatGPT that specializes in personalized nutrition, sleep, and mental coaching. It offers a science-based solution with a focus on accurate insights and tangible results. The tool functions as a 24/7 personal nutritionist, providing athletes with tailored meal, snack, and supplement recommendations. Its availability in over 35 languages allows for global support, making it accessible to athletes worldwide. One of the key features of PrevessAI is its real-time coaching, ensuring athletes can receive guidance whenever they need it. The tool claims to be the smartest nutritionist in the world, with access to over 5,000 scientific papers and the ability to provide reliable answers to nutrition questions within seconds. It aims to help athletes reach their nutrition goals 10 times faster by offering prompt and precise assistance.PrevessAI can be accessed through its app, available on iOS, Android, and web platforms. It offers various use cases, such as assisting athletes in selecting suitable recipes based on available ingredients, making better choices at restaurants, and providing guidance on nutrition-related matters wherever athletes may be. To utilize PrevessAI, the club or organization purchases a license, granting each athlete access. Athletes create profiles by sharing their personal information, training sessions, and allergies. They can also connect wearable devices for further data integration. PrevessAI introduces a simplified approach to performance nutrition by offering a personal nutritionist for athletes, emphasizing convenience and ease of use.
