About NewArc

NewArc.ai is an AI tool that allows users to generate fresh ideas for design projects using their own photos, illustrations, or sketches. This tool empowers creatives, from freelancers to big-name teams, by providing a simple and intuitive process to visualize multiple concepts in various styles.The tool works in three easy steps. First, users define their vision and composition by uploading an initial image, such as a photo or sketch. Second, they describe their desired result to guide the creative process. Lastly, the tool generates a variety of unique design options based on the input, providing a breadth of concepts and saving time on ideation.NewArc.ai caters to a wide range of design fields. It enables brainstorming and visualization for fashion design, allowing users to explore unique styles and looks. For footwear design, it turns sketches or illustrations into vivid visualizations, showcasing potential looks in different materials and styles. The tool also breathes life into bag concepts by generating multiple visualizations in various materials, styles, and colors.In addition, NewArc.ai extends its functionality to industrial design, interior design, and architecture. It quickly turns sketches into 3D renderings or color drawings, aids in room renovation styles and aesthetics, and generates 3D renderings of buildings in different styles.The tool offers plans for different needs and budgets, allowing users to choose between free, basic, and pro tiers. With NewArc.ai, creatives can save time and unleash their creative genius by generating a multitude of ideas and visualizations in minutes. The generated images are owned by the user and can be used without any legal restrictions.
