About Unfake

Unfake.png by Gaspard+Bruno is an AI tool which can transform fake.png images into true, background-less .png files. It is powered by VISIO Studio, and is free to use. To use the tool, users simply upload their image, which can be up to 200MB in size, and the AI will automatically clean the fake transparency grid out of the image. The final image can then be downloaded instantly. Unfake.png is offered by Gaspard+Bruno Ltd, a company incorporated and registered in England and Wales with company number 09363056 and VAT number GB257596750. Additionally, Unfake.png has an Android and iOS app, a Figma Plugin and a Chrome extension. It has offices in Lisbon, Portugal and London, UK.
