About Dwim

dwim.xyz, also known as 'dwim', is a low/no-code tool designed to simplify the building of complex software. Highlighting the overarching aim of allowing developers and stakeholders to focus more on the 'big picture', dwim streamlines the software development process by automating implementation details through AI capabilities, ushering in efficiency and reduced complexities. This powerful tool is designed in such a way to let developers, engineers, designers, and business stakeholders stop worry about infrastructural obstacles, deployment conundrums, backwards compatibility, refactoring, and compliance among many other challenges that plague the software development industry. By providing a seamless, intuitive platform, dwim.xyz envisions a scenario where software development stakeholders can refocus their efforts on areas that matter most such as modeling, development, and specifications. dwim.xyz is powered by Odoo and owned by Deronyan, LLC.
