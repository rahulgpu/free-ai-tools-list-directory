About Rely.io

Rely.io is an Internal Developer Portal designed for modern engineering organizations. Its main function is to track service quality, health, and maturity, all in a single platform. The platform facilitates efficient management of services and user journeys and allows users to train their AI on the companys engineering knowledge database. The portal promotes adoption of best practices and tracks progress through scorecards and leaderboards. The service reliability management feature helps to monitor and measure the reliability and quality of services. Rely.io ensures developers can build and manage their services and resources without compromising standards. The platform also provides a centralized location for all essential data, supporting integration with a variety of monitoring tools. Rely.io's AI Assistant expedites access to information scattered across various platforms such as cloud providers, observability tools, and git repositories, ultimately eliminating the time-consuming and frustrating process of hunting for relevant data. This efficient, self-service environment for developers improves developer experience and engineering efficiency while encouraging a culture of service ownership and engineering excellence.
