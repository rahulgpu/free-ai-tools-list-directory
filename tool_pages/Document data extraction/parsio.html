About Parsio

Parsio is an AI-powered tool designed to facilitate structured data extraction from various document types including emails, PDFs, business cards, and ID documents. It uses OCR technology to parse invoices, receipts, and other transactional documents. Primarily used for eliminating manual data entry, it automates the extraction process to export data into platforms like Google Sheets, databases, CRMs, webhooks and numerous other applications. One of its key features is Airparser, a GPT-powered parser that transforms unstructured, human-written documents into structured data. Parsio also includes built-in templates to parse emails from platforms like Airbnb, LinkedIn, et al. Beyond parsing, Parsio also offers integrations with various online tools and applications, enabling users to export extracted data directly to these platforms. It is usable across industries, including real estate and automotive, and is utilized for diverse use cases such as lead capturing and table extraction. Moreover, Parsio supports multiple languages and provides an option for custom data formatting.
