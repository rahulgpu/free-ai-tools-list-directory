About Cradl

Cradl AI is a document automation software designed to streamline internal document workflows. Its primary function is to extract necessary data from various types of documents such as invoices, receipts, contracts, payslips, ID cards, and tax forms using artificial intelligence.Cradl AI's no-code platform allows for custom automation workflows and the training of personalized AI models tailored to specific documents. With the capacity to process multiple document layouts in a single AI model, and support for Latin-based characters and digits, it caters to a broad range of document structures and languages.The tool integrates seamlessly with other automation platforms via native connectors and APIs, enhancing the utility of existing workflows. It also offers the ability to automatically retrain AI models over time using feedback collected from users. The software emphasizes data ownership and security, ensuring that users maintain control of their data while staying compliant with security standards.For training and support, Cradl AI offers a human review setup feature and extensive template libraries for quick-start setup and inspiration. Organizations use Cradl AI for its ability to automate and expedite data entry tasks, thus reducing document processing times and freeing up resources for other tasks.
