About Base64

Base64.ai is an AI-powered data extraction tool designed to extract text, data, handwriting, photos, and signatures from a variety of different document types. It is able to process IDs, driver licenses, passports, visas, receipts, invoices, forms, and hundreds of other document types from all over the world. The tool is powered by Cookiebot, a third-party service that helps to personalize content, provide social media features, and analyze traffic.
