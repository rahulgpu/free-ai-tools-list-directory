About AutoExtract.ai

AutoExtract.ai is a AI-powered data entry tool capable of handling various types of documents, including invoices, bank statements, paystubs, and W2s. This tool utilizes machine learning algorithms to go beyond OCR technology in order to accurately extract and structure information from any documents, converting all scanned invoices, paystubs, and other documents into structured data. The AI models of AutoExtract.ai are fed with examples to enhance their learning and improve data extraction accuracy. The system architecture of AutoExtract.ai is modular, which facilitates the incorporation of the latest research in AI, with no impact on your API integration. This tool provides a No-Code AI solution, meaning that data extraction from scanned or photographed documents can be achieved without the need of writing a single line of code or training a model. The tool also has built-in human validation for as long as needed to ensure perfect accuracy of the extracted data. AutoExtract.ai integrates well with other platforms, accepts documents in any format or from any data source and offers flexible export formats. The tool also supports pre-built & custom integrations to connect it with your other business services.
