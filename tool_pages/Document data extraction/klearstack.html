About KlearStack

KlearStack is a Document AI Platform that caters primarily to sectors like BFSI (Banking, Financial Services, and Insurance), Logistics and others. The platform provides a set of capabilities centered around document processing, interpretation and extraction, leveraging OCR (Optical Character Recognition) technology for precise and efficient data capture. Some of the key document processing features include invoice and receipt OCR across the board, as well as specialized OCR for items like airway bills, bill of lading, various identification documents, etc., belonging to specific sectors. Supporting a wide range of document types, KlearStack emphasizes on enhancing operational efficiency and curtailing costs associated with manual document handling. It offers diverse solutions catering to processes in accounts payable, supply chain, consumer loans and ID Card Verification among others. The platform supports integration with existing systems in the business environment, enhancing its ease of adoption. KlearStack also provides insights and guides via its resources platform for users to understand its product better. Established as a trusted platform by leading companies, KlearStack upholds the objective of simplifying and automating document-oriented processes in multiple industries.
