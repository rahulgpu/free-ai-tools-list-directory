About The Digital Dogs

Digital Dogs is an innovative tool that creates cross-app AI non-fungible tokens (NFTs) for virtual worlds, VR, AR, social apps, and games. Users can use these Digital Dogs as immersive companions throughout various digital realities, including metaverses, virtual reality, augmented reality, and social games. The tool attempts to replicate the real-life bonding process between an owner and their dog, allowing users to interact with and influence the traits of their digital canine companion. The underlying technology includes a variety of AI approaches, including natural language processing (NLP), image recognition, and reinforcement learning, to mimic the cognitive abilities of pets and the ways humans interact with them. Moreover, it has cross-app functionality, enabling users to take their Digital Dogs across different applications and platforms thanks to the Digital Dogs SDK. The appearance of the Digital Dog can change from one virtual environment to another, maintaining the personalized user experience. The tool also highlights the developers' commitment to social causes, as it contributes a portion of sales to Guide Dogs of America.
