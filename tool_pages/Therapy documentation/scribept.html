About ScribePT

ScribePT is a suite of Artificial Intelligence (AI) clinical documentation tools that are particularly designed for Physical Therapists (PT), Occupational Therapists (OT), and Speech-Language Pathologists (SLPs). The primary function of these tools is to assist in documentation by reducing the time spent on it, thus allowing for more patient engagement. ScribePT makes use of AI technology to efficiently generate personalized SOAP notes from audio recordings or typed information related to therapy sessions. Users can record encounters with patients, and the AI filters out irrelevant information to create an accurate transcript. The system is trained to adapt to the user's personal documentation style and preferences, thus producing a note output that mirrors the work already done. ScribePT also seamlessly integrates with the existing Electronic Medical Record (EMR) systems, offering the convenience to transfer composed SOAP notes directly into relevant EMR fields. This eliminates the need for manual copy/pasting, making the process user-friendly, quick and efficient. The resultant reduction in PT documentation burnout is one of the notable benefits of ScribePT. The service integrates with your current EMR and comes with a simple pricing structure.
