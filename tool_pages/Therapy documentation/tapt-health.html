About Tapt Health

Tapt Health is an AI Medical Scribe designed for the physical therapy sector, focusing on reducing the administrative burden and enhancing patient care. This tool aids in the documentation process by generating SOAP notes from recorded patient visits, enabling therapists to spend more time treating patients and less time documenting. Medical professionals can record an entire visit or dictate between patients, allowing Tapt Health to extract the relevant information to use in generating notes. These SOAP notes then appear in the existing EMR system for review and approval. Tapt Health thus allows streamlined management of documentation tasks, reducing after-session hours spent on paperwork. The software is designed to be user-friendly and integrates with existing EMR platforms, demonstrating an agnostic approach to varying health system software. Furthermore, Tapt Health places a premium on security factors, being fully HIPAA compliant, and using Vanta's platform for continuous monitoring of security measures.
