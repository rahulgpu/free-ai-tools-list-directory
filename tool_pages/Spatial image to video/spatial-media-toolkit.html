About Spatial Media Toolkit

The Spatial Media Toolkit is a versatile iOS application intended for the dynamic handling of multimedia content. The tool, compatible with a range of devices including iPhone, iPad, and iPod touch, provides a comprehensive solution to manage, review, and compare different forms of spatial media. The toolkit's functionality extends beyond conventional media management, by enabling users to recognize patterns and make insights based on the spatial characteristics of the media. The accessibility and clearly designed user interface of the toolkit make it easily navigable and suitable for a broad spectrum of users, from professional analysts to everyday consumers. It includes the ability to easily download and install across synced devices. While the application is a powerful tool by itself, it also synchronally works with various other applications and accessories, amplifying its utility in a digital eco-system.
