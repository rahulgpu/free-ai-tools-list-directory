About Contentinator

Contentinator is a powerful AI tool available through the Figma Community platform. It is designed to help users populate their designs with realistic content and data. Contentinator can upgrade placeholder text, generate high quality images from a text prompt, and provide inspiration for alternative approaches to copywriting. It is also capable of writing articles, blog posts and taglines. Contentinator is easy to use and can help users save time and stress-test their designs. It is available for as little as $5 per month with a free week-long trial. Contentinator is licensed under the Figma Community Resource License.
