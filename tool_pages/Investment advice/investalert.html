About InvestAlert

InvestAlert is an advanced artificial intelligence investing assistant dedicated primarily to retail investors. Its core offering is twofold: the Portfolio Copilot and InvestAlertGPT solutions. The Portfolio Copilot provides real-time assessments of investment portfolios and delivers accessible, performance-enhancing guidance built on the latest advancements in finance and AI. It also takes advantage of OpenFinance technologies. On the other hand, InvestAlertGPT, designed akin to a dedicated research analyst, can assist users in testing investment theses and offer fresh investment ideas. It is powered by up-to-date information to provide unique insights into stocks and the macro-economy, functioning as a dedicated GPT for investing. Offering AI-powered API solutions, InvestAlert also enables a transformative investing experience for users on trading platforms, delivering personalized insights, matched to individual investment goals and risk preferences. Additionally, InvestAlert can connect portfolios from multiple platforms, providing an instant risk assessment and regular health-checks to preempt potential losses. It is critical to note that this connection does not involve fund transfers, ensuring the user's continued control over their portfolio.
