About Streetbeat

Streetbeat is an AI-predicated investment platform designed to simplify and automate investing activities. It serves to guide users through the intricacies of financial markets and continuously process large data sets to generate insightful, actionable trends and decisions on a daily basis. This tool operates as an 'AI Copilot' in finance, much like how the J.A.R.V.I.S. AI system aids Tony Stark in the Marvel universe, providing a central hub for all investment-related information and activities.Streetbeat develops AI Agents tailored specifically to the needs of businesses, helping to streamline tasks and enhance services to clients. This capability allows a synchronicity between technology and business objectives, encouraging the potential for increased efficiency and decision-making precision.On an individual level, Streetbeat presents itself as a personal, AI-powered financial advisor. Users can interact with this advisor to ask about market conditions, get suggestions on investments, and even create tailored investment portfolios. The aim is to provide individual investors with a comprehensive and user-friendly solution for managing their investments.
