About Receiptor

Receiptor AI is an AI-powered tool designed to automate the process of receipt and invoice extraction from emails. This tool identifies emails containing receipts, attached receipts or links to receipts and extracts the crucial details such as purchase amounts, categories, dispute deadlines, and more. Furthermore, it categorizes these documents for efficient organization and management. It features the capabilities to go back and analyze entire email history to gather and organize missed invoices or receipts from previous years. Beyond extraction, Receiptor AI also integrates with popular expense management systems such as Xero, QuickBooks, and Expensify to streamline financial tracking. It offers multiple export formats and supports multi-language extraction, making it a versatile tool for international businesses. An additional layer of precision is provided through intelligent contextual categorization, which automates transaction classification based on actual purchase details rather than just keywords. Across diverse industries and varied roles from academic researchers, digital nomads, to e-commerce operators, Receiptor AI provides efficient receipt management, easing the burden of financial tracking and accounting.
