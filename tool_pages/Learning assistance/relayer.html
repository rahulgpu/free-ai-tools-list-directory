About Relayer

Relayer is an AI-powered tool designed to enhance the learning process. It offers features such as note-taking and video viewing that are designed with the user's convenience in mind. The most distinguishing feature of Relayer is its AI-powered note-taking feature. This simplifies the capturing of key points from video lessons, avoiding the need for manual transcription as the AI does the heavy lifting. The tool also offers an always-on-top video player, ensuring that your video lessons are always in view, thus making multitasking effective. In addition, Relayer stands out in encouraging collaboration among users. It allows users to share notes, highlight important sections, and work together in real-time, making it a tool of choice for group learning. Not only does Relayer automate note capture, but it also organizes notes automatically, ensuring an easy and neat arrangement for easy accessibility. The tool supports multiple devices, hence users can access their notes and videos from any device - computer, tablet, or phone. Through its features, Relayer aims to make studying efficient and retainable, thereby improving the performance of users in their studies.
