About MIRI

MIRI is an AI powered wellness platform designed to emulate a personal wellness coach. It provides comprehensive wellness advice, from meal plans to guidance on stress, nutrition, sleep, and weight management. MIRI utilizes AI technology to interpret input from users about their wellness history and goals. The tool then formulates personalized wellness recommendations based on this information. To ensure these recommendations are effective, MIRI incorporates knowledge from a team of experienced wellness experts spanning a range of fields such as functional medicine, nutrition, lifestyle medicine and chronic pain. User interactions with MIRI are confidential, and the platform can provide reminder notifications to users to help them stay on track with their wellness goals. MIRI's knowledge pool is updated using information provided by the team of experts, ensuring that the platform's recommendations are grounded in professional expertise.
