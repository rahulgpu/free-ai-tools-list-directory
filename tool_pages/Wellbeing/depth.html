About Depth

Depth is an AI-powered tool, developed by Reflection.app, that generates personalized journal prompts to assist users in understanding and navigating their emotions. It leverages AI technologies to formulate specific prompts based on how a user is feeling about a particular situation or in general. Depth aims to facilitate deeper self-awareness and self-understanding by guiding individuals to reflect on their emotions and experiences via specially tailored prompts. Users simply enter how they are feeling and the system provides a tailored journal prompt designed to guide introspection and emotional navigation. The primary goal of Depth is to harness the capabilities of AI to make journaling more insightful and beneficial for the user. Note that, for best results, users are encouraged to be specific while expressing their feelings. This tool is part of the broader Reflection.app, which may offer additional features and capabilities beyond those offered by Depth.
