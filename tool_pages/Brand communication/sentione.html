About SentiOne

SentiOne is a conversational AI platform and social listening tool designed to monitor and engage with online conversations about a brand or service. It uses artificial intelligence (AI) to automate customer service, analyze online sentiment, and even prevent potential online crises. The platform distinguishes two major functionalities: 'Listen' and 'Automate'. Under the 'Listen' functionality, it offers services like social listening, automated reports, online data analytics, and crisis prevention. This allows brands to closely monitor their digital performance, understand customer sentiment and predict trends. 'Automate' is more focused on customer service automation. It includes features like AI bots, bot analytics, a bot builder for creating customized chatbots and voice bots, advanced natural language understanding (NLU), and bot integrations. These tools help automate customer interactions, leading to improved engagement and response times. The platform's advanced NLU enables sophisticated understanding of input, resulting in more accurate and effective responses. Moreover, SentiOne offers diverse case studies showcasing how their solutions have helped different industries optimize their customer service, increase brand awareness, and improve their sales process. In the resources section, users can access informative ebooks and whitepapers on social listening and conversational AI.
