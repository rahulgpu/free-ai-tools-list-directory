About Affistash

Affistash is an AI and ML-powered tool designed to help brands discover and recruit affiliates and partners tailored to their target audience and business objectives. The tool uses automated processes to match brands with potential partners based on provided information, such as relevant keywords, target audience, and a company description. Affistash has a broad reach, not limited to a single network or database, researching across the entire internet for potential affiliates. Moreover, Affistash includes an analytics tool to provide insights about a partner's audience, including traffic, engagement, audience interests, topics, and geographical data. It facilitates connection with relevant people, like editors, publishers, or executives, based on these data. Within Affistash, a CRM portal for partnership managers is available to manage partnership campaigns, keep track of partner relationships, and add existing partners. Tailored outreach can be crafted using a personalized AI assistant. The tool can be utilized by anyone ranging from individuals looking to grow their affiliate programs to large agencies and brands seeking automated solutions.
