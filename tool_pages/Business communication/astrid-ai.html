About Astrid AI

Astrid AI is an intelligent business communication assistant designed to support multidisciplinary communication. The tool leverages the power of AI to integrate audience empathy, structured storylines, training & learning, relevance to context, industry & function insights, and on-brand design into its services. With this, Astrid ensures messages are personalized, impactful, and aligned with business requirements. A key feature of Astrid AI is its ability to understand the nuances of various industries and functions, offering tools that allow users to communicate with authority in their respective fields. It is also equipped with a training and enablement program led by industry experts that provide hands-on training, exercises, and feedback for continuous improvement.Astrid AI is built with several cutting-edge AI models and methods including Computer Vision, Natural Language Processing, and Large Language Models. These are designed to deliver responses that match user requirements. The responses are powered by a large dataset of exclusive metadata stored in secure AWS-managed data systems. One of Astrid's distinguishing features is its adherence to brand alignment, ensuring every piece of communication reflects the brand's identity and values. This has been achieved through design tools such as Doc Exchange and Brand Alignment Metrics.In addition, Astrid has a unique storytelling formula which goes beyond crafting slides. It understands the psychology of the desired audience, crafts tailored presentations following the pyramid principle and adapts insights according to the current environment, industry, and function.
