About Character GPT

Alethea AI is an R&D studio that specializes in the intersection of Generative AI and Blockchain technologies. Their flagship product, CharacterGPT, is a revolutionary artificial intelligence system that enables users to create interactive characters with distinct appearances, voices, intelligence, personalities, and identities simply by providing a natural language description. CharacterGPT is the first of many AI systems that will be available within the AI Protocol, a set of decentralized smart contracts that enable ownership, interoperability, and governance of AI Characters and Assets. The AI Protocol utilizes the Artificial Liquid Intelligence (ALI) Utility Token to regulate, incentivize and reward the various participants of the AI Protocol. The ALI Utility Token also enables decentralized governance, content moderation, and reputation staking within the AI Protocol.
