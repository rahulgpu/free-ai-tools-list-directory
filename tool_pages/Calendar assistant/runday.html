About Runday

Runday.ai offers AI agents to assist businesses in improving their client growth. These AI agents aim to enhance customer engagement by providing 24/7 support, instantly answering customer inquiries, and boosting appointment scheduling - all critical tasks in sales, service, marketing, and recruitment operations. The AI agents are capable of handling numerous conversations simultaneously, providing an effective solution for addressing large volumes of requests around the clock. This system also integrates with team calendars to more easily schedule appointments. For customer service, Runday.ai provides a conversational UX layer designed to streamline interactions and improve response times. Additionally, the AI agent can also facilitate payments within conversation threads, offering a fast and secure method for transactions. Moroever, the AI agents support recruitment efforts by screening applicants, thus saving hiring teams both time and resources. Built with OpenAI GPT-4, these AI agents offer high-performance, scalable solutions that can integrate into existing websites and landing pages. With omni-channel conversation capabilities, conversations can happen via the user's website, landing pages, SMS, and Whatsapp.
