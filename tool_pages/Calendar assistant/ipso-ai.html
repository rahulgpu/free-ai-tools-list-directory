About Ipso AI

Ipso AI is an artificially intelligent platform designed for the management of calendars and assisting in drafting emails. Its primary function is to ensure efficient scheduling, targeting busy professionals and their assistants who need assistance in managing their appointments and correspondence. The key technology powering this capability is ChatGPT, a well-known language model built upon machine learning principles to increase automation and reduce manual input time.
