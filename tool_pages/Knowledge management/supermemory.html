About Supermemory

Supermemory is a digital tool designed to function as a personal secondary brain. It enables users to consolidate information from various sources across the internet into a singular hub. This tool is designed with features that facilitate the organization, search, and utilization of saved information. It provides a range of services including a search engine, a writing assistant, and a canvas. This makes it useful for individuals of different professions or areas of study as well as general internet users. Supermemory also serves as an effective bookmarking tool with the ability to import and organize bookmarks, resurfacing them when needed. It also provides the option for users to input details about their contacts. The tool maintains a high level of commitment to privacy and security and offers the capability to self-host. Supermemory is also scalable, providing a platform for personal use, teams, or groups of friends. The tool also has the ability to integrate with various platforms such as Telegram and Twitter. A key feature of Supermemory is its capacity to learn based on the information saved, thereby transforming the accumulated content into a powerful knowledge base. Additional features include data collection from any website, a powerful search capability, a space for writing with AI assistance and a visual space to arrange saved information.
