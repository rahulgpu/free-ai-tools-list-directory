About Casc

Casc is an AI-powered tool designed to enhance team efficiency by allowing efficient and quick access to company information and documentation. Predominantly integrated with Slack, Casc serves as an internal knowledge base that connects a multitude of popular sources such as Google Drive, OneDrive, Confluence, Monday, GitHub, GitLab, and Notion. To use Casc, users connect their source platforms, add relevant files to build their knowledge base, and begin querying through Slack. The AI technology not only retrieves but also organizes and optimizes the information to meet the users' needs. Casc also features ingesting text and images from your knowledge base, effectively enhancing the searchability and accessibility of all content types. Users can optimize team workflows using TAGS and dedicated Slack channels for individual departments. Analytics are also a key feature of Casc, identifying popular questions and document usage, thereby connecting content with queries for efficient inquiry resolution and resource optimization. The intuitive design simplifies finding information, and its advanced AI ensures quick and precise responses enhancing productivity. Casc prioritizes security in its design, incorporating advanced encryption standards and protocols. Overall, Casc is a comprehensive tool designed for knowledge management, reducing the time spent in searching for information, and boosting productivity.
