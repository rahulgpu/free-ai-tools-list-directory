About Questflow

Questflow is a decentralized autonomous AI agent network developed by Questflow Labs. The platform's main objective is to act as a dispatcher of real-world incentives for autonomous AI agents. In a decentralized approach, it facilitates user orchestration of multiple AI agents, enabling them to take action independently. The tool offers a mechanism to distribute rewards or incentives to AI agents, contributing towards an ecosystem where AI agents engage with one another for mutual benefit. However, it is not stated what types of AI agents can be used, the form or value of the incentives given, or what kind of actions the AI agents can perform autonomously. While the tool promises decentralization, autonomy, and reward distribution, users need to evaluate it based on their specific use-case and requirements.
