About Latenode

Latenode is a low-code platform designed to facilitate integration and workflow automation. It offers an array of tools to cater to various needs, ranging from AI Automations, Data Pipeline handling, to Support for Developers, and tools specifically designed for Marketing, Customer Support, Sales Automations and Business Operations. Users can apply custom code with an AI Copilot onboard their in-built IDE, or employ codeless approaches with ready-to-use nodes for multiple SaaS services, and conditional logic for flexibility. Latenode's integrability with top AI models enables users to automate processes using AI, and scale up their operations. The platform also provides a headless browser to automate web interactions without an API, and pre-built connectors for popular services with APIs. Additionally, education and support are provided through resources like blogs, documentation, videos, and community forums. Latenode provides ready-to-use templates and supports external libraries to be incorporated in your automations. Use-cases cover a wide array of departments from marketing to sales to revenue operations, allowing seamless synchronization of various sections of a business.
