About Doflow

Doflow is an AI-enhanced visual workflow builder for Google Cloud workflows that allows you to combine Google Cloud services and APIs to construct reliable applications, automation processes, and data and machine learning pipelines. Doflow is designed to leverage AI in order to facilitate the design of comprehensive workflows or to simplify individual steps. This tool supports an intuitive, AI-aided creation process, hence making intricate process automation convenient and accessible for users at all skill levels. Additionally, Doflow enables real-time collaboration for teams, thus promoting team synergy via connected integration and interaction, allowing teams to co-create and administer workflows together efficiently. The platform ensures seamless integration, enhancing workflows' functionality and effectiveness through its connectivity with a wide range of popular Google Cloud Platform products and services. Doflow also provides a collection of pre-built workflow templates designed to aid quick-starts and customization of workflows without expending significant time and effort. Moreover, it can facilitate operations in parallel, using branches wherein a workflow can execute multiple and different steps concurrently. On top of this, it also offers a variety of connectors for different Google Cloud services like Document AI, Cloud Functions, Cloud Storage, and BigQuery Data Transfer within a workflow.
