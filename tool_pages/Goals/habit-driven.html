About Habit Driven

Habit Driven is a habit-building and goal-setting mobile app that utilizes artificial intelligence (AI) to help users establish and track their habits effectively. The app provides a personalized experience with an intuitive user interface that enables users to input and access information through text or voice commands. The AI-powered habit analysis offers personalized insights and recommendations in real-time to enable users to monitor their progress and make adjustments where necessary. The configurable habit tracking methods are based on personal preferences, enabling users to track their reading goals by a simple yes/no, time spent per day, pages read per day, a satisfaction score, and more. The app's power of AI enables users to transform their data into meaningful reports and summaries that offer insights on their progress. The app's founder, Mike Jalonen, aims to join users on his self-development mission by providing a platform that enables them to achieve their goals efficiently. The app provides curated content from world-class experts and offers users access to an extensive library of resources, inspired by Mike's two-decade experience building thriving software companies. Users have direct access to experts, and the AI coach feature provides real-time expert guidance that helps them customize their coaching experience and reach their goals. Overall, Habit Driven stands out from other habit-tracking apps due to its AI integration, personalized features, superior user experience, direct access to experts, and curated content. It aims to help users join the self-development mission and achieve a better life by building healthier and wealthier habits.
