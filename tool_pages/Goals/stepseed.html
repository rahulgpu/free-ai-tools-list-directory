About Stepseed

Stepseed: AI Goal Tracker is an application designed to assist users in setting, creating, and achieving their goals. This tool uses AI to help divide goals into manageable milestones, making them more attainable and simpler to monitor. The interface is sleek and user-friendly, providing efficiency in goal tracking. An integral feature is the AI assistant called 'Seedy', which gives help in formulating actionable plans and provides motivation. The application also incorporates a personal journal/diary function, allowing users to document and reflect on their journey towards their goals. In addition, it features curated content for personal growth, productivity, and motivation and uses analytics for tracking progress and providing insights. The app is ad-free, ensuring a distraction-free user experience. Furthermore, Stepseed supports daily reminders and notifications, helping users to stay on track. It is not only a goal tracking tool but also a platform that encourages personal growth and accountability, offered in an advertisement-free environment.
