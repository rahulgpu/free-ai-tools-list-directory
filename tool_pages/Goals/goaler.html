About Goaler

Goaler is a goal-setting AI tool designed to help users identify, articulate, and create action plans towards achieving their life goals. The AI tool guides users through a unique flow of questions, allowing for a deeper and more thorough understanding of their goals and aspirations. The users engage in a process which includes formulating what they want, answering to their personal AI coach, and reframing and tracking their goal on a new, deeper level. Upon completing the questionnaire, users get a clearly defined goal that resonates with their personal circumstances, equipped with further insights into the optimal timing, potential collaborators, and strategies for achieving it. Goaler is especially useful for individuals at a crossroads in life or professionals seeking career advancement. The tool also supports users in recognizing achievement of their goals, encouraging both personal perception and external validation of success. Goaler transforms goal-setting from vague aspirations into specific, actionable plans.
