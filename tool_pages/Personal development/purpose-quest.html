About Purpose Quest

Purpose Quest is an AI-powered tool that aids in increasing personal self-awareness and improving quality of life. It generates a comprehensive self-reflection report by analysing users' life story. The tool delves into the intricacies of this information to recognise hidden patterns, confront users with essential truths, and provide a deeper understanding of their motivations, desires, and core values. Through this process, it aims to help users identify their true passions, overcome obstacles, and develop steps to achieve their goals. Purpose Quest also has a focus on overcoming limiting beliefs by unlocking deep-seated insights and empowering users to take action. It utilizes various psychological frameworks, including Viktor Frankl's Logotherapy, Carl Rogers' Person-Centered Therapy, Positive Psychology, and the PERMA Model, to assist individuals in their quest for purpose and enhanced self-awareness. The insights are presented in an online report that can easily be accessed and retained by users. Users have found the detailed report to be beneficial, as it equips them with a different perspective on their lives and prompts actionable, realistic visions of their future.
