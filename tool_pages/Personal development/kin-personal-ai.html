About Kin – Personal AI

Kin is a personal AI designed to assist in diverse aspects of your private life. With its unique design of privacy and memory in consideration, Kin provides support and guidance suited to your personal needs. As an AI, Kin communicates with users and dispenses advice on approaching difficult situations, helping to increase the user's confidence, emotional preparedness, and reduce anxiety. In addition, Kin enables expression of feelings and offers fresh perspectives to provide clarity and calmness, and it can serve as a safe space for offloading thoughts. Kin is capable of inspiring users by generating refreshing ideas and offering support for any issues in a user's life. Kin can assist in navigating awkward or difficult conversations with people around you, and it can even offer aid and advice to optimize your time by managing tasks, organizing priorities, and dedicating more time for valuable things. Kin's design ensures that your data resides solely on your device, with the use of Self-Sovereign Identity (SSI) components to keep your data encrypted and under your control. It employs Azure Confidential Computing to securely run large models while preserving the privacy of your data. As an AI product, Kin focuses not only on delivering quality service but also on building a trusting relationship with its users.
