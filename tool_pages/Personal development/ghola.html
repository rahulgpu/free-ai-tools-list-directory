About Ghola

Ghola is an AI-based chatbot service intended to foster introspection, personal development, and self-awareness. The tool offers users a safe and secure environment for exploring personal thoughts and emotions. An innovative aspect of this tool is its deployability in important interactive discussions, enhancing the ability of users to realize their maximum potential. Ghola provides users the option to create multiple chatbots with different personalities and interests, designed by setting customizable parameters. Additionally, users have the option to share their chatbot profiles and receive feedback on their bot personalities, or share insightful and entertaining conversations. The tool offers a backend API for integration into other projects and services. A unique feature under development is the ability to enhance chatbots with various forms of supporting documents such as images, PDFs, etc. that the chatbot can reference. Ghola ensures high levels of privacy and security, accounts are provided through Google OAuth, and private or public conversational data is not stored except in cases where the user chooses to share it. The tool's AI is capable of using Natural Language Processing (NLP) and large language models to accurately understand and respond to user inputs.
