About Just Story It

Just Story It is a mobile app that allows users to create AI-generated audio stories based on their own inputs. With this tool, users can transform their ideas into engaging narratives by creating characters, choosing genres, durations, and custom inputs. The app uses AI to power its story creation process, generating unique and original content. In addition, each audio story comes with a cover image created by AI.The tool also offers a library called Discovery Stories, where users can listen to pre-generated audio stories. This provides a source of inspiration and entertainment for users.Just Story It offers a 7-day free trial of its Starter Package, allowing users to experience the tool before committing to a subscription. The pricing packages are designed to cater to various needs and preferences.Users can subscribe to the tool and enjoy their audio stories anywhere. Each month, users receive a quota for transforming their imagination into an audio story.The app is available for free download on both iPhone and Android devices. With its user-friendly mobile interface, Just Story It provides a convenient and accessible way for users to tap into the power of storytelling.Overall, Just Story It is a versatile tool that allows users to unleash their creativity and create personalized audio stories using AI technology.
