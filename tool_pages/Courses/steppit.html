About Steppit

Steppit is an AI-powered tool offering functionalities to create and sell online courses. It enables users to share their expertise step-by-step by developing guides and elaborate course structures with the assistance of artificial intelligence. The tool allows running interactive classes, useful for educators seeking dynamic and engaging ways to deliver their content online. Additionally, Steppit is not just for teachers or experts, but also for individuals interested in learning new skills. They can follow these created courses at their own pace, ensuring convenience and self-paced learning. It's important to note that to operate this tool, the user's device must have JavaScript enabled. While the tool promises ease of use for both course creation and follow-up, detailed specifics about how the AI is utilized, such as whether it helps in automating certain parts of the course creation process or in personalizing the learning experience, are not explicitly mentioned. Regardless, Steppit positions itself as a tool for effective online teaching and learning.
