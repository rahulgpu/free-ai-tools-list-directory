About 7taps

7taps is a microlearning platform designed to streamline content creation and distribution for organizational learning and training programs. It offers a highly accessible model of education, delivering lessons in manageable and short segments, for improved retention and efficient learning. For content development, users can utilize expert-made templates and a built-in microlearning framework. The platform also leverages AI to assist in the creation of mini-courses. These courses can then be easily shared across a number of channels including email, QR codes, SMS, MS Teams, Slack, and SCORM, without the need for app downloads or logins. 7taps also includes diverse sharing options, offering users the ability to embed mini-courses anywhere. For course management, the platform facilitates the tracking of learner progress, providing real-time insights and meaningful data. Statistics can be managed within the platform itself or easily sent to an organization's Learning Management System (LMS). Flexible and user-friendly, 7taps is equipped with features that support a wide array of learning needs ranging from new hire onboarding, and continuous training to compliance training.
