About Auto Streamer

Auto Streamer & Course Maker is an AI-powered tool primarily used for creating and streaming educational content and developing course websites. This tool allows the creation of course outlines across a variety of subjects, from humanities to programming, and it can accommodate over 50 languages with different vocal tones. Users have the ability to customize course presentations, including elements like headers, footers, and web links. They can also determine the length of their courses, from quick tutorials to more exhaustive classes. Furthermore, this application is visually adaptable, offering dark and light modes for course sites. Another notable feature is the flexibility to control the contents depth by setting a word limit for each chapter. The tool requires an OpenAI API key to function, which is used to generate text and audio content. Potential users of Auto Streamer & Course Maker range from students, educators, content creators, and corporate trainers, to web developers, edtech startups, live streamers and lifelong learners. Overall, this versatile tool offers educators and institutions the possibility to expand their curriculums into the digital space, cater to different learning styles, and break language barriers.
