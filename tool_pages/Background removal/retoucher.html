About Retoucher

Background Remover is a free online tool designed for automatic removal and replacement of backgrounds in images. Powered by artificial intelligence, this tool seamlessly removes backgrounds from personal or product photos, digital ads, logos, or icons. The tool supports usage with images of people, products, graphics, and cars, and allows the users to keep their foreground intact while removing or changing the background.Users can opt for a white, transparent, or custom background, or add a new background image to the foreground for diverse purposes. High-resolution images can be downloaded individually or in bulk. The tool also enables multiple images uploading and offers API support. This tool simplifies the editing process by providing essential features like cropping, image resizing, color editing and cutting out unnecessary image parts to meet specific marketplace's requirements. It's ideal for creating images for marketplaces like Facebook, Amazon, eBay, Walmart, Depop, Etsy, Best Buy. Another unique provision is the ability to test product photos in bulk, which is useful for better marketing and eCommerce service conversions. It eradicates the need to hire professional photographers or designers, or learn complex design tools like Adobe Photoshop, saving both time and monetary resources. Focused on user-convenience, the tool functionality is accessible without the need to sign up or create an account, thus simplifying the task of image background removal. By facilitating the creation of high-quality images and graphics, this tool significantly streamlines the process of image editing and background removal.
