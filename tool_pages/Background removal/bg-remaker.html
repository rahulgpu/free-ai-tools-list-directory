About BG Remaker

BG Remaker - Replace & Remove Background is an artificial intelligence (AI) tool designed for image background processing. This extension operates in the field of image processing, democratizing the process of removing an image background and replacing it with a new one. Conventionally, such image editing tasks would require in-depth knowledge of image processing skills and handling various tools. However, with BG Remaker, creators only need to input a brief prompt to generate images that comply with their requirements in a speedy manner. This practical tool not only improves work efficiency but also yields high-quality, high-definition images. This application uses pioneering artificial intelligence technology to automatically identify potential flaws in the image, thereby ensuring exquisite, natural, and precise images in terms of clarity, color, and outline. Features included in this tool are AI removing or replacing image background with the instruction given in a short prompt. The tool also allows for image descriptions to be optimized by ChatGPT and has a built-in feature that saves the history of images created for future reference.
