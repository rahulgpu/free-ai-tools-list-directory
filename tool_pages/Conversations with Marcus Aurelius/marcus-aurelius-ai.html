About Marcus Aurelius AI

Marcus Aurelius AI is an artificial intelligence tool that serves as a personal Stoic mentor. It provides guidance in conquering life's challenges based on the principles of Stoicism. Utilizing a chat interface, users interact directly with the AI, receiving lessons modeled after Marcus Aurelius, a renowned Stoic emperor. The platform offers two main services: a free tier with limited chats and daily stoic lessons and a premium membership called 'Stoic Club'. The premium tier expands the offerings with a larger message limit, personalized mentorship, and Stoicism quizzes. While the tool does require a subscription fee, it is billed annually. Additionally, the platform plans to introduce more personalized mentorship features. To access and utilize the AI, users interact via Telegram, typing in specific commands to initiate chats. To activate their account, users will need their Telegram chatId. Generally, Marcus Aurelius AI is an innovative application that merges ancient philosophy with modern technology to guide users in better navigating their life pursuits.
