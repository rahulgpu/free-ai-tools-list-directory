About SplitMyExpenses

SplitMyExpenses is a free tool designed to streamline the process of sharing expenses among friends, roommates, and couples. Users can create a group, add friends and bills, and track who owes whom. The tool allows users to itemize receipts from images and link credit cards for daily automatic expense imports. Within the platform, debts can be settled using integrated payment apps. SplitMyExpenses supports over 150 currencies and allows secure, fast, and private transactions without selling user data. It features a modern design that gives control and clarity when adding shared expenses with expansive split types, custom paid amount, AI-powered features, and customization. The AI-powered tool also automatically itemizes receipts, enabling users to split per item. Users can view spending charts, integrate with payment apps, and link credit cards for daily expense tracking. Debts are automatically and intelligently shuffled to minimize the number of payments needed for settling up in groups. For additional features, the tool offers premium plans.
