About ElectionGPT

ElectionGPT is an AI-powered tool designed to provide users with information about the various US presidential candidates for the 2024 election and their proposals. It operates as a chat interface, offering an interactive experience to learn about the candidates and their respective policies in a contextualized manner. Developed using Neum AI, ElectionGPT leverages AI technology to process and present information based on the candidates' proposals and other relevant details.The platform allows users to select a candidate from a list that is regularly updated and interact with the system through chats. This approach aims to provide a personalized experience by enabling conversations tailored to individual candidates. By navigating the chat interface, users can access insights and understand the different presidential candidates better.ElectionGPT serves as a valuable resource for those interested in exploring the upcoming election candidates and their policy stances. It does not provide specific numbers or quantitative data but emphasizes on delivering an engaging and informative platform focused on understanding the proposals put forth by the candidates. With its user-friendly interface and AI capabilities, ElectionGPT enables users to easily familiarize themselves with the various presidential candidates and their respective agendas for the 2024 US election.
