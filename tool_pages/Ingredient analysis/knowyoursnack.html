About KnowYourSnack

KnowYourSnack is an AI-powered application that aims to educate users about the ingredients in snacks. It provides insight into the composition of snack foods through an easy-to-use interface, requiring users to simply take a picture of the ingredient list on any snack package. The application then uses its AI capabilities to scan the list, identifying and providing details about all the components, with a particular focus on disclosing any potentially harmful ingredients. One of the key aims of the tool is to help users make healthier food choices by increasing their understanding and awareness of what goes into the snacks they consume. As well as identifying substances, KnowYourSnack also generates a comprehensive report on all ingredients present in the snack. This report gives users a more in-depth look at the content of their food, thus further encouraging healthier consumption habits. The application is presented as a personal guide to understanding snack ingredients, aligning with users' potential health and dietary concerns.
