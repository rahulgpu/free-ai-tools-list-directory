About TranslateImage

TranslateImage is an AI-driven tool designed to translate text from images into over 130 languages, including, but not limited to English, Hindi, Arabic, Chinese, Japanese, Korean, Spanish, and Russian. Leveraging advanced OCR (Optical Character Recognition) technology, it can recognize text within images and call upon Google's translation API to convert the text. A highlight of this tool is its ability to remove the original text in the image and seamlessly inpaint the image background, ensuring a smooth and realistic output. Another remarkable feature is its capability to preserve the original text format during translation, through the use of a powerful editor that can adjust the image's text format while preserving the original layout. Once the translation is complete, the text is written back into the image and can be downloaded in the original size and format. User reviews suggest the tool is beneficial in translating design architecture diagrams, advertisements, product images, and manuals into various languages, making it useful for a diverse array of sectors, from tech to marketing.
