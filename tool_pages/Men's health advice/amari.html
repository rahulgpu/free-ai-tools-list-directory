About AMARI

AMARI is an innovative AI-powered tool designed to revolutionize men's mental health. Acting as a personalized AI coach, AMARI leverages decades of psychological, social, and cultural data to provide adept mental health assistance specifically tailored to men's needs. The tool employs Cognitive Behavioural Therapy principles and personalization to deliver unique guidance based on users' thoughts, feelings, and behavior. Alongside its capabilities as a mental health companion, AMARI can act as a life coach and mentor, furnishing users with help and advice to navigate life's toughest challenges. AMARI is designed to resonate with all men, including those from different races and ethnic backgrounds, thus contributing towards its inclusivity. AMARI's effectiveness lies in its ability to listen to users, offering them the help they deserve. Neatly, AMARI is expected to be available on mobile and VR app stores for easy access. This tool is part of a broader community effort, TheBroCode, which aims to create a supportive network for men to aid their mental health journey.
