About Indie Logs

Indie Logs is a tool designed for startup validation. It provides users with a quick and straightforward way to examine the feasibility of their startup ideas. The tool conducts a swift SWOT Analysis (Strengths, Weaknesses, Opportunities, Threats), generates critical questions related to the business idea, presents potential competitor information, and delivers actionable next steps. The aim of Indie Logs is to offer users a comprehensive insight into the viability and potential challenges of their startup idea before its actual implementation. Indie Logs also offers a variety of additional features including a blog, pricing information, Frequently Asked Questions section, demo, and a dedicated app, providing users a full suite of resources to guide them in their startup journey. Indie Logs also emphasizes on maintaining privacy and adheres to cookie policy strictly for optimal site functioning.
