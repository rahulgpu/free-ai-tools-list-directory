About Pluralsight

Pluralsight is an AI-driven learning platform aimed at supporting organizations, teams, and individuals in boosting their skills and enhancing their processes. The platform offers a comprehensive suite of online courses and learning paths with a particular focus on technology skills. It includes products like Pluralsight Skills, aimed at building tech skills to drive results, A Cloud Guru for cloud-focused learning, and Flow for insights into workflows. Notably, the platform supports cloud transformation, security upskilling, tech fluency, and software delivery, among other areas. It also aids in overcoming complex challenges associated with cloud, reducing cyber risks, and building customer trust. Pluralsight provides various resources such as tech guides, case studies, infographics, and video series. Furthermore, it accelerates onboarding, innovation, collaboration, and streamlining of processes. The platform removes challenges and roadblocks, enabling a more efficient and effective tech workforce.
