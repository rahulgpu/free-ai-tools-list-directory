About SaveDay

SaveDay is an AI-driven tool designed to help users capture, organize, and utilize knowledge. The tool focuses on the efficient capture of information from the internet at a click, thereby enhancing productivity by saving time. It is also equipped with smart organizing features, which help users label and organize pieces of information automatically. Users can effectively search for and access the saved information when needed. SaveDay offers a range of functionalities such as summarizing YouTube videos and articles, enabling AI search, note-taking on websites, web highlighting, and bookmark managing. Its services can be accessed across a variety of platforms, including iOS, Android, and various internet browsers through extensions, making it highly versatile. SaveDay prioritizes user privacy by not storing any personal data. It retrieves information in real-time using search APIs and allows access control, meaning only the user can access the information they've saved. The tool is transparent about its privacy policy, offering clear guidelines in how user information is used and protected.It is important to note that SaveDay distinguishes itself from traditional note-taking apps by its efficiency in information capture across a vast range of products. This simplifies the flow of work as it eliminates the need to navigate through multiple pages or folders when saving information. Furthermore, SaveDay allows multi-device access for saved content, enabling a user to access their saved links, videos, photos, etc., from any device.Future plans for SaveDay include the release of more advanced features designed exclusively for premium users.
