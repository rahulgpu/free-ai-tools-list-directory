About CopyPartner

CopyPartner's AI Article Writer is a tool designed to streamline the process of creating long-form blog content. It allows users to generate unique and informative articles quickly and easily. With the ability to input their own research and expertise, users can generate content that aligns with their own ideas and insights. The tool supports all verticals and niches, making it suitable for a wide range of blogging topics.The AI Article Writer offers more customization and control compared to similar tools like ChatGPT. Users can add context and edit the generated content at each step. The tool ensures that the content is plagiarism-free and generates human-like output. The generated articles are typically around 1500-6000 words in length, depending on the outline provided.In addition to generating articles, CopyPartner provides a suite of editing tools to refine and polish the content. Users can fix grammar errors, expand or shorten the content, and instruct the AI to come up with lists or examples. This helps users perfect their articles before publishing.The tool operates in several steps, starting with providing a brief on what to write and inputting primary and secondary keywords. Users can also select the tone, language, and perspective of the writing. The AI then generates clickable article titles, comprehensive article outlines, and a full-length article draft. Users have the flexibility to edit, polish, and publish the content as needed.CopyPartner's AI Article Writer is suitable for content writers, bloggers, copywriters, and entrepreneurs who want to simplify and speed up their content workflow. The tool offers a variety of pricing plans to suit different needs and provides a free trial with 4000 words of generated content.
