About Mango AI

Mango AI is a tool centered around generating AI videos. It leverages AI-powered avatars, providing a seamless face swap feature to make video creation more immersive. Users can upload a portrait image and script to produce professional videos featuring a talking avatar. Additionally, it allows the creation of AI photos through Mango AI Image Generator. As part of Mango Animate's product suite that includes the Animation Maker, Whiteboard Animation Maker, Presentation Maker, and Character Maker, Mango AI is designed to cater to different video needs. Business users can make explainer videos, training videos, promotional videos, and more, while educators can incorporate AI avatars into onboarding videos, science videos, and educational videos for enhanced communication impact. For personal use, users can generate engaging AI videos with digital avatars to tell their unique stories and capture audience interest. Users have praised the tool for its avatar creation from a static image, time-saving AI voice feature, budget-friendly cost, and user-friendly interface. This online tool also offers a free animation maker for explainer videos, promo videos, training videos, whiteboard videos, character videos, and text videos.
