About Puppetry

Puppetry: For Talking Faces is an innovative application developed by ELBO AI Inc. designed to serve as an expressive animation tool. This application is designed for users who are in search of creative and engaging ways to draft and bring life-like characters to their stories. Puppetry offers an intuitive character creator that allows users to design their unique characters and ensures they reflect the specific traits the user envisions. Following character design, the application allows users to animate their characters, creating dynamic and interactive storytelling scenes. The application comes with an array of pre-made videos for instant use and also equips users with tools to design their unique animations. Puppetry further extends its animation capabilities to photos or images, providing room for users to animate their visual content. The application aims to make the art of digital animation accessible to both beginners and industry professionals, providing an easy-to-navigate interface and a fun-filled user experience. A distinct feature of Puppetry is its text-to-face animation function, designed to animate characters' faces according to the message typed by the user.
