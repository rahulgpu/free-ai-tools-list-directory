About Samp

Samp is an AI tool designed to facilitate and accelerate modernization projects and long-term service contracts for industrial systems. It primarily helps in connecting outdated technical drawings with up-to-date 3D reality captures of facilities, thereby bridging the gap between traditional schematics and modern representations. Samp has applications across various stages of the industrial asset lifecycle and is valuable for owners, operators, and contractors involved in engineering services. By facilitating rapid inventory generation and enabling teams to collaborate on consistent and engaging visual information from legacy IT systems, Samp enables users to identify orphan equipment, update technical information faster, and improve overall workflow. The tool assists users in conducting simple scans of facilities using standard techniques like laser scans, smartphones and drones to generate an inventory of equipment. It also aids in revalidating and updating existing technical diagrams against 3D scans and align names with record systems. Overall, Samp promotes greater safety, auditability, financial predictability, and longer-term business outlook by reducing risks associated with outdated and incomplete technical information. It also contributes to improved service contract execution, increased plant uptime, and reduced pre-visit requirements.
