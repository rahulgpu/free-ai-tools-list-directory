About Copresence

Copresence is an AI-powered tool that allows users to generate a digital avatar or digital likeness of themselves using their smartphone. The process is simple and does not require any special hardware. Users first scan their face from different angles using the Copresence app. The AI technology then creates an avatar from these scans in less than two minutes. The result is a photorealistic 3D model of the user's face that can be used on various devices, including PCs, smartphones, or VR headsets.Copresence offers a range of applications for its digital avatars. For gaming purposes, users can create a realistic digital twin to use in their favorite games, allowing them to become the main character. The tool also caters to virtual production needs for developers on a limited budget and accelerates the creation of 3D character assets, working on any game engine.Furthermore, Copresence enables users to be themselves across all immersive experiences in augmented, virtual, and mixed reality. The tool generates a true-to-life digital version of the user, incorporating high-precision tracking technology for realistic facial expressions. Users can control their avatars in real-time throughout the metaverse.Additionally, Copresence enhances video calls by translating real facial expressions and non-verbal cues to the user's avatar in real-time. This creates a true immersive atmosphere for virtual meetings, reducing Zoom fatigue and bandwidth usage.Overall, Copresence provides an accessible and powerful solution for users to create digital avatars, offering various applications in gaming, metaverse experiences, and video calls.
