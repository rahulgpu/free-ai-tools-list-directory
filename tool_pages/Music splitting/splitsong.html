About SplitSong

SplitSong.com is a web-based tool that leverages artificial intelligence to decompose songs into instrument tracks. Upon uploading a song directly or from YouTube, the AI within the platform processes the song and splits it into multiple tracks representing distinct musical components. For instance, the separated tracks may include drums and percussions, keyboards and other instruments, bass lines, and voices and choirs. Users have the ability to download these individual tracks once the splitting process is complete. This particular tool was created by Mark Doppler. It could potentially be a valuable asset for musicians, producers, or anyone interested in music production and editing. It effectively allows a deeper understanding of a song's structure by facilitating the study of its components separately. However, the quality or accuracy of the split tracks depends directly on the sophistication of the artificial intelligence used.
