About Learnt

Learnt.ai is an AI tool created for education professionals that aims to help them save time and effort by automating the creation of high-quality educational resources such as lesson plans, homework tasks, ice-breakers, assessment questions, and more. The tool harnesses the power of artificial intelligence to generate content quickly and effortlessly. Learnt.ai offers a wide range of functionalities, including the choice of over 65 tools and AI sidekicks that assist in generating a variety of educational support content. The process of using Learnt is quite simple and involves three easy steps: selecting a tool, entering a prompt, and clicking generate. After a few seconds, the AI-generated content is available for copying and pasting into the desired project. Learnt.ai is designed to augment and not replace the efforts of educators. The content generated by the tool aligns with best practices in education to ensure engaging and impactful lessons for students. The tool is supported by a constantly fine-tuned AI model, which utilizes user ratings to generate the best possible results. Learnt.ai offers a free trial for interested users, and the paid plans are flexible and affordable. Overall, Learnt.ai is a handy tool for education professionals to save time and effort in generating high-quality educational materials.
