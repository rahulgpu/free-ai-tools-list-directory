About BaruaAI

Barua.ai is an AI powered email sequence generator that helps users to craft high-converting email sequences in a fraction of the time it would take to do so manually. By utilizing AI technology, Barua.ai is able to analyze the target audience and generate personalized email sequences that are proven to convert. This eliminates the need to manually craft emails and allows users to focus on other important aspects of their business. Moreover, Barua.ai can help users to stay ahead of the competition by taking advantage of the latest technology. Barua.ai works on a credit-based system, where each credit corresponds to one email. The pricing plans include a custom plan, a standard plan, and a premium plan, each offering a different number of credits. The custom plan allows users to purchase credits at the rate of $0.1 per credit, while the standard plan offers 200 credits per month and the premium plan offers 650 credits per month.
