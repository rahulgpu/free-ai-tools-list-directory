About GPT Stylist

GPT Stylist is an AI plugin designed to work with Figma and FigJam, specializing in creating custom color palettes according to your product description. The primary purpose of this tool is to generate unique color schemes that capture the distinct personality of your brand or product. This makes it beneficial for designing a new product where a specific color palette is required or revamping an existing product's color scheme. Apart from automatic color generation, GPT Stylist provides a prompt library with a wide array of templates. These templates are designed to give designers a head start, simplifying the color selection process by giving quick access to a variety of color combinations. This tool is tailor-made to aid designers in making informed color decisions without settling for generic palettes.
