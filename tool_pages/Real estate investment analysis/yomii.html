About Yomii

Yomii is an artificial intelligence-powered assistant designed to simplify and optimize real estate investing. The Yomii platform offers a comprehensive suite of tools for investors, regardless of their experience level, looking at a variety of investment options, such as Real Estate Investment Trusts (REITs), timeshares, and real estate Exchange-Traded Funds (ETFs). Yomii provides personalized investment matching, by using AI to align real estate opportunities with the investor's profile and objectives. Yomii's significant advantage is access to expert consultations, allowing investors to make well-informed decisions. The platform provides intuitive analytics capabilities, helping users understand market trends, evaluate property values, and make data-driven decisions. The apps sharing feature enables effortless collaboration with partners or advisors, ensuring decisions are based on shared insights. Yomii also connects users with global real estate opportunities and allows them to interact with investor communities for shared knowledge and exclusive deals. Lastly, crucial aspects such as understanding complex concepts like REITs or starting investing with a limited budget are covered in user-friendly Q&A format.
