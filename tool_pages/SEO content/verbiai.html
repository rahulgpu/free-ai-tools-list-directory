About VerbiAI

VerbiAI is an AI-powered SEO and meta content creation tool designed for Shopify stores. It allows users to generate SEO-optimized content for their products, collections, pages, and blog posts in seconds and in any language, saving time and effort. VerbiAI uses OpenAI's gpt-3.5-turbo model to analyze the store's content and preferences, and creates engaging product descriptions that are designed to grab customers' attention and boost sales. The tool also checks grammar errors and spelling mistakes automatically, ensuring error-free content. The generated content is customizable, allowing users to adjust tone, style, and other parameters.VerbiAI's AI-powered content creation ensures that a store's products rank higher in search engine results, thus increasing sales. The tool is built natively for Shopify and is compatible with Online Store 2.0. VerbiAI offers a free plan with 2000 words usage, and users can select a monthly or yearly plan based on their needs. The Pro Plan and Business Plan offer higher words usage and unlimited product, collection, page, and blog post optimization. The Business Plan also includes the ability to generate ad copy for social media platforms such as Facebook, Instagram, TikTok, and Pinterest, as well as access to all beta features.VerbiAI provides live chat and email customer support to assist users with any questions or concerns. The tool ensures streamlined content creation, optimized SEO, and increased sales for Shopify stores.
