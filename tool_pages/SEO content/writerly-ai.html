About Writerly AI

Writerly is an AI-powered Software as a Service (SaaS) platform designed to enhance productivity by leveraging generative AI to support business outcomes. It provides no-code cloud-based tools tailored to meet the needs of both individual creators and large enterprise teams. Writerly includes a range of applications each suited to different teams and tasks. The 'Business Cloud' suite is designed for larger teams, offering department-specific templates, advanced approval flows, API integrations, custom AI models, and more. The 'Creator Cloud' offers a conversational AI workspace, curated templates, and collaboration tools specifically designed for individuals and small teams. Writerly also offers a Chrome extension, 'Writerly Go', that allows users to use Writerly's AI across the web. Additionally, Writerly Chat is a chat-based AI tool with memory, editing, and collaboration tools that ensures consistent branding in outputs. The platform provides templates mapped to various use cases, from product descriptions to job descriptions, ad copy and even press releases. It also allows users to create and share content seamlessly across teams and workflows, maintain brand consistency, and reduce operational costs.
