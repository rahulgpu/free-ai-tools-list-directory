About Predict

Predict by Neurons is an AI solution designed to simulate consumer attention on visual assets, permitting users to pre-test and optimize image and video content. Based on neuroscience, this AI tool offers a variety of features to enhance the performance of advertising campaigns. It employs attention heatmaps and automatically generated Areas Of Interest (AOIs) to visualize audience attention and comprehension of key elements in a given visual content. Predict also facilitates cognitive scoring and benchmarking, allowing users to anticipate customer responses and make comprehensively informed design choices. Agencies, marketers, and designers can use this advanced tool to reduce design revisions and fine-tune campaigns based on insightful data predictions. Additionally, Predict supports a compare view facilitating efficient A/B testing. Users can compare design variations side-by-side and decide the winner based on performance data, rather than solely relying on intuition. Predict is a versatile AI tool, adaptable to various use-cases and trusted by some of the world's biggest brands.
