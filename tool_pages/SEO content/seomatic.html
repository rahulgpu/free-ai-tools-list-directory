About SEOmatic

Seomatic is a no-code tool for automating and scaling content creation. It enables programmatic SEO, helping to boost website traffic. Through its AI writer, users can generate high quality content in bulk with minimal effort. It also provides pre-built templates for creating SEO-friendly pages and datasets for targeting low-competition keywords. Seomatic also provides analytics and fast indexation of generated pages, as well as bi-weekly SEO reports to track performance. Furthermore, the tool integrates with popular website builders and CMSs such as WordPress, Webflow, Shopify, Notion, Bubble and Prestashop, enabling users to optimize their SEO pages without any coding. Seomatic is suitable for creators, bloggers, affiliate marketers, niche owners and other users seeking to drive more traffic to their websites.
