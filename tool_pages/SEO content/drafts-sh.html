About Drafts.sh

Drafts AI is an AI-powered tool that enables users to generate a large number of SEO-optimized articles within seconds. By uploading a list of target keywords, users can receive high-quality articles that are ready for publication. The tool is designed to streamline the process of creating SEO-friendly content in bulk, making it particularly useful for startups looking to increase organic outreach and creators aiming to grow their audience. Drafts AI supports over 100 languages and claims to pass AI detection, ensuring that the generated content appears natural and authentic. Users also have the option to customize the content to align with their specific needs. One of the key features of Drafts AI is its seamless integration with leading blog providers such as Webflow, WordPress, Shopify, Ghost, and Webhook. This allows users to publish their articles directly to their blog or content management system (CMS) with ease. The tool offers a free plan that allows users to create and publish a limited number of AI-generated articles per month. It's important to note that, according to Drafts AI, Google does not penalize AI-generated content as long as it serves a legitimate purpose and does not manipulate search engine results or qualify as spam.Overall, Drafts AI provides a time-efficient and convenient solution for users who require a large quantity of SEO-optimized articles for their blogs or websites.
