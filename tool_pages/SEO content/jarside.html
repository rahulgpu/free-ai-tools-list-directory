About Jarside

Jarside is an AI tool that utilizes GPT (Generative Pre-trained Transformer) technology developed by OpenAI, along with their own solutions, to generate high-quality texts at an affordable price. This tool is particularly useful for individuals or businesses looking to launch a Private Blog Network (PBN), test niche markets, or simply generate cost-effective articles for their websites.With Jarside, users can sign up and gain access to a credit-based system. Each credit allows the generation of one article consisting of 500 words. The pricing for the generation depends on the model chosen, with the GPT-3.5-turbo model priced at €0.20 per 500 words and the GPT-4 model priced at €0.80 per 500 words. Jarside also offers the convenience of scheduling and directly publishing the generated articles on WordPress sites. For non-WordPress sites, the articles can be exported without any issues.The tool provides flexibility in content creation by offering pre-designed article templates or the option to create custom templates. Users can define their preferred themes and keywords to generate articles that align with their specific needs. Jarside also presents an "Automatic Mode" feature, where users can add their site, specify preferences, and let the tool automatically analyze, generate, and publish articles on a daily basis.Additionally, Jarside offers an API that allows integration with external applications, enabling users to generate hundreds of articles directly from their own applications. The tool supports multilingual content generation, ensures SEO optimized text, and provides access to an Affiliate Program for users interested in earning a commission on sales.Overall, Jarside offers a cost-effective solution for generating, publishing, and ranking high-quality articles, making it a valuable tool for content creators and website owners.
