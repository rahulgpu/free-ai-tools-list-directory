About Abun

Abun is an AI-powered platform designed to automate content creation and SEO optimization. It allows you to generate high-quality, SEO-optimized content that aims to drive organic traffic to your website. The AI tool manages your article generation, publishing, and tracking and does not require manual interaction. Abun uses AI to create a content plan detailing which content to write, which keywords to target, and when to post. Optimized content is generated based on targeted keywords, synonymous keywords, related searches, and other content ranking in Search Engine Results Pages (SERP). Abun's proprietary technology layer is built on top of state-of-the-art AI models. The tool aims to help you rank on search engines and increase organic traffic by auto-generating and posting SEO-optimized content for your niche. It also utilises AI for Long Tail Keywords research, creating a variety of content - How-To Articles, Programmatic SEO, Glossary Articles, Tutorials, Comparison Articles, etc. With features like AI keyword research, Automatic Internal Link addition, and Auto Publishing to WordPress, etc, Abun also allows you to create a content calendar that is auto-filled and auto-published.
