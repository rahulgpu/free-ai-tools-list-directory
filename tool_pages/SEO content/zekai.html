About Zekai

ZekAI is an AI platform primarily designed for retailers. It provides a variety of applications including Assistant, Author, Explorer, and Designer. The platform offers SEO optimized product description generation capabilities, unique advertising text creation, blog post generation, social media post and hashtag creation, and image creation or editing tools. ZekAI is designed to speed up and simplify the process of creating product promotional content. The ZekAI 'Author' tool handles writing tasks ranging from general advertising copy to more specific content such as user reviews or product names and descriptions. It also features grammar correction and text translation into simpler versions, and can even help generate content for educational purposes. The 'Designer' application creates unique images and artwork from a text description, and users can edit images using words. The tool aims to enhance the visual aspect of content creation without the need for design knowledge. The 'Explorer' tool learns the contents of uploaded documents and generates answers to questions from the document contents, simplifying document review and research. Overall, this platform offers an array of tools to support and streamline content and document-oriented tasks for retailers.
