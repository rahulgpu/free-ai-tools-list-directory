About Yarnit

Yarnit is an AI-based storytelling platform designed to reduce the time, cost, and complexity of digital content creation. It provides users with an intuitive storyboarding feature to help structure their content, an AI-powered copywriter to generate engaging copy, and a design feature to bring their content to life with auto-curated visuals and audio. It also includes a content auditing feature to measure the impact of the content and allows users to share their content in rich media formats and showcase it on Yarnit's portfolio. With Yarnit, users can create compelling content with the help of contextual story outlines, facts, quotes, and multimedia with ease.
