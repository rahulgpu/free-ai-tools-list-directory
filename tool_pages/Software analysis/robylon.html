About Robylon

Robylon offers an AI product that acts as a copilot for your software. The AI copilot can guide, assist, and perform actions on behalf of users using simple English commands, thus enhancing user interaction and productivity. Through intuitive interpretation of user commands into actionable tasks, the copilot offers seamless execution from start to finish. Additionally, it is equipped with underlying functionalities that schedule tasks silently and work extensively with Triggers and Loops to provide ongoing, uninterrupted automation. Moreover, its robust integration feature allows cross-platform operation, expanding its scope to software platforms like Google Sheets, Notion, Airtable, and more. Insights into the most used features of your product can be gained, thus facilitating an in-depth understanding of user needs and behaviours. You can also customise the appearance and message tone of the copilot to adhere it to your brand's image. The integration of Robylon with your product is as simple as embedding a small piece of code. The AI copilot facilitates an enhanced user experience that parallels with leading companies like Shopify, Amazon, and Microsoft.
