About Guidejar

Guidejar is an AI-powered tool designed to help create step-by-step guides and interactive demos with ease. It assists businesses in improving user onboarding, reducing support tickets, and potentially increasing conversion rates. Users can create complex process guides with just a few clicks, transforming them into interactive product demos, making the learning process intuitive and user-friendly. Guidejar offers the features to capture workflow steps, customize guides with text annotations, images or company branding, and then share the guide with teammates or customers via links or embed them on websites. With its AI capabilities, Guidejar can paraphrase sentences, translate guides into multiple languages, and provide voiceovers. It also allows customization options such as the addition of a company's branding, management of guide steps, selection from different guide views, and addition of a call-to-action at the end of the guides. Guidejar also offers SEO-friendly tags to make the guides easily discoverable and options for users to share their guides and product demos across different platforms. The tool allows the creation of a no-code help center and it organizes all guides under one host. Additionally, Guidejar provides feedback options and the organization of guides in a systematic manner with the help of an intuitive folder system.
