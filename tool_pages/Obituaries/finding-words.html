About Finding Words

Empathy’s Finding Words is an AI-powered obituary maker that makes it easier to create a meaningful tribute to a loved one. The tool guides users through a series of questions about the deceased's family, career and more, before generating a first draft of the obituary. Once the draft is created, users can revise it to fit their needs before downloading and sharing it with others. In addition to the obituary maker, Empathy provides personalized support for families dealing with loss through technology and real-time human assistance. The tool is free and secure, with Empathy never sharing or publishing a user’s obituary or personal information. They also provide a privacy policy, terms and conditions, and an FAQ page for additional information.
