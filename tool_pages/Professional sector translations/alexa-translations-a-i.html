About Alexa Translations A.I.

Alexa Translations A.I. is a professional machine translation platform tailored for the legal, financial, and securities sectors. Trained on legal, financial, and government data, this tool offers industry-specific and accurate translations, prioritising quality and speed. The technology underlying the platform is based on Neural Machine Translation (NMT). The platform also features an A.I. Optimizer that ensures more efficient post-editing, automatically updating A.I. translations with custom grammar, style and content adjustments. An innovative offering of the platform is Adaptive Translations, which allows for faster training of the A.I. on industry-specific terminology for specialized customer or department needs. Additionally, the platform can handle a variety of file types directly, eliminating the need for conversion to text, and provide seamless document collaboration tools. It also offers project management features, including the use of tags and P.O. numbers for easier tracking and billing. With its integrative functions, it supports translation within frequently used applications such as Microsoft Office, and translate selectable texts through the AT Anywhere desktop application. Overall, Alexa Translations A.I. is designed to streamline the translation process, thereby enhancing productivity and efficiency.
