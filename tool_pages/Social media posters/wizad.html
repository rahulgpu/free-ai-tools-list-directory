About Wizad

Wizad is a mobile-based application designed to automate the production of social media posters. Through the application, users can generate customized designs instantly that align with their brand's identity, eliminating the need to hire designers or spend extensive time configuring templates. The tool is particularly valuable for emerging brand owners, online sellers, e-commerce businesses, Instagram merchants, creators and marketers seeking to streamline their design process and bolster their social media presence. Additionally, Wizad offers a variety of functionalities to support diverse marketing needs such as daily posts, festival announcements, product offerings, and gamified content. Each poster produced retains the user's brand colors, font choices, personality and tone, resulting in unique designs that reflect their brand image. Wizad assists in reducing design costs and saving valuable time that was otherwise used to wait for marketers' collaterals to be created. Furthermore, Wizad also provides dedicated support, where social media experts are available to aid and guide users in managing their social media content.
