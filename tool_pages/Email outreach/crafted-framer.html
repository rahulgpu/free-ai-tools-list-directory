About Crafted Framer

Crafted is an AI-powered messaging tool specifically designed for salespeople. It aims to enhance the efficiency and engagement of prospect communication by providing personalized messages. The tool eliminates the need for time-consuming manual searching for prospect companies by offering tailored messaging capabilities. This enables sales teams to connect with their prospects in a more targeted and efficient manner.With Crafted, users can easily copy and paste messages, saving time and effort. The tool also claims to increase lead qualification rates by up to 30%, helping sales teams to identify quality leads more effectively. Additionally, it promotes an increase in sales team productivity of up to 25%.Crafted is suitable for marketing teams, as it allows them to craft personalized marketing messages that resonate with their target audience. Sales teams can automate repetitive tasks, improve lead engagement, and accelerate deal closures. Even small business owners can benefit from Crafted, as it enables them to compete with larger enterprises by delivering highly personalized messages.The tool offers both a free version and a premium subscription priced at $8.99 per month. The premium subscription provides unlimited access to the prospect list and offers features such as saving data and a team collaborative space.With a highly positive customer rating of 4.96/5 from over 4,000 customers, Crafted is highly regarded for its effectiveness.
