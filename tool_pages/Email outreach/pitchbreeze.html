About PitchBreeze

PitchBreeze is an AI-powered tool designed to assist with cold outreach in business marketing contexts. It functions by creating and sending hyper-personalized messages to potential customers. The AI achieves personalization by scanning data signals like company news and social media to create tailored messages. If you don't have a list of leads, the system can generate one for you, contributing up to 1,000 new leads monthly. Moreover, PitchBreeze's features include autopilot email outreach, control over lead approval, and options for sample outreach message confirmation to protect your brand voice. Also, the service needs only a warmed-up email mailbox for sending messages, making integration seamless. To get the system started, users need to provide information about their Ideal Customer Profile and Value Proposition. Additionally, it offers a simple onboarding process inclusive of lead approval and customer service support. The service comes with a pricing plan and aims to provide significant savings compared to the costs of employing a Sales Development Representative.
