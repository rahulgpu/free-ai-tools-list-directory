About Vikara

Vikara is a mobile app designed to help users achieve their weight goals without restrictive dieting. The app uses mindful eating practices and cognitive behavior therapy techniques to remove psychological inhibitors to weight-loss and prevent eating disorders. It helps users recognize their bodyâ€™s hunger and satiety cues to prevent overeating, curb binge eating and build a positive body image by providing positive affirmations.
