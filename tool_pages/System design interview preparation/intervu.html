About Intervu

Intervu is an AI-powered platform developed to help individuals enhance their system design skills. It provides an environment for conducting unlimited realistic system design interview simulations geared toward preparing users for actual interviews. Users can immerse themselves in a true-to-life interview experience that includes the pressures and unpredictabilities typically encountered. The platform offers practice on frequently asked system design questions in real interview style. It also allows for exploration and testing of user understanding through reading top technical papers like Google big table and testing of the acquired knowledge in an interview setting.Further, Intervu integrates a feature for reading top engineering blogs and testing comprehension through interviews. To enhance the learning experience, a whiteboard is integrated within the platform where users can draw diagrams during the interviews. A generative AI model used within the platform has been trained using millions of interview scenarios to provide a rich interviewing experience. To inspire competitiveness and track progress, Intervu features a leaderboard. After every simulation, the platform provides comprehensive feedback to help users learn from their mistakes and continuously improve. The platform emphasises continuous learning and is intended to be a reliable partner for system design mastery.
