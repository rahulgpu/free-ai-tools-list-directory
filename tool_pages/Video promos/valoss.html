About Valoss

Valossa AI Video Analysis is tool for transcription, indexing, and repurposing of video content. The tool utilities multimodal AI for recognizing video, image, and audio content, converting speech to text, computer vision, and emotion analysis within videos. Valossa offers a range of features aimed to facilitate video workflows. Some of these features include automating caption creation, content logging, and in-video search. The tool is also capable of enabling brand-safe contextual advertising by categorizing video scenes according to the Interactive Advertising Bureau (IAB) Content Taxonomy and Global Alliance for Responsible Media (GARM) categories. Other features use AI to clip promotional videos and analyze video sentiment through deep emotion analysis. In terms of safety, Valossa can identify sensitive content for compliance purposes and manage video and audio work for easier repurposing. Lastly, Valossa provides customized AI recognition features tailored to the user's specific use case requirements.
