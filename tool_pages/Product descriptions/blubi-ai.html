About blubi.ai

Blubi.ai is an Artificial Intelligence-driven writing tool designed specifically for content creators. It operates by studying the user's style of writing on social media platforms, then recommends topics, and supplies ready-to-post content based on this analysis. Initially, the tool supports Twitter, but it has plan to expand to accommodate other platforms like LinkedIn and Medium. While primarily focused on text-based content, there are plans to include support for images and videos. Blubi.ai positions itself as a solution to writer's block and a facilitator for content creation, enabling creators to focus more on the craft of their content. It also acts as a permanent repository for users' social media content, giving them the option to remix and repurpose this content using its AI capabilities. Joining the tool's waitlist gives creators early access to the platform when launched.
