About FinCheck by Trezy

FinCheck by Trezy is an AI tool designed to check the financial health of a business. It provides a comprehensive analysis based on an annual financial report that should be uploaded by the user. FinCheck leverages artificial intelligence to furnish a detailed breakdown of key financial health indicators and the company's valuation. This tool can thus enlighten business owners about the true financial pulse of their establishments, aiding strategic and financial decision-making. FinCheck prioritizes user privacy, with reports generated being confidential and only visible to the user unless shared. The user also has the capability to destroy the report at any time, providing further control on its access. Additionally, the tool supports multiple languages including French, English, and Dutch, making it accessible for a wider audience. A demonstration report can also be consulted for reference or to gain better insights into the type of analysis provided.
