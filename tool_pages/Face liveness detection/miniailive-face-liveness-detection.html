About MiniAiLive – Face Liveness Detection

The Face Liveness Detection SDK from MiniAiLive is a security solution that distinguishes between a live person and a synthetic or non-live representation. This technology includes measures against deepfake and spoofing attacks, making it a robust option for ensuring authentic customer interactions. This tool functions by means of 3D Passive Liveness Detection, an advanced method of biometric authentication based on unique physical characteristics. This system operates in the background, analysing facial characteristics for signs of spoofing or manipulation without requiring user interaction. In addition, this technology also leverages facial movements to enhance authentication procedures. The SDK is compatible with iOS and Android platforms and can be integrated via API. MiniAiLive's tool also provides protection against multiple spoofing methods, including paper masks, silicone masks, wax figures, high-resolution videos, and deep fakes. The product is customizable and capable of integration with both cloud and on-premises systems. This solution is particularly suitable for businesses seeking to elevate their security levels and robustness against identity fraud, particularly in KYC (Know Your Customer) processes and settings requiring physical verification of user presence.
