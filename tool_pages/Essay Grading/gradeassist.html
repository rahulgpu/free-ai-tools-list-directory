About GradeAssist

GradeAssist is an AI-powered tool designed to streamline the process of essay grading for teachers. It enables customization of rubric criteria which is then analyzed by AI for grading and feedback provision against specific metrics. It also features a plagiarism checker to ensure academic integrity, a grade prediction tool to facilitate grading decisions, and AI writing detection to differentiate between human and AI generated text. It's important to note that while leveraging AI capabilities, GradeAssist positions teachers in control, using AI to only analyze submissions based on teacher-determined rubrics. This tool effectively reduces grading bias by providing an objective preliminary analysis of student work. However, GradeAssist does not replace the role of educators but rather supports them by saving grading time and enhancing the objectivity of assessment. Its integration of several useful and necessary grading utilities into a single platform makes it a comprehensive grading partner for educators.
