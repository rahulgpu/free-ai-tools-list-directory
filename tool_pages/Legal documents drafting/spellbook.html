About Spellbook

Spellbook is the first generative AI copilot for legal professionals, using GPT and other LLMs to review and suggest language for your contracts and legal documents, right in Word. Helping you analyze contracts and documents holistically. Spellbook is trained on billions of lines of legal text, including case databases, form libraries, and statutes. Ensuring accurate and relevant language in a nuanced, legal context. Key Features Reviews: Instantly review contracts and find potential risks, errors, and negotiation points. Redlining: Suggests precise changes and comments based on your prompts. Insights: Get actionable information and suggestions automatically populated every time you open a document. Draft: Use Spellbooks legal-specific AI to draft relevant contracts, clauses, and client communication. AI Assistant: Task-based automations, helping you work through documents 10x faster. Clause Library: Automatically build you a searchable library of your most used legal language, which can be used while working in a document Chat: Use your own prompts to ask for specific tasks or outcomes. Spellbook takes care of the drudgery so you can focus on strategic work. Free trials available. Built for Law: Trained on billions of lines of legal text including statues, case databases, libraries, and the entire internet. Word Enabled: Integrated directly into Word, Spellbook runs alongside your contracts. Trusted by Lawyers: Powering over 2,000 legal teams
