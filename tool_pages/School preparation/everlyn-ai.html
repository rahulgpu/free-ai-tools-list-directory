About EverLyn AI

EverLyn AI is a platform designed to create personalized AI tutors for educational purposes. The AI tutors function as an interactive teaching tool that delivers bespoke support to every student 24/7. Incorporating an automated assessment system, the AI tutors auto-grade and provide automatic feedback on sessions submitted by students. The platform's versatility allows for the creation of AI tutors in any subject and language for any student group. It gives teachers, tutors, and parents the ability to customize the learning experience based on each student's unique needs. Users can upload learning materials like textbooks, videos, and podcasts to train the AI tutors. This platform also provides interactive learning tools such as quizzes, writing tests, and speaking tests. Once set up, users are able to deploy their AI tutors immediately. In addition to its functionalities, EverLyn AI brings efficiency by saving time for users through automation of assessments. It's a tool created for those seeking to optimize teaching and learning experiences through technology.
