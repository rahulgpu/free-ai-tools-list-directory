About Shepherd

Shepherd is a comprehensive AI-powered learning platform designed to support students in their studies. Its aim is to consolidate various elements of study life into a single tool, allowing for greater convenience and efficiency. The platform is equipped with features such as note-taking in a Notion-style editor, generation of learning assessments, and study planning tools. It also connects students with human tutors, as well as providing an AI tutor for independent study. Shepherd contains a library with various resources to facilitate learning. The platform also allows students to create flashcards, both manually and using AI-generated prompts, with the ability to apply spaced repetition techniques. Shepherd supports user interaction through a chatbot with a daily question limit, and provides smart reminders for study sessions. The platform caters to different budget and feature preferences with its Basic and Premium subscription options. The premium version boasts additional features such as ad-free usage, access to session transcripts, unlimited AI-generated flashcards, and increased file upload limits. However, both versions provide access to the tutor marketplace. Shepherd simplifies the learning process by acting as a one-stop-shop for study needs.
