About StudyAI – Pro AI Chat With GPT

StudyAI - Pro AI Chat With GPT is an AI-driven mobile application designed to serve as an educational tool and a chatbot interface based on the GPT-4 and GPT-3.5 Turbo algorithms. With a rich set of features, the app mainly hosts a variety of specialized AI assistants aimed at providing instant help in a broad range of topics including chemistry, biology, math, and writing assistance. These AI-powered assistants are also capable of conducting a celebrity interview and making art based on user instructions. The unique aspect of StudyAI is that the AI has vision capabilities, enabling the tool to interpret and provide feedback on images. Users can snap a picture for problem-solving or simply ask questions about the visual object. Another interesting feature is the app's ability to generate AI-created art within the chat. The app is equipped with end-to-end encryption to ensure user data security. Further, it can remember past conversations, allowing for engaging and continued interactions. Lastly, the application provides a text scanning feature that interprets scanned documents and prompts the AI accordingly. The app is available for iPhone, iPad, and iPod touch with continually updated features and improvements.
