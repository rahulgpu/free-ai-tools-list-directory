About Dhime

Dhime is an online platform designed as an AI-powered dance coach. It offers users the ability to learn dance effectively and conveniently through pre-recorded video tutorials. The AI software in Dhime analyzes users' dance performance in comparison to the tutorial and provides instant feedback, guiding users on areas of their dance performance that need improvement. Tutorials on the platform can be accessed anytime, allowing users flexibility in learning. Dance instructors also benefit from Dhime as it serves as a larger platform to reach students globally, providing an additional revenue stream. Dhime also offers benefits for dance academies, positioning itself as a home practice tool that reduces student dropout rates by facilitating more effective home practice. Its technology is designed to ensure user privacy, as recorded dance videos are only used for performance analysis and are not stored or shared. Dhime is designed for all dance enthusiasts including beginners, and can be accessed through its mobile application as well as web browsers including Chrome and Edge.
