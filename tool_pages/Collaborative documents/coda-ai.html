About Coda AI

Coda is an all-in-one AI tool for teams to collaborate efficiently and effectively. It is designed to be as familiar as a document, but with the power and flexibility of an app. It comes with powerful building blocks to help teams create the tools they need to succeed. Coda's connected data feature allows teams to roll up their plans into executive dashboards and sync updates automatically. It also integrates with other apps, such as Google Calendars, Slack, and more, to provide seamless two-way communication. Coda's pre-built templates and pressure-tested playbooks from some of the most innovative thinkers and makers out there help teams get started quickly. Coda is a leader in 10 software categories and has been awarded multiple G2 badges. It is also a great solution for enterprise, product teams, project management, startups, OKRs, meetings and more.
