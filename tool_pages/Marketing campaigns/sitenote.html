About Sitenote

sitenote.ai is a no-code AI tool that enables marketers to launch website notification campaigns without requiring any coding skills or developer assistance. It provides a variety of campaign options such as notifications, modals, AI-empowered chatbots and more to promote site engagement, thus boosting conversion rates. The platform presents an easy-to-use interface, which can be operated comfortably even by non-technical users. Apart from setting up the campaigns within minutes, users can also manage all their campaigns from an intuitive dashboard that delivers real-time tracking of campaign performance data. Generative AI functionality is used to create multiple versions of marketing copy, testing them to find the best performing version. The tool also supports deploying an AI chatbot, called Site Oracle Campaign, to assist customers. The AI learns from the site content and uses this knowledge while interacting with customers. The tool is designed with a mobile-first approach, making it easy to launch notifications and campaigns on-the-go.
