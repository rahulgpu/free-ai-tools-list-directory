About xPromo

xPromo is a platform designed to unite projects with similar audiences, facilitating collaborative marketing efforts that can yield views, leads, and potential customers. Leveraging artificial intelligence, xPromo conducts an intelligent analysis of your project and identifies non-competiting partners with similar target audiences who would likely be interested in your solution. To assist project integration, xPromo offers a specialized promotional page tool that recommends partner solutions to your audience and vice versa, thus expanding each project's reach and impact. This tool gives users the flexibility to decide where they prefer to place the promo page and which partners they want displayed on it. Additionally, xPromo employs a fair system approach where AI ensures cross-promotion is balanced fairly, with each project receiving as many views and clicks as it generates for others. Currently, the beta version is available for prospective users to sign up for.
