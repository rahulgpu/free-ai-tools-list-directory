About Steno

Steno.ai is an AI-powered solution specifically designed to offer transcription services for podcasts and YouTube videos. Its main function is to generate transcripts and summaries of content from a wide array of sources, aiding in the comprehension and digestion of information. Noteworthy content providers it services include well-known individuals and organizations such as Lex Fridman, Andrew Huberman, Joe Rogan, and TED Talks Daily. Additionally, the platform is equipped with a chatbot feature. This AI chatbot facilitates easy interactions and serves to answer common queries, allowing users to engage with the content on a more immersive level. As part of its key features, Steno.ai makes use of artificial intelligence technologies to automate the transcription process, reducing the time it takes to transcribe while maintaining a high level of accuracy. In essence, Steno.ai is a user-friendly tool that is aimed at amplifying user experience by providing effectively transcribed content, summarized information, and an interactive chatbot utility.
