About Summarify

Summarify is an iOS application that leverages AI technology for video summarization, focusing particularly on YouTube videos. Users can utilize a variety of summary styles, from simple overviews to detailed breakdowns, to meet their specific needs and enhance efficient video consumption. Each summary includes timestamps linked to the related parts in the video, allowing for easy navigation and deeper exploration of sections of interest. The app also supports custom summary creation by letting users select specific parts of videos. Additionally, Summarify features a search function to discover key moments within YouTube videos. A unique element of the app is the Time Savings Tracker that presents a real-time tally of time users save by summarizing videos instead of fully watching them. Furthermore, summaries can be exported in multiple formats including PDF, Markdown (MD), or plain text. The application also provides the option to download transcripts in multiple formats. Summarify is powered by OpenAI's ChatGPT, which ensures the generation of accurate and coherent video summaries.
