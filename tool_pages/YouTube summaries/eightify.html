About Eightify

Eightify AI ChatGPT is a Chrome extension that quickly and easily generates summaries of YouTube videos. It works by extracting key points from the video and compressing the content into a concise summary. The summary includes the main points and highlights of the video, allowing users to get the most out of the content without having to watch the entire video. Eightify AI ChatGPT is designed to save users time, allowing them to quickly find the information they are looking for. The extension also provides support for multiple languages, allowing users to easily understand videos from different countries. It is easy to install and use, and it can be accessed through the Chrome Web Store. The extension is free and secure, and it is backed by a privacy policy for added security.
