About Video Mood

Video Mood is an AI-based tool specifically designed to summarize YouTube videos and perform sentiment analysis on the videos' comments. This serves to save users' time by presenting a condensed version of both the video content and its feedback, allowing for quicker consumption and understanding of the material. The utility of the tool stretches to digesting lengthy content and facilitating better informed decision-making on whether to invest time in viewing the full video or not. Additionally, the sentiment analysis feature evaluates the comments' mood, offering an even deeper insight into the viewers' reception and reaction to the video. To use Video Mood, users can utilize their Open AI or Open Router API keys and simply paste the YouTube link or video ID into the tool which then generates the summary and comment sentiment analysis. These results can be easily shared with friends and colleagues. This tool, using powerful AI, assists users in navigating YouTube content more seamlessly and productivesly.
