About Glasp

Glasp is an AI-powered tool that enables users to quickly and effectively summarize YouTube videos. It utilizes the latest advancements in natural language processing and machine learning to provide users with a concise and accurate summary of the video in question. Combining the power of ChatGPT and YouTube, Glasp produces summaries that save users time. The tool can be quickly installed on Chrome, Safari, and other web browsers, and requires no sign up to use. Glasp is also available on a range of social media platforms, including Twitter, LinkedIn, Medium, Substack, Discord, and Slack. In addition to summarizing YouTube videos, Glasp also provides users with the ability to highlight and organize quotes and thoughts from the web, as well as access learning from other like-minded people. Glasp is dedicated to providing users with the best possible experience, and is available with a range of privacy policies and terms of service.
