About Skipit AI

Skipit is an AI-powered tool that offers summarization services for YouTube videos. Its aim is to save users' time by generating precise summaries of videos, responding to specific questions users may have about the content, and retaining the chat history. The content areas that the tool can be applied to are virtually limitless, spanning educational videos, tutorials, presentations, and much more. Users can interact with the tool using unlimited messages and enjoy the ability to revisit their saved chats and videos at any time. The platform is designed to support a maximum video length of up to 12 hours, ensuring users have ample coverage for most video content they encounter. Skiptit offers two subscription plans, a yearly and a monthly one, both of which provide unhindered access to its services including but not limited to using the latest AI model, prompt responses, and an inclusive prompt library.
