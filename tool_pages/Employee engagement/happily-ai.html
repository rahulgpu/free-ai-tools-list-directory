About Happily.ai

Happily.ai is an artificial intelligence (AI) platform designed to enhance workplace positivity, engagement and performance. This toolkit is geared towards HR professionals and managers, providing solutions aimed at promoting talent retention and team productivity. Instead of traditional employee surveys, Happily.ai analyzes employee sentiment to give actionable insights. With a prime focus on engagement, recognition, and performance management, it aims to revolutionize the process with more user-friendly and AI-centric solutions. This platform allows managers to understand, diagnose, and resolve engagement issues while also offering real-time performance tracking. The platform boasts features that can improve communication, uncover untapped opportunities and provide actionable insights. Notably, it uses an empirical approach to better manage people and achieve definitive outcomes. Happily.ai includes features for pulse surveys, feedback, real-time insights and analytics, skills assessment and development. Its capabilities extend to personnel hiring for culture fit, thus further endorsing its focus on creating a healthy organizational culture.
