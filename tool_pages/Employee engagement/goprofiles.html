About GoProfiles

GoProfiles is an AI-powered platform created to enhance employee engagements and connections, particularly for remote or distributed teams. The tool includes an array of features designed to foster a positive workplace culture and enable transparent communication. Some of these features include an employee dashboard, peer recognition, generative AI, employee analytics, and a team-building game module. GoProfiles offers a comprehensive employee directory that goes beyond basic information by incorporating rich profiles that showcase each team member's achievements, roles, and personal attributes. Additionally, it utilizes generative AI to facilitate improved interactions, making it easier to locate employees based on location or teams. The peer recognition feature enables employees to appreciate one another's achievements, creating a culture of appreciation and celebration. With the employee map, organizations can visualize their team distribution across geographical boundaries. It also presents an organizational chart to understand hierarchical reporting structures and team connections. Furthermore, the platform allows automated data synchronization from multiple HRIS systems, ensuring up-to-date and accurate employee databases. The service offers a browser extension that enables users to access profiles seamlessly during their workflow. Integration with GoLinks provides an additional method of access, allowing users to find profiles by using specific address bar commands.
