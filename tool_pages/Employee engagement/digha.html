About Digha

digha.app is a platform dedicated to career management and planning, aimed at meeting the needs of small and medium-sized businesses. It supports leaders in all stages of the employee lifecycle. The platform aims to create a culture of communication and increase employee engagement. It emphasizes the importance of individual conversations between leaders and employees to maintain involvement, communication, and motivation. By fostering these connections, digha.app aims to increase productivity and reduce turnover rates and burnout among employees. The platform offers personalized and scalable support to drive employee success. It features an AI-powered system that helps leaders understand and remember the individuality of each employee, providing them with the necessary information for effective one-on-one conversations. Additionally, the AI assists in preparing for and directing these conversations, including setting goals, managing tasks, and establishing deadlines. By providing a user-friendly interface and customizable templates, digha.app aims to simplify the process of building and managing career development conversations. The platform aims to reinvent career management by facilitating efficient and recurring one-on-one conversations between leaders and employees, without adding significant burden to leaders' daily routines. digha.app is trusted by a range of clients and partners who recommend its features and customization options.
