About Invoice Detector

Invoice Detector is an AI-powered tool designed to automatically fetch and organize invoices from various sources. The primary function of this tool revolves around streamlining the process of invoice collection from different emails and subscription services. Users grant access to their email accounts and other service providers, and the software systematically gathers invoice information from these sources. Notably, the automatic collection feature enables users to plug in their emails and services, with the AI automatically extracting any relevant invoices, reducing the manual effort and time consumption connected to hunting for invoices. The tool can also detect new suppliers, spot overcharges, and track renewals, providing smart notifications to help optimize users spending. Furthermore, it can generate expense reports providing a detailed insight into user expenditure with the help of comprehensive charts and graphs. The tool also ensures data security with encrypted data storage and restricted access controls. It also includes an API that allows embedding of the technology into other products to automate invoice extraction. The service offers a free trial and different pricing tiers based on the users particular needs.
