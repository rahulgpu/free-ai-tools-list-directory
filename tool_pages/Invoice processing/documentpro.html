About DocumentPro

DocumentPro is a cloud-native document processing tool that uses AI technology to extract invoices from images and PDFs and export them to CSV. The tool allows for the effortless extraction of invoice information such as party details, payment terms, and item information, using image and PDF files. It is faster and more accurate compared to traditional OCR and manual data-entry methods. Users can upload PDFs, PNGs, or JPGs of invoices, and the tool will accurately extract the relevant information. The tool is designed for small and medium businesses in various industries, such as business services, logistics and transportation, and retail. DocumentPro has a drag and drop interface that is easy to use for uploading invoices. It also provides an upload guide and sample invoices to help users meet the upload requirements accurately. As soon as the files are uploaded, the tool can process multiple invoices in one go, and the extracted data is exported into a CSV file. Overall, DocumentPro is a valuable tool for businesses that need to extract invoice data without wasting time or risking inaccuracies. With AI technology, the tool ensures faster and more accurate invoice extraction while helping businesses maintain efficiency in their operations.
