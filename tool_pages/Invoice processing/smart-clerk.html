About Smart Clerk

Smart Clerk is an AI-powered tool specialized in invoice processing. By converting documents to JSON format, it enables users to extract and manage their invoice data with utmost precision. The platform is designed to eliminate manual data entry, offering improved efficiency and reduction in errors. Users can simply import their invoices, regardless of the format, and extraction of data occurs instantly. Furthermore, the platform offers user-friendly and no-code integrations, eliminating the need for manual setup or adjustments. The tool also supports email forwarding of invoices, enabling users to use their dashboard to upload invoices or forward them via email with the invoice attached. Smart Clerk also ensures ease of invoice management with its straightforward dashboard. Users can review, approve, and edit invoices smoothly, and export data to Excel or integrate directly with commonly used bookkeeping tools. Moreover, the tool has a bank account integration feature, allowing for efficient financial management by automatically retrieving transaction details and matching them with corresponding invoices.
