About Mockitt

Wondershare Mockitt is an online design and prototyping tool geared towards fostering effective collaboration among designers. It offers a suite of intuitive features that simplify and fast-track the design creation and prototyping processes. Users can engage in collaborative UI/UX design work as a team, design interactive prototypes with ease, and create visual flowcharts online in minutes. The tool also provides features for sharing prototypes by providing a simple link, allowing others to preview your ideas easily. Leveraging AI capabilities, Mockitt introduces AI prototype, AI flowchart, and AI mind map tools. Designers can leverage the AI prototype to generate designs and simplify prototyping, while the AI flowchart and AI mind map offer a visually appealing way to represent information flow and brainstorm ideas. Also, there are capabilities for diagramming, creating mind maps, and prototyping. Wondershare Mockitt stands as a reliable solution for all-in-one online design, prototyping, and collaboration needs.
