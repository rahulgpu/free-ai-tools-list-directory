About ZonTools

Zon.Tools is an intelligent Amazon PPC (Pay-per-click) Management and Automation software, specifically designed to optimize Amazon marketing campaigns. Its ultimate goal is to increase the seller's sales and brand exposure precisely and profitably by automating the campaign management process. Powered by proprietary AI technology, Zon.Tools continuously and automatically optimizes PPC campaigns. This process enables users to grow their sales while freeing up their time. Furthermore, the tool aids in launching effective PPC campaigns for Amazon products, thus boosting sales and brand visibility. On top of that, Zon.Tools possesses smart automation features to put PPC campaigns on autopilot mode, creating convenience and productivity. Additionally, Zon.Tools helps expose your products to high-intent buyers with its capabilities in managing Sponsored Display, Sponsored Brand, and Sponsored Product Ads. It enables users to create, control and manage their PPC campaigns directly from the Zon.Tools platform. The software also allows for defensive campaigns and cross-promotion within a user's product portfolio, offering comprehensive marketing strategies. Zon.Tools is suitable for all types of Amazon sellers, including those using 'Merch by Amazon'.
