About Scale Insights

Scale Insights is an AI-driven software dedicated to automation and optimization of Amazon Pay-Per-Click (PPC) campaigns. Harnessed with proprietary Scale-matic Tech, it facilitates the management of multiple Amazon PPC campaigns, providing users with detailed insights and helping to maximize the return on investment (ROI). The product is designed for robust functionality serving Amazon FBA Business Owners. Key features of Scale Insights include mass campaign management with a few clicks and automation of PPC strategies integrated with superior AI technology. The software helps uncover hidden opportunities to increase profits and presents detailed insights intuitively to support easy scalability. The tool allows users to set personal PPC rules and engages in real-time bidding providing an effect similar to a dedicated PPC marketing team that works continuously, optimising campaigns. Additional capabilities include dynamic real-time bidding and keyword optimization all geared towards enhancing profit margin. Providing correlations between organic sales, PPC, and promotions; it offers real-time updates of sales, profits, advertising cost, ROI, and margins. Scale Insights is designed to cater to FBA sellers, advertising agencies, and aggregators, integrating fully managed automation with customized rules building on the power of AI and big data.
