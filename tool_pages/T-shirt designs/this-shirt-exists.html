About This Shirt Exists

ThisShirtExists.com is a website that utilizes the Cookiebot tool to manage and control the use of cookies. The tool helps the website comply with GDPR regulations by enabling users to give their consent for the use of cookies on the site. The website states that it uses cookies for various purposes, including personalizing content and ads, providing social media features, and analyzing traffic. It also mentions that information about users' site usage may be shared with social media, advertising, and analytics partners. The Consent Selection section allows users to customize their cookie preferences by selecting different categories such as Necessary, Preferences, Statistics, and Marketing. Necessary cookies are essential for the basic functioning of the website, while Preference cookies remember information that alters the website's behavior or appearance based on the user's preferences. Statistic cookies collect anonymous information to help website owners understand user interaction. Marketing cookies track visitors across websites to display relevant ads.The tool also provides details about specific cookies used on the website. For example, it mentions necessary cookies related to secure transactions provided by PayPal and cookies used for product presentation by WordPress.com. However, it is important to note that the specific cookies mentioned may change over time. The tool offers a link to learn more about each provider's privacy policies.Overall, ThisShirtExists.com utilizes Cookiebot to obtain user consent for cookie usage and provides transparency about the purpose of each category of cookies used on the site.
