About Slay School

Slay School's AI Flashcard Maker is a tool developed to facilitate effective studying through flashcard generation and interactive quizzes. This AI-powered flashcard generator creates customized flashcards from lectures, notes, or links and allows the addition of images and handwritten notes to the cards. Students can further export the generated flashcards to Anki or Quizlet. The AI constantly provides instant feedback in response to any questions.An extra feature is its ability to generate quizzes from course lectures or any preloaded material, promoting active recall. This feature also enables instant feedback on responses, which can help in strengthening memory and understanding. Students can generate alternative questions to broaden their scope of learning.The platform supports most document formats, making it versatile for various study materials. Users can upload their notes, and the softwares AI capabilities turn these notes into digestible flashcards. The application is compatible with different modes of learning as it can be used on mobile devices. Slay School's AI Flashcard Maker is particularly beneficial to medical and premedical students, providing an efficient method to manage their study materials and times.The tool is underpinned by principles of cognitive psychology such as working memory storage capacity and spaced repetition. The first suggests that our minds can hold up to four concepts at once, while the latter is a learning technique that reviews information at increasing intervals over time. By combining these scientific principles, this tool takes care of the organizing part of studying, leaving the students with more focus, space and time for actual learning.
