About Limbiks

Limbiks is an AI based flashcard generator designed to transform uploaded files into comprehensible flashcards in a matter of seconds. Supporting a variety of files including PDFs, presentations, images, notes, as well as online content from Youtube videos and Wikipedia articles, it provides users with a quick method to create study materials. Users can preview the generated flashcards before finalizing. In addition to generating flashcards, Limbiks also offers features such as generating hints and explanations, multiple choice practice tests, study guides and transformed decks. These studying tools created with the assistance of artificial intelligence are built to ease the studying process for users. The output flashcards can be downloaded and used with popular studying applications such as Anki, Quizlet, Tinycards, Cram, and others. It supports over 20 languages, widening its scope of usage. The platform has a free plan available with limited features, and offers full functionality with a paid subscription.
