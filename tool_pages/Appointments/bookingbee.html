About BookingBee

BookingBee.ai is an AI Appointment Scheduling Software primarily targeted at beauty businesses. Functioning as a conversational AI, it operates 24/7 and can manage calls, analyze client interactions, and market across channels to attract new clients. In addition to scheduling appointments, the AI can capture lead information, monitor interactions, and engage with clients for business growth. BookingBee.ai is designed to offer hands-free appointment booking convenience through its AI technology. The system is industry-trained to understand your business thoroughly and answer client questions reliably. Able to offer services in multiple languages, it operates efficiently even beyond regular business hours. The AI can swiftly redirect the call whenever expert advice is required. Beyond serving as a receptionist, BookingBee.ai ensures that calendars are booked, markets businesses across multiple channels, and helps to bring in new clients. It can also minimize no-shows or late arrivals by sending automatic service reminders to clients. Convenient features include text communication with clients and real-time business insights through a dashboard. The software can also help turn one-time clients into repeat clients by sending personalized emails to clients with deals on special occasions. BookingBee.ai is capable of integrating with other currently used apps, provided they have an open API.
