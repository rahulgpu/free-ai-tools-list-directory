About DorothyAI

DorothyAI is an AI-backed tool designed for intellectual property professionals. It combines artificial intelligence-based search capabilities with centuries of patent law experience to provide a suite of tools that compile data from various sources in minutes, significantly reducing the time and effort required for research and analysis. The tool offers several features for different aspects of intellectual property management. One of its key functionalities is Activ8 Freedom, which helps professionals assess the freedom to operate risk and identify litigious competitors and non-practicing entities (NPEs). This feature allows users to analyze novelty, compare competitor portfolios, and create an international filing strategy using Activ8 Procurement tools. It also provides insights into the market for clients' inventions, helping users identify competitors, quantify query-specific market share, and position clients' IP accordingly. Additionally, DorothyAI offers dynamic reports that bring research together in a shareable format. These reports can be updated on a defined schedule or on-demand, providing clients with up-to-date insights whenever needed. The tool's user-friendly interface and comprehensive functionality make it a valuable asset for intellectual property professionals. By harnessing the power of AI, DorothyAI streamlines the patent research process, enabling users to make informed decisions and minimize risks associated with intellectual property management.
