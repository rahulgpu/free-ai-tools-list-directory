About Tapdesk

Tapdesk is an AI-powered CRM software built with a focus on businesses and sales teams. Its main function is to boost efficiency and productivity by lending assistance to the sales process through data analysis and lead generation. Tapdesk's AI functionality helps to understand market performance and delivers high-priority leads efficiently. One of its notable features is its capacity to find relevant leads to a business's niche using machine learning and deep learning. It also aims to reduce loss rate and boost productivity. Tapdesk auto-generates a pool of leads which can enhance the closing ratio. From a pipeline and flow management perspective, Tapdesk provides an overview of the sales process, organizing deals in the pipeline, and tracking sales activities. The system is designed to closely follow your sales journey, from lead generation to deal closure, ensuring that every detail of your sales activity is monitored. It has an analytics feature that provides insights from your data to help with informed decision-making. Tapdesk is a cloud-based software, providing flexibility to manage sales processes anytime, anywhere. As an all-in-one system, it provides several features covering contacts, campaigns, calendar, invoicing, and app integrations.
