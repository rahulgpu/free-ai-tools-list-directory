About AI Sheet: Lead Gen – CapGo

AI Sheet: Lead Gen - CapGo by CapGo AI is an all-in-one AI spreadsheet tool, primarily designed for startups for lead generation, lead enrichment, lead qualification, and email marketing automation. The tool includes features like WebGPT that combines ChatGPT with internet access enabling users to find a list of companies based on specific criteria and searching for real-time information about target companies. Users can also use the ChatGPT feature within their spreadsheets for crafting personalized messages or extracting information in bulk. The 'Find Email & Verify Email' feature is integrated with third-party email finders to locate and verify email addresses. Emails can be sent directly from the spreadsheet, ideal for mass mailing, personalized customer outreach, and automating email notifications based on spreadsheet triggers. Furthermore, the ChatPDF feature allows users to extract and analyze text from PDF documents in bulk converting PDF content into editable spreadsheet formats. The Image Generation feature enables the creation of visuals, infographics, and custom images based on data in the spreadsheet, enhancing the visual appeal of data in presentations and reports.
