About MakePodcast

MakePodcast is an AI-driven tool designed to assist in streamlining the process of generating professional-quality podcasts. The tool leverages sophisticated AI technologies like Open AI TTS and Eleven Labs Voices to expedite podcast production. Users only need to provide their script and select their preferred voices to generate a podcast. MakePodcast accommodates various podcast formats, allowing for scripting for one single host or multiple co-hosts, and there are no strict limits on script length. The AI tool also facilitated users to incorporate their own voice for a personalized touch with support from Eleven Labs. MakePodcast also has multi-language support facilitating reach to worldwide audiences. In addition, it provides flexibility to convert written content into engaging podcast episodes, useful for re-purposing blog posts or articles. The platform also makes it easier for users to generate voice overs for promoting products or services, thereby benefiting marketers and advertisers. However, users need to sign up for both Open AI and Eleven Labs to obtain the necessary API keys for generating podcast audio. These keys are kept secured in the browser's web storage and are not saved on the servers of MakePodcast. The tool comes with lifetime access at an affordable price and the podcasts can be produced in less than a minute. Lastly, the platform ensures continued support for users while using the product.
