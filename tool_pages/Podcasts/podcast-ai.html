About Podcast.ai

Podcast.ai is an AI-generated podcast that explores a new topic in depth every week. It is a great resource for machine learning enthusiasts, as well as those who are interested in hearing their favorite topics covered in a new way. It is even possible to suggest topics or guests for future episodes, and the podcast can even bring back to life voices from the past. The technology behind Podcast.ai is powered by Play.ht, a platform that provides the necessary AI tools for creating and hosting the podcast. The podcast welcomes feedback and suggestions for future episodes, and users can submit their ideas through the podcast's website.
