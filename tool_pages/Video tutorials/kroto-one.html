About Kroto One

Kroto is an AI-based tool designed to streamline the creation of product tutorials and documentation. It takes raw video recordings of product walk-throughs and transforms them into professional quality videos with effects such as zoom-ins and pans. In parallel, Kroto also generates a step-by-step written guide, creating a holistic instructional package. Users can upload walk-through videos, which are then analyzed and processed by Kroto's AI. The result is a captivating, crystal-clear video and an easy-to-follow guide. Users have the option to export their content for local use or to host it directly on Kroto's secure platform, which offers a shareable link, customizable with a user's domain. Furthermore, Kroto allows for practical integration as it enables users to export video and documentation into various formats compatible with other platforms or help center tools. Currently, Kroto is in its private beta phase, continually gathering feedback for refinement. Its value lies in reducing the traditionally daunting task of creating top-notch product videos and associated documentation, striving for optimal user-friendliness and high-quality outputs.
