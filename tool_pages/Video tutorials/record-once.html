About Record Once

RECORDONCE is an AI-powered tool designed to create video tutorials in minutes. Leveraging artificial intelligence, it edits, translates, and eradicates mistakes from the recordings. Users can assemble tutorials quickly, primarily about their product demos, and the AI intervenes to remove any inaccuracies or errors. Besides editing, the tool offers features like automatic text guides with screenshots, immediate publishing, and drag-and-drop editing. Furthermore, the system is engineered to hide typos, making the recording appear as if the typo never occurred. Also, users can rearrange or remove steps easily without necessitating video editing skills. RECORDONCE aims to optimize the recording process by omitting concerns about mishaps and automating it to the extent that users can concentrate on the tutorial content itself. The tool is unique in its ability to record not only pixels but also code, providing unprecedented editing capabilities. It has an in-built browser that records all HTML and CSS of the web app, understanding interactions like button clicks and typing. Consequently, it can modify everything during playback, including translating the text inside the app to another language. The outcome is a high-resolution and smooth video, which in reality, is animated HTML and CSS governed by the AI. Additionally, automatic voice-overs and instant translations are available to produce identical videos in multiple languages.
