About Diglot

Diglot is an all-in-one bilingual writing app designed to enhance and streamline the writing process. The key features of this tool include being an AI co-writer, translator, a tool for checking grammar and spelling, for paraphrasing, searching for synonyms, generating citations, summarizing and even checking plagiarism. As an AI-enhanced assistant, Diglot refines and polishes your writing, ensuring grammatically correct and effective text. With the help of Diglot, writers can forget about constantly switching between multiple apps for different tasks, as it brings together all these features in a single-interface application.The software is built with AI-powered translation capabilities to tackle language barriers and ensure seamless communication, whether it involves single-word translation or entire phrase translation. Furthermore, Diglot aids in text originality and intellectual property maintenance through an efficient plagiarism checker. Its advanced paraphrasing tool allows for the rewording of text and its summarizing feature helps condense and clarify lengthy texts.For user comfort, hotkeys and a powerful rich text editor allow for precision in text editing with advanced formatting. Additional features like templates for business formats further enhance writing efficiency. The platform offers support for 24 languages and is secured under SOC2 and GDPR policies, demonstrating Diglot's commitment to user data protection. Diglot is compatible across multiple platforms and comes equipped with browser plugins that allow users to leverage its features across all major internet browsers.
