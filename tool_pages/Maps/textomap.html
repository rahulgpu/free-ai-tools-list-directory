About Textomap

Textomap is an AI tool that allows users to quickly and easily turn text into interactive maps. It is designed to save users time and effort by automating the process of creating maps from text. It can be used by content creators, bloggers, travel agents, operators, educational institutions, news and media outlets, and more. It supports many languages, including English, Spanish, Chinese, German, French, Italian, Portuguese, Russian, Japanese, and Korean. The tool can be used on both desktop and mobile browsers and does not require any coding, spreadsheets, or complex tools. Textomap also offers a free Chrome extension that allows users to generate maps in the same tab as they move between websites, social media, docs, and emails. It also allows users to embed the maps to any website or blog and supports the major website and blogging platforms.Textomap also offers a Pro version that unlocks additional features and allows users to create an unlimited number of maps with unlimited views, share map links and post on social media, export maps, and access priority chat and email support. Overall, Textomap is a powerful AI tool that allows users to quickly and easily turn text into interactive maps. It is designed to save users time and effort and can be used by content creators, bloggers, travel agents, operators, educational institutions, news and media outlets, and more.
