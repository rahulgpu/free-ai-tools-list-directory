About Adaface

AI Resume Parser, offered by Adaface, specializes in transforming resume data into a structured format. The tool is beneficial for HR or recruitment teams in addressing queries regarding candidates, summarizing candidate profiles, and identifying prospective applicants that are most apt for specific roles. The primary function of the AI Resume Parser is to analyze resumes systematically and extract relevant information, effectively determining the suitability of candidates for a particular job role. Adaface also provides a comprehensive platform for a variety of assessments such as programming, IT, Artificial Intelligence and cognitive ability among others. They maintain a diverse test library which encompasses different languages, role specific, typing and finance assessments to name a few. Also, this tool integrates with Adaface's other features including science-backed tests for accuracy and an assortment of resources for HR experts, thereby forming a cohesive recruitment and HR management platform.
