About Brainner

Brainner is an advanced AI-driven tool designed to enhance the recruitment process by automating resume comparisons and candidate search. This tool integrates seamlessly with top HR software, ensuring streamlined recruitment workflow. Brainner is capable of screening hundreds of resumes within a short span, enhancing speed, and accuracy in hiring.The tool has a unique feature where the user uploads a job description and it automatically analyzes and extracts key criteria for screening candidates. The AI categorizes the candidates into areas such as 'Work Experience', 'Education', 'Skills', among others. Additionally, the AI provides suggestions to make job descriptions more appealing and recommends criteria which should be considered mandatory or preferred.Another feature of Brainner is the customization of criteria for the role. Users can tailor the screening criteria based on their recruitment standards and can modify or delete less important criteria. Additional criteria that are not mentioned in the job description can also be added, and users can classify selected criteria as either mandatory or preferred.Additionally, Brainner offers real-time screening of applicant profiles, ranking candidates based on how they align with the role. Users can access detailed reports for each candidate to decide whether to proceed with an interview. The AI leverages tailored filters to analyze candidates based on unique recruiting parameters and provides dynamic candidate ranking based on various factors such as mandatory or preferred requirements.
