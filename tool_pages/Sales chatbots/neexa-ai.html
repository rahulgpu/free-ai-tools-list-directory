About Neexa AI

Neexa AI is an artificial intelligence-powered sales assistant application designed to assist businesses by handling a variety of sales-related activities. It operates on a 24/7 basis, engaging prospects with human-like text chat with the objective to convert them into customers. After creating an account, businesses can upload their product and service information onto the Neexa dashboard. Following this, the AI agent is available to be integrated as a widget on a user's website, a full-page link, or in tandem with a user's WhatsApp Business. As a tool, it ensures prospective customers are engaged and responded to strategically and in a timely manner. Furthermore, the platform includes negotiation skills, industry-specific knowledge, and human-like responses to assist users. Neexa comes with an in-built AI-powered dashboard that allows users to oversee and intervene in chats, update business and product details, and customize the tone and voice of the AI agent. It also provides real-time AI-generated reports and briefs, with the capacity to teach the AI agent about a user's process. Lastly, users have the option of upgrading their package and adding on several features and capabilities.
