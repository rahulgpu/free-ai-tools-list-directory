About Octocom

Octocom is an AI-powered chatbot specifically designed for eCommerce businesses. Its main features include instant 24/7 product explanations, comparisons, and recommendations, intending to increase conversion rates. It has the ability to connect directly with help desks such as Gorgias and Zendesk, allowing for seamless integration into existing workflows. It keeps updated with the product catalog, discounts, and orders by linking to all eCommerce platforms, including custom ones. Octocom strives to provide human-like conversations, aligning with the brand's personality. This solution includes a feature for intelligent human handoff, in which the AI can assess when a complex issue needs to be escalated to a human support team. This chatbot can handle inquiries across multiple channels including email, Instagram, Messenger, WhatsApp, SMS, and others, providing a consistent support experience. Additionally, it can assist with shopping choices, offering product recommendations, dealing with product inquiries and providing product comparisons. The tool can also be customized to not only match the brand voice but also to automate responses and proactively solve problems such as handling order tracking, returns, refunds, and complaints. It comes with analytics functionality for performance insights and is designed to be multilingual, managing customer interactions in over 100 languages.
