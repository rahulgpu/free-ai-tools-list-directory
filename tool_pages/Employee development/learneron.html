About LearnerOn

LearnerOn is an AI-powered smart learning platform designed to cater for the learning needs of companies irrespective of their size. The platform focuses on individualized learning experiences by offering highly customizable training and development programs. It provides talent leaders with features for tracking skills and knowledge progress on individual or team levels, assisting in the transition to a Learning Organization. The platform integrates learning resources, skills, and occupations, streamlining the learning and development (L&D) process. It employs an AI engine to offer personalized and tailored learning paths, fostering an engaging learning experience. The ability to enable user self-service, peer-to-peer, and top-down learning modes, along with tracking learning progress, helps monitor skill growth in colleagues. LearnerOn also facilitates the sharing of learning paths among team members, prompting spontaneous learning activities. A key feature of LearnerOn is its extensive catalogue that integrates a multitude of learning materials with a comprehensive range of roles and skills. The platform also offers ready-to-use and customizable learning paths, and a variety of templates for skills and roles. Users can engage in two-way feedback and mentorship channels, enhancing the learning experience.
