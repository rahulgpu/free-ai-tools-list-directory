About OneAccord

OneAccord is a real-time AI translation platform, specially designed for churches. With a focus on inclusivity and accessibility, it helps churches overcome language barriers and become accessible to the hearing impaired through live captions and translations. It also provides an AI model specially trained for a church context, enhancing the interpretation of biblical terminology and reducing speech comprehension errors. Users may access the translation and transcription directly on their mobile device's browser, without the need for a download. Brands can apply their custom branding and use a branded URL. The software includes a moderation feature to reach maximum transcription accuracy, giving users the ability to correct each line of text captured before it's translated and distributed. Transcripts and translations can be downloaded post-session. The platform is available either as a monthly subscription or one-off usage, with a pricing calculator to estimate costs based on hours, languages, and listeners. Supported languages currently include Norwegian, Finnish, Tamil, Dutch, and 22 others, and more languages can be added upon request. The platform aims to facilitate a place of belonging irrespective of native language.
