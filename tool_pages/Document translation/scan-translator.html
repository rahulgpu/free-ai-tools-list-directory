About Scan Translator

Scan Translator is an online extension tool designed for translating raw manga and scanned documents. Armed with artificial intelligence, it translates scans and images efficiently, promising a high degree of accuracy. Its ease of use makes it suitable regardless of the user's technological expertise or language proficiency. This tool goes beyond traditional text documents to accommodate manga fans and translation for graphical content. The Scan Translator supports a broad range of over 50 languages making it a versatile solution for professional translations and personal use as well. Its ability to translate even vertically aligned text enhances its utility when handling different document layouts. In addition to this, it provides translation capabilities for a variety of image types, irrespective of the original language. Another distinguishing feature of Scan Translator is its integration with APIs from Deepl and Google Vision to ensure optimal performance. It also offers a free plan with a certain number of translation credits per week. For more frequent usage, additional credits can be purchased to accommodate higher translation demands. Overall, Scan Translator stands out as a comprehensive and user-friendly interface for translating manga or any kind of scanned document with efficiency.
