About LANDR

LANDR Studio is a comprehensive music production suite that offers a range of tools and features to support musicians and producers. The core feature of the tool is its AI mastering engine, which listens to your music and creates precise and personalized masters without relying on presets. This allows users to put the finishing touches on their music with professional-quality results.In addition to mastering, LANDR Studio provides other functionalities such as music distribution, plugins, samples, and collaboration tools. Users can release unlimited music on over 150 streaming platforms and keep 100% of their royalties. The tool offers 1200 royalty-free sample credits per year, giving musicians access to a vast catalog of exclusive and curated samples from top producers. It also includes over 30 production plugins and essential tools, which ensures a studio-grade music production experience. LANDR Studio offers remote collaboration tools that enable users to collaborate with others remotely. These tools include stable video chat, messaging tools, project storage, and time-stamped feedback, allowing for smooth and efficient music creation.The tool is trusted by industry giants and has received positive reviews from users. It provides quick and responsive customer support to assist users in their music production journey. LANDR Studio aims to streamline the music production process by consolidating various services into one platform, making it easier for users to manage their projects and focus on creating music.
