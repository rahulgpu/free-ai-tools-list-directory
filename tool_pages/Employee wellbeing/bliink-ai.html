About BLiiNK AI

BLiiNK AI is an artificial intelligence desktop app developed to improve wellness of individuals working on digital screens. It aims to promote healthier habits to overcome the negative impacts associated with extended computer usage. The tool conducts regular checks on various aspects of body posture, distance from the screen, and blinking rate, notifying users if any deviations from optimal conditions occur. These assessments aim to reduce issues such as back pain, eye dryness, strain, and to promote overall well-being and productivity. The software also grants users access to historical performance records and reports, providing insights and encouraging informed decisions based on progress made towards healthier working habits. This app uses a locally based AI algorithm, allowing it to function without internet connectivity after the initial software download. BLiiNK AI offers smart notifications tailored to user preferences, ensuring a customizable and user-friendly experience, and is particularly targeted at office workers, who frequently suffer from eye strain and poor posture due to prolonged computer usage.
