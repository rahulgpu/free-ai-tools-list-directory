About iTextMaster

iTextMaster is an AI-driven tool designed for advanced text reciprognition, analysis, and summarization. It leverages the prowess of ChatGPT, and acts as an interactive text-processing utility to facilitate streamlined retrieval of information from PDFs or webpages. PDF documents can be interacted with in a conversational format to answer specific queries, moreover it can also analyze PDF content to generate a semantic index per paragraph, ensuring relevant and meaningful responses to user inquiries. Online web page interaction is another key feature, where inputting the webpage address initiates the retrieval of its content for interaction. Subject content can be thoroughly explored and key information summarised in an interactive manner utilizing the AI technology. Furthermore, a unique feature includes the suggestion of frequently asked questions for documents to aid in obtaining necessary information more quickly. With support for multiple languages, iTextMaster offers a wider servicing scope particularly handy for non-native speakers or for multi-lingual documents. The nature of interaction with this AI tool is designed to be user friendly, aimed at offering accurate and intelligent answers without needing extensive learning curves or complex operations.
