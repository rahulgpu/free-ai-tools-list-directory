About Adventureai

Adventure AI provides an engaging and social learning environment for children to acquire real-world AI skills. The platform uses a game-based approach facilitated over discord where kids engage in a self-paced curriculum. This curriculum covers a variety of AI-related interests such as AI-assisted art, GPT-powered creations, and AI coding among others. The learning journey is divided into the 'training school' which provides the foundational knowledge and the 'world school' where kids apply their skills to create competitive industry-standard AI-powered projects. Real-world value is fostered as students' projects in the 'world school' are targeted at matching the needs of the real-world market. In a bid to keep the learning process lively and captivating, the Adventure AI platform incorporates AI tools that kids enjoy, making them more intriguing through games such as art duels. Additionally, Adventure AI aims to not only develop individual student skills but also foster collaboration and teamwork. Thus, students can collaborate, provide mutual help and even 'riff' on others' work. Finally, Adventure AI does provide different service tiers which allow limited to unlimited access to AI tools, self-paced AI curriculum, and classes with AI experts.
