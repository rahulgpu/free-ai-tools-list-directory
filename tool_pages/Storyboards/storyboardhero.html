About StoryBoardHero

Storyboard Hero is an AI-driven storyboard generating tool designed to aid video agencies and creators in enhancing their video pre-production processes. The tool, founded by fewStones, leverages artificial intelligence to seamlessly produce video concepts, scripts, and storyboards. The AI storyboard generator also allows the generation of AI images for storyboard scenes, and offers an option to request hand-drawn images if needed. Sequence of concepts and scripts can be organized into a storyboard and exported in PDF format with user branding. The AI Storyboard Generator streamlines the often time-consuming and costly video production process by fostering faster, lower-cost creation of full concepts, scripts and storyboards. StoryboardHero stands out in offering a structured script creation, incorporating factors such as voice-overs, camera angles, transitions and more, integrated within the storyboard's structure. Flexibility comes in the form of editing scripts, regenerating them, or blending AI images with hand-drawn ones. Users have full rights to the generated storyboards, which can be presented to clients without any reference to the tool.
