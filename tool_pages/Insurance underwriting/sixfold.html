About Sixfold

Sixfold provides a Generative AI solution designed for insurance underwriters. The tool ingests underwriting manuals, and highlights positive, negative, and disqualifying risk factors. It also gathers risk data from underwriting submissions, supporting documents, and third-party sources. Sixfold then generates semantic matches, summaries, and underwriting suggestions that are tailored to the users' unique risk appetite. The tool aims to boost underwriting capacity, transparency, and enhance the accuracy in decision making. It provides complete transparency around underwriting decisions, which might be appreciated by compliance teams. It can automate workflows by identifying patterns from various sources and uses generative AI to summarize risks in an underwriter's format. Sixfold also offers full traceability of all underwriting decisions, no mixing of data, and provides enterprise-grade security. The tool is designed to integrate with existing technology, avoiding the need to overhaul legacy systems.
