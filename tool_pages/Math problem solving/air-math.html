About AI:R MATH

AI:R MATH is an AI-powered tool designed to assist students with math homework and test preparation. It allows users to take a photo of a math problem, after which the tool's AI reads and processes the problem to provide an instant solution. This feature can recognize and solve a wide range of math problems, from simple equations to more complex word problems. If the AI cant solve a problem, there are expert tutors available around the clock to assist. Users also have the option to receive step-by-step solutions to better understand how each problem is solved. Besides its mobile application, AI:R Math offers a Chrome Extension which functions essentially as a web version of the tool. With this extension, users can snap and crop math problems from the web and seek solutions directly in their browser. A significant feature of the platform is the synchronization between the mobile app and the web extension, ensuring seamless usability across devices.
