About Rivetto

Rivetto is an AI-based tool designed to assist creators in the production of engaging short videos from longer content. It provides a platform that simplifies content creation, making it quick and easy to produce captivating videos. Central to Rivetto's capabilities is its custom AI which scans and analyses content to extract the most viral clips. The AI system offers dynamic head tracking, ensures the active speaker remains in frame, and detects multiple speakers, making it ideal for podcast-style editing. Besides, Rivetto is equipped with integrated editing functions for flexibility in making any desired video modifications. An added feature of this tool is its ability to add B-roll images or videos to content to help boost viewer retention and increase engagement. It also incorporates a silence removal feature, which automatically eliminates silence and filler words from videos to maintain audience interest. This makes Rivetto a convenient solution for modern content creators looking for efficient ways to transform lengthy videos into viral shorts, thereby improving their content strategy.
