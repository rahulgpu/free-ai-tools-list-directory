About reap video

Reap is an AI-driven video repurposing tool designed to transform long-form content into engaging, social-ready shorts. This tool utilizes advanced AI technologies to automatically extract the most captivating segments from videos, making it easier for creators to generate high-quality short content on a daily basis. Features include AI curation, speaker detection, subtitle generation, multiple format support and AI B-rolls. Audio enhancements and one-click video issue corrections add to the overall quality and appeal of the final video. The AI also allows for smart tracking that adjusts the frame dynamically to ensure the speaker always stays in focus. The content can be easily repurposed across various social media platforms such as Instagram Reels, YouTube Shorts, and TikTok videos. In addition, the tool supports multiple languages, thereby enabling creators to subtitle and create content for a global audience. Further features include the ability to manage content with a team, create automatic thumbnails and banners for social media platforms, and customizable video templates to maintain brand consistency.
