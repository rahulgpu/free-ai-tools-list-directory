About Clipwing

Clipwing is an AI-powered video processing tool designed to convert long videos into concise, short clips. It provides a simple-to-use interface where users only need to upload a video, and get a generated transcript from which they can highlight the needful text parts to create short clips. The tool has features including automatic focusing, even in videos with multiple speakers, and allows the option to resize the video clip into different formats such as portrait, landscape, or square. It also allows users to add catchy subtitles to their videos and soundtracks with just a click. Furthermore, Clipwing supports the creation of unlimited clips from the video and also provides storage options. It is suitable for a wide range of video types including podcasts, interviews, product demos, educational lectures, and more. However, currently, it only supports English language videos. It also offers the convenience of transforming a landscape video into a vertical one or vice versa, enhancing its usability for various social media platforms. Clipwing offers a free plan, along with other subscription plans with enhanced features.
