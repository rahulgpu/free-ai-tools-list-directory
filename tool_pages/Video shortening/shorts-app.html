About Shorts App

Shorts is an AI tool designed to automate the process of repurposing YouTube video content for further dissemination. The tool allows users to connect their YouTube accounts, after which it automatically creates shorter, 'viral' clips from the full-length videos. These clips are then automatically posted to YouTube Shorts, with the aim of maximizing reach and visibility of the user's content, thus potentially broadening their audience base. Furthermore, Shorts is designed to perform these tasks twice a day on autopilot, sparing users the effort of manually cropping, resizing, and reposting their content. The tool is also programmed to highlight the active speaker in the generated clips, ensuring optimal focus and engagement. By automating the clip-creation and posting process, Shorts enhances content utility while minimizing unscalable and time-consuming manual labor.
