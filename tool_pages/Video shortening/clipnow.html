About ClipNow

ClipNow is an AI-based tool designed for repurposing long-form videos into short, captivating content suitable for various social media platforms. It's ideal for those wanting to turn YouTube videos into TikToks, Reels, Shorts, etc., potentially popularizing their content and reaching a broader audience. The software's AI capabilities enable users to drag and drop videos to convert them into shorter content forms easily. The features offered by ClipNow include automatic cropping, facilitating effortless resizing of videos to fit the requirements of short-form content. Its AI-powered auto captioning feature generates accurate captions, enhancing user engagement. Furthermore, the software supports multilingual content, making it a suitable choice for global users. Another notable benefit is the face tracking feature that ensures the speaker is always the primary focus of the video. This software helps users post more videos and escalate their growth without putting in considerable effort.
