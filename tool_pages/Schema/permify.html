About Permify

Permify AI is an innovative tool leveraging the power of Generative Pre-trained Transformer (GPT) technology to provide a new approach to writing permify schemas. Its primary function is to translate natural language inputs into permify schemas, making the process more intuitive and accessible for those not well-versed in the technical language of schema writing. This tool is particularly useful for data managers and developers, acting as an interface to ease the management of permissions schemas. It operates on the basis of an AI model trained to understand human language constructs and hence can interpret user inputs accurately to generate precise permify schemas. Its capabilities make it a progressive solution for handling various challenges encountered in the domain of schema writing. Those interested in contributing to the artificial intelligence ecosystem can star the project on GitHub. Additionally, there is a vibrant community available on Discord for discussions, inquiries and support related to Permify AI.
