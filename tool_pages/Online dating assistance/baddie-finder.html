About Baddie Finder

Baddie Finder is an AI-enhanced tool for automating the swiping process on popular dating apps Tinder and Bumble. After obtaining a string of text (cookies) from your browser, the tool uses this information to swipe based on the preferences already set up in your dating app. These preferences may include criteria like location and other filters. Using a cutting edge AI algorithm designed to evaluate profile photos, Baddie Finder automates the swiping process for you. Profiles that meet your preferences receive a 'right swipe', signalling interest, and those that don't, a 'left swipe'. The AI operates daily in the background, allowing users to review its activity at their convenience. Users also have the opportunity to upload a photo and get an evaluation of whether the AI would swipe right or not. Although the current version does not learn user preferences based on their 'likes', or include a messaging feature, future updates may include these functionalities. The tool aims to make the online dating process more efficient by automating it and reducing manual interaction.
