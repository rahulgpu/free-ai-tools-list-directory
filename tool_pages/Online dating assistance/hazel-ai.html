About Hazel AI

Hazel AI is an artificial intelligence assistant and coach geared towards supporting users in enhancing their online dating experience. As an AI dating coach, Hazel provides support in various aspects of the dating scene such as texting and creating catchy bio profiles to attract more engagement. Communication with Hazel is initiated via Telegram, with plans to include WhatsApp underway. To personalize the interaction, Hazel requests user-specific information such as quirks, preferences, and dating goals. Hazel has the ability to offer personalized conversation openers, analyze conversation screenshots provided by the user, and offer improved chat suggestions for better engagement. The AI software gets smarter and more attuned to the user's preferences and style over time. Hazel provides personalized and data-oriented advice, as it has been trained on countless real-life dating chats, making it a unique tool in the online dating landscape. Moreover, users can seek Hazel's input on their dating profiles for improvement. To ensure user privacy and data security, Hazel operates under the database of a custom trained OpenAI LLM.
