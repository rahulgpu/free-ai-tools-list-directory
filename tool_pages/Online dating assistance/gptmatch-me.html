About Gptmatch.me

GPTMatch is an AI-powered dating assistant that aims to save you time and get you more matches on dating apps. By connecting your existing dating app account to GPTMatch, you can use our streamlined swiping system to generate and send responses using AI. This is NOT like other apps where you take a screenshot and upload it. The entire experience is contained in GPTMatch. On the premium tier, you can use your own responses to train an AI model that responds to prompts in your style. The premium tier is half the cost of a human dating assistant but twice as powerful.
