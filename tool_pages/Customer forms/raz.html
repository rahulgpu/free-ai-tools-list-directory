About Raz

Raz is a next-generation form builder tool that allows users to build interactive and personalized forms. It leverages AI technology to provide actionable insights for constant improvement. With AI-powered follow-up questions and semantic analysis, users can collect deeper insights from their users and gain a comprehensive understanding of their customers. The tool enables users to streamline and enhance prospect intake information for lead qualification purposes. It also offers functionality for identifying and engaging with top-tier candidates during the hiring process.Raz allows users to deploy forms across multiple channels, including websites, mobile applications, and email, to ensure convenience for their users. The tool's dynamic forms feature provides users with deeper insights by adapting the form to user input, allowing for a more personalized and efficient user experience.The tool can be utilized across a variety of use cases within an organization, catering to different initiatives and needs. It aims to elevate customer satisfaction and product quality by providing actionable insights for improving the customer and product experience.Overall, Raz is a powerful AI-driven form builder that empowers users to collect valuable data and optimize their processes for better business outcomes.
