About Susterra

Susterra is a FinTech company leveraging AI-powered technology to modernize Public Finance. The company's analytics platforms are designed to empower public finance stakeholders with insightful data, aiming to facilitate growth in the Municipal Bond Market. Susterra focuses on integrating leading academic practices into public finance and thoroughly utilizing widely available public data to create pertinent insights. The goal is to enable issuers to make informed decisions when issuing new debt and investing proceeds. Susterra offers various platforms and services including TerraScore, a Capital Markets Municipal advisory platform; TerraVision, a Municipal funding smart advisor platform; TerraView, a Municipal program health monitoring platform; and Impact IQ, a bespoke analytics application for public finance stakeholders. The advanced analytics offered by Susterra, powered by Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning models, serve to evaluate the effectiveness of public benefit programs like utilities, public transport, green infrastructure, renewable energy, education, healthcare, food, and housing. Finally, Susterra also provides industry-leading data visualization tools, integrating human-centered design practices in their development.
