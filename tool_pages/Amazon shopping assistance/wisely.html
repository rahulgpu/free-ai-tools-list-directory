About Wisely

Wisely: AI Summary for Amazon Reviews is a Chrome extension that provides users with valuable insights from Amazon reviews, saving time, highlighting potential issues, and ensuring informed purchase decisions. This tool scans any product page on Amazon.com, analyzes the product reviews and information, and presents quick and useful insights.With support limited to Amazon.com, users can sign up for other Amazon domains separately. When available, Wisely offers a summary of pros and cons gathered from customer reviews, highlighting important information and red flags to be aware of. It also identifies any unhealthy ingredients in food or snacks, showcases notable product features, and provides a visual breakdown of user ratings.Using the extension is simple: users need to install it, visit any Amazon.com product page, and scroll down to access the valuable insights.For any assistance, the tool provides a help center and a dedicated email for inquiries. Privacy is a top priority for Wisely, with a commitment to protecting personal information and earning users' trust. Users are encouraged to review the privacy policy for a better understanding of how data is handled and secured.Overall, Wisely: AI Summary for Amazon Reviews is a powerful tool that leverages artificial intelligence to analyze customer feedback and provide valuable insights for a more informed online shopping experience on Amazon.com.
