About Legalyze

Legalyze.ai is a legal AI-assistant designed to streamline and enhance the document review process within law firms. This tool uses AI to analyze case data, providing summaries, fact-finding features, and answers to specific questions about case documents. The tool supports various file formats including PDFs, images, .docx, .xls, and scanned files, making it a versatile solution for managing a wide range of documents. One distinctive feature of Legalyze.ai is its capability to create legal documents using the context from your case files. Specifically, this function aids in generating documents like discovery questions. The AI assistant has been designed to work in tandem with your team, contributing to reducing case review time. Security is a priority for Legalyze.ai, attested by its external security audits. You can also ask the AI specific questions about a document, and each answer provided is sourced with a reference to the document text and page number. This not only expedites the review of documents but also makes the task more efficient and reliable.
