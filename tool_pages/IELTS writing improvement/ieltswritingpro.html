About IELTSWritingPro

IELTSWritingPro is an online tool designed to assist users in improving their IELTS writing skills. It provides a platform where users can submit their IELTS essays, for both Academic and General Training, inclusive of Tasks 1 and 2, and receive detailed, personalized feedback. The feedback focuses on areas such as grammar, task response, coherence, cohesion, lexical resource, and even the tone and flow of ideas. The tool uses artificial intelligence algorithms to provide a realistic band estimation and breakdown based on official IELTS grading criteria. Apart from submissions, IELTSWritingPro also offers over 250 practice questions. It includes features for academic chart questions, spelling and grammar corrections, task response assessment and tracking of user progress. Additionally, it gives users insights on repeated and linking words in their text. The tool is built to generate comprehensive reports on essays within a short timeframe and is available 24/7. It supports both IELTS Academic and General Training. A premium subscription offers benefits of unlimited usage, full correction reports, band breakdown, personalized improvement suggestions and more.
