About Evant

Evant is an AI-driven, interactive SMS platform designed to improve engagement and efficiency in communication. It leverages the high accessibility of SMS, allowing businesses to communicate efficiently with their customer base, handle tasks such as confirming appointments and automating payments, and creating effective marketing campaigns. With Evant, messages can be sent to precise segments of a community, with automation options for routine responses. It also facilitates automating RSVPs for events and enables users to set up donations. Furthermore, Evant assists in building customer loyalty by sending customized promotions and reminders. It also ensures customer satisfaction by conducting post-appointment satisfaction surveys and scheduling follow-ups. This tool can be beneficial for organizations seeking a comprehensive SMS-based communication and task automation solution without the necessity of app installation. In addition, the platform is capable of hosting a community platform where members can view available events, sign up, and even make optional donations.
