About CandideAI

Candideai is an educational platform designed to teach children and young people about Artificial Intelligence (AI) in an engaging and creative way. The tool provides a set of step-by-step online courses that encourage kids to create projects while learning foundational AI concepts. The range of AI-based projects available include creating a movie trailer, designing a unique AI Wall art, becoming a Netflix screenwriter, and even automating a Youtube channel. Committed to shaping the next generation of tech wizards, Candideai simultaneously fosters creativity and problem-solving skills. Special AI courses are tailored specifically for children of varying age groups, each instructed via video by a real-life teacher. Examples of the courses include learning basic photography using AI, creating a coloring book with endless pages, devising a family trip plan using ChatGPT, and designing a personalized Pokemon with AI technology. This tool seeks to equip children with knowledge and awareness of AI, preparing them for a future where such skills could be essential.
