About 60sec.site

60sec.site is an Artificial Intelligence (AI) powered website generator aimed at quick and efficient creation of landing pages. With this tool, users without any development experience can build professional-looking landing pages, as it requires no coding knowledge. Apart from generating content and designing the site, the tool also focuses on crafting valuable SEO metatags, optimizing site speed, creating a safe SSL certificate, hosting, and publishing the site. Unique images and themes are provided by the AI to further ease the design process. The platform also allows customization of theme, brand color, or content as per the users' requirements. For improved search engine visibility, it includes features of automatic SEO optimisation. Users can choose to publish their website on the platform's subdomain or opt for a custom domain. The tool includes the functionality to integrate the landing page with other popular tools and services, such as Google Analytics, FB pixel, online chats etc. Pricing plans are variable, with a free plan with limited features offered as well. Analytics or ad pixels can be added into the site, and the mobile-friendly themes ensure an optimized viewing experience across all devices.
