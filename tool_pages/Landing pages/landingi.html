About Landingi

Landingi is a comprehensive, user-friendly, no-code platform and landing page builder designed for marketers. This tool aids in creating, launching, and optimizing various digital marketing elements including landing pages, pop-ups, and funnels. The platforms features comprise a drag-and-drop builder, enabling users to create, publish, and optimize pixel-perfect and mobile-friendly landing pages. Users can also build microsites and one-pagers to establish their online presence, and ensure improved user engagement and search engine ranking through optimized mobile pages. It facilitates lead generation and data collection through forms and allows the integration of these with the existing marketing technology ecosystem to optimize customer journeys. Other features include A/B testing, AI Assistance for refining messages, and a range of customizable page templates. The platform is equipped to handle millions of visits, providing reliability and security for digital marketers. Landingi also offers functionality to track micro-conversions, analyze events, and clicks with visual map through its EventTracker add-on. It supports 170+ integrations including their likes of Google Analytics, Google Ads, MailChimp, HubSpot, and Zapier. Landingi is suitable for various industries like marketing, advertising, real estate, education, and e-commerce among others.
