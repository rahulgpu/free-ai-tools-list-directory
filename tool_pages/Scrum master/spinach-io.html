About Spinach.io

Spinach.io is an AI-powered tool that serves as a virtual Scrum master for software development teams. This tool is powered by GPT-4, a state-of-the-art natural language processing technology, and has integrations with Zoom, Atlassian, and Slack. The primary aim of Spinach.io is to provide actionable meeting summaries, suggest updates to Jira tickets, and write stakeholder recaps without the need for a human Scrum master. With Spinach.io, teams can receive meeting summaries tailored to the type of meeting they had, whether it's sprint planning, daily stand-up, retrospective, or weekly review. The tool can also suggest updates to Jira tickets based on decisions made during the meetings to help keep the tickets up-to-date. Moreover, the tool automatically writes status reports for stakeholders, keeping them informed about the weekly progress of the team.
