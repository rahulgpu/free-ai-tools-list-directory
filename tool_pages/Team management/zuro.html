About Zuro

Zuro is an AI tool designed to help distributed teams work more efficiently by providing various features, such as data ingestion and recall, content creation, scheduling meetings, and automating repetitive tasks. It is created to work with everyday apps that companies use, and it understands and communicates based on a company's brand, website, documents, and databases. With Zuro, users can put their existing data to work and get useful information instantly, saving time and making better decisions faster. Zuro can also help different teams work more efficiently by scheduling meetings, sales calls, and providing useful information in real-time. Moreover, Zuro can help users create on-brand content ten times faster by generating ideas for email subject lines, writing blog posts in a matter of minutes, creating tweets, LinkedIn posts, and more. It can even automate everyday tasks, such as reminders for tasks and meetings, daily async standups with a team, sending leads to a company's CRM, and visualizing data with charts, graphs, and diagrams. As a personalized AI tool, Zuro gets smarter as it is used and solves new problems every week. Zuro has received positive customer feedback as it boosts team productivity and efficiency, enabling cross-functional teams to work together more effectively and save valuable time and resources. Zuro offers two pricing plans, including Basic and Premium options, and annual subscriptions with 2 months free.
