About HowsThisGoing

HowsThisGoing is an AI tool designed to encourage efficient team communications within the popular messaging platform, Slack. This software allows for rapid setup of 'Standups', providing an opportunity for teams to share their progress on a consistent basis. One of the key offerings of this tool is the ability to schedule workflows which comprises selecting participants and formulating the set of questions to be addressed. It also provides the feature to run custom tests with different settings and participants, offering teams the flexibility to find what works best for their unique dynamics. Another distinctive feature is the generation of AI summaries, instant reports that track the completion percentages for each workflow. The AI also offers the ability to customize how you would like to receive your reports. Alongside these features, HowsThisGoing has a strong focus on analytics and reporting. It provides a personalised dashboard for teams to assess their performance, facilitating insight-driven decisions and improvements. Despite offering a range of benefits, HowsThisGoing maintains a flat pricing structure, with options for free and premium packages. Notably, the premium package includes benefits such as unlimited recurring workflows, real-time reports and quick support services.
