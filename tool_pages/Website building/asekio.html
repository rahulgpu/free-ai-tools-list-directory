About Asekio

Asekio is an AI-powered website builder, designed to allow users to swiftly generate unique websites. It employs AI-assistance to provide functional designs, text, imagery, and SEO-optimization. Users do not require experience with web design, as the AI develops a website layout, structure and content, and no coding knowledge is necessary, making it accessible to a broad audience. Additionally, Asekio hosts unique feature of a mobile editor, enabling capabilities to build and manage websites directly from a mobile device. Websites created with Asekio are hosted on EU-based servers, ensuring user data safety with a privacy-first approach. Asekio comes with different pricing models, offering options such as custom domain name, hosting, contact forms, and potential to embed external content. There are also tailor-made options for agency and enterprise needs, including advanced AI and enterprise features.
