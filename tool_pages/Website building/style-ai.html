About Style AI

StyleAI is an artificial intelligence marketing platform that offers a suite of AI tools focused on web building, SEO optimization, and Google Ads. The platform comprises of three AI personal assistants: Crafta, Seona, and Adwin. Crafta is an AI-powered website builder that helps create, edit, and optimize websites. It encompasses agency-grade editing features that facilitate the editing of copy, images, and layouts. Seona is an AI SEO tool that enhances a site's online presence. By analyzing a user's URL, Seona can automatically modify a site's content, tags, headers, and code to elevate its search rankings. The tool also offers auto-updating of keywords and content for continuous SEO optimization, alongside a blog generation feature for automatically publishing SEO-friendly content. Adwin simplifies the process of designing and activating Google Ads. The tool examines a website, /gains insights about a business, and crafts optimal ad campaigns. Collectively, these tools aim to support businesses in building a robust online presence without requiring expensive marketing agencies or expert skills.
