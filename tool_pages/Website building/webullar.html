About Webullar

Webullar is an AI-driven online tool designed to simplify the creation and management of websites and social media content. It uses AI to automate the process of creating an online presence. With Webullar, users can create a website and generate social media posts just by describing their business in a single sentence. Besides generating a customized website, the tool enables the creation of unlimited social media posts, including the associated copy and creatives, thereby boosting online activity. Webullar also offers features to manage leads and customers directly from a mobile device, thus appealing to businesses seeking an on-the-go solution for their digital presence. Furthermore, Webullar offers features like SEO optimization, auto-indexing of the website on Google, providing website analytics, and managing customer relationships. It also provides the option to generate and send newsletters, extending the opportunity for businesses to maintain an active and engaging relationship with their customers. Besides all these functionalities, Webullar includes a mobile app designed to be user-friendly and optimized for businesses to manage their online presence with ease. However, the number of languages, generated social media posts, newsletters, and managed customers varies depending on the subscription plan.
