About Localo Sites

Localo Sites is a tool designed to assist in creating simple and free websites for businesses. It aims to increase the visibility of your Google Business Profile by automatically gathering relevant information, photos, and customer reviews from your profile and incorporating them into the site. Localo allows businesses to check the current visibility, spread news about the business online, create and schedule posts, and automate responding to customer feedback. It also offers the ability to keep track of the business profile's performance and Google visibility. The process of setting up involves creating a Localo account, linking your Google Business Profile to this Localo account, and finally generating the website. The tool also supports optimization with the ability to display the business's offerings using photos, descriptions, and website posts, allows client reviews to be shown, and includes contact forms, social media buttons, and a location map. On top of the website building capabilities, Localo additionally offers free trackability and performance measuring tools with its freemium plan. Its positioned as a helpful tool in improving local SEO and is available in multiple languages.
