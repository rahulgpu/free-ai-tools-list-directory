About Typedream

Struggling to launch your website idea? Typedream is your one-stop shop to turn it into reality. Typedream offers an easy-to-use AI-powered website builder that can generate websites end to end, starting with helping business owners & designers create wireframes & copywriting, to editing and hosting a complete website on a domain. Typedream is your two-in-one solution: 1. AI Website Planner: allows you to enter notes about your business & generate website structure and wireframe with copywriting, which you can export to Typedream, Figma, and Framer. 2. Website Editor: user-friendly drag & drop web editor. Aimed for ease-of-use with features like auto-responsive, accompanied by powerful functionalities to build a complete website (SEO, custom domain, code injection, animation, etc.). So are you ready to build your own website? Get started for free at https://typedream.com/
