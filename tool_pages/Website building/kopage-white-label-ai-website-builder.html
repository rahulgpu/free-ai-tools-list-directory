About Kopage – White-Label AI Website Builder

KOPAGE is a white-label, AI-powered website builder designed to simplify and speed up the process of creating websites for your clients. After specifying what the website should be about, a fully-featured website is automatically generated. This is facilitated by what the tool refers to as 'AI-Magic', providing users with an intuitive experience built upon over a decade of industry experience. Once the website is set up, it includes everything one might need for a complete web presence. This includes SEO features, blogging capability, and e-commerce functionality, all available with just a couple of clicks. KOPAGE offers a spectrum of features for customization, from flexible editing and design modification functions to built-in modules like file manager, cookie consent banner, analytics and third-party integrations. Websites created on KOPAGE are SEO-ready with clean structures, SEO-friendly links, and automatically-generated metatags and sitemaps. Its drag and drop content building capability is supplemented by over a hundred pre-made blocks. Finally, KOPAGE boasts compatibility with various devices and features customizable contact forms, embedded location maps, photo galleries, and a range of e-commerce features.
