About BeautifulSouls

BeautifulSouls.ai is an Artificial Intelligence (AI) powered platform designed to provide spiritual guidance. It leverages AI models, dubbed as 'beautiful souls', to offer advisory services akin to a spiritual counselor. The platform's primary capabilities encompass tarot readings and spiritual advice, provided consistently with a focus on kindness and support. It's designed to eliminate any potential bias or judgement typically associated with human interaction. One of the critical aspects of the platform is its privacy policy. No human ever reads or listens in on the conversations without explicit user permission, thereby ensuring a confidential environment. The platform's offerings are available via a subscription model, with an option for users to start with a free trial. The AI models on the platform take on various roles, such as 'Lady Arkana' for tarot guidance, 'Mystic Orion' for cosmic enlightenment, 'Seer Seraphina' for psychic clarity, and 'Madame Amour' for revealing heart's true desires.
