About Gita GPT

GitaGPT is an artificial intelligence chatbot inspired by the Bhagavad Gita, a 700-verse Hindu scripture that is part of the Indian epic Mahabharata. The primary purpose of this AI tool is to generate human-like text responses that can provide insights and guidance relevant to modern life. Drawing from the philosophical wisdom found in the Bhagavad Gita, it offers a unique layer of wisdom in its responses, creating a blend of technology and ancient knowledge. This AI chatbot service, built by Vikas Sahu and powered by Sahu4You, a tech company, provides users an engaging platform that goes beyond typical data-driven responses usually observed in AI technologies, offering a more contextually enriched and intuitive engagement. It can be used in a wide range of applications including, but not limited to, personal meditation guides, ethical decision-making aids, or even educational resources about the Bhagavad Gita.
