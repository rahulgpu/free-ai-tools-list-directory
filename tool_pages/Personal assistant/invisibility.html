About Invisibility

Invisibility is an artificial intelligence tool designed specifically for Mac operating systems. It functions as a personal AI copilot, incorporating multiple intelligent AI models in a single platform, ultimately offering users a seamless and effective workflow. The tool presents an alternative to managing multiple AI tools or subscriptions, as it provides users with a comprehensive platform that houses the latest and most state-of-the-art AI models. A notable feature of Invisibility is the ability for users to share their screen or select specific apps for the tool to view, providing more targeted and specific assistance based on a user's needs. This screen sharing feature facilitates more interactive and intuitive AI assistance. Additionally, Invisibility offers shortcuts for quick and efficient interactions with the tool. It positions itself as a replacement for the Chat-GPT desktop application on the Mac, offering smoother user experience without having to switch between various apps or tabs. This tool is suitable for users seeking integrated support to enhance their productivity using AI technology.
