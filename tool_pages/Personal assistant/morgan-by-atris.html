About Morgan by Atris

Morgan is a text-based AI assistant designed to provide personal assistance, mentorship, and encouragement in a text-based format. It aims to enhance personal productivity, health, and motivation by providing daily reminders and goal tracking. Morgan is trained on a large array of online content and courses, which enables it to provide insights and guidance in a plethora of areas. Furthermore, it can connect to the internet and synchronize with your calendar to better manage daily tasks and schedules. The AI is like a personal coach in a user's pocket, always ready to assist, guide, and motivate. Its text-based nature makes it readily accessible, easy to use, and adaptable to any situation. Morgan is available for interaction via a provided phone number, allowing users to start texting with the AI assistant instantly. The AI assistant boasts a learning capability, which means it acquires knowledge and adjusts its responses over time based on user interactions. Its applications vary, as it can be a valuable tool for individuals who need a personal assistant, those who desire mentorship, or people requiring an accountability partner to stay on track with their goals.
