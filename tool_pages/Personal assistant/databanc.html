About DataBanc

DataBanc offers an end-to-end platform that centralizes and organizes your personal data, providing you access and control over it. It aims to bridge the gap between you and your scattered data profile from various tech platforms and social networks. This service allows users to discover and explore their online digital footprint, securely store it, and use it for more personalized experiences. Notably, one of DataBanc's key functions is its integration with 'Personal AI Assistant,' designed to create personalized experiences based on the users' stored data profile. Examples of this could be personal itinerary recommendations, lifestyle suggestions, or even tailoring your perfect first date. At the core of DataBanc's philosophy is the empowerment of users' data rights and promoting the principles of transparency, freedom, and accessibility in personal data management. Particular attention is given to privacy and security, with the service allowing users to have ownership control over their stored data. In essence, DataBanc acts more than just an AI provider, but a data bank, treating data as a valuable asset that users can secure, grow, and utilize according to their will, thus fostering increased personalization and privacy in the era of AI.
