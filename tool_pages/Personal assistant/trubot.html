About TruBot

TruBot is an all-inclusive AI-based application designed to enhance your digital experience by offering a wide array of AI tools. The tool set provided is versatile, catering to a range of users from students and professors to graphic designers wanting to explore AI capabilities. The app offers a chatbot allowing users to interact with the latest GPT-4 and GPT-3.5 Turbo models. Besides, it has an image generation feature, TruBot's Picasso AI Image Generation tool, which allows users to generate unique and visually appealing images from short prompts, providing a source of inspiration for potential projects. Additionally, TruBot hosts an AI-based doubt solver, where queries can be scanned and accurate solutions provided using the ChatGPT 4 model. Further utility is added to the app by its commitment to introduce new features and functionalities regularly. Furthermore, concerning data safety, TruBot assures data encryption in transit and also lets users request data deletion. It is worth noting that it does not share data with third parties.
