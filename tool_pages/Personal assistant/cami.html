About Cami

Cami is an AI assistant designed to be at your fingertips and is usable on various messaging applications. The tool brings a wide range of capabilities including planning trips and vacations, generating images, transcribing audio in multiple languages, offering homework assistance, crafting intro messages for dating apps, and more. It features text and audio response capabilities, allowing users to communicate with the assistant according to their preferences. The AI can converse like any chosen entity, enabling users to engage with a celebrity, TV show character, or historical figure. Cami utilizes advanced AI technologies including GPT-4, GPT-3.5, Whisper, and Stable Diffusion, which allows it to deliver intricate and excellent results such as high-quality, hyper-realistic image generation. It offers services around the clock in multiple languages and can help users learn languages or assist during visits to foreign countries. The tool supports numerous languages including English, Hindi, Spanish, Portuguese, French, Arabic, Dutch, German, Italian, Romanian, Russian, Indonesian, Japanese, Chinese, Korean, Malay, Vietnamese among others.
