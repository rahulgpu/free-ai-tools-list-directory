About Wandee

Wandee My AI Assistant is a personalized AI-driven app designed to simplify daily tasks. The assistant is powered by the OpenAI API with GPT3.5 Turbo for text generation and DALLE 3 for image generation. This provides a streamlined experience for users requiring fast and accurate replies to their inquiries. Wandee not only provides text-based responses but also integrates text-to-speech technology, giving audible responses for a more natural and interactive engagement. One key feature of the tool is its conversational interface that allows users to engage in natural conversations, whether for seeking information or a friendly chat. As the assistant is utilized, it learns from user interactions to better tailor its services. It efficiently generates helpful responses to various queries or prompts, ranging from recommendations to trivia answers. New features include a prompt-based image creation. With the focus on enhancing users' daily lives with accurate information and consistent support, Wandee My AI Assistant encapsulates the convenience of having an intelligent companion.
