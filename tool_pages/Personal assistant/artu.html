About ARTU

ARTU is an AI assistant designed to help users organize and process their ideas and information, readying them for action. It functions through various communication modes, allowing users to send emails, WhatsApp messages, share links, assign tasks, upload pictures, and send audio messages. This AI tool is particularly useful because it is available on popular platforms such as Discord and Telegram. Furthermore, ARTU offers a feature for meeting minutes, where the AI assistant can be invited to attend online meetings via the ARTU meetings Chrome extension and provide summaries of meetings and identify next steps. The user's information is processed, summarized, prioritized, tagged, and organized on a single board for easy access and management. The system ensures the safety and security of the user's data. ARTU also comes with 'ARTU Meetings' that helps in coordinating and organizing online meetings. The tool is available in different subscription plans: Personal, Business and Corporate, based on the needs and demands of the user. It is designed to be user-friendly, accessible, and efficient, offering users the capability to save ideas, tasks, videos, links, social media posts, and even emails from their favorite messaging and email platforms.
