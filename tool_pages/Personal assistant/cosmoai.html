About CosmoAI

Cosmo is an all-in-one AI browser extension designed to assist users online. It is particularly effective as a tool for content creation and communication. This encompasses generating emails, responding to social media posts (such as Tweets or LinkedIn posts), and even conducting research. This tool leverages the capabilities of AI model ChatGPT, accessible from any location on the web. Created and designed to be quite personalized, Cosmo allows users to develop their own prompts and shortcuts, enabling them to work efficiently. The tool integrates into popular websites and applications like Twitter, LinkedIn, Gmail, and Reddit, and comes with a user-friendly custom interface. To use, simply select a text on a site and let Cosmo perform interpretation and actions. The service also includes a feature called 'Ask Cosmo', which provides answers to users' queries. For use, the AI extension is installed on the browser and can be triggered using a shortcut. Cosmo aims to revolutionize the normalized browsing experience by offering a helping hand in content creation, communication, and general web research.
