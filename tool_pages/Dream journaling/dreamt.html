About Dreamt

Dreamt is an AI-enabled journaling tool designed to assist users in recording and reflecting on their dreams. With the ability to input entries through text or voice, it provides an accessible means for users to log their dream experiences in real time. The tool includes an analytics component that delivers insights into user sleep patterns and dream details, such as number of dreams and recurring themes or entities. Another key feature is the ability to transform written dream entries into illustrated story images, using AI technology. This provides a new visual dimension for dream exploration and understanding. The tool is also equipped with what is known as 'SentiMojis' and 'Auto-Tags'. 'SentiMojis' conduct an automated sentiment analysis of dreams using emojis, while 'Auto-Tags' automatically tag names of places, people, and organizations within dream entries. For the comfort and assurance of the users, Dreamt provides robust privacy measures, with a policy stating that no user data is collected or tracked, and no use is made of cookies or trackers. It also offers iCloud backup to ensure user data security, storage, and retrieval. Furthermore, an advanced search feature is incorporated into the tool's design to facilitate easy and efficient navigation of dream entries.
