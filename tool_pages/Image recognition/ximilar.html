About Ximilar

Ximilar is a visual AI solution designed to cater to the needs of businesses across various industries. It offers tools for image recognition and visual search, providing automated systems for the tagging, description, sorting, and searching of images. One of the features provided is a no-code machine learning platform that supports the training of image classification and object detection models. The platform also supports image regression models that can predict values such as age, size, or the level of wear and tear. Ximilar's object detection tool can automatically find and mark different types of objects in an image. To create more complex AI models, Ximilar offers a tool called Flows that allows for the combination and chaining of different models in a sequence. Also included is an image annotation tool, allowing for detailed marking and categorization of parts of images. Ximilar can be leveraged by businesses in the fashion, home decor and collectibles industries, among others, to provide tailored solutions such as product recommendation, reverse image search, image matching and custom visual search.
