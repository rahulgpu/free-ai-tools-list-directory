About EraseID

EraseID's AI face anonymizer is an AI-based tool that is designed to add a new layer of privacy and creativity for users working with images that contain faces. With an emphasis on authenticity and privacy, this tool has the capability to create new identities from the faces present in the images. AI face editing features allow modification of specific features such as expressions, ethnicity, or age to match particular requirements. This tool not only facilitates face replacement to protect privacy in images but also supports identity generation to create unique models with AI. An additional application of the tool is the generation of AI-edited pictures without the need for model releases, making it useful for marketing professionals to adapt visuals for global outreach campaigns. The tool has built-in measures to ensure compliance with GDPR, transition from blurring to AI face anonymization and the ability to retain visual aesthetics while protecting privacy. It's designed to be easy to integrate with various workflows, offering advanced creative capabilities and diversity with its API. The possible sectors that can benefit from the AI face anonymizer tool range from marketing and photography to graphic design and educational institutions, providing benefits such as model editing, visual creativity enhancement, and adherence to privacy protection standards.
