About Photo Anonymizer

Photo Anonymizer is an artificial intelligence tool designed to help individuals protect their identity online. It operates on the principle of generative media, replacing a user's real photo with a synthetically generated look-alike. Users simply need to upload their photo; the AI then produces a synthetic face that resembles the user, upholding factors such as skin color, age, and hair length. This AI-generated image allows for an accurate representation of the user's appearance, without exposing their true identity, thereby ensuring privacy. The synthetic photos created by the anonymizer can be used freely online, with no worry about likeness rights. Additionally, the tool is designed with an emphasis on user privacy. It does not store personal data or the original photos uploaded by users. The processing is done in the computer's RAM and the generated images are stored locally on the user's device. Designed for personal use, the Photo Anonymizer is also available for commercial use under purchasable licenses. This tool requires clear, forward-facing individual portraits for optimal performance.
