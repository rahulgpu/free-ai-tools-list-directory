About Calendly

Calendly is an AI-powered scheduling tool that aims to help teams improve their business results. By joining the waitlist, users gain access to Calendly's new AI-powered solutions. The tool offers exclusive sneak peeks of upcoming features, as well as new content on the future of AI and scheduling automation. Users also get early access to Calendly's new AI releases.Calendly simplifies the process of connecting with others and allows users to accomplish more by taking care of the scheduling tasks. It offers several features such as routing, availability management, team scheduling, and integrations. These features cater to various departments like sales, support, customer success, marketing, HR/recruiting, and product management.Targeting different industries like technology, financial services, professional services, and education, Calendly provides solutions that meet the specific needs of each sector.To enhance productivity, Calendly offers resources like a blog, a resource center, a comparison tool, and developer tools. Additionally, it allows users to download the Calendly app for iOS and Android, as well as Chrome and Firefox extensions for easy access and scheduling on various platforms.For support and contact, users can sign up for free, talk to sales, explore the help center, or contact Calendly directly.Overall, Calendly is a scheduling tool powered by AI that aims to streamline the process of connecting and scheduling for teams and businesses across different industries.
