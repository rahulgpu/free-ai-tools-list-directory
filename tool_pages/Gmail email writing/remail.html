About Remail

Remail is an AI-powered Chrome extension for Gmail that helps users compose high-quality email replies in seconds. It uses artificial intelligence to understand the sentiment and context of an email thread, and generates multiple options for users to choose from. Features include one-click responses for positive or negative replies, custom responses, scheduling links pro for adding scheduling links, and tones pro to set the tone of the reply. Remail aims to help users eliminate writer's block and get a good starting point for drafting emails.
