About Chatous

Chatous is a platform for creating custom AI personas that users can interact with through text and images. The software offers a variety of AI characters with distinct personalities and roles, such as a friendly assistant, adventurer, and archaeologist. Users can converse with these personas, offering both entertainment and functionality. The chatbots have capabilities to respond intelligently to user inputs, demonstrating traits such as being wise, rational, insightful, and knowledgeable. However, it's highlighted that these AI chatbots are purely for entertainment and should not be relied on for any information they provide. Prior to interacting with the chatbots, users are required to accept Chatous's Terms of Use, Privacy Policy, and Content Policy. Users also have access to support via email for any queries or concerns. All functionalities, guidelines, and features are designed while putting user convenience and safety first.
