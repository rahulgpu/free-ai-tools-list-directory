About AIChat

AI Chat is a chatbot assistant application available on Google Play that is powered by ChatGPT. The user-friendly app allows users to ask the AI chatbot any question and receive instant answers on a wide range of topics, including general knowledge and personalized discussions. The AI chatbot is designed to respond naturally like a human and remembers conversation context for more interactive and meaningful conversations.The AI chatbot is equipped with various features, including questions and answers, creative ideas and examples, language translations and grammar correction, explanations on various subjects, content and email writing assistance, interesting facts, and more. Users can easily interact with the chatbot through the user-friendly interface to obtain quick responses.The premium version of the app is powered by the fast model GPT 3.5 Turbo from OpenAI and offers additional features such as unlimited answers, voice chat with speech-to-text recognition, higher word limit for longer messages, and chat history for easy reference.It is important to note that the app is not officially associated with ChatGPT but utilizes an API by OpenAI. Furthermore, the app provides data safety and encryption in transit to ensure user privacy and security.The app is listed under the Productivity category on Google Play and is developed by Sonu Mohammad. It has garnered over 100+ downloads and a content rating of PEGI 3. The developer also offers other apps such as Nutrify - Nutrition Tracker and Live Train Status & PNR Status.
