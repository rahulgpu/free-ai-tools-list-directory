About Chatleh

Chatleh is an AI tool designed as an AI Copilot for Telegram. It offers users a convenient platform to interact with AI through the familiar Telegram interface. Chatleh aims to assist users with daily tasks, facilitate collaborative learning, and provide instant answers to questions. It supports various types of media, including text, audio, and images, allowing users to communicate with the AI in their preferred format. One of its unique features is the ability to add AI to Telegram chat groups, enabling users to collaborate and guide others in real-time while exploring the capabilities of AI together. Chatleh constantly updates and improves its AI models to provide the best user experience. It offers flexible pricing plans to accommodate different user needs, including a Basic Plan, Premium Plan, and Enterprise Plan. The number of bots and bot types available vary based on the plan chosen. The Basic Plan starts at $9.99 per month and includes one bot powered by GPT-3.5. The Premium Plan, priced at $14.99 per month, allows users to have up to two bots, powered by either GPT-3.5 or GPT-4. The Enterprise Plan, priced at $149.99 per month, supports up to 10 bots, also powered by either GPT-3.5 or GPT-4.Chatleh offers a demo bot for users to test its capabilities. However, the demo bot has a limit of seven messages per day. Users can interact with the demo bot by sending text, images, or audio to t.me/ChatlehDemoBot. Overall, Chatleh provides a user-friendly and collaborative AI experience within the Telegram platform, catering to a wide range of needs and offering flexible pricing options.
