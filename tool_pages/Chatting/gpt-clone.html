About GPT Clone

GPT Clone is a tool offered by Sink In that allows developers to create custom Stable Diffusion models. It provides fast, reliable, and quality APIs for developers to integrate into their applications. The tool offers different pricing plans, each with varying capabilities and limitations.The Basic plan, priced at $10 per month, allows users to create three clones with one hour of clone speaking time, which translates to approximately 500 to 1000 responses. The Pro plan, priced at $30 per month, offers the creation of ten clones and three hours of clone speaking time, providing around 1500 to 3000 responses. The Ultimate plan, priced at $90 per month, allows the creation of 30 clones and offers nine hours of clone speaking time, which corresponds to approximately 4500 to 9000 responses.Clone speaking time refers to the duration when the clone is actively generating responses. It does not include the time spent by the user talking or when there is no interaction.GPT Clone enables users to create clones of specific individuals such as Donald Trump, Elon Musk, or Taylor Swift. The tool aims to facilitate conversation with these clones that simulates real-life interactions.To access GPT Clone, users need to sign in using either their Google account or email. By subscribing to the tool, users agree to the terms of service and privacy policy. In case of questions or support, users can refer to the provided Discord link.
