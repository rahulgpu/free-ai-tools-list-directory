About TXTGPT

TxtGPT is a beta AI tool that allows users to have personalized and customizable text conversations with an AI assistant. It operates as a chat-based platform accessible without the need for an application. The tool enables users to create a companion AI with their preferred gender, age, personality, and disabilities. Users can engage with their AI buddy 24/7, discussing any topic they desire, as the AI is powered by a highly intelligent system. The platform aims to create an experience similar to conversing with a knowledgeable friend who is always available. TxtGPT has received positive feedback from users who find it helpful and appreciate its vast knowledge. These testimonials testify to the tool's ability to provide support and assistance in various areas, such as education, work, and personal life. The website offers options to sign up, personalize the AI buddy, and start texting. Additionally, users can subscribe to the newsletter to stay updated on TxtGPT updates. Support resources, including frequently asked questions, terms and conditions, privacy policy, and contact information, are also provided. Overall, TxtGPT serves as a convenient and accessible tool for users looking to engage in text conversations with a personalized AI assistant, providing them with knowledge and support across a wide range of topics.
