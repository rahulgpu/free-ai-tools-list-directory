About Mindie

Mindie is an AI tool designed to enhance your web browsing experience by allowing you to chat with various types of content from the web. It acts as your "second brain" by leveraging the power of ChatGPT, enabling you to strike up conversations with anything you encounter online, such as YouTube videos, PDF documents, or blog posts.With Mindie, you can enjoy the following features:1. Instant Page Summaries: Get a comprehensive understanding of any article or webpage with a single glance. This feature saves you time and effort by providing concise summaries without the need to read the entire content.2. Quick Queries: Pose your questions directly about a web page from your browser's toolbar. This eliminates the hassle of switching between multiple tabs and allows for seamless interaction with the content you're browsing.Mindie is offered by Squid & Fish Products and is available as a Google Chrome extension. The tool enhances your ability to interact with online content, bridging the gap between you and the digital universe. Please note that Mindie collects authentication information for secure usage but declares not to sell user data to third parties. For more detailed information on privacy practices, you can refer to the developer's privacy policy.
