About Ask Robi

Ask Robi is an AI-powered chatbot that goes a step beyond traditional chatbots. Enabled within the widely-used platform WhatsApp, this tool functions as an efficient helper for a myriad of tasks. Its capabilities include creating unique images, generating textual content, translating text into simpler words, and solving complex questions related to maths or coding. One key advantage is that no app download is required to access Robi. Users can simply add it to their WhatsApp contact list and start interacting as they would with a human. More aesthetically inclined users might find value in Robi's ability to produce original graphics ranging from concept art and wireframes to complete masterpieces. On the other hand, its prowess in writing is useful for creating essays, blog posts, or emails. Robi's ability to translate any text to the comprehension level of a 5-year-old child can be quite useful for understanding complicated concepts or ideas. It also boasts understanding programming, complex mathematical equations, and even the capacity to help with writing difficult queries. Concerning data safety, Robi adheres strictly to WhatsApp's data privacy standards ensuring safe and secure interactions.
