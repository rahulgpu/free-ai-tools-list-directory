About Cognitiev PRO

Cognitiev Pro is a multifaceted AI chatbot engineered by Kritrima ai Technologies Pvt. Ltd. The tool is privacy-centric and equipped to function across multiple platforms. Designed as an 'AI CxO' for startups and SMEs, Cognitiev Pro delivers a full suite of services designed to revolutionize the way users interact with technology. Its primary objective is to serve as an AI extension of your creativity while guaranteeing data privacy. The system ensures consistent brand voice across all platforms and gives users the capacity to manage their privacy with an option to delete all data upon cookie removal. Its output resembles human touch, bringing resonance and relatability to the content it creates. The tool is recognized by industry giants including AWS, Microsoft Azure, OpenAI, Vercel, and YCombinator among others, establishing it as a pioneer in enhancing interactions with AI. Furthermore, its features include superior context comprehension, robust tools for work sharing and saving, and intuitive as well as flexible system navigation. Productivity is fostered via intelligent plugins, and a straightforward pricing model ensures transparency with no hidden costs. Its uniquely designed Advanced Multi-shot Prompt Interface enables custom prompt designing, and users may access expert prompt designing services at no extra cost. With this, Cognitiev Pro offers a comprehensive approach to AI-powered conversations and innovation with a strong focus on data privacy and simplicity of use.
