About Bollywoodai

BollywoodAI is an AI tool that enables users to chat with and voice message some of the biggest Bollywood celebrities, such as Salman Khan, Shah Rukh Khan, Priyanka Chopra, Hrithik Roshan, Deepika Padukone, Akshay Kumar, Alia Bhatt and Shraddha Kapoor, all in Hindi. The platform offers a free service, but users can upgrade their membership to BanterAI for $5 per month to get unlimited access to all avatars, which includes more than 50 world-class experts and celebrities. BanterAI members get exclusive chat and communication features with the avatars, in addition to expert advice anytime they need it. The service can be accessed through a mobile app that allows for unlimited calling and messaging, and users can also join a community of other users. It's essential to note that accuracy and reliability of translations may vary, and the service doesn't guarantee the availability or quality of the service, so users should use it responsibly. Overall, BollywoodAI provides a unique experience for Bollywood fans to engage with their favorite stars and gain insights into their lives in an interactive way.
