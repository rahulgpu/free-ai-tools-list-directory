About InterAIct

_InterAIct_ is an AI-based application that allows users to engage in real-time conversations with various AI characters. Its primary functionality is to provide an interactive platform where users can select their preferred AI character to engage with. The innovation distinguishes itself through the creation of personalised discussions, offering users a unique interaction experience. One of the available AI characters is a psychologist, providing an artificial intellegence-based space for users to discuss what's on their mind. It should be noted that while this may simulate a therapeutic conversation, it's an AI led discussion and does not replace professional psychological consultation, demonstrating the tool's blend of technology and human-like conversation capabilities. _InterAIct_ requires JavaScript to run effectively, ensuring a smooth conversation flow with the AI characters. The tool also comes with a notification and user menu functionality, enhancing the user-friendliness of the platform by enabling ease of navigation. In summary, _InterAIct_ is a unique tool that embraces AI technology to create customizable, engaging, and real-time conversation experiences with different AI characters.
