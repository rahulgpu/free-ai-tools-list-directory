About Faraday.dev

Backyard AI is a powerful platform that offers AI-powered chat and interactive story creation tools, capable of working offline with zero configuration. It hosts diverse characters that users can thoroughly enjoy. This tool utilizes AI models locally, bringing advantages in data privacy, as the data is stored locally on the device, allowing for complete control and privacy, while also offering an offline working capability. It integrates automatic GPU acceleration, configuring the optimal performance on any given hardware with no technical knowledge required for setup. Moreover, Backyard AI's desktop experience extends to mobile through tethering, permitting remote usage from a web browser. The tool also provides an extensive range of AI language models for text adventure creation, with new models added regularly. Additionally, Backyard AI offers cloud services to enhance creativity with fast, performant AI models accessible from anywhere. Extremely data secure, the tool ensures data encryption at rest and remains private under all circumstances. The platform also offers ultra-high-quality language models and promising browsing speeds for immersive chat experiences. The software is available in both Desktop App and Web Browser formats, providing flexibility to users.
