About RecurseChat

RecurseChat is a personal Artificial Intelligence application that enables users to engage in conversations with local AI models. Primarily a Mac app, it is geared towards local AI interaction and can function offline, eliminating the need for complicated configurations. One of the notable features of RecurseChat is its swift full-text search functionality over thousands of messages. Users can also import their ChatGPT history to continue conversations with the local AI. In a single chat session, users have the ability to talk with multiple AI models. The app includes multi-modal inputs, allowing users to engage using images through the LLaVA model. RecurseChat places a strong emphasis on security and privacy with features such as the macOS App Sandbox that provides enhanced protection and private chat options. Users can also customize the AI's appearance and character attributes to their preferences. The interface of the app is user-friendly, and it does not require complex technical knowledge to perform various actions. It supports a multi-modal chat with images and also allows users to use their GGUF model.
