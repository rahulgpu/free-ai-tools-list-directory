About Ohayo

Ohayo AI is a first-of-its-kind web3-native platform offering an interactive interface for creating, customizing and sharing AI characters. Leveraging cutting-edge AI models, the tool enables users to design and generate visually captivating avatars with distinctive personalities and styles. It offers greater interaction with the virtual world through its chat capabilities powered by intelligent image and voice recognition technology. Each character created can be equipped with unique voices, thanks to Ohayo's proprietary Text-to-Speech (TTS) technology, providing an even more engaging user experience. The platform also extends its functionality to the digital art sphere by permitting users to mint their favorite AI-generated characters into non-fungible tokens (NFTs). This feature allows for the immortalization and ownership of digital assets in line with the web3 ethos. The tool fosters a community environment too, encouraging exploration and sharing of diverse AI characters created by users globally. Despite its distinctive offerings, Ohayo AI maintains a user-friendly interface, inviting creators and users from various backgrounds. Users can access the platform for free for a limited period, serving as a resourceful starting point for experiencing the evolution of character creation and interaction in the immersive web3 space.
