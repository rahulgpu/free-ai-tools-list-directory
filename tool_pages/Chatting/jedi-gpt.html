About Jedi GPT

JediGPT is an artificial intelligence tool designed to engage with users in a comprehensive conversation. This tool is created and powered by Tradomate. It is modelled after the concept of a 'Jedi Master' from the Star Wars universe, aiming to guide users on their journey, answer their queries, and provide insightful responses. One of the distinguishing features of JediGPT is the utilization of its chatbot system through the TELEGRAM platform. This allows users to engage in conversations with the AI, either for asks or general chats, benefiting from its intelligence and data processing capabilities. Moreover, the tool adheres to a comprehensive privacy policy and defined terms of service, that ensure user data is protected and the tool is employed in agreement with established guidelines. JediGPT does not only thrive on providing valuable information but also maintains an active stance in ensuring user data protection and responsible AI engagement.
