About Faune

Faune is a privacy-focused AI chat application that allows users to interact with various leading language models (LLMs) such as OpenAI's GPT-3 and GPT-4, and Mistral. The app offers dynamic content, and users have the capability to refine their prompts for richer conversations and more precise AI responses, enabling an engaging AI chat experience. The app provides an integrated editor, which makes crafting perfect prompts easier. Faune also enables anonymous use, with no user account creation required, ensuring user interactions remain private and secure. Furthermore, it supports dynamic prompts and image processing, enhancing the quality of interaction with the AI. Users interact with the AI using a flexible credit system that refreshes regularly as well as purchase options available for extended usage. The app is compatible with iOS 17.2+ and available across multiple devices within the Apple ecosystem, including the iPhone, iPad, MacOS, and Vision Pro, providing users with accessibility and convenience. Beyond its AI interaction capability, one of its unique features includes the ability to cater to multiple LLMs in one chat, giving users a wide array of perspectives and insights.
