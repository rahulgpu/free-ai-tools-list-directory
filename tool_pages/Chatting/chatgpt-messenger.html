About ChatGPT Messenger

ChatGPT Messenger is an application that allows users to engage in conversation with OpenAI's GPT model. It operates on a prepaid basis rather than on subscription model, making it a suitable option for those who wish to use sophisticated AI chat technology at their own pace and without any ongoing commitments. To use the service, users purchase credits, which can then be spent on chatting with the GPT AI. The application stands out for its simplicity; start by just logging in with Google, buy credits, and begin chatting. Notably, the service uses a proprietary algorithm to construct requests and responses to ensure efficient use of credits. A key limitation, however, is that the messenger service exclusively supports text-based interactions, which means users cannot upload PDFs or images. Unused credits requested within 3 days of purchase are eligible for a refund. The Messenger is powered by OpenAI's GPT model, so users can expect the same level of intelligent, natural language interaction they would if interacting with OpenAI's GPT directly.
