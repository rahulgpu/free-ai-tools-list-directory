About Emailbind

Email Bind is an innovative service that allows users to access AI models such as ChatGPT and Google Bard through email. With this tool, you can initiate an intelligent conversation by merely sending an email. To begin, users verify their email by sending a mail to [emailÂ protected] . Once verified, they can start a conversation with the AI models by sending an email to the same address. It creates an unprecedented level of convenience by making AI accessible through a familiar platform: email. The system functions on the GPT-3.5/4 model, providing a experience similar to the official website's web version. It also supports Google Bard, which has internet connectivity and can fetch the latest information from the web, including support for Chinese language. By default, users start in a free trial mode. Advanced features and full settings can be unlocked by downloading the 'Email Bind' App from the App Store or upgrading to the Pro version for complete functionality. Options for customization include prompt templates, enabling the GPT-4 model, max tokens, and temperature settings.
