About ChatGPT Microphone

ChatGPT Microphone is an extension for Google Chrome that enhances the capabilities of ChatGPT by incorporating voice-to-text and shortcut snippets functionalities. The tool features an integrated voice-to-text microphone button which allows for hands-free operation, as well as 'Shortcut Snippets'. These snippets permit the insertion of customizable text into your chats by triggering their associated keywords. The extension is designed to save time and increase productivity, particularly for professionals who prefer to focus more on tasks than on typing. Furthermore, the extension can be customized, allowing users to configure such features as 'clear text' or 'send message' keywords. It also includes a dark/light mode to match ChatGPT's appearance themes. Please note, however, that the extension is specifically designed for compatibility with Google Chrome, and may not support other Chromium-based browsers due to limitations with the Speech Recognition API. The ChatGPT Microphone is open-sourced, inviting contributions and improvements from the users' community.
