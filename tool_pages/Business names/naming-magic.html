About Naming Magic

Naming Magic is a tool created by Swift Ventures + OpenAI to facilitate the process of naming a company and finding a suitable domain. It is powered by GPT-3 natural language technology and designed to be used by entrepreneurs who are in need of a creative tool to brainstorm a company name. The tool is fairly straightforward to use, as the user just needs to input a product description and a few keywords, and the AI will suggest potential ideas. Naming Magic was originally founded by the TubeMogul founders, who wish they had this tool when they had to come up with a name for their own company. They have since gone on to invest in AI and data-first businesses and are open to hearing about new ideas.
