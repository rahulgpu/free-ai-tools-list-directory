About Namewizard

Namewizard.ai is an AI-powered domain name generator that helps users find the perfect business and domain name for their next project. The tool provides unlimited domain name suggestions and allows users to check the availability of any Top Level Domain (TLD). It also offers additional features such as free SSL certificates, multi-layered DDoS protection, and advanced DNS management, which are coming soon. The tool is offered with a no-commitment 7 day free trial, after which users can choose to pay a monthly fee of $2.50 or an annual fee of $30. Namewizard.ai is an easy and efficient way to find a suitable domain name for any project.
