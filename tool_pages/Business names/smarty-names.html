About Smarty Names

SmartyNames.com is an AI-driven tool for finding the perfect domain name for your business. It uses advanced algorithms to generate unique and creative domain names that are still available for purchase. SmartyNames.com offers a fast search and results are delivered in 20 seconds or less. It also offers premium .com domains and unique ALT TLDS. It also provides access to custom filters and hacked domains, and allows users to save searches to try again later. SmartyNames.com has generated over 1 million domains since launch and it also offers premium features such as social media handle availability, early access to new features, and domain availability across 500 extensions. Additionally, users can sign up for The Novice, a publication that describes AI in simple terms and provides examples of fun and easy AI things to do at home. Finally, SmartyNames.com works with trusted partners like Name.com, GoDaddy and Namecheap to make it easy to find the domain names users are looking for and purchase them directly.
