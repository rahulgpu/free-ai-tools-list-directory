About Kopia

Kopia is an AI Virtual Try-On Platform designed to create immersive and realistic shopping experiences for brands and their customers. Using state-of-the-art machine learning techniques, the platform allows users to accurately visualize themselves in different products in real time. This tool not only enhances user engagement and shopping experience, but also has the potential to increase conversion rates and decrease returns. Additional to these benefits, brands also gain access to actionable customer segmentation data. Brands have the option of integrating Kopia's functionalities to their websites either through a dedicated Shopify app plug-in or custom code integration. The integration process is streamlined and does not require extensive technical skills. It's essential to note that the platform offers scalable pricing plans, allowing businesses of varied sizes and growth stages to leverage the power of AI for their marketing and sales efforts. Kopia is a versatile tool and a substantial addition to any e-commerce toolkit.
