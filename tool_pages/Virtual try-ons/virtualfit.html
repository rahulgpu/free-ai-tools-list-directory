About VirtualFit

VirtualFit is an advanced AI-driven tool designed to transform your look in photos without the need for complex image-editing software. Leveraging AI technology, users can simply upload a photo and specify in prompt what they want to change, like a shirt or hairstyle, and what they want to replace with, such as a leather jacket or a new hair color. Once the prompts are filled and apply is clicked, its powerful AI processes the picture to reflect the changes. Beyond simply changing clothes, VirtualFit's advanced AI technology allows for more comprehensive image alterations such as altering hairstyles or hair colors. This suits users who do not have costly editing apps or skills to professionally edit images. Importantly, the changes made on the images are designed to retain a professional and natural look, ensuring your photos don't look artificial or AI-generated. Apart from the outfit replacer feature, VirtualFit also houses a set of additional AI tools. These include 'VirtualFit Image Restore', for restoring quality in damaged or older images, 'VirtualFit Generative Fill' for filling areas in the photo where information is missing, 'VirtualFit Object Recolor', for changing the color of any object in the image, and 'VirtualFit Background Removal', to remove or alter the backdrop of an image. VirtualFit therefore provides a suite of image editing tools to enhance and customize your photos providing a well-rounded AI-based image editing solution with a focus on changing appearance.
