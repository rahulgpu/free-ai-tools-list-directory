About Comigo

Comigo is an artificial intelligence tool specifically designed to assist individuals with ADHD. It serves as a companion to bridge the executive function gap experienced by over 300 million adults with ADHD. As a cognitive AI for ADHD, Comigo aims to help individuals thrive in an increasingly distracting world. It offers personalized advice and assists in structuring ideas into tasks, ensuring that users remain organized and focused. Comigo acts as a 24/7 confidant, providing continuous support to its users.Comigo acts as a co-brain, helping users achieve a sense of accomplishment by ensuring that daily tasks are completed and plans for the following day are prepared. It facilitates intelligent task planning, scheduling, sequencing, and prioritization. Additionally, Comigo resiliently reschedules tasks when interruptions occur and provides focus guarding and single task execution to enhance productivity. The tool also utilizes positive reinforcement and intrinsic reward mechanics to motivate users and foster a sense of achievement.With its specialized AI model, Comigo functions as an analyst for continual improvement. Users can track their personal growth and measure their progress over time. Comigo's self-training AI evolves to better serve and help individuals thrive with ADHD.Overall, Comigo provides personalized support, task management features, performance tracking, and continual improvement functionalities to assist individuals with ADHD in navigating their daily lives and achieving their goals.
