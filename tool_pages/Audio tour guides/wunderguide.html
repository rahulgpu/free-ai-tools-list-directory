About Wunderguide

Wunderguide is an artificial intelligence-powered travel companion designed to facilitate easier exploration of new cities. As a mobile application, it provides users with a personalised tour guide experience which they can access right from their smartphone. It curates a list of top attractions in the user's vicinity or in a chosen city, consolidating critical information into easy-to-use cards for each site. Wunderguide also enables users to read reviews and watch content from platforms such as Google Maps, YouTube, and TikTok, to help them decide what is worth visiting. Navigation is simplified through integration with Google and Apple Maps, making it easier for users to reach their chosen attractions. Another distinguishing feature of Wunderguide is the ability to create personal audio tours. Users can use the provided speech-to-text function to ask questions and create their own storytelling experience. This feature gives users the freedom to explore at their own pace without the need for structured guided tours. The application is designed to be flexible, personal and budget-friendly, making it an ideal tool for solo travellers, history enthusiasts, and those who enjoy immersing themselves in local culture.
