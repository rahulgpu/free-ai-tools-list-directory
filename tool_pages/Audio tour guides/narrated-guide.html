About Narrated Guide

Narrated Guide is a tool designed to facilitate self-guided tours with the aid of digital storytelling audio. It allows users to explore a city or destination at their own pace, regardless of their preferred travel method whether by walking, cycling, driving, or boat. The tool provides an audio guide that immerses the travellers in the history and culture of the location as if they had their own personal tour guide. Users can follow a suggested itinerary which not only makes navigating their surroundings a smooth experience but also enriches their understanding of the place. What sets Narrated Guide apart is the freedom it offers travellers to break free of group schedules or wait times. In addition, Narrated Guide offers customisation options for private guides catering to specific events or themes. It also displays regular updates on new destinations. Thus, Narrated Guide is an innovative tool that combines technology and storytelling to redefine traditional travel experiences.
