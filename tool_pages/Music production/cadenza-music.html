About Cadenza Music

Cadenza is an artificial intelligence (AI) tool aimed at facilitating the music production process. It is particularly beneficial for amateur producers, helping them to transform initial ideas into complete songs. This software tool employs cutting-edge AI-powered algorithms that generate the perfect midi chord progression based on user inputs. Users describe the vibe they aim for, including the type of chords or song descriptions they desire, and Cadenzas AI interprets this and generates suitable chord progressions; from straightforward pop progressions to advanced jazz chords. Once these chords are generated, users can drag and drop the created midi file into their digital audio workstation (DAW) of choice to further manipulate and build their song. The chords created by Cadenzas AI are designed to flow smoothly into one another, helping to maintain a cohesive and professional sound to your productions. This makes Cadenza a potent song-starting tool, and it takes advantage of AI's capacity to interpret and deliver on musical creation based on user prompts.
