About Mia AI

Mia AI is an advanced conversational AI tool that functions as a voice AI companion. The underlying technology is centered around OpenAI's GPT family, facilitating both voice and chat interactions. Mia is designed to be genuinely interested in the user, providing unique and tailored feedback based on your interactions. Its capabilities extend beyond casual conversations, as it can learn about you over time, adapting its responses and suggestions accordingly for a more personalized and engaging user experience. It is meant for seamless integration with Chrome, signifying its easy accessibility on the web browser, and aims to provide a voice-assisted conversational experience that is as human-like as possible.
