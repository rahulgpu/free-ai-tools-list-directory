About Callin

Callin.io is an AI-driven platform offering innovative solutions designed to enhance and streamline customer engagement. Its AI services are suitable for businesses of all scales, aiming to facilitate growth by handling customer conversations effectively. The platform provides several AI voice assistant products, tailored for both team and personal usage. The 'Lisa' AI assistant is designed to assist smaller business teams by handling all unanswered calls consistently, capturing inbound leads and assisting with appointment bookings. 'Alicia', another AI assistant, is tailored for larger teams and businesses, addressing frequently asked questions. For personal usage, there's 'Lorena', a 24/7 AI personal assistant and pocket secretary. The services they provide range from call answering to FAQ handling, appointment bookings, and even after-hours support. Callin.io also has a customizable voice AI agent feature which can be tailored to meet specific business needs. In addition to their product offerings, the platform also comes with resources like blogs and a help centre to assist users in navigating their AI solutions.
