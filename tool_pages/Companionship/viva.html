About VIVA

VIVA is an AI companion designed to enhance individual users' digital experiences through personalized interactions. It facilitates tailored conversations, understanding each user's unique style and deepening the connection with every interaction. It's accessible whenever users need, supporting them in their daily digital life. One of VIVAs distinct features, 'Core Memory', underlines its personalization capability. Core Memory enables the AI to capture and utilize significant interactions from user's chats ranging from travel stories to personal experiences. As conversations progress with this AI, it customizes its responses based on these 'core memories', making every interaction unique for each user. VIVA also supports effortless voice interactions, responding with personalized greetings and engaging in dialogue that feels natural and relationally connected. The AI is continuously adapting and learning from each conversation, thereby personalizing the user experience. VIVA is available on both iOS and Android platforms.
