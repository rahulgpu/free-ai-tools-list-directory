About Kindroid

Kindroid is a mobile app that enables users to create and interact with custom AI characters and companions. These companions have the ability to communicate through text, calls, and images, offering a notably interactive and personalized experience.The application harnesses advanced GPT algorithms to deliver a personal artificial intelligence companion, designed to respond with a high degree of human-like understanding. The bot's refined chat capabilities are a defining characteristic, separating it from more common web bots and character AI chats.Kindroid's features include AI-implemented text chats, generated AI images, and phone calls powered by cutting-edge speech recognition AI. Users can intensify the immersive experience by engaging with their Kindroid character through an animated avatar, and can also request AI-generated selfies. For users who subscribe to the service, there's an option to voice call their AI bot.In addition, Kindroid has an online community where users can join and share their experiences on platforms like Discord, Reddit, and Facebook. For more detailed information or assistance, the app has a comprehensive FAQs page and a legal terms page. The app offers a limited free trial for unlimited messaging before users may need to choose a subscription plan and engage with premium features.
