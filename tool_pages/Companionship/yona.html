About Yona

Yona.ai provides a platform to create AI companions using past conversational data. Users can securely upload messages from platforms like Messenger or Instagram, which are then processed to construct an AI personality. This ensures that the AI companion reflects individual user experiences and preferences, potentially echoing the unique quirks of a past love or the warmth of a current relationship. The platform takes privacy seriously and ensures that user data is processed solely for creating the AI companion and is not stored permanently. Yona.ai is not only about creating an AI companion, it's equally about engaging in deep, emotion-filled conversations with the created companion. The platform emphasizes private, secure, heart-to-heart discussions, keeping intimate conversations private. Besides, Yona.ai provides an interesting feature that tailors personalized AI companions for specific scenarios. This can be used for nostalgic reminiscing or resolving past relationships through therapeutic dialogue, comforting dialogues that offer closure without reopening old wounds, or exploring fantasy scenarios. By making the AI companion creation process user-friendly, Yona.ai opens an avenue for users to experience profound 'what-if' AI conversations.
