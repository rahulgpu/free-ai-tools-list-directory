About Ava

Ava is an AI tool that provides live captions and transcriptions for videoconferencing platforms such as Zoom and Google Meet, as well as in-person meetings. With a single click, Ava generates captions for meetings, lectures, medical appointments, and other vital conversations, making them more accessible for Deaf and hard-of-hearing individuals. The tool utilizes a combination of AI technology and professional captioners to deliver real-time, accurate transcriptions around the clock. In addition to live video captioning, Ava also offers 'Ava Scribe' designed to ensure ADA compliance by bringing together AI-powered speed and cost-effectiveness with professional scribes for high-quality captioning. Another product, 'Ava Connect', allows seamless integration with any video conferencing platform. Moreover, it offers features such as saving transcripts and creating conversation summaries which can assist in maintaining records of meetings. Ava's mission is to eliminate communication barriers by providing adaptable solutions for both professional and personal situations. Various business sectors like Office and Remote Work, Education and Learning, Healthcare and Telehealth make use of Ava to foster an ADA-compliant and inclusive environment.
