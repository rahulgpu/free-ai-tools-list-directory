About ListenRobo

ListenRobo is an AI tool specially designed for transcribing audio and video content into text and generating subtitles. It supports transcriptions in over 92 languages, making it globally accessible. Alongside the standard transcription feature, ListenRobo also offers translation capabilities, enabling users to convert their content into English. Besides, it can condense lengthy transcriptions into brief, easy-to-understand summaries by capturing key points and takeaways. The tool is especially suitable for individuals dealing with large amounts of audio or video data, such as journalists, researchers, and students. ListenRobo also addresses privacy concerns by securely storing all transcriptions using AES encryption. The tool supports various subtitle formats like SRT, VTT, and Text, ensuring compatibility with different platforms and media players. Beyond transcription, ListenRobo can improve SEO optimization by providing textual content for media, enhance accessibility with transcriptions and subtitles, boost audience engagement, assist in learning experiences, facilitate content repurposing, and expand the global reach of the content.
