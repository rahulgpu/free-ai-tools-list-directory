About NinjaVid

NinjaVid is an AI-powered tool designed to create high-quality, engaging videos with seamless subtitle integration swiftly and effortlessly. It supports various video file formats for seamless uploads. Upon uploading a video, its advanced AI system transcribes the content accurately, letting users customize the style, font, and color of the subtitles to match their brand. After customization, users can easily export their videos with embedded subtitles. Ideal for content creators, NinjaVid offers a user-friendly and intuitive design, ensuring smooth and rapid subtitle generation which enables creators to spend more time creating content and less time configuring. With cost-effective solutions tailored for creators of all sizes, it offers great value for its efficiency and functionality. NinjaVid also provides 24/7 support to assist with any technical or subtitle styling issues. Designed to cater to a global audience, it supports subtitles in over 50 languages. Data security is also a strong point, with state-of-the-art encryption and security protocols in place to safeguard user information and content. The tool allows you to customize subtitles according to your style and brand, granting the freedom to choose from different fonts, colors, and animations. A major selling point is its promise of quick turnarounds on projects.
