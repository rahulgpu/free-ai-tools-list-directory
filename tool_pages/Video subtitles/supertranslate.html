About supertranslate

Supertranslate is an AI tool designed to automatically generate English subtitles for video content in any language. It leverages the power of OpenAI's Whisper, a highly accurate speech-to-text engine. The user-friendly workflow of Supertranslate involves three simple steps: Upload the video, generate subtitles and then download the transcript in either .srt or .vtt format for use. Furthermore, Supertranslate offers an intuitive subtitle editor that allows users to fluidly split, merge, and adjust the timecodes of the generated subtitles. This ensures precision and user control over the content. The platform positions itself as an efficient, easy, and high-quality solution for all video subtitling needs, making the process as straightforward as possible for users of all levels.
