About Basil Finance

Basil Finance is an AI-powered tool designed to help users track and manage their investments. With Basil, users can securely link their financial accounts, allowing their portfolios to automatically sync every day. The platform supports thousands of different accounts, including popular crypto wallets and retirement accounts, providing users with a seamless integration experience. One of the key features of Basil is its ability to provide valuable insights on all of the user's assets. Users can easily track and monitor their overall portfolio performance, as well as individual account performance. Basil also offers detailed breakdowns of asset allocations and meaningful insights to help users make informed investment decisions.Another standout feature of Basil is its AI personal finance assistant, which utilizes cutting-edge chatbot technology. Users can engage in dynamic conversations with the AI assistant to access up-to-the-minute market data, delve deep into portfolio performance, and receive personalized suggestions and forecasts based on their financial goals.Basil also offers comprehensive research capabilities, allowing users to access in-depth insights on thousands of assets. Users can search for any asset and get granular details right at their fingertips.When it comes to security and privacy, Basil has been designed with the utmost consideration. The platform does not store or have access to user logins or API keys, and any data that is stored is anonymized to ensure user information remains protected.Overall, Basil Finance provides users with an all-in-one solution for tracking, managing, and gaining insights into their investments, leveraging AI and advanced features to enhance the financial management experience.
