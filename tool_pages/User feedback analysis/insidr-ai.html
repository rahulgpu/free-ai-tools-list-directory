About Insidr AI

Insidr AI is an intelligent tool designed to monitor and analyze competition to provide actionable insights in real-time. This tool allows enterprises, startups, and individuals to understand what users are saying about a product, identifying user sentiments, and trends to optimize product development and marketing efforts. It connects with real-world internet to gain recent knowledge on topics and is backed by proprietary technology developed from research papers. Insidr AI offers a comprehensive analysis toolset including sentiment analysis, trend analysis, SWOT analysis, root cause analysis, and various performance benchmarking features. The tool's API is soon to be available, allowing other applications to leverage its technology. To help in decision-making, it transcribes recordings, presenting reports and analysis for review. Additional features include a multilingual ability, secure payment processing, and an advanced dashboard that tracks various data points. Furthermore, Insidr AI has an affiliate system and easy export functionality, among other features.
