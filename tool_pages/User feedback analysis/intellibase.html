About Intellibase

Trill operates as an artificial intelligence (AI) powered research assistant specifically designed for user research. This tool offers support through all stages of the research process, aiming to accelerate progress from interviews to actionable insights. One of Trill's key features is its ability to provide a list of pertinent insights and observations for each interview, established on the unique objectives of the project on hand. This function enables users a starting point for deeper analysis. Trill also possesses the capability to identify collective themes and organize insight groupings instantly, eliminating the need for time-consuming coding of transcripts. Additionally, it provides potential options to organize and share findings, offering templates and a Notion-like editor to simplify the process of compiling comprehensive reports for stakeholders. The tool is especially beneficial for those seeking to enhance the efficiency of their research process and the delivery of their findings.
