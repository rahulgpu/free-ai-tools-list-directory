About SnapMeasureAI

SnapMeasureAI is an advanced computer vision solution that offers precise 3D body measurements and personalized size recommendations based on just a few images or a video. It aims to reduce clothing returns for online shoppers and ensure a perfect fit every time.With an impressive 99% accuracy in measuring 16 key body measurements, SnapMeasureAI achieves an average error of 7.0 mm (or 0.27 in). This tool allows users to confidently shop for clothing online, knowing that they will get accurate size recommendations.One of the main benefits of SnapMeasureAI is its ability to reduce returns and save costs by providing accurate body measurements. It addresses the issue of poor fit, which is responsible for over 70% of online returns. By improving brand loyalty and reducing landfill waste, it offers a solution that benefits both consumers and businesses.SnapMeasureAI relies on state-of-the-art AI technology, eliminating the need for a constant body pose or special clothing. It has been meticulously trained on a large dataset, ensuring reliable performance in diverse scenarios.Overall, SnapMeasureAI is a reliable and innovative tool that offers unparalleled precision in body shape and measurement. It eliminates the need for visiting a tailor and provides users with the convenience of finding the perfect fit without any hassle. For those interested in seeing it in action, a live demo or technology license can be obtained by contacting the SnapMeasureAI team.
