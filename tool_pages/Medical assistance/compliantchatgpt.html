About CompliantChatGPT

CompliantChatGPT is an AI-powered tool designed for enhancing healthcare-related work while maintaining the safety, security, and HIPAA Compliance of patients' data. The tool functions as an AI assistant that can aid healthcare professionals in various tasks including research, diagnosis, treatment planning, decision making, patient interactions, and paperwork. It also assists in reviewing patients' medication history, making the management of medical data easier. CompliantChatGPT is equipped with a user-friendly interface that treats queries with clear, straightforward responses while keeping patient healthcare information anonymous. It offers specialized modes to cater to different needs, including Bloodwork Analysis and PHI Anonymizer. CompliantChatGPT also features a Speech-to-Text utility, which transcribes or records meetings and interactions, providing automatic notes of input to the AI agent. Additionally, it offers a functionality to save hours of post-patient consultation documentation through automatic generation of SOAP notes after uploading audio consultations. Customization is another key feature, allowing users to tailor their mode, set prompts, and get personalized responses, hence providing a precise understanding every time. This tool is valuable to healthcare providers, research centers, health tech startups, medical institutions, and individal healthcare professionals.
