About Heeps

Heeps is a user-friendly AI platform aimed at automating the content creation process. Designed to generate and publish high-quality blogs and articles in bulk, it's an efficient tool for content marketers and publishers. This platform provides the capability to copy and paste or upload a list of article titles and take care of the rest. Content creators can streamline their workflow by allowing Heeps to automatically publish the generated articles to WordPress, or they can choose to download them in several formats such as markdown, HTML, or docx. The platform is built to handle any volume of content, whether it's one, a hundred, or a thousand articles. An added advantage of Heeps is its ability to produce beautifully formatted and SEO-optimized content with correct semantic HTML and keyword linking. By utilising the latest AI technology, the platform is able to generate articles that meet your specific requirements.
