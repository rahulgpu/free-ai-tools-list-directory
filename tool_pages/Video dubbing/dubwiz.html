About DubWiz

DubWiz is an advanced video translation and dubbing service. It utilises AI technologies to offer a user-friendly and efficient experience for translating and dubbing videos. Its features include Speech-to-Text transcription, Neural Machine Translation, and Neural Text-to-Speech for lifelike voiceovers. Designed with an emphasis on user-friendliness, DubWiz does not require professional translation or editing skills. The interface is intuitive and empowers users to start working immediately. Supported by modern neural networks, it streamlines the localization process and proposes to deliver fast results. Speech-to-Text transcription enables prompt conversion from human speech into text, with the added functionality of speaker distinction and the ability to upload custom dictionaries. The platform offers a transcript editor for refining AI-generated transcripts and an efficient translation editor. The Neural Machine Translation engine provides high-quality translations while maintaining the subject's context. Users can translate the entire text or selected sentences. DubWiz's innovative feature allows automatic removal of the original foreign-language voice from a video while retaining background sounds and music, allowing users to create professional voiceovers in their native language.
