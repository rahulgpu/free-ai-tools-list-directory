About EZdubs

EzDubs is an advanced digital language translation tool designed with the primary feature of overcoming language barriers. Available on both iOS and Android platforms, it comes with flexible functionalities such as chat translation, voice message dubbing, and live phone call translation. The chat translation feature enables users to communicate effectively across different languages, eliminating the tedious process of switching between translators and messaging apps. Alongside, the voice memo dubbing feature allows translating voice memos while preserving the speaker's voice and emotions. An essential video dubbing capability is included wherein users can record messages in their language and transform them to the language of the recipient, offering scope for further utilization. Another pioneering feature is the live phone call translation, facilitating conversations with people who don't speak your language. It can also be extended to landline communications, and the receiver isn't necessarily required to have the EzDubs application installed. In enterprise solutions, EzDubs provides video call translation on multiple platforms including Zoom, Teams, and Google Meets. It aids in communicating with diverse global teams and helps to reach out to foreign-language customers without a human interpreter requirement. The system also offers an innovative feature for low latency live stream dubbing with voice preservation ensuring effective translation for diverse business needs.
