About Viact

viAct is a Smart Site Safety System in Hong Kong that offers an AI-enabled centralized management platform and a digitalized permit-to-work system. It provides an artificial intelligence safety monitoring system for confined space work. The platform includes safety training with virtual reality technology and alerts for unsafe acts and dangerous situations in the mobile plant operation danger zone using AI video analytics.The system aims to reduce accidents and fatalities at construction sites, plants, and other workplaces through smart safety monitoring. It utilizes AI, IoT, and video analytics to enhance transparency of the workflow, improve operational productivity and efficiency, and enable smarter monitoring of jobsites.viAct is specifically curated for the construction, oil & gas, smart cities, manufacturing, and mining industries. It offers a series of smart solutions that proactively digitize safety monitoring to mitigate occupational injuries.The centralized management platform, viHUB, allows stakeholders to digitally monitor site safety performance and respond, manage, and record the insights captured by AI cameras and/or IoT devices. The digitalized permit-to-work system offers online application, issuance, and tracking of permits and operates using a mobile device.
