About TraceJourney

Tracejourney is an AI-powered tool designed to empower creatives by vectorizing and upscaling midjourney images. It's primarily integrated with the Discord platform, making it accessable and easily used within the creative community there. The tool works by replying with menu options to every midjourney upscale request, allowing users to choose their workflow and receive their vectorized or upscaled images quickly. Tracejourney uses cutting edge AI models that run on optimized GPU clusters to ensure quick results and enable a seamless creative process. The tool offers a range of features including batch processing of up to 10 images, background removal, quick adjustments like brightness, contrast, color correction, and sharpness, as well as file conversion to PNG and JPEG formats. For vectorization, users can choose their preferred file type between EPS, PDF or SVG. Another key feature of Tracejourney is that it gives upper hand to users in setting rules when it is invited to Discord servers, facilitating collaborative creation while accommodating user preferences. Lastly, users are able to connect with a large-scale community of fellow creatives on Discord thus enhancing their creative workflow.
