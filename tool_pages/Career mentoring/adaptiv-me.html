About Adaptiv Me

Adaptiv is an AI-powered platform designed to assist job seekers in refining their skills and securing employment. The tool provides personalized career mentoring and upskilling based on a unique combination of AI-driven career intelligence and human expertise. Adaptiv's main feature, Ada, an AI-powered career mentor, generates a 'Career Recipe' tailored to an individual's profile, skills, and career goals. Users can then follow a daily routine designed to enhance essential skills for their target jobs. The platform also facilitates weekly consultation with an industry expert for personalized guidance. Resources for learning are curated by AI and validated by these mentors to ensure quality and relevance. Furthermore, users have the opportunity to work on a professional project assigned by their mentor, exemplifying their newly acquired skills to potential employers. In the final stage of Adaptiv's 6-month program, users receive assistance in preparing for interviews and getting direct connections to recruiters and hiring managers. While the tool appears especially suited for individuals seeking careers within the expanding digital economy, its use extends to a broader range of professions and industries.
