About Ada

Adaptiv Academy is an AI-powered tool designed to help people upskill and advance their careers. It offers daily lessons, reminders and a Non-Fungible Token (NFT) profile, which is a digital asset stored on the blockchain. Through this tool, users can benefit from personalized lessons and reminders to stay on track with their career advancement goals. Adaptiv Academy also offers the ability to create a portfolio of digital assets that can be used to demonstrate skills and achievements. This makes it easier for users to find job opportunities and showcase their skills to potential employers. The app also provides access to a community of experts and mentors to help users stay motivated and reach their goals. With Adaptiv Academy, users can take advantage of AI-powered technology to upskill and advance their career.
