About AIclearing

AI Clearing is a digital field construction progress tracking tool that aims to decrease re-work costs and mitigate litigation risk in construction projects. It offers a fully databased solution that leverages machine learning technology, including drone-captured data and advanced 4D geospatial analytics, to generate comprehensive 3D site reports. These reports cover 100% of the construction area, facilitating accurate and efficient progress monitoring. The tool also provides automated and interactive online dashboards and PDF reports, streamlining reporting cycles and offering actionable insights. It can easily integrate with existing systems within organizations, ensuring seamless and efficient data flow. AI Clearing has partnered with renowned companies in the construction industry, indicating its credibility and reliability. It has been recognized as one of the top construction startups, and its solution is significantly faster than traditional quality surveying methods, with AI-based analytics results available overnight.By digitizing the entire construction site and visualizing data, AI Clearing enhances site awareness and reduces supervision costs. It also helps in early discrepancy detection, enabling prompt actions and minimizing issue escalation risks. The tool facilitates communication by providing transparent and real-time progress tracking monitoring reports, streamlining project documentation processes and engagement with stakeholders. Moreover, it assists in operations planning by estimating project deadlines based on the work progress.AI Clearing has proven success in supporting construction projects in various regions, including the US, Canada, Europe, and the Middle East. Its tool combines AI and GIS/CAD technologies to provide accurate and real-time insights into construction progress, elevating trust and productivity in the construction progress monitoring sector.
