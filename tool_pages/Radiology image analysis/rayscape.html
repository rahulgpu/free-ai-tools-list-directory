About Rayscape

Rayscape is an advanced Artificial Intelligence (AI) solution for radiology. It utilizes deep learning algorithms to improve X-ray and CT imaging accuracy and efficiency. Rayscape's AI assistant offers a range of features to assist radiologists in their diagnosis process, such as automatic detection and tracking of lung nodules, and evaluation of COVID patients. It provides fast integration and integration with existing infrastructure, and can be used on-premise or in the cloud. Rayscape also offers a dedicated team to help customers transition to the platform, and provides a range of resources and evidence to support its use. Its products and services are designed to make radiologists faster, better and more accurate in their diagnosis.
