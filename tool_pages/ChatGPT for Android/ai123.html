About AI123

AI123-AI Keyboard is a keyboard app that utilizes artificial intelligence to enhance the typing experience. The app features an AI-powered ChatGPT integration for intelligent conversations, powered by GPT for advanced language capabilities. The app also includes an auto-complete feature to speed up typing and a grammar and spelling checker for error-free writing. Additionally, the AI Reply Generator suggests contextually appropriate responses, saving users time and effort in crafting messages. This versatile writing tool can be used for any type of content and boasts an intuitive interface for a seamless typing experience. It can be used to write anything from essays to social media posts and can correct tone, spelling, and grammar for polished content. The app is targeted towards users looking to enhance their productivity and communication skills on their mobile devices. The app is available to download for free on Google Play, with in-app purchases available. It has received over 100 downloads and carries a PEGI 3 content rating. The app's developer, Like AI Inc, provides a privacy policy and contact information for users to address any questions or concerns.
