About Pitchleague

Pitchleague.ai is an AI-based platform that offers extensive assistance to startup founders looking to improve their pitch decks. PitchLeague.ai functions as a personalised AI coach that provides valuable feedback on how to enhance pitches at a slide-by-slide level. The tool employs AI technology to score the potential investability of a startup, providing founders with an analysis of their venture's attractiveness to investors. A standout feature of this tool is its comparative analysis feature, which enables founders to understand how their startup compares to others. The platform also increases visibility amongst potential investors. The user testimonials suggest that it has been instrumental in helping founders improve the structure and conciseness of their pitch decks. The tool also appears to offer some degree of validity and affirmation to founders who have scored highly on the platform's leaderboard. While useful to a broad range of founders, it may particularly benefit those seeking pre-seed funding. The tool is free to use and does not require any paid subscriptions or fees.
