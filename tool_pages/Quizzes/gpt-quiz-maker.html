About GPT Quiz Maker

GPT Quiz Maker for Forms is a powerful tool powered by AI ChatGPT, capable of creating comprehensive quizzes with a single click. The tool is ideal for teachers, students or any individuals or organizations that require forms with multiple-choice questions. The Quiz Maker supports multiple languages, difficulty levels, and tones. Users can conveniently input the desired quiz topics, choose the difficulty level, and the number of questions/answers for their quizzes. To create a quiz, users just need to click 'Generate Quiz' and let the tool do the rest. The quizzes generated could incorporate multiple question styles such as 'dropdown' and 'checkbox'. Developed leveraging the advanced GPT-4 model, the tool analyzes a given text source and forms questions based on it. It also allows users to conveniently dictate the number of correct answers, the number of questions, and the type of questions for their form-based quizzes. It also allows users to edit the generated questions before integrating them into Google Forms. The Quiz Generator significantly eases the process of creating multiple questions in bulk, making it worthwhile for the development of online quizzes, student assessments, or knowledge evaluations in various fields.
