About Larq

Larq is an AI-powered tool designed to enhance learning through the generation of quizzes. The primary function of Larq is to convert live meeting sessions, recorded courses, or uploaded videos into engaging quizzes. Adopting formats such as YouTube video links and PDF files, Larq enables the construction of quizzes based on pre-existing content. Moreover, the application works with text from meetings, providing comprehensible content for which quizzes can be created. An integral feature of Larq is its ability to assess the effectiveness of these quizzes by showing results with analytical precision. Users can view and analyze the performance of quiz participants, fostering an understanding of the information uptake, hence provoking possible adjustments and enhancements. This tool can be used among diverse teams - higher education institutions can verify the understanding and progress of remote students while employers can test employee comprehension concerning meetings. Additionally, it supports the creation and evaluation of quizzes for online courses at scale.
