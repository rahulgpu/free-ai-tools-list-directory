About Did You Learn

Did You Learn is an AI-powered Chrome extension that transforms your online experience into an active learning opportunity. With this extension, every webpage becomes an interactive learning environment. It utilizes advanced AI technology, specifically ChatGPT, to help you retain key concepts and crucial information. One of the main features of Did You Learn is the generation of thought-provoking multiple-choice questions based on the material you've comprehended. This helps you consolidate your understanding and reinforce your memory while staying on the same page. This extension is versatile and can adapt to various topics across the web, whether you're researching academic articles, industry news, or leisure articles. It seamlessly integrates into your browsing routine, ready to provide an engaging learning challenge whenever you're ready. Did You Learn offers a user-friendly experience with its intuitive interface. It allows you to understand and synthesize webpage content easier and faster, thanks to its advanced AI capabilities. If you're tired of passively browsing the web and want to actively enhance your knowledge, Did You Learn is the perfect tool for you. Install it now and embark on a journey of continuous growth and understanding.
