About Zenquiz

Zenquiz.app is an AI-based study tool that allows users to convert their notes into interactive quizzes. Users can import their study materials such as Word documents, PDF files, or even notes from integrations like Notion and Google Drive. The AI then generates practice exams from these notes, with question formats varying between multiple choice, true or false, and fill-in-the-blank. These generated quizzes provide a more engaging dimension to studying and can also be shared with friends and classmates for collaborative learning. There is also the added benefit of instant feedback that helps users gauge their understanding and correct misconceptions. In addition to students, the tool also benefits educators and businesses by providing a streamlined content development, assessment creation, and grading process. For businesses particularly, this tool could enhance knowledge and skill assessment during trainings and courses.
