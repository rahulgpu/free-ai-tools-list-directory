About OpExams

OpExams Questions Generator is a free AI-powered tool that allows users to generate various types of questions from any text. This includes multiple-choice questions, true or false, fill in the blanks, and open-ended questions. Users can also save the generated questions and utilize them in exams or quizzes.The tool offers the ability to generate questions based on a specific topic or by inputting a longer text. It supports multiple languages, allowing users to generate questions and quizzes in their preferred language. Additionally, it provides features to organize generated questions into collections, making it easy to manage and access them.OpExams Questions Generator keeps a history of all generated questions, allowing users to revisit previous generations as needed. It also offers the option to export questions and quizzes as CSV, XLSX, or DOCX files for further utilization or printing.The tool provides both a free and a paid subscription plan. The free plan allows for 10 initial generations and one generation per day, while the paid plan offers unlimited generations, the ability to share quizzes with unlimited users, multiple question collections, and more. OpExams Questions Generator offers an API for integrating its capabilities into other systems and provides customer support via email for any product-related concerns.Overall, OpExams Questions Generator is a versatile tool that simplifies the process of question generation for exams or quizzes, offering various question types, language support, organization features, and export options.
