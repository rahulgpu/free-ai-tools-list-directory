About Conker

Conker is an AI-powered tool designed to effortlessly create formative assessments and quizzes. It offers educators the ability to generate unique quizzes with a range of engaging question types to meet student needs as well as the demands of differentiated instruction. It also provides a library of ready-made, K12 standards-aligned assessments for convenience and reference. Another key feature of Conker is its focus on accessibility, offering integrated read-aloud accommodations for students who could benefit from additional support. Beyond this, it provides added flexibility by allowing quizzes to be exported to Google Forms or shared directly from the Canvas LMS, catering to a variety of quiz formats. Typical user reports suggest Conker can be an effective tool for quickly creating quizzes for immediate feedback, making it a valuable addition to the teaching workflow. Furthermore, it helps save time by automating the process of creating accurate quizzes, allowing educators to focus more on instruction. Conker also assists in ensuring that teaching meets curriculum standards through its standards-aligned quiz bank. Finally, due to its variety in question types, learning and assessment can become more interactive and engaging.
