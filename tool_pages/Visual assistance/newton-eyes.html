About Newton Eyes

Newton Eyes is an AI-based application designed to aid individuals with visual impairments. The application leverages AI technology to improve accessibility for visually challenged users in their day-to-day activities. Instead of relying on ambiguous directions and potentially misleading spatial cues, Newton Eyes provides a more intuitive and precise aid using AI. To utilize Newton Eyes, users should ensure that JavaScript is enabled. Although the specifics may be subject to change and updates, the underlying objective of the applicationassisting the visually challengedremains its core function. Notably, through the power of AI, Newton Eyes seeks to create a more accessible digital environment for all users, regardless of their visual capacity. The application aims to bridge the gap between technology and accessibility and to make daily tasks easier and more straightforward for visually impaired individuals.
