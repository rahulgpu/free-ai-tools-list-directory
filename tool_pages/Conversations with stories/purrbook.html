About Purrbook

PCM's Three Little Pigs is an educational application that offers an interactive reading experience. Aimed at children, it is a preview of the PurrBook app by Pajama Cats Media Inc. This app brings to life the classic tale of the Three Little Pigs through an interactive and educational platform aimed at improving the reading and speaking skills of children. Beyond the enchanting story, PCM's Three Little Pigs stands out with its unique multilingual option. This feature allows children to explore new languages in an engaging and easy way, further enhancing their linguistic abilities. A unique feature of the app is the 1:1 coaching feature which is designed to boost reading confidence and literacy. This makes PCM's Three Little Pigs not just entertaining for kids but a valuable educational tool for parents. The app is available on iPhone, iPad, and iPod touch, and requires iOS 13.0 or later. The primary language of the app is English and it is suitable for ages 4 and above. It has been designed with privacy in mind: while it may collect data, it doesn't link this data to the user's identity.
