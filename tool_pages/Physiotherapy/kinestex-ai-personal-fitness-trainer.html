About KinesteX AI – Personal Fitness Trainer

KinesteX is a disruptive tool in the fitness and health arenas, offering an innovative embeddable SDK. Using the device's camera, KinesteX's AI accurately analyzes human movements in real-time, delivering immediate, professional-level advice tailored to the user's form. The movement recognition models maintain a consistently high accuracy level, this greatly enhances the workout experience for users and assists them in achieving their fitness objectives more quickly. Notable features include personalized workout plans, rep, calorie, time and mistakes tracking during the workout, real-time data communication with your application, motivational voice feedback, and design customization options. Integration of the KinesteX API into your app or workflow is seamless, empowering end users with access to personalized workouts such as strength, cardio, weight loss, muscle gain, physiotherapy, and rehabilitation exercises. Additionally, the application promotes service enhancement with real-time health data sharing from end users, allowing tracking of reiterations of exercises, calories burned, mistakes made, and time spent. Companies that seek innovative AI fitness solutions that stimulate user engagement and deliver tangible results have successfully integrated KinesteX.
