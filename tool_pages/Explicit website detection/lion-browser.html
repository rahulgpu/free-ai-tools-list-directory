About Lion Browser

Lion Accountability Browser is a web browsing tool that utilizes Artificial Intelligence (AI) and machine learning algorithms to detect explicit images and websites in real-time. This app maintains a log of explicit websites visited during browsing and can send them as weekly logs to a partner or partners of the user's choice. With a customizable detection level, Lion Browser can fine-tune its sensitivity to track suggestive images or explicit images only. Additionally, Blacklist feature of the browser tracks or blocks over 4.5 million adult websites, and users can add any website to the custom blacklist. Lion Browser offers history logs, and users can select to send daily or weekly emails to their partners of their browsing history. Moreover, this browsing tool boasts a clean and intuitive minimalistic interface and offers Parental Controls, which help users disable images and media for added protection. The app runs smoothly like all the most popular browsers such as Safari, Firefox, Chrome, and DuckDuckGo. The application has received positive reviews for its design simplicity, accessibility, and efficient accountability options from various users. Lion Accountability Browser is available for download on the App Store and is constantly updated, with a newsletter available for subscribers to receive release notes and updates from the company.
