About Rokoko

Rokoko Vision is an AI-powered motion capture solution that allows creators to easily record and interpret human movement in 3D animation. By utilising machine learning algorithms, it processes video input, whether live from a webcam or a pre-recorded video, into digital movement data. This data can be reviewed, edited, and cleaned up via Rokoko's accompanying software, Rokoko Studio, offering features such as a foot-locking filter for enhanced accuracy. The data can then be exported to different 3D platforms and tools, including Blender, Unity, Unreal, or any others that support .FBX or .BVH file formats. One stand-out feature of Rokoko Vision is the option of dual-camera motion capture tracking, offering higher fidelity and accuracy in capturing the depth and detail of the captured motions, and minimizing any blind spots. While the solution is hailed for its simplicity and proficiency, it also offers an accessible starting point for hobbyists and newcomers with the single-camera setup, which remains free to use and provides a straightforward introduction to the realm of animation.
