About Astra

Astra AI is an artificial intelligence-powered tutoring tool designed to enhance the learning of mathematical concepts for students. It offers one-on-one math tutoring in a more accessible, affordable, and convenient manner overcoming the limitations of traditional tutoring in terms of cost, hassle, and availability. Astra AI includes a math keyboard that simplifies entering complex math formulas for problem-solving. The 'scan the problem' feature allows users to photograph equations for instant input into the platform, reducing the need for manual entry. In addition, there is an interactive graph feature providing a dynamic avenue to visualize and explore various mathematical concepts. A unique aspect of the tool is the 'Magic Notebook' back-end feature that is touted for producing better math results. Users can also anticipate a tool that encourages independent problem-solving rather than simply delivering answers. Student reviews suggest enhanced understanding of concepts, improved active participation in classes, better grades, and reduced dependency on traditional tutoring.
