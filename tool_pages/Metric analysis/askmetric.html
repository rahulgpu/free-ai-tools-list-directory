About AskMetric

AskMetric is an AI-powered tool that offers a wide range of services to empower merchants with platform data and insights. Through its AI technology, AskMetric provides answers and assistance with metrics visualization, keyword searching and recommendations to product, platform and ads strategies. The tool enables the user to monitor the keyword trends across a range of e-commerce platforms, including marketplaces, DTC and social media. It also offers deep insights into the performance of products across these platforms and provides effective tactics to improve their market position.AskMetric's capabilities include the ability to track core metrics in real-time, monitor competitors' strategies and make recommended changes quickly to stay competitive. The tool can generate reports that reveal product metrics and provide insights that allow users to discover the future of their market.The tool offers a variety of features, including product demos, product analysis, and keyword trending analysis that provide merchants with the most effective tactics to target their market and gain a competitive advantage. AskMetric also offers enterprise cooperation and elite talent recruitment services.Overall, AskMetric is a comprehensive e-commerce platform that offers a range of AI-powered services, giving merchants powerful insights into their market position and helping them to become more competitive by making data-driven strategic decisions.
