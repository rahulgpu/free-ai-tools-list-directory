About AIM

AIM Intelligent Machines is transforming the construction and mining sectors through autonomous technology. The companys primary function is to retrofit heavy machinery with their advanced system, enabling fully autonomous operation. This applies to a diverse range of equipment types, irrespective of the equipment's age, size, or brand. The conversion preserves the ability for manual operation, adding a layer of flexible control. Implementing AIM's technology results in the machinery being able to run continuously throughout the year, in any weather, without an operator, while also providing 360-degree safety measures to prevent potential accidents. AIMs advancements also make industrial workplaces safer for ground staff by adding multiple safety layers that protect them from hazardous environments.The capabilities AIM offers allow businesses to move their workforce to more advanced positions such as site planning, remote management, and data analytics. Furthermore, every AIM-enabled machine comes with insurance for autonomous operation from a global insurance company.The technology is underpinned by AI and machine learning, with the AIM team having extensive expertise in autonomy, robotics, and hardware. This broad knowledge base and proven track record in advanced machine deployment supply assurance and reliability to clients. Clients include both construction and mining industries, with the tool effectively being able to maximize resource utilization, significantly increasing efficiency and safety.
