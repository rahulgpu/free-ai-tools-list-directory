About Skepticaltom

Skeptical Tom is an AI tool designed to help users control their impulsive buying habits. With the influence of Skeptical Tom, users are encouraged to reconsider their purchases before making impulsive decisions. This tool utilizes artificial intelligence to assist individuals who may feel lucky and prone to making impulsive purchases. By acting as a virtual cat, Skeptical Tom discourages impulsive shopping by promoting thoughtful consideration.Skeptical Tom's goal is to bring a positive impact to users' purchasing habits. By intervening when impulsive buying tendencies arise, this AI tool helps users avoid making regrettable decisions.
