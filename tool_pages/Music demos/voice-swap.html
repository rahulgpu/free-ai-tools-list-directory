About Voice Swap

Voice-Swap is an AI-driven platform that enables the transformation of an individual's singing voice, offering users a new and exciting way to produce musical content. The system was crafted in collaboration with artists, who receive royalties for the use of their AI-generated voices. As such, Voice-Swap presents a unique opportunity for remote collaborations and novel musical explorations. Users can easily transform their vocals to match the style of various artists on the platform, affording them the opportunity to hear their musical ideas from fresh perspectives. One innovative feature of the platform is 'Stem-Swap,' which gives users the ability to replace the voice on their track with a voice from the platform's roster of artists. Moreover, artists have the potential to respond to requests and engage in various collaborations with users of the platform. Voice-Swap provides users with the power to share their utterances on social media or to employ the AI renditions of session singers in their tracks. The platform maintains a strict content screening to prevent hate speech or inappropriate content, fostering a safe and respectful environment. To utilize it, users can simply upload a WAV file, which will be processed and converted using the AI. Voice-Swap is as much an instrument as a tool, requiring skill to achieve optimum results. All in all, this platform offers a ground-breaking and collaborative approach to vocal manipulation and music production, supporting an entirely new creative process.
