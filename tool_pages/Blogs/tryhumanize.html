About TryHumanize

TryHumanize is an AI-enabled tool designed to simplify the process of generating long-form articles. It provides a no-code solution which allows users to automate their content generation. By utilizing complex data clustering and prompt engineering, users can define the parameters for their content and let the AI shape their articles. Features include one-shot prompting for swift content generation and the capability to publish drafts instantly on several platforms, including WordPress, Ghost, Shopify, and LinkedIn. The platform uses an encrypted system to protect all user information and data, ensuring a secure usage experience. It offers a variety of packages to cater to different user needs, providing options for solos, agencies, teams, and enterprises. Dominating the field of content crafting, TryHumanize provides integrations with several technologies, including Claude 2, Chat GPT, and Unsplash to help users generate more engaging content quickly and efficiently. In terms of ease of use, TryHumanize utilizes a single-click publish system that enables users to turn complex tasks into simple, effortless actions. Its design allows users to easily generate and share their content while simultaneously enhancing their online presence.
