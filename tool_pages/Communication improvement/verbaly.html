About Verbaly

Verbaly is an AI-enabled application designed to improve users' communication skills by providing in-depth analysis and coaching. The tool allows users to record speeches or presentations, which are then analyzed by the platform for various speech qualities. The AI, named Vee, reviews the recording, evaluates critical intents of communication, identifies areas for improvement, and provides pointed feedback to help users refine their speech and delivery. Verbaly also tracks users' progress over time, enabling them to measure their effectiveness and improvement in communication. Further, Verbaly provides a safe and private space for analyzing and improving speech with strict privacy protocols in place, ensuring the user's recordings and personal data are not shared with any third parties. The oratory practice, coupled with actionable analysis, helps individuals become more effective communicators by breaking bad speech habits and aligning the communication style closer to professional standards.
