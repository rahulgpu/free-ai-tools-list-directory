About SpeakHints

SpeakHints is an AI-powered tool designed to enhance communication by providing real-time, private speech suggestions to users. Primarily aimed at spoken interactions, it leverages AI to generate relevant cues on what to say next, enhancing conversational flow and confidence during online meetings, presentations, interviews, calls and more. SpeakHints accomplishes this by capturing spoken conversations in real time, utilizing OpenAI models to generate appropriate and context-based hints.The tool has an array of functionality enriching features. It gives the ability to automatically finish sentences, provide AI-assisted answers, summarize the preceding exchanges, and translate phrases across multiple languages instantly. This feature-rich tool is highly customizable, allowing users to harness built-in quick hints or personalize their own.SpeakHints operates across a variety of platforms and is compatible with several video conferencing apps such as Zoom, Google Meet, and Microsoft Teams. It supports over 30 languages, making it widely accessible. Through real-time transcription of mic or system audio, suggestions are generated and displayed in an overlay visible only to the user. To ensure privacy, user speech is processed securely, with data not stored without explicit consent.
