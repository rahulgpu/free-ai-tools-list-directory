About Millis AI

Millis AI is a developer-oriented tool designed for creating low-latency voice agents swiftly and affordably. It empowers users to build voice assistants that can handle complex dialogue dynamics, such as interruptions and understanding human intent, and bring about a more realistic and natural conversational flow. Its affordable rates make it a feasible option for a wide array of applications, from personal use to enterprise-level operations. Incorporating Millis AI into existing systems is made easy by its low code requirements, and it accommodates either the use of proprietary models or third-party, LLM-based chatbots for quick deployment. The platform is optimized for low latency to ensure a seamless conversational experience. To provide a scalable service that can stand up to enterprise-level demands, Millis AI is backed by a team of DevOps engineers adept at supporting systems capable of managing hundreds of millions of video call minutes on a daily basis. Millis AI offers impressive functionality in a cost-effective and developer-friendly package for those wanting to leverage cutting-edge conversational AI technology.
