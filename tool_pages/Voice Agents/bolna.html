About Bolna

Bolna is an end-to-end platform designed to build, deploy, and monitor voice-based AI agents. Its primary function is to automate calls and tasks by constructing AI agents that can carry out high-quality intent-driven conversations in various languages. Bolna's AI agents are capable of dealing with pauses and interruptions - nuances that usually occur in human conversation. They also possess infinite memory, allowing them to recall past interactions and personas for a more personalized conversation experience.The platform offers a selection of both proprietary and open-source models, allowing users to experiment and choose the most suitable one for their needs. The AI agents developed on Bolna platform can mimic human voices to deliver a more natural and human-like conversation experience. Bolna's AI agents excel in various areas such as understanding customer intent, scheduling meetings, automating initial interview processes and candidate screenings, and engaging in interactive dialogue for lead qualification. They can also be used creatively for personal or entertainment purposes.In addition, they can revolutionize customer service operations by managing inquiries, troubleshootings, and other support-related tasks. They can be employed to automate customer conversations for purposes such as EMI collections, defaulter management in the insurance and lending sectors. As a scalable solution, Bolna can facilitate large scale human-like conversations, making it a useful tool for businesses and organizations of various types and sizes. Comprehensive documentation is available for further assistance in leveraging the Bolna platform.
