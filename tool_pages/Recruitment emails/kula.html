About Kula

Kula AI is an AI-powered tool that helps recruiters craft personalized and thoughtful cold outbound recruitment emails quickly and easily. The tool aims to assist users in writing unique messages that stand out from the competition. It offers several features to enhance the recruitment process, such as automated passive candidate engagement and making employee referrals proactive. Users can analyze their entire recruitment funnel at once using the analytics feature.With Kula AI, users can generate highly personalized emails with the click of a button, increasing response rates and hiring conversions. The tool provides a step-by-step process to create these emails, allowing users to select their intent, state the role they're hiring for, add a dynamic AI block, and pick a tone for their email. Kula AI automates one of the longest steps in the recruitment workflow by diving deep into each prospect's unique experience and skills to create hyper-personalized messages.The tool also offers AI Blocks to spotlight a candidate's experience, skills, or background, saving time on candidate research. Kula AI takes data security seriously and ensures that user data is not sold or shared with third parties without explicit permission.Overall, Kula AI is designed to streamline the recruitment process, improve response rates, and help recruiters build genuine relationships with candidates.
