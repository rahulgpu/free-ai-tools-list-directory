About SimpliTerms

SimpliTerms is an AI-powered tool designed to assist users in understanding the key aspects of privacy or terms of use policies on any webpage. The tool doesn't make the user blindly accept terms and privacy policies. Instead, it generates concise summaries of these policies, effectively distilling them from thousands of words into a brief summary highlighting the crucial points. These summaries aid users in better understanding what they are consenting to when they agree to a website's policies. Besides summarizing, SimpliTerms offers other benefits such as saving users' time, protecting their privacy by helping them understand what data each website collects, and preventing potential legal issues by alerting users to policies they may not agree with. The application is simple to use, with the summaries available through the SimpliTerms extension. This means by clicking on the SimpliTerms extension, users can access a summary of the policies of the page they are on. SimpliTerms offers multiple subscription tiers, allowing users to choose the plan that best fits their needs.
