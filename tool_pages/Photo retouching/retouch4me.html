About Retouch4me

Retouch4me is a collection of AI-powered plugins designed for photo and video retouching. The software emulates professional retouching processes, maintaining the original skin texture for a natural look in the final image. Users can simply upload their photos to the plugins and let the AI handle the retouching process. The available plugins offer a wide range of operations from color matching and dust detection to specific retouches such as smooth skin areas, eye brilliance, and skin tone adjustment. The plugins also offer removal of minor skin defects and even out folds and wrinkles in fabrics. The interface is user-friendly with flexible settings. The software allows for work with layers, hence original files remain preserved. It provides faster processing time, enabling batch processing. Importantly, it's designed to work locally and doesn't require internet access for operation, ensuring the confidentiality of users' photos. The system requirements for Retouch4me are Windows 7 (x64), Windows 10 (x64), Windows 11, or macOS 10.14+, at least 6 Gb RAM, and a suitable processor and GPU.
