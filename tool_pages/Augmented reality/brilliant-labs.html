About Brilliant Labs

Brilliant Labs' Frame AI Glasses are part of an open-source ecosystem aimed at aiding developers and creatives in reimagining the future. These glasses are designed to provide 'AI superpowers' to the wearer, such as visual analysis of the world around you, and are powered by OpenAI and their Whisper technology. This allows the wearer to translate what they see and hear in real-time, blending digital and physical realities. The glasses also have the capacity to search the web for visuals in the wearer's field of view. The Frame AI Glasses encourage modification, hacking, and building, inviting the boldest ideas to augment their functionality. They are fully open-source and invite the wearer to modify the tool according to their own needs and preferences. Brilliant Labs provides ongoing developer resources and documentation and hosts a community of hackers, tinkerers, and artists through their dedicated Discord channel.The company's other tool, Noa app, is designed to pair with the Frame glasses, enhancing navigational capabilities and fostering a better understanding of the world the wearer navigates. The Frame AI Glasses represent a reimagining of eyewear for the Artificial Intelligence era, serving as a key tool in Brilliant Labs' mission to deepen understanding and foster creativity through open-source technology.
