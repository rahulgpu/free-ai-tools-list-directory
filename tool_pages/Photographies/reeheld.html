About Reeheld

The G-Series collection encompasses a vast array of photography enhancement tools. It features diverse types of lenses, including Telephoto 2x, Wide Angle 110, Fisheye 220, Macro 10x, Long Range Macro, and Anamorphic 1.33x lenses. Each lens serves a specific photographic function-- from achieving wide landscape shots to creating blur-free macro close-ups and cinema-like videography effects.The collection also offers various lens accessories, such as different iPhone case types and a compact tripod, catered to enhance the overall photography experience. In addition, there's a TechBag carrying case for convenient and safe equipment storage.Added to the product offering are the G-Series Bundles, which group different G-Series lenses together for different contexts: Macro, Action, Photo, and Cinematic. These differ based on the type of photography or videography they are designed for. Users can also opt for the full set for a comprehensive range of tools. Additionally, G-Series Filters are included as well. These comprise a set of magnetic filters in varying categories (CPL Polarizer, Black Mist, VND, ND) for controlled light passage and color balance, contributing to improved image quality.The brand also provides associated apps including Reeflex Pro Camera, ReeXpose, ReeHeld, and ReeLapse, each designed for specific shooting modes ensuring versatility in usage while enabling professional-grade photography on mobile devices.
