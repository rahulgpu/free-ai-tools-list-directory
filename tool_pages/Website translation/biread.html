About BiRead

BiRead is a web tool that utilizes artificial intelligence to facilitate instant bilingual reading. With a single click, users can transform webpage content into a bilingual format, making it easier to read and understand content in different languages. BiRead is designed to be versatile, applicable to a wide range of use cases such as translating social media posts, simplifying multilingual article reading, easing the process of participation in multilingual conversations, and assisting in language learning. The tool comes with a learning mode that facilitates reading of original texts in a new language, by displaying the users first language when necessary. It features two tiers a free version that leverages Google Translate and supports 32 languages and an advanced version that uses a proprietary AI translation system, supports over hundred languages and works on any website. BiRead's objective is to break down language barriers and provide users with seamless access to content on a global scale.
