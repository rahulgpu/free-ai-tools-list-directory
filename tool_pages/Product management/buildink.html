About Buildink

Buildink AI Product Manager is an artificial intelligence tool designed to streamline the process of product requirement documentation for various projects. It automates and optimizes discovery and requirements gathering, thereby enhancing communication among stakeholders and boosting productivity for software agencies, internal teams, freelance developers, and startup founders. With the help of an AI companion, users can easily interact to clarify ambiguities and refine product ideas. Following this, the tool generates a comprehensive document with product requirements, facilitating team alignment and streamlined project execution. This tool is especially beneficial in reducing time spent on scoping, discovery calls, and documentation, enabling users to focus more on building products and winning deals. In addition, Buildink AI Product Manager offers assistance in creating project briefs within minutes and enhancing stakeholder communication through effective project sharing options.
