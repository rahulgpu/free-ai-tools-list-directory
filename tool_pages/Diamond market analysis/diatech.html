About Diatech

Diatech AI is an advanced technology solution provider specifically tailored for the diamond industry. Their AI-powered solutions aim to empower businesses by offering a range of functionalities for both natural and lab-grown polished diamonds. With a focus on sourcing, marketing, and decision-making processes, Diatech provides tools for demand-supply analytics, price analytics, customer behavior analytics, and market prediction. In addition to these analytical capabilities, Diatech also offers a services marketplace where businesses can list their stones on multiple feeds to maximize coverage. They also provide the option of creating a white-labeled storefront or marketplace within minutes. Diatech's AI-powered analytics are designed to enable better decision-making by providing insights into the diamond industry. This allows businesses to track and analyze customer behavior across sales channels through their DIATRACK CRM tool. Diatech has established itself as a trusted provider within the industry and counts numerous leaders as their clients. They offer support through various channels such as email, phone, and physical location. The company also provides an app for convenient access to their analytics platform. To maintain transparency and adhere to privacy protocols, Diatech has a privacy policy in place. By offering comprehensive AI-driven solutions, Diatech AI empowers businesses in the diamond industry with valuable insights and tools to navigate and optimize their operations.
