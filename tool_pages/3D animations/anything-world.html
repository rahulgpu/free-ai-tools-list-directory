About Anything World

Anything World is an AI-powered 3D Animation and Automated Rigging platform. It allows users to upload static 3D models and transform them automatically into fully rigged and animatable models using AI technology. Moreover, the platform includes a vast library of AI-animated 3D models, providing a wide variety of assets that can be incorporated into games, apps, or metaverse environments. Anything World also offers SDKs for both Unity and Unreal, facilitating integration with these popular game engines. Further enhancing user interaction, the AI Creator feature enables users to create and control 3D worlds using voice or text commands. This tool is designed to speed up development and reduce costs, fuelling creativity and enabling users to bring 3D animations to life at scale. Alongside these features, a proprietary machine learning system underpins the platform's ability to understand, rig, and animate almost any 3D model. Overall, Anything World aims to be a comprehensive solution for generating and manipulating 3D content via AI driven processes.
