About Deepmotion

DeepMotion is an AI-powered motion capture and real-time body tracking tool that enables users to generate 3D animations from videos through any web browser. The tool offers four products - Animate 3D, Real-Time Body Tracking, Virtual Reality Tracking, and Motion Brain - each of which caters to different animation requirements. Animate 3D generates 3D animations from videos, while Real-Time Body Tracking offers the ability to capture and reconstruct 3D full body motion. Virtual Reality Tracking provides immersive full-body avatars using minimal trackers, and Motion Brain transforms digital beings from animated to alive. DeepMotion's technology is powered by AI and markerless motion capture, making it an industry-leading web and mobile solution for creating and animating digital characters. The team is led by industry veterans with years of experience from companies like Blizzard, Pixar, and Disney, working towards their mission of leading the field of Motion Intelligence. DeepMotion has partnered with leading companies like Samsung, Qualcomm, Nvidia, and Intel to provide optimized performance and better user experience. Users can try the tool for free or contact the sales team for more information.
