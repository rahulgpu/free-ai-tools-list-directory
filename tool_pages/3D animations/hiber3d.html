About Hiber3D

Hiber3D is an innovative tool that leverages artificial intelligence for 3D world creation using natural language processing. Users are offered the capability to construct interactive 3D worlds and publish them on the web with ease through the Hiber3D Development Kit (HDK). The goal of Hiber3D is to expedite the adoption of 3D web technologies, thereby enabling users to create and share their own digital spaces in a more immersive format. The tool is currently on a stage where interested parties can sign up for a waitlist to test out the generative AI capabilities it has to offer. Additional resources, such as documentation for the use of HDK, are available for users to learn how to maximize their experience with the platform.
