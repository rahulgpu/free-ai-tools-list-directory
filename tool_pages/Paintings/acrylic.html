About Acrylic

Acrylic is an AI-powered website developed using Typedream that allows users to create and purchase personalized paintings. This tool uses artificial intelligence technology to help users create unique artwork based on their individual style which can be previewed using augmented reality. With the AR staging feature, users can see how the paintings will look in their own homes before making a purchase. The generated images are of high quality and can be transformed into a painting on canvas at an affordable price.Acrylic provides an easy-to-use interface that offers preset selections to guide users through the painting creation process. Users can also opt to explore and experiment on their own. Purchasing made easy with support for Apple Pay directly from the app.Acrylic is designed to uplift home decor by enabling users to express their creativity and personalize their surroundings. It offers a unique and affordable solution to those who are accustomed to uninspiring wall decoration. Users can join the private beta to use this tool and purchase paintings directly from their phones. This AI-powered platform is ideal for people looking for personalized and unique artwork to decorate their homes.
