About Langdock

Langdock is an AI platform designed to increase productivity within teams. It acts as a daily GDPR-compliant chat companion for employees, providing a unified platform for automations and custom assistants. Langdock hosts locally learnt models (LLMs) which allows companies to maintain full control over their data. Notably, it supports GDPR-compliant servers in Europe, including third-party AI models such as the GPT-4 model, hosted by Microsoft Azure in Stockholm.On this platform, companies can automate tasks, generate insights through AI interactions and collaborate more efficiently. Langdock's features include AI chat for daily use, AI assistants for teams, the ability to automate workflows with AI, and an integration function that connects various tools and apps. The platform can be accessed via API, providing broad flexibility and scalability.Data security is a significant aspect of the platform, with AES-256 encryption for data at rest and in transit, and robust processes to ensure workspace privacy and information accuracy. It offers custom data retention and audit logs, and inputs aren't used for re-training models, ensuring data privacy.Furthermore, the platform's ISO 27001 and SOC 2 Type 2 certifications demonstrate adherence to strict security measures and data integrity.Langdock is also customizable, with users being able to connect their tools, choose their model and location, and access the API. Additional features include a built-in prompt library, multi-user collaboration capabilities, and the ability to train and prepare the workforce for the era of AI. Equipped with these tools and features, Langdock empowers employees to handle everyday tasks more effectively.
