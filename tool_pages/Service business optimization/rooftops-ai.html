About Rooftops AI

Rooftops AI is an AI-powered platform designed to transform service businesses with a focus on contracting and trade industries. The platform offers a number of AI-driven functionalities aimed at improving efficiency and profitability. This includes intelligent task management, automated marketing, and 24/7 customer support. One key feature of Rooftops AI is its AI Chatbot, designed to provide high-quality automated support and lead generation, capturing essential information about prospective clients. Rooftops AI also features Copilot, an AI system designed to assist in decision making through smart suggestions. It also includes a feature known as AI Creator Studio, capable of generating content to elevate the online presence of a brand. This system automates posts, analyses engagement, and helps users reach their target audience effectively. Another feature, AI City Maps, currently in beta, offers potential to unlock urban landscapes data, integrated with Google Maps, for location-based data analysis and solar potential assessments. Overall, Rooftops AI is aimed at leveraging the power of AI to help users grow and scale their revenue in highly competitive industries.
