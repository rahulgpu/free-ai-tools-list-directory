About SayData

SayData is a customer-facing analytics tool designed specifically for SaaS platforms. It seamlessly integrates AI-driven analytics into your application, providing beautiful visualizations and a user-friendly experience. With SayData, there's no need for complicated SQL queries or complex programming languages. Users can simply ask questions in plain English, and the tool will automatically process and analyze the data to provide insights.The tool offers a comprehensive suite of data visualization and analysis tools, enabling users to explore and uncover insights in real-time. Custom dashboards and reports can be created to showcase data in an easily understandable format. SayData is also tailored to the unique needs of each business, allowing users to ask questions that are specific to their industry and operations.SayData supports various data integrations, including MySQL, ClickHouse, Athena, MariaDB, PostgreSQL, MongoDB, Rocket, and Snowflake. It can be seamlessly integrated into dashboards, applications, Slack workspaces, and shared via links. The tool also offers white-label analytics, providing users with a secure portal to view their own data.When it comes to data security, SayData ensures complete privacy with its privacy-first architecture. The tool undergoes annual penetration tests to maintain top-notch security measures.SayData offers flexible pricing options, including a Standard Plan for individuals and small businesses, a Growth Plan for growing enterprises, and an Enterprise Plan for large organizations. Each plan has different features and support levels to cater to specific needs.Overall, SayData is a powerful AI analytics tool that simplifies data analysis, enhances data visualization, and provides valuable insights for SaaS platforms.
