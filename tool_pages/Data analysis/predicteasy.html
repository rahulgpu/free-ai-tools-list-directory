About PredictEasy

PredictEasy is a robust no-code AI-based data analytics platform used for a variety of tasks amongst which are auditing, describing, predicting, and forecasting data. It is featured prominently in the Google Workspace Marketplace and its major selling point is the ability to execute complex data tasks with no coding knowledge required. The platform is equipped with Advanced Machine Learning (AutoML) capabilities that allow the automation of building and selecting the best machine learning models for a set data, thus making it accessible to users without machine learning expertise. Its auditing and describing tools give users insight into the characteristics of their data, helping them identify any potential issues or biases, which is useful for ensuring that data is being used fairly and ethically. In a nutshell, PredictEasy is a comprehensive solution offering a wide range of features for data analysts, scientists, and businesses to effectively analyse and draw insights from their data for informed decision making. From preprocessing and visualization, to model building, PredictEasy helps users optimize their use of data.
