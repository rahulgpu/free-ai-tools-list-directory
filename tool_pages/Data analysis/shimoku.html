About Shimoku

Shimoku is an analytics and artificial intelligence SaaS (Software as a Service) solution aimed at enabling customers to build custom data products quickly. The AI-equipped software is able to produce predictive analytics products that can anticipate various factors such as churn, cohort behavior, user sessions, future purchases, perceived anomalies, and demand planning.Shimoku caters to a variety of industries, including but not limited to, eCommerce, Software as a Service (SaaS) businesses, the Data & AI sector, and Banking & Insurance. The platform is designed for roles such as CIOs, Heads of Sales, and Python Developers.As part of its offerings, Shimoku provides different applications like 'Revenue Copilot' that helps optimize marketing and sales performance, and 'Python AI Apps' that allow the creation of data-driven products. Additionally, they also offer an 'AI App Factory' for custom solutions and provide AI consulting services.Other features highlight the ability to predict upselling possibilities, the capacity to create data-driven products in minutes, and the provision for machine learning for non-experts, allowing for user data to be transformed into machine learning models.The platform also emphasizes transparency and comprehension of its AI processes, promoting 'explainability' to help users intuitively understand the drivers and barriers of their data.Overall, Shimoku represents a unified platform promising agility and efficiency in data-based decision making, fostering a more streamlined approach to adopting advanced AI and data tools.
