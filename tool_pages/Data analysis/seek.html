About Seek

Seek AI is a generative AI tool designed for advanced data analytics. Its major characteristic is the natural language interface for data, which allows users to query their data in plain English and extract insights in real-time. This means users can directly interact with their data, ask questions, and understand complex datasets without utilising traditional data tools. The application of Seek AI extends to various fields where data-driven insights are critical, as evident in company testimonials and case studies. Furthermore, Seek AI has achieved noteworthy accuracy, being listed as a top model on the Yale Spider benchmark. This technology securely breaks through data information barriers, helping businesses modernise their analytics with AI-generated database queries. Additionally, Seek AI is regularly updated with novel features and enhancements, ensuring its users are equipped with the most effective tool for their data analysis needs. Crucially, it also offers compatibility with popular data products, which adds further flexibility for its end users.
