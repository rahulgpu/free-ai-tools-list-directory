About CLEVR AI

ClevrAI is an AI-powered tool designed to help digital marketing professionals make informed decisions with connected data insights. It simplifies the process of gathering, analyzing, and presenting digital marketing data, allowing users to quickly identify what is and isn’t working, and make adjustments accordingly.ClevrAI provides an intuitive interface and easy-to-use features that don’t require any data science background or digital marketing experience. It offers transparent pricing and packages based on the depth of information provided and features used. With connected data insights across the most critical digital channels, users can access valuable marketing analytics without the need for data scientists or developers.ClevrAI also provides recommendations on what steps should be taken to increase reach and improve engagement, and can be used to create content tailored to target audiences. It can analyze keywords, paid ads, product pages, and social media channels, and provide actionable insights to drive growth. It also offers dedicated resources to help implement recommended website changes, as well as tools to track the digital marketing performance of any business.Overall, ClevrAI provides a powerful suite of tools to help digital marketing professionals maximize the performance of their website and marketing channels with ease. It offers an effective way to access the data and insights needed to optimize digital marketing campaigns and boost performance.
