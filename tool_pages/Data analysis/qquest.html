About Qquest

Qquest is a generative AI tool devised to simplify the process of querying business data. It enables users to inquire information without the requirement of any coding knowledge, making data analysis readily accessible. This characteristic simplifies complex data interactions allowing project managers and non-technical staff to quickly and efficiently generate insights, improving decision-making and productivity. Users can connect their database to Qquest and start asking product-related questions, contributing to a more streamlined workflow. The setup aims to be hassle-free, so users can concentrate on extracting critical insights from their data without having to engage in complicated processes. Moreover, Qquest provides the opportunity to link up with a range of databases, including snowflake, redshift, bigquery, MySQL, and PostgreSQL. Beyond database integration, Qquest also interfaces with a range of analytic tools and CRM Tools, promoting interoperability. The tool harnesses generative AI technology to provide immediate responses to data inquiries, facilitating instantaneous insights for better product planning, presentations, and project management.
