About Viable

Viable is an AI-powered qualitative feedback analysis tool that helps organizations understand their customers better. It uses advanced AI and NLP technology to provide detailed insights from customer feedback. It enables companies to measure and improve their product-market fit, launch stronger messaging with deep audience insights, and make data-driven decisions faster.Viable's AI-powered reports provide instant, accurate, and data-backed insights for every team. They go beyond sentiment analysis, tracking changes over time and providing summaries, urgency scores, product sub-themes, user profiles and more. It also uses customer traits and metadata to run true analysis and make recommendations based on details like NPS scores, customer tiers, region, and more.Viable can be used to analyze sales call transcripts, customer support tickets, market research, employee surveys, and more. It also offers automated PII redaction and a white-glove service team to respond rapidly to custom data management requests. It is SOC 2 Type 2 certified, and requires only 500 datapoints for users to get started. Viable offers a free 30-day trial to show off what its AI can do with users' data. It integrates directly with leading platforms like Zendesk, Intercom, or Front for customer support, Delighted for surveys, Gong for call transcripts, the app stores for product reviews, and more. It also supports data from virtually any source via Zapier, or files imported in CSV format.
