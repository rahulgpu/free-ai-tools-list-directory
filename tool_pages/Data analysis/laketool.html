About Laketool

Laketool is an AI experimentation platform that allows users to transform data into AI insights by leveraging AI-enabled data lakes. With the increasing volume of data generated by businesses, data lakes are becoming popular due to their affordability and user-friendliness. Laketool offers a solution to extract AI-driven insights directly from these data lakes.The platform provides seamless access and analysis of data lakes, enabling users to easily create AI experiments. It also supports the development and integration of state-of-the-art AI models. Collaboration with teams is facilitated, allowing for effortless collaboration.Laketool offers three simple steps to get started: connect your data lake to the platform, select the data and purpose of your project, and innovate and deploy your AI models through testing, refinement, and deployment.The platform runs directly on the data lake, eliminating the need for database maintenance. It offers fast data analysis through an automatically parallel process and provides insights from state-of-the-art AI models. Integration of AI models into business processes is made easy through API webhooks. Additionally, Laketool allows for easy model updates based on new data in the data lake.For further information or inquiries, users can reach out to the Laketool team via email. Laketool offers a newsletter subscription for users who wish to receive news and updates.
