About Corpus-X

Corpus-X is an AI-powered tool that offers a range of services to empower organizations in managing and analyzing their data effectively. With VizGPT Analytics, users can upload, visualize, and query CSV data, enabling data-driven decision-making. The tool also provides an Instant AI Search widget that offers swift and precise navigation through data. Corpus-X offers 24/7 engagement through dedicated Discord and Telegram bots, ensuring continuous community support. Additionally, users can leverage the Data Transformation feature to go beyond conventional data views and visualize and analyze data in real-time. The tool also enables advanced search and query capabilities for deep insights and context-rich and relevant results. Corpus-X supports flexible data integration from various sources such as website docs, CSVs, Notion, PDFs, and Slack data. Corpus-X positions itself as a trusted partner, championing innovation and offering custom AI solutions to meet specific requirements. The tool boasts an intuitive interface and cutting-edge AI technology, enhancing user interactions and providing a seamless and informative experience. Users can easily integrate VizGPT into their existing systems, transforming the way they perceive and interact with data. Corpus-X is powered by GPT-4 and promises faster responses, versatile integration options, and data safety. For pricing and data training with the chatbot, users can consult the frequently asked questions section, or reach out to Corpus-X via email for collaboration or general inquiries.
