About Dot

Dot is an AI tool that allows you to chat with your Data Warehouse, providing fast and trustworthy answers to most business questions 24/7. It aims to free up data teams from answering easy questions about dashboards, allowing them to focus on more in-depth work. Dot offers a 3-minute demo in Slack and supports natural language queries in multiple languages. It analyzes data for you and provides instant insights, eliminating the need to wait for days. Dot can be integrated with existing tech stacks through no-code integrations, ensuring answer accuracy and consistency. It also includes an automated semantic layer that analyzes data based on approved business logic. Dot offers enterprise-ready security, with a training space that allows data teams to ensure 100% trusted answers. It can be used to explore order data, conduct financial root-cause analysis, and find market insights. Dot has a large customer base of over 50,000 happy customers and receives positive testimonials from users. It integrates with popular databases such as Snowflake, BigQuery, Redshift, and more. Dot is designed to be simple and secure, and it is backed by YCombinator and built by Sled.
