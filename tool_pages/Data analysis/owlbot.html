About Owlbot

Owlbot is an advanced AI-based chatbot platform. It allows users to create an AI-powered chatbot rapidly without coding. Users can integrate the chatbot on their websites, mobile apps, or other platforms, enabling instant answers for customers or team members. Potential applications include answering customer queries with a high degree of accuracy, as well as providing employees with immediate responses. The platform features data integration capability, allowing the import of data from various sources such as documents and markdown. The chatbot interface is customizable, allowing design modifications to align with your brand. Users can access usage statistics and supervise each chatbot conversation to gain insights into their data. The bot is capable of answering queries in 95 different languages, providing multilingual support. It can ingest content from multiple sources instantaneously and begins answering queries immediately. Owlbot's chatbot can connect to your tools, enabling interactions with external systems and performing specialized tasks, enhancing their capabilities. Users can choose from 12 different AI models based on their particular needs, allowing for more specialized and effective outcomes. The chatbot also has lead generation capabilities, asking users for information, such as emails or phone numbers, which can be used for business marketing.
