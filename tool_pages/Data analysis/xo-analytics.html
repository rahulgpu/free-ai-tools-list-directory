About XO Analytics

XO Analytics is an AI-powered data analytics platform specifically designed for Shopify businesses. By seamlessly integrating your accounts, XO Analytics provides actionable insights to help boost your e-commerce growth. This platform aims to turn your data into growth opportunities, allowing you to maximize profits.With XO Analytics, you can connect data from across your entire business, enabling valuable insights that drive your bottom line. It provides data-driven growth tips, AI-powered weekly reports, and customizable alerts to keep you informed about real-time performance.The platform analyzes your data to identify underperforming products and offers suggestions to optimize your ad spend. Its unique weekly Insights feature provides personalized and actionable insights based on your real-time ad and selling data.Trusted by next-gen e-commerce founders, XO Analytics eliminates the guesswork from data analysis, helping businesses optimize their ad spend and boost their return on ad spend (ROAS).XO Analytics offers different pricing plans to meet your needs, from the Starter plan ideal for getting started, to the Pro plan which includes advanced insights and monetized growth opportunity insights. For enterprise-level users, XO Analytics offers a fully supported data pipeline, custom reporting landscape, and managed ETL services, with pricing options available upon request.In summary, XO Analytics is an AI-powered e-commerce analytics platform that empowers Shopify businesses to make data-driven decisions and seize growth opportunities.
