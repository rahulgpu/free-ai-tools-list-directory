About Hive

Hive is a cloud-based AI solution for understanding content, providing developers with pre-trained AI models to address complex content understanding needs and integrate them into their applications. Hive’s AI models are used for content moderation, paywall enforcement, visual moderation, text moderation, audio moderation, demographic identification, logo and logo location identification, visual context identification, OCR, speech-to-text, translation, AI-generated media recognition, web search, custom search, copyright search, NFT search, contextual search, moderation dashboard, content moderation, sponsorship intelligence, ad intelligence, context-based ad targeting, and more. With Hive, developers can unlock insights into visual, audio, and text content with human-level accuracy and machine-level efficiency. Their AI models are trusted by hundreds of the world’s largest and fastest-growing companies, including online communities, dating apps, NFT platforms, marketplaces, brands, publishers, teams, and leagues.
