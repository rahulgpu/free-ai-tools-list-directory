About Typefrost

Typefrost functions as a personal knowledge assistant, built to unify data from diverse sources and provide actionable insights from your collated information. Users can upload and connect data from various sources including, but not limited to, Google Drive, webpages, and YouTube, thus creating a personalised knowledge base. This tool leverages advanced language models such as GPT-4 to assist in interacting with the data, enabling users to ask questions or generate summaries from the provided data. Ensuring data security, it offers advanced authentication features that are compliant with SOC2 and GDPR regulations, allowing users to maintain complete control over their data with the ability to delete at any time. Typefrost also promotes AI-driven conversations, allowing users to interact with their knowledge base in a chat-like environment to find answers instantly. Additionally, it aids in document management by assisting users in tracking the sources for all their documents, eliminating extensive search processes. Future updates plan to further expand the list of integrations to include popular tools like Github and Confluence.
