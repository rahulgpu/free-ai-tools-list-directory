About Groupt

Groupt is an advanced software tool offering data categorization and analytics services. Its core functionality is to simplify the analysis process of complex data sets, typically qualitative, into clear and actionable insights. It achieves this by allowing the user to upload data in a CSV format, then employing its robust AI analysis to sort the data, including visualizing response groupings and categories, providing deeper insights. Data types that can be processed by Groupt range from user feedback to survey responses, or essentially any related qualitative data. As a user, you don't need to submit your credit card to use Groupt's services. Groupt also offers different tiered plans, including free, Pro, and Enterprise, each with its distinct features and capabilities. For instance, with a paid account, the limitations related to the number of rows per CSV upload imposed on free accounts are rectified. Groupt is committed to high accuracy and reliability in its categorization and analysis results, thereby aiding business and individuals in making well-informed decisions from their data.
