About MAIHEM

MAIHEM is an AI tool designed to assist in the automated testing and quality assurance of your AI applications. By creating AI agents, MAIHEM's system can provide continuous testing throughout the entire development and deployment process. It is designed to provide safety and performance analytics that may be valuable to your company. It provides automation of your AI application's quality assurance, saving significant time that would otherwise be spent on manual testing and probing for potential weaknesses in your AI models. The platform can also simulate thousands of realistic personas to interact with your conversational AI and evaluates entire interactions based on a customizable set of performance and risk metrics. Furthermore, MAIHEM offers a user-friendly web application with dashboards that integrate seamlessly into your developer workflow. The tool provides targeted improvements for your conversational AI by leveraging simulation data. It offers secure endpoint access to their cloud, dedicated cloud options, customizable on-premise solutions for enterprise customers as well as expert support for onboarding and AI related issues. Overall, MAIHEM's purpose is to help improve the performance of your conversational AI applications.
