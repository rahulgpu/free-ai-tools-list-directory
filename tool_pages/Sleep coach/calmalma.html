About CalmAlma

CalmAlma is an AI-based application designed to help users achieve restful sleep through personalization and intelligent learning. It utilizes various proven techniques to curate an auditory experience that matches the user's sleep patterns and preferences, promoting deeper and more restful sleep over time. This is materialized through the creation of unique, custom audio episodes which the user can listen to as they drift to sleep. This could include stories, documentaries, meditations, and more, all customized to the user's liking. As users interact with the app, it adapts itself to better fit their unique sleep rhythms, carefully curating subsequent auditory experiences that build upon this learned information. CalmAlma also employs the effectiveness of artistic visuals with each story, tapping into research which suggests that viewing artwork can have a calming effect and help reduce stress. This synergy between art and sound works together to aid the mind in reaching a state of restfulness.
