About Dr. Snooze AI

Dr. Snooze AI is an advanced chatbot constructed by professionals in the field of sleep science, encompassing sleep doctors, scientists, and technicians. Its core function is to provide a virtual sleep coach offering advice and solutions around the clock to improve sleep health, tailored to each user's unique sleep patterns. Beyond frequently asked questions, users can engage in detailed dialogues on sleep improvement with Dr. Snooze. The AI chatbot can address a vast range of sleep challenges, providing immediate response to user queries. The tool extends its coaching capabilities to premium members through live sleep technicians and private consultations with coaches. Dr. Snooze AI's functionalities can be enhanced by integrating it with various wearable devices such as Apple Watch, Oura Ring, Garmin, Biostrap, Beddit, and Withings. This compatibility permits accurate tracking and improving of users' sleep recovery score, also possible through the use of the SleepSpace Smart Bed. More offerings available to users include meditation and breathwork exercises to help stay asleep, and a free trial option to evaluate the tool's suitability. The tool also provides a simple procedure for subscription cancellation for users, regardless of whether the subscription was purchased via Apple App Store, Google Play Store, or the official website.
