About ddle.dev

ddle.dev is an AI-powered online tool designed for creating quick and easy website walkthroughs. This convenient tool allows users to generate an interactive walkthrough of their website without having to schedule or conduct a video call. It provides a means to record a full-screen walk-through of your website, which includes scrolls, with an AI-generated voice and face. The tool also generates a shareable link instantly for you to share with your customers or prospective clients. Furthermore, it's a browser-based tool meaning there's no need for users to download anything to use this service. ddle.dev offers pro access where users can access all its features including setting the narration tone, narration in various languages, and options to automate certain forms. The tool also removes its own branding from the walkthroughs in its pro version.
