About Wiseone

Wiseone is an AI-powered tool designed to enhance web searches and increase reading productivity. As a Google Chrome extension, its key purpose is to save time and boost efficiency in online research and reading. The tool features several functionalities, each designed to enhance your web experience in different ways. The 'Focus' feature helps simplify complex concepts and vocabulary making it easy for users to understand intricate subjects. 'Cross-check' provides access to various reliable sources reporting similar content, allowing users to gain diverse perspectives. The 'Ask Anything' feature offers simplified answers for any query a user might have on a web page or sources answers from top search engines instantly. With the 'Summarize' feature, Wiseone can provide summarised content and key points from any website, aiding in quick reading and retention of information. Their 'Explore' feature enables users to dig deeper into any topic by providing effortless navigation to related articles. This tool, trusted by professionals from leading tech companies and popular universities, aims to support individuals in expanding their knowledge effectively and efficiently.
