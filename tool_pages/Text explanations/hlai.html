About HLAI

HLAI is an AI powered tool serving as an extension that provides instant explanations on any text, requiring for users to simply highlight the desired text. It uses shortcut keys such as CTRL or CMD for quick accessibility. It presents an innovative and efficient way of navigating through text content, eliminating the need for manual lookup of unfamiliar terms or phrases. A unique feature included is the tool's ability to save responses for future reference, ensuring users can revisit the explanations when required. From a privacy perspective, the developer guarantees user data will not be sold to third parties, used for non-related purposes, or used to determine creditworthiness or for lending purposes. This offers assurance to users regarding their data safety. However, it is important to note that HLAI is a tool and will not collect or use data outside of its core functionality.
